# Program Name: Go Digital and Save!

#Program URL: https://www.anaheim.net/6548/Go-Digital-and-Save

## Program Details
- **Concrete Rebate Amounts:**
- $10 (Paperless Billing + Automatic Bill Pay)
- $5 (Anaheim Alert sign-up)

## Eligibility
- **Eligibility Requirements:**
- Must be a new participant in each program.
- Automatic payments must be set up using a checking account.
- For Anaheim Alert credit, must select "Yes" under "Sign Up for Electric Outage Notification".

## Utility Information
- **Utility Company Name:** Anaheim Public Utilities
- **Utility Company Size:** Not specified

# Program Name: TreePower Rebate Program

#Program URL: https://www.anaheim.net/6104/TreePower-Rebate-Program

## Program Details
- **Concrete Rebate Amounts:**
- $20 per tree (up to three trees)

## Eligibility
- **Eligibility Requirements:**
- Must be an Anaheim single family resident.
- Trees must be shade trees (fruit trees and palm trees do not qualify).
- Trees must be 5 gallons or larger at time of purchase.
- Trees must be planted at least 8 feet from the home on the east, south, or west side.
- Trees must be inspected by a TreePower specialist.

## Utility Information
- **Utility Company Name:** Anaheim Public Utilities
- **Utility Company Size:** Not specified
