# Program Name:Heating & Cooling Systems

#Program URL: https://www.anaheim.net/2579/Heating-Cooling-Systems

## Program Details
- **Concrete Rebate Amounts:**
- Air Duct Repair: 50% of repair cost (up to $300)
- Box Fan, Window Fan, Floor Fan: $20 (maximum 2)
- Central Air Conditioner: $200 per ton
- Heat Pump Ductless Mini Split: $200 per ton
- Heat Pump HVAC: $200 per ton
- Room Air Conditioner: $100 (maximum of 2)
- Smart Thermostat: $50 (maximum of 2)
- Whole House Fan: $100

## Eligibility
- **Eligibility Requirements:**
- Air Duct Repair: Must already have a central electric heating or cooling system
- Box Fan, Window Fan, Floor Fan: Must be ETL Listed
- Central Air Conditioner: Minimum 14.3 SEER2 or 15+ SEER
- Central Air Conditioner: Must be a replacement heating/cooling system
- Heat Pump Ductless Mini Split: Minimum 14.3 EER2 or 15+ SEER rating
- Heat Pump Ductless Mini Split: Must be a replacement heating/cooling system
- Heat Pump HVAC: Minimum 14.3 SEER2 or 15+ SEER rating
- Heat Pump HVAC: Must be a replacement heating/cooling system
- Room Air Conditioner: Must be ENERGY STAR certified
- Room Air Conditioner: Must be a replacement heating/cooling system
- Smart Thermostat: Must be ENERGY STAR certified
- Smart Thermostat: Must be Wi-Fi enabled
- Smart Thermostat: Must be a replacement of an existing thermostat
- Whole House Fan: Must be permanently mounted in attic
- Whole House Fan: Existing central air conditioning required

- **Utility Company Name:** Anaheim Public Utilities

- Must submit a copy of the most recent Anaheim Public Utilities water bill.
- Must submit copies of corresponding invoices, receipts, checks, or other documents showing leak repair expenditures.
- Must submit documentation of the leak, such as a spike in water bill, report after inspection of leak by APU staff, or invoice documenting leak.
