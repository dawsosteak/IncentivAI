# Program Name: Lifeline

#Program URL: https://www.lifelinesupport.org/

## Program Details
- **Concrete Rebate Amounts:**
- Up to $18.50 off the monthly cost of phone, internet or bundled services

## Eligibility
- **Eligibility Requirements:**
- Eligible individuals and households (specific income/program criteria managed via Lifeline Support Center)

## Utility Information
- **Utility Company Name:** Federal Program (administered by various internet/phone service providers)
- **Utility Company Size:** Not Specified
