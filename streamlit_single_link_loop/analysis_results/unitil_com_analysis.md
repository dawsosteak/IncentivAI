# Program Name: Schedule IC - Incentives for Energy Efficient Equipment

#Program URL: https://unitil.com/sites/default/files/2024-01/FGE-Schedule-IC.pdf

## Program Details
- **Concrete Rebate Amounts:**
- High Efficiency Boilers: $200 - $1,000
- High Efficiency Furnaces: $200 - $1,000
- Heat Pump Water Heaters: $500 - $1,000
- Air Source Heat Pumps: $500 - $2,000
- Variable Speed Drives: $15 - $30 per HP

## Eligibility
- **Eligibility Requirements:**
- Must be a Unitil customer.
- Equipment must meet specific efficiency ratings (e.g., AFUE, SEER, COP) as defined in the schedule.
- Application must be submitted within the timeframe specified by the utility.
- Equipment must be installed by a licensed contractor.

## Utility Information
- **Utility Company Name:** Unitil
- **Utility Company Size:** Not specified

# Program Name: Mass Save Community First Partner (Fitchburg)

#Program URL: FitchburgSave100.com

## Program Details
- **Concrete Rebate Amounts:**
- No-cost insulation
- No-cost air sealing
- 0% financing options for other upgrades (amounts determined via energy assessment)

## Eligibility
- **Eligibility Requirements:**
- Must be a resident of Fitchburg, MA
- Must be a homeowner, renter, or landlord
- Must schedule and complete a no-cost, no-obligation energy assessment through Mass Save

## Utility Information
- **Utility Company Name:** Unitil
- **Utility Company Size:** Approximately 110,100 electric customers and 105,000 natural gas customers
