# Program Name: WMLP Solar Rebate Program

#Program URL: https://www.ene.org/mlp-solar-rebate-program/

## Program Details
- **Concrete Rebate Amounts:**
- $1,200 per installed kW-direct current (DC) of solar generation capacity
- Maximum rebate of $12,000 for RS-1 Residential Service customers
- Maximum rebate of $15,000 for SGS-1 Small General Service customers
- Rebate cannot exceed 50% of total installed costs (labor and materials) excluding tax

## Eligibility
- **Eligibility Requirements:**
- Must be a WMLP customer served under RS-1 Residential Service or SGS-1 Small General Service rate schedule
- Must have an active electric account in good standing (at least 6 months of satisfactory payments with no past due balance)
- No municipal tax liens on the property or service address
- System must be fully owned by the customer (leased systems and PPAs are ineligible)
- Only new equipment is eligible
- New construction projects are ineligible
- Systems must not have already received an Authorization to Operate from WMLP prior to application
- Must have a minimum total annual solar access of 70% (maximum shading of 30%)
- Must have an azimuth between 80 and 280 degrees
- Must include a separate Also Energy LocusNOC solar production meter
- Must pass all building, electrical, and interconnection inspections
- WMLP must hold title to non-energy attributes and associated MA RPS Class I RECs

## Utility Information
- **Utility Company Name:** Wellesley Municipal Light Plant (WMLP)
- **Utility Company Size:** Not specified
