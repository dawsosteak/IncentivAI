# Program Name: The Minor Plumbing Repair Program (MPRP)

# Program URL: https://dallascityhall.com/departments/waterutilities/Pages/Minor-Plumbing-Repair-Program-(MPRP).aspx

## Program Details
- Free assistance with minor plumbing repairs and fixture replacement.

## Eligibility
- Must be a Dallas Water Utilities (DWU) customer
- Must be a homeowner
- Must reside on the property
- Must meet U.S. Department of Housing and Urban Development low-income guidelines
- Eligible repairs include leaking toilets, faucets, showerheads, outdoor hose bib leaks, and easily accessible pipe joint leaks
- Repairs are made on a first-come, first-served basis
- Wastewater and sewer problems are not included
- Work required under the house or behind the walls is not included
- Underground leaks are not included
- Program is not designed to respond to emergency repairs

## Utility Information
- **Utility Company Name:** Dallas Water Utilities
- **Utility Company Size:** Not specified

# Program Name: Residential Irrigation Rebate Pilot Program

# Program URL: https://dallascityhall.com/departments/waterutilities/Pages/Residential-Irrigation-Rebate-Program.aspx

## Program Details
- Rebate of up to $3,000.

## Eligibility
- Must be a Dallas Water Utilities (DWU) customer
- Must be a qualifying residential property
- Must upgrade an existing irrigation system with eligible water-saving equipment
- Eligible rebate options include installing an EPA WaterSense-labeled irrigation controller
- Eligible rebate options include converting a pop-up spray zone to drip irrigation or bubblers
- Eligible rebate options include converting fixed spray nozzles to multi-stream rotary nozzles in turf zones
- Eligible rebate options include splitting an existing single irrigation zone into separate zones for beds and turf
- For the current pilot phase, participation is limited to a pre-selected group of 100 residential properties that received an Irrigation System Evaluation and were identified as having high potential to benefit
- Must schedule a rebate Irrigation System Evaluation
- Must submit a completed application
- Must complete the project within one year and submit all required documentation

## Utility Information
- **Utility Company Name:** Dallas Water Utilities (DWU)
- **Utility Company Size:** Not specified
