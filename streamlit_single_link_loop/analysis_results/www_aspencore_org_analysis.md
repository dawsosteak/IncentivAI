# Program Name: CORE Energy Efficiency Rebates

#Program URL: https://www.aspencore.org/energy-assessments

## Program Details
- **Concrete Rebate Amounts:**
- Up to $15,000 for homeowners
- Up to $50,000 for businesses

## Eligibility
- **Eligibility Requirements:**
- Homeowners or businesses seeking energy audits, insulation upgrades, or heat pumps.
- Community Priority Participants qualify for free energy assessments.

## Utility Information
- **Utility Company Name:** CORE (Community Office for Resource Efficiency)
- **Utility Company Size:** Not Specified
