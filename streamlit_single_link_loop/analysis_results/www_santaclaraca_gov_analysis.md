# Program Name: Residential Landscape Conversion Rebate

#Program URL: https://www.valleywater.org/saving-water/rebates-surveys/landscape-rebates

## Program Details
- **Concrete Rebate Amounts:**
- $4 per square foot for the first 1,000 square feet
- $2 per square foot after the first 1,000 square feet
- Lifetime combined rebate cap of $5,000

## Eligibility
- **Eligibility Requirements:**
- Residential property
- Must submit an application
- Must receive written Notice to Proceed from Valley Water before starting work
- Fake grass/artificial turf is not allowed
- Plastic weed cloth and plastic sheeting are not allowed
- Hardscape (pavers, flagstone, etc.) cannot exceed 50% of the converted area

## Utility Information
- **Utility Company Name:** Valley Water / City of Santa Clara
- **Utility Company Size:** Not Specified

# Program Name: Commercial Landscape Rebates

#Program URL: https://www.valleywater.org/saving-water/rebates-surveys/landscape-rebates

## Program Details
- **Concrete Rebate Amounts:**
- Up to $110,000 per site (combined cap)
- Landscape Conversion: $3 per square foot for the first 10,000 square feet and $2 per square foot after that
- Lawn to Mulch: $2 per square foot for the first 10,000 square feet and $1 per square foot after that

## Eligibility
- **Eligibility Requirements:**
- Commercial, industrial, and institutional (CII) properties
- Multifamily sites of 5 or more units
- Must submit an application
- Must receive written Notice to Proceed from Valley Water before starting work
- Artificial turf/synthetic grass is prohibited
- Plastic weed cloth and plastic sheeting are prohibited
- Hardscape cannot exceed 50% of the converted area

## Utility Information
- **Utility Company Name:** Valley Water / City of Santa Clara
- **Utility Company Size:** Not Specified

# Program Name: Submeter & Private Well Rebates

#Program URL: https://www.valleywater.org/node/1292

## Program Details
- **Concrete Rebate Amounts:**
- $150 rebate per installed submeter
- $150 rebate for installing a new Valley Water approved water meter on private wells
- Up to $1,000 per Dedicated Landscape Meter

## Eligibility
- **Eligibility Requirements:**
- Submeters: Multifamily properties or mobile home parks
- Private Wells: Must be located within one of Valley Water's active groundwater charge zones

## Utility Information
- **Utility Company Name:** Valley Water / City of Santa Clara
- **Utility Company Size:** Not Specified
