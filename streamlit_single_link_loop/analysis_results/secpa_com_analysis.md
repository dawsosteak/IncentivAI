# Program Name: Electric Vehicle Charging Equipment Rebate

#Program URL: https://secpa.com/sites/default/files/2024-11/electric-vehicle-charging-equipment-rebate-application.pdf

## Program Details
- **Concrete Rebate Amounts:**
- Level 2 Charging Station: $500.00 per station

## Eligibility
- **Eligibility Requirements:**
- Must be a customer of a participating utility
- Must purchase and install a Level 2 EV charging station
- Must provide a copy of the paid invoice
- Must provide a photo of the installed equipment
- Must provide a copy of the utility bill

## Utility Information
- **Utility Company Name:** SECPA (South East Community Power Agency)
- **Utility Company Size:** Not specified
