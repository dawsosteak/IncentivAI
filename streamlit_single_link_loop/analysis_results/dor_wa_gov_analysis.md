# Program Name: Public utility tax credit for billing discounts and qualifying contributions

#Program URL: https://dor.wa.gov/forms-publications/publications-subject/special-notices/public-utility-tax-credit-billing-discounts-and-qualifying-contributions-annual-update-and

## Program Details
- **Concrete Rebate Amounts:**
- Lesser of: 50% of the business’s total billing discounts and qualifying contributions OR the business’s base credit.
- Statewide credit cap: $2.5 million.

## Eligibility
- **Eligibility Requirements:**
- Must be a light and power business or a gas distribution business.
- Must provide billing discounts or qualifying contributions to qualifying persons (Washington residents who qualify for LIHEAP assistance).
- Dollar amount of qualifying contributions must be 125% more than those given in either Fiscal year 2000 or the first year billing discounts/contributions were given (if none in FY 2000).
- Must submit a Public Utility Tax Credit Application postmarked by July 1, 2025.
- Credit must be claimed by December 31, 2025 (program repealed effective January 1, 2026).

## Utility Information
- **Utility Company Name:** Washington Department of Revenue / Department of Commerce (Administering entity)
- **Utility Company Size:** Not Specified

# Program Name: Sales and use tax exemption for zero-emission buses

#Program URL: https://dor.wa.gov/forms-publications/publications-subject/special-notices/sales-and-use-tax-exemption-zero-emission-buses

## Program Details
- **Concrete Rebate Amounts:**
- 100% exemption from sales or use tax on the purchase of zero-emission buses (capped at a total program limit of $14,000,000 in exemptions)

## Eligibility
- **Eligibility Requirements:**
- Must be a transit agency (city-owned transit system, county transportation authority, metropolitan municipal corporation, public transportation benefit area, unincorporated transportation benefit area, or regional transit authority) OR
- Must be a federally recognized Indian tribe providing public transportation services
- Vehicle must be a zero-emission bus (emits no exhaust gas from the onboard source of power, other than water vapor)
- Buyer must provide the seller with a completed buyer’s retail sales tax exemption certificate

## Utility Information
- **Utility Company Name:** Washington Department of Revenue
- **Utility Company Size:** Government Entity
