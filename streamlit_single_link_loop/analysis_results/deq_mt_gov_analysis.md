# Program Name: Montana Energy Conservation Credit

#Program URL: https://deq.mt.gov/energy/programs/code

## Program Details
- **Concrete Rebate Amounts:**
- $500 credit for ENERGY STAR-qualified heating models (rated 95 AFUE and above)
- 25 percent tax credit for new ENERGY STAR qualified light fixtures

## Eligibility
- **Eligibility Requirements:**
- Must purchase ENERGY STAR-qualified heating equipment with a rating of 95 AFUE or above for the $500 credit.
- Must purchase new ENERGY STAR qualified light fixtures for the 25 percent tax credit.

## Utility Information
- **Utility Company Name:** Montana Department of Environmental Quality (DEQ)
- **Utility Company Size:** Government Entity (State Agency)

# Program Name: Energy Efficient Transformer Rebates

#Program URL: https://www.energy.gov/clean-energy-infrastructure/energy-efficient-transformer-rebates

## Program Details
- **Concrete Rebate Amounts:**
- Total funding pool: $10,000,000

## Eligibility
- **Eligibility Requirements:**
- Industrial or manufacturing facility owners
- Commercial building owners
- Multifamily building owners
- Utilities
- Energy service companies
- Must replace a qualified energy inefficient transformer with a qualified energy efficient transformer

## Utility Information
- **Utility Company Name:** U.S. Department of Energy (DOE)
- **Utility Company Size:** Federal Agency
