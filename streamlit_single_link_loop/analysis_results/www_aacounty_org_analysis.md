# Program Name: Geothermal Energy Tax Credit

#Program URL: https://www.aacounty.org/finance/tax-information/tax-credits-exemptions

## Program Details
- **Concrete Rebate Amounts:**
- Up to $2,500 (One-time tax credit)

## Eligibility
- **Eligibility Requirements:**
- Residential dwellings
- Use of geothermal energy devices (heating or cooling device installed using ground loop technology)
- Installed on or after January 1, 2009

## Utility Information
- **Utility Company Name:** Anne Arundel County (Government Entity)
- **Utility Company Size:** Not Specified

# Program Name: Anne Arundel Watershed Restoration Grant Program

#Program URL: https://cbtrust.org/grants/anne-arundel-county-watershed-restoration/

## Program Details
- **Concrete Rebate Amounts:**
- Total available for Anne Arundel County: $1,000,000
- Total available for City of Annapolis: $300,000
- General funding requests: Up to $300,000 (requests larger than $300,000 require prior approval)

## Eligibility
- **Eligibility Requirements:**
- Must be a certified 501(c) non-profit organization (e.g., local watershed groups, community/homeowners’ associations, service/youth/civic groups, faith-based organizations).
- Application must include at least one partner representing a stakeholder group based in Anne Arundel County (including the City of Annapolis).
- Project must be located in Anne Arundel County (including the City of Annapolis).
- Projects must accomplish on-the-ground restoration that treats rainwater runoff from impervious surfaces or meets local water quality and runoff reduction goals.

## Utility Information
- **Utility Company Name:** Anne Arundel County Bureau of Watershed Protection and Restoration / City of Annapolis (Administered by Chesapeake Bay Trust)
- **Utility Company Size:** Not Specified

# Program Name: Pool Rewards

#Program URL: https://www.commuterconnections.org/

## Program Details
- **Concrete Rebate Amounts:**
- Up to $200 monthly subsidy

## Eligibility
- **Eligibility Requirements:**
- Must have an origin in Maryland, West Virginia, Pennsylvania, New Jersey, or the District of Columbia and a destination anywhere in the Washington 8-hour Ozone Non-Attainment area as defined by the U.S. Environmental Protection Agency.
- Must be a newly-forming vanpool (not previously in operation within 30 days prior to the submission of the application).
- Must be registered with Commuter Connections.
- Vehicle must have a 7 to 15 passenger capacity (including the driver).
- Must submit an application through a qualified vanpool provider: VPSI Inc. or Enterprise Rideshare.

## Utility Information
- **Utility Company Name:** Commuter Connections
- **Utility Company Size:** Not Specified
