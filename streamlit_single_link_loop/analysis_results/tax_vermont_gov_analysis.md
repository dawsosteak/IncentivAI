# Program Name: Remote Worker Grant Program

#Program URL: https://tax.vermont.gov/research-and-reports/legislative-updates/2018

## Program Details
- **Concrete Rebate Amounts:**
- Up to $5,000 per year
- Total of $10,000 per individual

## Eligibility
- **Eligibility Requirements:**
- Must be a new full-time employee of a non-Vermont business
- Must become a Vermont resident on or after Jan. 1, 2019
- Must work remotely in Vermont
- Expenses must be qualifying remote worker expenses (e.g., relocation, computer software, hardware)

## Utility Information
- **Utility Company Name:** Agency of Commerce and Community Development (ACCD)
- **Utility Company Size:** Government Entity

# Program Name: Vermont Higher Education Investment Plan (VHEIP) Tax Credit

#Program URL: https://tax.vermont.gov/individuals/personal-income-tax/tax-credits

## Program Details
- **Concrete Rebate Amounts:**
- 10% of the first $2,500 contributed per taxpayer per beneficiary to a beneficiary’s account.

## Eligibility
- **Eligibility Requirements:**
- Must contribute to a VHEIP account (also known as Vermont 529 Plan/VT529).
- Distributions must be used for specific allowable purposes (e.g., qualified higher education expense loan repayment for federal student loans).

## Utility Information
- **Utility Company Name:** Vermont Department of Taxes
- **Utility Company Size:** Government Entity

# Program Name: Vermont Veteran Tax Credit

#Program URL: https://tax.vermont.gov/my/individuals/income-tax-returns/who-needs-to-file/military-personnel

## Program Details
- **Concrete Rebate Amounts:**
- $250 refundable tax credit (for adjusted gross income ≤ $25,000)
- Sliding scale reduction (for adjusted gross income between $25,000 and $30,000)

## Eligibility
- **Eligibility Requirements:**
- Must be a Vermont resident or part-year resident
- Must have a discharge record or other record of separation from active duty verifying service in the uniformed services
- Adjusted gross income must be less than or equal to $30,000

## Utility Information
- **Utility Company Name:** Vermont Department of Taxes
- **Utility Company Size:** Government Entity

# Program Name: Renter Credit

#Program URL: https://tax.vermont.gov/es/renter-credit

## Program Details
- **Concrete Rebate Amounts:**
- Based on income and 10% of the U.S. Housing and Urban Development (HUD)'s fair market rent for an apartment of the applicant's family size in their county.

## Eligibility
- **Eligibility Requirements:**
- Lived within Vermont as main home for all of the past last year.
- Paid rent for at least six months in Vermont (not necessarily consecutive).
- Income is at or below the Renter Credit limits (based on county and number of family members).
- Not claimed as a dependent on anyone else's taxes.

## Utility Information
- **Utility Company Name:** Vermont Department of Taxes
- **Utility Company Size:** Government Entity

# Program Name: Property Tax Credit

#Program URL: https://tax.vermont.gov/es/property/property-tax-credit

## Program Details
- **Concrete Rebate Amounts:**
- Maximum credit of $5,600 for the State education property tax portion
- Maximum credit of $2,400 for the municipal property tax portion

## Eligibility
- **Eligibility Requirements:**
- Property must qualify as a homestead and a Homestead Declaration must be filed
- Must be domiciled in Vermont for the entire taxable year
- Must not be claimed as a dependent of another taxpayer
- Must have owned and occupied the property as a homestead as of April 1 (or leased for no more than 182 days in the claim year)
- Must meet household income criteria (e.g., 2025 maximum household income is $115,400)

## Utility Information
- **Utility Company Name:** Vermont Department of Taxes
- **Utility Company Size:** Government Entity
