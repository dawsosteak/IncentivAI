# Program Name: California LifeLine Program

#Program URL: http://www.californialifeline.com

## Program Details
- **Concrete Rebate Amounts:**
- Up to $19.00 per month (California LifeLine subsidy)
- Up to $20 per month for standalone broadband (Home Broadband Pilot)
- Up to $30 per month for bundled broadband and voice (Home Broadband Pilot)

## Eligibility
- **Eligibility Requirements:**
- Must be a qualified low-income household.
- Resident is eligible if already enrolled in: Medicaid/Medi-Cal, Low Energy Home Heater Assistance Program (LIHEAP), Supplemental Security Income (SSI), Federal Section 8, CalFresh, Food Stamps or Supplemental Nutrition Assistance Program (SNAP), Women, Infants and Children Program (WIC), National School Lunch Program (NSLP), Temporary Assistance for Needy Families (TANF), Tribal TANF, Bureau of Indian Affairs Income Eligible (Tribal Only), Food Distribution Program on Indian Reservations, or Federal Veterans and Survivors Pension Benefit.
- Only one discount per household is allowed (except for teletypewriter users and for Deaf and Disabled Telecommunications Program participants).

## Utility Information
- **Utility Company Name:** California Public Utilities Commission (CPUC)
- **Utility Company Size:** State Agency

# Program Name: Energy Savings Assistance (ESA) Program

#Program URL: Not explicitly provided for application (General CPUC/PG&E program)

## Program Details
- **Concrete Rebate Amounts:**
- Free home weatherization
- Free energy efficiency (EE) appliances
- Free energy education

## Eligibility
- **Eligibility Requirements:**
- Must be a PG&E customer
- Must live in a single-family, multifamily, or mobile home (homeowners and renters eligible)
- Total household income must be equal to or less than 250% of the Federal Poverty Guideline (FPG)

## Utility Information
- **Utility Company Name:** Pacific Gas and Electric Company (PG&E)
- **Utility Company Size:** Not specified
