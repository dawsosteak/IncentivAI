# Program Name: Home Performance with ENERGY STAR®

#Program URL: https://homeenergysavings.delmarva.com/md/residential/home-performance-with-energy-star-program/faqs

## Program Details
- **Concrete Rebate Amounts:**
- Up to $10,000 (General home improvements)
- Up to $15,000 (Electrification rebates for switching combustion fueled HVAC and/or Water Heating systems to electric)

## Eligibility
- **Eligibility Requirements:**
- Must be a current residential Delmarva Power customer with a valid account number.
- Must be the owner of the house where the assessment is performed.
- For electrification rebates: Home must have an operational combustion (gas, propane, or oil) fueled appliance (HVAC/water heater).
- For electrification rebates: Must complete weatherization upgrades (at least attic insulation and air sealing) prior to eligibility.

## Utility Information
- **Utility Company Name:** Delmarva Power
- **Utility Company Size:** Not specified

# Program Name: Home Performance with ENERGY STAR® Moderate Income Offering

#Program URL: https://homeenergysavings.delmarva.com/md/residential/home-performance-energy-starr-moderate-income-offering

## Program Details
- **Concrete Rebate Amounts:**
- Up to $15,000 for eligible electrification upgrades (changing fossil fueled HVAC or water heating systems to electric)
- Tier 1: $5,000 value (Customer pays $250) for air sealing and insulation upgrades
- Tier 2: $7,500 value (Customer pays $500) for air sealing, insulation, and a new heat pump water heater
- Tier 3: $10,000 value (Customer pays $750) for air sealing, insulation, and a new HVAC system
- Tier 4: $15,000 value (Customer pays $750) for attic air sealing, insulation, mechanical ventilation, and either a heat pump water heater or air-sourced heat pump

## Eligibility
- **Eligibility Requirements:**
- Must be a homeowner
- Must have an active residential Delmarva Power electric account
- Must reside in a single-family residence (not a condominium or apartment; no separately metered units above or below)
- Must reside in Maryland
- Must meet moderate income criteria based on family unit size (e.g., Family of 1: $68,501 - $85,620; Family of 4: $97,801 - $124,800)
- Home must be up to 3,000 square feet
- Home must have a single combustion appliance zone
- Must complete a no-cost home energy assessment

## Utility Information
- **Utility Company Name:** Delmarva Power
- **Utility Company Size:** Not specified
