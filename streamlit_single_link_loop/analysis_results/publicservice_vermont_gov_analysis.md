# Program Name: Community Energy Development Fund (CEDF) ARPA Program

#Program URL: https://publicservice.vermont.gov/sites/dps/files/documents/Renewable_Energy/CEDF/CED_Board/2021_Agenda-minutes/2021_Documents/CED%20Board_%245M%20ARPA%20Program%20Plan.pdf

## Program Details
- **Concrete Rebate Amounts:**
- Total program funding: $5 million (ARPA funds)
- Grants provided to eligible projects to support community energy development

## Eligibility
- **Eligibility Requirements:**
- Projects must be located within Vermont
- Projects must align with the goals of the Community Energy Development Fund
- Priority given to projects benefiting low-to-moderate income (LMI) communities
- Projects must demonstrate a commitment to energy efficiency and renewable energy deployment

## Utility Information
- **Utility Company Name:** Vermont Department of Public Service
- **Utility Company Size:** State Government Agency

# Program Name: SHARE (Sustainable Homes Affordable Relief Energy) Program

#Program URL: https://publicservice.vermont.gov/sites/dps/files/documents/2023%20SHARE%20Program%20RFP.pdf

## Program Details
- **Concrete Rebate Amounts:**
- Funding available for weatherization and energy efficiency measures (Specific individual rebate amounts are determined by the selected vendor/implementer based on the RFP award).

## Eligibility
- **Eligibility Requirements:**
- Low-income households in Vermont.
- Households meeting specific income guidelines (typically based on federal poverty levels).
- Residents of Vermont.

## Utility Information
- **Utility Company Name:** Vermont Department of Public Service
- **Utility Company Size:** State Government Agency

# Program Name: RFP for Advanced Metering Infrastructure Implementation

#Program URL: https://publicservice.vermont.gov/sites/dps/files/documents/Electric/Smart_Grid/AMI%20RFP.pdf

## Program Details
- **Concrete Rebate Amounts:**
- Up to 70 percent reimbursement for the implementation of Advanced Metering Infrastructure systems.

## Eligibility
- **Eligibility Requirements:**
- Must be a municipal electrical distribution utility.
- Must be a cooperative electrical distribution utility.
- System must be approved by the Public Utility Commission.

## Utility Information
- **Utility Company Name:** Vermont Department of Public Service
- **Utility Company Size:** State Government Agency
