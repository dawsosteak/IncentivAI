# Program Name: Renewable Energy Tax Abatement (RETA) Program

#Program URL: https://www.energy.nv.gov/programs/renewable-energy-tax-abatement-reta-program/renewable-energy-tax-abatement-projects-2026

## Program Details
- **Concrete Rebate Amounts:**
- Sales & Use Tax Abatement (3 years)
- Property Tax Abatement (20 Years)

## Eligibility
- **Eligibility Requirements:**
- Must be a renewable energy project (e.g., Solar and Storage)
- Must submit an application for review
- Subject to public hearing and approval via Legal Order, Agreement, or Certificate of Eligibility

## Utility Information
- **Utility Company Name:** Nevada Governor's Office of Energy
- **Utility Company Size:** Government Entity (State of Nevada)

# Program Name: Performance Contract Audit Assistance Program (PCAAP)

#Program URL: https://www.energy.nv.gov/programs/performance-contracting/performance-contract-audit-assistance-program

## Program Details
- **Concrete Rebate Amounts:**
- $0.10 per square foot (or other amount at the discretion of the Director)

## Eligibility
- **Eligibility Requirements:**
- Must be an eligible Nevada government entity (counties, cities, school districts, state colleges, state universities, and State of Nevada agencies).
- Must be a retail customer of a public utility subject to the portfolio standard established by the Public Utilities Commission of Nevada.
- Project must be for government-owned buildings.
- Proposed project must include measures to reduce electricity consumption or cost.
- Must use and authorize GOE staff to track the project in eProject Builder.
- Must submit a signed PCAAP application and a copy of the current FGO audit.
- Must provide a fully executed performance contract to GOE for funding approval.

## Utility Information
- **Utility Company Name:** Nevada Governor's Office of Energy (GOE)
- **Utility Company Size:** State Agency
