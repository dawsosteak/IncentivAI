# Program Name: Medical Telecommunications Equipment Program (Telemedicine Grant)

#Program URL: https://psc.wi.gov/Pages/ServiceType/Telecom/TelemedicineGrants.aspx

## Program Details
- **Concrete Rebate Amounts:**
- Up to $500,000 available on a fiscal year basis for the purchase of medical telecommunications equipment.

## Eligibility
- **Eligibility Requirements:**
- Must be a "Nonprofit medical clinic" (including certain hospitals governed by a board of directors) that:
    - Serves federally designated Health Professional Shortage Areas (HPSA) or Medically underserved populations.
    - Serves all patients regardless of health-insurance status, or serves uninsured patients using an income-base sliding fee scale.
- OR must be a "Public health agency," including:
    - Wisconsin Department of Health Services.
    - Local health departments as defined in Wis. Stat. § 250.01(4).
    - Tribal health care facility or program as defined in 25 USC § 450f et seq.

## Utility Information
- **Utility Company Name:** Public Service Commission of Wisconsin (Universal Service Fund)
- **Utility Company Size:** Not Specified

# Program Name: Second Line Voice Carryover (2LVCO) for Providers

#Program URL: https://psc.wi.gov/Pages/ServiceType/Telecom/SecondLineVoiceCarryover.aspx

## Program Details
- **Concrete Rebate Amounts:**
- Reimbursement for all waived intrastate recurring and nonrecurring charges (including one-time activation charges, monthly access charges, county and state taxes, 911 fees, police and fire protection fees, and state USF surcharges).

## Eligibility
- **Eligibility Requirements:**
- Must be an Eligible Telecommunications Carrier (ETC).
- Must provide a second line for simultaneous voice communications to consumers with hearing loss using captioned telephone service.
- Must waive all intrastate recurring and nonrecurring charges for the second line.
- Must file a reimbursement request letter via the PSC Electronic Records Filing (ERF) system in the current calendar year's docket (5-LL-[year]) including a link to or graphic of the provider’s tariff or current rate sheet.

## Utility Information
- **Utility Company Name:** Wisconsin Universal Service Fund (USF) / Public Service Commission of Wisconsin
- **Utility Company Size:** Not Specified

# Program Name: Grid Resilience Program

#Program URL: https://psc.wi.gov/Pages/ServiceType/OEI/GridResilience.aspx

## Program Details
- **Concrete Rebate Amounts:**
- Round One Total Funds: $8,512,887.35 (distributed among 19 grantees)

## Eligibility
- **Eligibility Requirements:**
- Must be one of the following: Electric grid operator, Electricity storage operator, Electricity generator, Transmission owner or operator, Distribution provider, or Fuel supplier.
- Projects must meet program objectives: modernize/harden the electric grid, reduce service disruptions in disadvantaged communities, or increase the skilled resilience workforce.
- Matching Funds Required: Small utility applicants must provide 50 percent of the grant award amount; all other applicants must provide 116 percent of the grant award amount.

## Utility Information
- **Utility Company Name:** Public Service Commission of Wisconsin (Office of Energy Innovation)
- **Utility Company Size:** Not Specified

# Program Name: Health Professional Shortage Area (HPSA) Primary Health Care Grant

#Program URL: https://psc.wi.gov/Documents/USF/2025-HPSA-PrimaryHealth-MAP.pdf

## Program Details
- **Concrete Rebate Amounts:**
- Up to $10,000 per site for eligible equipment and services

## Eligibility
- **Eligibility Requirements:**
- Must be a primary health care provider
- Must be located in a designated Health Professional Shortage Area (HPSA)
- Must be a utility customer within the service territory of the participating utility
