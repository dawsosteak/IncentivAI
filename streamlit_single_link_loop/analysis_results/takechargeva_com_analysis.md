# Program Name: Business Energy Solutions Program

#Program URL: https://takechargeva.com/programs/for-your-business/business-energy-solutions

## Program Details
- **Concrete Rebate Amounts:**
- Up to $50,000 in rebates for upgrade projects

## Eligibility
- **Eligibility Requirements:**
- Commercial and industrial customers
- Projects involving upgrades to workplace lighting, HVAC, or kitchen equipment

## Utility Information
- **Utility Company Name:** Appalachian Power
- **Utility Company Size:** Serves about 1 million customers in West Virginia, Virginia and Tennessee
