# Program Name: Statewide Rebate Program - Water Heater, Smart Thermostat, Room A/C

#Program URL: https://guide.pge.com/search?resource_id=26

## Program Details
- **Concrete Rebate Amounts:**
- $700 (Hybrid Heat Pump Water Heater)
- $600 (Hybrid Heat Pump Water Heater)
- $90 (Smart Room Air Conditioner)
- $60 (Smart Wifi Thermostat)
- $40 (Smart Programmable Thermostat)
- $75 (Natural Gas Water Heater)

- **Eligibility Requirements:**
- Purchase of ENERGY STAR® certified products (where applicable)
- Products must improve comfort, health, and energy efficiency of the home

- **Utility Company Name:** PG&E (Pacific Gas and Electric Company)
