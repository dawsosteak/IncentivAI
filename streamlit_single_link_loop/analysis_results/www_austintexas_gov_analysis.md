# Program Name: Rent and Austin Energy assistance

#Program URL: https://www.austintexas.gov/services/get-help-neighborhood-centers

## Program Details
- **Concrete Rebate Amounts:**
- Limited funds available (Specific dollar amounts not listed)

## Eligibility
- **Eligibility Requirements:**
- Must be a resident of Austin/Travis County.
- Must be a renter with your name on lease or contract.
- Rent/utility bill must be PAST DUE in order to receive help.
- Must provide documentation that household income is at or below 200% Federal Poverty Income Guidelines (FPIG) for the past 30 days.
- Must have proof of recent crisis or financial trouble causing the need for help (e.g., job loss, medical bills, new family members, funeral expenses).

# Program Name: Austin Water Residential Rebates

#Program URL: https://www.austintexas.gov/water/programs/rebates-tools-programs

## Program Details
- **Concrete Rebate Amounts:**
- WaterWise Landscape: Up to $3,000
- Landscape Survival Tools: Up to $120
- WaterWise Rainscape: $0.50 per sq. ft. converted (100 sq. ft. minimum), up to $1,500
- Rainwater Harvesting: Up to $5,000 based on storage capacity
- Irrigation Upgrade: Up to $1,000
- Watering Timer: Up to $40
- Laundry to Landscape: Up to $600
- Pool Filter: Up to $250
- Pool Cover: Up to $200

## Eligibility
- **Eligibility Requirements:**
- Must receive water from Austin Water or one of the following qualifying providers: Aqua Texas (Morningside, Night Hawk WSC, Rivercrest Water Systems), City of Rollingwood, City of Sunset Valley, City of Westlake Hills, Creedmoor-Maha WSC, High Valley WSC, Marsha WSC, Mid-Tex Utilities, North Austin MUD No. 1, Northtown MUD, Shady Hollow MUD, Travis County WCID No. 10, Village of San Leanna, Wells Branch MUD, or Windermere Utility.

## Utility Information
- **Utility Company Name:** Austin Water
- **Utility Company Size:** Not Specified
