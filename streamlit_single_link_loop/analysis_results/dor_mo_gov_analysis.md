# Program Name: Biodiesel Retailer and Distributor Tax Credit

#Program URL: https://dor.mo.gov/tax-credits/brd.html

## Program Details
- **Concrete Rebate Amounts:**
- $0.02 per gallon of biodiesel blend of at least 5% but not more than 10%
- $0.05 per gallon of biodiesel blend in excess of 10% but not more than 20%

## Eligibility
- **Eligibility Requirements:**
- Must be a Retail Dealer (person, firm, or corporation doing business in Missouri that owns or operates a retail service station in Missouri) or a Distributor (person, firm, or corporation doing business in Missouri that produces, refines, blends, compounds, manufactures, imports, or distributes motor fuel).
- Must sell a qualifying biodiesel blend (5% to 20% biodiesel fuel) to a final user in Missouri.
- Biodiesel fuel must meet ASTM International D6751 Standard Specification.
- Biodiesel produced from palm oil is ineligible unless contained within waste oil and grease collected within the United States.

## Utility Information
- **Utility Company Name:** Missouri Department of Revenue
- **Utility Company Size:** Government Entity

# Program Name: Disabled Access Tax Credit (DAC)

#Program URL: https://dor.mo.gov/tax-credits/dac.html

## Program Details
- **Concrete Rebate Amounts:**
- Up to 50 percent of “eligible access expenditures” which exceed $10,250
- Maximum credit of $5,000 a year

## Eligibility
- **Eligibility Requirements:**
- Must be a Missouri small business
- Gross receipts did not exceed $1 million dollars for the preceding tax year OR did not employ more than 30 full-time employees during the preceding year
- Access expenditures must exceed the $10,250 federal credit limit

## Utility Information
- **Utility Company Name:** Missouri Department of Revenue
- **Utility Company Size:** Government Entity
