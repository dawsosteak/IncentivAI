# Program Name: WMLP Solar Rebate Program

#Program URL: https://www.ene.org/mlp-solar-rebate-program/

## Program Details
- **Concrete Rebate Amounts:**
- $1,200 per installed kW-direct current (DC) of solar generation capacity
- Maximum rebate of $12,000 for RS-1 Residential Service customers
- Maximum rebate of $15,000 for SGS-1 Small General Service customers
- Rebate cannot exceed 50% of total installed costs (labor and materials) excluding tax

## Eligibility
- **Eligibility Requirements:**
- Must be a WMLP customer served under RS-1 Residential Service or SGS-1 Small General Service rate schedules
- Must have an active electric account in good standing (at least 6 months of satisfactory payments, no past due balance, and no municipal tax liens)
- System must be customer-owned (leased systems and PPAs are ineligible)
- System must be installed at the account's service address
- Must be new equipment (repairs or partial replacements are ineligible)
- New construction projects are ineligible
- System must not have already received an Authorization to Operate from WMLP prior to application
- Must meet technical requirements: azimuth between 80 and 280 degrees and minimum total annual solar access of 70%
- Must include an Also Energy Locus Meter (LocusNOC solar production meter)
- Must pass all building, electrical, and interconnection inspections
- WMLP must hold title to non-energy attributes and associated Massachusetts RPS Class I RECs

## Utility Information
- **Utility Company Name:** Wellesley Municipal Light Plant (WMLP)
- **Utility Company Size:** Not specified

# Program Name: Bring Your Own Charger (BYOC) Program

#Program URL: https://www.wellesleyma.gov/1339/BYOC-FAQ

## Program Details
- **Concrete Rebate Amounts:**
- $8 monthly credit

## Eligibility
- **Eligibility Requirements:**
- Must be a resident of Wellesley, MA
- Must have a WMLP account
- Must own a plug-in electric vehicle (verified via VIN)
- Must agree to charge off-peak (weekdays, 12 PM to 10 PM is considered peak; emergency on-peak charging limited to 3 times per month)

## Utility Information
- **Utility Company Name:** Wellesley Municipal Light Plant (WMLP)
- **Utility Company Size:** Not specified
