# Program Name: New Construction & Major Renovation Program

#Program URL: http://marbleheadelectric.com/pdfs/rebates/commercial/2024-MMLD-NZ-New_Construction-Major-Renovation-Prog-Description-ACC.pdf

## Program Details
- **Concrete Rebate Amounts:**
- Up to $0.15 per kWh of energy savings
- Up to $0.10 per therm of energy savings

## Eligibility
- **Eligibility Requirements:**
- Must be a new construction project or a major renovation.
- Project must be located within the MMLD service territory.
- Energy savings must be verified through a professional energy audit or engineered calculations.
- Equipment must meet program-specific efficiency requirements.
- Application must be submitted and approved prior to the purchase of equipment.

## Utility Information
- **Utility Company Name:** Marblehead Municipal Light Department (MMLD)
- **Utility Company Size:** Not specified
