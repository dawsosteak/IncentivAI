# Program Name: Energy Efficiency Financial Request (On-Bill Financing Application Program)

#Program URL: https://energyinsight.pge.com/FinancialRequestApplication?_gl=1%2A66uw41%2A_gcl_au%2AMTg0Nzg2OTU4MS4xNzA1NzEwMTIy

## Program Details
- **Concrete Rebate Amounts:**
- Interest-free loans ranging from $5,000 to $4,000,000 per premise
- Loan periods of up to 120 months

## Eligibility
- **Eligibility Requirements:**
- Must be a current PG&E commercial customer
- Project must involve replacing old and worn-out equipment with more energy-efficient models
- Eligible project types include: Agriculture, Appliances, Boilers & Water Heating, Food Service, HVAC, Lighting, and Refrigeration

## Utility Information
- **Utility Company Name:** PG&E (Pacific Gas and Electric Company)
- **Utility Company Size:** Not specified
