# Program Name: Impact Fee Conservation/Reuse Credits

#Program URL: https://www.fortworthtexas.gov/impact-fees/water-impact-fees

## Program Details
- **Concrete Rebate Amounts:**
- 5% credit of collected water and/or wastewater impact fees (Single Family Residential Land Uses)
- 5% credit of collected water and/or wastewater impact fees (Irrigation-Only Land Uses)
- 2% credit of collected water and/or wastewater impact fees (Other Land Uses)

## Eligibility
- **Eligibility Requirements:**
- Must be a new development (Retail or Wholesale Customer).
- Must implement eligible conservation or reuse measures that exceed City code, design standards, or the City’s Water Conservation Plan.
- Measures must be permanent improvements (not temporary or portable).
- Must submit a detailed study signed and sealed by a licensed professional engineer in the State of Texas.
- Must fund and install system-level metering infrastructure.
- Must complete a 5-year Monitoring Period to verify projected performance.
- **Specific Performance Criteria:**
    - **Single Family:** Average day water usage factor $\le$ 68 gpcd; max day to average day peaking factor $\le$ 2.25; peak hour to max day peaking factor $\le$ 2.00.
    - **Other Land Uses:** Demonstrate need for and installation of a water meter smaller than the pre-conservation size; demands must not exceed projected demands per City Installation Policy.
    - **Irrigation-Only:** Zero irrigation ordinance violations (excluding approved variances) and measures must remain functional.
