# Program Name: Portland Residential and Multifamily Program

#Program URL: https://insider.energytrust.org/wp-content/uploads/Portland-Residential-and-Multifamily-Program-Updates.pdf

## Program Details
- **Concrete Rebate Amounts:**
- Heat Pump Water Heater: $1,000
- Heat Pump Space Heating: $1,000
- Combined Heat Pump Space Heating and Water Heating: $2,000

## Eligibility
- **Eligibility Requirements:**
- Must be a residential or multifamily property located within the Portland General Electric (PGE) or Pacific Power service territories.
- Equipment must meet specific energy efficiency standards as defined by Energy Trust of Oregon.

## Utility Information
- **Utility Company Name:** Portland General Electric (PGE) and Pacific Power
- **Utility Company Size:** Not specified

# Program Name: Enhanced CPF Windows Incentive

#Program URL: https://insider.energytrust.org/wp-content/uploads/Enhanced-CPF-Windows-Incentive-Summary-2024.pdf

## Program Details
- **Concrete Rebate Amounts:**
- $10 per square foot for windows meeting ENERGY STAR requirements (Standard)
- $15 per square foot for windows meeting higher performance requirements (Enhanced)

## Eligibility
- **Eligibility Requirements:**
- Must be a qualifying customer of a participating utility
- Windows must meet specific ENERGY STAR performance criteria
- Installation must be verified by a participating contractor or through approved documentation

## Utility Information
- **Utility Company Name:** Energy Trust of Oregon
- **Utility Company Size:** Not specified
