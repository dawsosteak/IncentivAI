# Program Name: Appalachian Community Grant Program

#Program URL: https://development.ohio.gov/community/governors-office-of-appalachia/appalachian-community-grant-program

## Program Details
- **Concrete Rebate Amounts:**
- $500 million total program funding
- $15 million to $30 million allocated for planning

## Eligibility
- **Eligibility Requirements:**
- Must be an entity within Ohio's Appalachian communities
- Projects must focus on infrastructure development, physical and behavioral health, or workforce development

## Utility Information
- **Utility Company Name:** Ohio Department of Development (Governor’s Office of Appalachia)
- **Utility Company Size:** Government Entity
