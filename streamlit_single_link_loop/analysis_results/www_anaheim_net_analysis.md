# Program Name: TreePower Rebate Program

#Program URL: https://www.anaheim.net/6104/TreePower-Rebate-Program

## Program Details
- **Concrete Rebate Amounts:**
- $20 per tree (up to three trees)

## Eligibility
- **Eligibility Requirements:**
- Must be an Anaheim single family resident.
- Trees must be shade trees (fruit trees and palm trees do not qualify).
- Trees must be 5 gallons or larger at time of purchase.
- Trees must be planted at least 8 feet from the home on the east, south, or west side.
- Trees must be inspected by a TreePower specialist.

## Program Details
- **Concrete Rebate Amounts:**
- $30 bill credit (Enrollment reward for customers with central air conditioner and pool pump)
- $20 bill credit (Enrollment reward for customers with central air conditioner)
- $10 bill credit (Enrollment reward for customers with wall air conditioner or EV charger)

- **Eligibility Requirements:**
- Must be an eligible residential customer of Anaheim Public Utilities
- Must have an interval recording smart meter installed at the home
- Must enroll in the Program by filling out the application
- Must sign-up to receive Program event notifications via email or text message

# Program Name: Dusk-to-Dawn Lighting

#Program URL: https://www.anaheim.net/6475/Multi-Family-Property-Owner-Programs

## Program Details
- **Concrete Rebate Amounts:**
- Complimentary high-efficiency LED exterior fixtures (maximum of 10 wall-pack fixtures)

## Eligibility
- **Eligibility Requirements:**
- Must be Anaheim Public Utilities customers with good-standing accounts
- Participant must be the property owner or legal representative
- Lights must be installed within 30 days of receipt
- Fixtures must remain illuminated throughout the entire period of darkness
- Fixtures must be consistently maintained and operational for five years from the installation date
- Must grant permission for an Anaheim Public Utilities representative to carry out an inspection of the installed fixture(s)
- Subject to approval from Anaheim Public Utilities and availability of funding

## Utility Information
- **Utility Company Name:** Anaheim Public Utilities
- **Utility Company Size:** Not Specified

# Program Name: Weatherization and Home Efficiency Upgrade Program

#Program URL: https://www.anaheim.net/946/Weatherization-Program

## Program Details
- **Concrete Rebate Amounts:**
- No-cost home repairs
- No-cost replacement of inefficient appliances
- No-cost installation of:
    - Water Use Evaluation
    - Energy Use Evaluation
    - LED Lamps (10 max)
    - Smart Thermostat
    - Smart Power Strip (2 max)
    - High Efficiency Clothes Washer
    - A/C Tune-Up
    - Automatic Sprinkler Controller Programming
    - Duct Seal and Test
    - Ceiling Fan Replacement
    - Room A/C Replacement
    - Premium High-Efficiency Toilet
    - HVAC Brushless Fan Motor
    - HVAC Efficient Fan Control Switch
    - Weather Based Irrigation Controller
    - Thermostatic Tub Spout
    - Thermostatic Shower Valve
    - High-Efficiency Showerheads
    - High-Efficiency Faucet Aerators
    - Tankless Water Heater
    - Water Heater Pipe Insulation
    - Water Heater Blanket
    - Attic Insulation

## Eligibility
- **Eligibility Requirements:**
- Must be an income-qualified renter or homeowner
- Renters must have written permission from property owners
- Low-Income Limits as defined by the California Department of Housing and Community Development
- Certain measures require being a Southern California Gas Company (SoCal Gas) customer
- Participation is limited to once every 5 years
- Subject to available funding

## Utility Information
- **Utility Company Name:** Anaheim Public Utilities
- **Utility Company Size:** Not specified
