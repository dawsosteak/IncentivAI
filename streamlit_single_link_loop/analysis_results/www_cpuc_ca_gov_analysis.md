# Program Name: California Alternate Rates for Energy (CARE)

#Program URL: https://www.cpuc.ca.gov/industries-and-topics/electrical-energy/electric-costs/care-fera-program

## Program Details
- **Concrete Rebate Amounts:**
- 30–35% discount on electric bill (for utilities with 100,000+ accounts)
- 20% discount on electric bill (for utilities with <100,000 accounts)
- 20% discount on natural gas bill

## Eligibility
- **Eligibility Requirements:**
- Household income must be at or below the following limits (June 1, 2025 – May 31, 2026):
    - 1-2 person household: $42,300
    - 3 person household: $53,300
    - 4 person household: $64,300
    - 5 person household: $75,300
    - 6 person household: $86,300
    - 7 person household: $97,300
    - 8 person household: $108,300
    - Each additional person: +$11,000
- OR enrollment in any of the following: Medicaid / Medi-Cal, WIC, Healthy Families A & B, National School Lunch Program (Free Lunch), Food Stamps / SNAP, LIHEAP, Head Start Income Eligible (Tribal Only), SSI, Bureau of Indian Affairs General Assistance, TANF or Tribal TANF.

## Utility Information
- **Utility Company Name:** PG&E, Edison, SDG&E, SoCalGas, Alpine Nat'l Gas, Bear Valley Elect, PacifiCorp, Liberty Utilities, Southwest Gas, West Coast Gas
- **Utility Company Size:** Mixed (Large: 100,000+ accounts; Small: <100,000 accounts)
