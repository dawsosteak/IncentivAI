# Program Name: Non-Residential Small Business Direct Install Program

#Program URL: https://ma-eeac.org/wp-content/uploads/Non-Residential-Small-Business-Direct-Install-Program-Multi-Tier-Program-Structure-Assessment-Process-Evaluation-Final-Report.pdf

## Program Details
- **Concrete Rebate Amounts:**
- No-cost installation for eligible energy efficiency measures (Direct Install model)

## Eligibility
- **Eligibility Requirements:**
- Must be a non-residential small business
- Must be located within the service territories of participating utilities
- Must meet specific energy usage or business size criteria defined by the program tiers

## Utility Information
- **Utility Company Name:** Participating Massachusetts Utilities (administered via Mass Save)
- **Utility Company Size:** Not specified
