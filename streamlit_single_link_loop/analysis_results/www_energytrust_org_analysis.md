# Program Name: Windows Incentives

#Program URL: https://www.energytrust.org/residential/incentives/windows/details

## Program Details
- **Concrete Rebate Amounts:**
- $10 per sq ft of window installed

## Eligibility
- **Eligibility Requirements:**
- Residence Type: Owner-occupied or rental property (Single Family Home, Manufactured Home, Townhouse, Duplex, Triplex, or Fourplex).
- Location: Southwest Washington.
- Utility: Must be a customer of NW Natural.
- Heating System: Home must be primarily heated by gas provided by NW Natural.
- Installation: Must be installed by an actively licensed contractor.
- Product Requirements: Must replace single pane (any material) or double pane (metal frame only) windows, glass doors, or skylights.
- Efficiency Standard: U-value must be 0.30 or less.

## Utility Information
- **Utility Company Name:** Energy Trust of Oregon (serving NW Natural in Washington)
- **Utility Company Size:** Not specified

# Program Name: Gas Fireplace Incentives

#Program URL: https://www.energytrust.org/residential/incentives/fireplace/all

## Program Details
- **Concrete Rebate Amounts:**
- $150 (for fireplaces with FE rating between 70% – 74.99%)
- $250 (for fireplaces with FE rating of 75% or greater)

## Eligibility
- **Eligibility Requirements:**
- **Residence Types:** Owner-occupied or rental properties including Single Family Homes, Manufactured Homes, and Townhouses, Duplexes, Triplexes, or Fourplexes.
- **Installation:** Must be installed by an actively licensed contractor.
- **Technical Specifications:** Must be a natural gas fireplace with an electric intermittent pilot light ignition, vented outside with sealed combustion, and listed on the qualified products list.
- **Application:** Must be received within 60 days of installation.

## Utility Information
- **Utility Company Name:** Energy Trust of Oregon (Serving PGE, Pacific Power, NW Natural, Cascade Natural Gas, and Avista)
- **Utility Company Size:** Not specified
