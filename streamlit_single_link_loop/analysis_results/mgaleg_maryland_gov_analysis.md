# Program Name: Maryland Farms and Families Fund

#Program URL: https://mgaleg.maryland.gov/mgawebsite/Legislation/Details/sb0483?search=True&ys=2019RS

## Program Details
- **Concrete Rebate Amounts:**
- $100,000 annual appropriation to the Fund

## Eligibility
- **Eligibility Requirements:**
- Nonprofit farmers markets
- Local nonprofit organizations
- Preference may be given to organizations with a record of providing services in food deserts

## Utility Information
- **Utility Company Name:** Maryland Department of Agriculture
- **Utility Company Size:** Government Entity

# Program Name: Comprehensive Flood Management Grant Program

#Program URL: https://mgaleg.maryland.gov/mgawebsite/Legislation/Details/SB0269?ys=2019RS

## Program Details
- **Concrete Rebate Amounts:**
- Grants available for subdivisions that incurred infrastructure damage of at least $1,000,000

## Eligibility
- **Eligibility Requirements:**
- Must be a local jurisdiction or subdivision
- Must have incurred infrastructure damage of at least $1,000,000 caused by a flood event
- Flood event must have occurred on or after January 1, 2009
- Funds must be used for infrastructure repairs, watershed restoration, and emergency protection work

## Utility Information
- **Utility Company Name:** Maryland Department of the Environment
- **Utility Company Size:** Government Entity
