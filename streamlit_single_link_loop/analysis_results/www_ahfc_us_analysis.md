# Program Name: Jumpstart Incentives & Rewards

#Program URL: https://www.ahfc.us/tenants/jumpstart/incentives

## Program Details
- **Concrete Rebate Amounts:**
- Initial Employment (3 months): $250 for three consecutive months
- Sustained Employment (9 months): $250 for nine months in a one-year period
- Sustained Employment (18 months): $250 for 18 months in a two-year period
- Tuition Assistance (Level 1): Up to $5,000
- Tuition Assistance (Level 2): Up to $3,000
- Educational Rewards: Up to $500 upon completion ($100 per credit)
- Savings Match: Up to $500 each year, maximum of $1,500
- Support Services: Up to $300 annually
- Maximum Total Incentive (Level 1): $5,000
- Maximum Total Incentive (Level 2): $3,000

## Eligibility
- **Eligibility Requirements:**
- Must be enrolled in Jumpstart
- For Work Rewards: Must be working less than 30 hours a week at enrollment and then average 30+ hours a week for the specified period
- For Savings Match: Must successfully complete Alaska Housing's Financial Literacy Requirement and end housing assistance
- Must not have a past due balance with Alaska Housing
- Must be in compliance with Alaska Housing program obligations
- Level 1 classification: Families paying 50% or more of income towards shelter costs (rent and utilities)
- Level 2 classification: Families paying less than 50% of income towards shelter costs (rent and utilities)

## Utility Information
- **Utility Company Name:** Alaska Housing Finance Corporation
- **Utility Company Size:** Not Specified

# Program Name: New Home Construction Rebate

#Program URL: https://www.ahfc.us/blog/posts/apply-ahfcs-10000-new-home-construction-rebate

## Program Details
- **Concrete Rebate Amounts:**
- $10,000

## Eligibility
- **Eligibility Requirements:**
- Building or purchasing a newly constructed home
- Home must be owner-occupied
- Home must meet 5-Star Plus or higher energy efficiency standard

## Utility Information
- **Utility Company Name:** Alaska Housing Finance Corporation (AHFC)
- **Utility Company Size:** Not Specified

# Program Name: Weatherization

#Program URL: https://www.ahfc.us/efficiency/weatherization

## Program Details
- **Concrete Rebate Amounts:**
- Program services provided at no cost to qualified applicants.

## Eligibility
- **Eligibility Requirements:**
- Must meet specific income limits for Alaska.
- Must be a homeowner or renter.
- Includes rentals and condos.
- Homes weatherized prior to May 14, 2008, are eligible for re-weatherization.

## Utility Information
- **Utility Company Name:** Alaska Housing Finance Corporation (AHFC)
- **Utility Company Size:** Not Specified
