# Program Name: Medium-Duty and Heavy-Duty Zero-Emission Vehicle Grant Program

#Program URL: https://energy.maryland.gov/transportation/Pages/MediumandHeavyDutyGrant.aspx

## Program Details
- **Concrete Rebate Amounts:**
- Up to 75% of the incremental cost of eligible vehicles and equipment.

## Eligibility
- **Eligibility Requirements:**
- Must be a Maryland fleet company, organization, or community.
- Funding must be used for the purchase of qualified, newly manufactured medium-duty or heavy-duty zero-emission vehicles (including electric and hydrogen fuel cell electric vehicles).
- Funding must be used for qualified zero-emission heavy equipment for commercial or industrial use.

## Utility Information
- **Utility Company Name:** Maryland Energy Administration
- **Utility Company Size:** Government Entity (State Agency)

# Program Name: Solar Energy Equity Grant Program

#Program URL: https://energy.maryland.gov/residential/pages/incentives/lowincomesolargrants.aspx

## Program Details
- **Concrete Rebate Amounts:**
- Up to 100% of solar photovoltaic (PV) system design and installation cost, up to $25,000 per home.

## Eligibility
- **Eligibility Requirements:**
- Applicants must be non-profit organizations or local governments.
- Beneficiaries must be Marylanders experiencing low-to-moderate income, or residing in overburdened or underserved communities (as defined by §1-701 of the Environmental Article, Annotated Code of Maryland).
- Beneficiaries must live in single-family homes.
- Each home must have completed energy efficiency and weatherization-type upgrades through one or more of the following:
    - MEA's FY19 - FY25 Energy Efficiency Equity Grant Program (EEE Program).
    - Maryland Department of Housing and Community Development (DHCD) FY19 - FY25 Weatherization Assistance Program.
    - DHCD FY19 - FY25 EmPOWER Maryland Limited Income Energy Efficiency Program.

## Utility Information
- **Utility Company Name:** Maryland Energy Administration (MEA)
- **Utility Company Size:** Government Entity (State of Maryland)

# Program Name: Fiscal Year 2026 Commercial & Industrial Grant Program

#Program URL: https://energy.maryland.gov/business/Pages/incentives/empowermdcigp.aspx

## Program Details
- **Concrete Rebate Amounts:**
- Typical awards: $100,000 to $250,000 per project
- Maximum award: $500,000 per single award
- 100% cost reimbursement for facilities where 50% or more of clientele are from low-income populations

## Eligibility
- **Eligibility Requirements:**
- Commercial and Industrial building owners across Maryland
- Projects must improve energy performance beyond existing codes
- Proposals must result in 20% or more annual reduction of greenhouse gas emissions (unless qualifying for the low-income clientele 100% reimbursement)
- Application submitted by Friday, January 30, 2026, at 3:00 p.m.

## Utility Information
- **Utility Company Name:** Maryland Energy Administration
- **Utility Company Size:** Government Entity (State Agency)

# Program Name: Maryland Electric School Bus Grant Program

#Program URL: https://energy.maryland.gov/Pages/ElectricSchoolBus.aspx

## Program Details
- **Concrete Rebate Amounts:**
- Up to 85% of the incremental cost of purchasing qualified electric school buses
- Up to $1.5 million per grantee
- Up to $2 million per grantee for income-qualified applicants or large school districts

## Eligibility
- **Eligibility Requirements:**
- Local school districts
- Private vendors or contractors (via district application)
- Publicly funded school buses used for routine transportation of students for school or school-related programming/activities
- Vehicles must be purchased new (retrofits or repowered vehicles are not eligible)
- Vehicles must not have been funded in whole or in part by another Maryland state agency grant

- **Utility Company Name:** Maryland Energy Administration
- **Utility Company Size:** State Government Agency
