#Program URL: https://dallascityhall.com/departments/community-care/Pages/socialservice.aspx

## Program Details
- **Concrete Rebate Amounts:**
- Not specified (Based on availability of funds and caseworker determination)

## Eligibility
- **Eligibility Requirements:**
- Must reside within City of Dallas city limits
- Must have experienced a temporary financial crisis
- Must provide U.S. Government issued ID or secondary identification
- Must provide Social Security Cards for all household members
- Must provide lease agreement or proof of homeownership
- Must provide proof of household income for the past 30 days
- Must provide utility bill
- Must contact intake line (214-670-8416) to speak with a caseworker

# Program Name: Residential Irrigation Rebate Pilot Program

#Program URL: https://dallascityhall.com/departments/waterutilities/Pages/Residential-Irrigation-Rebate-Program.aspx

## Program Details
- **Concrete Rebate Amounts:**
- Up to $3,000

## Eligibility
- **Eligibility Requirements:**
- Must be a Dallas Water Utilities (DWU) customer.
- Qualifying residential properties.
- Must upgrade to more efficient irrigation systems.
- Eligible upgrades include:
    - Installing an EPA WaterSense-labeled irrigation controller.
    - Converting a pop-up spray zone to drip irrigation or bubblers.
    - Converting fixed spray nozzles to multi-stream rotary nozzles in turf zones.
    - Splitting an existing single irrigation zone into separate zones for beds and turf.
- Must schedule an Irrigation System Evaluation.
- Pilot phase is limited to a pre-selected group of 100 properties; subsequent phase will be first-come, first-served for up to 200 properties.

## Utility Information
- **Utility Company Name:** Dallas Water Utilities (DWU)
- **Utility Company Size:** Not specified
