# Program Name: Home Performance with ENERGY STAR®

#Program URL: https://homeenergysavings.pepco.com/md/residential/home-performance-with-energy-star-program/faqs

## Program Details
- **Concrete Rebate Amounts:**
- Up to $10,000 in general rebates (varies by breadth and scope of improvements)
- Up to $15,000 for electrification rebates (switching combustion fueled HVAC and/or Water Heating systems to electric)

## Eligibility
- **Eligibility Requirements:**
- Must be a current residential Pepco customer with a valid account number
- Must be the owner of the house where the assessment is performed
- For electrification rebates: Home must have an operational combustion (gas, propane, or oil) fueled appliance (HVAC and/or water heater)
- For electrification rebates: Must complete weatherization upgrades (at least attic insulation and air sealing) prior to eligibility

## Utility Information
- **Utility Company Name:** Pepco (Potomac Electric Power Company)
- **Utility Company Size:** Not specified

# Program Name: Appliance Rebate Program

#Program URL: https://homeenergysavings.pepco.com/md/residential/appliance-rebate-program

## Program Details
- **Concrete Rebate Amounts:**
- Up to $100 for smart thermostats
- $100 instant discount for ENERGY STAR® certified clothes washers
- $1,600 for ENERGY STAR certified heat pump water heaters

## Eligibility
- **Eligibility Requirements:**
- Residential customer
- Purchase of qualifying ENERGY STAR certified appliances (for washers and water heaters) or smart thermostats

## Utility Information
- **Utility Company Name:** Pepco (Potomac Electric Power Company)
- **Utility Company Size:** Not specified
