## Eligibility
- **Eligibility Requirements:**
- Must purchase or lease an eligible pre-owned electric vehicle.
- Application must be submitted within 180 days of the date of vehicle purchase or lease.
- Must have a PG&E account number and electric service agreement ID.
- Vehicle must be registered at the address corresponding to the PG&E electric service agreement.
- Must provide proof of vehicle registration, a valid California driver license, and a vehicle purchase or lease agreement (or Certificate of Title for private-party sales).
- PG&E gas-only residential customers who receive electric service from a municipality are ineligible.
- For "Rebate Plus" option: Must meet specific income-based requirements or be enrolled in an approved Public Assistance Program.
