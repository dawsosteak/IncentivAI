# ​​​​Documents & Resources  

On September 25, 2024, ForwardDallas was adopted by Dallas City Council and is listed at the top of this page.
See below for resources and drafts on ForwardDallas update. The resources, reports, presentations, and analyses, are meant for you! They provide background on the city's land use history, summarize community input to-date, and explore the possibilities for an equitable future.
See recent draft plans, videos on [What is ForwardDallas](https://youtu.be/xYDyHZ_kQBw)?", videos on "[How ForwardDallas will be used](https://youtu.be/NybraRPOO5I)" and much more! Have questions, view the [ ForwardDallas FAQs](https://dallascityhall.com/departments/pnv/Forward-Dallas/Pages/FAQs.aspx).

## Planning Scale  

Dallas spans over 380 square miles with over 1.3 million residents, 1.2 million jobs and a great deal of diversity among neighborhoods. For a large city like Dallas it is very important that citywide planning create a framework to facilitate effective planning at smaller scales as well. The ForwardDallas Land Use Update process will work towards enabling planning at four primary scales as illustrated below. Visit the interactive maps below to view outlines of the various planning scales.
