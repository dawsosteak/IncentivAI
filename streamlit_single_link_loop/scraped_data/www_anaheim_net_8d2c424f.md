#  Ivan Castillo 
## District 1
### Appointment Date: August 2, 2025
  

Mr. Castillo is a proud graduate of Western High School (Class of 2000). He went on to earn his Bachelor’s degree in Political Science from Loyola Marymount University in 2004. His professional journey includes 17 years with the Irvine Company, where he gained extensive experience in business operations and strategic development. He currently serves as a Business Advisor at Insperity, supporting small and mid-sized businesses across Southern California.
Deeply rooted in family and community, he has been married for 17 years and is the father of three boys. He actively coaches his middle son's football team with Los Alamitos Pop Warner, while his eldest son plays for Servite High School. His commitment to youth development and civic engagement reflects his dedication to the Anaheim community and its future.
