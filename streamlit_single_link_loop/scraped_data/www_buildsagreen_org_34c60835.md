# Bring Solar Home
**Bring Solar Home** is a free, unbiased, technical consumer decision tool for solar options here in San Antonio. This program, **brought to you by**[**Build San Antonio Green**](https://buildsagreen.org/) , is designed to help you discover if solar generation is right for you, and to connect you to solar options.
