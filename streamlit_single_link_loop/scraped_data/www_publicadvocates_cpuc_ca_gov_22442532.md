#  Careers
Start a challenging and rewarding career in advocating on behalf of California ratepayers 
#### How to Start a Career with the Public Advocates Office
**Step One:** [Take an Exam to Qualify](https://www.publicadvocates.cpuc.ca.gov/job-opportunities/public-advocates-office-jobs-open-exams-for-public-advocates-office-positions)
**Step Two:** Once you have passed an exam for your job classification (Top 3 out of 5 Rank), you will be notified for all relevant job openings. Follow the instructions exactly of how to apply for a specific job.
Our current openings are listed below. This list is regularly updated with new opportunities. If you have questions about the application process, please contact andrew.rubang@cpuc.ca.gov.
#### CURRENT PUBLIC ADVOCATES OFFICE JOB OPENINGS
