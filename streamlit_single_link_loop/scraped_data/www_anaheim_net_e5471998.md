#  Underground Storage Tank Cleanup Program 
Underground storage tank (UST) regulations require cleanup of contamination resulting from underground storage tank leaks.
All UST cleanup cases in Anaheim are now managed by the California Regional Water Quality Control Board, Santa Ana Region.
Records of UST cleanups may be accessed in several ways:
  1. The State of California maintains a database called [GeoTracker](https://www.waterboards.ca.gov/ust/electronic_submittal/), which has all known UST cleanup cases in California. The database is searchable by city and address.
  2. Underground storage tank cleanup records may also be accessed from Anaheim Public Utilities by contacting us via phone or email.
     * If records other than an NFA letter and/or case summary are required, a [records request form](https://www.anaheim.net/DocumentCenter/View/1849/Public-Utilities-Records-Request) is required. 
     * The [Regional Water Quality Control Board website](http://www.waterboards.ca.gov/santaana/water_issues/programs/ust/index.shtml) provides information on accessing information on UST cases they manage. 


Anaheim Fire and Rescue, available by calling 714-765-4040, regulates the underground storage of hazardous materials, including issuing permits and overseeing the removal of underground storage tanks.
