#  California Electric Rate Comparison
Your Options from Electric Power Providers 
![Lit up lightbulb inside a model of a house](https://www.cpuc.ca.gov/-/media/cpuc-website/divisions/news-and-outreach/images/istock-852468878/istock-1160036843.jpg?mw=1440&hash=E83888803B4004A696DC7A5146086FE5)
### Find and Compare Electric Rates in Your Area
To see what rates your utility and a community choice aggregator ([CCA](https://www.cpuc.ca.gov/consumer-support/consumer-programs-and-services/electrical-energy-and-energy-efficiency/community-choice-aggregation-and-direct-access-/cca-regulatory-information)s) may offer:
  1. enter your zip code _or_ county _or_ city; _AND_
  2. select the type of electric rate you are interested in: 
     * Residential (most commonly chosen), 
     * CARE ([consumer discount program](https://www.cpuc.ca.gov/industries-and-topics/electrical-energy/electric-costs/care-fera-program)), or
     * EV (for electric vehicle charging rates with a separate meter).

##  Consumer Programs 
![overhead view of Mom, Dad and two small children playing on the floor](https://www.cpuc.ca.gov/-/media/cpuc-website/divisions/news-and-outreach/images/190x130/190x130_0009_care_fera.jpg)[](https://www.cpuc.ca.gov/industries-and-topics/electrical-energy/electric-costs/care-fera-program)
[California Alternative Rates for Energy (CARE)/ Family Electric Rate Assistance (FERA) Programs ](https://www.cpuc.ca.gov/industries-and-topics/electrical-energy/electric-costs/care-fera-program)  
The CARE discount is a monthly discount of 20-35% for electric bills for income-qualified utility and CCA customers.  
The FERA discount is a monthly discount of 18% applied to electric bills for income-qualified utility and CCA customers.
  

![Young woman bringing oxygen breathing mask up to her face](https://www.cpuc.ca.gov/-/media/cpuc-website/divisions/news-and-outreach/images/190x130/190x130_0008_medical_baseline.jpg)[](https://www.cpuc.ca.gov/consumer-support/financial-assistance-savings-and-discounts/medical-baseline)
[Medical Baseline Rate Program](https://www.cpuc.ca.gov/consumer-support/financial-assistance-savings-and-discounts/medical-baseline)  
For utility and CCA customers, Medical Baseline ensures a set amount of electricity at the lowest monthly discounted rate for residential customers, who have qualifying energy needs due to medical conditions.
  

![Couple sitting on the couch, looking at utility bills and a laptop](https://www.cpuc.ca.gov/-/media/cpuc-website/divisions/news-and-outreach/images/190x130/190x130_0002_utility_emergency_assistance.jpg)[](https://www.cpuc.ca.gov/consumer-support/financial-assistance-savings-and-discounts/utility-company-emergency-assistance)
[Utility Company Emergency Assistance](https://www.cpuc.ca.gov/consumer-support/financial-assistance-savings-and-discounts/utility-company-emergency-assistance)  
Some electric utilities offer cash assistance and special payment plans to consumers facing financial hardship.
  

![Public Safety Power Shutoff logo](https://www.cpuc.ca.gov/-/media/cpuc-website/divisions/news-and-outreach/images/190x130/190x130_0000_power_down.jpg)[](https://prepareforpowerdown.com/)
[Prepare for Power Down](https://prepareforpowerdown.com/)  
Utility and CCA customers living in high fire threat areas can prepare for and/or navigate public safety power shutoffs using the programs and resources centralized here.
  

![overhead picture of woman standing next to an induction stovetop.](https://www.cpuc.ca.gov/-/media/cpuc-website/divisions/news-and-outreach/images/190x130/190x130_rebates.jpg)
**Customize Your Cost Savings**  
Shop top energy-efficient products, pick rebates, and customize programs for your home energy needs.   
**PG &E** area [Home Energy Guide](https://guide.pge.com/) (includes [Product Cost-Comparison](https://guide.pge.com/browse/thermostats), Rebates, and Programs) | **SCE** area [Home Energy Guide](https://www.sce.com/residential/home-energy-guide), [Product Cost-Comparison](https://marketplace.sce.com/), [Rebates](https://www.sce.com/residential/rebates-savings/rebates), and Programs) | **SDG &E** area [Rebates](https://www.sdge.com/rebates)
![Worker cutting insulation for the attic](https://www.cpuc.ca.gov/-/media/cpuc-website/divisions/news-and-outreach/images/190x130/190x130_0007_energy_savings_program.jpg)[](https://www.cpuc.ca.gov/consumer-support/financial-assistance-savings-and-discounts/energy-savings-assistance)
[Energy Savings Assistance Program (ESA)](https://www.cpuc.ca.gov/consumer-support/financial-assistance-savings-and-discounts/energy-savings-assistance)  
ESA provides no-cost weatherization services for renters and homeowners, who income-qualify for CARE, for utility and CCA customers alike. The services offered include energy-efficient refrigerators, energy-efficient furnaces, attic insulation, and low-flow showerheads among others.
![lightbulb lying on a bed of green clover](https://www.cpuc.ca.gov/-/media/cpuc-website/divisions/news-and-outreach/images/190x130/190x130_energy_efficiency.jpg)  

[Energy Efficiency Programs](https://climateaction.ca.gov/home-energy/)  
All customers may check their local utility’s website for residential or commercial energy efficiency programs to reduce costs from audit to implementation. [Pacific Gas & Electric](https://www.pge.com/en_US/residential/save-energy-money/help-paying-your-bill/energy-reduction-and-weatherization/energy-savings-assistance-program/energy-savings-assistance-program.page) | [Southern California Edison](https://www.sce.com/residential/assistance/energy-saving-program?from=/esap) | [San Diego Gas & Electric](https://www.sdge.com/residential/pay-bill/get-payment-bill-assistance/assistance-programs/no-cost-energy-efficient-home-improvements) | [PacifiCorp](https://www.pacificpower.net/my-account/payments/bill-payment-assistance/california-liheap-weatherization.html) | [Liberty Utilities](https://california.libertyutilities.com/topaz-lake/residential/smart-energy-use/electric/energy-savings-assistance-program.html) | [Bear Valley Electric](https://www.bvesinc.com/) | [Southern California Gas](https://www.socalgas.com/save-money-and-energy/assistance-programs/energy-savings-assistance-program) | [Southwest Gas](https://www.swgas.com/en/california-energy-savings-assistance-program) | [Alpine Natural Gas](https://alpinenaturalgas.com/programs/) | [West Coast Gas (CARE only)](https://www.westcoastgas.com/programs/c-a-r-e/)
  

![Gas meter outside of mobile home](https://www.cpuc.ca.gov/-/media/cpuc-website/divisions/news-and-outreach/images/190x130/190x130_0001_mobile-_home_meter.jpg)[](https://www.cpuc.ca.gov/regulatory-services/safety/mhp/mobilehome-park-utility-upgrade-program)
[Mobilehome Park Utility Conversion Program ](https://www.cpuc.ca.gov/regulatory-services/safety/mhp/mobilehome-park-utility-upgrade-program)  
A statewide effort to upgrade existing gas and electrical systems at mobilehome parks. Program benefits include installing individual meters and offering cost savings and energy efficiency programs to decrease upgrade costs.
  

![Young boy on stepstool washing hands in kitchen sink](https://www.cpuc.ca.gov/-/media/cpuc-website/divisions/news-and-outreach/images/190x130/190x130_0006_water_company_assistance.jpg)[](https://www.cpuc.ca.gov/consumer-support/financial-assistance-savings-and-discounts/water-company-assistance)
[Water Company Assistance](https://www.cpuc.ca.gov/consumer-support/financial-assistance-savings-and-discounts/water-company-assistance)  
Depending on your household income, you may qualify for discounted water services.
  

![Woman and teenage daughter on the couch, looking at a mobile phone](https://www.cpuc.ca.gov/-/media/cpuc-website/divisions/news-and-outreach/images/190x130/190x130_0005_lifeline.jpg)[](https://www.cpuc.ca.gov/consumer-support/financial-assistance-savings-and-discounts/lifeline)
[California LifeLine](https://www.cpuc.ca.gov/consumer-support/financial-assistance-savings-and-discounts/lifeline)  
Provides discounted home phone and cell phone services to qualified households.
  

![children in a school library gathered around a laptop on a table](https://www.cpuc.ca.gov/-/media/cpuc-website/divisions/news-and-outreach/images/190x130/190x130_0004_teleconnect.jpg)[](https://www.cpuc.ca.gov/consumer-support/financial-assistance-savings-and-discounts/california-teleconnect-fund)
[California Teleconnect Fund (CTF)](https://www.cpuc.ca.gov/consumer-support/financial-assistance-savings-and-discounts/california-teleconnect-fund)  
50% discount to qualifying K–12 schools, libraries, community colleges, government-owned hospitals and health clinics, community-based organizations, and community-based healthcare organizations.
  

![Hand pushing button on large button phone](https://www.cpuc.ca.gov/-/media/cpuc-website/divisions/news-and-outreach/images/190x130/190x130_0003_ddtp.jpg)[](https://www.cpuc.ca.gov/consumer-support/financial-assistance-savings-and-discounts/ddtp)
[Deaf and Disabled Telecommunications Program (DDTP)](https://www.cpuc.ca.gov/consumer-support/financial-assistance-savings-and-discounts/ddtp)  
Provides assistive telecommunications equipment, speech-generating devices, and relay services to consumers with hearing, vision, speech, cognitive, and mobility disabilities.
