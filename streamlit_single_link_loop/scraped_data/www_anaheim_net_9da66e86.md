#  Ponderosa Park & Family Resource Center 
  1. ![Miraloma Logo for Slideshow](https://www.anaheim.net/ImageRepository/Document?documentID=64337)
  2. ![Happy Family](https://www.anaheim.net/ImageRepository/Document?documentID=64658)
  3. ![Girl and boy smiling in a picture frame](https://www.anaheim.net/1136/Ponderosa-Park-Family-Resource-Center)
  4. ![Group of women smiling after excersize](https://www.anaheim.net/ImageRepository/Document?documentID=64657)


  

The Ponderosa Park and Family Resource Center is one of three family-friendly centers operated by the City of Anaheim. These centers bring together residents, public agencies, and private organizations to deliver services that enhance the Anaheim families' safety, stability, and healthy development. 
On-site staff provide needs assessments, coordinate human services and programs, and ensure comprehensive support for families. Bilingual (English/Spanish) staff are available at all three centers.
### **Case Management Services**
Our case management service can help you sustain your household by providing individualized case assessment, planning, and care to promote quality living,
  * Community Resource Information & Referrals
  * Transportation Resources
  * Diaper Distributions
  * Food Distributions
  * Utility Application Assistance
  * Form Translation & Completion Assistance
  * Enrollment Assistance
    * Medi-Cal, CalFresh, Covered California
