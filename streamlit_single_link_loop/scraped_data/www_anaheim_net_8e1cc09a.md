#  Volunteer at the Library 
## Teen Volunteers
  * Volunteers at Anaheim Public Library must be at least 14 years old and in high school.
  * An accepted application does not guarantee volunteer hours. Hours are assigned on an as-needed basis.
  * Anaheim Public Library does not offer opportunities for court-appointed volunteering. Teens requiring court-appointed community service hours can contact the [OneOC Court Referral Program](https://oneoc.org/crp/).
  * Adults interested in volunteering can contact the [Library Foundation](http://anaheimlibraryfoundation.com/) or the [Friends of the Library](https://anaheim.net/1291/Friends-of-the-Library).
