#  Survey 
The Survey section performs survey monument preservation, provides topographic surveys and construction staking for the City's Capital Improvement projects. We provide surveying services for street construction, storm drains, sewers, well sites, electrical work and water improvements within the City's right-of way and for City owned park facilities. 
  

## Survey Request Form
  

Please fill out the [survey request form](https://www.anaheim.net/DocumentCenter/View/40785/SURVEY-REQUEST-CONTRACTOR) and return to City Surveyor Rick Hill at RWHill@anaheim.net [](https://www.anaheim.net/351/bcrowley@anaheim.net)a minimum of two working days prior to when staking is to begin. Survey requests submitted as a JPEG will be rejected.
  

## Bench Mark System
  

The Survey section also maintains a city-wide bench mark system. Access the latest [bench mark information](https://gis.anaheim.net/portal/apps/webappviewer/index.html?id=e1e1ec14535146f692dfe832391c92ec) for more information. Information on center-line ties and corner records can be found at the public counter on the 2nd floor of City Hall East.
##    
Private Surveyor
  

The City's Survey section does not perform surveys for private citizens or groups. If you are looking for a private surveyor please visit the [California Land Surveyors Association](http://www.californiasurveyors.org/).
