#  Utility Audits Risk and Compliance Division
The Utility Audits, Risk and Compliance Division (UARCD) carries out its responsibilities on behalf of the CPUC by identifying and advising executive management and the Commission members on potential organizational risks and ensuring utilities implement various policies and programs in accordance with applicable laws, rules, regulations, and CPUC directives. 
UARCD encompasses two branches: **Utility Audits Branch** and **Risk and Compliance Branch**. [Click here for UARCD's Org Chart](https://www.cpuc.ca.gov/about-cpuc/divisions/utility-audits-risk-and-compliance-division/uarcd-organizational-chart "UARCD Org Chart").
## Utility Audits Branch
The [Utility Audits Branch](https://www.cpuc.ca.gov/about-cpuc/divisions/utility-audits-risk-and-compliance-division/utility-audits-branch "Utility Audits Branch") performs audits on regulated utilities and monitors compliance with laws and CPUC directives. 
## Risk and Compliance Branch
The [Risk and Compliance Branch](https://www.cpuc.ca.gov/about-cpuc/divisions/utility-audits-risk-and-compliance-division/risk-and-compliance-branch "Risk and Compliance Branch") manages the enterprise risk system by identifying, measuring, reporting, and monitoring risk that affects CPUC’s objectives.
