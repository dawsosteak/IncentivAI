# ​​Spring Budget Town Hall Meetings  

#### WE WANT YOUR INPUT ON THE CITY’S BUDGET!
**  
**
Join us at a town hall meeting to weigh in on how the City's tax dollars are spent for Fiscal Year 2026-27​​.  

Residents can voice their opinion virtually or in person.  

Our series of Budget Town Hall meetings will be hosted by members of the Dallas City Council. 
If you don't know your City Council district or Council Member, please [click here](https://dallascityhall.com/government/Pages/city-council.aspx) for more information.
