#  Solar for Schools 
The Solar for Schools Program installs solar on local public schools throughout Anaheim. Nine schools participated in the program as shown below. Construction was completed in December 2018.   
  
Solar energy produced from APU-owned systems is available to income-qualified residential customers for financial assistance on their bills, [learn more](http://www.anaheim.net/5083/Income-Qualified-Solar-Power-Discount-Pr).
