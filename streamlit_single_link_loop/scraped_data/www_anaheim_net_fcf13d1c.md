#  Concerts in the Park 
We are excited to announce our 2026 Concerts in the Park dates!
Shows will be held on the following days, times and locations:
  * Thursday, June 11, 2026 at Modjeska Park from 6:30pm-8pm
  * Thursday, June 18, 2026 at Ponderosa Park from 6:30pm-8pm
  * Thursday, June 25, 2026 at Pioneer Park from 6:30pm-8pm
  * Thursday, July 9, 2026 at Ronald Reagan Park from 6:30pm-8pm
  * Thursday, July 16, 2026 at Ronald Reagan Park from 6:30pm-8pm
  * Thursday, July 23, 2026 at Ronald Reagan Park from 6:30pm-8pm
  * Thursday, July 30, 2026 at Ronald Reagan Park from 6:30pm-8pm


Mark your calendars! We will release our performance line up soon!
