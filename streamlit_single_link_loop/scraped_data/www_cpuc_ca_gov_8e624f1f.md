#  Affordability Rulemaking 
Phase 3 – Strategies to mitigate future energy rate increases 
**2022 Affordability En Banc on Energy Costs and Rates  
February 28, 2022 to March 1, 2022**
This two-day En Banc examines in more depth affordability proposals addressing ratemaking reforms, equity, environmental concerns, and non-ratepayer funding strategies. This En Banc follows-up on the [rates en banc](https://www.adminmonitor.com/ca/cpuc/en_banc/20210224/) held in [February 2021](https://www.cpuc.ca.gov/industries-and-topics/electrical-energy/en-banc-rates-and-costs), which discussed proposals in an [Energy Division whitepaper](https://www.cpuc.ca.gov/-/media/cpuc-website/divisions/energy-division/documents/en-banc/senate-bill-695-report-2021_en-banc-white-paper.pdf) evaluating utility costs and rate forecasts.
[Media Advisory](https://www.cpuc.ca.gov/news-and-updates/all-news/cpuc-to-hold-hearing-on-affordability-of-essential-utility-services-2022)
[Agenda](https://www.cpuc.ca.gov/-/media/cpuc-website/industries-and-topics/documents/energy/electric-energy/affordability_enbanc_agenda.pdf)
