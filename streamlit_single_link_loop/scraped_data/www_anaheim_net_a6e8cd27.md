Join us for a family-friendly cultural event featuring Native food, live performances, and free books!
Vendors will not accept all forms of payment. Please bring various forms to avoid possible issues.
FREE DROP-IN EVENT! There is no cap on tickets/attendance.
###   

### FREE Parking options for the Native Art Festival:
  

-Founders’ Park lot (Handicapped parking): will be minimal, and NO spaces are guaranteed 
-Anaheim High School lot on West Street (photo below)
-Street parking: West and Sycamore streets & neighborhoods
-FRAN - opens at 12 p.m. (noon): connecting stops available at Central Library and Center Street Parking Structure. Download the app [here.](https://rideart.org/fran/)
