  1. [Community Engagement](https://pd.anaheim.net/177/Community-Engagement)
Find out what services the Police Department has that help engage community members.
  2. [Crime Prevention](https://pd.anaheim.net/176/Crime-Prevention)
The Anaheim Police Department has several services available to help residents and businesses with crime prevention.
  3. [Jail](https://pd.anaheim.net/175/Jail)
Learn about the services that the jail provides.
  4. [Open Data - Police Dashboard](https://main-anaheim.opendata.arcgis.com/datasets?t=Public%20Safety)
The crime data posted does not include all reports. It includes arson, assault, burglary, disturbing the peace, drugs / alcohol, DUI, fraud, homicide, robbery, sex crimes, stolen vehicle, theft, vandalism, vehicle break-in, and weapons.
  5. [Property & Evidence](https://pd.anaheim.net/174/Property-Evidence)
Property and Evidence is responsible for receiving, storing, and maintaining items obtained as evidence during the course of an investigation, as well as storing found property and property turned in for safekeeping.
  6. [Records](https://pd.anaheim.net/173/Records)
Learn about the services involving various records that are offered by the Police Department.
  7. [Retired Senior Volunteer Patrol](https://pd.anaheim.net/171/Retired-Senior-Volunteer-Patrol)
Learn about the services that are part of the Retired Senior Volunteer Patrol.
  8. [Tips](https://pd.anaheim.net/170/Tips)
Discover the ways you can share tips with the Anaheim Police Department.
