#  OAKS Day Camp 
  1. ![Slideshow Staff](https://www.anaheim.net/ImageRepository/Document?documentID=69651)
  2. ![Volunteen](https://www.anaheim.net/ImageRepository/Document?documentID=69688)


**ATTENTION ALL 5-12 YEAR OLDS****!**
Calling all young explorers and nature lovers for a summer of outdoor fun. Campers will be able to experience themed art, nature, Native American traditions, games, and more! Participants must bring their own snacks and lunch.
**Spring 2026:**
[**Medieval Adventure**](https://secure.rec1.com/CA/city-of-anaheim/catalog/index/5319d52dd9b5999941925a93e21df0f3?filter=c2VhcmNoPTU5MDE2)**from March 30 - April 3:** Step into a world of knights, castles, and enchanted forests during our Medieval Spring Day Camp, where adventure awaits around every corner. Campers will enjoy hands-on crafts, lively medieval-themed games, and guided hikes through the blooming spring trails.
**Summer 2026:**
[**Shark Week**](https://secure.rec1.com/CA/city-of-anaheim/catalog/index/5319d52dd9b5999941925a93e21df0f3?filter=c2VhcmNoPTU5Mzkx)**from June 15 - June 19:** Dive into the fantastical underwater world as we explore our oceans. We will learn jaw-some facts about sharks, create themed art, and enjoy some water-themed activities to help keep us cool!
[**Wild Western**](https://secure.rec1.com/CA/city-of-anaheim/catalog/index/5319d52dd9b5999941925a93e21df0f3?filter=c2VhcmNoPTU5Mzky)**from June 22 - June 26:** Saddle up and head on over to the town of Old Oak. As you hang around our watering hole, prepare for a wild silver rush as you learn about Oak Canyon’s own silver mine, hike the wild terrain, and play some root-and-tootin’ games!
[**Super Sleuth**](https://secure.rec1.com/CA/city-of-anaheim/catalog/index/5319d52dd9b5999941925a93e21df0f3?filter=c2VhcmNoPTU5Mzkz)**from June 29 - July 2:** Join our team of high level detectives as we solve mysteries and search for clues. Receive daily top-secret briefings, find fingerprints, and help us solve a canyon-wide mystery!
[**Silly Science**](https://secure.rec1.com/CA/city-of-anaheim/catalog/index/5319d52dd9b5999941925a93e21df0f3?filter=c2VhcmNoPTU5Mzk0)**from July 6 - July 10:** We’re crazy for chemistry! Get ready for a week packed with sensational science experiments that will bubble, grow, fizz, and pop. Grab your lab coats and goggles as we dive into hands-on discoveries, exciting reactions, and plenty of fun.
[**Pirate Adventure**](https://secure.rec1.com/CA/city-of-anaheim/catalog/index/5319d52dd9b5999941925a93e21df0f3?filter=c2VhcmNoPTU5Mzk1)**from July 13 - July 17:** Ahoy, there, mateys! Join our swashbuckling team of buccaneers as we sail the seven seas following our treasure maps to the buried treasure!
[**Diggin' Up Dinosaurs**](https://secure.rec1.com/CA/city-of-anaheim/catalog/index/5319d52dd9b5999941925a93e21df0f3?filter=c2VhcmNoPTU5Mzk2)**from July 20 - July 24** : Hop in our time machine as we journey back to the Jurassic Era. We will have a blast digging up dinosaurs, discovering ancient fossils, erupting volcanoes, and exploring the prehistoric history of our trails during this adventure-filled week!
[**Star Wars**](https://secure.rec1.com/CA/city-of-anaheim/catalog/index/5319d52dd9b5999941925a93e21df0f3?filter=c2VhcmNoPTU5Mzk3)**from July 27 - 31:** Hold on to your lightsaber and - three, two, one – BLAST OFF! Prepare to help the Rebel Alliance defend against the Galactic Empire as we launch into a fun-filled week of all things Star Wars!
[**Harry Potter**](https://secure.rec1.com/CA/city-of-anaheim/catalog/index/5319d52dd9b5999941925a93e21df0f3?filter=c2VhcmNoPTU5Mzk4)**from August 3 - August 7:** Congratulations! You have been accepted to the 2026 session at Hogwarts School of Witchcraft and Wizardry. Grab your wands and join us as we create “potions,” study real-life magical creatures, and fend off the forces of destruction.
[**Super Hero**](https://secure.rec1.com/CA/city-of-anaheim/catalog/index/5319d52dd9b5999941925a93e21df0f3?filter=c2VhcmNoPTU5Mzk5)**from August 10 - August 14:** POW! BOOM! BAM! Discover your hidden superpowers, test your skills, and join the elite crime-fighting superhero league!

# Wilderness Survival Camp
## Calling adventurous 12-15 year olds!
Learn the fundamentals of surviving in the wilderness in this adventurous camp for teens ages 12-15. Teams will work together to build shelters, hone their archery skills, and learn basic first aid in these weeks of survival fun! 
**Summer 2026:**
[**Mountain week**](https://secure.rec1.com/CA/city-of-anaheim/catalog/index/5eca08b973d835eafd681a22a58e81a6?filter=c2VhcmNoPTU5NDAw)**from June 22 - June 26:** Adventure awaits in Tri Canyon! This week, participants will venture into "mountain" terrain and develop key survival skills through interactive challenges and guided exploration. Get ready for discovery, teamwork, and an unforgettable wilderness experience!
[**Wetlands week**](https://secure.rec1.com/CA/city-of-anaheim/catalog/index/5eca08b973d835eafd681a22a58e81a6?filter=c2VhcmNoPTU5NDAy)**from July 6 - July 10:** Venture into the rugged landscapes of Tri Canyon this week as participants explore the unique "desert" environment. Through hands-on activities and guided discovery, they’ll learn how plants, animals, and people adapt to extreme conditions while building essential survival skills, environmental awareness, and outdoor safety knowledge.
[**Desert week**](https://secure.rec1.com/CA/city-of-anaheim/catalog/index/5eca08b973d835eafd681a22a58e81a6?filter=c2VhcmNoPTU5NDAz)**from July 20 - July 24:** Join us this week as we explore the wild landscapes of Tri Canyon! Participants will venture into the "wetlands" to discover how plants, wildlife, and people adapt to the unique environment. Through hands-on activities, we’ll learn essential survival skills, environmental awareness, and outdoor safety in an exciting natural setting.
