#  Save Gas this Winter
On this page you will find information on energy conservation and thermostat rebates to help Californians respond to operational limitations in the natural gas pipeline and storage systems in Southern California as we approach the next winter.
  * **[Thermostat Rebates](https://www.socalgas.com/save-money-and-energy/rebates-and-incentives):** SoCalGas offers a $50 rebate for customers who purchase eligible thermostats.
  * **[Five Tips to Lower Your Natural Gas Bill](https://www.socalgas.com/save-money-and-energy/energy-saving-tips-tools/winter/winter-bills)**
  * **[$125 Joint SoCalGas and Southern California Edison Rebate](https://pages.email.sce.com/SCESmartBonus/ "$125 Joint SoCalGas and Southern California Edison Rebate"):** Residential customers who buy or already have an eligible thermostat can earn $125 from the two utilities, plus the potential to earn $60 in bill credits each year, for signing up for the Save Power Days program.
  * **[Seasonal Savings Program](https://nest.com/support/article/What-is-Seasonal-Savings):** SoCalGas is offering a residential program this winter to help customers conserve natural gas and save on their heating bills. Customers opt into the program through their Nest thermostat, which then subtly fine tunes their heating use. Last year customers on the program saved 8 percent on their heating bill.
  * **[Energy Upgrade California](https://www.energyupgradeca.org/):** This is a statewide initiative committed to helping Californians take action to save energy, conserve and preserve natural resources, reduce demand on the energy grid, and make informed choices about their energy use at home and at work.
  * **[More Information on Conserving Energy in Southern California](http://conserveenergysocal.com/)**
  * Nov. 28, 2017: **[Agencies Release Energy Assessment for Southern California, Express Concern About Existing Pipeline Outages](http://docs.cpuc.ca.gov/PublishedDocs/Published/G000/M199/K876/199876868.PDF "Agencies Release Energy Assessment for Southern California, Express Concern About Existing Pipeline Outages")**
    * **[Aliso Canyon Winter Assessment](http://docketpublic.energy.ca.gov/PublicDocuments/17-IEPR-11/TN221863_20171128T103411_Aliso_Canyon_Winter_Risk_Assesment_Technical_Report_201718_Supp.pdf) **
    * **[Presentation on Winter Assessment](http://docketpublic.energy.ca.gov/PublicDocuments/17-IEPR-11/TN221862_20171128T103410_Aliso_Canyon_Update_Winter_201718.pdf)**
  * **[Winter 2023 Natural Gas Prices](https://www.cpuc.ca.gov/industries-and-topics/natural-gas/winter-2023-natural-gas-prices)**
