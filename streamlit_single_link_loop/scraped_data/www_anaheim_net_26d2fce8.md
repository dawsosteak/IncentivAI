**[Access World News](https://anaheimlibrary.idm.oclc.org/login?url=https://infoweb.newsbank.com/apps/news/easy-search?p=AWNB) **
Access World News features Orange County Register articles from 1987 to present, as well as international, national, regional, and local news, supporting a diverse range of research needs across many subject areas. Includes access to more than 13,000 sources from over 200 countries and territories.
[**ASLdeafined**](https://anaheimlibrary.idm.oclc.org/login?url=https://www.asldeafined.com/login/library/?org=anaheimpl)
ASLdeafined is a leading provider of online American Sign Language learning resources, with a comprehensive platform of over 300 themed video lessons, an 18,000+ video dictionary, a free mobile app, and taught by a diverse group of Deaf and Hard of Hearing (DHH) mentors, advocates, and professionals from all across the United States.
[**AtoZDatabases**](https://anaheimlibrary.idm.oclc.org/login?url=https://www.atozdatabases.com)
Search businesses, people, mailing lists and sales leads, jobs, and more. Includes 30 million business and executive profiles; 240 million residents with interests, hobbies and lifestyles information; real-time job listings; new businesses; healthcare professionals; new movers; new homeowners. Ideal for sales leads, mailing lists, market research, employment opportunities, finding friends and relatives. Email, print or download 1,000 records per search with no overall limit.
[**Audiobooks & eBooks**](https://ebook.yourcloudlibrary.com/library/AnaheimPublicLibrary/Featured?media=all&src=lib)
Browse the cloudLibrary collection and download audiobooks and eBooks for free to your computer or device.
[**Biblioboard**](https://library.biblioboard.com/welcome)
This collection uses geolocation to provide access to anyone physically located in the state of California. The library includes several thousand simultaneous use ebooks from leading publishers as well as the Indie Author Project Select collection, which features over 400 award-winning indie-published ebooks, some of which are right here in California!
[**Brainfuse Help Now**](https://anaheimlibrary.idm.oclc.org/login?url=https://landing.brainfuse.com/index.asp?u=main.anaheim.ca.brainfuse.com)
Connect 24/7 with tutors in English and Spanish with help in Language Arts and Mathematics. For all major K-12 subjects, live tutors in English, Spanish, Mandarin, Cantonese, Vietnamese, and Tagalog are available from 10 a.m. - 10 p.m.
**[California Missions eBook Collections](https://search.follettsoftware.com/metasearch/ui/131005)**
A comprehensive guide of the California Missions.
**CloudLibrary: NewsStand**
Browse thousands of magazines from around the world through CloudLibrary: NewsStand. Access magazines simultaneously through the CloudLibrary app on your [Apple](https://apps.apple.com/us/app/cloudlibrary-by-bibliotheca/id466446054) or [Android](https://play.google.com/store/apps/details?id=com.txtr.android.mmm&hl=en_US&gl=US&pli=1) device or on your desktop [here.](https://cloudlibrary.magzter.com/login/user)
[**CultureGrams**](https://anaheimlibrary.idm.oclc.org/login?url=https://online.culturegrams.com)
CultureGrams provides concise cultural information on countries around the world. Country reports feature basic information as well as perspectives on daily life and culture, including background, customs, and lifestyles of the world’s people.
**[Digitallearn.org](https://www.digitallearn.org/)**
If you are new to computers, haven't used them for a while, are a little unsure and uncomfortable, or just need a bit of a refresher, we have the tools to help you tackle technology at your own pace and gain the confidence you need to succeed.
[**eLibrary**](https://anaheimlibrary.idm.oclc.org/login?url=https://explore.proquest.com/elibrary/?accountid=40026)
eLibrary consists of a general reference collection of periodical and digital media content with editorial guidance for novice researchers, helping them choose research topics and find authoritative information to support research claims.
[**Encyclopedia Britannica Escolar**](https://escolar.eb.com/)
Explore the world in Spanish authentically, confidently, and curiously. Britannica Escolar delivers native Spanish content written by Latin American experts, combining trusted knowledge, cultural relevance, and a modern design to empower bilingual, multilingual, and Spanish-first learners across every grade level.
[**Encyclopedia Britannica Escolar - Primaria level**](https://escolar.eb.com/levels/primaria)
Designed for young learners, Escolar Primaria introduces the joy of learning through simple language, colorful visuals, and engaging articles that build vocabulary and foundational knowledge. Features include Biografía destacada and ¿Sabías que…? videos to spark curiosity.
[**Encyclopedia Britannica Escolar - Secundaria level**](https://escolar.eb.com/levels/secundaria)
For middle-level learners, Escolar Secundaria deepens understanding with multimedia-rich content across subjects. Explore Latinoamérica en foco, a vibrant window into Latin American culture, history, and identity.
**[Encyclopedia Britannica School Edition](https://school.eb.com/)**
Empower every learner and educator with Britannica School, the award-winning online learning platform trusted by millions worldwide. Designed for every grade level, it combines credible content, engaging media, and intuitive tools that make learning accessible, inclusive, and unforgettable.
  1. [G - L](https://www.anaheim.net/3260/List-of-Online-Resources#tab68d63ef6-21be-4150-b17b-0b1a1ca08480_1)


[**Gale Courses**](https://education.gale.com/l-anaheim_main/)
Library cardholders can select from hundreds of free instructor-led courses covering technology, personal finance, career development, including test prep and certification.
[**Gale in Context: Biography**](https://anaheimlibrary.idm.oclc.org/login?url=https://infotrac.gale.com/itweb/anaheim_main?db=BIC%26id%3Danaheim_main)
Gale in Context: Biography contains biographical information on authors, musicians, politicians, world leaders, historical figures, and more. All articles retrieved are full-text and can be printed or emailed.
[**Gale in Context: Elementary**](https://anaheimlibrary.idm.oclc.org/login?url=https://infotrac.gale.com/itweb/anaheim_main?db=ITKE%26id%3Danaheim_main)
Gale in Context: Elementary
**[Gale OneFile: Entrepreneurship](https://anaheimlibrary.idm.oclc.org/login?url=https://infotrac.gale.com/itweb/anaheim_main?db=PPSB%26id%3Danaheim_main)**
Gale OneFile: Entrepreneurship provides insights, tips, strategies, and success stories for business schools and entrepreneurs.
**[Gale in Context: Environmental Studies](https://anaheimlibrary.idm.oclc.org/login?url=https://galesupport.com/cageo?loc=anaheim_main&db=GRNR)**
Gale in Context: Environmental Studies helps students understand environmental issues that affect people globally through topic overviews, journals, news, and multimedia content.
**[Gale OneFile: Home Improvement](https://anaheimlibrary.idm.oclc.org/login?url=https://infotrac.gale.com/itweb/anaheim_main?db=PPHI%26id%3Danaheim_main)**
Gale OneFile: Home Improvement places the research of hobbyists and professionals on a solid foundation with articles from more than 200 home improvement-focused titles. Home Improvement covers topics such as architectural techniques, tool and material selection, zoning requirements, and more.
**[Gale Interactive: Science](https://galesupport.com/cageo?loc=anaheim_main&db=ISSCI)**
Using Gale Interactive: Science, students can manipulate 3D interactive models to visualize and understand concepts in biology, chemistry, earth, and space science.
[**Gale OneFile: U.S. History**](https://anaheimlibrary.idm.oclc.org/login?url=https://infotrac.gale.com/itweb/anaheim_main?db=PPUS%26id%3Danaheim_main)
Gale OneFile: U.S. History contains scholarly journals and magazines that can be used by novice historians as well as advanced academic researchers. Updated daily, U.S. History offers balanced coverage of events in U.S. history and scholarly work being established in the field.
[**Gale OneFile: World History**](https://anaheimlibrary.idm.oclc.org/login?url=https://infotrac.gale.com/itweb/anaheim_main?db=PPWH%26id%3Danaheim_main)
Gale OneFile: World History provides
**[Gale Presents: Peterson's Test and Career Prep](https://anaheimlibrary.idm.oclc.org/login?url=https://link.gale.com/apps/PTCP?u=anaheim_main)**
(Click on "Log In or Sign Up" to create a free account that you will use to access this online resource.)
Prepare for standardized tests with Gale Presents: Peterson’s Test Prep. This valuable resource includes full-length practice tests for GED, SAT, ACT, AP, PSAT, GRE, LSAT, MCAT, TOEFL, U.S. citizenship, and more. It offers information on undergraduate and graduate programs and tuition and scholarship assistance, as well as a resume builder and interviewing advice. Whether you’re a student planning for college or a professional embarking on a new career path, Gale Presents: Peterson’s Test Prep can help you along the way.
[**Historic Anaheim Photos**](http://anaheim.net/1294/Anaheim-Photo-Collections)
A digital archive of nearly 2,000 historic photographs dating back to 1860. These unique local history resources have been organized for easy browsing by Anaheim residents, scholars, and history buffs throughout California and the world.
[**Kelley Blue Book**](https://www.kbb.com/)
For the past 91 years, Kelley Blue Book has published printed guide books, providing many different values for the automotive and motorcycle industries. Sign up for free to access the digital publication.

**[Mitchell1 ProDemand Automotive Repair](https://www.prodemand.com/)**
**Access only at an APL (Anaheim Public Library) location.** (When viewing website from an APL Branch, click on "Login" for in-library access.)
**[National Geographic Kids](https://anaheimlibrary.idm.oclc.org/login?url=https://galesupport.com/cageo?loc=anaheim_main&db=NGMK)**
Visit National Geographic Kids to explore amazing adventures in science, nature, culture, archaeology, and space! Designed for children ages 6-14.
[**The New York Times**](https://nytimes.com/subscription/redeem?campaignId=89YWX&gift_code=465c46c81425d03e)
Enjoy complimentary access to the world’s finest journalism on nytimes.com. To get started follow these simple steps: 1. Click on the "Redeem" button; 2. Enter your email address and choose "Continue"; 3. Create a password; 4. Choose "Create Account" to enjoy three days' unlimited access. After the three days are up, you can repeat the process to enjoy continued nytimes.com access.
**[O.C. Register](https://anaheimlibrary.idm.oclc.org/login?url=https://infoweb.newsbank.com/apps/news/browse-multi?p=AWNB&t=favorite%3AOCRBBOCR%21Orange%20County%20Register%20Collection)**
Includes the O.C. Register database for articles from 1987 to present, for local and regional news stories.
[**PebbleGo Science**](https://shell.pebblego.com/modules/?sqs=SQSih8+D6ovlMr6zbFbQoLvtQ==)
PebbleGo Science, PebbleGo Ciencia, and Capstone Interactive eBooks are specifically designed for K-2 students. These resources help students enhance their science, technology, engineering and research skills. Find informational articles, ready-made activities, and early literacy support for students of all abilities, to boost engagement and foster independent learning.
**[Performing Arts Collections](https://anaheimlibrary.idm.oclc.org/login?url=https://video.alexanderstreet.com/collections/)**
Access a truly remarkable and diverse collection of music, dance and theatre content, covering hundreds of genres and a wide range of content types, from instruction to performance. Leading content providers include National Theatre, Royal Shakespeare Company, Qwest TV, Smithsonian L.A. Theatre Works, Broadway On-Demand, and many more.
[**Pronunciator**](https://learning.pronunciator.com/getstarted.php?library_id=20168)
Learn over 160 different languages, as well as study English (ESL). This full-spectrum language learning program features speech recognition, scored quizzes, a virtual coach, downloadable MP3s, and virtual conversations, as well as a free app for your tablets, smartphones and listening devices. Choose from a variety of learning levels, even for children as young as 3.
**[ProCitizen](https://learning.pronunciator.com/getstarted-procitizen.php?library_id=20168)**
With over 100 instructional videos, plus interactive drills and quizzes, ProCitizen provides the civics, vocabulary, plus reading and writing practice that new citizens need to succeed in the United States naturalization test. _[ProCitizen en español](https://learning.pronunciator.com/getstarted-procitizen-ES.php?library_id=20168)._
[**ProQuest Central Student**](https://anaheimlibrary.idm.oclc.org/login?url=https://search.proquest.com/?accountid=40026)
This database of millions of articles from more than 10,000 full-text scholarly journals provides information in science, technology, education, social sciences, humanities, and news.
[**ProQuest Ebook Central**](https://anaheimlibrary.idm.oclc.org/login?url=https://ebookcentral.proquest.com/lib/anaheimpublib)
Designed specifically for research, with input from students. All information in Ebook Central has been vetted with librarians for content you can trust. Includes helpful tools like simple and advanced search, bookshelves, highlighting, notes, downloads, mobile access, and automatic, proper citations.
[**Research Companion**](https://anaheimlibrary.idm.oclc.org/login?url=https://pqrc.proquest.com/?accountid=40026)
Information literacy is a key component of the education process today. Research Companion tools and tutorials help to guide students through the research process and develop critical thinking in information literacy skills.
[**SIRS Discoverer**](https://anaheimlibrary.idm.oclc.org/login?url=https://explore.proquest.com/sirsdiscoverer/?accountid=40026)
SIRS Discoverer consists of selected information for beginning researchers, especially elementary and middle school students and educators, helping them find reliable age-appropriate content for classwork, homework, and research assignments.
[**SIRS Issues Researcher**](https://anaheimlibrary.idm.oclc.org/login?url=https://explore.proquest.com/sirsissuesresearcher/?accountid=40026)
Examining over 360 complex issues, SIRS Issues Researcher is a curriculum-aligned database of content geared to middle and high school students and educators, with a focus on current and relevant analysis of today’s most important controversial issues.
**[Teaching Books](https://library.TeachingBooks.net/?divid=170366)**
Teaching Books features a suite of instructional resources that enrich the fiction and nonfiction books read by children and young adults. Includes Book Guides & Lesson Plans, Diverse Books, Literature-Based Vocabulary Lists, Meet-the-Author Videos and Book Readings, and Resources for Award-Winning Books.
**[Tumblebooks](https://www.tumblebooklibrary.com/autologin.aspx?userid=U7XSf2zM6d3a%2fyYFPUvLaw%3d%3d)**
Online storybooks and reading games for kids. Includes a Language Learning section with stories in French and Spanish.
