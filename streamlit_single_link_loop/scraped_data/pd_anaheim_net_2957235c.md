#  Participate In 
9
1
1
2
  1. [Business Watch](https://pd.anaheim.net/188)
Businesses working together in cooperation with law enforcement can make one of the best crime-fighting teams around.
  2. [Coffee with a Cop](https://pd.anaheim.net/187)
This is a great opportunity for residents to talk and get to know Anaheim Police Officers in a casual setting where all questions, concerns, and comments are welcome.
  3. [Crime Prevention](https://pd.anaheim.net/176)
The Anaheim Police Department has several services available to help residents and businesses with crime prevention.
  4. [Neighborhood Watch](https://pd.anaheim.net/180)
Neighborhood Watch is a great way for neighbors to get to know each other and work together, get trained to recognize and report suspicious activities in your neighborhood, implement crime prevention techniques such as home security and security through environmental design, and enlist the active participation of citizens in cooperation with law enforcement to reduce crime in your community.
  5. [Public Awareness Through Citizen Education (PACE)](https://pd.anaheim.net/207)
The Anaheim Police Department is currently seeking participants and is now accepting applications for the upcoming PACE Class.
  6. [Volunteers in Police Services (VIPS)](https://pd.anaheim.net/205)
The mission of the Volunteers in Police Service (VIPS) program is to provide volunteer support staff to aid police personnel in the delivery of services to the citizens of Anaheim.
