#  2026 State of the City 
![Graphic with Mayor Ashleigh Aitken and date, time and location for the 2026 State of the City](https://www.anaheim.net/ImageRepository/Document?documentID=69690)
**Anaheim Mayor Ashleigh Aitken** delivered her 2026 State of the City Address on April 29 at the Grove of Anaheim.
  

Always uplifting and entertaining, this year's State of the City included some special guests: emcee **Ally Kay** of the Anaheim Ducks, national anthem singer **Tim Kepler** of the Ducks, humorist **Charles Phoenix** and Disneyland Resort President **Jill Estorino**.
  

Proceeds from this nonprofit event touch the lives of many in our community through the Anaheim Community Foundation.
