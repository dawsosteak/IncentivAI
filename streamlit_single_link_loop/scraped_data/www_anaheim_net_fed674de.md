#  Family Resource Centers 
  1. ![Testimony Image for Slideshow 2](https://www.anaheim.net/ImageRepository/Document?documentID=64343)
  2. ![FRC Homepage Image](https://www.anaheim.net/ImageRepository/Document?documentID=64738)


Family Resource Centers are family-friendly facilities that promote and support the safety, stability, and healthy development of Anaheim families. On-site staff is available to provide an assessment of family needs, and coordination of human services, programs, and classes to ensure that needs are addressed to the benefit of the family unit.
Bilingual programs and supportive services are available at the Downtown Anaheim, Ponderosa Park and Miraloma Park Family Resource Centers for families, individuals, children, teens and older adults free of charge.
