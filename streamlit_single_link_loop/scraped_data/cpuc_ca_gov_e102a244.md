ORDER EXTENDING STATUTORY DEADLINE
* * *
Summary This decision extends the statutory deadline in Rulemaking 14-10-003 to September 15, 2022. 1. Background Public Utilities Code (Pub. Util. Code) Section 1701.5(a)1 provides that ratesetting cases must be resolved within 18 months of the date the Scoping Memo is issued, unless the Commission makes a written determination that the deadline cannot be met and issues an order extending the deadline. On August 5, 2021, the Commission adopted Decision (D.) 21-08-020, which extended the statutory deadline by one month to consider a proposed Order Closing Rulemaking 14-10-003. The proposed Order Closing Rulemaking 14-10-003, issued on June 30, 2021, stated the Commission had largely resolved the issues it sought to accomplish
