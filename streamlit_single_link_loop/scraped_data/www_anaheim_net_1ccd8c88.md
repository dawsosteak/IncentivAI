#  Submit a Job Order 
Anaheim Workforce Connection is available to assist you with your hiring needs. If you would like to post your current employment opportunities on our online job board please register your business on [CalJOBS](https://www.caljobs.ca.gov/vosnet/Default.aspx) and complete the Job Order Form below. For any questions, contact one of our Business Services Representatives at 714-765-4350 to discuss your hiring needs. 
[Job Order Form](https://www.anaheim.net/FormCenter/Anaheim-Jobs-17/Submit-a-Job-Order-145)
