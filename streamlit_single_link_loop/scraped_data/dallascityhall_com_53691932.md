Page Content
​The Fiscal Year 2025-26 City of Dallas budget includes Sanitation fee increases necessary to support the operation of the City's solid waste management utility. The following rates ​have been approved by the City Council.
  

**​Service​​** | **​​FY 2025-26 Fee ​  
**(Effective October 1, 2025)  
---|---  
​Residential Collection Services (garbage, recycle, brush and bulky items)* | **$40.09****/month**  
​Additional Garbage Collection Service (per cart)* | **$15.51/month**  
​Additional Recycle Rollcart Handling and Delivery Fee (one time fee for 3rd and subsequent recycle rollcarts)* | $50.00/cart  
  
​Packout Collection Service* | $138.38/month  
​Commercial Collection Services (garbage)* | **$42.28/month**  
​Commercial Collection Services (recycle)* | **$27.49/month**  
​McCommas Bluff Landfill Commercial Gate Rate | **$43.60/ton**  
​McCommas Bluff Environmental Fee | $2.00/ton  
​McCommas Bluff Customer Processing Fee | $2.00/ton  
​McCommas Bluff Unload Assistance Fee* | $54.00/load  
​McCommas Bluff Tipper Use Fee* | $100.00/use  
​McCommas Bluff Uncovered Load Fee | $40.00/load  
​Bachman Transfer Station Commercial Gate Rate* | **$71.28/ton**  
​Bachman Transfer Station Environmental Fee | $2.00/ton  
​Bachman Transfer Station Customer Processing Fee | $2.00/ton  
__* Subject to additional 8.25% sales tax  
** Common fees listed, other fees can be found in Dallas City Code, Chapter 18, Article I​__
