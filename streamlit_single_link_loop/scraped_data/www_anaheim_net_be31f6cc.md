#  Tech Scholar 
![Tech Scholar 2025](https://www.anaheim.net/ImageRepository/Document?documentID=66299)
The **Tech Scholar Program** looks to **empower college-bound high school juniors and seniors in Anaheim**.
The program provides **laptops or tablets** to Anaheim students for the next stage of their academic careers.
**Students can apply** to be part of the Tech Scholar Program below. Laptops or tablets
_Students who were previously awarded this scholarship are ineligible to apply._
# **General Information**
Applications are open to students about to complete their junior or senior year of high school and who have expressed interest in enrolling in a college or university.
The scholarship award will consist of: 
  * one personal laptop or tablet
  * one device bag
  * _As needed: internet service with wireless connection_


### **Student Criteria and Information**
Student applicants must be college-bound and currently enrolled as a high school junior or senior.
The tech scholar program is designed to help students in our city.
So you must live in Anaheim to be considered.If not, the Tech Scholarship Committee reserves the right to disqualify the applicant.
Have a minimum 2.0 grade point average
Financial need and community service will also be taken into consideration.
_*This is a competitive scholarship. Candidates may be invited to interviews at the discretion of the Tech Scholarship Committee._
###   

### **How to Apply**
The student should submit the following as part of the scholarship packet:
  * Completed Tech Scholar application 
  * One copy of high school transcript with cumulative GPA
  * Personal statements (two are required)


### **Timeline and Application Deadline**
The application is currently closed. Check back again in 2026!
[![interest form button Opens in new window](https://www.anaheim.net/ImageRepository/Document?documentId=57295)](https://www.anaheim.net/FormCenter/City-Administration-10/Anaheim-Tech-Scholar-Program-Interest-Fo-316)
### **Questions?**
Please email TechScholar@anaheim.net or call (714) 765-4592.
### **Frequently Asked Questions:**
  1. [How do I apply?](https://www.anaheim.net/6454/Tech-Scholar#tab1e9515ff-8961-4946-a3db-04500fbbb9be_0)


Fill out the online application when it opens.[](https://www.anaheim.net/FormCenter/City-Administration-10/Anaheim-Tech-Scholar-Application-317) Submit the signed application along with your current high school transcript and your personal statements online. This application is currently closed. Check back again in 2026!
Previously awarded students are ineligible
  1. [Do I need permission from a parent or legal guardian to apply? ](https://www.anaheim.net/6454/Tech-Scholar#tab1e9515ff-8961-4946-a3db-04500fbbb9be_1)


Yes. The application requires you to include a parent or legal guardian's contact information.
  1. [Will my computer and internet use be monitored?](https://www.anaheim.net/6454/Tech-Scholar#tab1e9515ff-8961-4946-a3db-04500fbbb9be_2)


No, but we encourage parents or legal guardians to monitor their student's internet use.
  1. [Do I have to return the laptop?](https://www.anaheim.net/6454/Tech-Scholar#tab1e9515ff-8961-4946-a3db-04500fbbb9be_3)


No, the laptop is yours to keep and to assist you in your future education.
  1. [What if I already have a computer?](https://www.anaheim.net/6454/Tech-Scholar#tab1e9515ff-8961-4946-a3db-04500fbbb9be_4)


You can still apply but the goal of this program is to provide a computer to those who may not have access to one.
  1. [Are my family members allowed to use the computer?](https://www.anaheim.net/6454/Tech-Scholar#tab1e9515ff-8961-4946-a3db-04500fbbb9be_5)


The computer will be awarded to the students, and they can determine how and who gets to use it.
  1. [Will I be interviewed for the scholarship?](https://www.anaheim.net/6454/Tech-Scholar#tab1e9515ff-8961-4946-a3db-04500fbbb9be_6)


An interview is not required. But since this is a competitive scholarship, candidates may be asked to interview at the discretion of the Tech Scholar Program Committee.
  1. [When will I know if I was awarded the scholarship?](https://www.anaheim.net/6454/Tech-Scholar#tab1e9515ff-8961-4946-a3db-04500fbbb9be_7)


Applicants that are chosen to receive the scholarship will be informed in mid-November.
  1. [I am not sure if I want to attend college. Can I still apply?](https://www.anaheim.net/6454/Tech-Scholar#tab1e9515ff-8961-4946-a3db-04500fbbb9be_8)


This program is geared toward college-bound students. The laptop or tablet is meant to assist students in their school work and their college search. We encourage students to take the opportunity to talk to a guidance counselor about options to pursue higher education.
  1. [I do not have any extracurricular activities. Can I still apply?](https://www.anaheim.net/6454/Tech-Scholar#tab1e9515ff-8961-4946-a3db-04500fbbb9be_9)


Yes. The application requires a personal statement which is your chance to explain to the Tech Scholarship Committee why you deserve to receive this scholarship. Everyone's life and responsibilities are different, we want to hear about you in your personal statement and you can explain what you do in your spare time.
