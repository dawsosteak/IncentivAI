**Texas Comptroller Transparency Star Program**  

The Texas Comptroller of Public Accounts' Transparency Stars program recognizes local governments for going above and beyond in their transparency efforts. The City of Dallas strives to ensure that residents are able to readily access and view financial summary information.   
  
To learn more about the program, please visit the [Texas Comptroller Transparency Star program](https://comptroller.texas.gov/) website.  

#  **Traditional Finance Summary**
The financial data is collected through collaborative efforts with multiple departments and reports such as the [Budget Accountability Report](https://dallascityhall.com/departments/budget/financialtransparency/Pages/Budget-Accountability-Report.aspx), [Adopted Budget](https://dallascityhall.com/departments/budget/financialtransparency/Pages/Current-Budget.aspx), and the [Annual Comprehensive Financial Report (ACFR)](https://dallascityhall.com/departments/budget/financialtransparency/Pages/annualreport.aspx). These reports include detailed information to support financial transparency efforts. 
The per capita figures are based on the City's 2023 estimated population of 1,300,642*.
