#  E-file a Document
The Electronic Filing (E-Filing) System allows you to file documents in formal proceedings at the California Public Utilities Commission from your office using a web-based application. 
. 
**ATTENTION: Please review the step-by-step instructions for Electronically Filing a document click[HERE](https://www.cpuc.ca.gov/-/media/cpuc-website/proceedings-and-rulemaking/documents/electronic-filing-guide_2023.pdf).**
**NOTE** : If you need to make a Transportation Insurance E-Filing, please [click here](https://www.cpuc.ca.gov/regulatory-services/licensing/transportation-licensing-and-analysis-branch/licensing-requirements-for-carriers-under-cpuc-authority/insurance-electronic-filing-requirements).
You can search and [retrieve documents that have been electronically filed](http://docs.cpuc.ca.gov/EFileSearchForm.aspx "EFiled Document Search"). If you are interested in following a particular proceeding or industry or type of document, check out the Commission's proceeding **[Subscription Service](http://subscribecpuc.cpuc.ca.gov/ "Check out the Subscription Service").**
Please read the [disclaimer](https://docs.cpuc.ca.gov/PublishedDocs/Published/G000/M159/K615/159615370.PDF) before submitting any supporting documents. [Instructions for using the supporting documents](https://www.cpuc.ca.gov/-/media/cpuc-website/divisions/administrative-law-judge-division/documents/supporting-documents.pdf) feature and an explanation of the [naming conventions for supporting documents](https://www.cpuc.ca.gov/-/media/cpuc-website/divisions/administrative-law-judge-division/documents/naming-convention-for-electronic-submission-of-supporting-documents.pdf) are also provided.
**Before you start E-Filing you need:**
  * An e-mail Account to receive communications about your filing.
  * An Internet connection. The faster your connection (DSL, cable, etc.) the better.
  * A web browser. In Windows, Internet Explorer, Firefox or Chrome can be used.
  * For the Macintosh, Safari, Firefox or Chrome are recommended.
  * A program able to create PDF/A compliant documents. Adobe Acrobat 7.0 (or higher version) of Professional works. **SCANNING DOCUMENTS IS STRONGLY DISCOURAGED!**
  * Copy of the [Rules of Practice and Procedure](https://www.cpuc.ca.gov/-/media/cpuc-website/divisions/administrative-law-judge-division/documents/rules-of-practice-and-procedure-may-2021.pdf) for the e-filing rules (Rule 1.13).


[E-FILE A DOCUMENT](https://efile.cpuc.ca.gov/)
Please note that you still need to perform service of your document on the service list consistent with Rules 1.9 and 1.10 of the [Rules of Practice and Procedure](https://www.cpuc.ca.gov/-/media/cpuc-website/divisions/administrative-law-judge-division/documents/rules-of-practice-and-procedure-may-2021.pdf). You should NOT submit a paper copy to the Commission's Docket Office if you e-file. Upon acceptance of your document for e-filing, you will receive an email notifying you that it has been accepted and providing you with a link to where it is posted.
**Comments / Questions?**
Please give us your feedback on your experience using the Commission's E-Filing System.
Need HELP? Please email us at Efile-help.
