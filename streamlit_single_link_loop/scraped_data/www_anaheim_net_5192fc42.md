#  Neighborhood Improvement Projects 
In Anaheim, **we’re committed to making our neighborhoods better**!  
[![PW neighborhood improvement image \(2\) Opens in new window](https://www.anaheim.net/ImageRepository/Document?documentId=63822)](https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=apd5O9nD1OY)  
With **more than 600 miles of roadway** to care for across our city, we are**always looking at ways to improve our community** by replacing damaged sidewalks, paving streets, updating street signs, ensuring accessibility standards and more.  
  
We do this thanks to our **Public Works Neighborhood Improvement Program** , an innovative approach that **keeps our streets and neighborhoods preserved and safe**.
