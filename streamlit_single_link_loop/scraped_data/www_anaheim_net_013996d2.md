#  Media Lab 
The Media Lab is a public space for audio, video, and photo production. The lab is equipped with a recording booth, green screen, photo/video camera, teleprompter, three-point lighting set up, as well as an editing bay fitted with an Apple Mac computer and professional creative software. This room also houses the [Memory Lab](https://www.anaheim.net/6670/Memory-Lab).
