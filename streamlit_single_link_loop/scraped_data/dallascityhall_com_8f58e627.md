![Lorie Blair](https://dallascityhall.com/government/PublishingImages/Lorie-Blair.jpg)
Councilmember Lorie Blair proudly represents District 8 on the Dallas City Council. A committed public servant, advocate, and longtime resident of southern Dallas, Councilmember Blair brings a deep passion for community empowerment, equity, and responsive leadership.
With a background in public policy, civic organizing, and community engagement, Councilmember Blair has spent years working directly with residents, neighborhood leaders, and youth to address critical issues across southern Dallas—including infrastructure, affordable housing, economic development, and public safety.
She is committed to ensuring every voice in District 8 is heard and that communities receive the attention and investment they deserve. A strong believer in transparent governance, accessible communication, and neighborhood-led solutions, Councilmember Blair leads with both compassion and accountability.
**Key Priorities:**
  * Expanding economic opportunities for District 8 residents
  * Improving infrastructure and city services
  * Advocating for safe and diverse housing
  * Supporting youth programs, education, and workforce development
  * Strengthening public safety partnerships and accountability


Councilmember Blair is a hands-on leader who believes that progress is built through unity and collaboration. She is driven by her faith, her love for community, and her belief in the power of people to create lasting change.
She invites all District 8 residents to stay connected, get involved, and help shape the future of our district.
**Background & Experience**
  * **Former City Planning Commissioner (2020–2024):** Led key zoning decisions with a focus on community-centered development across District 8, including projects in Kleberg-Rylie and the International Inland Port of Dallas.
  * **35 Years at AT &T:** Rose from customer service representative to IT Project Manager, earning a strong reputation for collaboration, ethics, and operational excellence.
  * **Local Journalist with _Elite News_ :** Spent seven years covering Dallas politics and community issues, strengthening communication and public awareness.
  * **Certified Civil Court Mediator:** Trained at El Centro College; volunteered to help resolve civil and family disputes, advocating for fairness and constructive dialogue.


**Vision & Focus**
With a strong focus on neighborhoods and families, Councilmember Blair is committed to: 
  * Reducing crime and increasing public safety across District 8
  * Protecting neighborhood character while expanding access to affordable housing
  * Investing in infrastructure and job opportunities, particularly near UNT Dallas and Paul Quinn College
  * Delivering city services that are efficient, equitable, and responsive


**Rooted in District 8**
A proud resident of District 8 for more than 12 years and a Dallas resident for over 27, Councilmember Blair understands the unique needs and potential of her community.
During her time on the City Plan Commission, she worked with residents to modernize land use policies, prevent incompatible industrial encroachment near neighborhoods, and support smart, sustainable development. Notably, she helped bring an equine veterinary facility to the Kleberg-Rylie area and played a leadership role in shaping **Forward Dallas 2.0** to reflect the district’s diverse geography—from rural communities to urban corridors.
**What She Brings to the Council**
  * Proven leadership in civic engagement, planning, communications, and mediation
  * Deep roots in southern Dallas and a clear understanding of neighborhood concerns
  * A collaborative, transparent approach to governance
  * A bold vision for District 8 grounded in equity, opportunity, and smart development
