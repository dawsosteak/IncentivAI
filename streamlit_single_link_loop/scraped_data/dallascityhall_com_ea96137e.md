The Department of Housing & Community Development strengthens families and neighborhoods to cultivate a diverse and economically inclusive City by creating affordable and safe housing and mitigating community member displacement.   

  

  * Home Repair Programs
  * Home Buyer Assistance
  * Lien Release Assistance


* [ARPA NRP - American Rescue Plan Act Neighborhood Revitalization Program ](https://dallascityhall.com/departments/housing-neighborhood-revitalization/Pages/American-Rescue-Plan-Act-Neighborhood-Revitilization-Program.aspx)
Apply for home repair grant assistance through our American Rescue Plan Act Program.
* [ARPA ST - American Rescue Plan Act Residential Septic Tank Program](https://dallascityhall.com/departments/housing-neighborhood-revitalization/Pages/ARPA-Septic-Tank.aspx)
The American Rescue Plan Act Residential Septic Tank Program will provide for the cost of single-family residential homes to convert from On-Site Sewer Facilities (septic systems) to City water/wastewater/sewer utilities.
* [HIPP - Home Improvement & Preservation Program](https://dallascityhall.com/departments/housing-neighborhood-revitalization/Pages/Repair-Programs.aspx)
Apply for major home repair assistance through our Home Improvement and Preservation Program. Applications are currently not being accepted at this time.
* [DHAP - Dallas Homebuyer Assistance Program](https://dallascityhall.com/departments/housing-neighborhood-revitalization/Pages/Dallas-Homebuyer-Assistance-Program-DHAP.aspx)
Provides financial assistance for low-to-moderate income homebuyers, when purchasing a home in the City of Dallas.
* [CLP - Compliance & Lien   
Portfolio](https://dallascityhall.com/departments/housing-neighborhood-revitalization/Pages/Lien-Releases.aspx)
Request that the City of Dallas release a lien placed on your home by the Housing & Neighborhood Revitalization Department.
