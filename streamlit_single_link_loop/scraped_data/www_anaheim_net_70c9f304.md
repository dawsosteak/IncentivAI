#  Lighting Incentives Program 
## Lighting upgrades can deliver larger savings than you think. 
  
Anaheim Business Energy Efficiency Solutions (ABEES) Program offers incentives to help customers upgrade to energy-efficient LED lighting and lighting control systems. With rapid advancements in LED technology, there are now a wide variety of options to replace your existing lighting with more efficient, cost-saving solutions.
Anaheim customers can take advantage of Performance-Based energy efficiency projects, where incentives are calculated based on measurable energy savings in kilowatts (kW) or kilowatt-hours (kWh). This ensures that your investment in energy-efficient lighting not only reduces costs but also delivers tangible, measurable results.

### **Program Steps**
  * Download the Program Guidelines and [Lighting Incentive Table](https://www.anaheim.net/DocumentCenter/View/27035/ABEES-Lighting-Incentives-Table) documents.
  * Download the [Lighting Incentive Application](https://www.anaheim.net/DocumentCenter/View/26602/Lighting-Application) and all required forms (including the W-9).
  * Questions about the program details? Simply fill out our [online form](http://anaheim.net/FormCenter/Public-Utilities-6/Energy-Efficiency-Solutions-61). A representative will contact you within 2-3 business days.
