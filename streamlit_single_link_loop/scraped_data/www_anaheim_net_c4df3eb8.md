#  Ramp N’ Roll 
Ramp N’ Roll is a FREE drop-in program designed to bring skate park recreation to Anaheim youth in parks and neighborhoods, ages 5-16. The program offers drop-in ramps, rails, and fitness fun while providing a safe environment with the supervision of trained Anaheim City staff members. Helmets are required. For more information, please call (714) 765-5191. During extreme heat or rain, please call our Inclement Weather Hotline at (714) 765-4090 to receive up to date information of site cancellations.  
  
**Important Update** : Ramp N' Roll is currently **closed**. Please check back for an update or call (714) 765-5191. We apologize for any inconvenience.  
  
**Actualización Importante** : Ramp N 'Roll esta **cerrado**. Por favor, vuelva a consultar para obtener una actualización o llame (714) 765-5191. Nos disculpamos por cualquier inconveniente.
