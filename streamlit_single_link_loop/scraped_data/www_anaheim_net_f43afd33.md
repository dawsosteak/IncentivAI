#  TreePower 
**
## Trees are more than just for the birds. They can:
**
  * **Beautify your yard.**
  * **Cut lawn irrigation in half.**
  * **Improve air quality.**
  * **Make summer air feel cooler.**
  * **Plus, shade trees around your home can reduce your air conditioning needs by up to 40 percent.**


#  [Sign up for your FREE trees today!](https://www.anaheim.net/FormCenter/Public-Utilities-6/TreePower-Program-58)  
or call 714-491-TREE (8733)
## Program Details
  

Just say the word and you’ve got it made in the shade – with up to six (6) free trees from our TreePower program. Choose from more than 40 available species! We’ll even send a TreePower specialist to your home to help you select the best trees for your location and give you easy tips on planting and care.
If you have questions, please contact TreePower at 714-491-TREE (8733).
