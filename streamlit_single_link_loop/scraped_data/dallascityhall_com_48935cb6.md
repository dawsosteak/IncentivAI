###  NEWS: DAS has launched a NEW WEBSITE!
### [![Website Announcement image](https://dallascityhall.com/departments/dallas-animal-services/PublishingImages/Pages/default/Website-Announcement.jpg)](https://www.bedallas90.org/)  

### DAS has launched a supplemental Dallas90 campaign website ([www.BeDallas90.org](https://www.bedallas90.org/)), which showcases our lifesaving #Dallas90 community outreach campaign designed to generate public support of our effort to find positive outcomes for all healthy and behaviorally sound pets. The new site will link to and from this site. It is interactive, mobile friendly, easy to navigate, and highlights DAS’ core programs and services, lifesaving statistics and ways for citizens to get involved and help save more pet lives.   

  
  

  

###  **Dallas90:** Thinking Outside the Box to Save Lives  
  
![D90Logo WhiteBackground NoTag 01 image](https://dallascityhall.com/departments/dallas-animal-services/PublishingImages/Pages/default/D90Logo-WhiteBackground-NoTag-01.jpg)   

**OUR VISION:**  
Lead the nation in compassionate care of animals while finding positive outcomes for all placeable pets through innovative shelter operations and community engagement.   

**OUR PLAN:**  
Dallas90 is a community outreach campaign designed to generate support of and participation in Dallas Animal Services' effort to find positive outcomes for all healthy and behaviorally sound pets, which we believe exceeds 90% of our shelter population.  

[Learn more about Dallas90 and how you can get involved. ](https://www.bedallas90.org/)
