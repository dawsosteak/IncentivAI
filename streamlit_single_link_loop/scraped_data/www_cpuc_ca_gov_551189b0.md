#  Creating Formal Documents - Templates Provided
All formal documents must follow formatting guidelines in the [Rules of Practice and Procedure](https://www.cpuc.ca.gov/-/media/cpuc-website/divisions/administrative-law-judge-division/documents/rules-of-practice-and-procedure-may-2021.pdf). Specifically, they must follow Rule 1.5 (Form and Size of Documents) and Rule 1.6 (Title Page Requirements). In addition to these Rules, other Rules detailing the content of the documents must be followed as well. These are specified below next to the type of document being filed. Below we provide templates to help you get started on the creation of your documents.  
**Note:** It is the participant's responsibility to ensure they are following all of the relevant Rules when creating their formal documents for a proceeding.
**Filing a motion for party status. This type of motion must fulfill[Rule 1.4.b](https://www.cpuc.ca.gov/-/media/cpuc-website/divisions/administrative-law-judge-division/documents/rules-of-practice-and-procedure-may-2021.pdf)**
  * For an Application Proceeding (e.g. [A.12-11-009](http://apps.cpuc.ca.gov/apex/f?p=401:56:12287052622475::NO:RP,57,RIR:P5_PROCEEDING_SELECT:A1211009 "Application 12-11-009")) 
    * [Template of Motion for Party Status](https://www.cpuc.ca.gov/-/media/cpuc-website/divisions/administrative-law-judge-division/documents/template-of-motion-for-party-status.pdf) and [Template of Certificate of Service](https://www.cpuc.ca.gov/-/media/cpuc-website/divisions/administrative-law-judge-division/documents/template-of-certificate-of-service.pdf)
    * [Example of a motion for party status](http://docs.cpuc.ca.gov/PublishedDocs/Efile/G000/M155/K876/155876397.PDF "Example of a motion for party status") and [accompanying certificate of service](http://docs.cpuc.ca.gov/PublishedDocs/Efile/G000/M155/K873/155873647.PDF "accompanying certificate of service")
  * For a Rulemaking Proceeding (e.g. [R.12-12-011](http://apps.cpuc.ca.gov/apex/f?p=401:56:2071393201708::NO:RP,57,RIR:P5_PROCEEDING_SELECT:R1212011 "Rulemaking 12-12-011"))  

    * [Template of Motion for Party Status](https://www.cpuc.ca.gov/-/media/cpuc-website/divisions/administrative-law-judge-division/documents/template-of-motion-for-party-status.pdf) and [Template of Certificate of Service](https://www.cpuc.ca.gov/-/media/cpuc-website/divisions/administrative-law-judge-division/documents/template-of-certificate-of-service---rulemaking-comments.pdf)
    * [Example of a motion for party status](http://docs.cpuc.ca.gov/PublishedDocs/Efile/G000/M151/K820/151820424.PDF "Example of a motion for party status") and [accompanying certificate of service](http://docs.cpuc.ca.gov/PublishedDocs/Efile/G000/M151/K794/151794332.PDF "accompanying certificate of service")


**Filing a protest or response to an application. Refer to[Rule 2.6](https://www.cpuc.ca.gov/-/media/cpuc-website/divisions/administrative-law-judge-division/documents/rules-of-practice-and-procedure-may-2021.pdf)**
  * [Template of a Formal Protest](https://www.cpuc.ca.gov/-/media/cpuc-website/divisions/administrative-law-judge-division/documents/template-of-formal-protest.pdf) and [Template of Certificate of Service](https://www.cpuc.ca.gov/-/media/cpuc-website/divisions/administrative-law-judge-division/documents/template-of-certificate-of-service-protest.pdf)
  * [Example of a Protest](http://docs.cpuc.ca.gov/PublishedDocs/Efile/G000/M084/K764/84764730.PDF "Example of a Protest") and [accompanying certificate of service](http://docs.cpuc.ca.gov/PublishedDocs/Efile/G000/M084/K772/84772557.PDF "accompanying certificate of service")
  * For a Response to an application, you can use the template of a formal protest - change the word "protest" to "response"


**Filing comments in response to a rulemaking. Refer to[Rule 6.2](https://www.cpuc.ca.gov/-/media/cpuc-website/divisions/administrative-law-judge-division/documents/rules-of-practice-and-procedure-may-2021.pdf)**
  * [Template of Opening Comments](https://www.cpuc.ca.gov/-/media/cpuc-website/divisions/administrative-law-judge-division/documents/template-for-comments---rulemaking.pdf) in a rulemaking and [Template of Certificate of Service](https://www.cpuc.ca.gov/-/media/cpuc-website/divisions/administrative-law-judge-division/documents/template-of-certificate-of-service.pdf)
  * [Example of opening comments](https://www.cpuc.ca.gov/-/media/cpuc-website/divisions/administrative-law-judge-division/documents/example-of-opening-comments_rulemaking-proceeding.pdf) filed in a rulemaking and [accompanying certificate of service](http://docs.cpuc.ca.gov/PublishedDocs/Efile/G000/M090/K601/90601566.PDF "accompanying certificate of service")
