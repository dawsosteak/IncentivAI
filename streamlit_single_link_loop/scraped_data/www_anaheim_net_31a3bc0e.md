#  Project ViewFinder 
**![Project View Finder!](https://www.anaheim.net/ImageRepository/Document?documentId=3652)Project ViewFinder gives a glimpse into Anaheim's future.**
As one of the largest cities in the United States, with more than 345,000 residents and 20 million visitors a year, Anaheim is leading the way in providing up to date information about the city's plans. Project ViewFinder is Anaheim's latest mapping tool in helping keep residents, businesses, developers, contractors and utility companies informed about Anaheim's many current and upcoming projects.
Launch [Project ViewFinder](https://gis.anaheim.net/portal/apps/experiencebuilder/experience/?id=85be53780e6b439facdf843b39f3ff7a).
## How it Works
To use Project ViewFinder, select the type of project you'd like to see by its icon or type in an address or intersection in the search bar. Just hover your cursor over any of the icons on the map to find project details and who to contact for additional information for each project.
## Residents
You can use Project ViewFinder to learn more about the construction projects and improvements taking place in you neighborhood or along your commute.
## Business Professionals
Use Project ViewFinder to track Anaheim's future and current construction projects, as well as find out how you can partner with the city in providing high quality, cost effective services in the Anaheim community.
