#  Construction Services Division 
Surveys and inspects all City capital improvement projects, inspects privately funded right-of-way improvements, and manages contracts for all City capital projects to ensure contract compliance.  
  

Our **Mission  
**
We are a team of construction professionals working collectively to deliver quality projects for our Anaheim community.
