Be Ready Anaheim is the power of preparedness. 
Every day, Anaheim's emergency response teams ― police, fire, public utilities and public works ― are ready to serve our city in times of need.
But community preparedness is more than that.
It is people, families, volunteers, neighborhoods, workplaces, community groups and organizations prepared for emergencies and resiliency.
While not fun to think about, the threat of earthquakes, wildfires, severe storms and other natural and human-made disasters is real and always with us.
Be Ready Anaheim is our partnership with the community to be ready for, to ride out and to recover from disasters and other challenges that confront our city.
