# City of Dallas Forestry  

The City of Dallas Forestry team consists of staff from multiple departments who focus on preserving, increasing and managing public natural resources, including but not limited to trees, native prairies and wildflower areas, wetlands and the Great Trinity Forest.   

  

[About Our City of Dallas Partners](https://dallascityhall.com/projects/forestry/Pages/Dallas-partners.aspx)
  

[About Our External Partners](https://dallascityhall.com/projects/forestry/Pages/external-partners.aspx)
  

![](https://dallascityhall.com/projects/forestry/Image%20Slides/Medium%20Community%20Forester%20Emblem.png)
Dallas Community Foresters are trained volunteers who are dedicated to the preservation, maintenance and expansion of a healthy urban forest in the Dallas community and region.
