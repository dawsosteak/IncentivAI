#  STEAM on the Go! Kits 
![Website Assets_Header](https://www.anaheim.net/ImageRepository/Document?documentID=34247)
![Website Assets_Program Subheader](https://www.anaheim.net/ImageRepository/Document?documentID=34250)
The STEAM on the Go! Program is designed for kids, teens and families to explore a variety of STEAM Subjects. STEAM stands for science, technology, engineering, the arts, and math.   
  
Each kit will allow you to dig deep into science and art concepts while completing hands-on experiments and projects. Take a peek into our collection through the [STEAM on the Go! Kit Visual Catalog](https://www.canva.com/design/DAFmSmSPQNc/-rp7LSgvriuUy_pETOPMQA/view?utm_content=DAFmSmSPQNc&utm_campaign=designshare&utm_medium=link&utm_source=editor).
STEAM on the GO! kits are available to request through the Anaheim Public Library’s [catalog](https://ana.ent.sirsi.net/client/en_US/virtual/search/detailnonmodal/ent:$002f$002fSD_ILS$002f0$002fSD_ILS:2708054/one?qu=steam+on+the+go%21&rt=false%7C%7C%7CSUBJECT%7C%7C%7CSubject) or by calling your [neighborhood Anaheim Library](http://anaheim.net/903/Locations-Hours)! Need a STEAM kit now? Visit [East Anaheim](https://ca-anaheim.civicplus.com/Facilities/Facility/Details/East-Anaheim-Branch-Library-25)[](https://ca-anaheim.civicplus.com/Facilities/Facility/Details/Ponderosa-JointUse-Library-19) or [Sunkist](https://ca-anaheim.civicplus.com/Facilities/Facility/Details/Sunkist-Branch-Library-23) Branch Libraries to browse their kits and check them out the same day!  
  
Each kit also contains a [STEAM on the Go! Parent Manual](https://www.canva.com/design/DAExcAusNc0/WOfRFmycC5jo0ViJeQN3PA/view?utm_content=DAExcAusNc0&utm_campaign=designshare&utm_medium=link&utm_source=editor) for parents/guardians to refer to as an FAQ.
