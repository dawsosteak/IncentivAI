#  Public Utilities Board Photo Gallery 
![State of the City - PUB](https://www.anaheim.net/ImageRepository/Document?documentID=70712)
**April 29, 2026**
Members of the Board attended the 2026 State of the City Address. The event highlighted the city's achievements and plans for the future.
* * *
![Electric and water apprentices posing with the Public Utilities Board](https://www.anaheim.net/ImageRepository/Document?documentID=70583)
**April 22, 2026**
In celebration of National Apprenticeship Week, the Public Utilities Board recognized the contributions of those participating in our apprenticeship programs.
The programs span across critical trades in various electric and water workgroups to develop the future workforce with real-world training.
* * *
![Customer service staff group photo](https://www.anaheim.net/ImageRepository/Document?documentID=70218)
**March 25, 2026**
For National Consumer Protection Month, members of the Public Utilities Board recognized the service and impact of our Customer Service team.
Representatives from each subgroup shared how they protect and monitor customer accounts and support customers in times of need.
  

  

  

* * *
![Oath of Office - Kewalramani](https://www.anaheim.net/ImageRepository/Document?documentID=69526)
**January 28, 2026**
We welcomed Shashi Kewalramani as our newest Public Utilities Board member, representing all districts of Anaheim.
![Janis Lehman Recognition](https://www.anaheim.net/ImageRepository/Document?documentID=69528)
Members of the Public Utilities Board recognized Janis Lehman for her 33 years of service to Anaheim Public Utilities.
![Recognition for Geo Cuevas](https://www.anaheim.net/ImageRepository/Document?documentID=69527)
Geovani Cuevas was recognized by the Board for his commitment to the safety of the Anaheim community and assistance to an Anaheim senior.
* * *
![eramirez](https://www.anaheim.net/ImageRepository/Document?documentID=67752)
**December 17, 2025**
Public Utilities Board members recognized Eddie Ramirez for his 37 years of service as a City of Anaheim employee.
![mentorship 2025](https://www.anaheim.net/ImageRepository/Document?documentID=67753)
Public Utilities Board members recognized students from Anaheim Union High School District’s Anaheim Innovation Mentoring Experience (AIME) Program, which aims to empower future generations through career exploration, resume building, and job-related skills. 
* * *
![ivan-castillo](https://www.anaheim.net/ImageRepository/Document?documentID=66588)
**September 24, 2025**
We welcomed our newest Public Utilities Board member, Ivan Castillo, representing District 1.
* * *
![WaterSmart Landscape Award Commercial Winner](https://www.anaheim.net/ImageRepository/Document?documentID=66269)
**August 27, 2025**
Local Anaheim business InfoSend Inc. (pictured) and residents   
Raymond R. and Whitney K. were recognized as winners of the annual WaterSmart Landscape Award.
Each landscape showcased drought-tolerant plants, inspiring design, and variety—all while saving water compared to traditional landscapes.
* * *
![High School Scholarship and Internship Program](https://www.anaheim.net/ImageRepository/Document?documentID=65826)
**July 23, 2025**
Students participating in the Anaheim Public Utilities Scholarship and Internship Program, selected and awarded by the Public Utilities Board from a competitive pool of applicants, shared their experiences and achievements gained through the program.
* * *
![AUHSD Career Connections Unlimited](https://www.anaheim.net/ImageRepository/Document?documentID=64062)
**April 23, 2025**
Anaheim Union High School District's Career Connections Unlimited student participants were recognized by City Council Member Kristen Maahs and the Public Utilities Board for the work they have supported this academic year.
The Career Connections Unlimited program team was honored for their role in fostering a more inclusive and supportive Anaheim community.
