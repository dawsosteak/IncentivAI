#  Lobbyist Registration Program 
[Chapter 1.11](https://codelibrary.amlegal.com/codes/anaheim/latest/anaheim_ca/0-0-0-53496) (Sunshine Provisions) of the Anaheim Municipal Code (AMC) was adopted to increase transparency in government operations. Section [1.11.070](https://codelibrary.amlegal.com/codes/anaheim/latest/anaheim_ca/0-0-0-53546) of the (AMC) (Lobbyist Registration, Reporting, and Regulation) include provisions regarding public disclosure of the activities of individuals who influence Anaheim officials, creating the Lobbyist Registration Program. This program requires individuals who either 1) receive compensation or entitled to receive $500 or more in a calendar month for lobbying the City of Anaheim, or any regional agency in which Anaheim has a voting role (list of regional agencies); or 2) is employed or contracted for a role that includes influencing legislative or administrative action as a substantial or regular portion of the employment or contract, must register with the City Clerk as a lobbyist. 
### Lobbyist Registration (Initial and Annual) Information
All Lobbyists must register, by filing a [Lobbyist Registration (Initial) form](https://www.anaheim.net/DocumentCenter/View/17926/Lobbyist-Form-Registration-Amendment-Termination), with the Office of the City Clerk within fifteen (15) days of the date of qualifying as a lobbyist. 
All Lobbyists must renew their register with the Office of the City Clerk by January 15 of each year.
Lobbyists are required to submit a registration (Initial and Annual) fee of $100.00. Fees are due with submission of the completed [Lobbyist Registration (Initial and Annual) form](https://www.anaheim.net/DocumentCenter/View/17926/Lobbyist-Form-Registration-Amendment-Termination).
### Lobbyist Quarterly Reports of Activity
Lobbyists will be required to file a [Lobbyist Quarterly Report of Activity](https://anaheim.net/DocumentCenter/View/17923/Lobbyist-Quarterly-Report) with the Office of the City Clerk disclosing any lobbying of the City of Anaheim or any regional agency in which Anaheim has a voting role, as well as report any campaign contributions made during the reporting period to the Mayor or any City Council Member. 
### Penalties
Late submittal of Lobbyist Registration Form and/or Quarterly Report may result in a late filing penalty of $50.00. In September of 2022 the City Council adopted [Ordinance No. 6535](https://records.anaheim.net/CityClerk/DocView.aspx?id=4247639&dbid=0&repo=CITYOFANAHEIM) amending sections of [Chapter 1.11](https://codelibrary.amlegal.com/codes/anaheim/latest/anaheim_ca/0-0-0-53496) of the Anaheim Municipal Code by adding Subsection .080 of [Section 1.11.070](https://codelibrary.amlegal.com/codes/anaheim/latest/anaheim_ca/0-0-0-53546) relating to criminal penalties for inaccurate information or information required to be disclosed on the Quarterly Report of Activity.
Questions about the Lobbyist Registration Program can be directed to the Office of the City Clerk at (714) 765-5166 or via email at tbass@anaheim.net.
