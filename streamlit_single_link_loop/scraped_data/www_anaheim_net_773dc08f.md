#  Student Success for AESD 
![StudentSuccess_AESD Web Banner](https://www.anaheim.net/ImageRepository/Document?documentID=38523)
**Anaheim Public Library** (APL) and **Anaheim Elementary School District** (AESD) are working together to make it easy for students and staff to access tutoring, eBooks, language learning, and more for FREE on Anaheim Public Library's Student Success portal.
### Student Success Instructions
Access the Student Success resources by following the steps below.
  1. Click the buttons or links below to unlock resources for Student Success and start learning.  

  2. Students and Staff: Use your AESD user name (the first part of your school email address, everything before the @ symbol) any time you are prompted to enter a library card number.
  3. You may be asked for your PIN, which is the year you were born (all 4 digits). 
  4. You may be prompted to create a user name and password for individual resources.
