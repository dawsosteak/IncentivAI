#  California Pro-Housing Designation 
## Public Review Notice
The City of Anaheim has prepared a draft Pro-Housing Designation Program (PDP) Application and invites public review and comment.
The Pro-Housing Designation Program, administered by the California Department of Housing and Community Development (HCD), recognizes jurisdictions that have adopted policies and programs that promote housing production, affordability, and accessibility. Achieving Pro-Housing Designation can enhance the City’s competitiveness for State funding and support the implementation of local housing goals.
This application builds upon Anaheim’s recently adopted Anaheim Local Housing Trust Fund, Housing Element and ongoing Environmental Justice planning efforts, reflecting community input received through workshops, stakeholder engagement, and public hearings.
## Public Review Period
The PDP draft application and attachments are available to view online or in person for a 30-day public review period beginning on May 12, 2026 and ending on June 11, 2026.
  * Online: View the draft application [here](https://www.anaheim.net/DocumentCenter/View/70827/Anaheim-Prohousing-Designation-Program-Application_DRAFT). View the application attachments [here](https://www.anaheim.net/DocumentCenter/View/70832/City-of-Anaheim---California-Pro-Housing-Designation-Program-Application-Attachments).
