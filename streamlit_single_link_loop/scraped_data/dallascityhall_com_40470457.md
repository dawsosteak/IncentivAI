**​​​TRANSPORTATION REGULATION**
DCH Body
Page Content
​Transportation Regulation is a division of the Transportation and Public Works Department.  
  
The division ensures public safety and consumer protection by enforcing and regulating ordinances related to for-hire transportation, emergency vehicles, vehicle towing, vehicle immobilization (booting) services operating in the city, andtransportation  services at  [ **Dallas Lo​ve Field Airport**](https://www.dallas-lovefield.com/).
##  Ambulances, emergency wreckers, tow vehicles, and vehicle immobilization services:   

**![Picture of an ambulance.](https://dallascityhall.com/departments/transportation/PublishingImages/Pages/transportation_regulation/cc1.gif)![Picture of a tow truck.](https://dallascityhall.com/departments/transportation/PublishingImages/Pages/transportation_regulation/cc2.gif)![Picture of a ](https://dallascityhall.com/departments/transportation/PublishingImages/Pages/transportation_regulation/cc3.gif)  
** [**Click here for frequently asked questions (FAQs)**](https://dallascityhall.com/departments/transportation/Pages/transportation-regulation-FAQ.aspx) Dallas City Code includes ordinances for the following vehicles (includes links):  
[**15D. Ambulances**](https://codelibrary.amlegal.com/codes/dallas/latest/dallas_tx/0-0-0-106382)  
[**15D. Emergency Wreckers**](https://codelibrary.amlegal.com/codes/dallas/latest/dallas_tx/0-0-0-106843)  
[**48A. Vehicle Tow Service**](https://codelibrary.amlegal.com/codes/dallas/latest/dallas_tx/0-0-0-123409)  
[**48C. Vehicle Immobilization Service (Booting)**](https://codelibrary.amlegal.com/codes/dallas/latest/dallas_tx/0-0-0-124200)
##  Regulatory activities include criminal history checks for company owners and drivers, vehicle inspections, verification of insurance coverage, resident complaint investigations, and field inspections.  
  
Transportation-For-Hire:  

**![A taxi sign.](https://dallascityhall.com/departments/transportation/PublishingImages/Pages/transportation_regulation/cc11.jpg)![A picture of a group of limousines.](https://dallascityhall.com/departments/transportation/PublishingImages/Pages/transportation_regulation/cc12.jpg)![A silhouette of a horse-drawn carriage.](https://dallascityhall.com/departments/transportation/PublishingImages/Pages/transportation_regulation/cc13.gif)![A picture of a pedicab.](https://dallascityhall.com/departments/transportation/PublishingImages/Pages/transportation_regulation/cc14.jpg)  
** [**Chapter 47A of the Dallas City Code**](https://codelibrary.amlegal.com/codes/dallas/latest/dallas_tx/0-0-0-122870) regulates and enforces transportation-for-hire services in the city. Regulatory and enforcement activities include criminal history checks for drivers, third party vehicle inspections, verification of insurance coverage, citizen complaint investigations, and field inspections.​ The following are copies of the different applications. [**TFH Company Application.pdf**](https://dallascityhall.com/departments/transportation/Documents/Transportation%20regulation/TFH%20Company%20Application_TPW2025.pdf)  
[**TFH Vehicle Application.pdf**](https://dallascityhall.com/departments/transportation/Documents/Transportation%20regulation/TFH%20Vehicle%20Application_TPW2025.pdf)  
[**TFH Driver Application.pdf**](https://dallascityhall.com/departments/transportation/Documents/Transportation%20regulation/TFH%20Driver%20Application_TPW2025.pdf) **TRANSPORTATION FOR HIRE ANNOUNCEMENT** : All Transportation Network/Digital Network Companies and drivers -- including those operating as limousines or sedan rides (Uber Black) -- governed by the State of Texas must apply for the City of Dallas' Transportation-for-Hire company operating authority, driver permit, or vehicle permit(s).  [ **Click here to read the Transportation-for-hire State of Texas Legislation Notice**](https://dallascityhall.com/departments/transportation/Documents/Transportation%20regulation/TFH%20TX%20LEGISLATION%20NOTICE.pdf).
