#  Visit Anaheim Subcontractor Agreements Approved by the Executive Director of Convention, Sports, and Entertainment 
Per the Amended and Restated Agreement with Visit Anaheim, effective June 13, 2025, Visit Anaheim will request and secure the City’s written consent to enter into any contract with any subcontractor for services if the subcontract amount exceeds $150,000 either in an individual contract amount or in an aggregate amount paid to a subcontractor as calculated on annual calendar year basis. Effective November 2025, the award of professional consultant contracts shall be published on the City’s website after an agreement is finalized and are displayed below for a period of 30 days after award. Persons desiring more information about these contracts should contact Tarisa Calato (714) 765-8983. 
* * *
# **MARCH 2026**
### [Tiger House Films](https://www.anaheim.net/DocumentCenter/View/70455/tiger-house-films-3-13-26)
  * Develop and produce a comprehensive suite of high-quality brand storytelling assets that serve as a cornerstone of Visit Anaheim's brand relaunch and the public expression of the destination's new identity. The investment delivers the core creative platform that powers Visit Anaheim’s brand relaunch and supports ongoing destination marketing by providing a scalable, multi-year content library. _Tiger House Films' original contract, approved in February 2026, had a not-to-exceed amount of $333,000. Due to cost increases in casting, visual effects, equipment, music, and location fees, the not-to-exceed amount will need to increase to $370,000._  
**Award Date:** March 25, 2026  
**Award Amount:** $370,000.00


### [Rowe Creative Union](https://www.anaheim.net/DocumentCenter/View/70454/ROWE)
  * Provide creative direction and related services to support the continued development and activation of Anaheim’s evolving brand platform and integrated marketing campaign.  _Rowe Creative Union’s original contract, approved in November 2025, had a not-to-exceed amount of $150,000. This amendment will increase the not-to-exceed amount to $180,000._   
**Award Date:** March 27, 2026  
**Award Amount:** $180,000.00


# **FEBRUARY 2026**
### [Tiger House Films](https://www.anaheim.net/DocumentCenter/View/70091/Tiger-House-Agreement)
  * Provide creative direction and related services to support the continued development and activation of Anaheim’s evolving brand platform and integrated marketing campaign. _Rowe Creative Union’s original contract, approved in October 2025, had a not-to-exceed amount of $150,000. This amendment will increase the not-to-exceed amount to $180,000._  
**Award Date:** February 2, 2026  
**Award Amount:** $333,000.00


# **JANUARY 2026**
### [Tourism Economics](https://www.anaheim.net/DocumentCenter/View/69766/TE-EI-AND-VISITOR-FORECAST-ANALYTICS-AGRMT-126202670)
  * The contractor will develop an Anaheim Economic Impact and Forecast report. The report covers various essential factors, including the total visitor volume and spending by category, the impact on employment and household income, and the federal, state and local tax contributions stemming from visitor activities.   
**Award Date:** January 20, 2026  
**Award Amount:** $92,500.00  
Note: Tourism Economics has existing agreements with Visit Anaheim. The aggregate amount, including this agreement, exceeds $150,000 and is required to be posted on city's website.
