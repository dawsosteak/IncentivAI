#  Youth Programs 
Anaheim’s youth programs provide a safe environment for the youth in our community. Using a variety of activities (such as recreational games, arts, crafts, and special events) we help develop social skills and encourage positive self-image. Our programs will help you keep the kids entertained, physically and emotionally fit, as well as meet new people and enable them to lead constructive lives. To learn more about the services we offer, please click on one of the programs below.  

To find out more about the services we offer, please access the individual programs:  
  

  * [Anaheim STARS (Study Time, Arts, Recreation & Sports)](https://www.anaheim.net/2385/art-in-the-park-and-kids-in-motion-locations)
  * [CampVenture](http://ca-anaheim.civicplus.com/2285/CampVenture)
  * [Fun on Wheels After School Programs ](https://anaheim.net/2240/Fun-on-Wheels)
  * [O.A.K.S. Day Camp](http://ca-anaheim.civicplus.com/1167/OAKS-Day-Camp)
  * [Project SAY (Support Anaheim's Youth)](http://ca-anaheim.civicplus.com/1483/Project-SAY-Support-Anaheims-Youth)
  * [Tiny Tots Academy](https://www.anaheim.net/2287/Tiny-Tots-Academy)
  * [Ramp N' Roll](https://www.anaheim.net/4576/Ramp-N-Roll)
