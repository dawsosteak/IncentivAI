#  Section 3 Contractor Portal 
Anaheim Workforce Connection is available to assist you with meeting your Section 3 requirements or Good Faith Efforts. 
In accordance with subpart 75.15, 75.25 and City requirements, Contractors who receive funding through a City contract are required to meet the Section 3 goals or engage in qualitative efforts to satisfy its benchmark goals. Click [here](https://www.anaheim.net/DocumentCenter/View/57963/City-of-Anaheim-Section-3-Policy-and-Compliance-Plan) to see the City of Anaheim Section 3 Policy and Compliance Plan.
Please complete the following steps to comply with this requirement:
  1. Complete the [Section 3 Contractor Registration Form](https://www.anaheim.net/FormCenter/Anaheim-Workforce-Connection-17/Section-3-Contractor-Registration-Form-252)
  2. Submit your employment opportunities using the [Section 3 Job Order Form](https://www.anaheim.net/FormCenter/Anaheim-Workforce-Connection-17/Section-3-Contractor-Job-Order-Form-251)


  
The Business Services Team at Anaheim Workforce Connection provides assistance in finding qualified candidates with our recruitment services.
We can help you:  
• Coordinate and host job fairs and customized recruitment events  
• Recruit qualified applicants and make candidate referrals  
• Provide a resource room for interviewing and hiring  
• Post job opportunities on our online job boards
For more information, please contact one of our Business Services Representatives at 714-765-4350 or email Section3@anaheim.net.
