#  Disneyland Resort Specific Plan 
The Disneyland Resort Specific Plan (DRSP) was adopted in 1993 and was amended in 2024 in conjunction with the DisneylandForward project. The specific plan encompasses approximately 490 acres of The Anaheim Resort and provides for the development of an international vacation destination resort (The Disneyland Resort®) that allows the development of theme park, hotel and entertainment areas, administrative office facilities, back-of-house facilities, parking facilities, an internal transportation system, and the on-going modification of Disneyland. The DRSP includes four districts: the Theme Park District, the Parking District, the Southeast District, and District A; it also includes two overlays: the C-R Overlay and the Anaheim GardenWalk Overlay.
Environmental Impact Report No. 311 (EIR311) was certified in conjunction with the original adoption of the DRSP. An addendum to EIR 311 was prepared in conjunction with a 1996 amendment to the DRSP in conjunction with the approval of Development Agreement No. 96-01 by and between the City of Anaheim and Walt Disney World Company. Subsequent Environmental Impact Report No. 352 (SEIR352) was approved in 2024 in conjunction with the DisneylandForward project and the First Amended and Restated Development Agreement No. 96-01.
