#  Electric Vehicles 
### Anaheim Public Utilities offers EV rebates and programs to support our customer's path towards electrification. Whether you are new to the process or need hands-on support with your site plans, we're here to help you every step of the way. 
###   

### For more information about Anaheim Public Utilities' future adoption of EV’s in our community, please review our [Integrated Resources Plan](https://www.anaheim.net/4864) and [Southern California Association of Governments study](https://www.anaheim.net/DocumentCenter/View/50587/APU-Southern-California-Association-of-Governments-study).
