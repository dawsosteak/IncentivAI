![Store One Exterior View](https://www.anaheim.net/ImageRepository/Document?documentID=54702)
Store One will be a hub for culinary entrepreneurship and workforce training, built at the former site of the first Northgate González Market at 722 N Anaheim Boulevard. A public-private partnership with the Anaheim Community Foundation, Store One will aim to support food business startups and build the local culinary workforce, while also providing a gathering place that amplifies community through food.
## Follow Our Progress
Follow our journey to Store One’s grand opening! Join our email list [here](https://www.storeoneanaheim.org/contact) for the latest community updates, and go to [storeoneanaheim.org](http://storeoneanaheim.org) to learn more about how Store One will serve Anaheim residents.
If your community organization is interested in learning about partnership opportunities with Store One, please contact programs@storeoneanaheim.org.
