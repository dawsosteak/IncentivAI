#  Commercial - Rebates & Programs 
![EV Truck \(1\)](https://www.anaheim.net/ImageRepository/Document?documentID=47484)

## Description

## Amounts  
---|---|---  
[**Public Access EV Charger Rebate**](https://www.anaheim.net/3312/) | Rebates for the installation of Level 2 or higher plug-in EV chargers that are made available for the general public to use.  |  Up to $3,500 per Level 2 charger for commercial sites, up to maximum of $25,000  
  
Up to $7,500 per charger for schools, affordable housing properties, and DC Fast Chargers, up to a maximum of $40,000  
  
 _Rebates are subject to fund availability._  
[**Private Use EV Charger Rebate**](https://www.anaheim.net/593/) | Rebates for the installation of EV chargers that are for private use at business sites.  | Up to $300 for the Level 2 charger and   
  
Up to $600 for the networked charger on a time-based rate  
  
 _Rebates are subject to fund availability._  
[**EV Fleet**](https://www.anaheim.net/5889/EV-Fleet-Charger-Infrastructure-Rebate)[**Charger**](https://www.anaheim.net/5889/EV-Fleet-Charger-Infrastructure-Rebate)[**Rebate**](https://www.anaheim.net/5889/EV-Fleet-Charger-Infrastructure-Rebate) | Rebates for fleet operators to electrify their fleets. | Up to $5,000 per charger and infrastructure costs for commercial fleet, up to a maximum of $75,000  
  
Up to $10,000 per charger and infrastructure costs for schools, up to a maximum of $75,000  
  
 _Rebates are subject to fund availability._  
[**Municipal EV Charger Assistance Program**](https://www.anaheim.net/DocumentCenter/View/50016/Municipal-EV-Charger-Assistance-Program-Application--Fillable) | Rebates for City of Anaheim facilities that install EV chargers. Subject to application review.  | Up to $10,000 per charger and infrastructure costs for municipal customers, up to a maximum of $50,000  
  
 _Rebates are subject to fund availability._
