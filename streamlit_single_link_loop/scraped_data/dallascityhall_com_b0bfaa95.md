​Authorized HearingS  

DCH Body
Page Content
  

An Authorized Hearing (city-initiated zoning) is a process  initiated by either the City Council or City Plan Commission (CPC) to review and determine the proper zoning for an area, often to align zoning with an adopted future land use plan, correct inconsistencies between zoning and existing land uses, and/or address outdated zoning.    

Learn more about the Authorized Hearing process below   
- [Authorized Hearing prioritization criteria](https://dallascityhall.com/departments/pnv/Documents/AH%20Memos/AH%20Prioritization%20Checklist%20-Scoring%20Criteria.pdf)  
- [Briefing to Economic Development Committee ](https://dallascityhall.com/departments/pnv/Documents/AH%20Memos/AuthHearing%20Program%20Update%20-%20Eco%20Dev%20Cmte%2012-1-2025%20%28002%29.pdf)(12/1/2025)​​

##  **Authorized Hearings - In Progress**
  * [**South Dallas Fair Authorized Hearing (Z223-121)**](https://dallascityhall.com/departments/pnv/Pages/SDFPAH.aspx)  
This authorized hearing is part of the implementation of the South Dallas/Fair Park Area Plan (SDFP), an area plan unanimously adopted by City Council on June 25, 2025. The plan examines existing conditions, sets a future land use and urban design vision, and provides recommendations for how zoning can support that vision.
  * [**Singleton Westmoreland Authorized Hearing**](https://dallascityhall.com/departments/pnv/Pages/singletonwestmorelandah.aspx)**(Z223-174)** - In Process  
This Authorized Hearing area is located in West Dallas and encompasses 256 acres and 518 parcels. The process provides an opportunity to reexamine existing zoning and guide future development in a way that aligns with community values, goals, and the City’s long-term vision for West Dallas
