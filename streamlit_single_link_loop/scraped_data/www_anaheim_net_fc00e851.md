## Current Housing and Community Development Commission Openings 
There are currently two vacancies on the Housing and Community Development Commission, an advisory body to the Housing Authority and its governing Board (the City Council). These two seats are designated for Anaheim residents participating in the Section 8 Housing Choice Voucher Program, regardless of council district. The Commission advises on matters including the Anaheim Housing Authority’s Annual Action Plan, affordable housing strategies, homelessness initiatives, and community development programs. Items reviewed by the Commission are typically forwarded to the City Council for final consideration. For questions or more information, please contact Edwin Puebla at epuebla@anaheim.net or 714-765-4300 ext. 4838.
**Online applications are accepted and can be found[here](https://anaheim.granicus.com/boards/forms/283/apply/4400896?code=21c71612-c153-44c3-8979-c0c4d846afac). **

## CHILDHOOD LEAD POISONING PREVENTION PROGRAM (CLPPP)
Lead is a toxic heavy metal used in many products over time. Did you know that there is no safe level of lead? Even a small amount of lead can cause learning and behavior problems for children. Lead poisoning is one of the most common and preventable environmental diseases in children; therefore, preventing exposure to lead is critical! The Childhood Lead Poisoning Prevention Program (CLPPP) provides case management and environmental investigations, community outreach, and education regarding environmental lead exposures. **To learn more about CLPPP, please visit : <https://www.ochealthinfo.com/lead> **

## Family Self-Sufficiency Program
The FSS Program is a voluntary program available to all participating families where we help them achieve their personal and career goals! AHA provides participants with referrals for supportive services such as job training/education, child care, transportation assistance, budget/debt counseling, and more. And there is a savings bonus provided to those who graduate the program![click here](https://www.anaheim.net/736/Family-Self-Sufficiency-Program).
