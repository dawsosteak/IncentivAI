#  Current Building Codes and Design Criteria 
The 2025 California Building Codes and City of Anaheim Amendments are enforceable for all residential and non-residential construction projects submitted on or after January 1, 2026. Projects submitted prior to January 1, 2026 shall comply with the 2022 Codes. The state codes have been amended by the City of Anaheim to address local topographic, climatic, and/or geologic conditions, and to include provisions pertaining to the administration of these codes. The amendments can be found under Title 15 of the Anaheim Municipal Code.
  * [**City of Anaheim Local Amendments**](https://codelibrary.amlegal.com/codes/anaheim/latest/anaheim_ca/0-0-0-62428)

# Local Design Criteria
  * Seismic Design Category D, E or F as determined by USGS Maps and CBC Chapter 16
  * Wind: 
    * Risk Category I: 90 mph
    * Risk Category II: 95 mph
    * Risk Category III: 100 mph
    * Risk Category IV: 105 mph
  * Climate Zone 8
