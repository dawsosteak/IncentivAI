**Environmental and Social Justice High Road Workforce En Banc**
**  
OCTOBER 17, 2023 | 9:00 a.m. 4:30 p.m.  
Be A Part Of A Conversation That Matters**
You’re Invited to the Environmental and Social Justice High Road Workforce En Banc
Join us for an impactful day of discussions on critical workforce development efforts in key policy areas. 
  * California Public Utilities Commission
  * California Workforce Development Board
  * California Labor & Workforce Development Agency
  * Governor's Office of Tribal Affairs
  * California Energy Commission
  * Office of Energy Infrastructure Safety
  * Local community-based organizations, labor unions, academia, Tribal representatives, and regulated investor-owned utilities


**Where:**
  * **In-person:** The California Endowment’s Regional Office at the Center for Healthy Communities, Adelante Meeting Room, 1414 K Street, Sacramento, CA 95814
