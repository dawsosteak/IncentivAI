#  **This February: Opening our Doors and Raising the Bar**  

Last night, we hosted an open house at our newly renovated office in Basalt and were so energized by the turnout. Seeing our partners, supporters, and community members gathered together was a meaningful way to celebrate how we’ve grown. This new space gives our expanding team the room and resources to better serve our community and advance local climate solutions together.

# Celebrating Our Renovated Basalt Office
After several months of construction, CORE's newly renovated Basalt office is officially open. Yesterday's open house brought familiar faces, new connections, and great conversations about CORE's ambitious plans for 2026. With office renovations complete, CORE is excited to be working with the building owners, Harry Teague Architects, and Basalt River Lofts HOA, to upgrade the building's mechanical system. With assistance from CORE and Holy Cross, the natural gas boilers that heat the building will be replaced with cold-climate air-to-water heat pumps to improve energy efficiency and indoor/outdoor air quality.

# Own or Manage a Commercial or
#  Multifamily Building in Eagle County?  

![](https://lirp.cdn-website.com/06c12080/dms3rep/multi/opt/unnamed+%288%29-1920w.jpg)
New in 2026, CORE's commercial and multifamily programming is now available throughout all of Eagle County, from Vail to Basalt. If you own, manage, or are a part of an HOA for a commercial property or a multifamily building with four or more units, you can access free energy assessments, expert energy advising, and up to $200,000 in grants and rebates for energy efficiency and electrification projects. Commercial and multifamily buildings have the potential to make the biggest impact on reducing greenhouse gas emissions and reaching our climate action goals.
[**Learn more about eligible commercial and multifamily energy efficiency projects**](https://www.aspencore.org/commercial-multifamily-funding).

# You Met the Match & It's Back for 2026
![](https://lirp.cdn-website.com/06c12080/dms3rep/multi/opt/Atlantic+Aviation-1920w.png)
In 2025, Atlantic Aviation generously stepped forward with a $300,000 challenge grant, which inspired $600,000 in donations, for a total impact of $900,000 in philanthropic support for CORE. Given that success, Atlantic Aviation is renewing its commitment for 2026, and all donations to CORE this year will once again have a 1.5 multiplier.

That success and our shared commitment made renewing Atlantic Aviation’s challenge grant for 2026 an easy decision. We are proud to continue this partnership and accelerate practical, on-the-ground climate action across the Roaring Fork Valley." – Jonathan Jones, General Manager, Atlantic Aviation

#  Stop Losing Heat Every Winter
If your home or building is drafty, you could be losing heat and money all winter. To identify solutions, contact CORE to set up a home energy assessment. After an assessment, a CORE Energy Concierge will help you make a plan to improve the energy efficiency of your home through upgrades like air sealing, insulation, or heat pumps. CORE funding helps make improvements more affordable across Pitkin, Eagle, and Garfield Counties.
  

Rebates for homes up to $10,000  
Grants for businesses up to $200,000
  

Home energy assessments are FREE for community priority participants and $100 for all other residences.
  

Upgrade once. Save every winter.
