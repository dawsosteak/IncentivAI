**​​Disaster Preparedness**
DCH Body
Page Content
Disaster preparedness engages residents, families, and neighborhood leaders in interactive discussion and activities on what it means to be ready for any emergency.   

Being prepared is vital for staying safe and weathering any emergency. We encourage that everyone create and review their disasters or emergency preparedness plans, to build resilient homes, businesses, and communities. It's important to know which disasters could affect you and your family and how to create a plan that considers the unique needs of your family and pets.
  

**Know Your Hazards, Build a Kit, Make a Plan:**
​There are three elements to being prepared for emergencies, incidents, and disasters. It's important to [Know Your Hazards](https://dallascityhall.com/departments/officeemergencymanagement/Pages/Threats-and-Hazards.aspx), [Make a Plan](https://dallascityhall.com/departments/officeemergencymanagement/Pages/Make-a-Plan.aspx), and [Build a Kit](https://dallascityhall.com/departments/officeemergencymanagement/Pages/Make-a-Disaster-Kit.aspx). 
You can also learn more about being prepared by [Requesting a Group Presentation](https://dallascityhall.com/departments/officeemergencymanagement/Pages/Request-a-Presentation.aspx).
