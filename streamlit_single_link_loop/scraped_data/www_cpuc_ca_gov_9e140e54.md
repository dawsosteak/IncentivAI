#  Brochures on CPUC Processes
  * [CPUC Overview (English)](https://www.cpuc.ca.gov/-/media/cpuc-website/divisions/news-and-outreach/documents/pao/cpuc_overview_081121.pdf): Provides an overview of the work of the CPUC. [Spanish version](https://www.cpuc.ca.gov/-/media/cpuc-website/divisions/news-and-outreach/documents/pao/cpuc_overview_spanish_081121.pdf).
  * [Understanding a CPUC Proceeding](https://www.cpuc.ca.gov/-/media/cpuc-website/divisions/news-and-outreach/documents/pao/proceedings_081121.pdf): Covers what happens in a proceeding and the types of proceedings. Also explains informal and formal participation.
  * [Participating at a CPUC Voter Meeting](https://www.cpuc.ca.gov/-/media/cpuc-website/divisions/news-and-outreach/documents/pao/voter_meeting_081221.pdf): Covers the rules on making public comments at a CPUC Voting Meeting.
  * [Participating at CPUC Public Participation Hearings and Workshops](https://www.cpuc.ca.gov/-/media/cpuc-website/divisions/news-and-outreach/documents/pao/public_forums_0821.pdf) (English): Explains public participation hearings and workshops. [Spanish version.](https://www.cpuc.ca.gov/-/media/cpuc-website/divisions/news-and-outreach/documents/pao/publicforums_spanish_0323.pdf)
  * [How to Become a Party in a CPUC Proceeding](https://www.cpuc.ca.gov/-/media/cpuc-website/divisions/news-and-outreach/documents/pao/party_081221.pdf): Provides an overview of how to become a party to a CPUC proceeding.
  * [CPUC Intervenor Compensation Program](https://www.cpuc.ca.gov/-/media/cpuc-website/divisions/news-and-outreach/documents/pao/intervenor_081121.pdf): Provides an overview of the CPUC's Intervenor Compensation Program, which is for formal participants in CPUC proceedings.
  * [Ex Parte Communications at the CPUC](https://www.cpuc.ca.gov/-/media/cpuc-website/divisions/news-and-outreach/documents/pao/exparte_081121.pdf): Provides an overview of ex parte communications, which must be followed by those wishing to communicate with CPUC decision-makers.
  * [Filing a Formal Complaint and Alternative Dispute Resolution at the CPUC](https://www.cpuc.ca.gov/-/media/cpuc-website/divisions/news-and-outreach/documents/pao/formal-complaints_081121.pdf): Provides an overview of the CPUC's formal complaint process and Alternate Dispute Resolution Program. [Spanish version.](https://www.cpuc.ca.gov/-/media/cpuc-website/divisions/news-and-outreach/documents/pao/formal-complaints_spanish_032823.pdf)
  * [Resolving an Issue with a Utility](https://www.cpuc.ca.gov/-/media/cpuc-website/divisions/news-and-outreach/documents/pao/cab_081121.pdf) (English): The CPUC’s Informal Process: Covers the informal complaint process, which helps customers resolve issues with utility companies under CPUC jurisdiction. [Spanish version.](https://www.cpuc.ca.gov/-/media/cpuc-website/divisions/news-and-outreach/documents/pao/cab_spanish_022823.pdf)
  * [Inform CPUC Decision-Making by Providing Public Comments](https://www.cpuc.ca.gov/-/media/cpuc-website/divisions/news-and-outreach/documents/pao/publiccomments_0821.pdf) (English): Information on how to comment on the CPUC proceedings. [Spanish version](https://www.cpuc.ca.gov/-/media/cpuc-website/divisions/news-and-outreach/documents/pao/publiccomments_spanish_0323.pdf).
  * [Broadband Deployment, Affordability, and Digital Equity Programs](https://www.cpuc.ca.gov/-/media/cpuc-website/industries-and-topics/documents/telecommunications/broadband-overview_060922.pdf): Broadband programs overview brochure
