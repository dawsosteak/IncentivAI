#  Partners & Community Services 
![Community-Partnership-Banner_1028x228-pixels](https://www.anaheim.net/ImageRepository/Document?documentID=42803)
Anaheim Workforce Connection partners with many different agencies throughout Orange County and the surrounding areas. By working together we are able to help our community reach their employment goals. Please visit our partner links to learn more about the services they provide.
