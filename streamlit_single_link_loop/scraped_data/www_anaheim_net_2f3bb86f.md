#  Ticket Disclosure (Form 802) 
In accordance with the provisions set forth in Section 18944.1 of the [Fair Political Practices Commission](http://www.fppc.ca.gov/), view the [City’s ticket policy](https://www.anaheim.net/DocumentCenter/View/270). [Ticket disclosure](http://nf4.netfile.com/pub/AGR.aspx?aid=ANA) ([Form 802](https://www.anaheim.net/DocumentCenter/View/271)) is posted within 30-days after distribution of tickets.
