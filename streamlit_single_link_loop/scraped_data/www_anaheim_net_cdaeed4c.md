# OCVibe
![MEADOW PARK](https://www.anaheim.net/ImageRepository/Document?documentID=35098)
![project map edit](https://www.anaheim.net/ImageRepository/Document?documentId=35100)**OCVibe** is a **92****-acre master planned campus** taking shape around Honda Center with shopping, dining, sports, entertainment, open space, apartments, offices, parking and more underway now and unfolding in the next few years.
The $4 billion project is bringing **the kind of excitement and experiences** seen around arenas and stadiums in San Diego, Sacramento, Los Angeles and other cities while also being uniquely Anaheim.
OCVibe falls under of Anaheim’s **planning for the Platinum Triangle** ― the area around Honda Center, Angel Stadium of Anaheim and the ARTIC transit center. Our planning for the area calls for a **modern urban village** built around**sports and entertainment** for our residents and all of Orange County to enjoy.
Development in the Platinum Triangle brings **added revenue for city services** and new **entertainment, shopping, dining, workplaces and parks and open space** for Anaheim residents. 
OCVibe, proposed and financed by the owners of the Anaheim Ducks and operators of Honda Center with no city funding, was unanimously approved by the **Anaheim C****ity Council with votes in 2022 and 2024.**
