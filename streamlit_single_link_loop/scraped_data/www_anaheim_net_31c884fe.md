#  Anaheim Chief Communications Officer: Mike Lyster 
Mike Lyster serves as chief communications officer for the city of Anaheim, one of California's largest cities with 350,000 residents, 25 million yearly visitors and 20,000 businesses.
He joined as chief communications officer in 2015.
Lyster oversees city communications, including working with the media, social media, AnaheimTV, Anaheim magazine, newsletters, graphic design and other types of information sharing.
Lyster works directly with Anaheim's mayor, City Council and departments. He also works with residents, businesses and community groups in the city, all with a goal of informing people about Anaheim.
Anaheim Communications is part of the city manager's office and represents Anaheim Public Utilities, the city attorney's office and works with city departments across Anaheim. 
Before coming to Anaheim, Lyster served as vice president of corporate communications for the Irvine Company, one of the nation's largest real estate developers and owners.
He started his career as a journalist, including serving for more than a decade as editor of the Orange County Business Journal.
He grew up going to Angels games, Disneyland and riding his bike or taking the bus to Anaheim Plaza, among other many fond Anaheim memories.
