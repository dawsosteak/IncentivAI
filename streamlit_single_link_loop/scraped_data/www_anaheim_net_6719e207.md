### **Congregate Lunch Program**
The Lunch Café serves hot meals every Tuesday, Wednesday, and Thursday at 11:00 a.m., following federal nutritional guidelines. Open to anyone 60 or older, the café suggests a $3.00 donation, but all are welcome regardless of ability to pay. Guests under 60 can enjoy lunch for $5.00. The program also includes social activities, such as dancing to a live band on most Thursdays. Located at Brookhurst Community Center, 2271 W. Crescent Ave., Anaheim, CA 92801. Free transportation to and from lunch is available. For more information, please call 714-765-4511
### **Programs, Classes, and Activities**
Discover a wide range of activities across Anaheim tailored to every skill set and interest for our active older adults.
### **Fitness and Wellness**
Longevity Stick, Qigong, and Tai Chi enhance your balance, flexibility, strength, mental focus, and breathing with simple, standing movements. Bring your own stick. Classes are available at the Downtown Anaheim Active Older Adult Center and West Anaheim Youth Center.
### **Games & Social Activities**
Enjoy a variety of games at our program locations, including Bridge, Billiards, Pinochle, Canasta, Chess, Bingo, Mahjong, and Bobble Ball. Join the fun at the Downtown Anaheim Active Older Adult Center and East Anaheim Community Center.
### **Educational Classes**
Expand your horizons with classes in Art, Tone Chime Choir, English as a Second Language, Crochet, Line Dance, and Digital Literacy. A wide range of short-term classes on various topics is also available at centers throughout the city.
### **Athletic & Recreational Activities**
Stay active with Health and Wellness exercise groups for all fitness levels. Activities include Volleyball, Pickleball, Zumba Gold, and Table Tennis, offered at multiple locations citywide. Whether you’re a beginner or experienced, you’ll find something to help strengthen your muscle tone and flexibility. Locations include East Anaheim Gymnasium, Downtown Anaheim Active Older Adult Center, Downtown Anaheim Youth Center, and West Anaheim Youth Center.
