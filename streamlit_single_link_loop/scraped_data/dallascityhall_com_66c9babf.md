​​Permit Information  

Food establishment permits are required for restaurants, bars, grocery stores, schools and other retail food establishments.  
The applicant is responsible for scheduling and coordinating all required health inspections with their permitting inspector after application.  
For additional information, contact the Consumer Health Division from 8 am to 12 pm and 1 pm to 4 pm at (214) 670-8083. ***New construction, change in ownership, remodels or changing the use of an existing facility please contact the [Sustainable Development & Construction ](https://dallascityhall.com/departments/sustainabledevelopment/buildinginspection/Pages/index.aspx)at 320 E. Jefferson Blvd. or call 214-670-4127.*** | |  Fixed Food Establishment Forms & Fees
