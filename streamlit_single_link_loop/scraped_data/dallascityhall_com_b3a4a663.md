## Overview
The Thoroughfare Plan and the Central Business District Streets and Vehicular Circulation Plan (CBD Plan) are long range roadway planning documents. These plans determine the required right-of-way and designation/operation of the roadways within the Dallas city limits.  

[ ![Interactive Thoroughfare Plan Map](https://dallascityhall.com/departments/transportation/PublishingImages/Public%20Works/interactive-plan-map.jpg)](https://dallasgis.maps.arcgis.com/apps/webappviewer/index.html?id=61c4bfdad6ec4c85bc794dad5044288e)Click here to view the [City of Dallas Thoroughfare Plan](https://dallascityhall.com/departments/transportation/DCH%20Documents/Transportation_Planning/pdf/ThoroughfarePlan.pdf "City of Dallas Thoroughfare Plan'") (adopted June 1993). A table [of streets covered by the thoroughfare is available here.](https://dallascityhall.com/departments/transportation/DCH%20Documents/Transportation_Planning/CPCTC/2025-07_ThoroughfarePlan_Table.pdf "Table of streets covered by the City of Dallas Thoroughfare Plan \(PDF format\)")  

[Here is a copy of the City of Dallas CBD Streets and Vehicular Ciruclation Plan](https://dallascityhall.com/departments/transportation/DCH%20Documents/Transportation_Planning/pdf/DALLAS_CBD_PLAN.pdf "City of Dallas Central Business District Streets and Vehicular Circulation Plan"), first adopted in 1971 and updated in 1988.​ Also download[ a table of streets included in the CBD Plan](https://dallascityhall.com/departments/transportation/DCH%20Documents/Transportation_Planning/pdf/2023-09_CBD_Plan_Table.pdf "Table of streets covered by the CBD Vehicular Circulation Plan \(PDF format\)").​  

[View a GIS map of the Thoroughfare and CBD plans](https://dallasgis.maps.arcgis.com/apps/webappviewer/index.html?id=61c4bfdad6ec4c85bc794dad5044288e "GIS Map of the Thoroughfare and CBD plans").  

To apply for an amendment to the Thoroughfare Plan or CBD Plan, [download a Plan Amendment Application* **​​​​**](https://dallascityhall.com/departments/transportation/Documents/Thoroughfare_CBD_Plan_Application%2020260122.pdf "Thoroughfare/CBD Plan Amendment Application")**.**
