## Digital Media Campaigns  

[Stay On Guard ](https://dallascityhall.com/departments/communication-outreach-marketing/Pages/Stay-On-Guard.aspx)  
Stay On Guard Dallas is a bilingual public awareness campaign that engages residents, community leaders and organizations to slow the spread of COVID-19 through personal testimonials. We challenge you to Stay on Guard Dallas, as businesses reopen, students return to campuses and families celebrate holidays.  
  
[Equity and Inclusion Toolkit](https://dallascityhall.com/DCH%20Documents/covid19/City%20of%20Dallas%20%20-%20Equity%20and%20Inclusion%20Toolkit.pdf)  
The City of Dallas has launched an Equity and Inclusion social media campaign. The campaign is intended to share important messaging that helps ensure we remember that #WeAreAllinThisTogether during the COVID-19 pandemic. Please click the link below to download the toolkit and share the content and graphics.
