#  External Wireless Networks 
Major cell phone companies (AT&T, Verizon, and T-Mobile) are working to improve service for their customers in Anaheim. As part of these improvements, they are replacing some of the streetlight poles to allow installing their wireless equipment inside an enclosure at the top. These installations are referred to as Small Cell Sites. To streamline the process, APU has adopted a standard license agreement and wireless-ready streetlight poles shown under the following links:
  

  * [Sample Street Light License Agreement](http://www.anaheim.net/DocumentCenter/View/23292/Sample-Street-Light-License-Agreement-and-Rate-for-Wireless-Deployment)
  * [L 146: Marbelite Wireless-Ready Pole](https://www.anaheim.net/DocumentCenter/View/30056/L-146-Marbelite-Wireless-Ready-Pole)
  * [L 144: Tranditionaire Wireless-Ready Pole](https://www.anaheim.net/DocumentCenter/View/30057/L-144-Traditionaire-Wireless-Ready-Pole)
  * [L 147: Proposed Anaheim Resort Wireless-Ready Pole](https://www.anaheim.net/DocumentCenter/View/30058/L-147-Proposed-Anaheim-Resort-Wireless-Ready-Pole)
  * [L 148: Downtown Anaheim Wireless-Ready Pole](https://www.anaheim.net/DocumentCenter/View/30134/L-148--Downtown-Anaheim-Wireless-Ready-Pole)
