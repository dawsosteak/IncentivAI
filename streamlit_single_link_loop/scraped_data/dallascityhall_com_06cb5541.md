# 2026 MPO Pool Operation Class Schedule Now Avaliabl‚Äã‚Äãe ‚Äã  

Chapter 43A of the Dallas City Code requires all pool and spa facilities to have a certified Manager of Operations. Certifications must be renewed every two (2) years. All certification courses are provided by the Department of Code Compliance. 
To register for a class, please complete and submit the enclosed Manager of Pool Operations ‚Äì Class Registration Form to the Department of Code Compliance. Be sure to indicate your preferred class date on the form. A separate registration form is required for each individual attending. 
The registration fee is $50.00 per person for both City of Dallas residents and non-residents. Please submit your completed registration form along with a check or money order made payable to the City of Dallas. 
Payments must be made in person or mailed to:   
Special Collections Division   
1500 Marilla St., Suite 2DS   
Dallas, Texas 75201 
_**Payments will not be accepted at the class. All registration fees are non-refundable.**_
To cancel or substitute a registration, you must notify Code Compliance staff by phone at (2‚Äã14) 671-2791 at least three (3) business days prior to your scheduled class. Failure to do so will result in forfeiture of payment. 
Spanish-language instruction is available on designated dates. All classes are held from 9:00 a.m. to 1:00 p.m. 
For additional information, please contact the Code Compliance Multi-Tenant Inspection/Pool Program at (214) 671-2791.
