Page Content
An inventory of all trees has never been completed within the Dallas city limits. TreeKeeper tree inventory software will allow multiple departments to input data across the city.
City staff have been diligently working on tree inventory, with focus on ash trees and tagging significant ash trees for treatment, since initial discovery of the Emerald Ash Borer beetle in Dallas County in 2022. If you would like to check it out, click on the link below to the TreeKeeper website!
