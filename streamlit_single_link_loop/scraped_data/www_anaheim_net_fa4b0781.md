  1. [What is Amnesty Month?](https://www.anaheim.net/6583/Amnesty-Month#tabd107d8bb-2bc8-4631-8cba-c299aa0666aa_0)


We are pleased to announce that the Anaheim Public Library is now fine-free and you can enjoy your library card without ever having to worry about paying overdue fees. During Amnesty Month, we invite the community to rediscover the joy of reading by visiting your most convenient library branch to have your entire outstanding balance cleared!* Take this opportunity for a fresh start and delve into a world of imagination, stories, knowledge, and learning. 
  

*Charges related to STEAM science kits, Ukuleles, and/or Chromebooks are excluded.
  

  1. [What does fine-free mean?](https://www.anaheim.net/6583/Amnesty-Month#tabd107d8bb-2bc8-4631-8cba-c299aa0666aa_1)


Anaheim Library is now fine-free! We are no longer collecting fees for media materials* and charging overdue fines.
  

  * *Fees for video games not included


  1. [Where can I go to have my fees waived?](https://www.anaheim.net/6583/Amnesty-Month#tabd107d8bb-2bc8-4631-8cba-c299aa0666aa_2)


You can visit any Anaheim Public Library branch and see the Circulation Desk to have your account fees waived. Please visit the **[Library Locations](https://anaheim.net/903/Locations-Hours)** page for a list of all our branches. 
  1. [Will I still be able to renew items?](https://www.anaheim.net/6583/Amnesty-Month#tabd107d8bb-2bc8-4631-8cba-c299aa0666aa_3)


Anaheim Library auto-renews patrons’ items on the due date up to two times. If the item has a hold, the item will not be renewed and is expected to return to the library.
  1. [Will I accrue fines after September?](https://www.anaheim.net/6583/Amnesty-Month#tabd107d8bb-2bc8-4631-8cba-c299aa0666aa_4)


No, as long as materials are returned by their due date. Patrons who do not return their library items will be responsible for the replacement cost, which will be charged 30 days after the item's due date. 
If an item is returned damaged, the patron will be responsible for a damage fee. Damage fees will vary by item. 
  1. [How much is the replacement cost of an item?](https://www.anaheim.net/6583/Amnesty-Month#tabd107d8bb-2bc8-4631-8cba-c299aa0666aa_5)


Replacement costs vary by item.
