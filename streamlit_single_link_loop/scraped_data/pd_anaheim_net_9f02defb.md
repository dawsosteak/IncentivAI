#  Personnel Complaint / Compliment 
## Message from the Chief of Police
Professionalism is what our community should expect from every member of the Anaheim Police Department. Your willingness to share your concerns helps us uphold this standard and maintain trust.
Any allegation that a member may have fallen short of this expectation is taken seriously and reviewed without bias, with respect for everyone involved. Each matter is given a thorough review, guided by department policy and the law. We appreciate you taking the time to bring this to our attention and allowing us the opportunity to review it.
MANNY CID
CHIEF OF POLICE
### [FAQs](https://pd.anaheim.net/Faq.aspx?TID=16) 

[ How do I file a compliment? ](https://pd.anaheim.net/178/Personnel-Complaint-Compliment) 
    
If you want to share your experience and provide us with your feedback to help us recognize our department personnel for delivering exceptional service, please [complete the online form in English](https://pd.anaheim.net/FormCenter/Customer-Service-5/Customer-Service-Personnel-Compliment-En-46) or [complete the online form in Spanish](https://pd.anaheim.net/FormCenter/Customer-Service-5/Servicio-al-Cliente-Elogio-de-un-Emplead-47). 

[ How do I file a complaint? ](https://pd.anaheim.net/178/Personnel-Complaint-Compliment) 
    
If you wish to file a formal complaint, please complete a Personnel Complaint Form. You may obtain this form at any of these locations:
  * Any police department station
  * The City Clerk's Office
  * Any Anaheim Public Library
  * The Community Services Office
  * Print the [Personal Complaint Form (PDF)](https://pd.anaheim.net/DocumentCenter/View/139)


All forms should be submitted to:
Anaheim Police Department  
425 S Harbor Boulevard  
Anaheim, CA 92805
Or, you can [complete the form online in English](https://pd.anaheim.net/FormCenter/Customer-Service-5/Customer-Service-Personnel-Complaint-Eng-49), or [complete the form online in Spanish](https://pd.anaheim.net/FormCenter/Customer-Service-5/Customer-Service-Personnel-Complaint-Spa-48). 

[ What will happen to the officer / employee? ](https://pd.anaheim.net/178/Personnel-Complaint-Compliment) 
    
It will depend on the situation and what the officer's or employee's actions were. If the employee's actions were criminal, he / she will be dealt with in the same manner as any other citizen. If the officer's actions were improper but not criminal, he / she may be disciplined. 

[ Will I be told of the results of the investigation? ](https://pd.anaheim.net/178/Personnel-Complaint-Compliment) 
    
Yes. You will receive a letter from the Office of the Chief of Police advising you of the disposition of your complaint.
