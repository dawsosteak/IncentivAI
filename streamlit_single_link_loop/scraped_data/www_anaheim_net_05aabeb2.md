#  SB 205 Stormwater Discharge Compliance 
Senate Bill 205 (SB 205), signed by Governor Gavin Newson, requires a person or entity applying for a new or renewed business license to demonstrate enrollment in an NPDES permit, if such a permit is required.
SB 205 applies to all applications for initial business licenses and business license renewals submitted on and after January 1, 2020.
Completion and submission of the **SB 205 Business Licenses Stormwater Discharge Compliance Form** is required with your new 2020 business license and renewals.
