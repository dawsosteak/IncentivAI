#  Heat Pump Incentives Program 
###    
Replacing your old and inefficient heat pump units can save more money than you think.
  

Incentives are available for installing new high-efficiency heat pump (HP) units to replace existing commercial heat pump systems. As your current unit approaches or reaches its typical useful life of about 15 years, upgrading to a new, high-efficiency heat pump can reduce electricity use by 40–60% compared to the existing system—resulting in significant long-term savings.
  

**Units Type** | **Units Ton Range** | **Efficiency Rating** | **Incentive Amount** | **Maximum Incentive**  
---|---|---|---|---  
PTHP Units | ≤ 2.5-Ton |  20% > Minimum EER from Title -24 standards | $100 per unit |    
  
limited Incentive amount, up to $75,000, per customer per year  
Package HP | 2.0- ≤ 5.3-Ton AC | ≥15 thru ≥17 | $100 - $400 per Ton  
Package HP | ≥ 5.4-Ton & Greater AC |  ≥ 9.5 EER thru ≥ 11.0 EER 12.5 IEER thru ≥ 14.1 IEER   
| $0.15/ kWh annual savings or $400/ kW, whichever is less  
  

  

  

If you are ready to replace your old unit with a new high-efficiency heat pump unit, please download the following information and contact us about your project. All projects are subject to funding availability.
  

### Program Steps
  

  * Download the Program Guidelines and [Heat Pump Incentive Table](https://www.anaheim.net/DocumentCenter/View/26609/ABEES-Heat-Pump-Incentives-Table-FY-20-21) documents.
  * Download the [Heat Pump Application](https://www.anaheim.net/DocumentCenter/View/26608/Heat-Pump-Application) (Rename your workbook using your account address and name)
  * Please download and complete all required forms (including the tax forms - W-9).
  * Questions about the program details, simply fill out our [online form](https://www.anaheim.net/FormCenter/Public-Utilities-6/Energy-Efficiency-Solutions-61). A representative will contact you within 2-3 business days.
