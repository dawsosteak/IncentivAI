#  Vendor Outreach Day Registration 
Are you interested in doing business with the city? Join us for **Vendor Outreach Day** , an opportunity to meet with procurement staff, ask questions, and more. 
We are working on getting these scheduled on a quarterly basis. New dates to be published in the Fall.
