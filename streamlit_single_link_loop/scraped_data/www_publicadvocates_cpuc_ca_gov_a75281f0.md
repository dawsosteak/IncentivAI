#  Press Room
We strive to ensure our work is as accessible and transparent as possible. Please find our most recent publications below. 
## [ Reports & Analyses ](https://www.publicadvocates.cpuc.ca.gov/press-room/reports-and-analyses)
Our reports, memos, and other analyses on energy, water, and communications topics 
## [ Commentary ](https://www.publicadvocates.cpuc.ca.gov/press-room/commentary)
Our take on trending topics affecting customers 
## [ Legislative Bill Position Letters ](https://www.publicadvocates.cpuc.ca.gov/legislation/letters-to-the-legislature)
Our positions on proposed state legislation that impact utility regulation 
## [ In the News ](https://www.publicadvocates.cpuc.ca.gov/press-room/in-the-news)
Our press releases and latest news articles 
## [ Proceedings and Filings ](https://www.publicadvocates.cpuc.ca.gov/search#f:sections=\[Proceedings\])
Our proceeding library provides access to our filings for current and past proceedings
