# **Energy Spotlight: The Osburn Family**
![](https://lirp.cdn-website.com/06c12080/dms3rep/multi/opt/unnamed+%284%29-1920w.jpg)
Beth and Shannan Osburn transformed their 1980 mobile home in Lazy Glen with CORE’s support and Holy Cross Energy rebates covering 75% of costs. Step by step, they installed a heat pump, insulation, a programmable thermostat, an upgraded electrical panel, and more. Once challenged by temperature swings, their home now stays comfortable year-round with lower utility bills. Their journey proves how accessible energy upgrades can truly improve lives.
