The Dallas City Council believes it is in the interest of public health, safety, and welfare to regulate and license boarding home facilities. The ordinance adding [Chapter 8](https://codelibrary.amlegal.com/codes/dallas/latest/dallas_tx/0-0-0-3278), "Boarding Home Facilities" and amending of Chapter 33 regulating "Personal Care Facilities" were amended and approved on May 27, 2015. 
For additional information or questions, please contact the Boarding Home Facilities Office at 214-670-1972.
