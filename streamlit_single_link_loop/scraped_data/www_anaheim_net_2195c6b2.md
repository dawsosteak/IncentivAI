#  Water Conservation Student Poster Contest 
### This contest is now closed. The next contest will reopen in 2027. 
### Stay tuned for more details. 
![2025 Student Water Poster Contest Banner](https://www.anaheim.net/ImageRepository/Document?documentID=67986)
Celebrate and explore your creativity by participating in the Annual Water Conservation Student Poster Contest. Anaheim Public Utilities invites**all 1st through 8th grade students, attending a public or private school located in Anaheim,** to express their creativity and inspire the community to save water for a sustainable future.
## Rules:  
  

  * Theme: **Being Water Wise Is...**
  * Bold, bright, brilliant colors are best.
  * Check for correct spelling. Posters with misspelled words will not be selected.
  * Posters should reflect students' own ability.
  * Artwork with copyrighted characters or images (e.g., SpongeBob, Mickey Mouse, etc.) will be disqualified.


## Submit:  
  

  * ### Mail or drop-off to: 
**Anaheim Public Utilities**  
**Attention: Student Poster Contest**  
**201 S. Anaheim Blvd., 8th Floor**  
**Anaheim, CA 92805**
  * Artwork must be 12” x 18” drawn on white paper in landscape format.
  * Do not fold or roll the artwork.
  * Questions? Call 714-765-4453.  
  
Note: Schools and students submitting winning entries will be notified by mail or telephone.
