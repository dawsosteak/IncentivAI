#  Seasonal Book Lists 
##  **Summer 2025****[![The Counselors](https://www.anaheim.net/ImageRepository/Document?documentId=65599)The Counselors by Jessica Goodman](https://ana.ent.sirsi.net/client/en_US/virtual/search/detailnonmodal/ent:$002f$002fSD_ILS$002f0$002fSD_ILS:2742319/one?qu=The+counselors+Jessica+Gooman)**
Working at the summer camp you attended when you were younger is not as easy as it sounds. Your rich best friends are camp counselors and lifeguards there as well but when a dead body shows up in the lake, a mystery reveals the complexities of a secret.
  

  

  

  

  

## **![Five Survive](https://anaheim.net/ImageRepository/Document?documentId=65598)**
### [**Five Survive by Holly Jackson**](https://ana.ent.sirsi.net/client/en_US/virtual/search/results?qu=Five+survive+Holly+Jackson&te=)
On break from school, five friends take a road trip in a borrowed RV. It does not go well, for both the RV and the five friends in this suspenseful page-turner. 
  

  

  

  

  

  

**![Long Story Short](https://anaheim.net/ImageRepository/Document?documentId=65597)**
### [**Long Story Short by Serena Kaylor**](https://ana.ent.sirsi.net/client/en_US/virtual/search/detailnonmodal/ent:$002f$002fSD_ILS$002f0$002fSD_ILS:2747928/one?qu=Long+story+short+Serena+Kaylor)
Getting into your dream college is sometimes not the best thing in the world, especially if your parents want you to practice your social skills before they let you go! Cringe. Off to theatre camp where humor, intelligence, and a light romance make this _School Library Journal_ starred novel a refreshing read. 
  

  

  

  

### [**![Mermaid Scales](https://www.anaheim.net/ImageRepository/Document?documentId=65596)Mermaid Scales and the Town of Sand by Yoko Komori**](https://ana.ent.sirsi.net/client/en_US/virtual/search/detailnonmodal/ent:$002f$002fSD_ILS$002f0$002fSD_ILS:2765588/one?qu=mermaid+scales+and+the+town+of+sand)
This calming graphic novel tells the story of Tokiko, who moves with her father from Tokyo to a small seaside town after her mother abandons them. At school Toki makes friends with a fellow outsider who is open to fantasy and exploration but decidedly not the existence of merfolk. Toki's belief in merfolk lingers in this quiet story about loss and hope.
  

  

  

  

  

### [**![The Summer I Ate the Rich](https://www.anaheim.net/ImageRepository/Document?documentId=65595)The Summer I Ate the Rich by Maika Moulite and Maritza Moulite**](https://ana.ent.sirsi.net/client/en_US/virtual/search/results?qu=The+summer+I+ate+the+rich&te=)
Haitian zombie folklore inspires the tone of this revenge horror novel. The protagonist is technically a zombie but not the insane, brainless type. She is smart, crafty, and supportive of her mother in their struggle to financially survive in modern day Miami.
  

  

  

  

  

  

### [**![Slip: a graphic novel](https://www.anaheim.net/ImageRepository/Document?documentId=65607)Slip: a graphic novel by Marika McCoola and illustrated by Aatmaja Pandya**](https://ana.ent.sirsi.net/client/en_US/virtual/search/detailnonmodal/ent:$002f$002fSD_ILS$002f0$002fSD_ILS:2740388/one?qu=Slip+Marika+McCoola)
Before Jade, the protagonist, goes away to a summer art camp, her BFF is hospitalized. Jade tries to focus on her animated ceramic work until she hits a wall creatively and emotionally. She perseveres and it is illustrated brilliantly. _Slip_ appeared on the Young Adult Library Association's 2023 "Great Graphic Novels for Teens" list.
  

  

  

  

  

  

## **![Run Away with Me](https://anaheim.net/ImageRepository/Document?documentId=65594)**
### [**Run Away with Me by Brian Selznick**](https://ana.ent.sirsi.net/client/en_US/virtual/search/results?qu=Run+away+with+me+Brian+Selznick&te=)
Beautiful love story that takes place in Rome, Italy, where a teen American boy is stuck for a summer while his mom does her book preservation work. Author Selznick also illustrates stunning black and white images adding layers of mystery and depth to this critically acclaimed novel. 
  

  

  

  

  

  

### [**![Night Swimming](https://www.anaheim.net/ImageRepository/Document?documentId=65593)Night Swimming by Aaron Starmer**](https://ana.ent.sirsi.net/client/en_US/virtual/search/detailnonmodal/ent:$002f$002fSD_ILS$002f0$002fSD_ILS:2780930/one?qu=Night+swimming+Aaron+starmer)
It's the summer of 1994, in hot and humid upstate New York, where two high school graduates take to the pools of their neighborhood every night. The friends discuss many topics from a fear of the unknown to mix-tape music with a will-they-won't-they romantic feeling in the air.
  

  

  

  

  

## **![Breathe and Count Back from Ten](https://anaheim.net/ImageRepository/Document?documentId=65600)**
### [**Breathe and Count Back from Ten by Natalia Sylvester**](https://ana.ent.sirsi.net/client/en_US/virtual/search/results?qu=Breathe+and+count+back+from+ten+Natalia+Sylvester&te=)
Swimming is more than a recreational activity for Veronica. With hip dysplasia, the water provides a sense of freedom, safety, and possible employment--that is if she can keep it a secret from her over-protective parents. This 2023 Pura Belpré Award-winning novel delivers a message of empowerment and authenticity.
  

  

  

  

  

### [**![Brownstone](https://www.anaheim.net/ImageRepository/Document?documentId=65592)Brownstone by Samuel Teer and illustrated by Mar Julia**](https://ana.ent.sirsi.net/client/en_US/virtual/search/results?qu=Brownstone+Samuel+Teer+Mar+Julia&te=)
Winner of the 2025 Printz Award, _Brownstone_ is a realistic graphic novel about identity. Familial, cultural, you name it, protagonist Almudena is stuck figuring it out with a father she does not know for a whole summer. The emotional facial expressions drawn in the vivid comic panels help in making this story unforgettable.
  

  

  

  

### [**![Never Coming Home](https://www.anaheim.net/ImageRepository/Document?documentId=65591)Never Coming Home by Kate Williams**](https://ana.ent.sirsi.net/client/en_US/virtual/search/detailnonmodal/ent:$002f$002fSD_ILS$002f0$002fSD_ILS:2743631/one?qu=Never+coming+home+Kate+Williams)
A group of social media teen influencers are selected to go on a free vacation to a resort on "Unknown Island" where the situation is less than ideal. There is no Wi-Fi in the resort, no air-conditioning, and the only food option is junk food. Plus, there's a murder and there may be more murders in this suspenseful thriller.
  

  

  

  

  1. [Winter 2024](https://www.anaheim.net/6634/Seasonal-Book-Lists#tab423f29cb-f716-4bf0-a50e-a31547aabbeb_0)


![Two boys holding each other face to face](https://www.anaheim.net/ImageRepository/Document?documentId=58848)
### [**Icebreaker by A.L. Graziadei**](https://ana.ent.sirsi.net/client/en_US/virtual/search/results?qu=icebreaker&te=)
Heavy NHL hockey aspirations, depression, and a complicated romance for two teen boys during their first semester of college. Michael. L. Printz Award honoree.
  

  

  

  

  

  

![Book Cover with the San Francisco Bridge and sky line](https://www.anaheim.net/ImageRepository/Document?documentId=58849)
###  [**The Coldest Winter I Ever Spent by Ann Jacobus**](https://ana.ent.sirsi.net/client/en_US/virtual/search/detailnonmodal/ent:$002f$002fSD_ILS$002f0$002fSD_ILS:2773688/one?qu=The+Coldest+I+ever+Spent)
It can get awfully chilly during the summertime in San Francisco especially when dealing with some emotionally hard issues. A teen girl survives new sobriety, depression, and anxiety, all while living with her terminally ill aunt before going away to college. 
  

  

  

  

  

  

![every reason](https://www.anaheim.net/ImageRepository/Document?documentId=58914)
### [**Every Reason We Shouldn't by Sara Fujimura**](https://ana.ent.sirsi.net/client/en_US/virtual/search/detailnonmodal/ent:$002f$002fSD_ILS$002f0$002fSD_ILS:2703032/one?qu=Every+Reason+We+Shouldn%27t)
A sixteen-year-old competitive figure skater returns to "normal" high school and has to help out her parents' struggling independent business-- an ice rink. She meets a boy there who is a speed skater and a light romance ensues. 
  

  

  

  

  

  

![those pink cover](https://www.anaheim.net/ImageRepository/Document?documentId=58913)
### [**Those Pink Mountain Nights by Jen Ferguson**](https://ana.ent.sirsi.net/client/en_US/virtual/search/detailnonmodal/ent:$002f$002fSD_ILS$002f0$002fSD_ILS:2773223/one?qu=Those+pink+mountain)
Friends in chilly Alberta, Canada work together at Pink Mountain Pizza where they organize over difficult topics including the disappearance of their friend-- a missing Indigenous teen girl.
  

  

  

  

  

  

![83 days in](https://www.anaheim.net/ImageRepository/Document?documentId=58912)
### [**83 Days in Mariupol by Don Brown**](https://ana.ent.sirsi.net/client/en_US/virtual/search/detailnonmodal/ent:$002f$002fSD_ILS$002f0$002fSD_ILS:2749436/one?qu=83+days+in+Mariupol+%3A+A+war+diary+%2F+%5Bgraphic+novel%5D)
It's freezing cold in Mariupol, Ukraine when the Russian troops started attacking the city in 2022. With no heat, water, or electricity, how did some of the innocent people survive? Read this 2023 Blue Ribbon award (_The Bulletin of the Center for Children's Books)_ to find out and remember.
  

  

  

  

  

![check plesae](https://www.anaheim.net/ImageRepository/Document?documentId=58920)
### [**Check Please!, Book 1: #Hockey by Ngozi Ukazu**](https://ana.ent.sirsi.net/client/en_US/virtual/search/detailnonmodal/ent:$002f$002fSD_ILS$002f0$002fSD_ILS:2693920/one?qu=Check+please%21+%2F+%5Bgraphic+novel%5D&dt=list)
Humorous webcomic turned into a graphic novel series about teen boy on a hockey scholarship during his first semester of college. 
  

  

  

  

  

  

![junkwraith](https://www.anaheim.net/ImageRepository/Document?documentId=58919)
### [**Junkwraith by Ellinor Richey**](https://ana.ent.sirsi.net/client/en_US/virtual/search/detailnonmodal/ent:$002f$002fSD_ILS$002f0$002fSD_ILS:2740354/one?qu=junkwraith+%2F+%5Bgraphic+novel%5D&dt=list)
Frustrated by her skating teammates, a teen throws away her ice skates. But in this futuristic fantasy world, there is a curse for garbage, and a spooky quest begins! 
  

  

  

  

  

  

![michigan](https://www.anaheim.net/ImageRepository/Document?documentId=58918)
### [**Michigan vs. the Boys by Carrie S. Allen**](https://ana.ent.sirsi.net/client/en_US/virtual/search/detailnonmodal/ent:$002f$002fSD_ILS$002f0$002fSD_ILS:2760344/one?qu=michigan+vs+the+boys&dt=list)
After the girls hockey team is cut, one girl committed to the sport decides to try-out for the boys team. She makes the team but encounters some series sports issues-- hazing and sexism.
  

  

  

  

  

  

![morgana](https://www.anaheim.net/ImageRepository/Document?documentId=58916)
### [**Morgana and Oz by Miyuli**](https://ana.ent.sirsi.net/client/en_US/virtual/search/detailnonmodal/ent:$002f$002fSD_ILS$002f0$002fSD_ILS:2771122/one?qu=morgana+and+oz&dt=list)
A vampire vs. witches family feud complete with a humorous curses and funny illustrations. 
  

  

  

  

  

  

  

  

![eight nights](https://www.anaheim.net/ImageRepository/Document?documentId=58917)
### [**Eight Nights of Flirting by Hannah Reynolds**](https://ana.ent.sirsi.net/client/en_US/virtual/search/detailnonmodal/ent:$002f$002fSD_ILS$002f0$002fSD_ILS:2744259/one?qu=eight+nights+of+flirting&dt=list)
A cozy East Coast setting in Nantucket where a large family gathering for the holidays is halted by an unexpected snowstorm. A teen girl decides to make the best of it and learn something new from her sworn enemy-- an old crush.
