## 2019 Transportation Fair 
Thank you to everyone that participated in the Annual Transportation Fair & Chili Cook-Off! This was the most successful event to date. Proceeds will be donated to  
  
The Transportation Fair is hosted by the Public Works Department. The intent of this fair is to educate the community and promote alternative modes of transportation such as walk, bike, carpool, bus, vanpool, train, and electric vehicles. We invite transportation related vendors to showcase their services to the public and employees.  
  
Contact the Anaheim Transportation Center for more questions about the event (714) 765-KARS (5277).
