# Anaheim Fire & Rescue FireMedics Program
## (Formerly known as Paramedic Membership Program)
  

If you are experiencing an emergency, dial 911.
It is important to make sure you and your family are protected in the event of an emergency. One emergency call can cost thousands of dollars. That is why Anaheim Fire & Rescue offers the FireMedics Program, which can help you avoid paramedic response and ambulance transportation fees following a medical emergency in Anaheim.
### **How it Works**
The FireMedics Program is a low-cost voluntary membership program available to Anaheim residents and businesses.  
  
When enrolled in the FireMedics Program, Anaheim Fire & Rescue will waive the remaining costs of emergency medical services and ambulance transportation not covered by medical insurance. Payments made by insurance will be accepted as payment in full, ensuring no out-of-pocket expenses to members. 
### **For Anaheim residents:**
  * The program provides unlimited paramedic response and emergency ambulance transportation coverage for you and your household anywhere in Anaheim. The program also covers your family and friends if they have a medical emergency while at your home.


### **For Anaheim businesses:**
  * The program covers all employees while working at the enrolled address. Customers may be covered by their residential memberships if they live in Anaheim.


## **What it Costs**
The cost for this membership is only $5 per month and is added to your Anaheim Public Utilities bill. If you do not receive a utility bill from the City of Anaheim, you can enroll by application with check or money order and pay the annual fee of $60.
## **3 Ways to Sign Up**
  1. Sign up, pay, and manage your membership online by visiting : <https://aca-prod.accela.com/ANAHEIM>
  2. Add to your Anaheim Public Utilities bill by emailing FireMedics@anaheim.net. Include your name, address, telephone, and account number.
  3. Mail a completed application and payment to:

Additionally, members will have access to hands-on CPR, First Aid, and AED courses. This two-hour educational course will be conducted by Anaheim Fire & Rescue personnel in an interactive setting using a hands-on approach with manikins. No certification provided.
These classes are free for members and will be held on a quarterly basis. There will be a maximum of 25 participants per class and are first come, first served. Registration is required.
