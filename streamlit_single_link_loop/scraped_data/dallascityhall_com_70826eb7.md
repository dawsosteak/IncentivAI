​The Civil Service Board consists of seven members appointed by the Dallas City Council for two year terms who are responsible for overseeing the civil service system established in a Code of Rules and Regulations which are authorized by Chapter XVI of the Dallas City Charter.
The Civil Service Board meetings have been changed to the first Tuesday of the month at 9:30 a.m. **(change in time)** , until further notice, in Suite 1C-South, City Hall, 1500 Marilla, Dallas, Texas.
