#  Refund Information 
## **Refund Policies**
**  
** Requests for  _RECREATION & YOUTH SPORTS PROGRAM CLASSES_**must be made in writing** and returned to our office by one of the following methods:
**Mail:** Attn: Refund. 200 S. Anaheim Blvd. Anaheim, CA 92805  
**Fax:** (714) 765-5223  
**E-mail:** Subject: Refund  
To: play@anaheim.net
**Refund request for RECREATION & YOUTH SPORTS PROGRAM CLASSES (classes meet 3 or more times are as follow):**
  1. Requests for refunds made within 5 days or less before the first day of class meeting are subject to a 10% withdrawal fee.
  2. Requests for refunds made 24 hours or less to the first class meeting are subject to a 15% withdrawal fee.
  3. Refunds will not be granted after second class meeting. Refunds must be requested prior to the start of the second class meeting. 


**Refund request for RECREATION WORKSHOPS (classes meet 1 or 2 times are as follow):**
  1. Requests for refunds made within 5 days or less before the workshop begins are subject to a 10% withdrawal fee.
  2. Requests for refunds made 24 hours or less prior to when the workshop begins are subject to a 15% withdrawal fee.
  3. Refunds will not be granted after the workshop begins.


### **Refund request for CAMPVENTURE**
Refund requests will be granted only if in writing and received 7 days prior to the first day of camp.  
_*Refund request received less than 7 days prior to camp may be honored if the camp has a waiting list._
