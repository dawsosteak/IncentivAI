#  Anaheim's Older Adult Program 
![older adults ](https://www.anaheim.net/ImageRepository/Document?documentID=64363)
Anaheim’s Older Adult Program fosters a vibrant and supportive community for those 55 and older.
Through a broad range of activities, the program encourages physical and social engagement for those of all abilities. Centered at the Downtown Anaheim Older Adult Center and hubs across the city, this initiative ensures that everyone can find something to suit their interests.
Whether you're seeking gentle exercises for limited mobility or more challenging fitness including longer walks, the program accommodates all levels and abilities.
In addition to physical wellness, you can benefit from health-focused lectures, insurance information and access to community resources. Special seasonal workshops and luncheons enhance the experience, making it a wonderful place to stay active, learn things and build lasting friendships.
We invite you to discover the activities and opportunities available and to join the thriving community at the Downtown Anaheim Older Adult Center, along with our hub locations.
