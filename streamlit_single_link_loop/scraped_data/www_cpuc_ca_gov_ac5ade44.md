#  Administrative Committee for California Teleconnect Fund
## Administrative Committee
The CTF-AC functions, pursuant to Public Utilities Code Section 280(a), to advise the Commission regarding the development, implementation, and administration of a program to advance universal service by providing discounted rates for telecommunications services to qualifying schools, libraries, hospitals, health clinics, and community organizations, and to carry out the program under the Commission's direction, control, and approval.
The next California Teleconnect Fund-Administrative Committee Meeting will be held in hybrid setting on **June 8, 2026,** 10:30 am - 2:30 pm at Sacramento Public Library -West Meeting Room, 828 I Street, Sacramento, CA 95814.
## Consider Serving on the CTF Administrative Committee
Your perspective is essential to ensuring California’s most critical institutions remain connected. By serving on the CTF-AC, you bring the real-world needs of your sector, whether community based organizations, deaf/hard of hearing, education, local exchange carriers, public hospitals, public libraries, or rural Clinics/telemedicine, directly into statewide decision-making. This committee helps shape how discounted broadband and telecommunications services are delivered to organizations like yours, especially those in rural or underserved areas. Your expertise ensures the program remains responsive, equitable, and effective.
Please review our [flyer](https://www.cpuc.ca.gov/-/media/cpuc-website/divisions/communications-division/documents/california-teleconnect-fund/ctf-ac-nomination-guidelines/ctf-ac-outreach-flyer-final.pdf) for more information. (Available in [Spanish](https://www.cpuc.ca.gov/-/media/cpuc-website/divisions/communications-division/documents/california-teleconnect-fund/ctf-ac-nomination-guidelines/ctf-ac-outreach-flyerfinalspanish.pdf) and [Chinese](https://www.cpuc.ca.gov/-/media/cpuc-website/divisions/communications-division/documents/california-teleconnect-fund/ctf-ac-nomination-guidelines/ctf-ac-outreach-flyerfinalchinese-simplified.pdf))
