## Plans  

For a large city like Dallas, several scales of plans are developed that represent the various communities throughout the city. At a macro level, it is very important that citywide plans create a framework to facilitate effective planning at smaller areas and neighborhood scales.   

  

  * [Citywide Planning  
ForwardDallas 2.0 is a the City of Dallas' comprehensive land use plan. It lays out a long-term land use vision. ](https://dallascityhall.com/departments/pnv/Pages/Plans.aspx#citywide-planning)
  * [Area Planning  
Planning efforts that focus on specific sections of the City, that include clusters of properties that operate distinctly from the city whole](https://dallascityhall.com/departments/pnv/Pages/Plans.aspx#area-planning)
  * [Neighborhood Planning  
Planning efforts organized and driven by residents of a localized community](https://dallascityhall.com/departments/pnv/Pages/Plans.aspx#neighborhood-planning)


### Citywide Planning
ForwardDallas 2.0 is a refreshed guide that takes into account how our City has evolved over the last nearly two decades and how we should plan for what is on the horizon – from our continued economic growth to our long-term social vibrancy. It identifies opportunities to support the City's growth in a way that makes sense while at the same time, considering residents who have made Dallas their home for decades.   
  
[ForwardDallas 2.0](https://dallascityhall.com/departments/pnv/Forward-Dallas/Pages/default.aspx)   
  
The ForwardDallas Land Use Plan aims to enable planning at four (4) primary scales illustrated below. Click the image below to learn more about the ForwardDallas 2.0.
  

![FwdDallas Update Logo_FullColor.png](https://dallascityhall.com/departments/pnv/PublishingImages/Pages/Plans-new/FwdDallas%20Colored%20Logo.png)   

### Area Planning
Area Plans focus on specific sections of the City, that include clusters of properties that operate distinctly from the city whole, to provide tailored policies, programs, detailed targets, regulations and/or incentives. These efforts can be initiated by City Council or Staff and are ultimately adopted by Council and become City policy.   
  
[Adopted Area Plans](https://dallascityhall.com/departments/pnv/Pages/Area-Plans.aspx)   
  
The interactive map below provides brief descriptions, area boundaries and links to each of the City's adopted Area Plans. To access a particular Area Plan document, just click on the file name in the list to zoom to the shape, click the shape and select the "View" option next to "Plan URL" within the pop-up window.
