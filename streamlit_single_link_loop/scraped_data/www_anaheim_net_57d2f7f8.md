  1. [Gale in Context: Elementary](https://www.anaheim.net/1107/Biographies-History#tab775b9cfd-623a-4d39-a7c3-15fe4e7d9dcf_2)


[](https://anaheimlibrary.idm.oclc.org/login?url=https://infotrac.gale.com/itweb/anaheim_main?db=ITKE%26id%3Danaheim_main)[**Gale in Context: Elementary**](https://anaheimlibrary.idm.oclc.org/login?url=https://infotrac.gale.com/itweb/anaheim_main?db=ITKE%26id%3Danaheim_main)
Gale in Context: Elementary meets the research needs of students in Kindergarten through Grade 5. It features a developmentally appropriate, visually graphic interface, a subject-based topic tree search, and full-text, age-appropriate, curriculum-related magazine, newspaper and reference content for information on current events, the arts, science, health, people, government, history, sports and more.
**Keywords:** K-5, elementary, geography, research skills, famous people, animals, art and music, social studies, technology, and inventions. 
  1. [Gale OneFile: U.S. History](https://www.anaheim.net/1107/Biographies-History#tab775b9cfd-623a-4d39-a7c3-15fe4e7d9dcf_3)


[**Gale OneFile: U.S. History**](https://anaheimlibrary.idm.oclc.org/login?url=https://infotrac.gale.com/itweb/anaheim_main?db=PPUS%26id%3Danaheim_main)
Provides access to scholarly journals and magazines useful to both novice historians as well as advanced academic researchers. Updated daily, this library resource offers balanced coverage of events in U.S. history and scholarly work being established in the field. 
**Keywords:** U.S. History, United States, states, academic journals, current events, resource, database, CNN, NPR, and research.

[**Historic Anaheim Photos**](https://www.anaheim.net/1294/Anaheim-Photo-Collections)
A digital archive of nearly 2,000 historic photographs dating back to 1860. These unique local history resources have been organized for easy browsing by Anaheim residents, scholars, and history buffs throughout California and the world.
**Keywords:** History, Anaheim, Hirahara, Bullard, Muzeo, Heart Mountain, Japanese American, immigrant, and local history.
