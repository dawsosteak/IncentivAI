### CCY Architechts
Local architecture firm tackles building efficiency with an all-electric rooftop heat pump. Discover how CCY Architects implemented sustainable solutions to reduce its energy consumption and carbon footprint.
[ Read More ](https://irp.cdn-website.com/06c12080/files/uploaded/CORE_Case+Study_CCY_Final_3.14.25.pdf)

### Halle Center for Hope and Healing
Local nonprofit utilizes grants from CORE to help fund its all-electric heat pump system and energy modeling. Learn how strategic funding and planning led to successful energy efficiency improvements.
[ Read More ](https://irp.cdn-website.com/06c12080/files/uploaded/CORE_Case+Study+Response+%281%29.docx)
[](https://irp.cdn-website.com/06c12080/files/uploaded/One+Riverfront+Case+Study+Avon+Colorado.pdf)
### One Riverfront Townhomes
All-electric HVAC, water heating, snowmelt, pool heating, and appliances — including ranges and fireplaces — powered by high-efficiency chillers and boilers, a first for luxury design.
[ Read More ](https://irp.cdn-website.com/06c12080/files/uploaded/One+Riverfront+Case+Study+Avon+Colorado.pdf)
### Alta Verde Neighborhood
Alta Verde is a net-zero, all-electric apartment community combining critical workforce housing with ambitious sustainability goals, including energy-efficient systems and onsite solar to fully offset energy use.
[ Read More ](https://irp.cdn-website.com/06c12080/files/uploaded/Alta+Verde+Case+Study.pdf)
### Wapiti Commons
A high-performance envelope underpins all subsequent systems, paired with cold-climate heat pumps, energy recovery ventilators, heat pump water heaters, and a 6.5kW per-unit solar array to fully offset energy use.
[ Read More ](https://irp.cdn-website.com/06c12080/files/uploaded/Wapiti+Commons+Case+Study.pdf)
### Horse Mountain Ranch Residence
This home seamlessly integrates stunning design, energy efficiency, and sustainability into one cohesive vision, featuring an all-electric split system heat pump with energy recovery for heating and cooling.
[ Read More ](https://irp.cdn-website.com/06c12080/files/uploaded/Vail+Custom+Single+Family+Home+Case+Study.pdf)
