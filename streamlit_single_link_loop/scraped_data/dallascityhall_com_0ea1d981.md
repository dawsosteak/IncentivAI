The Dallas Municipal Court provides various options for you to pay for your citation. See below for options on paying online, by phone, at a select retail store, by mail, in-person, and payment plans.​​

#### Pay Online
#### Online
**Terms and Conditions:** By paying your fine on this website you understand:
  1. A plea of No Contest and a disposition of Guilty will be entered on your case.  

  2. Fines and or warrant payments paid after hours, weekends and holidays, will not post until the following workday.
  3. Payments can be verified by calling 214-670-0109 or going under [**Citation Search**](https://www.municipalrecordsearch.com/dallastx/Cases). Please allow time for the payment to process (See #2 above).
  4. Failure to make payments by the due date may result in additional fines, fees and possible warrants for your arrest.

#### Pay by Mail or In-Person
#### By Mail or In-Person
**By Mail:** The Court uses the postmark date on the letter (stamped by the postal service) as the received date. Mailing certified is recommended but is not required. Do not send cash. Send only a check or a money order through the mail. Be sure to clearly print the citation number on the check or money order to ensure the correct citation is credited.
**In-person:** Pay with cash, debit card, credit card, check or money order. Visa and MasterCard are accepted. (No credit card fees are charged when paying in-person at the Municipal Court.) Spanish speaking representatives are available.
