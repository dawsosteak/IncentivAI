# Fun Things To Do
Participate in a reading program or find free activities for teens in 7th to 12th grade at your neighborhood branch. Activities include 3D printing, educational workshops, game days, movie screenings, and more. Teen areas at the library are available for hanging out, studying, making and learning new things.

# **Student Success for Teens**
Anaheim Public Library and local high schools are working together to make it easy for students and faculty to access the many valuable online learning resources available through Anaheim Public Library's website.
Most students can access the library’s online resources with their school-issued ID number. Visit the Student Success Portal below or explore a complete list of Online Resources for everyone.
