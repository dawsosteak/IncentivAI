#  Undergrounding within Wildfire Threat Zones 
This project will underground seven segments of overhead lines that are located within/adjacent to the various Fire Threat Zones. These seven segments include Martella Lane, Del Giorgio Road, Timken Road, Cooks Corner, Country Hill Road, Oak Canyon Nature Center Park near Walnut Canyon Road, and the wilderness area north of Nohl Ranch Road from Royal Oak to Anaheim Hills Road.
Anaheim Public Utilities (APU) collaborated with Anaheim Fire & Rescue to apply for FEMA grant funding to help offset the project’s cost and accelerate the undergrounding of these lines. 
The undergrounding is currently under construction and is estimated to be completed by Winter 2026.
Find out more about the [Wildfire Mitigation Plan](https://www.anaheim.net/5487).
