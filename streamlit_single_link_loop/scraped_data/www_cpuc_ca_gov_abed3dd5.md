#  Winter 2023 Natural Gas Prices
**Proceeding Background**
On [March 16, 2023](https://www.cpuc.ca.gov/events-and-meetings/cpuc-voting-meeting-2023-03-16), the CPUC [opened](https://docs.cpuc.ca.gov/PublishedDocs/Published/G000/M503/K282/503282787.PDF) a [new proceeding](https://apps.cpuc.ca.gov/p/I2303008) to investigate:
  * The causes and impacts of the winter 2022- 2023 natural gas price spikes and the potential for recurrence; 
  * The impact of the price spikes on natural gas and electric prices and customer bills; 
  * The potential threats to natural gas and electric reliability and price volatility in summer 2023 and beyond, and potential mitigations; and 
  * Utility communications to customers to determine whether they were sufficient or require modifications.

**Related Actions**
  * Directed utilities to enhance customer communications via email, social media, customer calls, bill inserts, community-based organizations, television spots, and even text messages to those enrolled in[CARE and FERA](https://www.cpuc.ca.gov/industries-and-topics/electrical-energy/electric-costs/care-fera-program), enrolling in seasonal billing plans to stabilize monthly bills, opting into rate forecast tools, and strategies to safely conserve while retaining health, comfort, and safety during the holidays.
  * Accelerated the timeframe in which residential energy customers will receive a [Climate Credit](https://docs.cpuc.ca.gov/PublishedDocs/Published/G000/M501/K885/501885176.PDF "https://docs.cpuc.ca.gov/PublishedDocs/Published/G000/M501/K885/501885176.PDF") on their bills in 2023.
