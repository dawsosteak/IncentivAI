#  Office of Governmental Affairs
#####  Office of Government Affairs (OGA) facilitates the CPUC’s interaction with the Legislature, provides expert policy advice to Commissioner offices, management and staff, informs and lends technical assistance to legislative staff, and engages in the legislative process to ensure that the organization’s perspective is represented in policy discussions and legislation. OGA is available to assist California ratepayers as well as state and federal legislative offices with issues pertaining to the industries under the regulatory jurisdiction of the CPUC.
