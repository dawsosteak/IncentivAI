On November 19, 2019, City Council directed the staff to complete and closeout the engineering and environmental phase of the Lincoln Widening Project from West Street to Harbor Boulevard with formal adoption of the environmental documents. Therefore, the environmental documents will be considered for adoption on Tuesday, August 25, 2020 Council Meeting.  
The recommendation on August 25, 2020 also include reaffirming City Council direction to cease work on the right-of-way acquisition and construction phases of Project until there is sufficient community support.
