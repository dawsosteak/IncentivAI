#  Center Gallery 
The Center Gallery is a hidden gem within the Downtown Anaheim Art District. For twenty-five (25) years, this art space has highlighted the work and talents of selected visual artists. We feature mixed media and community-friendly works of art throughout the year. Our beautiful gallery showcases craftsman-style walls, counters, pedestals, and hallway niche display cases. If you would like to be considered, submit an online Fine Arts Gallery Application. Click [here](https://www.anaheim.net/FormCenter/Community-Services-4/Fine-Arts-Gallery-Application-44)
### _Review of applications is ongoing; submit applications at any time during the year._
##   

### Goals
  * Provide local visual artists with a 6 to 8 week-long opportunity to exhibit artwork at the Downtown Anaheim Community Center
  * Nurture working relationships/partnerships between local and regional artists, presenters, art organizations, and the Anaheim community
  * Encourage creative growth among artists of all ages
  * Exhibit artwork of Anaheim and the Greater Southern California region residents


### Artist Qualification
  * Demonstrate artistic creativity and ability in recent works
  * Demonstrate the ability to share their creative approach and engage in dialogue with City Council, the Cultural and Heritage Commission, students, and the Anaheim community


### Application Guidelines
  * Complete the Fine Arts Gallery Application
  * Submit six recent work samples (photos or digital format acceptable) labeled with artist name, title, medium, and dimensions
  * Current resume with education, exhibitions, collection(s), review, publications, and awards
