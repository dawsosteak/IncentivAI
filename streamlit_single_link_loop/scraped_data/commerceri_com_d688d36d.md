## YOUR Business is OUR Business
Rhode Island Commerce is the state’s economic development agency focused on fostering business growth, attracting new investments, creating jobs, and strengthening the state’s economy. We provide businesses with the resources they need to land, grow and flourish in Rhode Island. Our suite of support includes grants, loans, tax incentives, regulatory streamlining and tailored business assistance.
Our support includes [business grants](https://commerceri.com/grants/), [loans and financing programs](https://commerceri.com/financing/loan-programs/), [tax incentives](https://commerceri.com/financing/tax-credits/), regulatory streamlining, and [tailored business assistance](https://commerceri.com/business-assistance/).
