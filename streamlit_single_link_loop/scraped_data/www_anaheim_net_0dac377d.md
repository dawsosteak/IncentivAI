# The Latino Family Literacy Project
  
  
The [Latino Family Literacy Project](https://www.latinoliteracy.com/) offers trainings and workshops for parents and teachers to develop reading routines with their families. 
  1. [Children’s Home Society of California](https://www.anaheim.net/5715/Early-Literacy-Resources-for-Families#tab5ae935c8-bfed-47b0-8c08-a35d0a0124e1_1)


# Children's Home Society of California
  
  
[The Children's Home Society of California](https://www.chs-ca.org/family-education-program) offers free resources for families such as a development stages wheel, school readiness kits, and more.
  1. [First5 California ](https://www.anaheim.net/5715/Early-Literacy-Resources-for-Families#tab5ae935c8-bfed-47b0-8c08-a35d0a0124e1_2)


# First5 California
  
  
[First5 California](https://www.first5california.com/en-us/) provides information and resources vital to the first 5 years of a child's life. Their resources range from nutrition to early literacy.
  1. [First5 Orange County ](https://www.anaheim.net/5715/Early-Literacy-Resources-for-Families#tab5ae935c8-bfed-47b0-8c08-a35d0a0124e1_3)


# First5 Orange County
  
  
[First5 Orange County](http://occhildrenandfamilies.com/) connects parents with vital information for the development of the first 5 years of a child's life.
  1. [Pretend City](https://www.anaheim.net/5715/Early-Literacy-Resources-for-Families#tab5ae935c8-bfed-47b0-8c08-a35d0a0124e1_4)


# Pretend City  

  
[Pretend City](https://www.pretendcity.org/) is a children's museum that incorporates real-world everyday activities and their imagination as they develop critical skills.
  1. [Colorín Colorado](https://www.anaheim.net/5715/Early-Literacy-Resources-for-Families#tab5ae935c8-bfed-47b0-8c08-a35d0a0124e1_5)


# Colorín Colorado
#    

  
[Colorín Colorado](https://www.colorincolorado.org/families) is a bilingual parent resource offering tips on helping your child learn to read, succeed in school, and learn a new language.  

  1. [help me grow](https://www.anaheim.net/5715/Early-Literacy-Resources-for-Families#tab5ae935c8-bfed-47b0-8c08-a35d0a0124e1_6)


[**Help Me Grow**](https://helpmegrow.choc.org/) seamlessly connects you with essential local resources and services in Orange County, including developmental screenings, care, and support.
