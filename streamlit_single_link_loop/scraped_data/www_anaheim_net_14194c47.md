#  Support Services 
The Support Services Division:  

  * Provides non-emergency support to the [Operations Division](https://www.anaheim.net/678/Operations)
  * Monitors all capital improvement projects
  * Coordinates facility and apparatus repairs and replacements
  * Processes large equipment purchases
  * Provides for the purchase, storage and inventory of all equipment and expendable supplies needed for the day-to-day operations of the department. 

This division manages the Service Section, Emergency Management and Preparedness Section, and the Department’s Information Technology Section.
