#  Public Power Week 
Public power utilities across the U.S. celebrate Public Power Week, the first full week of October every year. Anaheim Public Utilities recognizes the week to help customers engage with their community-owned utility and benefit from all its offerings.

  * focus on the specific needs of their communities.
  * are uniquely accountable due to local governance.
  * provide responsive customer service in their communities.
