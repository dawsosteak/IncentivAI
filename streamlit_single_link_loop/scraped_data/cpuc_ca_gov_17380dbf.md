cpuc.ca.gov 0325 THE CALIFORNIA TELECONNECT FUND (CTF) for eligible Public Schools, Private Schools, Libraries, 2-1-1 Providers, Community Colleges, and Government Hospitals provides a 50% discount on monthly recurring broadband and advanced communication services. Who is eligible? • Public and Private K-12 schools and instructional annexes with a County District School Code (CDS). • Libraries, Library annexes, kiosks and bookmobiles. • Government Operated County or Municipal Hospitals and California Critical Access Hospitals (CCAH). • California Community Colleges. • 2-1-1 Providers must be approved by Commission Resolution.* How does it work? • Your organization submits a CTF application through their eCAP account. • CTF staff reviews the application and sends a determination letter to the organization • Organization provides their service provider with the CTF Approval Letter • Service provider applies a 50% discount to the organizations bill To apply: Applications are submitted through our online portal, eCAP: rebrand. ly/eCap Use the link to log in and register. Additional resources and information: California T eleconnect Fund: www.cpuc.ca.gov/ctf CTF application video: rebrand.ly/CTF_eCAP CTF Applicant and Participant Guidebook: rebrand.ly/CTFguide Please contact us at CTFHelp@cpuc. ca.gov with any additional questions. *2-1-1 Providers must be approved by Commission resolution. Organizations that receive referrals from 2-1-1 providers, Community Based Organizations (CBOs), and Healthcare Community Based Organizations (HCBOs) may qualify and have alternate eligibility.requirements. Please contact CTF with questions.

PU Code Section 280 directs the CPUC to provide discounts to telecommunications services to qualifying K-12 schools, community colleges, libraries, school and library annexes, hospitals, health clinics, and community-based organizations. SCHOOL K-12 Schools • 4,255 impacted public & non-profit private schools & their annexes • Must have annual endowment under $50 million • SCHOOL ANNEXES must have an existing main School account within eCAP • 547 impacted libraries & annexes • Must be eligible to participate for funds under Title III of the federal Library Services and Technology Act • LIBRARY ANNEXES must have an existing main Library account within eCAP • 97 impacted community colleges Healthcare Community-Based Organizations • 688 impacted healthcare CBOs • Must be 501(c)(3) and provide healthcare services Hospitals & Health Clinics • 246 impacted hospitals and clinics • Hospitals must be owned and operated by a governmental entity or hospital district Community-Based Organizations • 9,167 impacted community-based organizations • Must be 501(c)(3) or 501(d) providing qualifying services For more information on California Teleconnect Fund: www.cpuc.ca.gov/ctf • The program provides 50% off the monthly recurring charges for internet and community-based broadband. • CTF discount is capped at E-Rate discount dollar amount for qualified schools. • CPUC staff reviews both participant applications and reimbursement claims from participating service providers. • The CTF awarded over $1 million in digital divide grants through the Digital Divide Grant program.
