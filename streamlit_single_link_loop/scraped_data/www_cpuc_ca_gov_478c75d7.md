#  Safety Issues and Regulatory Policy
We work to make sure regulated services are delivered in a safe and reliable manner. 
## [ Electric Safety and Reliability Branch ](https://www.cpuc.ca.gov/about-cpuc/divisions/safety-and-enforcement-division/electric-safety-and-reliability-branch)
Safety for overhead and underground electric and communications facilities, and power plants. 
## [ Emergency Reporting ](https://www.cpuc.ca.gov/regulatory-services/safety/emergency-reporting)
Instructions and advice on reporting when an emergency arises or when you notice a safety concern. 
## [ Mobile Home Parks ](https://www.cpuc.ca.gov/regulatory-services/safety/mhp)
Inspecting and maintaining the safety of mastered metered natural gas systems in mobile home parks. 
## [ Gas Safety and Reliability Branch ](https://www.cpuc.ca.gov/regulatory-services/safety/gas-safety-and-reliability-branch)
Making sure that intra-state natural gas and liquid petroleum gas pipeline systems are designed, constructed, operated, and maintained according to safety standards. 
## [ Risk Assessment ](https://www.cpuc.ca.gov/about-cpuc/divisions/safety-policy-division/risk-assessment-and-safety-analytics)
Making sure entities integrate risk analysis and risk management practices into their current operations, future planning, and decision-making processes. 
## [ Safety Policy and Action Plans ](https://www.cpuc.ca.gov/regulatory-services/safety/safety-policy-and-action-plans)
Our plan of priorities, clear and measurable goals, and objectives will continue improving safety enforcement and compliance. 
## [ Storage Best Practices ](https://www.cpuc.ca.gov/regulatory-services/safety/storage-best-practices)
Safety Best Practices for the Installation of Energy Storage. 
## [ Annual Electric and Gas Incidents ](https://www.cpuc.ca.gov/regulatory-services/safety/annual-electric-and-gas-incidents)
Gas Safety and Reliability Branch (GSRB) and Electric Safety and Reliability Branch (ESRB) Incidents 
## [ Digging Safety ](https://call811.com/)
Call 811 at least a few days before you start any digging project to make sure you don’t dig into an underground utility line.
