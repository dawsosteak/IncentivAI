#  HUD Reports 
## Consolidated Plans
The City is required by the U.S. Department of Housing and Urban Development (HUD) to prepare a 5-year strategic plan called the Consolidated Plan. The Consolidated Plan guides the City to invest federal HUD funds on unmet community needs that were identified during the public outreach process. The 2025-2029 Consolidated Plan will cover five Annual Action Plans starting with the 2025 Annual Action Plan.
  * **2025-2029 Consolidated Plan**
    * The City of Anaheim is in the process of developing its 5-Year Consolidated Plan, which helps decide how federal grant money is spent on housing and community development needs. The City is partnering with Mosaic Community Planning as a consultant on this project and, as part of the process, is looking for feedback from community members, stakeholders, and local service providers about Anaheim's most important needs.
    * The Housing & Community Development Commission (HCDC) will hold a Public Hearing on **Wednesday, July 2, 2025** at**5:00 P.M**. in the City Council Chamber, 200 S. Anaheim Blvd., Anaheim, CA 92805, to obtain comments on the proposed [Fiscal Year 2025-2029 Consolidated Plan](https://www.anaheim.net/DocumentCenter/View/64628/DRAFT-Anaheim-2025-2029-Consolidated-Plan-Public-Comment-5292025) and the Fiscal Year 2025-2026 Annual Action Plan. In addition, members of the public can submit comments electronically for the Housing and Community Development Commission consideration by sending them to LLy@anaheim.net or by calling (714) 765-4300 ext. 4728.
      * View the proposed Fiscal Year 2025-2029 Consolidated Plan and the Fiscal Year 2025-2026 Annual Action Plan [here](https://www.anaheim.net/DocumentCenter/View/64628/DRAFT-Anaheim-2025-2029-Consolidated-Plan-Public-Comment-5292025).
        * Language revised, see Sections SP-45, AP-20, AP-55, and AP-65. (July 1, 2025 at 3:14 p.m.) 
        * Language revised, see Sections SP-35, SP-45, AP-15, AP-20, AP-38, AP-55, AP-70, and AP-90. (June 26, 2025 at 6:19 p.m.)
  * [2020-2024 Consolidated Plan](https://www.anaheim.net/DocumentCenter/View/56855/2020-2024-City-of-Anaheim-Consolidated-Plan)

## Annual Action Plans
The City is required by the U.S. Department of Housing and Urban Development (HUD) to prepare an annual plan called the Annual Action Plan. The Annual Action Plan outlines specific projects funded with CDBG, HOME, ESG, and HOPWA funds. The projects selected for funding are in keeping with the objectives and goals of the Consolidated Plan.

## Consolidated Annual Performance and Evaluation Reports (CAPER)
The City is required by the U.S. Department of Housing and Urban Development (HUD) to prepare a progress report called the Consolidated Annual Performance and Evaluation Report (CAPER). The report includes status on completed and ongoing projects and the number of households that benefited from these projects. 
  * ### **Fiscal Year 2024-2025 Consolidated Annual Performance and Evaluation Report**
    * Read the drafted Fiscal Year 2024-2025 Consolidated Annual Performance and Evaluation Report [here](https://anaheim.net/DocumentCenter/View/66334/DRAFT-City-of-Anaheim-FY-24-25-Consolidated-Annual-Performance-and-Evaluation-Report-CAPER).
      * ** **15****day** Public Comment period conducted: **Tuesday, September 2, 2025****through Wednesday, September 17****2025** **
      * See Public Notice for information about how to submit your comments [here](https://www.anaheim.net/DocumentCenter/View/66335/Public-Notice-for-City-of-Anaheim-FY-2024-25-Consolidated-Annual-Performance-and-Evaluation-Report-CAPER).


## Citizen Participation Plan
The City is required by the U.S. Department of Housing and Urban Development (HUD) to prepare a document called the "Citizen Participation Plan" that is approved by the City Council. This document outlines public outreach processes and amendment processes to the Annual Action Plans and the Consolidated Plan.
  * [The Citizen Participation Plan](https://anaheim.net/DocumentCenter/View/66253/City-of-Anaheim-2025-2029-Citizenship-Participation-Plan)
