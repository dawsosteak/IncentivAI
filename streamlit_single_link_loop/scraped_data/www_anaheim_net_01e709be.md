#  Residential Rehabilitation Program 
## Announcements
The City of Anaheim is no longer accepting new applications for the Residential Rehabilitation Program and has developed a wait list to notify interested homeowners of when the program reopens. All program updates will be provided at [www.Anaheim.net/5192/Residential-Rehabilitation-Program](http://www.Anaheim.net/5192/Residential-Rehabilitation-Program) and any questions are best directed to the City of Anaheim Department of Housing and Community Development at (714) 765-4300.

## Home Repair Program Resources
  

Please find a list of local home repair programs that are accepting applications.
  * [Community Action Partnership (CAP) OC Emergency Crisis Intervention Program](https://www.capoc.org/addressing-immediate-needs/) (ECIP)
    * The Emergency Crisis Intervention Program (ECIP) offers repair or replacement of heating and cooling (HVAC) systems as well as water heating appliances in emergency cases. The ECIP program is to mitigate and completely resolve the emergency that arises during the respective season (e.g. furnace in winter and air conditioning in summer). Households must meet income and property eligibility requirements. To see if you are eligible and to apply for emergency weatherization assistance, please email energy@capoc.org or call 714-839-6199.
  * [Community Action Partnership (CAP) OC Healthy Homes Program](https://www.capoc.org/empowering-communities/)
    * The Healthy Homes Program assists and educates qualified low-income families and individuals in weatherizing their homes to save energy and money. Through the Weatherization Assistance Program, CAP-OC offers no-cost energy upgrades for your house, mobile home, condo, townhouse or apartment. Upgrades include attic insulation; repair/replace water heater, AC, furnace/heater; mechanical ventilation and more. For questions about the program or to apply, please email energy@capoc.org or call 714-839-6199.
