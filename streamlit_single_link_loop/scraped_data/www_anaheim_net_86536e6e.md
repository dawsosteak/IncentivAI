#  Anaheim Senior Citizen’s Club 
![ASCC TRIP  KNOTTS BERRY M 2023 B X - Copy](https://www.anaheim.net/ImageRepository/Document?documentID=55649)
### About Us
The Anaheim Senior Citizen’s Club (A.S.C.C.) is a non-profit volunteer organization. Founded in the 1950s, it has been an integral part of the senior community for decades. The Club strives to enhance older adults 50 and over, providing a wide range of experiences and entertainment.
### What do we do?
The A.S.C.C. offers a variety of recreation and social opportunities organized and directed by volunteers and hosted at the senior hubs. Lunches, dances, game days, special events, travel opportunities, and multiple daily activities are offered to help enrich life and build friendships. The Club pays for the Courier newsletter and publishes it monthly connecting older adults with information.
**For more information, please call (714) 765-4511.**
