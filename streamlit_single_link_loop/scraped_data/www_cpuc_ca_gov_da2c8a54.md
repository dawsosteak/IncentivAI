#  Consumer Support
We support California residents through complaint resolution, late bill assistance, disconnection information, and financial assistance. CEP TEST 4.18
## [ File a Complaint ](https://www.cpuc.ca.gov/consumer-support/file-a-complaint)
Let us know about public safety issues, public utility misconduct, or a transportation service problem. 
## [ Late Bill Assistance ](https://www.cpuc.ca.gov/consumer-support/late-bill-assistance)
If you have fallen behind on your utility bills, we can assist you to negotiate a payment plan. 
## [ Financial Assistance ](https://www.cpuc.ca.gov/consumer-support/financial-assistance-savings-and-discounts)
You might be eligible for energy, phone, or water service assistance that will reduce your monthly bills. 
## [ Public Safety Power Shutoffs (PSPS) ](https://www.cpuc.ca.gov/consumer-support/psps)
Learn what we are doing to minimize impacts of utility PSPS events. 
## [ Power Outage Maps ](https://www.cpuc.ca.gov/consumer-support/power-outage-maps)
Locate outages in your area to see if your household is affected. 
## [ Cooling Centers ](https://www.cpuc.ca.gov/consumer-support/cooling-centers)
A Cooling Center provides a place for you and your family to go to cool down during hot summer days. 
##  All Consumer Programs & Services
Apart from services like filing a complaint, resolving a billing problem, or seeking financial assistance, there are other programs and services that may offer help to you. 
[Explore all Programs and Services](https://www.cpuc.ca.gov/consumer-support/consumer-programs-and-services)
