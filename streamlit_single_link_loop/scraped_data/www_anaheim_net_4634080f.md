#  Block Party(s) 
## Block Party Rules
  

  * A petition signed by a responsible adult from every residence on the block (please include the resident's address and apartment number) along with the application. This list along with a map of the intersections to be barricaded must be submitted to Traffic Engineering at **minimum 2 weeks** in advance of the event.
  * On the map, include simple hand-drawn markings to show where the barricades will be placed.
  * Attendance at the Block Party is limited to those people living on the block. The residents will remove the necessary barricades.
  * The residents will be responsible for setting up and removing the barricades. Barricades must be equipped with lights for after-dark use, and of a type that can be easily removed in case of emergency. Vehicles **cannot be used as barricades**.
  * Live or amplified music is **not** permitted.
  * The residents will be responsible for cleaning up any debris that may be left on the street after the Block Party.
  * All lights and noise must be curtailed by 11 p.m.
  * The Fire Department would like to remind all Anaheim residents that **all****firework use, including sparklers, is prohibited** in the City of Anaheim. Safe & Sane fireworks may ONLY be used on Independence Day and ONLY at locations west of the 55 freeway. Illegal firework use is subject to citation and fines.
