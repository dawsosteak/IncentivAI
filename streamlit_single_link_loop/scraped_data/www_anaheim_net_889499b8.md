#  Christmas Tree Pickup 
## **Christmas Tree Collection**
Christmas trees will be picked up starting **Dec. 29, 2025, through** **Jan. 16, 2026** , **on your regular collection day**. Place your tree next to — not in front of — your containers. If your tree is more than 6-feet tall, cut in half as you're able and remove all tinsel, ornaments and tree stands. You can **recycle** your Christmas tree by cutting it into small pieces and placing it in your brown yard waste container, or you can add it to your compost bin. If you live in an association community, check with your management company. Go to **Anaheim.net/recycle** for more information.
## Tree disposal options
The following options are available for tree disposal after Jan. 16, 2026:
  * Cut your tree into small pieces to fit in your brown yard waste container
  * Schedule a bulky item collection by contacting [Republic Services Customer Service](https://www.republicservices.com/municipality/anaheim-ca) at (714) 238-2444


## Prepare your tree for recycling
  * Remove all ornaments, tinsel and garland
  * Remove the tree stand
  * Cut tree into 4-foot lengths
