- 3 Cc: ALJ_Support ID; ALJ Docket Office; ALJ Process Subject: R1410003 Email Ruling Noticing July 30, 2015 Cost-Effectiveness Workshop
* * *
This Email Ruling provides notice of a July 30, 2015 workshop in Rulemaking (R.) 14-10-003, the Rulemaking to create a framework for integrated demand side resource programs. The purpose of the workshop is to provide an overview of a cost-effectiveness mapping project recently completed by the Commission’s Energy Division.
* * *
The workshop will be held in the Commission’s Courtyard Training Room, 505 Van Ness Avenue, San Francisco, CA 94102, beginning at 10:00 am. This workshop will be facilitated by the Energy Division and will not be recorded. Energy Division will provide additional information to the service list prior to the workshop.
* * *
The Docket Office shall formally file this Email Ruling.
