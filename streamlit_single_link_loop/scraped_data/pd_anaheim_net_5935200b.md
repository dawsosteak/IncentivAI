#  Orange County Human Trafficking Task Force 
The Orange County Human Trafficking Task Force (OCHTTF) is committed to combating all forms of human trafficking through the rescue and long-term support of victims, prosecution of offenders, training of fellow law enforcement professionals, community awareness, and effective public / private partnerships.
## OCHTTF Goals & Objectives
  * Conduct proactive investigations of sex trafficking and labor trafficking crimes within Orange County
  * Support the prosecution of trafficking suspects at both the state and federal levels
  * Increase the identification of all forms of human trafficking within Orange County
  * Offer a comprehensive array of services to victims of all types of human trafficking encountered by the OCHTTF
  * Enhance community outreach and education to increase awareness and the identification and reporting of human trafficking related crimes.


The OCHTTF will work toward these goals through a collaborative effort among law enforcement, non-governmental, and faith-based organizations. Regularly scheduled meetings with all partners of the OCHTTF ensure the continuous focus on achieving the goals and objectives of the OCHTTF.
## Contact Us
To learn more about the Orange County Human Trafficking Taskforce, please [visit the OCHTTF website](http://www.ochumantrafficking.com).
To report suspected cases of Human Trafficking, please contact your local police department. Additional information is available by contacting the Anaheim Police Department by phone at 714-765-1900.
