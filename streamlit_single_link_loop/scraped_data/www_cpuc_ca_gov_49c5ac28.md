#  California Advanced Services Fund Public Workshop
Add to Your Calendar
Date | 04/22/2026   
---|---  
Time | 10:00 AM  
Location | CPUC Auditorium 505 Van Ness Ave., San Francisco, CA 94102  
Webcast | <https://www.adminmonitor.com/ca/cpuc/>  
The California Public Utilities Commission (CPUC) will hold its annual California Advanced Services Fund (CASF) Public Workshop to facilitate collaboration among regional consortia, stakeholders, local governments, facility-based broadband providers, and consumers regarding cost-effective strategies to reach unserved areas.
The CASF promotes broadband adoption and infrastructure deployment in unserved areas with a goal of providing broadband access to no less than 98 percent of California households.
The workshop will be in-person and virtually via webcast. More information is available at: <https://www.cpuc.ca.gov/industries-and-topics/internet-and-phone/california-advanced-services-fund>
If you have any questions about the 2026 Annual Workshop, please email CASF_Workshop@cpuc.ca.gov.
