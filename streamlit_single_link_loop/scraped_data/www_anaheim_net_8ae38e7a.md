#  Swimming Pool Electrical Upgrade 
## Upgrading Residential Electrical Panels for New Pools, Spas, or Wading Structures
### Step 1: Application & Meter Spot
  * Contact Customer Service at 714-765-3300 to apply for upgraded electrical service and meter installation. If you prefer, you may also apply in person at Anaheim West Tower or [online](https://www.anaheim.net/formcenter/public-utilities-6/upgrading-residential-panel-request-form-52).
  * An Anaheim electrical utility inspector will contact you within 2-3 business days to schedule a time to check your proposed meter location and to assess whether your pool is clear from any overhead utility lines.   
 _Upon approval of your meter location, you will be given a meter spot report -__please keep this with you to present to a building inspector at a later time_.


### Step 2: Electrical Permit
  * Once your meter location has been approved, you may apply for a city building permit over the counter at City Hall, located at 200 S. Anaheim Blvd., or by calling 714-765-5153. There is a separate fee for the building permit.
      * For larger projects, the Anaheim Building Department may require a formal plan check rather than an over-the-counter review. A finalized permit will be required before an electric service plan can be issued for construction. Please visit the following [link](https://www.anaheim.net/5252/Building-Division) or call 714-765-5153 for more information and fee requirements. 
  * After receiving your electrical permit, you may install your electrical panel.


### Step 3: Construction & Inspection
  * There are three sets of final inspections needed: 
    1. A building inspection to check your panel
    2. A utility inspection to check between the power source to the panel
    3. A meter shop inspection
  * First, contact the building inspector at 714-765-5153 to approve the panel installation and house wiring. 
  * Once you have passed the building inspection, contact our electrical utility inspector at 714-765-6847 and arrange for the final utility and meter shop inspection. 
  * Upon passing the utility and meter shop inspections, a service crew will be scheduled to energize your panel within 3-5 business days. If you prefer to schedule installation for a specific time/date, please let the utility inspector know.


While these steps apply to the majority of our residential customers, there may be additional steps and fees in some circumstances, such as when your panel is over 200 amps or when city-owned facilities require relocation. If you believe any of these situations apply to you, please call Electrical Engineering at 714-765-5156 for additional information on how to proceed with your service request.
