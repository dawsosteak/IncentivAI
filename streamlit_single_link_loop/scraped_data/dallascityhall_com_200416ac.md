##  Welcome  

The Office of the City Auditor conducts audits and attestations of City departments, offices, agencies, and processes. Also, advisory services can be agreed upon for City Council or City Administration specific requests.  

## Our Mission
We collaborate with elected officials and employees to elevate public trust in government by providing objective assurance and advisory services.
