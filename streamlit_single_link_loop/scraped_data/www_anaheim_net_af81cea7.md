### Congratulations on deciding to move forward with an ADU Express project on your property! View the approved plans below to decide which option works for you. Sample elevations and floor plans for each plan type is below the table. 
###   

### Are you eligible for ADU Express? If so, please select your plan and email us at **ADUAnaheim@anaheim.net** to provide your address, your selected plan number, and a copy of your meter spot. From there, staff will conduct a screening process to verify eligibility, and if additional information is needed to move forward. This process can take a few weeks to complete, and we will update you throughout this process. 
###   

### Check back often, as we will continue to add additional plans.
###   

### *Please note that all plans include Building Code requirements that come into effect on July 1, 2024.
