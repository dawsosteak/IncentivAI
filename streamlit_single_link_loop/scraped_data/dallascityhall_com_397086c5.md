# ​Welcoming Communities and Immigrant Affairs Home Page
##  Immigrants comprise 23% of our city and serve a crucial role in its vitality - 42% of Dallas households speak a language other than English, and 35% of the labor force in the Dallas metro area are immigrants. Welcoming Communities and Immigrant Affairs Division aspires to be a bridge connecting Dallas’ diverse immigrant community with existing Dallas residents so that common ground and shared leadership can be realized.
