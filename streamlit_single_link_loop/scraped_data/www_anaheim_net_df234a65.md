#  Energy Efficiency in California’s Public Power Sector 
California Senate Bill 1037, enacted in 2005, established a policy that utilities must prioritize energy efficiency programs, and requires the preparation of an annual report that demonstrates progress toward California’s clean energy and climate goals. 
California Public Utilities Code §9505 establishes the core energy efficiency reporting requirements for publicly owned utilities (POUs) in California. 
Anaheim Public Utilities’ SB-1037 report provides an overview of key performance metrics, including investments in energy efficiency and demand reduction programs, and the cost-effectiveness of each program.
View the report here: [Energy Efficiency in California’s Public Power Sector](https://www.anaheim.net/DocumentCenter/View/11240/2026-POU-EE-Report)
