**​City Plan Commission Thoroughfare Committee**  

DCH Body
Page Content
The City Plan Commission Thoroughfare Committee (CPCTC) is a subcommittee of the City Plan Commission (CPC). They are appointed by and responsible to the CPC. The CPCTC is responsible for reviewing potential amendments to the Thoroughfare Plan and the Central Business District Streets and Vehicular Circulation Plan (CBD Plan). Changes to the Plans are reviewed by this Committee, then recommendations are forwarded to the [CPC](https://dallascityhall.com/government/Boards-and-Commissions/City-Plan-and-Zoning-Commission/Pages/default.aspx), then [City Council](https://dallascityhall.com/government/citysecretary/Pages/cMeetings.aspx) for final action.
