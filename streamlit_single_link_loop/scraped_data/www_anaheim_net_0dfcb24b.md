#  Turf Replacement Program for Commercial, Industrial, Institutional, and Homeowner's Associations 
Through the Metropolitan Water District of Southern California’s (Metropolitan) SoCal Water$mart Program, Anaheim Public Utilities Commercial, Industrial, Institutional, and Homeowner's Associations (CII) customers are eligible to receive $2 per square foot of turf removed, up to 50,000 sq. ft.  
  
**Program Requirements**  
In order to be eligible for a rebate, **you must apply and receive project start approval before removing any turf (grass)**. Please visit [socalwatersmart.com](http://www.socalwatersmart.com/) to view additional program requirements and begin the application process.
* * *
Anaheim recognizes that certain CII landscapes may not be able to meet the requirements set in place by the Metropolitan Turf Replacement Program, particularly in areas such as:
  * Parkways and medians
  * Parking Lots
  * Street facing storefronts
  * Monuments and sign landscapes


**In place of the Metropolitan program, Anaheim has its own rebate of $2 per square foot, for up to 2,500 square feet (or $5,000)**. This rebate is not available to those landscapes that receive a Metropolitan rebate and receipt of the Anaheim rebate also prohibits receiving funds from the Metropolitan rebate. An application for this program can be found [here](https://www.anaheim.net/DocumentCenter/View/59074/Turf-Removal-Rebate-Application-Commercial).
New front yard and/or parkway projects must follow [City of Anaheim Landscaping Requirements.](http://anaheim.net/1064/) Prior to installation, the Anaheim Planning Department must review and approve the proposed landscape design. Please call a Planner at 714-765-5139 for assistance.
