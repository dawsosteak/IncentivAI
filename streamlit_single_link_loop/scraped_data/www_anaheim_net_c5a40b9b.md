#  Downtown Guide to Development 
The City of Anaheim has made a strong commitment to supporting and enhancing the Greater Downtown area. Current successes include the preservation of many existing historic resources and the facilitation of new development throughout the larger Colony District. The next step is to strengthen the visibility of the Greater Downtown area as a destination and to improve pedestrian connections and the pedestrian experience throughout the Colony. This study describes a series of goals, strategies and actions by which the Greater Downtown will become a well-recognized urban city center, where people can come together to live, work, play, and prosper.
