#  Bear Valley Electric Services GRC Proceedings (Phase I)
Most recent GRC proceeding A.22-08-010 for Test Year 2023.
On August 30, 2022, Bear Valley filed its 2023-2026 GRC Application (A.22-08-010).
On November 1, 2024, Bear Valley, Cal Advocates, and Snow Summit jointly filed a Joint Motion for Commission Approval and Adoption of Settlement Agreement.
On January 22, 2025, the Commission issued Decision 25-01-007 approving the Settlement Agreement and authorizing Bear Valley to collect the revenue requirements agreed upon in the Settlement Agreement.
Regarding wildfire risk mitigation, the settling parties agreed on an annual revenue requirement of $2.7 million from 2023 to 2026 for vegetation management activities; any vegetation management costs above the $2.7 million, up to a cap of $3.1 million, will be tracked in the new Vegetation Management Balancing Account.
Please see the decision for more information.
  * [D. 25-01-007](https://docs.cpuc.ca.gov/PublishedDocs/Published/G000/M555/K442/555442117.PDF) (revenue requirement for Test Year 2023 and increases for 2024-2026).


Previous Bear Valley Electric Services GRC Decisions:
  * [D.19-08-027](https://docs.cpuc.ca.gov/PublishedDocs/Published/G000/M311/K496/311496182.PDF) (revenue requirement for Test Year 2018 and increases for 2019-2022).
  * [D.16-02-021](http://docs.cpuc.ca.gov/PublishedDocs/Published/G000/M159/K272/159272400.pdf) (granted petition to modify D.14-11-002 to file Bear Valley’s next GRC application for Test Year 2018 prior to March 31, 2017).
  * [D.14-11-002](http://docs.cpuc.ca.gov/PublishedDocs/Published/G000/M137/K963/137963775.PDF) (revenue requirement for Test Year 2013 and increases for 2014-2015).
