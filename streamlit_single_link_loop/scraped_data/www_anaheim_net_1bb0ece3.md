#  Outdoor Dining Parking Permits 
![Outdoor Dining](https://www.anaheim.net/ImageRepository/Document?documentID=44532)
Accessory outdoor dining and seating may be permitted with new or existing restaurants. Proposed locations for outdoor dining and seating must comply with Zoning Code requirements including provisions pursuant to Anaheim Municipal Code (A.M.C.) [Section 18.38.220 (Restaurants-Outdoor Seating and Dining)](https://codelibrary.amlegal.com/codes/anaheim/latest/anaheim_ca/0-0-0-68912). Proposed permanent structures may also require processing a building permit. For questions pertaining to building permit requirements, please visit the [Building Division](https://anaheim.net/5252/Building-Division) public counter or call (714) 765-5153.
Outdoor dining areas may not encroach into public right of way or setback areas, however, restaurants located within a "Large Commercial Shopping Center" may convert up to three parking spaces into an outdoor dining area upon review and approval of an Outdoor Dining Permit. The following links include the applicable requirements, application, and submittal information:
  * [Section 18.38.220 (Restaurants-Outdoor Seating and Dining)](https://codelibrary.amlegal.com/codes/anaheim/latest/anaheim_ca/0-0-0-68912)
  * [Section 18.42.130 (Outdoor Dining Parking Permits)](https://codelibrary.amlegal.com/codes/anaheim/latest/anaheim_ca/0-0-0-80669)
  * [Outdoor Dining Permit Application Form](https://www.anaheim.net/DocumentCenter/View/1992/Administrative-Permit-Application?bidId=)
    * Submit the application and supporting documents via the [**_GoPost_** online portal](https://anaheim.eplansoftreview.com/#/login?portal=AnaheimPlanning).
    * The [fee](https://anaheim.net/DocumentCenter/View/22436/) for an administrative Outdoor Dining Parking Permit is $832. Upon receipt of a complete application, staff will contact you to collect the payment.


For questions on proposing outdoor seating or outdoor dining, please contact the Planning Services Division at (714) 765-5139.
