#  Rooms & Features 
Discover the perfect venue our centrally located facility, where a diverse range of spaces awaits to bring your vision to life. Whether you’re planning a grand celebration, a creative workshop, a professional meeting, or an outdoor gathering, our thoughtfully designed rooms and areas offer the ideal backdrop. Explore our unique offerings below to find the space that best suits your needs:
### Click on the image below to download or enlarge the facility map.
[![Layout of Rooms at Downtown Anaheim Community Center Opens in new window](https://www.anaheim.net/ImageRepository/Document?documentId=65669)](https://www.anaheim.net/DocumentCenter/View/65666/DACC-Rooms)  

### **Assembly Hall (4,270 sq. ft.)**
Our largest event space, the Assembly Hall features 4,270 square feet ideal for large gatherings, receptions, or conferences. This versatile space includes all necessary amenities and can be arranged in a variety of seating configurations to suit your event. 
### **Board Room (430 sq. ft.)**
Perfect for smaller meetings, the Board Room offers 430 square feet of professional space that accommodates 15-20 people in a conference-style setup. It includes a coffee area, projection screen, and dry-erase board for presentations and planning sessions.
### **Performing Arts Room (1,306 sq. ft.)**
With 1,306 square feet of acoustically treated space, this room is ideal for dance, theater, or other performing arts. It features specialized flooring and an 8-foot mirrored wall with an adjustable ballet bar to support a wide range of creative activities.
### **Visual Arts Room (900 sq. ft.)**
This 900-square-foot room is designed to inspire creativity and is perfect for hands-on workshops and classes such as painting and ceramics. It offers ample space for both instruction and individual work.
### **Weingart & Rotary Meeting Rooms (Weingart: 627 sq. ft., Rotary 1 & 2 Combined: 368 sq. ft.)**
These flexible meeting spaces can each accommodate up to 30 participants, depending on the setup. Both rooms include dry-erase boards and limited audiovisual equipment, making them well-suited for trainings, presentations, or collaborative sessions.
### **Outside Patio**
This outdoor cement-floored space offers flexible setup options and can accommodate up to 30 people in a casual setting or up to 40 people in banquet-style seating. Please contact staff for details on specific configurations and availability.
