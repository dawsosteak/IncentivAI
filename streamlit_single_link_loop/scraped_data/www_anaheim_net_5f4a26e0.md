#  Miraloma Park & Family Resource Center 
  1. ![Miraloma Logo Image 2](https://www.anaheim.net/ImageRepository/Document?documentID=64654)
  2. ![Female dancers smiling](https://www.anaheim.net/ImageRepository/Document?documentID=64651)
  3. ![Two women and a girl showing off their fresh vegitables](https://www.anaheim.net/ImageRepository/Document?documentID=64652)


The Miraloma Park and Family Resource Center is one of three family-friendly centers operated by the City of Anaheim. These centers bring together residents, public agencies, and private organizations to deliver services that enhance the Anaheim families' safety, stability, and healthy development.
On-site staff provide needs assessments, coordinate human services and programs, and ensure comprehensive support for families. Bilingual (English/Spanish) staff are available at all three centers.
### **Case Management Services**
Our case management service can help you sustain your household by providing individualized case assessment, planning, and care to promote quality living,
  * Community Resource Information & Referrals
  * Transportation Resources
  * Diaper Distributions
  * Food Distributions
  * Utility Application Assistance
  * Form Translation & Completion Assistance
  * Enrollment Assistance
    * Medi-Cal, CalFresh, Covered California
