#  Transient Occupancy Tax 
For the privilege of occupancy in the City of Anaheim, each transient is subject to and shall pay a transient occupancy tax of 15% of the rent charged by the operator. The 15% tax MUST be separately stated from rent pursuant to Chapter 2.12 Subsection 2.12.020.010 of the Anaheim Municipal Code.
  

For more information, please refer to the [Transient Occupancy Tax Ordinance](https://codelibrary.amlegal.com/codes/anaheim/latest/anaheim_ca/0-0-0-51668) (Chapter 2.12) for detailed information.
  

If you should have a change in your operation, or a change in controllers or staff who prepare the monthly TOT documents, please do not hesitate contacting the Business License staff with any questions at (714) 765-5194. This is a great complimentary service to help avoid audit findings.
### **TOT Collection**
Please ensure your payment along with your TOT Return Notice is received in our office no later than 5 p.m. on the last city business day of the month. If you plan on walking the payment into City Hall please note our modified hours. 
  * **For Overnight/Express Delivery Only** : City of Anaheim, License Collector, 200 S. Anaheim Blvd., #136 Anaheim, CA 92805
  * **Mail** : City of Anaheim, License Collector, P.O. Box 61042, Anaheim, CA 92803-6142


**Public Counter Hours: 8 a.m. - 3 p.m.**
