**What is Period Access Dallas (P.A.D.)**
The City of Dallas is making free period products, including pads and tampons, available at many of our public facilities through an initiative called Period Access Dallas (PAD).  If you are unable to afford period products, you can visit one of the sites below to receive a one month’s supply. To receive products in a discreet shopping bag, complete the request card found in the restrooms and present it to the librarian or recreation center staff.
**Why focus on period access?**  

The City of Dallas Office of Community Care & Empowerment (OCCE), in partnership with Dallas Public Library and Dallas Park and Recreation, is excited to announce the expansion of the Period Access D​allas (PAD) initiative. The initiative, was piloted with funding from the American Rescue Plan Act and expanded with support from the General Fund, to provide free period products in high-need neighborhoods at recreation centers, community cen​ters and libraries.
