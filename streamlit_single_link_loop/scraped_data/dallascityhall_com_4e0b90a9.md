Short-Term Rentals​
​The City has considered amendments to Dallas Development Code Chapter 51A-4.205 "Lodging Uses", to define a new use called "Short-term rental lodging" and related regulations. 
DCH Body
A code amendment was initiated by City Plan Commission (CPC) at the December 2, 2021 meeting. CPC authorized a hearing to consider specifically define a new use, "Short-term rental lodging" within the Lodging Uses sections of Chapters 51 and 51A, as well as related regulations.
Information on public meetings, including case reports, virtual meeting links, and meeting recordings (posted after each meeting) are below.
On June 14, 2023 City Council discussed and approved amendments to Chapters 51 and 51A of the Dallas City Code to create a new lodging use called "Short-term rental lodging" and related regulations. The final approved ordinance can be accessed [here](http://citysecretary2.dallascityhall.com/resolutions/2023/06-14-23/23-0844.pdf).
At the same meeting, City Council also approved a registration ordinance that will be administered by Code Compliance Services. This ordinance can be accessed [here](http://citysecretary2.dallascityhall.com/resolutions/2023/06-14-23/23-0833.pdf). More information related to the registration ordinance can be found on the Code Compliance Services webpage.
More information can be found in this [Frequently Asked Questions (FAQ)](https://dallascityhall.com/departments/codecompliance/short-term-rentals/Pages/default.aspx).
All short-term rentals must comply with both zoning and registration ordinances to legally operate within the City of Dallas.
