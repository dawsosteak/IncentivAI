## **CloudLibrary: NewsStand Help**
Access CloudLibrary: NewsStand from your desktop by following the instructions below. If you have previously registered via the mobile app, do not register again and use the email address and password created. 
  1. Visit CloudLibrary: NewsStand [here.](https://cloudlibrary.magzter.com/login/user) Proceed to enter your email address and password if previously registered. If not, click **Not Registered? Create an Account.**
  2. A registration screen will appear. Fill in the fields and accept the terms, and click**Next.**
  3. Select your country and library from the drop-down menus, then tap on **Register.**
  4. It will then ask for your library card number and PIN, fill in the fields and click**Register** again.
  5. It will take you back to an initial login screen. Fill in the fields with your email address and newly created password. Enjoy reading magazines and newspapers!
