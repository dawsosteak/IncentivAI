#  Community Workshops 
As part of the public engagement process, the City held a series of community workshops throughout the development of the specific plan aimed at soliciting direct input from residents, business owners, services providers and other West Anaheim stakeholders. The workshops were "mobile" and primarily conducted at existing venues and regularly occurring events throughout the West Anaheim community during the specific plan process.
