#  Trees for Turf 
Cut your lawn irrigation in half! Save water by planting trees in place of your turf removal area.
Participants can receive trees up to four (4) FREE trees to place in areas where turf was removed.
  * Participation in Turf Replacement program is not required. 
  * Must have removed your Turf within the past 12 months.
  * Over 40 available species to choose from.


If you have questions, please contact TreePower at 714-491-8733
