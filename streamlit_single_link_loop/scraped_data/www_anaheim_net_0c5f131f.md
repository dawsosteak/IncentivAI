With the theme parks of the Disneyland Resort, the Anaheim Convention Center and hotels, shops and restaurants, there's a lot going on — and moving — in The Anaheim Resort.
In a typical year, The Anaheim Resort sees more than 25 million visitors, while more than 30,000 people work at businesses in the area. And it's still growing! There's more than $2 billion in recent or planned development in and around the resort.
That's why we're working on new ideas to make it easier for residents, employees and visitors to get around. The Anaheim Resort Mobility Study looks at new forms of transportation, better wayfinding signs and other ways to improve mobility in and around the resort.
Read the study and learn more below.
