#  Opt Out of Automated Meter Installation 
Most customers look forward to having an automated meter installed at their home and taking advantage of the many benefits that this technology offers, such as reduced restoration times after power outages, fewer visits from field service representatives and access to consumption information. However, if you are an Anaheim resident and would prefer not to have an automated meter installed at your home, please call us at 714-765-3300 and let one of our customer service representatives know that you would like to opt out of our automated meter program.  
  

## Other Utility Providers
  
Please note that other utility providers in Anaheim, including The Gas Company and AT&T, are installing their own automated meters and other wireless devices throughout the city. Your opt-out request with Anaheim Public Utilities pertains only to your electric and water meters.
