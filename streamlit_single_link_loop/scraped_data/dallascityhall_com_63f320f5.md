​Welcome to the Dallas Water Utilities Infrastructure Information Center Vault, your source for water and wastewater infrastructure open records in the City of Dallas. Our mission is to provide accurate, timely information to the public and construction professionals through our streamlined online process. All requests are processed online only through our online request portal.  
  

Page Content
## ​What We Provide
  * **Fire Hydrant Flow Test Maps**


> ​Map showing the two closest hydrants to your property location  
> 
  * **​Water & Wastewater Infrastructure Maps**


> Street-level maps of water and wastewater mains near the location requested  
> 
  * **Utility Engineering Design Sheets**


> Available utility ​design sheets/plans for your requested area  
>

## Processing Times  

**Fire Hydrant and Water/Wastewater Maps:**
  * Requests before 2 p.m. – Same-day processing  

  * Requests after 2 p.m. – Next business day processing
  * Utility Engineering Design Sheets take **1 to 2 business days**(up to **3 days** during high-volume periods)

### FAQs
**1. Can my request be processed faster if I make the request in person?**
We operate on a first-come, first-served basis through the Formstack Request portal. Coming to our office will not speed up the process. Walk-ins will still be directed to complete the online form.
**2. Do you provide public or private sewer/water service connection maps?**
No, we do not provide public or private sewer/water service connection maps.
**3. How can I request stormwater infrastructure information?**
For stormwater inquiries, please click [here​](https://dallascityhall.com/departments/public-works/Pages/engineering_construction.aspx) to visit the Department of Transportation and Publics Works webpagefor information.​​
