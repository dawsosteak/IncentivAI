#  Appointments 
## Link Labs stations are available by appointment only.
Sign up for your first orientation from the options below. Please note:
  * All appointments have a maximum of 2-hours.
  * Appointments are set up for one user at a time. Please notify staff if you are bringing guests.
  * Please limit booking to one appointment per patron per day.


### First-time Equipment Users
First-time equipment users are required to take an orientation with staff, regardless of skill level. We will review policies and basic operation in first 15-30 minutes of your appointment, followed by independent work time. Staff will be available for assistance for the entire appointment.
*****No appointments for new users in May. Orientations resume in June.*****
### Returning Users
All users who have completed the corresponding Equipment Orientation can reserve any station in that category. Staff may provided limited assistance as availability permits.
Note: Your appointment may be rescheduled if we do not have record of you completing the correct equipment orientation.

### 3D Printing
The Orientation to 3D Printing will introduce basic operation of our FDM 3D printers. **Skills covered:**
  * Selecting and setting up files for 3D printing
  * Importing files into slicing software
  * Preparing the 3D printer
  * Sending a file to print
  * Post-processing
  * Safety, best practices, and troubleshooting common issues
