#  Darrell Chin, Senior Project Manager 
![City of Anaheim_0006](https://www.anaheim.net/ImageRepository/Document?documentID=53299)
**Mr. Darrell Chin** has over 18 years of government experience as an AICP certified land use planner. Prior to joining Anaheim, he was with the City of Irvine’s Development Assistance Center reviewing development proposals and conducting impact analysis for property development. He currently assists property owners, business operators, and real estate professionals to develop property and expand new and existing businesses in Anaheim. He is also engaged in implementing strategic economic initiatives, such as the Storefront Improvement Program, to create innovative public-private partnership opportunities for business owners and aspiring entrepreneurs.
Mr. Chin holds a Masters Degree in Land Use Planning from USC and dual Bachelor degrees in Environmental Analysis & Design, and Political Science from UC Irvine. He is also a licensed California Real Estate Broker.
