#  Access to Local Business Resources 
Anaheim Workforce Connection can provide you with access to a variety of resources to assist with your business needs.
[City of Anaheim - Planning & Building](https://www.anaheim.net/490/)  
[City of Anaheim - Economic Development Division](https://www.anaheim.net/559/)  
[Employment Development Department](http://www.edd.ca.gov/)  
[California Manufacturing Technology Consulting](https://www.cmtc.com/)
[](https://ociesmallbusiness.org/)[Orange County Inland Empire Small Business Development Center](https://ociesmallbusiness.org/)
For more information please contact one of our Business Services Representatives at 714-765-4350.
