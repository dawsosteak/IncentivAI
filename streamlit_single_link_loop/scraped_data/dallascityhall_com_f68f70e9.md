### Frequently Asked Questions list
#### I need home repair assistance. Can you help?
The Department of Housing & Community Development has a [Home Improvement & Preservation Program](http://www.dallascityhall.com/HIPP) (HIPP) that provides minor and major home repairs. Call us at 214-670-3644 or visit the HIPP website to learn more about our program.  
  
---  
  

Category: Homeowners
#### Is this the Dallas Housing Authority?
No, this is the City of Dallas Department of Housing & Community Development. The [Dallas Housing Authority](https://dhantx.com/)'s telephone number is 214-951-8350 or 214-951-8330. Their address is 3939 N. Hampton Road, Dallas, Texas.  

Category: Homeowners, Homebuyers, Lenders & Realtors, Renters & Landlords, Contractors, Developers, Housing Policy, Data and Reports, Forms & Documents
#### I am having trouble getting my landlord to fix my apartment. Who can I call?
The City of Dallas Department of Housing & Community Development does not intervene in landlord-tenant disputes. The [Department of Code Compliance](https://dallascityhall.com/departments/codecompliance/Pages/default.aspx) offers helpful [Know Your Rights as a Tenant](https://dallascityhall.com/departments/codecompliance/Pages/Multi-Tenant.aspx) resouces and can be reached at 214-670-5708. Organizations that may be able to assist are the: (1) [Housing Crisis Center](https://www.hccdallas.org/) at 214-828-4244, (2) [Texas Tenants Union](https://www.txtenants.org/) at 214-823-2733, (3)[ Legal ](https://legalaidtx.org/) at 214-748-1234.  

Category: Homebuyers, Lenders & Realtors, Renters & Landlords
#### I just lost my home/apartment. Who can help me find temporary shelter?
Call the [Office of Homeless Solutions](https://dallascityhall.com/departments/homeless-solutions/Pages/default.aspx) at 214-670-3696 or visit their website and click on the Crisis Resources-Referral Sheet.  

Category: Homeowners, Renters & Landlords
#### Is your home repair program reserved only for elderly/disabled persons?
You are not required to be elderly or disabled to be eligible for our program. Visit the [Home Improvement & Preservation Program](http://www.dallascityhall.com/HIPP) (HIPP) website to find out more about the HIPP program.  

Category: Developers, Contractors, Homeowners
#### I am trying to sell my home, but the City has a lien on my property. How do I get it removed?
If you previously received home repair or homebuyer assistance from the City or purchased a home that was built on a Land Bank lot, please call 214-670-3644 or visit the [Compliance & Lien Portfolio](https://dallascityhall.com/departments/housing-neighborhood-revitalization/Pages/Lien-Releases.aspx) website to complete the Lien Release Intake Form. Please note the process and timeline of a release of lien can be from 30-45 business days. If the lien is a mow-clean lien, weed, junk, or debris removal please contact the [Department of Code Compliance](https://dallascityhall.com/departments/codecompliance/Pages/default.aspx) at 214-670-5708. For unpaid water bills please contact [Dallas Water Utilities](https://dallascityhall.com/departments/waterutilities/pages/default.aspx) at 214-651-1441. And for paving assessments please contact the [Special Collections Department](https://dallascityhall.com/departments/waterutilities/special_collections/Pages/default.aspx) at 214-670-3438.  

Category: Homeowners, Housing Policy
#### Is your Homebuyer Assistance Program only for first-time homebuyers?
You are not required to be a first-time homebuyer to be eligible for our program. To learn more about eligibilty and how to apply, please visit the [Dallas Homebuyer Assistance Program](https://dallascityhall.com/departments/housing-neighborhood-revitalization/Pages/Dallas-Homebuyer-Assistance-Program-DHAP.aspx) website.   

Category: Homebuyers, Housing Policy
#### What is Land Bank and Land Transfer?
​The Land Bank and Land Transfer programs sell vacant lots to developers to build affordable homes for low to moderate income homebuyers seeking to purchase a home in the city limits of Dallas. A program overview, the application, and a list of lots for each program can be found on each website: [Dallas Housing Acquisition and Development Corporation](https://dallascityhall.com/departments/building-services/real-estate/Pages/Urban-land-bank-demonstration-program.aspx) (Land Bank) and [Land Transfer Program](https://dallascityhall.com/departments/housing-neighborhood-revitalization/Pages/Land-transfer.aspx) (LTP). To contact please email: dallasulbdp@dallascityhall.com.  

Category: Homebuyers, Contractors, Developers, Housing Policy, Forms & Documents
#### I have a lien on my home that I need to be released. Can you help?
​Visit the Housing & Community Development [Compliance & Lien Portfolio](https://dallascityhall.com/departments/housing-neighborhood-revitalization/Pages/Lien-Releases.aspx) page to complete the Lien Release Intake Form. Please note the process and timeline of a release of lien can be from 30-45 business days. If the lien is a mow-clean lien, weed, junk, or debris removal please contact the [Department of Code Compliance](https://dallascityhall.com/departments/codecompliance/Pages/default.aspx) at 214-670-5708. For unpaid water bills please contact [Dallas Water Utilities](https://dallascityhall.com/departments/waterutilities/pages/default.aspx) at 214-651-1441. And for paving assessments please contact the [Special Collections Department](https://dallascityhall.com/departments/waterutilities/special_collections/Pages/default.aspx) at 214-670-3438.  

Category: Homeowners, Homebuyers, Lenders & Realtors, Housing Policy, Forms & Documents
#### I need mortgage/rental assistance Can you help?
​The Department of Housing & Community Development does not currently have a mortgage or rental assistance program. The [Dallas County Housing Programs](https://www.dallascounty.org/departments/housing/) offers two options; for the [Emergency Rental Assistance/Welfare Program](https://www.dallascounty.org/departments/dchhs/human-services/welfare-assist.php) please call 214-819-2000; or for the [Section 8 Rental Assistance Program](https://www.dallascounty.org/departments/dchhs/human-services/housing-agency.php) please call 214-819-1871 (Applications for assistance are accepted when the waiting list is open). Other organizations that may be able to assist are: (1) [Abounding Prosperity, Inc.](https://www.aboundingprosperity.org/cod-erap-2/) at 214-421-4802, (2) The Stewpot at 214-746-2785, or (3) The International Rescue Committee at 214-461-9781.  

Category: Renters & Landlords, Housing Policy
#### I have a water leak and need plumbing repairs. Can you help?
The [Home Improvement & Preservation Program](http://www.dallascityhall.com/HIPP) (HIPP) provides minor and major home repairs. Call us at 214-670-3644 or visit the HIPP website to learn more about the program and it's current status. Dallas Water Utilities offers a [Minor Plumbing Repair Program](https://savedallaswater.com/rebates-and-incentives/minor-plumbing-repair/). Contact the City of Dallas Water Conservation division for more information at 214-670-3155.  

Category: Homeowners, Housing Policy
#### I don't have heat/AC. Can you help?
The [Home Improvement & Preservation Program](http://www.dallascityhall.com/HIPP) (HIPP) provides minor and major home repairs. Call us at 214-670-3644 or visit the HIPP website to learn more about the program and it's current status. The [Dallas County Housing Programs](https://www.dallascounty.org/departments/housing/) offers a [Weatherization Program](https://www.dallascounty.org/departments/dchhs/human-services/wap.php) that provides energy-related improvements to low-income residences. For more information about this program, please visit their website or call 214-819-1909.  

Category: Homeowners, Housing Policy
