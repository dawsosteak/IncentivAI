#  ENERGY STAR® 
ENERGY STAR®-qualified products and practices help you save electricity and money, as well as reduce greenhouse gas emissions by meeting strict energy efficiency guidelines set by the US Environmental Protection Agency (EPA) and the US Department of Energy (DOE). The ENERGY STAR label designates superior energy performance in homes and buildings.   
  
Anaheim Public Utilities offers a range of incentives and rebates to encourage Anaheim residents to replace old, inefficient appliances and equipment with energy-efficient, ENERGY STAR-qualified appliances, to save energy and money.   
  
Our Home Incentives Program is specifically designed to encourage installation of ENERGY STAR-rated appliances - including refrigerators, air conditioning and lighting - and high-efficiency household conservation measures.  
  
More About ENERGY STAR  
You can learn more about ENERGY STAR at [energystar.gov](http://www.energystar.gov/).
