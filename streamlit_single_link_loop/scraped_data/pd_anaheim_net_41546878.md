#  Home & Business Security Inspections 
The Anaheim Police Department offers **free** Security Inspections to Residents and Businesses in the City of Anaheim. The inspection will include a review of your home or business's existing locks, lighting, and landscaping, and identify any problematic areas where your home or business is unprotected. The inspection typically takes approximately 20 to 30 minutes. The goal of the Police Department is to enhance your personal protection and safety.
