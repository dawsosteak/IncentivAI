# State of the City
Anaheim Mayor Ashleigh Aitken held her inaugural State of the City address and luncheon on May 23, 2023, at City National Grove of Anaheim.
Centered around the theme of "We Are Anaheim," Mayor Aitken talked about how Anaheim neighborhoods are seeing renewal and a focus on services touching the daily lives of residents as the city’s record economic recovery continues with major investment planned in years to come.
You can read Mayor Aitken's State of the City remarks below and watch an archived livestream of the event below.
The 2023 State of the City was a benefit supporting a stronger Anaheim with proceeds going to the Anaheim Community Foundation, a nonprofit that supports an array of programs, services and other nonprofits, all to enrich the lives of Anaheim residents and our community.
