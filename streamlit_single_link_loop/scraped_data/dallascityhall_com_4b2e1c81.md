The Dallas Financial Empowerment Center (Dallas FEC) in partnership with International Rescue Committee Dallas and Ascend Dallas provides free access to a personal professional financial coach. In short, a financial coach is someone that helps you money related issues.  

### Did you know?
The survey, "[Stress in America](https://www.apa.org/news/press/releases/stress/2022/infographics-march)," from the American Psychological Association, found that 87 percent of Americans say the rising prices of everyday items due to inflation is a significant source of stress.  

**If you are feeling this way, you can do something about it today.** Set up a financial counseling session by submitting your information here. (Services are temporarily unavailable.) A financial counselor will contact you to find a time to meet in person or virtually.
### We can work on:  

  * Reducing Financial Stress  

  * Planning for financial emergencies


### Setting and Achieving Financial Goals:
  * Eliminating Debt  

  * Increasing Your Credit Score
  * Increase your Earning Potential  



**If you have additional questions, please contact us!**
