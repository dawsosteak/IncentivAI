# Government and Legislative Affairs: State
Government and Legislative Affairs
## State
  
We partner with state leadership in Austin to support and advance legislation that promotes a vibrant quality of life for residents and business choosing to call Dallas home. 
  

During the 89th Texas Legislative Session, the City of Dallas will pursue and support legislation that reinforces the City’s ability and discretion to meet the needs of residents and will defend against legislation that inhibits or retrains its local autonomy.
In addition, The City of Dallas will pursue and support legislation that maintains local control, ensures fiscal stewardship and provides additional financial tools to maintain the City’s economic vibrancy. The City works diligently to implement fiscally sound and responsible budgets and will pursue legislation that fairly allocates collected fees for City services.
