**​​​​​​​Technological & Accidental Hazards​**
DCH Body
Page Content
Not all hazards are natural disasters (storms) or attacks (active shooters). Some hazards are considered either technological or accidental. 
You can learn more about how to stay safe during technological or accidental hazards -- including:
  * what to do in a power outage
  * what steps to take to store or dispose of household chemicals
  * how to build a kit and make a plan.
