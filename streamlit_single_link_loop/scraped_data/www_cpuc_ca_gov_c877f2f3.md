#  CTF Program Participant Recertification
### Information for Participants
The California Public Utilities Commission requires all CTF participants to periodically recertify their eligibility for the program. To recertify eligibility, participants must submit a Recertification Application through the [eCAP website](https://ecap.cpuc.ca.gov/s/) prior to their Eligibility End Date. Refer to the [CTF Participant Report](https://ecap.cpuc.ca.gov/s/ctf-participants) for participants’ Eligibility End Dates. The CTF program will send participants a notice to recertify prior to their Eligibility End Date. Participants have the option to submit a Recertification Application three months prior to their Eligibility End Date.
To submit a Recertification Application, participants must access their [eCAP](https://ecap.cpuc.ca.gov/s/)[ account](https://ecap.cpuc.ca.gov/s/) and click the “Recertification Application” button for the appropriate service address. [Click here](https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=l6b6cujT2bM) for an instructional video on how to complete the Recertification process within eCAP. Do **not** create a new eCAP account to complete the recertification process. Contact the program at CTFhelp@cpuc.ca.gov for assistance accessing your eCAP account.
Failure to recertify will result in revocation of eligibility and a termination of discounts.
Refer to the [CTF Applicant & Participant Guidebook](https://www.cpuc.ca.gov/-/media/cpuc-website/divisions/communications-division/documents/california-teleconnect-fund/guidebooks/ctfapplicantandparticipantguidebook-v9-published-03022026.pdf) for the program's application requirements and current eligibility criteria.
Please note that it is possible for a participant to obtain reinstated eligibility after eligibility of their old Application Number or CTF-ID ended. When this occurs, the program will assign the participant a new CTF-ID with a new Eligibility Start Date. There will be a gap in eligibility from the participant's prior Eligibility End Date and new Eligibility Start Date. A participant with reinstated eligibility will receive a new CTF-ID and an updated Approval Letter to indicate eligibility.
If you have any questions or need assistance, please contact the program at CTFhelp@cpuc.ca.gov.
