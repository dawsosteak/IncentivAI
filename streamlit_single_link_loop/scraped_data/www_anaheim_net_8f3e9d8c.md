#  About Lead 
## Anaheim has no known lead service lines. 
Anaheim has determined it has no lead or galvanized requiring replacement service lines in its distribution system, including any privately-owned or customer-owned service lines. This determination was based on the following actions as approved by the State Water Resources Control Board, Division of Drinking Water: 
  * Review of available construction and plumbing codes, 
  * Review of water system records, such as maps and drawings, sampling results, and meter installation records, 
  * Inspections conducted during the course of normal operations, and 
  * Representative random field sampling of over 3,000 service lines in Anaheim’s distribution system. 


Anaheim meets all regulatory requirements for lead in its drinking water system: 
  * Anaheim’s water supply does not contain lead and is non-corrosive. 
  * Water samples collected from various locations in Anaheim are tested every three years by an independent laboratory. All results remain in compliance with the U.S. Environmental Protection Agency’s Lead & Copper Rule. 


[Lead and Copper Rule | US EPA](https://www.epa.gov/dwreginfo/lead-and-copper-rule)
## Volunteer to Sample your Water 
  

Anaheim has been regularly testing water within customer’s homes since 1993 in adherence with the U.S. EPA’s Lead and Copper Rule. 
Volunteer to be a water quality sample site for the next round of lead testing by emailing waterquality@anaheim.net. 
If you have immediate concerns about lead in your drinking water and would like a water quality test, you may call us at 714-765-4556.
