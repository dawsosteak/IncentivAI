#  Archery 
### **Beginner Archery**
Do you know how to shoot a bullseye? Join us for open range archery and we will teach you the basic skills needed to point, aim, and shoot an arrow. We will cover the correct way to draw a bow and talk about range safety. Ages 10 and up.
All equipment is provided. Our beginner bows have a 10 lb. draw weight. We recommend that participants are comfortable lifting this amount of weight before enrolling in the course.
Courses are offered every Saturday at the following times:**10:00 am - 11:00 am & *11:30 am - 12:30 pm **
Cost:**$12 per person**
### **Intermediate Archery**
Join us for open range archery at an advanced level. Pre-approval is required by Range Master at Beginner Archery.
Courses are offered every Saturday at the following times:***11:30am - 12:30pm & 1:00pm - 2:00pm **
Cost:**$22 per person**
  

### **Registration**
Pre-registration is required for both beginner & intermediate. Classes marked with * are subject to change.
