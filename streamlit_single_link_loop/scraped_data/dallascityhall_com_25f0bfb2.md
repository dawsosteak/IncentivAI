# Dallas City Attorney's Office Home Page
The City Attorney’s Office mission is to provide the highest quality legal services to the City of Dallas in the most ethical, timely, efficient, and cost-effective manner. To accomplish our mission, Our Office is organized into three key areas.

## About Us
The Dallas City Attorney’s Office represents the City of Dallas in a wide variety of legal matters and is among the largest municipal legal departments in the nation. The Dallas City Attorney’s Office provides transactional and advisory services as general counsel, and litigation services, handling the bulk of lawsuits in-house.
##  Objectives  

  * Timely respond to City Council and department requests for legal services.
  * Actively pursue state law and city ordinance violations that affect residents’ quality of life and negatively impact the community.
  * Zealously defend claims and litigation against the city, its officials, and employees.
  * Continue to develop in-house legal expertise in various areas of practice to minimize the financial impact on taxpayers.


  * ## General Counsel
Our office drafts thousands of contracts, ordinances, and resolutions each year. Daily, we provide legal advice and counsel to city elected officials, city manager, city departments, and boards and commissions regarding Dallas’ strategic and progressive policies, laws, agreements, programs, projects, and services.
[Learn More](https://dallascityhall.com/departments/city-attorney/Pages/General-Counsel.aspx)
  * ## Community Advocacy
Community Prosecution enhances the quality of life for Dallas residents by focusing on proactive, community-oriented solutions to code issues throughout the city.
[Learn More](https://dallascityhall.com/departments/city-attorney/Pages/Community-Prosecution.aspx)
  * ## Litigation
Our Office handles hundreds of lawsuits and other civil matters involving the city every year. Additionally, our Office handles over 100,000 cases involving violations of city ordinances and state law that are filed annually in municipal court.
[Learn More](https://dallascityhall.com/departments/city-attorney/Pages/litigation.aspx)
