#  Public Works - Electronic Plan Review 
Anaheim Public Works pleased to announce the acceptance of online plan submittal using our new Go Post portal.
[Go Post](https://anaheim.eplansoftreview.com/#/login?portal=anaheim) is your one stop place to:
  * Upload Initial and Resubmittals of Plans
  * Submit Applications and Forms
  * Receive Corrections
  * Track Status of Plan Review


Please Note…for the best experience, we recommend using an updated version of **Chrome or Edge**
**Payment Required**  
New projects require a [DTF form](https://www.anaheim.net/DocumentCenter/View/35424/TRUST-ACCOUNT-DTF-SET-UP-FORM) and payment of funds before projects can be processed. Upon submittal of your project including the Signed DTF form (if required) submitted with your plans and acceptance of your plans; you will be directed to our [online portal](https://aca-prod.accela.com/anaheim/default.aspx). Upon payment, your project will be processed and you will receive a receipt.
**Pavement Cut Fee**  
City Council approved a New Pavement Trench Cut Fee on 09/13/2022, effective on 10/28/2022. Click [HERE](https://local.anaheim.net/docs_agend/questys_pub/34987/35017/35020/35077/Documents.htm) to review the approved Ordinance and Resolution.  
  
The Pavement Trench Cut Fee will be charged for any excavation within the public right of way. This includes, but is not limited to; trenching, micro-trenching, bore pits, and potholes within City of Anaheim public streets. This fee will range from $100 to $50,000 +, depending on the extent of the work. A check for the Pavement Trench Cut Fee must be submitted prior to permit issuance at the Public Works Counter and made payable to the City of Anaheim. Please click [](https://www.anaheim.net/DocumentCenter/View/66262/Pavement-Cut-Fee-Calculator-and-Instructions-Rev-8-15-25)[HERE](https://www.anaheim.net/DocumentCenter/View/46624/Pavement-Cut-Fee-Calculator-and-Instructions-Rev-10-28-22) for the Pavement Cut Fee form instructions, and form/estimator.
**Project Types**  
We are offering the following project types for electronic plan submittal. With the submittal of your plan, please include a completed plan check application and any forms that are applicable to your project. For your convenience, we have placed links to the Public Works, Development Services project requirements site below.
  * Abandonments ([_ABA submittal requirements and application_](http://www.anaheim.net/DocumentCenter/View/500/Abandonment-Information-and-Application-PDF?bidId=))
  * Certificate of Compliance (COC) ([_COC submittal requirements, application_](http://www.anaheim.net/DocumentCenter/View/17583/Certificate-of-Compliance-and-Application?bidId=) _and checklist_)
  * Dedication of Right of Way or Easement ([Submittal requirements and checklist](https://www.anaheim.net/DocumentCenter/View/502/Dedication-of-Right-of-Way-or-Easements-PDF?bidId))
  * Encroachment License (ENC) ([_ENC Submittal requirements application and checklist_)](http://www.anaheim.net/DocumentCenter/View/504/Encroachment-License-Information-and-Application-PDF?bidId=)
  * Lot Line Adjustment (LLA) ([_LLA checklist_](http://www.anaheim.net/DocumentCenter/View/508/Lot-Line-Adjustment-Plan-Check-Checklist-PDF?bidId=)) ([_LLA submittal requirements_)](http://www.anaheim.net/DocumentCenter/View/509/Lot-Line-Adjustment-Procedures-PDF?bidId=)
  * Grading (GRA) ([_GRA application_](https://www.anaheim.net/DocumentCenter/View/65754/GRA-PERMIT-APPLICATION-EPR)) ([_GRA checklist_](http://www.anaheim.net/DocumentCenter/View/9129/Grading-Permit-Plan-Checklist-Revised-6-11-15?bidId=)) ([Hillside Grading submittal requirements](https://www.anaheim.net/331/Hillside-Grading-Procedure))
  * Right of Way Improvements/Permit (RCP) ([_RCP application_](https://www.anaheim.net/DocumentCenter/View/65753/RCP-PERMIT-APPLICATION-EPR)) ([_RCP checklist_](http://www.anaheim.net/DocumentCenter/View/9759/Right-of-Way-Construction-Permit-Checklist?bidId=)) ([_RCP submittal requirements_](http://www.anaheim.net/518/Improvement-Procedures))
