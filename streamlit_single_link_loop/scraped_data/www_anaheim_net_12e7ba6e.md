Every year, our audience continues to grow and our goal is to provide high-quality performances for our community. Sponsorships directly offset the production costs and are the reason Southern California residents can continue to savor the rich, cultural entertainment at Pearson Park Amphitheatre. Your contribution is tax-deductible. We are excited to share our line-up with the community. Join us as we make this summer BIGGER, BRIGHTER, and BETTER than EVER!
