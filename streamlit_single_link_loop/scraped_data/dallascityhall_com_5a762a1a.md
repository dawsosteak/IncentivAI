#### Welcome
I am pleased to welcome you to the Dallas Fire-Rescue website, a valuable resource for the city's residents and visitors.
Dallas Fire-Rescue is an innovative department of 2000 members that mirrors our community. We are fortunate to have some of the most talented individuals in the fire service working here. We value the history and tradition of the Dallas Fire Department, an organization built upon the strong shoulders of our past leadership; without them, we could not tackle the challenges of the future. With a new vision, Dallas Fire-Rescue is well on the way to enhancing our world-class status.
As you visit our website, you will find information about the services our department can provide and the projects we are working on to continue to make Dallas a great place to live. Here, you will notice information related to the following divisions:
  * Fire Operations
  * Fire Administration
  * Fire Communications
  * Fire Prevention, Education & Inspection
  * Emergency Medical Services


We are a vibrant department that values our past, sees present possibilities, and embraces future challenges. We are committed to providing the citizens of Dallas with the best service possible. Please let me know if you have any suggestions for improving this website or our fire department.
Thank you for visiting our website.
Chief Justin Ball
