#  Get an Energy Assessment  

Take the easiest and best first step to making your property and your wallet more comfortable.
[ Book an Energy Assessment ](https://energysmartcolorado.formstack.com/forms/residential_enrollment)
A home energy assessment is your roadmap to improving comfort, cutting costs, and reducing emissions. We'll evaluate your building’s performance, identify inefficiencies, and deliver a custom report with prioritized upgrades, estimated ROI, and next-steps.
![CORE staff member conducting a home energy assessment in Aspen, demonstrating their hands-on approach to climate solutions.](https://lirp.cdn-website.com/06c12080/dms3rep/multi/opt/CORE-311-Independence-PL.9.1.22.567-45ecdc63-1920w.jpeg)
## Schedule an Energy Assessment for Your Home
To schedule a free or discounted energy assessment for your residential building, fill out the [form.](https://energysmartcolorado.formstack.com/forms/residential_enrollment)
#### How It Works
**What an Assessment Costs**
  * Free for [Community Priority Participants](https://www.aspencore.org/community-priority-participants). Select "CORE Community Priority" in the Locally Qualified Program section of Step 4 in the [form](https://energysmartcolorado.formstack.com/forms/residential_enrollment).
  * $100 (a $600 value) for residential properties that don't qualify as a Community Priority Participant
  * If the building is between 3,001 square feet and 5,999 square feet, an additional $0.05 per square foot is added to the cost of the assessment
  * If the building is 6,000 square feet or greater, an additional $0.10 per square foot is added to the cost of the assessment


  

**What an Assessment Includes**
  * Blower door test to detect energy leaks
  * Detection of carbon monoxide and gas leaks
  * Free, quick-fix solutions such as LED lights, pipe wrap, and boiler blankets
  * Comprehensive report outlining your home’s inefficiencies and opportunities for improvement
  * Personalized guidance by an [Energy Concierge](https://www.aspencore.org/energy-concierge) to go through the report and identify energy saving opportunities to improve your property moving forward
  * An [Energy Concierge](https://www.aspencore.org/energy-concierge) connecting you with [funding options](https://www.aspencore.org/grants-and-funding-programs) to cover improvement costs


  

**What You Will Provide to Complete the Assessment  
**
  * Utility bills
  * Basic information about your home
  * Access to your space
