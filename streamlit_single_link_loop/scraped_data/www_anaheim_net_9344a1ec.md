# The 2026 fireworks fundraiser season is here!
Each year, eight Anaheim-based nonprofits and eight high schools sell Safe and Sane fireworks —the only fireworks allowed in Anaheim and only on July 4 —to raise money they use all year for their community programs.
A lottery is held yearly to select the nonprofits that will operate stands. Operation includes submitting a permit, staffing the stand and providing onsite security 24 hours a day, seven days a week, from June 28 through July 4.  
  
**Completed Firework Stand Permit applications must be submitted by mail to Anaheim Fire & Rescue Community Risk Reduction Division, 201 S. Anaheim Blvd., Suite 300, Anaheim, CA 92805, postmarked no later than June 1, 2026.**
[![Fireworks application Opens in new window](https://www.anaheim.net/ImageRepository/Document?documentID=70741)](https://www.anaheim.net/DocumentCenter/View/70742/Final-Fireworks-Permit-Application-2026-fillable)
##  **Important dates:**  
  

  * **Thursday, March 19: Applications posted**
  * **Thursday, April 23: Applications due**
  * **Wednesday, April 29: Lottery at 4 p.m. via Microsoft Teams**
  * **Friday, May 1: Permit application posted**
  * **Monday, June 1: Permit applications due**
  * **Sunday, June 28: Sales begin**
