#  Utility Advice Letters for Emergency Supply Procurement
## Utility Advice Letters for Emergency Supply Procurement
In February 2021, the CPUC [directed](https://docs.cpuc.ca.gov/PublishedDocs/Published/G000/M366/K441/366441341.PDF) PG&E, SCE, and SDG&E to contract for capacity that is available to serve peak and net peak demand in the summer of 2021 and seek approval for cost recovery in rates. Following are Advice Letters submitted by the CPUC as mandated by the CPUC's Decision.
  * [SDGE AL 3868-E](https://www.cpuc.ca.gov/-/media/cpuc-website/divisions/energy-division/documents/summer-2021-reliability/sdge-al-3868-e-non-standard-disposition-letter.pdf)
