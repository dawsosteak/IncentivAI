#  Anaheim Public Library Free Training Courses 
The Anaheim Public Library offers a range of free online resources. Use them for your research, learning and information needs. Choose from free technology courses, language learning programs, GED test preparation, and much more. You will need your Anaheim Public Library Card number to access many of these sites. Don't have a card? Apply for an online services card [here](http://anaheim.net/FormCenter/Library-8/Library-Account-Application-68).
**Gale Courses** offers a wide range of highly interactive, instructor led courses that you can take entirely online. Library cardholders can select from hundreds of free instructor-led courses covering technology, personal finance, career development, including test prep and certification. Courses run for six weeks and new sessions begin every month. Click [here](https://education.gale.com/l-anaheim_main/) for more information. 
**Testing and Education Reference Center (TERC)** is designed to help students and career seekers prepare for their future, and excel at the next level. Includes over 300 practice tests and courses, from SATs to Law Enforcement Exams. Also offers dozens of eBooks containing valuable study material and practice tests, information on over 4,000 accredited schools, a scholarship search tool, resume builder and career modules covering subjects from career change to salary negotiations. Click [here](http://ana.rpa.sirsidynix.net/rpaana/webauth.exe?rs=TERC) to log in using your Anaheim Public Library card number. For more information visit the Anaheim Public Library [Careers and Education](https://www.anaheim.net/1125/Careers-Education) page.
**Brainfuse Help Now** offers live interactive help for all ages from 1:00 to 10:00 pm daily. Connect with live tutors on just about any subject, and submit your written reports for writing reviews and suggestions. The Adult Learning Center connects you with MS Office experts and help with resume writing, test prep and job search tips. Click [here](http://ana.rpa.sirsidynix.net/rpaana/webauth.exe?rs=brainfuse) to log in using your Anaheim Public Library card number. Use this link if you are [inside the library or city network](https://alc.brainfuse.com/alc/home.asp?adult=1&a_id=A261C51C).
