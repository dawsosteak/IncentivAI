**Anaheim Anytime** provides our residents, businesses and visitors the ability to **share problems, issues or other concerns they see in their neighborhood or around town** with the city 24 hours a day, seven days a week.
Anaheim Anytime is our **p****artnership with our community to address issue and maintain neighborhoods**. Use it to report **graffiti, abandoned items, streetlight outages** and other issues. It's the most effective way to bring issues to our attention, with every Anaheim Anytime inquiry **assigned and followed up on**. 
**Please note:** Requests are monitored during business hours. If you have an **emergency, please call 911**. If you have a **non-emergency police issue** , question or request, please call (714) 765-1900.
