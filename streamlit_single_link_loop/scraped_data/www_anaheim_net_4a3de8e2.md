#  Green Power Enrollment 
* * *
### The enrollment period will be open from April through May of 
### each calendar year to existing participants only.
* * *
![greenpower how it works](https://www.anaheim.net/ImageRepository/Document?documentID=42708)
### Are you interested in purchasing more renewable energy?
  
If so, Anaheim Public Utilities is offering an easy way with the Green Power Program. This voluntary program allows residents and businesses to support more renewable energy above Anaheim’s state-mandated target to meet clean energy goals.
You select your contribution level, and an additional 4.8¢ per kilowatt-hour (kWh) will be billed to the portion of your energy needed to meet your 60% or 100% renewable power selection. Anaheim will purchase clean, green power on your behalf, and you will receive a certificate each year to thank you for your commitment to green power.
If average residential monthly consumption is 500 kWh, the following applies:
  * 60% Green Power: If you are already enrolled and continue next year, your monthly cost will decrease by approximately $0.48.
  * 100% Green Power: If you are already enrolled and continue next year, your monthly cost will increase by approximately $0.32.

## Green Power Program Calculator
A typical residential customer consumes approximately 500 kWh a month. However, any residential participant may enter their estimated monthly consumption and their desired green power percentage to receive an approximate monthly average cost of participating in the program beginning in July 2026.
  * Renewable Portfolio for 7/1/25 - 6/30/26: 48%
  * Green Power Rate per kWh ($0.044)
  * Renewable Portfolio for 7/1/26 - 6/30/27: 51%; Green Power Rate per kWh ($0.048)
