#  Resources and Reporting
**Safety Checklist:** The Safety and Enforcement Division has worked with Energy Division to develop safety requirements checklists associated with the IOUs’ transportation electrification programs that provide guidelines to ensure the safety of utility workers, contractors, and program participants.
  * For the [large IOUs](https://www.cpuc.ca.gov/-/media/cpuc-website/files/legacyfiles/s/6442458882-safety-requirements-checklist-final-draft-.pdf)
  * For the [small IOUs](https://www.cpuc.ca.gov/-/media/cpuc-website/files/legacyfiles/s/6442462124-safety-requirements-checklist-for-d1809034-final-.docx)


**Data Templates:** Most of the current transportation electrification infrastructure programs require the IOUs to utilize templates for their annual data reporting.
  * [Priority Review Programs](https://www.cpuc.ca.gov/-/media/cpuc-website/divisions/energy-division/documents/sb-350-te/sb-350-data-template-feb-2021.xlsx)
  * [Standard Review Programs](https://www.cpuc.ca.gov/-/media/cpuc-website/divisions/energy-division/documents/sb-350-te/sb-350-data-template-feb-2021.xlsx)
  * [SCE Charge Ready 2](https://www.cpuc.ca.gov/-/media/cpuc-website/divisions/energy-division/documents/transportation-electrification/charge-ready-2-data-template-_08122022.xlsx)
  * [AB 841](https://www.cpuc.ca.gov/-/media/cpuc-website/divisions/energy-division/documents/transportation-electrification/copy-of-ev-infrastructure-rules-data-collection-template.xlsx)

**Research Database:** In 2018, CPUC collected pilot project information through [survey](https://ia.cpuc.ca.gov/zevsurvey/ "survey on zero-emission vehicle infrastructure and vehicle-grid integration research") on ZEV infrastructure and VGI research. The [survey results](https://ia.cpuc.ca.gov/form/result.aspx?id=13 "survey results") are publicly available and can be downloaded as an Excel file. The database includes for each project: contact information, location, participants, objectives, rates, funding, timeline, vehicles, equipment, standards, and software.
