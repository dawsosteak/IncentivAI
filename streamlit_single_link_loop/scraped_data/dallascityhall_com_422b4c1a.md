### ​​When is a super graphic permit required?
​The City of Dallas Super Graphic program was designed to add life and color to the city’s streetscape while at the same time promoting special events and other cultural or sporting activities of benefit to the City. A permit is required for the placement of oversized event signage, including sponsor or identification signs, placed in or over a public right-of-way, or on private property, where otherwise prohibited by ordinance.
​ 
## Procedure
###  1Gather Information Required on Application
Property address and authorization (if applicable), scheduled dates of installation and removal, proposed super graphic design, City of Dallas contractor registration number, and certificate of insurance.  

###  2Complete Application
Complete and submit the application through Eproval within the applicable number of days prior to the first day of setup for your super graphic. Incomplete applications will not be processed.​  

###  3Pay Application Processing Fees
All application processing fees must be paid to secure your dates and location and to begin the permit review process. OSE will send you an invoice via email after the application has been accepted.  

  

###  4Permit Review Process
Numerous City Departments and partner agencies are involved in the application review process.​  

###  5Applicant Submittals
As the reviews are completed, any additional requirements from reviewing departments must be completed and submitted through your Eproval portal in order to issue your permit.​  

###  6Complete All Requirements
​Submit all required items in your Eproval super graphic application.​  

###  7Permit Issuance & Final Conditions
Upon confirming all requirements have been met, the Office of Special Events will issue the permit. The permit will list the conditions and/or restrictions for which the applicant must uphold. It is imperative to read the permit very carefully to understand all of the final conditions.​
