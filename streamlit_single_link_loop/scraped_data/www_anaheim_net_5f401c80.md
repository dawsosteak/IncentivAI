#  Anaheim Library App 
![Library app banner](https://www.anaheim.net/ImageRepository/Document?documentID=65984)
## The Anaheim Public Library has a new app where you can discover your next favorite read, check your account, and reserve titles at your local branch.
  

**Apple Devices using the Apple App Store:**
  * Go to the Apple App Store and search **"Anaheim Public Library"**


![Apple store library app](https://www.anaheim.net/ImageRepository/Document?documentId=65995)
  * Download the App and Open
  * Click **"Login"** and enter your Library Card Number (Borrow Number) and PIN
    * Library Card Number can be found underneath the barcode.
    * Your PIN is the year of birth (ex. 1980)
  * Now you're ready to explore! 


**Androids using Google Play:**
  * Go to the Google Play Store and search **"Anaheim Public Library"**![Google play store app](https://www.anaheim.net/ImageRepository/Document?documentId=65997)
  * Download the App and Open
  * Click **"Login"** and enter your Library Card Number (Borrow Number) and PIN
    1. Library Card Number can be found underneath the barcode.
    2. Your PIN is the year of birth (ex. 1980)
  * Now you're ready to explore!


## Explore your Anaheim Library at your fingertips  
  

Library users can manage their account, keep track of reading history, renew books, and place holds ― right from   
your device.
Read the instructions here in [English.](https://www.anaheim.net/DocumentCenter/View/66211/New-App-Flyer---English)
The new app will allow you to:
  * Search the Library Catalog and place items on hold
  * Manage your account, renew materials, and pay fines
  * Access your digital library card for contactless in-person checkouts
  * Find hours, location information, and the nearest branch to your location


Download the app today, and start exploring!
