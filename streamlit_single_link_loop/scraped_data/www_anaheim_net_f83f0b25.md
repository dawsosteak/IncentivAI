#  Anaheim Religious Community Council 
![ARCC Logo](https://www.anaheim.net/ImageRepository/Document?documentID=10576)
## Mission
Our mission at the Anaheim Religious Community Council (ARCC) is to provide a platform where religious leaders can voice concerns regarding community needs in Anaheim. We aim to strategize collectively on addressing these needs and to collaborate with individuals or congregations to implement solutions.
### About ARCC
Comprising ministers and lay leaders representing religious organizations citywide, ARCC is an inclusive interfaith initiative fostering a healthier, more welcoming, and safer community in Anaheim. We extend a warm invitation to all churches, synagogues, mosques, temples, and faith-based groups to participate in this collaborative endeavor.
Recognized for its significant contribution to the well-being of Anaheim's residents and neighborhoods, the religious community plays a pivotal role in our city's long-term vitality. ARCC was born out of a recommendation from the Gang Drug Task Force in 1993, with a primary focus on facilitating communication among religious leaders to address youth development challenges.
One of our earliest objectives was the establishment of Youth/Family Activity Centers in Anaheim, many of which are hosted within church facilities. These centers serve as hubs for sports events and educational programs, contributing to the holistic development of our youth.
Through regular engagements with the City of Anaheim, ARCC strives to optimize the utilization of community-wide resources and promote cooperative strategies. At the heart of ARCC's endeavors lies the ethos of people helping people, as we endeavor to meet the ministering needs of our community through compassionate action.
