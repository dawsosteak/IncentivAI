# Think Globally, Act Locally
## CORE works collaboratively with local municipalities to cut carbon in the Roaring Fork Valley
Recognizing that we’re stronger together, these valued partners contract with CORE to provide energy saving programs and services.  

Our local governments are turning climate ambition into real-world impact—leading the way with bold policies, smart planning, and community-driven solutions.
