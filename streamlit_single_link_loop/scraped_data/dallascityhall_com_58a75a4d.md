    * Service Alert: Possible Collection Delays Next Week Due to Winter Weather
Sanitation is closely monitoring weather forecasts that indicate a strong likelihood of dangerously low temperatures on Monday and Tuesday mornings next week, with the potential for unsafe road conditions.
  

If conditions warrant, brush and bulky item collections and routes that require manual cart servicing may be temporarily suspended to ensure employee safety. These suspensions could result in cascading collection delays throughout the week.
  

If your garbage or recycling carts are not serviced as scheduled, please leave them at the collection point until collection occurs. Customers are encouraged to continue monitoring [ **City communications**](https://www.dallascitynews.net/city-of-dallas-facility-closures-and-service-updates-due-to-inclement-weather-2026)for updates and additional guidance.

Materials Accepted
  * Commercial waste
  * Household waste
  * Municipal waste
  * Grass clippings/brush
  * Appliances (refrigerants removed, if applicable)
  * Furniture
  * Tires (limit 6 per year from Dallas residents, not exceeding 25 inches in diameter)  

  * Electronics (residents only)  



Information for dallas residents  

  * There is no charge for Dallas residents in passenger cars, station wagons, pickups, and trailers less than 15 feet long hauling their own waste from their residences to the landfill or transfer station (A current, valid Texas driver’s license showing a Dallas address or a current Dallas water utilities bill is required as proof of residency.)  

  * Loads with loose materials are required to be covered to prevent spillage. Uncovered loads entering the landfill are subject to a $40 fee.
