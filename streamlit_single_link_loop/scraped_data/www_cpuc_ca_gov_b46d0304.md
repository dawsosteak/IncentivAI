#  New Filings - Week of March 16-20, 2026 
![New Filings graphic with page coming out of a computer screen](https://www.cpuc.ca.gov/-/media/cpuc-website/divisions/news-and-outreach/images/featured-stories/header-graphics/new-filings.png?mw=1440&hash=972FD667893B12BD41DC6BF64C6BD182)
March 23, 2026 - 
Each week, new filings are added to ongoing and new proceedings that impact California’s utility services, communities, and consumers. These include Proposed Decisions, many of which are open for public comment, as well as new complaints or applications that may shape future policy and regulatory actions.
If a Proposed Decision is listed, you can submit a written comment directly on the proceeding’s Docket Card. For any proceeding, you can also subscribe to the CPUC’s Subscription Service using the proceeding number to receive updates, rulings, and notices.
