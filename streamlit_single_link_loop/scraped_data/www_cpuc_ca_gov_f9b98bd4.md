#  CPUC Annual Reports
As part of its mission to serve the public, and as required by state law, the CPUC provides the Governor and Legislature an Annual Report on its activities for the prior year and a look ahead at the work expected over the next 12 months. 
## Summary of Annual Reports to the Legislature
Each year the CPUC issues an Annual Report to the Legislature highlighting activities and providing a work plan for the coming year. See our Annual Reports below.
For Gas Safety and Reliability Branch (GSRB) and Electric Safety and Reliability Branch (ESRB) incidents reports, please visit this [page](https://www.cpuc.ca.gov/regulatory-services/safety/annual-electric-and-gas-incidents). 
For other reports to the Legislature (on energy, telco, etc.) see [Reports to the Legislature](https://www.cpuc.ca.gov/about-cpuc/divisions/office-of-governmental-affairs) on our Governmental Affairs page.
