# Pearson Park Pool
400 North Harbor Blvd. Anaheim, CA 92805
The city of Anaheim partners with the Anaheim Family YMCA to offer swim lessons at Pearson Park Pool. For more information and to register, please contact the Anaheim Family YMCA at (714) 635-9622 or visit [anaheimymca.org online](http://www.anaheimymca.org)[.](http://www.anaheimymca.org)
The Anaheim Community Services Department has opened its Aquatics programs at Pearson Park Pool, Canyon High School, and Anaheim High School with a thorough safety plan that follows state and county guidelines and public health best practices. Online registration is now available for weekday and weekend swim lessons and recreation swim. 
[**Download the swim levels with class descriptions.**](https://www.anaheim.net/DocumentCenter/View/32367)
