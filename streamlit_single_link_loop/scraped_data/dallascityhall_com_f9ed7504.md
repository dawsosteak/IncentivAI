Consumer Protection  

DCH Body
​
The Department of Code Compliance's Consumer Protection Unit investigates claims of fraudulent business practices in service-related industries and regulates the following types of licenses.
  

  * Commercial Business District (CBD) Vacant Building
  * Convenience Store
  * Credit Access Business
  * Unattended Drop Box
  * Electronic Repair
  * Home Repair
  * Mobile Tire Repair Decal(s)
  * Motor Vehicle repair
  * Scrap Tire
  * Scrap Tire transporter Repair Decal
  * Wood Vending
  * Vacant Building & Vacant Lot
