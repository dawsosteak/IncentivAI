#  Memory Lab 
The Memory Lab is a DIY space for digitizing and archiving analog media: photographs, film, documents, VHS, audiocassettes, etc. Patrons are responsible for handling their own media. Staff is not responsible for damage to media during the digitization process
## Why Digitize?
### Preserve Memories 
Turn boxes of old photos and home videos into digital copies to keep them accessible and safe from damage.
### Share Heritage 
Save culture and history from being lost to time, and pass on traditions to future generations.
### Archive Knowledge 
Keep a record of lectures, journals, artwork and more to build your personal digital collection.
