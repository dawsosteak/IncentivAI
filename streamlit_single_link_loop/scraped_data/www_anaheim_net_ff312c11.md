#  Central Cashiering 
Central Cashiering for the City of Anaheim is located at 201 South Anaheim Boulevard, 1st floor.  
The division collects and processes all city billed revenue from city wide departments, including but not limited to:  

  * Anaheim issued parking citations
  * Anaheim Police booking fees
  * Anaheim Police officer witness fees
  * Anaheim Public Utility bills
  * City-Finance invoices
  * Code enforcement civil citations

Payments may be made in person at the above address. Cash, checks, Visa, MasterCard, and Discover are accepted for most payment types. Payments mailed in to P.O. Box 3222, must accompany original documentation along with check or money order. Please visit [Anaheim ePay](http://anaheimepay.net/) for payment types that may be made via the city’s website. The central cashier does not receive or process any payments over the phone.
