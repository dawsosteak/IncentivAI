#  Services 
The Services Section of the Anaheim Fire & Rescue plays an integral role in the daily emergency and non-emergency operation of the Fire Department. Our mission is to keep our stations supplied, personnel protected and apparatus ready and operating. The Services Section is led by an Administrative Battalion Chief who has direct supervision of:  
  

  * Services Coordinator
  * Senior Store Keeper
  * SCBA/Small Tools Repair Technician 
  * Driver/Clerk
  * Fire Cadet

The Administrative Battalion Chief reports directly to the Division Chief of Support Services.
