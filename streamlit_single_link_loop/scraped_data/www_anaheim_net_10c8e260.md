Welcome to the home page for the Building Division of the City of Anaheim. Our team of professional engineers, inspectors, and friendly support staff is dedicated to providing the highest level of plan review and inspection services to our community. The goal of our staff is to help our customers build safe and sustainable buildings that comply with applicable codes and regulations.
Please contact our division directly at (714) 765-5153 or select one of the links below to learn more.
