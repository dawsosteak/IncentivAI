#  Stay Housed Anaheim Program 
**UPDATE:** The Stay Housed Anaheim program has temporarily stopped accepting applications at this time. Applicants who are currently under review and have previously received notification requesting documentation will be reviewed in the order received on a first-come, first-served basis. Applicant documentation will be reviewed in accordance with all program requirements. 
This includes verification of:
  * Past due rent/rental arrears or security deposit owed to your landlord
  * Residence within city of Anaheim for the past 12 months as a renter
  * Documentation of being at risk of housing instability or facing a destabilizing event
  * Household income at 50 percent of Orange County's median income or lower
  * Documentation that demonstrates ability to maintain housing beyond assistance period


**_______________________________________________________________**
The Stay Housed Anaheim program promotes housing stability for Anaheim renters by offering one-time emergency assistance with rental arrears _or_ security deposit owed up to $3,000. Assistance with utilities owed up to $500 may only be considered with a rental arrears request.
Applicants must review the eligibility requirements and submit all required documentation to be eligible for review. Incomplete applications will not be considered for assistance.
Submitting an application does not guarantee assistance. If an application is determined not eligible, an applicant may not reapply for a minimum six-month period.
Landlord or utility provider participation is required. If an application is approved, payments are made directly to a landlord or utility provider. 
Additional documentation or verification may be requested at any time to determine eligibility.
Funding for this program is limited. This program may close at any time.
