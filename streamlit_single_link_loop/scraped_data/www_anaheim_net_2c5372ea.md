#  Coronavirus Business Resources 
The City of Anaheim is a thriving community rooted in its support of its local businesses, its neighbors and each other. The City continues to actively monitor the situation with coronavirus to safeguard the health and safety of our residents and businesses. The City continues to work closely with the Orange County Health Care Agency to track the situation and has created the [**Anaheim.net/coronavirus**](http://Anaheim.net/coronavirus) webpage to provide our community with daily updates.
During these difficult times, Anaheim’s Economic Development Team remains committed to providing helpful resources and information to assist our business community. Find helpful links to federal, state and local information on business loans and grants, business recovery tools, workplace health and safety and more below.
