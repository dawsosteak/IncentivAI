# Rulings Search Form
Decisions, Resolutions, and Rulings signed by the Commission starting **July 2000** are now available online. If you need documents issued before July 2000, you can request copies from Central Files by filling this [online form](https://www.cpuc.ca.gov/orderadocument/).

---|---

Proceeding Number: |  (Example: R9901021)  
Publication date: From |  To: (use mm/dd/yyyy)  
* * *
## SEARCH TIPS:
  * Search by Document Title:Enter the last name of the Administrative Law Judge or Commissioner who issued the ruling or a short title of the subject matter.  

  

  * And/or by Industry: if you know the pertaining industry to the ruling, select the appropriate industry from the drop down list to search by industry.  

  

  * And/or by Publication Date:enter the date range of which the ruling was published.
