#  Winter Reading Program 
![WRP 2026 Banner final](https://www.anaheim.net/ImageRepository/Document?documentID=67346)
## Winter Reading 2026
## Jan. 12 - Feb. 22 
## ALL AGES
  

The Anaheim Public Library’s annual **winter reading program** offers epic prizes to encourage you to meet your reading goals. The more you read, the more chances you'll get to win awesome prizes. 
Visit your local [APL branch](https://anaheim.net/903/Locations-Hours "https://anaheim.net/903/Locations-Hours") to pick up a reading log, or register and log your reading on Beanstack.
APL is now on Beanstack. Click [**here**](https://anaheim.beanstack.com/) to check out Beanstack and the app to log your reading progress, complete challenges, check out book lists, and more!
