#  Sale of city property in central Anaheim 
The city is selling vacant property in central Anaheim.
The land is located next to the southbound 5 Santa Ana Freeway, at Lincoln and Manchester Avenues.
Anaheim is selling the empty parcels to Manchester Lincoln, LLC at a sale price of approximately $9.2 million.
This sale would generate an estimated $2.9 million in revenue that would go to Anaheim's general fund for daily services and operations.
![An aerial photo showing a map and location of city property](https://www.anaheim.net/ImageRepository/Document?documentID=63474)
A public hearing will be held on this item during the City Council meeting on Tuesday, April 8 at 5:30 p.m. Anyone interested in providing comments concerning the sale may do so during this time.
In addition, written comments may be submitted to the City Clerk prior to the hearing. For more information and assistance on submitting comments, contact the City Clerk's Office at (714) 765-5166.
The proposed agreement and summary report are available for public viewing at the City Clerk's Office between 8 a.m. and 5 p.m., Monday through Friday at Anaheim City Hall.
The agreement is set to go before the City Council for consideration on April 8.
View relevant documents:  
[Disposition and Development Agreement](https://www.anaheim.net/DocumentCenter/View/63472/Disposition-and-Development-Agreement-1501-W-Lincoln)  
[Proposed Sale Report](https://www.anaheim.net/DocumentCenter/View/63473/Proposed-sale-report-Manchester-Lincoln-LLC)  
[Quitclaim Deed](https://www.anaheim.net/DocumentCenter/View/63471/Quitclaim-Deed-Splinter)
For more information, contact Economic Development Director Sergio Ramirez at (714) 765-4627.
