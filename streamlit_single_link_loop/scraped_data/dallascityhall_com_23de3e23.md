When is a streetlight pole banner permit required?
Streetlight pole banners in association with special events, Public Improvement Districts, or other activities such as art shows, concerts, festivals, major sports celebrations, parades and runs are eligible to apply for a streetlight pole banner permit.   

Please note, the placement of banners for the purpose of commercial advertising is not allowed as part of this program.
  

## Procedure
###  1Gather Information Required on Application
​Identify all pole locations (block number, street name, and FLN), and include a sample of your graphics. Please reference the [guidebook](https://dallascityhall.com/departments/office-special-events/Documents/Streetlight%20Pole%20Guide%201.5.2022.pdf) for more information.  

###  2Complete Application
Complete and submit the application within the applicable number of days prior to the start of your banner campaign. Incomplete applications will not be processed.  

###  3Pay Application Processing Fees
All application processing fees must be paid to secure dates and pole locations and begin the review process. OSE will send you an invoice via email after reviewing and accepting the application.  

###  4Permit Review Process
Numerous City Departments and partner agencies are involved in the application review process.
###  5Preliminary Letter
Once the reviews are complete, the OSE Coordinator will send you an email outlining and identifying what requirements must be met in order to issue your permit.
###  6Permit Issuance & Final Conditions
Upon confirming all requirements have been met, the Office of Special Events will issue the permit. The permit will list the conditions and/or restrictions for which the applicant must uphold. It is imperative to read the permit very carefully to understand all of the final conditions.

## When should I apply?
## 30 Days
Submit a complete application online at least 30 days prior to the banner installation date.  

## 2 Weeks
Extension requests shall be submitted no earlier than two weeks prior to the expiration of an existing streetlight pole banner permit.
