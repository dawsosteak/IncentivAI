#  East Anaheim Community Center 
Let the East Anaheim Community Center assist you in planning your next event. The center offers splendid accommodations for intimate weddings, baby showers, family, school and community events. Our 4 meeting rooms and Board Room provide business and adult education groups with a professional setting for training sessions, seminars, and classes.

## Amenities
To enhance your program, audio visual equipment and a podium are available at no additional charge. The center has:
  * Meeting accommodations for 12 to 120 people
  * Banquet seating for up to 120 people
  * Kitchen with microwave, refrigerator, food warmer, coffee maker, sink, and ice maker
  * Alcohol service permitted upon approval (Additional fees apply as security guards are required)
  * Round and rectangular banquet tables
  * Four meeting rooms
  * Board room and Sycamore room with dry-erase boards
  * Dance room with oak floors and mirrored walls
  * Portable speaker sound system with Bluetooth compatibility
  * Free parking
  * Free Wi-Fi
  * Patio access off of Oak Room (great for outdoor food vendors)
