#  Business Resources 
Here are some helpful links to resources for businesses in Anaheim:  

  * ### [California Governor's Office of Business and Economic Development (GO Biz)](https://business.ca.gov/)
    * **Sales & Use Tax Exemptions (for equipment purchases and property):**[**Full Sales & Use Tax Exclusion (CAEATFA)**](https://www.treasurer.ca.gov/caeatfa/ste/index.asp)
    * **Expansion & Growth: **[**California Competes Tax Credit**](https://business.ca.gov/california-competes-tax-credit/)
    * **Financing Support:**[**Industrial Development Bonds (IDB)**](http://www.ibank.ca.gov/industrial-development-bond-financing/)
    * **Employment (workforce training):**[**Employment Training Panel (ETP)**](https://etp.ca.gov/)
  * ### [Small Business Administration](https://www.sba.gov/business-guide/grow-your-business)
    * [**Funding Programs**](https://www.sba.gov/funding-programs)**:** Find funding to start or grow your business, or to help you recover after a declared disaster.
    * [**Business Guide**](https://www.sba.gov/business-guide/10-steps-start-your-business)**:** 10 steps to start your business | U.S. Small Business Administration
    * [**Grow your Business**](https://www.sba.gov/business-guide/grow-your-business)**:** Expand your business when times are good. Find new funding, locations, customers and resources for minority, veteran and rural businesses.
  * ### [Service Corps of Retired Executives (SCORE)](https://score.org/)
    * [**Plan + Start**](https://www.score.org/view-business-stage/plan-start)**:** Planning and starting a small business can be overwhelming. Whether you have a business idea or you’ve just started selling your first products or services, SCORE can help you find your footing. Get matched with an [expert business mentor](https://www.score.org/find-mentor), complete the [Startup Roadmap](https://www.score.org/startup-roadmap) course, and check out our educational resour[ces](https://www.score.org/get-business-training) designed to help you get your business up and running.
    * [**Manage + Grow**](https://www.score.org/view-business-stage/manage-grow)**:** Your business can change over time; you might have new goals you want to achieve, or you may face new challenges. SCORE is available to you no matter where you are in your business lifecycle. Take advantage of mentors and tools to run and grow your business.
  * ### [Small Business Development Center (SBDC)](https://ociesmallbusiness.org/)
    * [**Access to Capital**](https://ociesmallbusiness.org/access-to-capital/)**:** The Orange County Inland Empire SBDC Finance Center helps guide you through the loan process, from assessing your business and preparing a strong loan package to connecting you with reputable lenders. Our consultants work with you to create a bank-ready package, then present it to a network of trusted financial partners, including banks, Community Development Financial Institutions, and nonprofit lenders.
    * [**Contracting and Procurement**](https://ociesmallbusiness.org/contracting/)**:** Government and corporate procurement can be an incredible opportunity to grow your business revenues in a new and diversified manner. The **SBDC Center for Contracting** is here to help you with education, training, and one-on-one consulting, all in support of your efforts to win procurement contracts at the federal, state, local, or private level.
    * [**Manufacturing**](https://ociesmallbusiness.org/manufacturing/)**:** Manufacturing businesses fuel innovation, create quality jobs, and strengthen our local economy. Whether you’re making food, textiles, components, or consumer products, the SBDC is here to help you succeed at every stage — from setup and certifications to process improvement and international sales.
    * [**SBDC Intel**](https://ociesmallbusiness.org/sbdc-intel/)**:** SBDC Intel is a resource available to our clients to supply tools, analysis, and information to improve and optimize the decisions and performance of their business. The SBDC Intel team utilizes resources and databases including ESRI, ISBS World, Profitcents, US Census Bureau, and several others to gather intelligence.
  * ### [Orange County Business Council (OCBC)](https://ocbc.org/)
    * Orange County Business Council (OCBC) has been representing and promoting the region’s business community together with government and academia to enhance the Economic Development of the nation’s sixth most populous county.
