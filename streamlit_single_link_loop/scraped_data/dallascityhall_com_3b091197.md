[​Convention and Event Services (CES)](https://dallascityhall.com/departments/conventioneventservices/Pages/default.aspx) oversees the [Office of Special Events (OSE)](https://dallascityhall.com/departments/office-special-events/Pages/default.aspx) and manages several contracts on behalf of the City of Dallas, including Oak View Group which manages day-to-day operations of the [Kay Bailey Hutchison Convention Center Dallas (KBHCCD)](https://www.dallasconventioncenter.com/). The Convention Center is one of the largest convention centers in the nation, offering 1 million square feet of exhibit space, three ballrooms, 88 meeting rooms, a 1,750-seat theater, and a 9,816-seat arena. Cvent ranks KBHCCD fourth in the nation for meetings and conventions. We welcome over a million visitors annually.  

  

CES also manages the contracts on behalf of the City of Dallas including [The Black Academy of Arts and Letters (TBAAL)](https://www.tbaal.org/), [Visit Dallas](https://www.visitdallas.com/), [Omni Dallas Hotel](https://www.omnihotels.com/hotels/dallas), the [Eddie Bernice Johnson Union Station](https://www.unionstationdallas.com/) and [American Airlines Center](https://www.americanairlinescenter.com/). CES also oversees several outdoor spaces including Pioneer Plaza located just outside KBHCCD.  

  

For more information about the new convention center please visit [DallasCCMasterPlan.com](https://www.dallasccmasterplan.com/).

# Rosa Fleming
Director, Convention and Event Services
## Experience
Rosa Fleming has more than 30 years of progressive work experience in municipal government, business administration, fund development, contract management, contract compliance and hospitality. Rosa is currently the Director for Convention and Event Services. In this role, Rosa is responsible for a portfolio which includes contract management and capital project management for the Kay Bailey Hutchison Convention Center Dallas (KBHCCD), and contract management for the Eddie Bernice Johnson Union Station, American Airlines Center, the Omni Dallas Hotel and LGC, and VisitDallas, among other smaller contracts. In this capacity, Rosa oversees an annual operating budget of over $114 million, a capital budget of more than $62 million, and is currently leading several City of Dallas departments and external partners in the implementation of the multi-layered Kay Bailey Hutchison Convention Center Dallas Master Plan.   
  
Rosa has led the development of this master plan for the last 18 months, with the most recent milestone being the passage of Proposition A which completes the multipart funding plan for the project. Rosa holds several certifications in business administration, fundraising and contract management. She is currently also active on several boards, including VisitDallas, the Dallas Tourism Public Improvement District, and Beyond the Ball.   
  
Born in Brownsville, Texas, Rosa holds degrees in political science and English from Southern Methodist University (SMU). She also attended graduate school at the University of Texas at Arlington where she focused on city and regional planning and holds a Master of Liberal Arts from SMU. She currently lives in Duncanville where she enjoys spending time with her family, and writing, reading, and gardening.
