#  Business Air Purifier Rebate Program 
![Air Purifier](https://www.anaheim.net/ImageRepository/Document?documentID=33398)
Anaheim Public Utilities offers business customers rebates for room air cleaners, also known as air purifier. Air purifiers are portable appliances that remove fine particles, such as pollen, dust, and unpleasant odors from the air. Using ENERGY STAR® portable air purifiers is a great way to improve indoor air quality in a single room or area while using energy efficiently.  
  

  * ENERGY STAR® certified air purifier: $75 per unit (limit 4)
  * For complete rebate program[ guidelines,W-9 and application](https://www.anaheim.net/DocumentCenter/View/33400/Business-Air-Purifier-App).  



  

For more information about ENERGY STAR® certified room air purifiers and a list of certified models, visit [energystar.gov/productfinder](https://www.energystar.gov/productfinder/product/certified-room-air-cleaners/results)
