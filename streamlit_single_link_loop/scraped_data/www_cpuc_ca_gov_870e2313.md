#  Join our Team!
### A Great Place To Work
The California Public Utilities Commission (CPUC) is recognized internationally for its innovative and proactive policy initiatives. The policy standards we create for California are often used to influence the national regulatory trends and federal policies. We work on a variety of initiatives that provide safe, reliable services and infrastructures that ensure that consumers are protected from fraud. We also ensure that all members of our diverse populations within California have equal accessibility to utility services.
### Help Make A Difference
Do you want to make a difference in your career? Then become part of a progressive and influential regulatory Commission that actively creates changes and enforces policies that matter to the lives of Californians. Through the work done at the CPUC, you can, Protect Consumers, Protect the Environment, and help to Establish Innovative Regulatory Policies.
