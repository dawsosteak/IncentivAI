#  Graffiti Removal 
Community Preservation has a proactive graffiti removal program that works 7 days a week and maintains effective working relationships with various organizations to educate and assist with helping keep Anaheim graffiti free. Below are our graffiti removal services that we offer:  

  * **Community Outreach** is provided by our graffiti crew through community events, school programs, neighborhood clean-ups and City sponsored events.
  * **Community Service Hours**


![A man painting over graffiti](https://www.anaheim.net/ImageRepository/Document?documentID=1315)
## Report Graffiti
  

You can [request graffiti online](http://anaheim.net/2671/Anaheim-Anytime-Request?id=2882), or call 311 or 714-765-4311.   
  
To report graffiti in progress contact Anaheim Police Department at 714-765-1911. You may qualify for a $500 reward. Please contact the code enforcement office at 714-765-5158 for detail on how to qualify for a reward.
