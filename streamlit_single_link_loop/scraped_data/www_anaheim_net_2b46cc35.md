Exploration on Wheels, also known as the STEAM van, visits set afterschool program sites and Mobile Family Resource Center outreach sites. Each visit from the STEAM van has a fun hands-on activity for elementary-age youth. Anaheim Public Library's STEAM Adventures mission is to inspire creativity, spark imagination, and discover possibilities through hands-on STEAM education. Interested in viewing our service locations? [View this months schedule](https://www.anaheim.net/DocumentCenter/View/63797/APR-2025-STEAM-Calendar). 
  

![Thank you](https://www.anaheim.net/ImageRepository/Document?documentID=35590)

We are thankful for their support to make this project a reality.
