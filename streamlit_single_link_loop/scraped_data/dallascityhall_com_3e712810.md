### Frequently Asked Questions list
What authorizes the City Attorney's Office?
The Dallas City Charter (Ch. VII).
What does the Dallas City Attorney do?
The Dallas City Charter provides that the City Attorney shall, among other things, represent the city in all litigation and controversies, prosecute all cases brought before the municipal court, approve as to form proposed ordinances, and be the legal adviser to the City manager, the city council, all official boards and commissions, and all city officers and employees. [Dallas City Charter, Ch. VII, Sec. 3]
Is the City Attorney the only attorney that works for the city?
No. The City code requires that the City Attorney hire assistants called "Assistant City Attorneys." [City Code Sec. 2-18.] The city council determines how many Assistant City Attorneys there are and confirms each individual hired.
Can I contact an attorney in the CAO for questions about [my neighbor, the law, a code violation, etc.] by calling the front desk?
Please call 311 and the city will be happy to direct your call.
I have been sued; can the City Attorney represent me as my attorney?
No. The Dallas City Charter provides that the City Attorney shall, among other things, represent the city in all litigation and controversies, prosecute all cases brought before the municipal court, approve as to form proposed ordinances, and be the legal adviser to the City manager, the city council, all official boards and commissions, and all city officers and employees. [Dallas City Charter, Ch. VII, Sec. 3]
How do I get a copy of a city contract?
Please go to the City Secretary's website and submit an open records request at this link: [https://dalla](https://dallastx.govqa.us/WEBAPP/_rs/%28S%280yooy134ya1j2ojodgjbwlxb%29%29/SupportHome.aspx?sSessionID=)  

Where can I get a copy of the City of Dallas’ ordinances?
A digital copy of the Dallas City Code is available at this website: [https://dallascityhall.com/government/Pages/city-codes.aspx​](https://dallascityhall.com/government/Pages/city-codes.aspx) .  

Does the City Attorney’s Office offer an internship or externship program?
Yes, the City Attorney’s Office offers internship programs for current law students. Students who are interested can reach out to the City Attorney’s Office to inquire about availability, applying, and internship dates.
Where can I apply for a job with the City Attorney’s Office?
Employment opportunities with the City of Dallas can be found at this website: [https://dallascityhall.w](https://dallascityhall.wd5.myworkdayjobs.com/CODCareers) .
