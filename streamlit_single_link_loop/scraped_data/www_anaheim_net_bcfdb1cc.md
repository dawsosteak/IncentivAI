#  Streetlight Services 
### To report a streetlight issue, please [submit a request ](https://www.anaheim.net/2671/Anaheim-Anytime-Request?id=2969)or contact us at 714-765-3300.
  
**For streetlight related problems that require immediate attention, such as broken wires, contact us immediately at 714-765-3300.**
The majority of streetlight issues are typically repaired within 3 to 5 working days. However, there are instances where repairs may take longer due to issues resulting from faulty cables, vandalism, and vehicle accidents. 
When submitting a streetlight service request, **please include the street address and streetlight pole number to assist** in identifying the correct streetlight and help expedite the repair process. 
Thank you for your commitment to improving streetlight reliability in Anaheim.
### Streetlight Replacement Program
  
The city of Anaheim has an ongoing program to improve roadway visibility and safety within various neighborhoods. New streetlight requests are coordinated by the Neighborhood Services section within the Community Services Department, and prioritized based on community feedback, assessed needs by other departments such as Police or Code Enforcement, and available funding. Roadways with minimal existing lighting and/or experiencing reports of illicit activities are generally assigned a higher priority. Once a list of priority locations has been established, a project will be formed and engineered to install the new streetlights.
