#  Platinum Triangle 
Anaheim’s Platinum Triangle blends leading-edge business, high-salary employment, world champion entertainment and exciting residential neighborhoods, creating a unique opportunity in the heart of Orange County. Here, amidst millions of square feet of new development opportunities for office, restaurant and residential projects, is an established destination featuring high-rise lofts, two championship sports teams, an exciting array of dining and entertainment, plus immediate access to the rest of Southern California from three freeways and a major transit center.
  * The Platinum Triangle is an area within Anaheim that generally surrounds and includes Angel Stadium, [Honda Center](https://www.anaheim.net/5157/) and The Grove of Anaheim.
  * Urban development, guided by The Platinum Triangle Master Land Use Plan, is bringing high density, mixed-use, office, restaurant, and residential projects to replace older industrial developments.
  * [The Platinum Triangle Mixed Use Overlay Zone (Chapter 18.20 of the Anaheim Municipal Code)](https://codelibrary.amlegal.com/codes/anaheim/latest/anaheim_ca/0-0-0-81543) allows development of residential, retail, restaurant and office developments. Existing industrial businesses are also welcome to continue operating.
  * Environmental Impact Reports have been prepared to analyze the environmental impacts related to the permitted development intensities.
  * Many different modes of transportation are anticipated to provide access to and within The Platinum Triangle including the I-5, SR-57 and SR-22 freeways; a network of pedestrian friendly local streets; bikeways; ARTIC (Anaheim Regional Transportation Intermodal Center); Anaheim Resort Transit (ART); and, OCTA buses. 
  * A [Community Facilities District (CFD) and Platinum Triangle Implementation Plan](https://www.anaheim.net/568/Platinum-Triangle-CFD-Information) have been approved to finance and identify public infrastructure improvements.
