#  Community Crime Map 
We believe that a safer community starts with shared information. To help you stay informed about what’s happening in your neighborhood, we provide an interactive Community Crime Map (powered by LexisNexis). This tool gives you a near real-time look at local incidents directly from our official records.
[See Community Crime Map](https://www.communitycrimemap.com/?address=Anaheim%20CA&%20=&zoom=14&sidebar=collapsed)
[![Accurint Community Crime Map \(example\) Opens in new window](https://pd.anaheim.net/ImageRepository/Document?documentID=387)](https://www.communitycrimemap.com/?address=Anaheim%20CA&%20=&zoom=14&sidebar=collapsed)
**What can you do with this map?**
  * Search Your Neighborhood: Enter your address or zip code to see recent activity within a specific radius of your home, school, or office.
  * Filter by Date and Type: Interested in the last 24 hours or the last 30 days? You can customize the view to show only the types of incidents you care about, such as theft or traffic updates.
  * Sign Up for Alerts: You don’t have to check the map every day. You can set up automated email alerts to notify you weekly or monthly if an incident is reported near your saved locations.


**Important Things to Know**
To keep this tool helpful and respect everyone's privacy, please keep the following in mind:
  * Your Privacy Matters: To protect the identity of victims, incident locations are never shown as a specific house number. Instead, they are rounded to a block-level address (for example, "1200 Block of Main St").
  * Preliminary Data: The information you see is pulled from our initial police reports. As investigations progress or more facts come to light, the details or classification of a crime may change.
