#  Human Services Division 
Located at the east end of the center, the Human Services Section includes:
  * To view Downtown Community Center programs and services at the, click [here](https://www.anaheim.net/1018/Downtown-Community-Center).
  * To view Miraloma Park Family Resource Center programs and services, click [here](https://www.anaheim.net/2206/Miraloma-Park-Family-Resource-Center).
  * To view Ponderosa Park Family Resource Center programs and services, click [here](https://www.anaheim.net/1136/Ponderosa-Park-Family-Resource-Center).
  * **Legal Assistance** - Free and low cost civil legal services for low and middle income Orange County residents. For more information and to schedule an appointment, call The Legal Aid Society of Orange County at 800-834-5001. 
  * **Youth and Adult Job Placement**
  * **OC Free Tax Preparation Assistance**
    * [Download English Flyer](https://www.anaheim.net/DocumentCenter/View/47802/OC-Free-Tax-Prep-VITA-Locations-Flyer_English)
    * [Download Spanish Flyer](https://www.anaheim.net/DocumentCenter/View/47803/OC-Free-Tax-Prep-VITA-Locations-Flyer_Spanish)
  * **Bilingual Referral Assistance** - Service for food, shelter, and many county-wide community resources
  * **English as a Second Language Class** - This class is for multiple levels of comprehension of the English language. This free class meets Monday through Thursday from 8:30 to 11:20 a.m. New students are always welcome and can sign-up and start class on the same day.
