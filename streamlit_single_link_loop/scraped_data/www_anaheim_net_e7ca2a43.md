  1. [Registration (INITIAL AND ANNUAL)](https://www.anaheim.net/4735/FormsResources#tab51d79ab3-1f2e-4471-99f6-98ca44bd9453_0)


All Lobbyists must register with the Office of the City Clerk within fifteen (15) calendar days of the date of qualifying as a lobbyist, by completing a [Lobbyist Form](https://anaheim.net/DocumentCenter/View/17926/Lobbyist-Form-Registration-Amendment-Termination).
All Lobbyists must renew their register with the Office of the City Clerk by January 15 of each year.
Lobbyists are required to submit a Registration (Initial and Annual) fee of $100.00. Fees are due with submission of the completed [Lobbyist Registration (Initial and Annual) form](https://anaheim.net/DocumentCenter/View/17926/Lobbyist-Form-Registration-Amendment-Termination) and are payable to the City of Anaheim by check, money order, or debit/credit card (Visa or Mastercard).
  1. [Filing a Quarterly Report of Activity](https://www.anaheim.net/4735/FormsResources#tab51d79ab3-1f2e-4471-99f6-98ca44bd9453_1)


After each quarter, lobbyists are required to file a [Lobbyist’s Quarterly Report of Activity](https://www.anaheim.net/DocumentCenter/View/17923/Lobbyist-Quarterly-Report) with the Office of the City Clerk disclosing any lobbying of the City of Anaheim or any regional agency in which Anaheim has a voting role. If the lobbyist did not engage in any lobbying activities during the reporting quarter, a report is still required indicating “No Lobbying Activity”.
**Deadlines**  
---  
| **Period Covered** | **Filing Deadline**  
**1st Quarter** | **Jan. 1 - Mar. 31** | ** Apr. 30**  
**2nd Quarter** | **Apr. 1 - Jun. 30** | ** Jul. 31**  
**3rd Quarter** | **Jul. 1 - Sept. 30** | ** Oct. 31**  
**4th Quarter** | **Oct. 1 - Dec. 31** | ** Jan. 31**  
  
The Quarterly Reports of Activity are to be filed with the Office of the City Clerk by 5 pm on the filing deadline date. If the due date falls on a weekend, the reports will be due on the following business day. 
  1. [Amending Lobbyist Registry](https://www.anaheim.net/4735/FormsResources#tab51d79ab3-1f2e-4471-99f6-98ca44bd9453_2)


Amendments are due within (ten) 10 calendar days of any change in information.
  

Complete a [Lobbyist Form](https://www.anaheim.net/DocumentCenter/View/17926/Lobbyist-Registration-Form) and mark the box titled "Amendment".
  * To add a lobbyist/client after the initial registration, check the "Add" box, and provide requested contact information.
  * To delete a current lobbyist/client, check the "Delete" box, and provide requested contact information.
  * To update a current lobbyist's/client's information, check the "Modify" box, and provide the new contact information.


  

  

  1. [Termination of Lobbyist](https://www.anaheim.net/4735/FormsResources#tab51d79ab3-1f2e-4471-99f6-98ca44bd9453_3)


A Notice of Termination removes an individual lobbyist and all their clients from the City’s lobbyist registry. Complete a [Lobbyist Form](https://www.anaheim.net/DocumentCenter/View/17926/Lobbyist-Form-Registration-Amendment-Termination?bidId=) and mark the box titled "Termination" and indicate the date of termination. A Quarterly Report of Activity must be submitted with the Notice of Termination, even if the termination occurs prior to the end of the quarter. Note, a new [Lobbyist Form](https://www.anaheim.net/DocumentCenter/View/17926/Lobbyist-Form-Registration-Amendment-Termination?bidId=) (Registration) must be completed if the lobbyist resumes lobbying activity with the City of Anaheim or any regional agency in which Anaheim has a voting role.
