#  Natural Gas and Oil Pipeline Regulation
The CPUC regulates natural gas utility service for approximately 10.8 million customers that receive natural gas from Pacific Gas and Electric (PG&E), Southern California Gas (SoCalGas), San Diego Gas & Electric (SDG&E), Southwest Gas, and several smaller investor-owned natural gas utilities. The CPUC also regulates independent storage operators Lodi Gas Storage, Wild Goose Storage, Central Valley Storage and Gill Ranch Storage.
If you are looking for information on the Aliso Canyon Well Failure, please visit this [page](https://www.cpuc.ca.gov/regulatory-services/safety/gas-safety-and-reliability-branch/aliso-canyon-well-failure).
If you are looking for information on PG&E's GT&S (A.17-11-009) and GCAP (A.17-09-006) proceedings, please visit this [page](https://www.cpuc.ca.gov/industries-and-topics/natural-gas/information-on-pge-gts-and-gcap-proceedings). 
If you are looking for information on the **Ventura Compressor Station** , please see the CPUC's Executive Director's Aug. 10, 2022 [letter](https://www.cpuc.ca.gov/-/media/cpuc-website/divisions/energy-division/documents/natural-gas/ventura-compressor-station/2022-08-10-letter-to-socalgas-re-ventura-compressor-station.pdf) confirming that issues related to the Ventura Compressor Station will be taken up in Sempra's General Rate Case. For background plase see our Aug. 5, 2021 Executive Director's [letter](https://www.cpuc.ca.gov/-/media/cpuc-website/divisions/energy-division/documents/natural-gas/ventura-compressor-station/executive-director-letter-to-socal-gas-re-ventura-compressor-station-08052021.pdf) to SoCalGas and the [reply](https://www.cpuc.ca.gov/-/media/cpuc-website/divisions/energy-division/documents/natural-gas/ventura-compressor-station/ed-dr5--ventura-compressor080621.pdf) by SoCalGas, and our Aug. 20, 2021 [letter](https://www.cpuc.ca.gov/-/media/cpuc-website/industries-and-topics/documents/executive-director-letter-to-socalgas-re-ventura-compressor-station-august-20-2021.pdf), as well as our data requests [here](https://www.cpuc.ca.gov/-/media/cpuc-website/divisions/energy-division/documents/natural-gas/ventura-compressor-station/ventura-compressor-station-data-requests_new.zip?sc_lang=en&hash=2F2C5DD1A9182BB94C1DB49BAC19B104). 
  * June 21, 2022: [SoCalGas Completion Letter](https://www.cpuc.ca.gov/-/media/cpuc-website/divisions/energy-division/documents/natural-gas/ventura-compressor-station/scg-completion-letter-ventura-compressor-0621.pdf) to the CPUC Concerning Ventura Compressor Station in Response to the CPUC Request Letters, dated August 5, 2021 and August 20, 2021
  * June 23, 2022: [30-Day Notice](https://www.cpuc.ca.gov/-/media/cpuc-website/divisions/energy-division/documents/natural-gas/ventura-compressor-station/scg--ventura-soil-remediation-30-day-notification-6-23-22.pdf) Prior to Initiating Soil Remediation Project at the Ventura Compressor Station
