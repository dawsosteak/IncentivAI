​forward Dallas! Comprehensive Plan (2006)
DCH Body
**FowardDallas 2.0, adopted by Dallas City Council in September 2024, in an update to this plan.  
Learn more about this update at **[**www.dallascityhall.com/ForwardDallas.  
**  
](https://dallascityhall.com/departments/pnv/Forward-Dallas/Pages/default.aspx)
forwardDallas! is Dallas' citywide comprehensive plan. It is the first of a kind city-led, citywide comprehensive plan. Driven by direct citizen involvement, forwardDallas! envisions the Dallas residents want to live and do business. Dallas City Council adopted forwardDallas! Comprehensive Plan June 2006.   
  
The full forwardDallas components, provided below, include the Vision Documents, Policy Plan Documents, Implementation Plan Documents, Action Plans, Monitoring Plan Documents, Appendix, Public Involvement and Related Adopted Plans.   
[Amendments](https://dallascityhall.com/departments/pnv/strategic-planning/Pages/forward-dallas.aspx) have been made to the  forwardDallas! Comprehensive Plan since the original adoptions.
