The issues in this proceeding are technical in nature and require more time than initially expected. As indicated above, over the past year this proceeding has focused solely on addressing the complex issue of updating policies for the Avoided Cost Calculator. This Avoided Cost Calculator work deterred any work on alternative sourcing mechanisms. The development of alternative sourcing mechanisms is on course but needs additional time to finalize the proposal and allow for party comment and Commission deliberation. Accordingly, it is necessary to extend the statutory deadline of this proceeding until February 15, 2021 to complete the work on distributed energy resources alternative sourcing mechanisms. 3. Waiver of Comment Period Under Rule 14.6(c)(4) of the Commission’s Rules of Practice and Procedure, the Commission may reduce or waive the period for public review and comment of proposed decisions extending the deadline for resolving ratesetting proceedings. Accordingly, the otherwise applicable period for public review and comment is being waived. 4. Assignment of Proceeding Marybel Batjer is the assigned Commissioner and Kelly A.
