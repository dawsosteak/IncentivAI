#### Testing Process
### Steps to become a Fire & Rescue Officer Trainee
#### STEP
# 1
### Fire-Rescue Officer Trainee: FROT
Join our team!
[Apply here for Fire Rescue Officer Trainee](https://www.governmentjobs.com/careers/dallas/jobs/4676250/fire-rescue-officer-trainee-fy25?pagetype=jobOpportunitiesJobs)
#### STEP
# 2
### Pass Civil Service Exam
The age requirement for our program is 18 years of age but not have turned 36 by the date of your test.
[](https://dallascityhall.com/departments/fire-rescue/Pages/Fire-Rescue-Officer-Trainee.aspx)
#### STEP
# 3
### Pass CPAT
Pass the Candidate Physical Agility Test (CPAT). We offer a ten-week mentorship program to prepare applicants for the test. But if you do not wish to participate in the Mentorship Program, you must fill out the Waiver of Practice Form.
[Learn More](https://dallascityhall.com/departments/fire-rescue/Pages/recruiting-cpat.aspx)
#### STEP
# 4
### Interview Process
Typical Interview process & Background/Fingerprints check plus a Polygraph and Medical/Physical Exam
[](https://dallascityhall.com/departments/fire-rescue/Pages/Fire-Rescue-Officer-Trainee.aspx)
#### STEP
# 5
### Academy
28 Week Fire Academy consisting of 468 hours for TCFP curriculum and skills. 500 hours DFR curriculum and skills. 5 Weeks Emergency Medical Technician. 5 Months Paramedic School. 6 Months probationary period.
[](https://dallascityhall.com/departments/fire-rescue/Pages/Fire-Rescue-Officer-Trainee.aspx)
