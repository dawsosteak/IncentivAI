#  City Clerk Bookshelf & Records 
Welcome to the City Clerk Bookshelf and Records – City resources at your fingertips. The Office of the City Clerk administers the records management program for the City of Anaheim. If you need assistance, please contact our office at (714) 765-5166 or cityclerk@anaheim.net.
* * *
## Records
  
[**Records Public Portal**](http://records.anaheim.net/cityclerk/Welcome.aspx) : City records readily available to the public online through the Public Portal include City Council Minutes, Resolutions, and Ordinances; Boards and Commissions minutes; Investment Portfolio Reports; Building Permits; Planning Commission records; Public Works Grading Plans; and Public Works Improvement Plans.
If you are unable to find the records you are looking for, please submit a [**Public Records Request**](http://www.anaheim.net/2153/Request-a-Public-Record).
  * [Public Records Request Archive](http://www.anaheim.net/2924/Public-Records-Request-Archive)
