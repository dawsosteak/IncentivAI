  * Modified Collection Schedule & Transfer Station Hours for Thanksgiving
In observance of Thanksgiving Day, there will be no garbage or recycling collection on Thursday, November 27. Collection schedules will be adjusted as follows:
  

    * ​Thursday routes will be collected on Friday
    * Friday routes will be collected on Saturday
Additionally, the McCommas Bluff Landfill and all Transfer Stations will be closed on Nov. 27. Fair Oaks and Westmoreland Transfer Stations will have limited public hours on Saturday, Nov. 29, open from 7–9 a.m. only. This is necessary to accommodate garbage and recycle collection vehicles servicing delayed routes.
  

[Click here f​or more information. ​](https://dallascityhall.com/departments/sanitation/Pages/holiday-schedule.aspx)
  

  

  * Northwest Transfer Station: Location Change for Scale House Service Window
​Please note that the service window at the Northwest Transfer Station (Bachman) has been moved from the side to the front of the building. Residents are requested to use the designated crosswalk and follow the railing to reach the new service window location.
