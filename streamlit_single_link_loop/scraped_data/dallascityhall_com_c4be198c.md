Page Content
Many of our services can not be done online at [DallasNow](https://dallascityhall.com/departments/sustainabledevelopment/Pages/DallasNow.aspx). However, our Permit Center is also here to assist and answer questions for you in-person with services including: 
  * [Certificate of Occupancy](https://dallascityhall.com/departments/sustainabledevelopment/buildinginspection/Pages/certificate_occupancy.aspx)
  * [Residential plan review](https://dallascityhall.com/departments/sustainabledevelopment/buildinginspection/Pages/residential.aspx)
  * [Water and wastewater permits](https://dallascityhall.com/departments/waterutilities/Pages/developers01.aspx)
  * [Building Permit FAQ](https://dallascityhall.com/departments/sustainabledevelopment/buildinginspection/Pages/faqs.aspx)
  * [Subdivision of property](https://dallascityhall.com/departments/sustainabledevelopment/land-management/Pages/Subdivision.aspx)
  * ​[Plat and addressing information](https://dallascityhall.com/departments/sustainabledevelopment/land-management/Pages/Subdivision.aspx)
  * Handicapped Group Dwelling Unit
Please see the Dallas Development Code, Chapter 51A-4.209(3.1) for more details. No Certificate of Occupancy is required; however City Registration may be required in addition to State licensing. City Registration and distance verification letter requests should be directed to the Department of Code Compliance at 214-670-1972.

​​Zoning Verification Letters
  
Our deparment will provide a letter stating the current zoning on any parcel within the City of Dallas, along with a copy of a portion of the zoning map showing the location of the property, and a copy of the regulations for that zoning district from Chapter 51A, the Dallas Development Code, of the Dallas City Code.
