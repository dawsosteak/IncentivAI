#  PUBLIC ADVOCATES OFFICE JOBS: Open Exams for Public Advocates Office Positions
## Step One: Take an Exam to Qualify 
You must pass a civil service exam for your desired position(s). These are the types of Public Advocates Office (the Office) positions, which require an exam.
### Regulatory Analysts
  * [PURA I External Link](https://www.jobs.ca.gov/JOBSGEN/7PB10.PDF "PURA I")
  * [PURA II External Link](https://www.jobs.ca.gov/JOBSGEN/7PB11.PDF "PURA II")
  * [PURA III External Link](https://www.jobs.ca.gov/JOBSGEN/7PB12.PDF "PURA III")
  * [PURA IV External Link](https://www.jobs.ca.gov/JOBSGEN/7PB13.PDF "PURA IV")
  * [PURA V External Link](https://www.jobs.ca.gov/JOBSGEN/7PB16.PDF "PURA V")


### Engineers
  * [Utilities Engineer External Link](https://emp.cpuc.ca.gov/JobDetails.aspx?Postings=290 "Utilities Engineer")
  * [Senior Utilities Engineer External Link](https://emp.cpuc.ca.gov/sigmadocs/5UCSP.pdf "Senior Utilities Engineer")


### Financial Examiners
  * [Financial Examiner II External Link](https://emp.cpuc.ca.gov/sigmadocs/5UC11.pdf "Financial Examiner II")
  * [Financial Examiner III External Link](https://emp.cpuc.ca.gov/sigmadocs/5UC12.pdf "Financial Examiner III")
  * [Financial Examiner I External Link](https://emp.cpuc.ca.gov/sigmadocs/6UC35.pdf "Financial Examiner IV")


See the full the list of CPUC [Open Exams External Link](https://emp.cpuc.ca.gov/joblistings.aspx "Open Exams"), which provides details of qualifications and salary ranges, as well as links to take the exams. 
After you have taken a CPUC exam, you will receive notice of your results. You must have achieved status in the top three out of five ranks in order to be eligible to apply for the [open job postings External Link](http://www.cpuc.ca.gov/openings/ "open job postings"). 
**ACTION:** Take an exam for all job classifications that you are interested in and qualified for, so that you are eligible to apply for the Office job when it becomes available.
