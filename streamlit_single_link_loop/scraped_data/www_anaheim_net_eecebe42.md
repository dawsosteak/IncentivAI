#  License & Permits 
License & Permit Requirements
If you are planning to conduct business in the City of Anaheim, you must obtain a [City of Anaheim business license](https://www.anaheim.net/494/Business-License).
  

Depending upon what type of business you are starting, you may have to obtain additional permits and licenses from Federal, State and Local agencies. For assistance in determining the different types of permits, go to the State of [California Cal Gold Website](https://www.anaheim.net/494/Business-License).
  

Common Permits & Licenses
  * If your business is selling or leasing tangible property
    * Sellers Permit from the [State Board of Equalization](http://www.boe.ca.gov/)
      * Call 800-400-7115
  * If your business intends to prepare and/or sell food of any type
    * Health Permit from the [Orange County Health Care Agency](http://ochealthinfo.com/)
      * General Information: Call 714-433-6000
      * New Food Facilities: Call 714-433-6026
      * Existing Food Facilities: Call 714-433-6000 
  * If your business will sell or serve alcoholic beverages
    * Verify zoning approval from the [Planning Department](https://www.anaheim.net/490/Planning-Building)
      * Call 714-765-5139
    * ABC License from the [Alcoholic Beverage Control Board](http://www.abc.ca.gov/)
      * Call 657-205-3533 (Santa Ana District Office)
