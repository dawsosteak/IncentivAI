#  Zoning 
It is important that the location you choose for your business is properly zoned for your business activity. When choosing your site, be sure to research the following:  

  * How is the property zoned?
  * How is the building use classified?
  * What are the uses allowed within the guidelines of the city zoning code?
  * What are the parking and landscape requirements?


## Location Assistance
  
Our Planning and Zoning staff can determine whether you are allowed to conduct business at the site you have selected or if you are still looking for a location, where your business location can be. You can either talk with staff directly, or submit an [Anaheim Anytime](https://user.govoutreach.com/anaheim/faq.php?cmd=shell) request. We have also created zoning handouts for your reference.
