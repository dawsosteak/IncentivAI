#  Investor Relations 
Anaheim Public Utilities strives to remain financially strong, competitive, and compliant with all applicable financial regulations in order to enjoy the confidence of the investor community. As the only combined electric and water utility in Orange County, Anaheim Public Utilities serves approximately 360,000 residents and more than 20,000 businesses in Anaheim.
