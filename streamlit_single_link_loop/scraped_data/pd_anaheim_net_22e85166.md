#  Jail 
The Anaheim Detention Facility is located in the Anaheim Police Department main station at 425 South Harbor Boulevard in Anaheim. The public entrance to the Detention Facility is located on the south side of the building on Santa Ana Street, west of Harbor Boulevard.
The Detention Facility is a Type 1 jail facility and can house approximately 120 pre-trial arrestees for both felony and misdemeanor bookings.
## Visitations
A person who has been arrested and booked in the Anaheim Detention Facility may be allowed visitors between the hours of 10 am and 2 pm daily, but is not allowed visitors on the day they are booked.
Work Furlough inmates are allowed visitors only on weekends and holidays between the hours of 2 pm and 5 pm.
## Prison Rape Elimination Act (PREA)
## Zero-Tolerance for Sexual Abuse in Detention
Sexual abuse in detention is a vastly underreported crime. As in the community, many victims of sexual abuse in custody do not report abuse due to shame or fear that they will not be believed. It is also common for victims to face retaliation for speaking out. In recognition of these barriers, the Anaheim Police Department Detention Facility applies the Prison Rape Elimination Act (PREA) standards to allow for reporting to be safer and easier for victims of sexual abuse and sexual harassment. An example of one of these reporting provisions is to accept third-party reports of sexual abuse and sexual harassment.
A third-party reporter is someone who reports sexual abuse and sexual harassment but is neither the victim nor the abuser. The person may have been told by the victim about the abuse or harassment, or witnessed it first-hand. "Third-party" includes other inmates, staff members, family members, legal representatives, contract employees, service providers, community or religious volunteers, among others.
The Anaheim Police Department investigates all in-custody allegations of sexual abuse.
### How to Report
What can I do if I suspect that my loved one is being subjected to harassment, pressured to have sexual relations, or is a victim of abuse or assault?
  * Immediately contact the on-duty Supervisor at the Anaheim Police Department Detention Facility at 714-765-1826 or the Anaheim Police Department at 714-765-1900
  * Encourage your loved one to report the incident and / or find support


The Anaheim Police Department has a zero tolerance for all forms of sexual misconduct and is dedicated to providing a safe and secure environment for detained individuals and personnel.
