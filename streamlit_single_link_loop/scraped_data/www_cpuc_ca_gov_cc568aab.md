#  August 2020 Heat Wave
In August 2020, California experienced a heat wave and subsequent rotating outages. On this page you will find information about the rotating outages and tips to reduce energy use.
  * January 13, 2021: [Final Root Cause Analysis: Mid-August 2020 Extreme Heat Wave](http://www.caiso.com/Documents/Final-Root-Cause-Analysis-Mid-August-2020-Extreme-Heat-Wave.pdf)
  * Nov. 19, 2020: [CPUC Acts to Establish Policies and Procedures for Ensuring Grid Reliability During Extreme Weather Events](https://docs.cpuc.ca.gov/PublishedDocs/Published/G000/M351/K624/351624178.PDF)
  * Oct. 6, 2020: [CAISO, CPUC, and CEC Issue Preliminary Report on Causes of August Rotating Outages](https://docs.cpuc.ca.gov/PublishedDocs/Published/G000/M348/K229/348229612.PDF)
  * Sept. 4, 2020: [CPUC President’s Letter to Utilities Regarding Conservation](https://www.cpuc.ca.gov/-/media/cpuc-website/divisions/news-and-outreach/documents/news-office/key-issues/summer-reliability/cpuc-president-letter-to-utilities-regarding-conservation-090420.pdf?sc_lang=en&hash=04C2BAC60046ADBE17FD200BED6878E5)
  * Sept. 4, 2020: [CPUC Letter to Utilities on Emergency Action to Combat Heatwave](https://www.cpuc.ca.gov/-/media/cpuc-website/divisions/news-and-outreach/documents/news-office/key-issues/summer-reliability/cpuc-letter-to-utilities-on-emergency-action-to-combat-heatwave-090420.pdf?sc_lang=en&hash=6E0B648A43DCDDFEC185FF4DD90F16D5)
  * Aug. 27, 2020: [CPUC Acts to Ensure Electricity Reliability Through Supply and New Technologies](https://docs.cpuc.ca.gov/PublishedDocs/Published/G000/M345/K822/345822548.PDF)
  * Aug. 19, 2020: [CPUC, CAISO, and CEC Response Letter to Governor Newsom](https://www.cpuc.ca.gov/-/media/cpuc-website/divisions/news-and-outreach/documents/news-office/key-issues/summer-reliability/cpuc-caiso-and-cec-response-letter-to-governor-newsom-081920.pdf?sc_lang=en&hash=9F04C3F6DD45D9314E86EE8CCFBA019F)
  * Aug. 16, 2020: [CPUC President’s Letter to Utilities Regarding Conservation](https://www.cpuc.ca.gov/-/media/cpuc-website/divisions/news-and-outreach/documents/news-office/key-issues/summer-reliability/cpuc-president-letter-to-utilities-regarding-conservation-081620.pdf?sc_lang=en&hash=9BDF3908CB68FCD38C5094F56F4B0FBB)
