#  Frequently Asked Questions (F.A.Q.) 
# **What is included in the Circulation Element Update process?**
The update process consisted of the following steps:
  * Reviewing existing transportation conditions and policies;
  * Developing updated transportation goals, policies, and programs;
  * Preparing the City’s planned transportation network maps;
  * Developing strategies to manage transportation demand and congestion;
  * Updating the City’s transportation study guidelines;
  * Preparing draft and final Circulation Element updates; and
  * Conducting public outreach (including community workshops and an online survey)


# **What travel modes will the Circulation Element Update include?**
The City’s Circulation Element includes policies, programs, actions, and priority transportation networks to support the safe and efficient movement of people driving, walking, biking, and taking transit in Anaheim.
