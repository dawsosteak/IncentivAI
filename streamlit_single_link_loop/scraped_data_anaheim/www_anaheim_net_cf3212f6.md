## About
The City of Anaheim’s Annual Neighborhood Cleanup Program gives residents the perfect opportunity to properly dispose of bulky items that won’t fit in their regular residential trash cart or bin. It also encourages neighborhoods to come together for a community cleanup event to dramatically improve the look and feel of the area.
The City of Anaheim, in partnership with its waste services provider, offers up to five (5) large 30-yard roll-off dumpster bins, free of charge, to neighborhoods, Homeowner Associations (HOAs), and mobile home parks for Friday-Monday-hosted cleanup events.
### Who qualifies for this program?
Any neighborhood associations, HOAs, or mobile home parks situated within Anaheim city limits are eligible to participate in the program. Cleanup events must be coordinated and led by official neighborhood representatives, HOA board members, or authorized leaders from mobile home parks.
This program is not intended for individual homeowners to dispose of materials related to personal home renovations, remodeling, or private projects.
### What to show your neighbors to get them on board? 
If this is the first time you are organizing a clean-up, here are sample documents that you can print out and present to your neighbors as you generate interest for the event:
  * [Neighborhood Clean-up Guidelines and Proecdures](https://www.anaheim.net/DocumentCenter/View/8018 "Neighborhood Clean-up Info")
  * [Neighborhood Clean-up Flier](https://www.anaheim.net/DocumentCenter/View/22020/sample-flier-2 "Neighborhood Clean-up Flier")


### How to schedule a clean-up?
To schedule a Neighborhood Cleanup, please fill out our online [request form](https://www.anaheim.net/FormCenter/Community-Services-4/Neighborhood-Cleanup-70) with your information. Submit your request at least three (3) weeks in advance of your preferred cleanup date.
A maximum of five (5) containers are available per cleanup event, and scheduling is handled on a first-come, first-served basis. Once submitted, a Neighborhood Services Coordinator will contact you to confirm the details.
### Questions?
To reach the appropriate staff for your area, please be prepared to provide your [council district](https://gis.anaheim.net/portal/apps/instant/lookup/index.html?appid=1fb012fbdc094352acfdce4c6015dfb7). Then call (714) 765-4456 and ask to speak with a Neighborhood Services Representative.
