### **How often will I be billed?**
The timing of your bill depends on the billing cycle your service address is in. Most residential customers are billed bimonthly and can expect to receive a bill approximately every 60 days. Commercial customers are billed monthly and will receive a bill approximately every 30 days.   
  
Bills are due and payable upon receipt and become delinquent after 15 days.
### **Can I receive my bills monthly instead of bi-monthly?**
We now offer a monthly billing option to our customers who agree to the program’s guidelines. For more information on monthly billing please call customer service at 714-765-3300 or [complete the form online](https://www.anaheim.net/FormCenter/Public-Utilities-6/Monthly-Billing-Enrollment-Form-130).

Anaheim Public Utilities offers several convenient options for customers to pay their utility bills*.
  * In-person / Cashier's office: 
    * Cash 
    * Cashier's check
    * Check
    * Debit/credit card (Visa, MasterCard, Discover) 
    * Money order 
  * Automated phone service: 
    * Automated Clearing House (ACH)
    * Debit/credit card (Visa, MasterCard, Discover)
  * Online:
    * Automated Clearing House (ACH)
    * Debit/credit card (Visa, MasterCard, Discover)
  * Mail
    * Check 
    * Money order


Note: Anaheim Public Utilities does **not** accept debit/credit card payments from medium or large-rate commercial customers.
*Digital wallets are not accepted at this time.

For your convenience, we offer multiple methods for paying your utility bill:
  * **By Mail** : You may mail your payment using a check, money order, or cashier’s check to:
    * City of Anaheim  
P.O Box 3069  
Anaheim, CA 92803
  * **In-Person Payment** : 
    * Visit our cashier's office within the Anaheim West Tower located at 201 S. Anaheim Blvd. 
    * Alternatively, utilize the drop box located on the south side of Anaheim West Tower, to the left of the main entry doors, for dropping off checks, money orders, or cashier’s checks.
  * **Online** : Access our secure online system, [MY ACCOUNT](https://myapu.anaheim.net/iwr/user/login.seam?cid=150), 24/7 to pay using a credit card or ACH.  
  

  * **By Phone** : Utilize our automated phone system by calling 714-765-3300, **option 1** , to pay with a credit card or ACH, available 24 hours a day.

Help us reduce the need for paper bills and SAVE! Visit [MY ACCOUNT](https://myapu.anaheim.net/iwr/user/login.seam?cid=150) to enroll and easily access your bills anytime, anywhere.
  1. To enroll in paperless billing, first log into your online account 
  2. On the left-hand side of the page, click “My Profile” 
  3. Then click on “Communication Preferences” 
  4. On this page, elect to have paperless / email notifications


  1. [ENROLL IN RECURRING PAYMENTS](https://www.anaheim.net/6523/Billing-and-Payment-Info#tab7d3c1254-e9e3-4784-9b5f-b4bb478e36db_1)


Simplify your bill management and never miss a payment! 
Visit [MY ACCOUNT](https://myapu.anaheim.net/iwr/user/login.seam?cid=150) to set up automatic, recurring payments and enjoy peace of mind.
To enroll in recurring payments, first log into your online account. Once you are logged into your account, select the following:
  1. On the left-hand side of the page, click “Payment Options”
  2. Click on “View Online Payments”
    1. If you have not previously saved a payment method, click “Add Credit Card” or “Add Bank Account” in the bottom right-hand corner. Once completed, click “Payments” on the top of the page.
  3. On the right-hand side of the “Payment History” page, click on “Recurring Payments”
  4. Next, click on “Add Recurring Payments” on the right-hand side
  5. Fill out the page, click “Submit”, then your autopay will be activated!


*Please note: If you have an active outstanding balance, you would be required to manually pay your balance in full. Autopay will begin on the next billing cycle.
