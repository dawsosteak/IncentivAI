#  Sign Up for Outage Alerts 
![California's statewide power supply outlook](https://www.anaheim.net/ImageRepository/Document?documentID=399)
Receive outage alerts via email or text message any time there is a known power outage affecting your area. 
  * [Anaheim Alert](http://www.anaheimalert.net/register.php)
  * [PowerWatch](https://www.anaheim.net/445/PowerWatch-Sign-Up)


## Who to Contact
  
Call 714-765-3300 to report a power outage or other problems with high voltage electrical equipment.   
  
Call 911 report a fire or medical emergency.
