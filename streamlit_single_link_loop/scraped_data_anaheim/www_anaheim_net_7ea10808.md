#  WaterSmart Landscape Award 
## Show Off Your Landscape
Anaheim Public Utilities hosts the annual WaterSmart Landscape Award program. The contest is open to Anaheim residents and businesses that have implemented California-Friendly landscapes that excel in sustainable landscape design, plant selection, water use efficiency, and creativity. Winners will receive a WaterSmart Landscaping Care Kit, WaterSmart Landscape Awardee Yard Sign, and be recognized at an Anaheim Public Utilities Board Meeting! As well as:
  * **Residential landscape winners will receive a $250 gift card to Home Depot or Lowes**
  * **Commercial landscape winners will receive a $500 gift card to Home Depot or Lowes**


### **Award application will reopen in Spring 2026.**
## Ways To Nominate: 
  1. Complete the [ONLINE FORM](https://www.anaheim.net/FormCenter/Public-Utilities-6/WaterSmart-Landscape-Award-Nominations-172)
  2. Email entries to akanzler@anaheim.net
  3. Call in entries to 714-765-4218


### Provide detailed information, i.e.: 
  * Name 
  * Address 
  * Short description explaining what you liked about the landscape

## Judging Criteria
###    
1. Appropriate Plant Selection
  * California Friendly®/native plants


###  2. Project Creativity
  * Creative and innovative landscape design


###  3. Landscape Design
  * Plant color, textures, form, repetition, organization, and unity will be considered


###  4. Irrigation System
  * Designed to reduce runoff, use of water-efficient irrigation (drip system, micro spray, rotating nozzles, etc.)


Note: Only front yard, parkways, and common areas are eligible. Artificial turf projects are ineligible.
## Program Guidelines  
  

### Participating nominees will need to submit the following:
  * Short description of your project (include plant list)
  * Submit 4 color photos of landscaped area (include 2 wide-angle view photos) 
  * Provide a signed waiver to release photos to the public 
  * Copy of your water bill
    * Must have an active Anaheim Public Utilities water account

## Turf Replacement Program
For a limited time, the Turf Replacement Program rebate is $4 per square foot of turf removed (up to 5,000 sq. ft. for residential and 50,000 sq. ft. for commercial customers). Turf must be replaced with California Friendly® plants. Please visit [socalwatersmart.com](http://socalwatersmart.com) to view additional program requirements and begin the application process.
