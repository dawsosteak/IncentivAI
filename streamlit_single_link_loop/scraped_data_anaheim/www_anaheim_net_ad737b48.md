#  Rebate Guidelines & Application 
Anaheim Public Utilities offers a wide variety of rebates to assist our residential customers with energy savings. Please feel free to read through our application process and program guidelines below before completing the online [application](https://apu.conservationrebates.com/). If you prefer to apply using a paper application, please contact us and we would be happy to send you one.  

  1. [Application Process](https://www.anaheim.net/1481/Rebate-Guidelines-Application#tab776017ee-7fba-4336-aba0-69d932a96052_0)
  2. [Program Guidelines](https://www.anaheim.net/1481/Rebate-Guidelines-Application#tab776017ee-7fba-4336-aba0-69d932a96052_1)
  3. [Application Assistance](https://www.anaheim.net/1481/Rebate-Guidelines-Application#tab776017ee-7fba-4336-aba0-69d932a96052_2)


  1. [Application Process](https://www.anaheim.net/1481/Rebate-Guidelines-Application#tab776017ee-7fba-4336-aba0-69d932a96052_0)


After purchasing a qualifying conservation measure or ENERGY STAR® certified replacement appliance, please follow the steps below:
  1. Collect all appropriate supporting documents such as original, paid sales receipts or invoices, which must include the product’s brand name, the manufacturer model number, the purchase price and date, and, if available, the product’s efficiency information.
  2. Apply online by clicking the link and creating an account.
  3. Follow the instructions within the online rebate processing tool.
  4. Once you have submitted your application online, you can check its status by logging into your rebate account. Please note that applications will be denied if no return communication is received from the customer after three outreach attempts and/or within one year from the submission date.
  5. Paper applications may be requested by contacting by phone, email, or downloading online, [ENGLISH](https://www.anaheim.net/DocumentCenter/View/70621/Home-Incentives-Program-Application) or [SPANISH](https://www.anaheim.net/DocumentCenter/View/70622/Home-Incentives-Program-Application-SPANISH)
  6. An Anaheim Public Utilities representative may call to schedule a physical inspection of your installed appliance or conservation measure before issuing a rebate.
  7. We ask that you try to keep your inspection appointment to ensure that your application is processed in a timely manner. If you need to reschedule, please contact Anaheim Public Utilities at 714-408-4161.
  8. Rebate checks are normally mailed within 30 days from the date of approval.


*If you are applying for ENERGY STAR certified window rebates, you must also include a [window incentive worksheet (PDF)](https://www.anaheim.net/DocumentCenter/View/3723) (completed by your contractor) and the stickers that display the actual ultraviolet (UV) value and solar heat gain coefficient (SHGC).
  1. [Program Guidelines](https://www.anaheim.net/1481/Rebate-Guidelines-Application#tab776017ee-7fba-4336-aba0-69d932a96052_1)


  * **Residential Incentive** : Home Incentive Program provides customers with rebates to help lower the cost of investing in high efficiency products and measures. The incentive offer is limited to residential customer for residential use. The appliance must be installed in a residential dwelling within Anaheim Public Utilities (APU) service area. A residential dwelling is defined a single family home, condominium, apartment, or mobile home permanently located within APU service area. The dwelling must be fully constructed and occupied (no new construction).
  * **Required documentation** : This application and a paid sales receipt for each product must be returned to the Rebate Processing Center office listed on the bottom of the application. Online purchases require submittal of the delivery slip. To expedite your application, please upload a copy of your APU bill. Window rebates require submittal of a [window incentive worksheet.](https://www.anaheim.net/DocumentCenter/View/3723/Window-Incentive-Worksheet-PDF)
  * **Limitations** : In order to qualify for the Program, Applicant must be an APU electric service customer with any and all APU accounts in good standing. The incentive offer is on a first-come, first-service basis and is effective until funding is expended or the program is discontinued by APU. Maximum of one application per measure type per account service address every 5 years, unless approved by APU. The incentive amount will not exceed the purchase price. The incentive check will be mailed in approximately 30 days following application approval.
  * **Installation verification** : Applicant understands and agrees that APU or its representative may inspect the installed product for installation verification before the incentive is paid. **APU also reserves the right to inspect any measure(s) within six (6) months of issuing a rebate check to ensure continued compliance with Program requirements.**
  * **Delinquent accounts** : Applicant agrees that any incentive may be credited towards any delinquent (as currently defined in the APU’s Rates, Rules and Regulations) utility bill(s) for which the Applicant is responsible whether the account(s) is open or closed.
  * Applicant understands APU may withhold payment of incentive until all of the conditions listed herein are met.
  * APU does not warrant, endorse, or assume liability for the quality, performance, or safety of the contractor and/or retailer or wholesaler. Performance of any product and acceptance of materials used is solely the applicants responsibility.
  * Applicant is responsible for meeting all program requirements and for checking with state/county/city governments and homeowner’s association (if any) in the area regarding local conditions, restrictions, codes, ordinances, rules, and regulations prior to installation. 
  * Applicant must submit application within 1 year of purchase of the product.
  * Note that insufficient or incorrect data may delay your rebate check.
  * The incentive offer is on a first-come, first-serve basis and is effective until funding is expended or the program is discontinued by Anaheim Public Utilities.


  1. [Application Assistance](https://www.anaheim.net/1481/Rebate-Guidelines-Application#tab776017ee-7fba-4336-aba0-69d932a96052_2)


For assistance with your application, or more information on qualifying criteria or the status of your rebate application, please call our rebate processing team at 714-408-4161, which is open Monday through Friday from 8 a.m. to 5 p.m., excluding holidays.
