#  Start, Stop, Move Service 
Anaheim Public Utilities is a **city-owned, not-for-profit, utility** that delivers reliable water and electricity to the residents and businesses of Anaheim. As a customer, **you are also an owner** of this utility.
## How do I start water and power service?
  
You are able to **complete an application online** 24 hours a day by visiting [MY ACCOUNT](https://myapu.anaheim.net/iwr/services/newService.seam?cid=41843&rvn=1).
If you would like to **come into our office** to have someone help you in person, we could assist you at:
_201 S. Anaheim Blvd.,1st Floor,__  
__Monday - Friday: 8 a.m. to 5 p.m._
If you wish to complete the application **over the phone** with a representative, you can call our Customer Service during normal business hours:
_Monday - Thursday: 7 a.m. - 6 p.m.  
Friday: 7 a.m. - 5:30 p.m._
  1. [Turn On Service](https://www.anaheim.net/6522/Start-Stop-Move-Service#tab677c6870-0a48-4c13-bdde-37f48e67051a_0)


### Please have the following information available so that we can quickly get your services established:
  
**Residential service**
    * Address and phone number of employer
    * Date of birth
    * Daytime phone number
    * Driver's license number
    * Employer name
    * Mailing address
    * Marital status: If married, spouse's name plus all of the above information
    * Name of responsible party
    * Service address
    * Social Security number
    * The same information is required for all roommates
    * Turn-on date


**Sole ownership / partnership business** – In addition to the information required for residential service, you will need to provide us with: 
    * Anaheim business license number
    * Business partner’s information
    * Business type
    * Doing business as (DBA)
    * Square footage of the property


**Corporation / LLC business**
    * Service Address
    * Anaheim business license number
    * Business description
    * Date and state of incorporation
    * Federal Tax ID number
    * Mailing address
    * Name and telephone numbers of all corporate officers 
    * Telephone number
    * Turn-on date  
  


  
Anaheim requires all new customers to pay a security deposit before electricity can be connected.  
In compliance with the Federal Fair and Accurate Credit Transaction Act of 2003, Anaheim will check your credit rating with Equifax before connecting your electric service. You must provide your Social Security number so that we may check your credit rating with Equifax. Based on information returned from Equifax, Anaheim may waive the deposit requirement. If your Equifax report indicates that a security deposit is required, you may provide Anaheim with a credit reference letter from your previous utility company. Credit reference letters must be on company letterhead and must show credit information from the previous 12 months to be considered. If Anaheim approves the credit reference letter that was provided, your deposit will be waived. Deposits are refunded or credited to the account after the customer has paid 12 consecutive bills on time.

  1. [Turn Off Service](https://www.anaheim.net/6522/Start-Stop-Move-Service#tab677c6870-0a48-4c13-bdde-37f48e67051a_1)


We are happy to assist you over the phone at 714-765-3300. 
We advise calling 5 days in advance to ensure that we will be able to accommodate your desired date. You will need to provide: 
  * Full legal name
  * Service address
  * Turn-off date 
  * Telephone number
  * Forwarding address where we can mail your final bill and deposit refund (if applicable)


  1. [Restore Service](https://www.anaheim.net/6522/Start-Stop-Move-Service#tab677c6870-0a48-4c13-bdde-37f48e67051a_2)


When we must disconnect service for non-payment, we require an additional fee to be paid upfront in addition to the delinquent amount.
You may **come into our****office** to pay the required amount or, 
You may pay **over the phone** with our automated phone system (714-765-3300, **option 1**).   
You can then speak with a customer service representative to ensure an order has been issued to restore your services. 
Payments made _before_ 5:30 p.m. will be restored the same day. Payments made _after_ 5:30 p.m. will be restored the next business day.
  1. [Moving Within Anaheim](https://www.anaheim.net/6522/Start-Stop-Move-Service#tab677c6870-0a48-4c13-bdde-37f48e67051a_3)


To**new service within Anaheim** , the primary account holder is required to provide the following information:
  * Proof of Ownership or Proof of Tenancy 
  * Current Identification
  * Social Security Number or Federal Tax ID number (EIN)*


*A deposit would be required if a valid Social Security Number is not provided.
If you are **moving outside of Anaheim** , please contact customer service during our normal business hours to close your Anaheim Public Utilities account.
  

[MY ACCOUNT](https://myapu.anaheim.net/iwr/services/newService.seam?cid=41843&rvn=1) would allow you to set up utility service in Anaheim. Standard lead time can take up to a week to process an online application. If you need to establish service on a sooner date, then please call customer service during our normal business hours.
