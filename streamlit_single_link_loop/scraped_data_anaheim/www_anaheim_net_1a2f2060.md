#  Fee Information 
## **Fee Deferral Program**
This program allows for payment of the impact fees for new construction (both commercial and residential) to be deferred until the development is ready for final inspection, a temporary or final certificate of occupancy. The purpose of this program is to reduce the up-front costs and encourage new development within the City of Anaheim. 
  * [Fee Deferral Guide](http://www.anaheim.net/DocumentCenter/View/17867)
  * [Fee Deferral Application](http://www.anaheim.net/DocumentCenter/View/11267)
