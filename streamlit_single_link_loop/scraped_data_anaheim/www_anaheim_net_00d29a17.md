#  Recycled Water Fill Station 
The Recycled Water Fill Station is a part of the Anaheim Public Utilities’ ongoing effort to provide programs and services to help conserve water.  

The Fill Station will be used by the City to fill mobile water containers with recycled water for cleaning the City’s parking structures and local streets. Potable water will be conserved by using recycled water to pressure wash the City’s parking lot structures. The Fill Station is located at a block east of the City Hall parking structure at 200 South Anaheim Blvd.
