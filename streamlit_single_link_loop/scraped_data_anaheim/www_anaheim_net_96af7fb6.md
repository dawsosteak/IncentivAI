#  13th Marine Expeditionary Unit 
## Overview
  

The city of Anaheim adopted Marines of the 13th Marine Expeditionary Unit in 2007 to show and provide community support. Annually the 13th MEU receives support for the Marines and Sailors from private donations. The Marines and Sailors participate in various events throughout the year including the annual Veterans Day celebration. 
The city organizes various fundraisers throughout the year to support the Marines and Sailors of the 13th MEU. To provide support or to learn more please contact Alyssa Guerrero at (714) 765-4610 alguerrero@anaheim.net. 
If you would like to donate $25 gift card(s) for Thanksgiving to the 13th MEU, please drop them off at City Hall, 7th Floor or contact Alyssa Guerrero.
