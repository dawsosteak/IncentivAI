#  Printing at the Library 
Print documents from your desktop, laptop, or mobile device using our wireless printing service Princh. Upload your print job by email, mobile app, or web browser, and pick up your documents at your neighborhood branch.   
  
To retrieve your print job, enter your email address at the print release terminal at your library or find instructions below. 
  1. [Computer](https://www.anaheim.net/3267/Printing-at-the-Library#tab58869be9-900f-43c7-b3f3-f57253577ab9_0)

## **Printing from a Web Browser (all computers and devices)**
Click on the web address for your neighborhood branch, then follow steps 1-7 below.
  * Central: <https://print.princh.com?pid=106760>
  * Canyon Hills: <https://print.princh.com?pid=106765>
  * East Anaheim: <https://print.princh.com?pid=106766>
  * Euclid: <https://print.princh.com?pid=106762>
  * Haskett: <https://print.princh.com?pid=106761>
  * Ponderosa: <https://print.princh.com?pid=106763>
  * Sunkist: <https://print.princh.com?pid=106764>


  

  1. Choose "**Click here to select and upload a file**."
  2. Select a file from your computer or device, then wait for processing to finish.
  3. (If you don't change any settings, it will print 1 copy, all pages, in black & white.)
  4. Click on **Settings** if you'd like to print in color, more than 1 copy, or a specific page range – then choose "**Accept** ".
  5. Check the "**Accept Terms and Conditions" box** and choose "**Continue** ".
  6. Enter your **email address** and select "**OK** ".
  7. When the **Print Queue box** shows as "**Ready** ", you can pick up your print job. Go to any of the **print release stations** at the branch you selected, click on "**Release a Print Job** ", and then enter **your email address**. If you see multiple print jobs listed, click on the "**Print Preview** " icon on the right side to confirm which document(s) you wish to print.

## **Printing by Email (all computers and devices)**
**For Apple devices:** You will need to install and use an email app such as Gmail. Printing by Email does not work with Apple’s pre-installed Mail app.
Choose one of the email addresses for your neighborhood branch, for either black & white or color print jobs. Send an email message with the document you want to print as an attachment to the email.
In a few minutes, you will receive an email, “Print jobs submitted to [your branch]”, from no-reply@ewprints.com. Go to any of the print release stations at the branch you selected, click on “Release a Print Job”, and then enter your email address. If you see multiple print jobs listed, click on the “Print Preview” icon on the right side to confirm which document(s) you wish to print.

## **Printing with the Princh App (Android and Apple devices)**
**For Android devices:** install the Princh app from the Android Google Play Store. Open the app and give it permission to access content on your device. You will also need to "Enable printing to Princh" by allowing Princh to display/appear on top of other apps. Download your document or photo, so that it is saved to your phone/tablet.   
  
**For Apple devices:** install the Princh app from the Apple App Store.
  1. Open the document or photo that you want to print.
  2. Click on the share menu:
     * **For Android:** Select 'Print' and choose Princh from the printer dropdown menu then tap the printer icon
     * **For Apple:** Select 'Print with Princh' and open the Princh app
  3. Select the printer for your neighborhood branch, either by enter the printer ID or scanning the matching QR code, both shown below. 
  4. Adjust the print job settings if you want to print multiple copies, selected pages, or in color.
  5. Tap "**Print** ", check "**Accept Terms and Conditions** ", and choose **Continue**.
  6. Enter your **email address** and "**Submit Print Job** ".
  7. Once you see "**Your print job is ready for release** ", go to any of the print release stations at the branch you selected, click on "**Release a Print Job** ", and then enter **your** email address. If you see multiple print jobs listed, click on the "**Print Preview" icon** on the right side to confirm which document(s) you wish to print.
