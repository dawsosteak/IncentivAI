#  Human Resources 
We partner with City departments to hire, compensate, support and create a workforce dedicated to delivering high-quality services, which makes Anaheim a special place to live, work and play. The Human Resources Department consists of four collaborative and cross-functional teams focused on: 
  * [Employment](https://www.anaheim.net/1954/Employment)
  * [Benefits/Wellness](http://www.myanaheimbenefits.com/)
  * [Employee Relations](https://www.anaheim.net/2012/Labor-Contracts-Personnel-Rules)
  * [Risk Management](https://www.anaheim.net/414/Risk-Management)
