#  Election Calendar 
## General Municipal Election Calendar, November 3, 2026
The next General Municipal Election will be held on **Tuesday, November 3, 2026**. The election will be for the Mayor (At-large) and three (3) Council Member District Seats (Districts 2, 3, and 6), each for a term of four (4) years.
## FPPC Filing Deadlines
**DATE** | **EVENT/REQUIREMENT**  
---|---  
July 31, 2026 |  **SEMI-ANNUAL STATEMENT DUE (Form 460)**  
(Period: January 1 through June 30, 2026)  
Within 24 Hours |  **CONTRIBUTION REPORTS (Form 496/Form 497)**  
(contribution/expenditure of $1,000 or more, aggregate)  
(Period: August 5 through November 3, 2026)  
September 24, 2026 |  **1ST PRE-ELECTION STATEMENT DUE (Form 460)**  
(Period: July 1 through September 19, 2026)  
October 22, 2026 |  **2ND PRE-ELECTION STATEMENT DUE (Form 460)**  
(Period: September 20 through October 17, 2026)  
February 1, 2026 |  **SEMI-ANNUAL STATEMENT DUE (Form 460)**  
(Period: October 18 through December 31, 2026)  
## General Municipal Election Calendar, November 3, 2026
**DATE** | **EVENT/REQUIREMENT**  
---|---  
July 13, 2026 - August 7, 2026 |  **NOMINATION PERIOD**  
Between these dates, candidates may obtain nomination materials and file documents with the City Clerk (Monday – Friday, 8 a.m. – 5 p.m., **by appointment**).  
August 10, 2026 - August 12, 2026* |  **EXTENDED CANDIDATE FILING PERIOD**  
*Filing is extended if an incumbent eligible for re-election **does not** file for re-election prior to 5:00 p.m. on August 7, 2026. Incumbents are not eligible to file during the extended period.  
August 13, 2026 |  **RANDOMIZED ALPHABET DRAWING**  
Secretary of State to conduct a random alpha draw to determine the order of candidate's name on the ballot.  
August 8, 2026 - August 17, 2026 |  **PUBLIC EXAMINATION PERIOD**  
Public review period for the candidate statements and ballot designations.  
*(Examination period extended to August 24, 2026 only for a candidate seat if it has a nomination filing period extended through August 12, 2026.)  
September 7, 2026 - October 20, 2026 | **FILING PERIOD FOR WRITE-IN CANDIDATES**  
October 19, 2026* |  **15-DAY VOTER REGISTRATION DEADLINE FOR THE NOVEMBER 2026 ELECTION**  
*Conditional voter registration extends the 15-day registration deadline, allowing voters to register and vote provisionally 14 days prior to an election and on Election Day.  
November 3, 2026 | **ELECTION DAY**  
December 2026 |  **CERTIFICATION OF RESULTS**  
Certification of election results and installation of newly elected officials  
(Council meeting date to be determined).
