#  Olive | Vermont 
City Council approved the Underground District Formation for Olive/Vermont in October 2023. The project is currently in the design phase and will underground approximately 1.8 miles of overhead electrical and telecommunication lines, as well as two railroad crossings. The project requires extensive collaboration with adjacent schools, businesses, residents, city departments and other utilities and agencies. The schedule for the start of construction is early 2026 and is estimated for completion in late 2027.
