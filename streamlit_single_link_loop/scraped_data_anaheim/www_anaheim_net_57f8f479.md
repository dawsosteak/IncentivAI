# Final Circulation Element
The Anaheim City Council approved [Resolution No. 2025-033](http://records.anaheim.net/CityClerk/DocView.aspx?id=5836925) on April 22, 2025 amending the City's Circulation Element of the General Plan. 
Click the links below to review the approved Circulation Element documents and contact CirculationElement@anaheim.net with any questions. 
## [**View Circulation Element**](https://www.anaheim.net/DocumentCenter/View/9520/)
## [**Attachment A - Roadway Classification and Land Maps**](https://www.anaheim.net/DocumentCenter/View/64974/)
## [**Attachment B - Existing Intersection Level of Service (LOS) Worksheets**](https://www.anaheim.net/DocumentCenter/View/64975/)
## [**Attachment C - Existing and Future Intersection Lane Configurations**](https://www.anaheim.net/DocumentCenter/View/64976/)
## [**Attachment D - Future Intersection LOS Worksheets**](https://www.anaheim.net/DocumentCenter/View/64977/)
# Environmental Analysis
The Anaheim City Council approved [Resolution No. 2025-032](http://records.anaheim.net/CityClerk/DocView.aspx?id=5836922) certifying Program Environmental Impact Report (PEIR) No. 356 (State Clearinghouse Number 2022020363) and adopting Findings of Fact, a Statement of Overriding Considerations, and Mitigation Monitoring Program No. 394 for the General Plan Update Project which includes an update to the City's Circulation Element. For questions, contact Nick Taylor at NJTaylor@anaheim.net or (714) 765-4323. 
## [View PEIR No. 356 Environmental Documents](https://www.anaheim.net/6093/)
# Past Public Input Survey
The City’s public input survey has closed. Results about your experiences traveling in the city and your priorities for the City’s transportation network have been incorporated into the Final Circulation Element. Your feedback informed the policies and improvements included in the Circulation Element.
