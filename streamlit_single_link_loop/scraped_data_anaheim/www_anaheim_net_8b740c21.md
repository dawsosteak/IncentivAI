  1. [APL Accessibility ](https://www.anaheim.net/6597/APL-Accessibility)
Our goal at Anaheim Public Library is to provide all patrons a welcoming experience where they feel valued and have equal access to resources and materials.
  2. [Programs & Events](https://www.anaheim.net/4745/Programs-Events)
Find out about all the upcoming special programs and events at your library.
  3. [Heritage Services](https://www.anaheim.net/2474/Heritage-Services)
The Heritage Center has an inventory of almost a million items organized and cataloged for use by scholars, students and researchers in the Disney Resort Reading Room. The center is located at Carnegie Plaza in the Anaheim Muzeo complex. Heritage Services also oversees the historic Mother Colony House and Woelke-Stoffel Victorian on the Founders' Park site. 
  4. [For Kids & Families](https://www.anaheim.net/1095/For-Kids-Families)
Discover all the Library has to offer for children of all ages, from eBooks and homework help resources, to storytime videos and reading lists.
  5. [STEAM Services](https://www.anaheim.net/5508/STEAM-Services)
