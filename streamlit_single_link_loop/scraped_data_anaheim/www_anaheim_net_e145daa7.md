#  Cover to Cover 
![In n out cover to  cover logo](https://www.anaheim.net/ImageRepository/Document?documentId=57328)
## October 4 - November 15, 2025
  

The Cover-to-Cover club is back for ages 4-12 at the Anaheim Public Library. For every five books or 300 minutes read, participants can earn 1 free hamburger or cheeseburger.
Each book read and logged will be worth 60 points, and each minute read is worth 1 point. Prizes are awarded when patrons have earned 300, 600, and 900 points.
To register, visit your [local neighborhood branch](https://anaheim.net/903/Locations-Hours) or Beanstack at [Anaheim.beanstack.com](https://anaheim.beanstack.com/reader365).
