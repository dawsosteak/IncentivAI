  1. [Programs](https://www.anaheim.net/6451/Chicano-Heritage-Month#tabfcf03256-b959-4066-8ae8-68e8f27175cf_1)


  * Chicano Pride Zine Making for Teens at Sunkist Branch 
    * Friday, Aug. 8, 3 p.m. - 4 p.m.
    * Celebrate Chicano Heritage Month by making your very own zine. All materials will be provided. Ages 13 and up.
  * **How-2-Zine for Teens at Central**
    * Saturday, Aug. 9, 3 p.m.
    * Learn what a zine is and how to make one in the Teen Lounge. We will look at zines by Chicano artists and writers in the Library's collection for inspiration. Teens only!
  * **Art Lab for Teens at Central**
    * Monday, Aug. 11, 5 p.m.
    * Try some cool art mediums and concepts, after learning about them in this relaxed studio environment in the Teen Lounge. August is printmaking in celebration of Chicano Heritage Month!
  * **Chicano Heritage Monthly Family Storytime at Sunkist Branch**
    * Tuesday, Aug. 12, 5:30 p.m. - 6:30 p.m.
    * Join us for a Chicano Heritage Month themed storytime and crafts. Make a piñata craft and papel picado. All ages.
  * **Sunkist STEAM: Frida Kahlo Craft for Chicano Heritage Month at Sunkist Branch**
    * Friday, Aug. 15, 4 p.m. - 5 p.m.
    * For Chicano Heritage Month, we will honor celebrated Mexican artist Frida Kahlo by creating portraits of the artist herself using bright, bold colors and of course, her iconic flowers. Sunkist STEAM, a monthly program for kids 13 and under that presents concepts in science, technology, engineering, the arts and mathematics using fun activities, is generously funded by the Anaheim Public Library Foundation.
