#  Shant Boodanian, Business Development Manager 
![Shant Boodanian, Business Development Manager SM](https://www.anaheim.net/ImageRepository/Document?documentID=47002)
**Mr. Shant Boodanian** joined the City of Anaheim in 2013 and has over 20 years of experience in city government. As the Business Development Manager in the Economic Development Department, Shant provides Anaheim’s business community with a high-level of customer service by assisting existing and new businesses in a timely and streamlined manner through interdepartmental coordinated efforts. His goal is to help you start, grow your business, and keep your business within the great City of Anaheim!
Mr. Boodanian has a Bachelors Degree in Business Administration from California State University, Los Angeles.
