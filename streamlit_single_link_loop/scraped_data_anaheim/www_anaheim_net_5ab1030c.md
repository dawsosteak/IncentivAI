#  First-Time Homebuyer Program 
## **Program Overview**
Anaheim's First Time Homebuyer program help low- to moderate-income households achieve homeownership by bridging the gap when prices and down payments would otherwise be out of reach. 
##  **“NEW OPPORTUNITY”** My Anaheim Home
**The program offers a $50,000 loan to help with a down payment on a home.**  
It’s a 30-year deferred loan with no monthly payments, charged at 1% simple interest. Buyers must contribute at least 3% of the purchase price, and up to 5% of the down payment can come from family gifts. Total down payment assistance can cover up to 20% of the purchase price.
**To qualify, applicants must:**
  * Meet the income limits
  * Not have owned a home in the last 3 years
  * Buy a home within Anaheim (not unincorporated areas)
  * Live in the home as an owner-occupant
  * Choose an eligible property type (single-family, townhouse, or condo)
  * Stay within the maximum purchase price allowed
  * Complete an approved homebuyer seminar
  * Priority is given to people who live or work in Anaheim for the past 12 months**[![First-Time Homebuyer Interest List Portal Opens in new window](https://www.anaheim.net/ImageRepository/Document?documentId=67479)](https://housingportal.anaheim.net/Fthb)**
