#  Directions to City Hall 
![City Hall parking map](https://www.anaheim.net/ImageRepository/Document?documentID=39536)
## Directions To City Hall, 200 S. Anaheim Blvd.  

  

### From the North
  

  * 1-5 - Santa Ana Freeway Exit Lincoln Avenue (East)
  * Lincoln Avenue (East) to Anaheim Boulevard and turn right (South)
  * Anaheim Boulevard turn left at Broadway
  * Turn left on Mito Way*
  * Mito Way* will lead into the parking structure  
*


### From the South
  

  * 1-5 - Santa Ana Freeway exit Katella / Anaheim Boulevard
  * Turn right off the exit ramp and right immediately onto Anaheim Boulevard, continue to curve to the right as you turn onto Anaheim Boulevard Broadway turn right
  * Turn left on Mito Way*
  * Mito Way* will lead into parking structure  
*Mito Way is listed as 42nd Street on Google Maps


### From the, West & East
  
**SR91 - 91 Freeway West**  

  * Exit Harbor turn left
  * Turn left on La Palma
  * Turn right on Anaheim Boulevard
  * Turn left on Broadway
  * Turn left on Mito Way*
  * Mito Way* will lead into parking structure  
*Mito Way is listed as 42nd Street on Google Maps

**SR91- 91 Freeway East  
**
  * Exit Harbor, straight to stop sign
  * Turn right on Lemon
  * Left on to La Palma
  * Right on Anaheim Boulevard
  * Left on Broadway
  * Left on Mito Way*
  * Mito Way* will lead into parking structure  
*Mito Way is listed as 42nd Street on Google Maps

**From the North**  

  * SR57- Freeway to Lincoln Avenue
  * Lincoln Avenue, turn right 
  * Left on Anaheim Boulevard
  * Left on Broadway
  * Left on Mito Way*
  * Mito Way* will lead into parking structure  
*Mito Way is listed as 42nd Street on Google Maps
