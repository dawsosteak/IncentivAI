##    
  
Downtown Anaheim  
Sunday, Dec. 7, 2025  
  
Nutcracker Holiday Village  
noon  
  
Tree Lighting  
6 p.m.
#   

  

_Corner of Clementine Street and Center Street Promenade. Ring in the holidays with a very special tree lighting celebration. Join the Nutcracker Holiday Committee, the City of Anaheim, the Downtown Anaheim Association, and the Anaheim Community Foundation for holiday entertainment food, and fun! Guests will enjoy a variety of holiday happenings, including dog costume contest, holiday crafts, unique shopping, pictures with Santa, and the tree lighting at the steps of City Hall._
