#  Forms & Applications 
### **Short-Term Rental Conveyance Applications**
Anaheim Municipal Code Section 4.05.180.010.0102 (a), allows a CC&R Restricted Short-Term Rental to relocate to a new location with an approved Administrative Permit or a Minor Conditional Use Permit (MCUP). Administrative Permit applications will be processed in 10 business days and MCUP requests are generally processed in 30 to 60 days. If you are requesting a relocation, you are required to submit the items below along with the applicable fee. 
  * [Short-Term Rental Conveyance Submittal Checklist](https://www.anaheim.net/DocumentCenter/View/27339/0Short-Term-Rental-Conveyance-Submittal-Checklist)
    * [Conveyance Application](https://www.anaheim.net/DocumentCenter/View/27340/1Short-Term-Rental-Conveyance-Application)
    * [Local Contact and Property Management Form](https://www.anaheim.net/DocumentCenter/View/27334/2Local-Contact-and-Property-Management-Form)
    * [Property Details Form](https://www.anaheim.net/DocumentCenter/View/27335/3Property-Details-Form)
    * [Ownership Information Form](https://www.anaheim.net/DocumentCenter/View/27336/4Ownership-Information-Form)
    * [Neighbor Consent Form](https://www.anaheim.net/DocumentCenter/View/27337/5Neighbor-consent-form)


A limited number of "Qualifying Pre-Moratorium Short-Term Rental" properties may request a relocation based on compatibility issues pursuant to Anaheim Municipal Code Section 4.05.180.010.0103, please provide the following information for consideration by the Planning Director.
  * [Compatibility Conveyance Requirements](https://www.anaheim.net/DocumentCenter/View/27338/6Compatibility-Conveyance-Requirements)


### Change of Ownership
If you are requesting a change of ownership, you are required to submit a completed application along with the $498 fee. A new application must be filled out and submitted within 14 days of such a change. 
  * Change of Ownership Application and the following forms:
    * [Ownership Change Application](https://www.anaheim.net/DocumentCenter/View/28528/Ownership-Change-Application)
    * [TOT Contact Form](https://www.anaheim.net/DocumentCenter/View/33274)
    * [Affidavit of Notice](https://www.anaheim.net/DocumentCenter/View/28530/Affidavit-of-Notice)
    * [Indemnification Agreement (Owner)](https://www.anaheim.net/DocumentCenter/View/28529/Indemnification-Agreement-Owner)
    * [Indemnification Agreement (Lessee - If Applicable)](https://www.anaheim.net/DocumentCenter/View/28526/Indemnification-Agreement-Lessee)
    * [Self-Certification Form](https://www.anaheim.net/DocumentCenter/View/28527/Self-Certification-Form)
    * [Authorized Agent Appointment form](http://anaheim.net/DocumentCenter/View/1083) (If owner is not managing STR)[](http://anaheim.net/DocumentCenter/View/1083)
    * Fee: $498 


If you have any questions regarding the application process, please contact the City of Anaheim’s Business License staff at 714-765-5194.
### Renewals
Short-Term rental permit renewals are due annually on or before July 31st. Please contact the City of Anaheim’s Business License staff at 714-765-5194.
