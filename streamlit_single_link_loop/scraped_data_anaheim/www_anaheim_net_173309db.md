#  Downtown Anaheim Family Resource Center 
  1. ![Downtown Logo for Slideshow](https://www.anaheim.net/ImageRepository/Document?documentID=64338)
  2. ![Family in front of a celebration banner](https://www.anaheim.net/ImageRepository/Document?documentID=64647)
  3. ![Group of women holding flowers](https://www.anaheim.net/6662/Downtown-Family-Resource-Center)
  4. ![Woman smiling in a room](https://www.anaheim.net/ImageRepository/Document?documentID=64649)


  

The Downtown Anaheim Family Resource Center is one of three welcoming, family-focused centers operated by the City of Anaheim. Designed as a community hub, the center brings together residents, public agencies, and local organizations to offer a wide range of supportive services that promote safety, stability, and healthy development for Anaheim families.
On-site staff provide needs assessments, coordinate human services and programs, and ensure comprehensive support for families. Bilingual (English/Spanish) staff are available at all three centers.
### **Case Management Services**
Our case management service can help you sustain your household by providing individualized case assessment, planning, and care to promote quality living,
  * Community Resource Information & Referrals
  * Transportation Resources
  * Diaper Distributions
  * Food Distributions
  * Utility Application Assistance
  * Form Translation & Completion Assistance
  * Enrollment Assistance
    * Medi-Cal, CalFresh, Covered California
