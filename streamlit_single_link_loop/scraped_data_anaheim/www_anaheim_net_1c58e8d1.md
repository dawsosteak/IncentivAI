#  Fire Construction Permits 
## **Community Risk Reduction Division**
To provide and maintain a safe environment for the citizens and businesses in Anaheim through a professional balance of education, engineering, enforcement and evaluation; and by visualizing problems through the eyes of those we serve.
[![Anaheim Next - Fire Opens in new window](https://www.anaheim.net/ImageRepository/Document?documentId=68260)](https://aca-prod.accela.com/ANAHEIM/Default.aspx)
[![Electronic Plan Review Button](https://www.anaheim.net/ImageRepository/Document?documentId=68261)](https://www.anaheim.net/6760/Electronic-Plan-Review-Information)
## **Electronic Plan Review**
Fire Construction Permit applications are accepted and reviewed through the [GoPost online portal](https://anaheim.eplansoftreview.com/#/login?portal=AnaheimFire). By using GoPost, you can submit applications online to the Anaheim Fire & Rescue portal and upload all plans and supporting documents.
## **Hard Copy Plan Review**
Application materials may also be submitted in-person by appointment. Please contact our Fire Development team at (714) 765-4000 between the hours of 8 A.M. and 5 P.M. Monday through Friday to schedule an appointment to submit plans.
The following items will be required to submit hard copy plans:
  1. Three (3) sets of hard copy plans.
  2. A [Plan Review Application](https://www.anaheim.net/DocumentCenter/View/54379/2023-Fire-Construction-Permit-Application----Online-Fillable) with complete addresses for Contractor and Billing Party that includes city, state, and zip code.


## **Inspection Requests**
To schedule an inspection once your permit is issued, visit our [Fire Inspection Survey](https://gis.anaheim.net/portal/apps/experiencebuilder/experience/?id=fa703833165549e6ae183e1710a980bb) to submit a request. Requests received before 2:00 P.M. will receive a callback the same day between 2:00 P.M.-5:00 P.M. and requests received after 2:00 P.M. will receive a callback the following business day between 2:00 P.M.-5:00 P.M.
