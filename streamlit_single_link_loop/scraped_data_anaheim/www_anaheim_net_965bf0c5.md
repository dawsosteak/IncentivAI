Public art makes life better in Anaheim.
Murals, sculptures and other works across Anaheim build community, honor history, drive imagination, enhance well-being and make our city an even more fun place to visit.
We welcome and promote public art through the Anaheim Public Art Program, the result of a multiyear effort to develop and implement a public art plan.
See the Public Art Plan [here](https://www.anaheim.net/DocumentCenter/View/63298/Anaheim-Public-Art-Plan-FINAL).
Explore here to learn more about public art in Anaheim, including applying for murals.
