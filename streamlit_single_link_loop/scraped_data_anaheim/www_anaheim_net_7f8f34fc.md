#  Commercial Rates 
Did you know that the time of day in which you recharge your electric vehicle (EV) can impact your electricity bill? Energy used during peak demand (4 p.m. to 9 p.m.) is the most expensive time for Anaheim Public Utilities to buy energy, because that's when most people get home from work or school and use electricity. Simple habit changes can help you reduce costs, and so can the right rate plan.   
  
So, when should you recharge your EV?  
  
**The best time to recharge your EV is during oﬀ-peak hours.**
Check out the video below to learn more how a Time-Of-Use (TOU) plan can help you lower costs.
