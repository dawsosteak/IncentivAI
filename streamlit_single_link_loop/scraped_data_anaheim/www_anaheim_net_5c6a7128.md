## What is MY ACCOUNT?
MY ACCOUNT is a convenient way to manage your Anaheim Public Utilities account online. Among other great features, it allows you to:
  * Pay your bill
  * Request a payment extension
  * Request a duplicate bill
  * Enroll in paperless billing
  * Enroll recurring payments 
  * Update your mailing address 
  * View billing and payment details 
  * View usage history


Find out more by visiting the [MY ACCOUNT FAQ](https://anaheim.net/faq.aspx?TID=25) page.
