#  Anaheim's Older Adult Program Brookhurst Community Center 
  1. ![Brokhurst 1](https://www.anaheim.net/ImageRepository/Document?documentID=58659)
  2. ![Brookhurst 2](https://www.anaheim.net/ImageRepository/Document?documentID=58660)
  3. ![Seniors at a Table](https://www.anaheim.net/6567/Older-Adult-Program---Brookhurst-Communi)
  4. ![Senior Group Photo](https://www.anaheim.net/ImageRepository/Document?documentID=57847)


Anaheim’s Older Adult Program offers a vibrant array of activities that cater to a wide range of interests and abilities for those age 55 and over. Each day at the Anaheim's Older Adult Hubs, you’ll find a dynamic environment where connections flourish and friendships are formed.
The Hubs provide something for everyone, whether you’re looking for gentle exercises, like classes for those with limited mobility, or more challenging activities, like longer walks. In addition to fitness options, we offer health lectures, information on health insurance, community resources, and seasonal workshops and luncheons.
Whether your goal is to stay active, learn something new, or simply enjoy the company of others, we invite you to join us.**For more information, please call 714-765-3415.**
