#  Passport Services 
Anaheim offers passport services **by** **appointment**. Appointments are available **Monday through Friday, from 9 a.m. to 3 p.m**.
Plan for your visit by taking a moment to read the information and instructions here before scheduling your appointment below.
To**make an appointment,** select an available date and time for passport services. Each appointment requested is for one applicant, so make one appointment for each person applying if coming with multiple people.
For more help, please call (714) 765-4540.

##  ****Requirements
  1. **Completed Application**[unsigned application (Form DS-11) completed single-sided in black ink]:
     * Social Security Number is required on the application
     * Forms can be found online at <https://eforms.state.gov/>
  2. **Evidence of U.S. Citizenship**(one of the following):
     * Certified Birth Certificate
       * Minors under age 16 must submit Certified Birth Certificate to show parental relationship
       * Abstract Birth Certificates nor Hospital Birth Certificates are acceptable
     * Consular Report of Birth Abroad
     * Certificate of Naturalization
     * Previously issued undamaged U.S. Passport
       * Applicable for adult applicants not eligible to renew their passport by mail
  3. **Photo Identification**(one of the following):
     * Valid Driver's License
       * Note: Out of State Identifications require a secondary form of identification
     * Current Valid Passport
     * Valid Military Identification
  4. **All Applicants must be present** :
     * All applicants, including children, must be present during application process. For children under 16, both parents (with identification) must be present at the time of applying. If both parents are not able to appear in person, call before your appointment for additional requirements.
  5. **Passport Photo** :
     * One 2" X 2" Passport Photo
     * Photo Services available onsite
     * Visit [travel.state.gov/photos](https://travel.state.gov/photos) for passport photo requirements
  6. **Payments**(two separate payments, **Cash not accepted**) see Fees:
     * Application Fee (U.S. Department of State) paid by Check or Money Order only
     * Execution Fee (City of Anaheim) paid by Check, Money Order, or Visa or Mastercard
