#  Basketball Leagues 
The Anaheim Community Services Department offers basketball leagues for players 18 years and older. The leagues operate 4 seasons annually, with all teams scheduled to play a minimum of 10 games. Every season is concluded with a single elimination playoff.  
  
For more information and to register please visit [www.teamsideline.com/anaheim](http://www.teamsideline.com/anaheim).
## Seasons
Winter: January - March
Spring: April - June
Summer: July - September
Fall: September - December
## Cost
Team Registration: $365
Deposit to Reserve Spot: $100 (remaining balance of $265 due day after second game). 
Weekly Official’s Fees: $35 (paid in cash at gym)
## League Nights
Sunday Men’s at [Downtown Anaheim Gym](https://www.anaheim.net/2208/Downtown-Anaheim-Youth-Center), [East Anaheim Gym](https://www.anaheim.net/966/East-Anaheim-Gymnasium), and/or [West Anaheim Gym](https://www.anaheim.net/2209/West-Anaheim-Youth-Center).
Tuesday Men's at [East Anaheim Gym](https://www.anaheim.net/966/East-Anaheim-Gymnasium), 
Wednesday Men's at [East Anaheim Gym](https://www.anaheim.net/966/East-Anaheim-Gymnasium).
Locations may change based upon gym availability and demand.
