## Street Closure Information
###   

### **Firecracker 5K/10K**
The Firecracker 5K/10K run and walk starts at 7:00 a.m., with check-in starting at 5:00 a.m. 
The route begins at Canyon High School at the intersection of Imperial Highway and E. Santa Ana Canyon Road and ends behind Festival Shopping Center at Monte Vista and Weir Canyon Road. Closures will begin at 6:00 a.m. until about 9:00 a.m. Eastbound lanes will be closed along E. Santa Ana Canyon Road from Imperial Highway to E. Monte Vista and Weir Canyon Road. Residents can still travel westbound during the closure. 
### **Fourth of July Parade**
The Fourth of July parade begins at 1 p.m. at Canyon High at the intersection of Imperial Highway and E. Santa Ana Canyon Road. Closures begin at 10:30 a.m. until 2:30 p.m. The closure starts at Imperial Highway and goes along E. Santa Ana Canyon Road to Fairmont Boulevard. The road will be closed in both directions. 
### **Park Extravaganza**
Closures are along E. Santa Ana Canyon Road from Lakeview Avenue to Pinney Drive/Royal Oak Road from 3:00 p.m. to 10:30 p.m. Residents and attendees can reach the park by entering Maude Lane from E. Santa Ana Canyon Road. 
_The City of Anaheim complies with the Americans with Disabilities Act. People with access and functional needs who require accommodation to participate in a program should direct such requests either in-person or by telephone at (714) 765-5191._
