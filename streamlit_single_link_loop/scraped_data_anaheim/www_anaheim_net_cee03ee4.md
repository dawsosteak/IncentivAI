  1. [Air Duct Repair](https://www.anaheim.net/2579/Heating-Cooling-Systems#tab3ec14250-34c1-4f95-af45-093c2ddcd754_0)


In a typical home, about 20% of the air is lost due to leaks, holes, and an improperly connected duct system, which results in an increase on your energy bill. A well-designed and properly sealed air duct system can make your home more comfortable and energy efficient.
## Installation/Efficiency Criteria 
Must already have a central electric heating or cooling system 
## Incentive amount 
50% of repair cost (Up to $300)
[Click here](https://www.anaheim.net/1481/Home-Incentives-Rebate-Guidelines-Applic) for guidelines and how to apply.

  1. [Box Fan, Window Fan, Floor Fan](https://www.anaheim.net/2579/Heating-Cooling-Systems#tab3ec14250-34c1-4f95-af45-093c2ddcd754_1)


Any of these fan options provide cooling relief during hot and muggy weather and save on energy costs by reducing the need for turning on your central A/C.  
Additionally, these fans provide cooling in occupied rooms rather than areas not in use.
## Efficiency Criteria
Must be ETL Listed
## Incentive Amount
$20 (maximum 2)

  1. [Central Air Conditioner](https://www.anaheim.net/2579/Heating-Cooling-Systems#tab3ec14250-34c1-4f95-af45-093c2ddcd754_2)


ENERGY STAR® certified central air conditioners have a higher seasonal energy efficiency ratio (SEER) and use 8% less energy than conventional new models. Replacing air conditioners that are more than 10 years old with ENERGY STAR certified units can save up to 20% on heating and cooling costs. 
## Installation/Efficiency Criteria
  * Minimum 14.3 SEER2 or 15+ SEER
  * Must be a replacement heating/cooling system


  

## Incentive Amount 
$200 per ton
[Click here](https://www.anaheim.net/1481/Home-Incentives-Rebate-Guidelines-Applic) for guidelines and how to apply.

  1. [Heat Pump Ductless Mini Split](https://www.anaheim.net/2579/Heating-Cooling-Systems#tab3ec14250-34c1-4f95-af45-093c2ddcd754_3)


A mini-split heat pump provides a highly efficient heating and cooling system, where ductwork does not exist. Mini-splits help keep each room or spaces a customized temperature via remote access.
## Installation/Efficiency Criteria 
  * Minimum 14.3 SEER2 or 15+ SEER rating
  * Must be a replacement heating/cooling system


## Incentive Amount 
$200 per Ton
[Click here](https://www.anaheim.net/1481/Home-Incentives-Rebate-Guidelines-Applic) for guidelines and how to apply.

  1. [Heat Pump HVAC](https://www.anaheim.net/2579/Heating-Cooling-Systems#tab3ec14250-34c1-4f95-af45-093c2ddcd754_4)


A high-efficiency heat pump transfers central air conditioning as well as heating and uses less energy than traditional electric systems. Heat pumps transfer heat in both directions, using the outside air to heat a home in winter and cool it in summer.
## Installation/Efficiency Criteria
  * Minimum 14.3 SEER2 or 15+ SEER rating
  * Must be a replacement heating/cooling system


## Incentive Amount
$200 per Ton
## Additional Rebate Information
South Coast AQMD offers an additional Heat Pump HVAC rebate that is separate from APU's rebate. Please visit South AQMD link below for additional information and questions:
[www.aqmd.gov/gozero](http://www.aqmd.gov/gozero)

  1. [Room Air Conditioner](https://www.anaheim.net/2579/Heating-Cooling-Systems#tab3ec14250-34c1-4f95-af45-093c2ddcd754_5)


Room air conditioners cool rooms rather than the entire home and can provide cooling only where needed. ENERGY STAR certified room air conditioners can save more energy and money through improved sealing and insulation around the unit to keep you cool and comfortable.   

## Installation/Efficiency Criteria 
[ENERGY STAR](https://www.energystar.gov/productfinder/product/certified-room-air-conditioners/results) Certified
  * Must be a replacement heating/cooling system 


## Incentive Amount
$100 (Maximum of 2)   

[Click here](https://www.anaheim.net/1481/Home-Incentives-Rebate-Guidelines-Applic) for guidelines and how to apply.

  1. [Smart Thermostat](https://www.anaheim.net/2579/Heating-Cooling-Systems#tab3ec14250-34c1-4f95-af45-093c2ddcd754_6)


Unlike programmable or manual thermostats, ENERGY STAR certified smart thermostats can deliver greater convenience, control, and can help you save money on your energy bill. Smart thermostats utilize motion sensors, which recognize when you have left the home and have the potential to learn you daily schedule, making energy-saving temperature changes automatically.
## Installation/Efficiency Criteria
[ENERGY STAR](https://www.energystar.gov/products/smart_thermostats)
  * Wi-Fi Enabled 
  * Must be a replacement of an existing thermostat 


## Incentive Amount 
$50 (Maximum of 2) 
[Click here](https://www.anaheim.net/1481/Home-Incentives-Rebate-Guidelines-Applic) for guidelines and how to apply.

  1. [Whole House Fan](https://www.anaheim.net/2579/Heating-Cooling-Systems#tab3ec14250-34c1-4f95-af45-093c2ddcd754_7)


A whole house fan is an efficient way to reduce the use of your air conditioner and maintain a comfortable temperature within your home. A whole house fan draws in cool outside air from open windows and releases excess hot air inside the home through your attic.  

##    
​Installation/Efficiency Criteria  

Permanently mounted in attic  
Central air conditioning existing  

  

## ​Incentive Amount
