Track your reading progress, earn prizes and more!
  5. [Amnesty Month](https://www.anaheim.net/6583/Amnesty-Month)
This September, we celebrate Amnesty Month and Library Card Sign-up Month, where all account fees are waived*. Fine amnesty gives you a fresh start to delve into a world of stories, knowledge, and learning. APL is now fine-free! Check out materials without the worry of overdue fines. Take advantage of this opportunity to clear your library record, upgrade to our new special edition library card, and dive back into our shelves stocked with new arrivals and beloved classics.

  7. [Black History Month](https://www.anaheim.net/6626/Black-History-Month)
Explore Black history, Black voices, and Black stories in Anaheim and the greater O.C. through engaging programs, resources and services. 
  8. [Chicano Heritage Month](https://www.anaheim.net/6451/Chicano-Heritage-Month)
Celebrate Chicano Heritage acknowledging the social equity movement through programs and events at the library. 
  9. [Hispanic Heritage Month](https://www.anaheim.net/6453/Hispanic-Heritage-Month)
Celebrate the rich cultures of Americans whose ancestors came from Spain, Mexico, the Caribbean, and Central and South America.
  10. [AnaCon](https://www.anaheim.net/4743/AnaCon)
The Comic and Science Fiction event feature authors, comic book artists, book giveaways and many more surprises.
  11. [Indie Author Day](https://www.anaheim.net/6272/Indie-Author-Day)

  12. [Native Art Festival](https://www.anaheim.net/6596/Native-Art-Festival)
  13. [Spinal Dread: Horror Literature and Culture Festival](https://www.anaheim.net/6686/Spinal-Dread-Horror-Literature-and-Cultu)
Step into the darkest corners of literature, pop culture and more with vendors, workshops and panels.
