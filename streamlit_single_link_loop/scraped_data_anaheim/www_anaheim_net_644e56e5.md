#  Anaheim Library Foundation 
The Anaheim Public Library Foundation is an independent, non-profit organization dedicated to supporting and enhancing the Library's rich diversity of resources and programming for generations to come. Along with sponsoring author visits throughout the year, Foundation members host a popular Mystery Authors Luncheon each Spring to raise funds that support our eBook and audiobook collection, children's and teen programs, and other necessary services for Anaheim's diverse community.

## Ways to Give
A gift to the Anaheim Public Library Foundation will support necessary programs, services, and resources for the diverse community. Your donation is tax-deductible (Tax ID #33-0672558), and each dollar you contribute will improve the lives of children, teens, and adults in Anaheim.
