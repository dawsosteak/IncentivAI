#  Water Awareness Month 
May is Water Awareness Month. Anaheim Public Utilities recognizes the month through educational programs and activities designed to increase the understanding of the value of water and sustainability.

Being water-wise helps lower water use and ultimately the cost of your bill. Here are some ways you can be sustainable at home:
  * Collect rainwater to water the lawn or garden.
  * Run your dishwasher and washing machine only when full.
  * Turn off the tap when not in use, especially when brushing your teeth and shaving.


Anaheim Public Utilities, along with other state agencies, offer rebates and incentives for indoor and outdoor water-saving devices like:
  * High-Efficiency Clothes Washer
  * Premium High-Efficiency Toilets
  * Rain Barrels & Cisterns
  * Rotating Sprinkler Nozzles
  * Soil Moisture Sensors
  * Weather-Based Irrigation Controllers
