## Book Club Kits
Is your book club looking for a great book to discuss at your next book club meeting?
Check out our Book Club Kit collection! Book Club Kits contain 10 to 13 copies of a book, suggested discussion questions, an author biography, and a summary of the book, with all items provided in a convenient tote bag.
Book Club Kits can be requested online, picked up at your neighborhood branch, and checked out for six weeks. The borrower is responsible for ensuring that all items in the kit are returned in the tote bag to the Central Library's Check-Out Desk by the due date. Anaheim Public Library cardholders in good standing may borrow two kits at a time. 
Find the Book Kits in the [](https://ana.ent.sirsi.net/client/en_US/virtual/search/results?qu=book+club+kit&te=)[Library Catalog](https://ana.ent.sirsi.net/client/en_US/virtual/search/results?qu=book+club+kit&te=). Check back often, as new Book Club Kits are added regularly.
