S.T.A.R.S. (Study‑Time, Arts, Recreation, and Sports) is a free, drop‑in program offered throughout Anaheim for children ages 5–16. The program provides academic support, art enrichment, sports, and recreation activities in a safe and welcoming environment. Participants are encouraged to stay on site; however, this is a drop‑in program, and children may come and go freely. **Staff are not responsible for children who choose to leave the program area.**
### **Summer Hours**
**12:00 p.m. – 5:00 p.m., Monday through Friday.** Hours may vary by location during school breaks, and the program is closed on holidays.
### **Community Program Locations**
  * **Brookhurst Community Center** – 2271 West Crescent Avenue, Anaheim, CA 92801
  * **Miraloma Family Resource Center** – 2600 East Miraloma Way, Anaheim, CA 92806
  * **Pearson Park** – 400 North Harbor Blvd., Anaheim, CA 92805
  * **Twila Reid Park** – 3100 West Orange Avenue, Anaheim, CA 92804
  * **Rio Vista Park** – 201 N Park Vista St., Anaheim, CA 92806


## **S.T.A.R.S. + AESD ELOP Partnership (Closed during Summer)**
We are excited to share that the City of Anaheim’s S.T.A.R.S. program has partnered with the Anaheim Elementary School District’s ELOP program. S.T.A.R.S. is now available at six elementary school campuses, operating **after school Monday–Friday**. These sites follow the district’s bell schedule and school-year calendar.
### **Elementary School Locations**
  * **Clara Barton Elementary** – 1926 W Clearbrook Lane, Anaheim, CA 92804
  * **Thomas Edison Elementary** – 1526 E Romneya Dr., Anaheim, CA 92805
  * **John Marshall Elementary** – 2066 W Falmouth Ave., Anaheim, CA 92801
  * **Benito Juarez Elementary** – 841 S Sunkist Street, Anaheim, CA 92806
  * **Ponderosa Elementary** – 2135 S Mountain Ave., Anaheim, CA 92802
  * **Roosevelt Elementary** – 1600 E Vermont Ave., Anaheim, CA 92805


For more details, call **(714) 765‑5191**. 
For updates during extreme heat or rain, call the **Inclement Weather Hotline: (714) 765‑4090**.
We look forward to seeing you this summer!
