## **Overview**
**  
  
** A certified landscape irrigation auditor will survey and provide written recommendations for qualifying commercial properties that have a minimum of one acre of irrigated area **at no cost**. Qualifying properties include commercial and industrial sites, common areas within a homeowners association, and institutional sites like schools, parks, and government facilities.
  

To request a survey, please submit an online application. Surveys are scheduled on a first-come, first-served basis. Funding is limited and submitting an application does not guarantee a survey will be provided.
  

For more information, contact the Water Efficiency Programs Manager at (714) 765-5029 or [Click here](http://www.bewaterwise.com/landscape_irrigation_survey.shtml).
