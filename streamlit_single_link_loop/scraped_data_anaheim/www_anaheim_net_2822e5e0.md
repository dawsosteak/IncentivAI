#  Trade Shows & Special Event Information 
_Having a Special Event or Trade Show in our City? Download important information, permits and instructions here.  
_
[Floor Plan Submittal Info](https://www.anaheim.net/DocumentCenter/View/50631/Floor-Plan-Submittal-Info) for trade shows, general sessions, banquets & breakout sessions.
We have provided requirements that are site specific for the following venues: [Anaheim Convention Center](https://www.anaheim.net/DocumentCenter/View/12514), [Anaheim Marriott Hotel](https://www.anaheim.net/DocumentCenter/View/12519), [Disneyland Resort Hotels](https://www.anaheim.net/DocumentCenter/View/12517), [Anaheim Hilton Hotel](https://www.anaheim.net/DocumentCenter/View/28379/Hilton-Hotel-Requirements-2019), [Anaheim Sheraton Park Hotel](https://www.anaheim.net/DocumentCenter/View/67529/Sheraton-Park-Hotel-Requirements), & [Glover Stadium](https://www.anaheim.net/DocumentCenter/View/46571/Glover-Stadium-Requirements).
If you are an exhibitor in a trade show or planning an indoor event, please review our [Exhibitor & Event Info](https://www.anaheim.net/DocumentCenter/View/50634/Exhibitor-and-Event-Info) for general information and items that require a permit. For companies that are licensed through the California State Fire Marshal's Office for flame treatment of drapes, curtains or decorative material, we have provided a [Flame Retardant Applicators List](https://www.anaheim.net/DocumentCenter/View/52025/Flame-Retardant-Applicators). 
## Special Events
For a list of all events or activities that require a permit, please refer to our [Event Permit Application](https://www.anaheim.net/DocumentCenter/View/50633/Event-Permit-Application). We have provided permit instruction for [Tents](https://www.anaheim.net/DocumentCenter/View/12521), [Carnivals/Outdoor Festivals](https://www.anaheim.net/DocumentCenter/View/12516), [Vendor Festival Requirements](https://www.anaheim.net/DocumentCenter/View/64360/Festival-Vendor-Requirements), [Live Christmas Trees in Public Buildings](https://www.anaheim.net/DocumentCenter/View/46618/Live-Christmas-Trees-in-Public-Buildings), [Christmas Tree & Pumpkin Lots](https://www.anaheim.net/DocumentCenter/View/46619/Christmas-Tree-and-Pumpkin-Patch-Lots), and the use of [Candles](https://www.anaheim.net/DocumentCenter/View/12515) for banquets, weddings and religious ceremonies.
The use of fireworks, sparklers, bottle sparklers, special effects, or cold spark devices requires a permit from Anaheim Fire & Rescue. All items listed including cold spark devices are considered a pyrotechnic device by the CA State Fire Marshal’s Office and requires you to hire a fireworks company to submit all required state licensing and documentation. It also requires fire standby by the fireworks company and Anaheim Fire & Rescue. Some facilities and hotels do not allow pyrotechnics inside their facility.
All special event applications and floor plans must be submitted prior to the sale of any booth space and not less than 14 days before the scheduled event date. Failure to meet this deadline will jeopardize approval.
Submittals received fewer than 14 days before the event are subject to denial without review and, if considered, will incur a mandatory after-hours expedited plan review fee (minimum two hours). Late submissions will not be accommodated except at the sole discretion of Anaheim Fire & Rescue.
Please contact tradeshowinspector@anaheim.net for more information.
