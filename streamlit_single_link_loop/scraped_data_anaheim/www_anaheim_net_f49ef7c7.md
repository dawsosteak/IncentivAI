#  Business Uninterruptible Power Supply Rebate 
Anaheim Public Utilities now offers business customers rebates for uninterruptible power supply (UPS) devices, also known as battery backup. An uninterruptible power supply is a device that provides backup power to electronic equipment in case of a power outage or fluctuations in the power supply.
### Installation/Efficiency Criteria
  
Must be [ENERGY STAR® ](https://www.energystar.gov/productfinder/product/certified-uninterruptible-power-supplies/results)Certified  

  

### Incentive Amount
  
$150 (Maximum of 5). The incentive amount per UPS will not exceed $150 or 75% of the purchase price, whichever is the lesser of the two.  

For complete rebate[ program guidelines, W-9 and application](https://www.anaheim.net/DocumentCenter/View/35739/Business-UPS-Application).
