#  Genre Book Lists 
  1. [Romantasy](https://www.anaheim.net/6635/Genre-Book-Lists#taba23606fa-dd36-4ac7-bf62-3fc4c8ec4baf_4)


### [**![WinterDuke](https://www.anaheim.net/ImageRepository/Document?documentId=65235)The Winter Duke by Claire Eliza Barlett**](https://ana.ent.sirsi.net/client/en_US/virtual/search/detailnonmodal/ent:$002f$002fSD_ILS$002f0$002fSD_ILS:2703078/one?qu=The+winter+duke+Claire+Eliza+Bartlett)
A Sleeping Beauty / Briar Rose (Brothers Grimm) re-telling with a political twist and magical underwater kingdom. Nevertheless, the emotions are quite real in this enchanting love story.
  

  

  

  

  

  

###   

  

### [**![ShadowsBetweenUs](https://www.anaheim.net/ImageRepository/Document?documentId=65248)The Shadows Between Us by Tricia Levenseller**](http://ana.ent.sirsi.net/client/en_US/virtual/search/results?qu=The+shadows+between+us+Tricia+Levenseller&te=)
Sometimes falling in love is dangerous, especially in a kingdom full of villains. However, in this novel there is an extra plot twist and a mystery driven by ambition. Sound intriguing?
  

  

  

  

  

  

  

  

### [**![ThisWovenKingdom](https://www.anaheim.net/ImageRepository/Document?documentId=65247)This Woven Kingdom by Tahereh Mafi**](https://ana.ent.sirsi.net/client/en_US/virtual/search/results?qu=This+woven+kingdom+Tahereh+Mafi&te=)
Princes and prophecies, forbidden love, and a kingdom inspired by the Middle East. Fun fact-- this epic novel was actually inspired by an epic Persian poem-- Shahnameh.
  

  

  

  

  

  

  

###   

### [**![SorceryofThorns](https://www.anaheim.net/ImageRepository/Document?documentId=65243)Sorcery of Thorns by Margaret Rogerson**](https://ana.ent.sirsi.net/client/en_US/virtual/search/detailnonmodal/ent:$002f$002fSD_ILS$002f0$002fSD_ILS:2693736/one?qu=Sorcery+of+thorns+Margaret+Rogerson)
Raised in the world of magical libraries, a dangerous book of spells may produce some unsettling events for the protagonist. There is a murder, some adventure, a little humor and an unpredictable ending.
  

  

  

  

  

  

  

  

### [**![DivineRivals](https://www.anaheim.net/ImageRepository/Document?documentId=65241)Divine Rivals by Rebecca Ross**](https://ana.ent.sirsi.net/client/en_US/virtual/search/results?qu=Divine+rivals&te=)
The impacts of war set the stage of this light fantasy, heavy romance story. A series of letters supposedly penned by the protagonist (who is a journalist) suggest a strange supernatural power is at play.
  

  

  

  

  

  

  

  

### [**![TheseHollowVows](https://www.anaheim.net/ImageRepository/Document?documentId=65240)These Hollow Vows by Lexi Ryan**](https://ana.ent.sirsi.net/client/en_US/virtual/search/results?qu=These+hollow+vows&te=)
Suspenseful Cinderella / Aschenputtel (Brothers Grimm) retelling with evil faeries, a goblin, and a love triangle.
  

  

  

  

  

  

  

  

  

### [**![Lore by Alexandra Bracken](https://www.anaheim.net/ImageRepository/Document?documentId=65492)Lore by Alexandra Bracken**](https://ana.ent.sirsi.net/client/en_US/virtual/search/results?qu=Lore+Alexandra+Bracken&te=)
Greek mythology informs this fantasy-adventure novel that takes place after the protagonist's family was murdered. Fast plot and intense New York City setting make for a real page-turner!
