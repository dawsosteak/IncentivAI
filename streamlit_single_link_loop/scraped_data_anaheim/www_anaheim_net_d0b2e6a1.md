#  Small Business Energy & Water Direct Install Program 
The enhanced and modified program is established to meet the specific requirements of small business customers. While seeking to achieve energy and water efficiency objectives, we will provide valuable assistance and effectively manage your utility costs. An assorted selection of energy and water efficiency measures within the program can provide options ranging from LED lighting upgrades, sophisticated lighting controls, refrigeration enhancements, HVAC tune-ups, programmable/smart thermostats, water-efficient toilets/urinals, aerators, and an extensive list of other impactful measures.
![HVAC Tume up](https://www.anaheim.net/ImageRepository/Document?documentID=32320)
### Program Benefits   
  

Our certified energy specialists will guide you through the program requirements while offering valuable insights into enhancing your facility's energy and water efficiency. A complimentary energy assessment will provide conservation recommendations to help identify outdated technology and support the implementation of new technologies to achieve energy and water savings goals. Installing efficient equipment prolongs equipment life, enhances performance, and significantly reduces operating costs. For a **limited time,** eligible small business customers may benefit from the program's allowance of up to $3,000 for high-energy measures and up to $1,250 for high-efficiency water measures.
How the Program Works 
  * Qualified small business customers will receive a complimentary energy assessment and proceed with the following: 


    1. Recommend high-efficiency energy and water measures intended to achieve cost savings
    2. Identify high-efficiency approved measures and estimate annual energy and water savings 
    3. Describe the optimal turnkey solution for implementing the recommended measures
    4. Identify other financial responsibilities before installation (if applicable)
    5. Scheduling the installation time to implement high-efficiency measures
  * New installation measures may be subject to an inspection
  * Program funding limits and restrictions may apply
  * Funding is available on a first-come, first-served basis
  * Call us at 714-765-4258 to confirm eligibility for the Small Business Energy and Water Direct Install Program
