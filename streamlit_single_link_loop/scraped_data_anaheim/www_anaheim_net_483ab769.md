# Brookhurst Corridor Study
We are working with our community to create a **better Brookhurst** Street. 
The 3.5-mile corridor through west Anaheim is a vibrant mix of restaurants and businesses serving a diverse population and distinct cultures.
Along with the businesses of Brookhurst, the street includes nearby neighborhoods, a community center, schools, parks and places of worship, with a mix of old and new. 
Brookhurst is also home to **Little Arabia** , a culturally unique area officially recognized by the Anaheim City Council.
Little Arabia is a collection of restaurants, shops and other businesses built by immigrants from the Arab world and the greater Middle East since the 1980s. Spanning roughly a mile of Brookhurst from Broadway to about Ball Road, Little Arabia is a favorite for Mediterranean food and other culturally unique businesses.

# About the study
From spring to late 2023, the study looked at how best to **advance Brookhurst for everyone** now and in the years to come.
The **first phase** covered issues and opportunities related to how land along Brookhurst is used, getting around, parking, signs and other ways to improve the street. The first phase also studied the recently designated Little Arabia district and looked at whether the district's initial boundaries should be updated.
We saw **extensive community input**. We set out to better understand community needs, goals and visions for the corridor. The feedback was used to develop recommendations for the city to consider related to land use regulations, public improvements, funding, identity and more.
A community working group was created and helped serve as the conduit between the residents and businesses along Brookhurst and the city.
A series of community events, such as pop-ups, walking tours and open houses were hosted to learn more about the study and offer feedback.
The first phase culminated in a workshop before the Anaheim City Council in December 2023.

[![City Council presentation 12-12-23 Opens in new window](https://www.anaheim.net/ImageRepository/Document?documentId=52557)](https://www.anaheim.net/DocumentCenter/View/52558/City-Council-presentation-12-12-23)
# Second Phase
The **second phase** of the Brookhurst Corridor Study will play out in 2024 with formal consideration of recommendations by the Anaheim City Council.
The City Council is expected to formally look at adopting recommendations in early 2024.
From there, the study will serve as a guide for how Brookhurst develops in the years and even decades to come.
