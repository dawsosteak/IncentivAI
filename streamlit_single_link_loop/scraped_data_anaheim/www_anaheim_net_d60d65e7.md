#  Resource Links for Older Adults 
Aging gracefully is about staying informed, connected, and empowered. At our Anaheim's Older Adult Program resource page, we provide a wide range of tools, services, and information designed to help older adults and their families navigate the challenges and opportunities of aging. Whether you're looking for health and wellness resources, social activities, housing options, or caregiving support, we're here to guide you every step of the way. Explore our community of support and discover how you can live a vibrant, fulfilling life at every stage of your journey.
Most of these links are to third party organizations or agencies, therefore, City staff are not responsible for answering general or detailed questions about the resource. We encourage you to to click the link, if applicable, or call the associated number to speak to a subject matter expert of that resource.
