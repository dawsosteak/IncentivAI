#  Rapid Business Assistance 
Anaheim businesses facing the difficult prospect of downsizing, closing or relocating can get timely, urgent help from our Rapid Economic Development Team. 
Our RED Team can assist with a group of specialists from across the city of Anaheim who are trained to provide emergency business support.
Our RED Team incudes our Economic Development Department, Workforce Connection, Anaheim's employment services group, Anaheim Public Utilities, Planning & Building and other key departments committed to helping businesses thrive in Anaheim.
We'll meet with business owners and key executives on ways to stabilize existing operations and identify services available to address business challenges.
To work with our RED Team, call Business Development Manager Shant Boodanian at (714) 765-4285 or email him at sboodanian@anaheim.net.
