#  Neighborhood Services 
![Helping Neighborhoods Help Themselves](https://www.anaheim.net/ImageRepository/Document?documentID=14164)
## Mission
To lead a city-wide initiative aimed at enhancing the quality of life in Anaheim neighborhoods. We empower residents to take charge of their communities by facilitating collaboration between the city and various community resources. Together, we strive to create safe, vibrant, and thriving neighborhoods where every resident can thrive.
### About Us
Neighborhood Services is the dedicated resource for residents seeking assistance and support. Unsure of which city department to contact? Have questions, ideas, or concerns about your neighborhood? Look no further – we're here to help! At Neighborhood Services, we believe in fostering partnerships between residents, stakeholders, and city resources to address neighborhood concerns and drive positive change.
### Council Districts
Anaheim operates under a "By-District" system for electing City Council members, consisting of six electoral districts, with the Mayor elected at large. Understanding your district is key to engaging with local governance and making your voice heard in shaping the future of Anaheim.
