  1. [Business Watch](http://www.anaheim.net/2602/Business-Watch)
Business Watch is a simple concept of businesses watching out for other businesses, reporting suspicious activity and crime to the police. Business Watch focuses on teaching the business personnel on how to be observant and aware, prevent crime in your business community, and how to report suspicious activity and crime. Businesses working together in cooperation with law enforcement can make one of the best crime-fighting teams around. 
  2. [Coffee with a Cop](http://www.anaheim.net/2605/Coffee-with-a-Cop)
  3. [Crime Prevention](http://anaheim.net/242/Crime-Prevention)
The Anaheim Police has several services available to help residents and businesses with crime prevention.
  4. [Neighborhood Watch](http://www.anaheim.net/2601/Neighborhood-Watch)
Neighborhood Watch is a great way for neighbors to get to know each other and work together, get trained to recognize and report suspicious activities in your neighborhood, implement crime prevention techniques such as home security and security through environmental design and enlist the active participation of citizens in cooperation with law enforcement to reduce crime in your community. 
  5. [Public Awareness Through Citizen Education (PACE)](https://www.anaheim.net/238/Public-Awareness-Through-Citizen-Educati)
The Citizen Academy teaches community members about the philosophy, policies and guiding principles of the department.
  6. [Volunteers in Police Services (VIPS)](https://www.anaheim.net/240/Volunteers-in-Police-Services-VIPS)
The mission of the Volunteers in Police Service (VIPS) program is to provide volunteer support staff to aid police personnel in the delivery of services to the citizens of Anaheim.
  7. [Youth Services](http://www.anaheim.net/2627/Youth-Services)
Discover the many youth programs in the Anaheim Police Department.
