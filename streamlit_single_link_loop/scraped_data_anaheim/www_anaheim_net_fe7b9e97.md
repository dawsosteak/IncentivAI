#  Substation Improvement 
Substations are a critical part of the local grid as they distribute electricity to area residents and businesses. Anaheim Public Utilities relies on operational flexibility and maintenance of infrastructure to preserve the integrity of our electric system, which reduces the duration of power outages affecting areas throughout the city. 
As part of APU's ongoing capital project improvements, substation improvements to replace aging equipment maximize system performance and resiliency, alleviate congestion on existing infrastructure, and ensure long-term reliability for all Anaheim customers.
  1. [Sharp Substation](https://www.anaheim.net/6756/Substation-Improvement#tabf26fbc86-16f9-4866-9df1-ac9a955263ea_0)


**Project Timeline**
Phase 1: Fall 2023 – Spring 2025  
Phase 2: Fall 2027 – Spring 2029
To maintain continuous, safe, and reliable service, while utilizing the existing limited space on site, the Sharp Substation Upgrade Project has been split into two (2) separate phases. This approach allows crews to upgrade one side of the substation at a time while keeping the other side in service, avoiding impacts to customers. 
The first phase of the project has been completed, which has replaced half of the substation equipment nearing the end of its life. The second phase of the project will mirror the first phase to complete the substation upgrade. 
  1. [Southwest Substation](https://www.anaheim.net/6756/Substation-Improvement#tabf26fbc86-16f9-4866-9df1-ac9a955263ea_1)


**Project Timeline**
Design: Winter 2024 – Winter 2025   
Construction: Fall 2025 – Summer 2027 
As electrification increases power demand citywide, the Southwest Substation upgrade will replace aging utility equipment by installing new equipment on a site adjacent to the current substation. The new site's location allows construction to occur without interrupting existing service. 
The project's improvements offer grid modernization, capacity upgrades, and operational flexibility during outages. 
  1. [Vermont Substation](https://www.anaheim.net/6756/Substation-Improvement#tabf26fbc86-16f9-4866-9df1-ac9a955263ea_2)


**Project Timeline**
Project Award: Spring 2026  
Design: Spring 2026 – Fall 2027   
Construction: Spring 2028 – Summer 2029 
This will be the first installation of its kind in the United States. The planned expansion to Vermont Substation will help maintain reliable power to residents and local businesses and minimize strain on our existing power grid.
APU is committed to lowering our carbon footprint and prioritizing green energy. The newly installed Gas Insulated Equipment (GIE) will be “vacuum” technology, which has a Global Warming Impact of zero and 100% California Air Resources Board (CARB) compliant.
