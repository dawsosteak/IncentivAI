![Pedestrian study logo 8-22-24](https://www.anaheim.net/ImageRepository/Document?documentID=57506)
**Anaheim is looking at how people get around on foot in The Anaheim Resort, home to our theme parks, convention center, hotels and more.**
**Each year, the resort sees more than 25 million visitors, with many setting out on foot along Harbor Boulevard, Katella Avenue and other streets.**
**The results of the study will help guide the planning, design, and implementation of walking improvements throughout the resort, including planned f****oot bridges throughout the area.**
  

**The goal is ensure those walking can get around safely and efficiently to enjoy all The Anaheim Resort has to offer.**
