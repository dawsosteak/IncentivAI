#  Energy Code Forms 
Effective July 1, 2014, the new 2013 Building Efficiency Standards are in effect. The California Energy Commission has all their regulations and manuals [online](http://www.energy.ca.gov/title24/2013standards/index.html).  
  
**Note:** Please make sure you have the required documents complete when you come to apply for a permit. Without these forms, we will not be able to process your permit the same day. 
**California Energy Codes and Forms:**
  * [https://www.energy.ca.gov/title24/2019standards](https://www.energy.ca.gov/title24/2019standards/index.html)
