#  Anaheim Canyon 
![The Anaheim Canyon - Banner 1](https://www.anaheim.net/ImageRepository/Document?documentID=47065)
Anaheim is renowned for its tourism industry with the Disneyland Resort, Angels Baseball, the Anaheim Ducks and the Anaheim Convention Center.
Did you know Anaheim also has one of the largest areas for industry and technology in all of Orange County?
The Anaheim Canyon is one of the leading industrial and innovation centers in California. Its strategic location provides access to freeways and a commuter rail. 
Anaheim is determined to preserve and enhance the area’s economic vitality by fostering a positive business climate. The city has reduced and streamlined various regulations affecting development and businesses in Anaheim Canyon. 
In addition, public areas have become a focus of improvements, such as new sidewalks, landscaping, and roadways. Throughout the Canyon, signs highlight and enhance the area's unique sense of place for business and innovation.
The Economic Development Department is proud to serve Anaheim businesses in the Canyon through:
  * Site selection assistance
  * Business concierge services
  * Business matchmaking
  * And more
