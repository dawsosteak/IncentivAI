Enrollment Process and What to Expect Next • Complete applications will be processed in the order they are received. The application will remain open until all program funds are committed. Due to high demand, eligible residents are encouraged to apply as soon as possible. • Eligible applicants will receive a Preliminary Qualification Notice. Grants are awarded on a first-come, first-served basis when a qualified applicant opens escrow on an eligible home. • Applications are available in English, Spanish, Khmer, and Tagalog, with additional languages available upon request. Hard copies can also be provided upon request.
