#  A/C Tune-Up 
The Anaheim Public Utilities A/C Tune-Up Rebate Program provides residential customers a rebate of up to a $100 ($150 for income-qualified customers) for getting an A/C tune-up. Customers are only eligible to participate in this Program once every twelve months and applications must be submitted within 1 year of A/C tune-up service.
### How the Program Works
1. Schedule an A/C tune-up with a licensed HVAC contractor  
2. Apply [online](https://apu.conservationrebates.com/) OR Mail in the following documents:
  * A/C Tune-Up Rebate Application: [ENGLISH](https://www.anaheim.net/DocumentCenter/View/56308/AC-Tune-Up-Application-8_6_24_FILLABLE_ENGLISH) or [SPANISH](https://www.anaheim.net/DocumentCenter/View/56309/AC-Tune-Up-Application-8_6_24_FILLABLE_SPANISH)
  * Invoice that details the job performed, amount paid, and serviced date
  * Mail documents to:


Anaheim Public Utilities  
Attn: A/C Tune-Up  
201 S. Anaheim Blvd., Suite 801  
Anaheim, CA 92805
Rebates are limited and subject to available funding. Incentive amount not to exceed purchase price.
  

### Qualifying Income Levels
[*Low-Income Limits as defined by the California Department of Housing and Community Development (adjusted annually).](https://www.anaheim.net/6423)
