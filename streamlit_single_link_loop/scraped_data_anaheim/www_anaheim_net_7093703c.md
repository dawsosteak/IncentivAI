![Makerspace_Website banner](https://www.anaheim.net/ImageRepository/Document?documentID=65110)
## Welcome to the Link Labs
a collaborative environment designed to foster creativity, innovation, and exploration. Here, your Anaheim Public Library card connects you to our Makerspace, Media Lab, and Memory Lab—all equipped with advanced technology to help you develop new skills and bring your ideas to life.
## Free for Anaheim Public Library card holders 13 & up
[Visit any branch to sign up for a library card.](https://anaheim.net/903/Locations-Hours)
## All experience levels welcome
First-time visitors will receive an orientation to our space and equipment.

# FAQS
  1. [Who can use the Link Labs?](https://www.anaheim.net/6445/Link-Labs#tab95acc396-bc5b-46ce-8270-b0d67f0aaf89_0)


The Link Labs is open to all Anaheim Public Library cardholders 13 and up. Parent/guardian permission is required for cardholders ages 13-17. Children 12 and under must always be accompanied by a parent or guardian at the same station in the labs; children cannot use a station by themselves. The computers in this space are for Link Labs projects only; patrons needing computers for general use may utilize the Adult, Teen, and Children's computer labs as appropriate. 
  1. [Where is the Link Labs?](https://www.anaheim.net/6445/Link-Labs#tab95acc396-bc5b-46ce-8270-b0d67f0aaf89_1)


The Link Labs is located at Haskett Branch, 2650 W Broadway, Anaheim, CA 92804.
The Link Makerspace can be accessed through the double doors across from the Check-Out desk. The Link Media Lab is located to the right of the red tunnel leading to our Children's library. The Link Memory Lab is located inside the Link Media Lab.
  1. [How do I get started?](https://www.anaheim.net/6445/Link-Labs#tab95acc396-bc5b-46ce-8270-b0d67f0aaf89_2)


## Available by appointment only.
**Plan ahead.** [First-time users must complete the Orientation to the Link Labs as well as a corresponding Equipment Orientation](https://www.anaheim.net/6671/Request-a-Link-Labs-Appointment), regardless of skill level. In-person orientations are scheduled per staff availability and will be conducted during the first 15-30 minutes of a reservation. 
Once you are a member of the Link Labs, you can reserve any station for which you’ve completed an Equipment Orientation. Reservations may be made at least 48 hours in advance. You may reserve a station for two (2) hours per reservation per day. Contact staff for more information.
  1. [Do I need training or experience? ](https://www.anaheim.net/6445/Link-Labs#tab95acc396-bc5b-46ce-8270-b0d67f0aaf89_3)


No experience is required. [Equipment Orientations](https://www.anaheim.net/6671/Request-a-Link-Labs-Appointment) include a basic introduction to the station and how to start your first project. If you would like more guidance, we will be offering classes soon: [Check out our branch calendar](https://anaheim.net/library). We also have many resources in our physical and digital collections. 
  1. [Will you have classes?](https://www.anaheim.net/6445/Link-Labs#tab95acc396-bc5b-46ce-8270-b0d67f0aaf89_4)


We offer drop-in programs bi-monthly on Saturdays from 11 AM to 3 PM. No orientation or registration is required. [Check out our branch calendar for more information. ](https://anaheim.libcal.com/calendar/haskett)
  1. [How much does it cost to use the Link Labs?](https://www.anaheim.net/6445/Link-Labs#tab95acc396-bc5b-46ce-8270-b0d67f0aaf89_5)


The Link Labs is available with an Anaheim Public Library card, which is free for California Residents. [Learn more about obtaining a library card here.](https://anaheim.net/1229/Get-Started)
  1. [Do you provide materials?](https://www.anaheim.net/6445/Link-Labs#tab95acc396-bc5b-46ce-8270-b0d67f0aaf89_6)


## Bring your own materials.
The Link Makerspace has a limited amount of materials available free to use for patrons. Monthly limits may apply. Otherwise, you may bring in your own materials that are on the staff-approved list. Other materials may be denied use in the lab unless or until staff can verify its safe handling. Please contact staff for a complete list of approved materials. 
  1. [How can I get updates for the Link Labs?](https://www.anaheim.net/6445/Link-Labs#tab95acc396-bc5b-46ce-8270-b0d67f0aaf89_7)


Sign up for our newsletter for all Anaheim Public Library updates, or follow our social media channels.
# About the Link Labs
## Our Mission 
The Link Labs is a hub for innovation, education, and collaboration. Our aim is to make advanced skills accessible by connecting library patrons to cutting-edge technology, as well as resources and education guided by experienced creators. We encourage learning from process and practice that becomes experience for developing new skills. Whether you are an aspiring hobbyist or a dedicated maker, all are welcome in this space to take creative risks, solve problems, and learn something new. 
## Our Vision 
With the help of the Link Labs, library patrons will feel empowered to utilize technology and seek information to reach their project goals. The open working space and its resources will provide new grounds for people to connect to technology, experienced creators, and each other. By dedicating a space to collaboration and creativity, new and diverse voices in the community can take a step forward to honor the past, shape the present, and envision the future.
