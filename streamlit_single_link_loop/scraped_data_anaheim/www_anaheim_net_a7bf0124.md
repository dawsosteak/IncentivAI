#  Mylar Balloons 
## Mylar balloons can cause serious problems
when they come into contact with power lines. Keep them weighted down and dispose of them properly after they deflate! 
Mylar balloons can misdirect the electrical flow within power lines and cause outages due to their metallic outer surface. Help avoid power outages!
