#  Anaheim Heritage Center 
## Anaheim Heritage Center ~ Disney Resort Reading Room at the MUZEO
  
Please visit our library in Downtown Anaheim on the promenade. Opened in 1967, the history room contains almost a million items organized and cataloged for use by scholars, students, researchers and community residents. Trained library personnel are available to offer assistance.
