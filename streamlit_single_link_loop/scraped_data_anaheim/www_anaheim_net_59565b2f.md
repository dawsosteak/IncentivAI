#  Older Adult Special Events 
At the City of Anaheim's Older Adult Program, we believe that life is a celebration, and every moment matters. Our special events are designed to bring joy, connection, and creativity to our Older Adult community. Whether you're looking to meet new friends, explore new hobbies, or simply enjoy a fun day out, we have something for everyone. From engaging workshops and lively social gatherings to enriching educational sessions, we invite you to join us in making wonderful memories together. Check out our upcoming events and discover how you can participate in the joy of community!
