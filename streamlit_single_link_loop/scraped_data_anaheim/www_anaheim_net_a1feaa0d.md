#  Income Qualified Third-Party Water Bill Rebate 
## Program Overview 
  
Anaheim Public Utilities’ Income-Qualified Water Rebate Program provides**up to a $120 rebate per year** to qualifying customers who receive a third-party water bill.  
  

##  How it Works  
  

###  To be eligible, customers must:   
  

    * Receive their water bill from a third-party provider 
    * Be a senior (62 years of age or older), long-term disabled, or military veteran 
    * Meet the [income qualifications](https://anaheim.net/6423/Qualifying-Income-Levels)
    * Have an electric account with Anaheim Public Utilities that is in good standing 


  

### The application window to apply is from **January 1 – January 31** , and**June 1 – June 30**. 
###   

Submissions will be closed outside of these two periods. 
  

## How to Apply 
###    
Customers**already enrolled in our Utility Discount Program** require the following:   
  

  1. A completed Income Qualified Water Rebate Application ([English](https://www.anaheim.net/DocumentCenter/View/58586/IQ-Water-Rebate-Application-as-of-010925_)) ([Spanish](https://www.anaheim.net/DocumentCenter/View/58640/IQ-Water-Rebate-Application_Spanish))
  2. Up to the past 12 immediate months’ water bills from a third-party provider 


###  Customers**not enrolled in the Utility Discount Program** require the following:   
  

  1. A completed Income Qualified Water Rebate Application ([English](https://www.anaheim.net/DocumentCenter/View/58586/IQ-Water-Rebate-Application-as-of-010925_)) ([Spanish](https://www.anaheim.net/DocumentCenter/View/58640/IQ-Water-Rebate-Application_Spanish))
  2. Proof of senior, disability, or veteran status 
     * _To be eligible as a senior_ , you must attach a copy of identification proving your age is at least 62 years old 
     * _If you are claiming a disability_ , you must attach a copy of your latest SSI statement or a letter from your physician, if you are claiming a disability 
     * T _o be eligible as a military veteran,_ you must attach a copy of either a DD-214, Veteran Heath Identification (VHIC), or Common Access Card 
  3. Up to the past 12 immediate months’ water bills from a third-party provider 


  
  

###  **Submit** your application and supporting documents to:
