#  Rebates & Programs
###  Bright Energy Solutions ® Residential Rebate Program
Get rewarded for your energy-efficient home. We offer incentives for energy-saving projects for residential customers and access to our network of Trade Ally contractors. These programs are made available to you through ALP and our power supplier, Missouri River Energy Services.
Find, fill and submit a rebate to receive energy savings for your home.
If you have any questions, please contact us at [**320-763-6501**](tel:320-763-6501).
[ Explore Rebates & Programs ](https://www.alputilities.com/residential/rebates-programs/#explore "Explore Rebates & Programs")
###  Explore Rebates & Programs
[](https://www.alputilities.com/wp-content/uploads/2026-Central-AC-Heat-Pump-Tune-Up.pdf "main Form URL for Central A/C or Heat Pump Tuneup")
Earn a **$40 rebate** when you get your central A/C or heat pump tune-up. Tune-ups keep your equipment running efficiently and help cut cooling costs. Apply for this rebate every other year.

Nearly half of the energy used in your home goes to heating and cooling. Making smart decisions about your home’s heating, ventilating and air conditioning (HVAC) system can have a big effect on your utility bills — and your comfort. Plus, if you choose a certified Quality Install contractor to install your HVAC system, you can receive an**additional $100 rebate.**
[View a list ](https://brightenergysolutions.com/find-a-dealer/)of certified quality install contractors.

  * Air-source heat pump (gas/propane backup): $600 – $1,000
  * Central air conditioner: $250 – $350
  * Mini-split heat pump: $250 – $400 per outdoor unit
  * Mini-split air conditioner: $150 – $200 per outdoor unit
  * Central A/C or heat-pump tuneup: $30
  * Geothermal heat pump: $200/ton
  * ENERGY STAR smart thermostat: $25
  * Heat-pump water heater: $200
  * ECM domestic hot water recirculating pumps: $75 – $900

[](https://www.alputilities.com/wp-content/uploads/2026-Energy-Star-Rebate-Form-ALPupdate.pdf "main Form URL for Smart Thermostat")
Earn a **$25 rebate** when you install an ENERGY STAR smart thermostat. Smart thermostats automatically adjust to save energy when you are asleep or away. They can be controlled remotely using a smartphone, tablet or computer.
* * *
[](https://www.alputilities.com/wp-content/uploads/2026-Energy-Star-Rebate-Form-ALPupdate.pdf "form URL for Smart Thermostat")
[](https://www.alputilities.com/wp-content/uploads/ALP-Water-Heater-Rebate-Form_Update2026.pdf "main Form URL for Water Heater")
Purchase a new electric water heater, participate in ALP’s load management program and you can receive a rebate.
50g or larger on cycling program **– $100**
80g or larger on curtailing program **– $250**
Requirements:
  * Must have a minimum tank capacity of 50 gallons
  * Must have at least a 10 year or greater warranty
  * Must be connected to an ALP load control receiver and participate in ALP’s Load Management Program.
