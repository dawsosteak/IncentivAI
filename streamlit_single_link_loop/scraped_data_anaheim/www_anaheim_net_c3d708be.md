#  Business License Application 
Please select the applicable section below. Once you have completed your application, please submit it to our office **with your payment**.  
  
Applications are accepted by mail or in person Monday through Friday between 8 AM and 4 PM. If you have any questions, please contact the business license staff at (714) 765-5194.
