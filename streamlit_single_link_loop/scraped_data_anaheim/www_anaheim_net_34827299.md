#  Short-Term Rental Program 
## **CORONAVIRUS UPDATE**
For guidance and best practices on operating a short term rental during the coronavirus pandemic, [click here](https://files.covid19.ca.gov/pdf/guidance-hotels-lodging-rentals.pdf).
**——————————**
As of mid-2019, there are 222 permitted short-term rentals in Anaheim.
In June 2019, our City Council adopted an ordinance updating our policy covering STRs. You can view the short-term rental ordinance (Chapter 4.05) [here](https://codelibrary.amlegal.com/codes/anaheim/latest/anaheim_ca/0-0-0-55214#JD_Chapter4.05).
The new policy reverses a 2016 STR ban and phase-out, which proved challenging as STR owners sought decades of continued operation to recoup investments in their properties.
The new policy allows the majority of STRs to continue operating in Anaheim under strict regulations and good neighbor policies.
The opening of entirely new STRs remains prohibited under the new policy.
You can read more in our fact sheet [here](https://www.anaheim.net/DocumentCenter/View/26468/Short-term-rentals-fact-sheet-summer-2019).
