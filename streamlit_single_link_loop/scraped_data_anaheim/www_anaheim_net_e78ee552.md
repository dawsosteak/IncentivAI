The 2026 free Anaheim Health Fair is back for its 20th anniversary!
Anaheim's annual health fair allows residents to receive free medical, dental, vision, women's and other health care.
This free event could not be possible without the doctors, healthcare professionals and volunteers. If you are interested in volunteering for the 2026 event, please see the link above.
Everyone is welcome — we do not collect or ask for citizenship status. Your participation is safe and confidential.
_If you encounter issues with registering for any of the above or have more questions, please email healthfair@anaheim.net._
