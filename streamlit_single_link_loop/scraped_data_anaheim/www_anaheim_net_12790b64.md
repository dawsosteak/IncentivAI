#  Picnic Shelters & Open Areas 
Reserve a **Picnic Shelter** [online](https://secure.rec1.com/CA/city-of-anaheim/catalog)! Whether you are having a birthday party or a family picnic, we have shelters throughout Anaheim that can host your event. Our shelters vary in size and offer different amenities including barbecues, sinks, and close accessibility to the playgrounds. Please note that not all Picnic Shelter facilities offer sink and/or electrical access. Please visit [anaheim.net/play](https://anaheim.net/play) for more detailed facility information.
**Open Area** [applications](https://www.anaheim.net/DocumentCenter/View/68055/2026-Open-Area-Application) are available for gatherings of 25 or more i**n parks that do not offer Picnic Shelter facilities**. Please note that gatherings over 100 are considered Special Events. Applications must be submitted to [facilityscheduling@anaheim.net](https://facilityscheduling.net) _at least_ 10 working days prior to your reservation date. All applications received within 10 days of the reservation date will be assessed a **$28 permit rush fee.**

## Picnic Shelter & Open Area Regulations
The following is prohibited in any Anaheim Park in accordance with [Anaheim Municipal Code Chapter 13.08](https://codelibrary.amlegal.com/codes/anaheim/latest/anaheim_ca/0-0-0-60982): 
  * Alcohol
  * Amplified Sound
  * Barbecues (other than those provided at the park facility)
  * Bounce Houses
  * Canopies exceeding 10 ft x 10 ft 


Please reference your permit document as well as the [Anaheim Municipal Code Chapter 13.08](https://codelibrary.amlegal.com/codes/anaheim/latest/anaheim_ca/0-0-0-60982) for a full list of restrictions.
