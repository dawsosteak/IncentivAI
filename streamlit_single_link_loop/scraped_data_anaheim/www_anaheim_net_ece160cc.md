  1. [Community Emergency Response Team (CERT)](https://www.anaheim.net/887/Community-Emergency-Response-Team-CERT)
Anaheim CERT promotes the ongoing training and participation of our volunteer force by providing many educational opportunities to better prepare for and respond to disasters.
  2. [EMT Scholarship Program ](https://www.anaheim.net/6450/EMT-Scholarship-Program)
Anaheim Fire & Rescue, in conjunction with the Anaheim Community Foundation, has established an Emergency Medical Technician (EMT) Scholarship Program for our Anaheim community interested in pursuing a career in Public Safety. 
  3. [Explorer Program](https://www.anaheim.net/5427/Explorer-Program)
Is open to youth between 15-21 years of age, designed to provide hands-on experience for those interested in building Leadership, Teamwork, and Accountability skills, as well as for those considering a career as a firefighter. For more information or to learn when our next quarterly recruitment meeting is scheduled, please call 714-765-4036.
  4. [FireMedics Program](https://www.anaheim.net/615/FireMedics-Program)
The Paramedic Membership Program is an official program offered by Anaheim Fire & Rescue, which offsets the cost of emergency medical responses.
  5. [RACES](https://www.anaheim.net/1209/RACES)
Thank you for your interest in the Anaheim Radio Amateur Civil Emergency Service (RACES) program, sponsored by the Anaheim Fire & Rescue's Office of Disaster Preparedness. 
  6. [Small Hydrocarbon Pick-Up Program ](https://www.anaheim.net/728/Small-Hydrocarbon-Pick-Up-Program)
Read about efficient and effective means of managing and handling certain wastes on public property generated by spills, abandonment and traffic accidents.
  7. [Used Oil & Household Hazardous Waste](https://www.anaheim.net/729/Used-Oil-Household-Hazardous-Waste)
The Anaheim Fire Department’s Community Relations Section and the California Integrated Waste Management Board (CIWMB) maintain a program that promotes the proper management of used oil and household hazardous waste.
  8. [READY, SET, GO!](https://www.anaheim.net/5629/Preparing-for-Wildfire)
Ready, Set, Go! gives you the tools to protect you, your family, property and community from wildfires. Many residents have built their homes and landscaped without fully understanding the impact that could result from wildfire.
  9. [Advanced Provider Response Unit (APRU) ](https://www.anaheim.net/6783/Advanced-Provider-Response-Unit-APRU)
The Advanced Provider Response Unit (APRU) is a Mobile Integrated Health program developed by Anaheim Fire & Rescue that goes beyond the traditional 911 response. The APRU pairs a highly trained Nurse Practitioner with Anaheim Fire paramedics to follow up after critical incidents where additional support may be needed after emergency crews leave the scene.
