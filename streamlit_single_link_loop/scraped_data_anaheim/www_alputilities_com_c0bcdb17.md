##  About ALP
ALP has been serving the Alexandria area for over 130 years with a commitment of affordability, reliability and service excellence. ALP is owned by those we serve and governed by local community members.
[ More About ALP ](https://www.alputilities.com/who-we-are/ "More About ALP")
####  Mission of Service
As a publicly-owned not-for-profit organization, we have a mission of service, not of profit. To that end, we want to help make our corner of the world a better place.
####  Reliability
ALP conducts annual reviews of its system to identify areas that may need improvement and performs ongoing maintenance to prevent service interruptions. According to the American Public Power Association e-reliability tracker for 2022, ALP’s average outage time was only 6.94 minutes, compared to the industry average of 140.45 minutes.
![APPA RP3 logo](data:image/svg+xml,%3Csvg%20xmlns='http://www.w3.org/2000/svg'%20viewBox='0%200%20271%20117'%3E%3C/svg%3E)
In 2026, ALP was recognized as a Platinum Reliable Public Power Provider (RP3) ® (3 should be small) by the American Public Power Association for providing reliable and safe electric service. The designation, which lasts for three years, recognizes public power utilities that demonstrate the highest proficiency in four key disciplines: reliability, safety, workforce development and system improvement.
