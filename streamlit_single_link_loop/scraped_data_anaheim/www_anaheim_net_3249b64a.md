#  Hispanic Heritage Month 
![Hispanic Heritage Month banner](https://www.anaheim.net/ImageRepository/Document?documentID=66239)
  * **Community Ofrenda at Sunkist Branch**
    * Wednesday, Oct. 1 - Sunday, Nov. 2 
  * **Hispanic Heritage Month Happy Art Hour at Canyon Hills Branch**
    * Wednesday, Oct. 1, 1 p.m. - 2 p.m.
    * Join us for a special Happy Art Hour, where we'll immerse ourselves in a Frida Kahlo art project to celebrate Hispanic Heritage Month!
  * **Coco Movie Day at Sunkist Branch**
    * Wednesday, Oct. 1, 2 p.m. - 4 p.m.
  * **Decorate Sugar Skulls at Sunkist Branch**
    * Thursday, Oct. 2, 3 p.m. - 4 p.m.
  * **Mural Painting with Artist: Carla Roque at Central**
    * Sunday, Oct. 5, 1 p.m.
    * Join us and learn the basics to paint a mural. Please wear wear clothing you don't mind getting dirty. For ages 8-12 years old. REGISTRATION REQUIRED.
  * **Día de Los Muertos - Pet Portrait Altars at Central**
    * Monday, Oct. 6 - Monday, Nov. 3
    * Remember the pet that has loved and played with you but has now gone to the afterlife. Bring a photo of your pet to add to the altar.
  * **3-4-5 Jump and Jive Storytime with Isabel Brazon at Euclid Branch**
    * Friday, Oct. 10, 11:30 a.m. - 12:30 p.m.
    * Our 3-4-5 Jump and Jive Storytime will celebrate Hispanic Heritage Month, featuring an exceptional bilingual performance by Isabel Brazon from Baila Baila.
  * **Ink and Imagination with Guest Artists: Relief Printmaking for Teens at Central**
    * Saturday, Oct. 11, 2 p.m. - 3:30 p.m.
    * Get inspired by the incredible works of Mexican and Chicano printmakers and learn how to make your own relief print! Teens will have the opportunity to draw, cut, and print an original image using ancient and modernized techniques. Taught by Chicana artist-educators, **Anna M. Gaitan** and **Fabiola Ramirez** , this special workshop is funded by the Friends of the Anaheim Public Library. All supplies included! No reservation needed but this workshop is limited to ten teens due to space and materials. **For teens only** -- in high school and junior high.
  * **Buñuelos Cooking Demo at Central**
    * Tuesday, Oct. 21, 4 p.m.
    * Learn how to make delicious cinnamon buñuelo as a tasty snack!
  * **Encanto Movie & Scratch Art at Central**
    * Tuesday, Oct. 14, 4 p.m.
    * Learn to scratch out colorful art inspired by the movie Encanto.
