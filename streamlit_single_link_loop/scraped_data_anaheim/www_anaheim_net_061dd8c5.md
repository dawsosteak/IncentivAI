## A new way to connect with your city — on your schedule
Our Community Open Houses make it easier than ever to stay informed, ask questions and share what matters most to you — all in a casual, welcoming setting.
Our open houses are designed as drop-in events. Come by whenever it’s convenient during the two-hour window and visit the city booths and staff that interest you most. No formal presentations, no pressure — just real conversations about your neighborhood.
