#  Parking Control / Citations 
**Parking Control**  
Signs warning motorists of "No Parking for Street Sweeping" restrictions are posted every 250 feet on main streets such as State College and Lincoln and are also at the entrances to residential tracts, per CVC 22507.6.  

  

## Citations
  
You must respond within 21 calendar days of the issue date of your citation.
