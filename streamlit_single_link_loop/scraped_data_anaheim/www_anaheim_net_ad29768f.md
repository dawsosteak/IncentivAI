#  Water Rebates 
Anaheim Public Utilities, in collaboration with the Metropolitan Water District of Southern California’s SoCal Water$mart Program, offers incentives and programs for indoor and outdoor water-saving devices. Replacing inefficient devices can provide water savings and a reduced water bill.

# Water Programs  

Participate in one of our water-saving programs to help minimize your reliance on water usage inside and outside of your home. We can perform an in-home evaluation of your water and electric usage and even help with the costs associated with a landscape transformation project.
