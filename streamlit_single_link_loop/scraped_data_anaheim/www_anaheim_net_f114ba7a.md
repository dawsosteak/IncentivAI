#  Makerspace 
The Link Labs Makerspace is an open, collaborative workshop equipped with technology for fabricating physical products, including 3D printers, sewing machines, vinyl cutting machines, laser cutter and engraver, and sublimation heat presses.
## Dress Code
The Makerspace is a work designated area. For safety, all visitors in the lab must wear closed-toed flat shoes, long hair must be tied back, and no loose clothing or dangling jewelry may be worn.[Read more about the Link Labs Policies.](https://www.anaheim.net/6738/Link-Labs-Policies)
