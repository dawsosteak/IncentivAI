#  Housing Element Sites (2021-2029) 
![Housing element logo 2-15-21 png](https://www.anaheim.net/ImageRepository/Document?documentID=36050)
# Summary of Housing Element Sites
The City of Anaheim has identified potential sites to meet housing needs for all income levels across the community. These housing needs are determined through a State-mandated process called the Regional Housing Needs Assessment (RHNA).
For more background on RHNA, visit our [Housing Element Update webpage](https://anaheim.net/5848/) and see [Appendix B of the Housing Element](https://www.anaheim.net/DocumentCenter/View/67126/Appendix-B) for detailed information on housing sites for 2021–2029.
## Anaheim’s RHNA Allocation
The [Southern California Association of Governments (SCAG)](https://scag.ca.gov/about-us) determined that Anaheim must plan for 17,454 new housing units between 2021 and 2029, broken down as follows:
  * 7,869 units for lower-income households
  * 10,014 units for moderate-income households
  * 2,677 units for above moderate-income households


## Planning for More Units
To ensure Anaheim can meet this allocation, the City has identified more potential sites than required. This approach provides flexibility if some sites are not developed to their maximum capacity or are not used for housing. The summary table below (Table B-1) highlights Anaheim’s RHNA allocation.
[![Table B-1 Summary of RHNA and ASA Opens in new window](https://www.anaheim.net/ImageRepository/Document?documentId=66207)](https://www.anaheim.net/ImageRepository/Document?documentId=66207)
  

#  Types of Housing Element Sites  

Table B-1 above, lists different types of sites that are planned to accommodate SCAG's RHNA allocation of 17,453 housing units. A summary of the types of sites is included below. For detailed descriptions, please see [Appendix B of the Housing Element. ](https://www.anaheim.net/DocumentCenter/View/67126/Appendix-B)
  * **Pipeline Projects** are sites where the City has received a development application, sites that are under construction, and sites that have been approved for construction. See Table B-2 in [Appendix B of the Housing Element](https://www.anaheim.net/DocumentCenter/View/67126/Appendix-B) for a list of pipeline projects or [click this link](https://www.anaheim.net/DocumentCenter/View/66219/). 
  * **Projected ADU Construction** is an estimated number of future accessory dwelling units (ADUs) based on current building permit trends within the City. See Table B-3 and Table B-4 in [Appendix B of the Housing Element](https://www.anaheim.net/DocumentCenter/View/67126/Appendix-B) for the [projected number of ADUs for each year](https://www.anaheim.net/DocumentCenter/View/66215/) and the [projected number of ADUs for each income category](https://www.anaheim.net/DocumentCenter/View/66216/Table-B-4-Projected-ADU-Construction-Affordability-Assumptions) through 2029. 
  * **Candidate Sites** are sites that were analyzed for its potential to result in housing development at specific income levels. The analysis included multiple criteria to assess whether a site can be developed for at each income level. Candidate sites include properties that are zoned and designated for residential or mixed uses, or properties that were determined to be an adequate housing site if a Zoning or General Plan Land Use designation change were approved. All candidate sites went through several public reviews and the proposed Zone and General Plan Land Use designation changes were approved by the City Council on April 22 and May 13, 2025 ([Resolution No. 2025-033](http://records.anaheim.net/CityClerk/DocView.aspx?id=5836925) and [Ordinance No. 6609](http://records.anaheim.net/CityClerk/DocView.aspx?id=5883100)). The analysis for candidate sites can be found starting on page B-13 in [Appendix B of the Housing Element](https://www.anaheim.net/DocumentCenter/View/67126/Appendix-B). See Table B-10 in [Appendix B of the Housing Element](https://www.anaheim.net/DocumentCenter/View/67126/Appendix-B) for the list of candidate sites or [click this link](https://www.anaheim.net/DocumentCenter/View/67126/Appendix-B). The corresponding maps are included in the next section. For more information on public input, see the [Project Documents page](https://www.anaheim.net/6704/).
