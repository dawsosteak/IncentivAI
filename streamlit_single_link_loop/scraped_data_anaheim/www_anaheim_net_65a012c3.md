#  Water Inspections 
As part of the Water Field Operations Division, the Water Utility Inspection Section ensures that the construction of water facilities in the Anaheim Public Utilities service area are built in conformance with: 
  * Contract documents
  * Engineered construction drawings
  * Permits
  * Safety standards
  * Standard specifications

Water Utility Inspections are performed on: 
  * City
  * County
  * Developer
  * Federally funded construction projects
  * State

## Staff & Duties
  
The Water Utility Inspection Section consists of: 
  * 1 water utility inspector
  * 2 senior water utility inspectors
  * A water inspection supervisor

Some of their roles and duties include documentation of construction activities on: 
  * As-builts
  * Daily inspection reports
  * Field notes
  * Record drawings

Other duties include assisting, coordinating and acting as liaison between the numbers of stake holders on numerous construction projects. Water Utility Inspection staff perform customer notification for interruption of water service by contractors and closely oversee water utility construction. Of cardinal importance is excellent customer service to all customers both internal and external doing so in a safe work environment.   
  
Please contact Water Inspection Supervisor Stewart Noble at 714-765-4591 for questions regarding Water Utility Inspections.
