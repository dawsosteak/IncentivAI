#  Air Conditioner Incentive 
**  
Replacing your old and inefficient air conditioner units can save more money than you think.**
Incentives are available for installing new high-efficiency air conditioning (AC) units. If your current system is nearing or has reached its typical useful life of about 15 years, now is an ideal time to consider a replacement.
Upgrading to a modern, energy-efficient AC unit can reduce electricity usage by 40–60% compared to older systems—delivering long-term energy savings and improved comfort.
  

**Units Type** | **Units Ton Range** | **Efficiency Rating** | **Incentive Amount** | **Maximum Incentive**  
---|---|---|---|---  
PTAC Units | ≤ 2.5-Ton |  20% > Minimum EER from Title -24 standards | $100 per unit |    
  
  
limited Incentive amount, up to $75,000, per customer per year  
Package AC | 2.0- ≤ 5.3-Ton AC | ≥ 15 thru ≥17 | $75 - $400 per Ton  
Package AC | ≥ 5.4-Ton & Greater AC | ≥ 9.7 EER thru ≥ 11.2 EER 12.5 IEER thru ≥ 14.8 IEER | $0.15/ kWh annual savings or $400/ kW, whichever is greater  
  

If you are ready to replace your old unit with a new high-efficiency air conditioner unit, please download the following information and contact us about your project. All Projects are subject to funding availability.
  

**Program Steps**
  

  * Download the Program Guidelines and [Air Conditioner Incentive Table](https://www.anaheim.net/DocumentCenter/View/26610/ABEES-Air-Conditioner-Incentives-Table-FY-20-21) documents.
  * Download the [Air Conditioner Application](https://www.anaheim.net/DocumentCenter/View/26611/Air-Conditioner-Application): (Rename file using your account address and name)
  * Please download and complete all required forms (including the tax forms - W-9)
  * Questions about the program details, simply fill out our [online form](https://www.anaheim.net/FormCenter/Public-Utilities-6/Energy-Efficiency-Solutions-61). A representative will contact you within 2-3 business days.
