#  Energy & Water Incentives for Multi-family Housing Projects 
Anaheim Public Utilities provides financial incentives for new construction and rehabilitation of multi-family dwellings in order to promote sustainability, decrease tenant utility costs, and lower Greenhouse Gas (GHG) emissions. Installation of high efficiency energy and water measures improves the overall affordability of housing projects. See how you can improve the footprint of your projects with the [following incentives](http://www.anaheim.net/DocumentCenter/View/22037/affordable_housing_projects_flyer_2018_MV_FINAL).  

## Application Process Overview
  

  1. Contact us at 714-765-4250 (at prompt enter 1) to set up an initial consultation and pre-inspection (if applicable)
  2. After purchasing a qualifying energy/water efficient appliance or measure, please visit our rebate Guidelines and Application link below to review participation guidelines and complete your incentive application 
  3. The online application, along with all supporting documentation and receipts, can be scanned and submitted through **email**or mailed to the following address:


  
  
  
  
  

## Required Documentation
  

  * Energy & Water Incentives for Multi-family Housing Projects Program [GUIDELINES and APPLICATION](https://www.anaheim.net/DocumentCenter/View/26870/Multi-Family-Picklist-Program-Application_FINAL_July-2019)
  * [Internal Revenue Service Form W-9](https://www.anaheim.net/DocumentCenter/View/26871/Form-W-9-Rev-October-2018) (Request for taxpayer identification number and certification)
  * Paid sales receipt(s) or invoice(s) depicting that an identified appliance type or measure has been paid for in full


![36685](https://www.anaheim.net/ImageRepository/Document?documentID=26840)
Applications must be submitted within 1 year of purchase. A paid sales receipt or invoice, depicting that an identified appliance type or measure has been paid in full, is required. Applicants must receive electric service from Anaheim Public Utilities. The incentive offer is on a first-come, first-service basis and is effective until funding is expended or the program(s) are discontinued by Anaheim Public Utilities. Applicant understands and agrees that Anaheim Public Utilities or its representative may inspect the installed equipment/product for installation verification before the incentive is paid.
