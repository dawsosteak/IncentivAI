#  Financial Assistance, Savings, and Discounts
Learn how to reduce your utility bill. Reduce cost for your electrical, gas, telecommunication, or water utility bill. Receive assistive telecommunication equipment.
##  Internet and Phone
![Mother and daughter exploring content on a mobile phone](https://www.cpuc.ca.gov/-/media/project/placeholder-images/placeholder-image-400x300/lifeline/callifeline-1223256256.jpg?mw=645&hash=D54DBFDEB78BEFA0A1D451BD3B9D29D3)
### [ California LifeLine ](https://www.cpuc.ca.gov/consumer-support/financial-assistance-savings-and-discounts/lifeline)
Provides discounted home phone and cell phone services to qualified households. 
![School children using a computer](https://www.cpuc.ca.gov/-/media/project/placeholder-images/placeholder-image-400x300/lifeline/ctf-1126244845_rev.jpg?mw=645&hash=61B41C909A2AE17E85FE53F0CDAF45D2)
### [ California Teleconnect Fund (CTF) ](https://www.cpuc.ca.gov/consumer-support/financial-assistance-savings-and-discounts/california-teleconnect-fund)
50% discount to qualifying K–12 schools, libraries, community colleges, government-owned hospitals and health clinics, community-based organizations, and community-based healthcare organizations. 
![A person using a low-vision telephone dials](https://www.cpuc.ca.gov/-/media/project/placeholder-images/placeholder-image-400x300/lifeline/ddtp-173829786.jpg?mw=645&hash=998EA11323DA2235C07D415F33AF602C)
### [ Deaf and Disabled Telecommunications Program (DDTP) ](https://caconnect.org/)
Provides assistive telecommunications equipment, speech-generating devices, and relay services to consumers with hearing, vision, speech, cognitive, and mobility disabilities. 
##  Free Appliances and Appliance Rebates
Most energy utilities offer free energy-efficient appliances to consumers with limited household income. Most energy utilities offer rebates to consumers who purchase energy-efficient appliances. 
**PG &E** area [Home Energy Guide](https://guide.pge.com/) (includes [Product Cost-Comparison](https://guide.pge.com/browse/thermostats), Rebates, and Programs)**SCE** area [Home Energy Guide](https://www.sce.com/residential/home-energy-guide), [Product Cost-Comparison](https://marketplace.sce.com/), [Rebates](https://www.sce.com/residential/rebates-savings/rebates), and Programs)**SDG &E** area [Rebates](https://www.sdge.com/rebates)
  

##  Electrical Energy and Energy Efficiency
![Mother and daughter researching something on a computer](https://www.cpuc.ca.gov/-/media/project/placeholder-images/placeholder-image-400x300/lifeline/care-690415740.jpg?mw=645&hash=90F5446CF2CDEF07BEF1A1393B981BFB)
###  California Alternate Rates for Energy (CARE)
Provides a 30-35% discount on your electric bill and a 20% discount on your natural gas bill to qualified households. 
![A family with small children enjoying time together ](https://www.cpuc.ca.gov/-/media/project/placeholder-images/placeholder-image-400x300/lifeline/fera-693399990_rev.jpg?mw=645&hash=3FF5E2E8B1A31B910FECFB867F8D80D0)
###  Family Electric Rate Assistance (FERA)
Provides an 18% discount on your electricity bill if your household income slightly exceeds the CARE qualifications. 
![A constructor laying down the thermal isolation on the floor](https://www.cpuc.ca.gov/-/media/project/placeholder-images/placeholder-image-400x300/lifeline/energysavings-1182458609.jpg?mw=645&hash=FA27AEC2B8776333B4DA00F3F2AF9662)
### [ Energy Savings Assistance Program ](https://www.cpuc.ca.gov/consumer-support/financial-assistance-savings-and-discounts/energy-savings-assistance)
Provides no-cost weatherization services to consumers who meet the CARE income limits. 
![A woman managing her smart phone on a tablet](https://www.cpuc.ca.gov/-/media/cpuc-website/images/careers/adobestock_362669754_preview/adobestock_253248222_preview/adobestock_224725144.jpeg?mw=645&hash=1EABCCAEEF762FFFC79ACF2C1BD44DD0)
### [ Energy Upgrade California ](https://www.cpuc.ca.gov/consumer-support/financial-assistance-savings-and-discounts/energy-upgrade-ca)
A state initiative to help you take action to save energy and conserve natural resources, help reduce demand on the electricity grid, and make informed energy management choices at home and work. 
![A person fixing window isolation](https://www.cpuc.ca.gov/-/media/project/placeholder-images/placeholder-image-400x300/lifeline/liheap-184844728.jpg?mw=645&hash=9441EDD69F85410302010E60E94D23FC)
### [ Low Income Home Energy Assistance Program (LIHEAP) ](https://www.cpuc.ca.gov/consumer-support/financial-assistance-savings-and-discounts/liheap)
Provides weatherization services and cash to help low-income customers pay their bills. 
![Two partners looking at the bills. ](https://www.cpuc.ca.gov/-/media/project/placeholder-images/placeholder-image-400x300/lifeline/emergency-assistance-1028900500.jpg?mw=645&hash=418EC82C415CCE3DA6919BAD24542483)
### [ Utility Company Emergency Assistance ](https://www.cpuc.ca.gov/consumer-support/financial-assistance-savings-and-discounts/utility-company-emergency-assistance)
Some gas and electric utilities offer cash assistance and special payment plans to consumers facing financial hardship. 
##  Natural Gas
![A person with a medical device](https://www.cpuc.ca.gov/-/media/project/placeholder-images/placeholder-image-400x300/lifeline/medical-1287185159.jpg?mw=645&hash=3BD07BB254AA14C526407F2A74206167)
### [ Medical Baseline ](https://www.cpuc.ca.gov/consumer-support/financial-assistance-savings-and-discounts/medical-baseline)
Consumers are billed for natural gas and electricity use at their utility company's lowest residential rate. 
![A young woman paying bills](https://www.cpuc.ca.gov/-/media/cpuc-website/images/consumer-support/financial-assistance/paymentplan/adobestock_323451812.jpeg?mw=645&hash=EBFF85743820D0D8F79B705FB7EA223B)
### [ Utility Company Emergency Assistance ](https://www.cpuc.ca.gov/consumer-support/financial-assistance-savings-and-discounts/utility-company-emergency-assistance)
Some gas and electric utilities offer cash assistance and special payment plans to consumers facing financial hardship. 
##  Water
![A child washing hands](https://www.cpuc.ca.gov/-/media/project/placeholder-images/placeholder-image-400x300/lifeline/water-1202688044.jpg?mw=645&hash=8899453C8A2E44C1D1046D613D744B45)
### [ Water Company Assistance ](https://www.cpuc.ca.gov/consumer-support/financial-assistance-savings-and-discounts/water-company-assistance)
Depending on your household income, you may qualify for discounted water services.
