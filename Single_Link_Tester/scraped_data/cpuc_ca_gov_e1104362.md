#  Regulatory Services
We regulate "investor-owned" or privately owned electric, natural gas, telecommunications, railroad, rail crossings, rail transit, passenger carriers — limousines, charter buses, ferries, transportation network companies, such as Uber or Lyft — and water utilities.
## [ Audits ](https://www.cpuc.ca.gov/about-cpuc/divisions/utility-audits-risk-and-compliance-division/utility-audits-branch)
Auditing, accounting, financial, and advisory services on regulated utilities and law and Commission directive compliance monitoring. 
## [ Enforcement and Citations ](https://www.cpuc.ca.gov/regulatory-services/enforcement-and-citations)
Ensuring that the CPUC’s statutory mandates are carried out and that ratepayers and the public are protected from safety, reliability, service quality, and other violations. 
## [ Licensing ](https://www.cpuc.ca.gov/regulatory-services/licensing)
Licensing and registration of transportation, communications, and energy service providers. 
## [ Rail ](https://www.cpuc.ca.gov/about-cpuc/divisions/rail-safety-division)
We regulate rail safety including freight, inter-city and commuter railroads, rail transit and rail crossings. 
## [ Safety ](https://www.cpuc.ca.gov/regulatory-services/safety)
Oversight, monitoring, and audits to ensure the safety and reliability of regulated consumer services.
