This Email Ruling grants party status to EnergySavvy as described further below.
* * *
On May 13, 2015, EnergySavvy filed a motion for party status (Motion) in Rulemaking (R.) 14-10-003. In its Motion, EnergySavvy states that it serves utility and government administered demand-side management energy efficiency programs to more than twenty-five utility or energy efficiency programs across the country. EnergySavvy seeks to provide the Commission with information about new technology, savings measurement software, and enhancing the evaluation, measurement and verification for demand-side management energy efficiency programs. Additionally, EnergySavvy states that it can provide comments and expertise to the Commission on automating,

- 4 assisting and enhancing evaluation, measurement and verification operations for demand-side management programs.

Given EnergySavvy’s expertise in evaluation, measurement, and verification, its participation in R.14-10-003 should be constructive relative to the scope of R.14-10-003. As such, it is reasonable to grant EnergySavvy party status in R.14-10-003.
