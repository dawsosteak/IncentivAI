#  Power Outage Maps
The State’s investor-owned electric utilities, Pacific Gas and Electric Company (PG&E), Southern California Edison (SCE), San Diego Gas & Electric (SDG&E), Bear Valley Electric Service, Liberty Utilities and PacifiCorp, may shut off electric power, referred to as “de-energization” or Public Safety Power Shut-offs, to protect public safety. 
## [ PG&E ](https://pgealerts.alerts.pge.com/outages/map/)
Learn more about the PG&E Public Safety Power Shut Off. View a current outage map as well as more information. 
## [ San Diego Gas & Electric ](https://www.sdge.com/residential/customer-service/outage-center/outage-map)
Learn more about the San Diego Gas & Electric Public Safety Power Shutoff and view a current outage map. 
## [ Southern California Edison ](https://www.sce.com/outage-center/check-outage-status)
Learn more about the Southern California Edison Public Safety Power Shutoff and view a current outage map. 
## [ Bear Valley Electric Service ](https://www.bvesinc.com/about/news)
Learn more about the Bear Valley Electric Service Public Safety Power Shut Off and view current outage information. 
## [ Liberty Utilities ](https://california.libertyutilities.com/truckee/residential/emergencies/electrical/outages-map.html)
Learn more about the Liberty Utilities Public Safety Power Shut Off. View a current outage map as well for more information. 
## [ PacifiCorp ](https://www.pacificpower.net/about/newsroom/news-releases/outage-update.html)
Learn more about the PacifiCorp Public Safety Power Shut Off and view current outage information. 
##  More information
### [ Cooling Centers ](https://www.cpuc.ca.gov/consumer-support/cooling-centers)
A Cooling Center provides a place for you and your family to go to cool down during hot summer days. 
### [ De-Energization ](https://www.cpuc.ca.gov/consumer-support/psps)
Electric power shut-offs, referred to as “de-energization” or Public Safety Power Shut-offs, aim to protect public safety under California law. 
### [ Preparing for a Power Outage ](https://www.cpuc.ca.gov/consumer-support/preparing-for-a-power-outage)
We created a checklist to prepare you and your family if there is a power outage. 
### [ Outage Reporting for Power Plants ](https://www.cpuc.ca.gov/consumer-support/power-outage-maps/web-based-power-plant-outage)
Power plants can report outages directly to us. Customers should report outages on their utility websites. 
### [ Electric Reliability and Safety ](https://www.cpuc.ca.gov/industries-and-topics/electrical-energy/infrastructure/electric-reliability)
The CPUC enforces rules and regulations to ensure that power plant operators and utility companies run a safe and reliable electric or communication system.
