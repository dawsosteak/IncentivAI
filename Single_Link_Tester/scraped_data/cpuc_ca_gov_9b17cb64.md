- 3 This Email Ruling denies, without prejudice, the motions for party status filed by Enphase Energy Inc. (Enphase) and Nest Labs Inc. (Nest) in Rulemaking (R.) 14-10-003.
* * *
On May 8, 2015, Enphase filed a motion for party status in R. 14-10-003. In its motion, Enphase states that it is a leading microinverter systems provider for residential and commercial solar systems. Enphase claims that it brings a systems-based approach to distributed energy, providing a platform for intelligent energy management. Enphase contends that it can meaningfully contribute to this proceeding by offering a unique and valuable perspective.
* * *
On May 28, 2015, Nest filed a motion for party status in R. 14-10-003. In its motion, Nest states that it provides customers with hardware, software and services that help reduce energy consumption and keep families comfortable and safe. Nest asserts that it believes that most customers do not make a distinction between energy efficiency and demand response and therefore supports the concept of Integrated Demand Side Management.
* * *
The motions for party status filed by Enphase and Nest Lab are hereby denied, without prejudice. Commission Rules of Practice and Procedure, Rule 1.4(b) states that a person seeking party status by motion shall fully disclose the persons or entities in whose behalf the filing is made, the interest of such entities, and the factual and legal contentions that the entity intends to make. Neither Enphase nor Nest addressed any factual or legal contentions they intend to make in this proceeding.
* * *
The Docket Office shall formally file this Email Ruling.
