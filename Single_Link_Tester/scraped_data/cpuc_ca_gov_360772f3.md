ORDER CORRECTING ERROR IN DECISION 21-02-006
* * *
Pursuant to Commission Rules of Practice and Procedure, Rule 16.5, this order corrects several obvious errors in Decision (D.) 21-02-006, Decision Adopting Pilots to Test Two Frameworks for Procuring Distributed Energy Resources That Avoid or Defer Utility Capital Investments. As described below, Ordering Paragraphs 5, 6, 9, and 11 contain inadvertent errors and should be corrected. Ordering Paragraph 5 states that Energy Division is authorized to invite party proposals on criteria for the Partnership Pilot, adopted in Ordering Paragraph 2, and the Standard Offer Contract Pilot, adopted in Ordering Paragraph 9. However, the Standard Offer Contract Pilot was adopted in Ordering Paragraph 12. This is an inadvertent error and should be corrected. Ordering Paragraph 6 directs Pacific Gas and Electric Company, San Diego Gas & Electric Company and Southern California Edison Company (Utilities) to submit an advice letter, no later than 30 days from the date of the workshop in

- 2 Ordering Paragraph 4. However, the referenced workshop is ordered in Ordering Paragraph 5. This is an inadvertent error and should be corrected. Ordering Paragraph 9 directs Utilities to track the costs of implementing the Partnership Pilot webpage in Ordering Paragraph 7. However, implementation of the Partnership Pilot webpage is ordered in Ordering Paragraph 8. This is an inadvertent error and should be corrected. Ordering Paragraph 11 directs the launch of Requests for Offers. This ordering paragraph contains an incongruent phrase, “Pilot in the Requests for Offer Process”, which is inadvertent and should be deleted. Pursuant to Commission Resolution A-4661 and Rule 16.5 of the Commission’s Rules of Practice and Procedure, the Commission’s Executive Director is authorized to sign, on behalf of the Commission, orders involving the correction of clerical and other obvious inadvertent errors and omissions in Commission decisions. Accordingly, the corrections to D.21-02-006 should be adopted pursuant to Commission Resolution A-4661 and Rule 16.5 of the Commission’s Rules of Practice and Procedure.
