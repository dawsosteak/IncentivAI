* * *
workshop will not be webcast. Space is limited and organizations in this proceeding are asked to send only the most appropriate representative. More information will provided at a later date.
* * *
The Learning Session agenda is currently being developed and is anticipated to include a discussion on customer needs and out-of-state integrated demand side management programs.
* * *
Regarding the discussion on out-of-state programs, several presentations are currently being considered including a presentation on non-California Zero Net Energy programs and an integrated residential energy efficiency/demand response program in the state of Nevada. I would like to include additional out of-state IDSM presentations as well, and invite parties to email recommendations for presentations and panelists (Please note that we do not have travel funds to support in-person presentations). Recommendations should include an overview of the out-of-state IDSM presentation topic, why your organization believes it is needed in this proceeding, and include suggested panelists with contact information.
* * *
There will be also be a lengthy discussion on customer needs. The four utilities in this proceeding (Pacific Gas and Electric Company, San Diego Gas & Electric Company, Southern California Edison Company, and Southern California Gas Company) are directed to work together to summarize and present a recent residential customer segmentation profile overview. The four utilities are also directed to work together to develop a presentation on commercial sub-markets or “verticals” and their different customer profiles and needs. In addition, parties are invited to email ideas for presentations and panelists regarding
