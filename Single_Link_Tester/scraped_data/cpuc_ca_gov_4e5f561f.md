Pursuant to the discussion during the December 5, 2014 prehearing conference, a workshop to discuss integrated demand side management (IDSM) activities will be held on Thursday, January 22, 2014 in Rulemaking (R.)14-10-003. The workshop will be facilitated by the assigned Administrative Law Judge (Judge) and will begin at 10:00 am. As requested by parties, the workshop will be held in the Commission Auditorium and will be webcast. Additional details will be provided in early January 2015.
* * *
Currently, the agenda for the workshop is in the development stage. At this time I envision one or more presentations or panels to discuss 1) an overview of the Commission’s IDSM Omnibus, referenced in the Order Instituting Rulemaking,

At this time, I direct Pacific Gas and Electric Company, San Diego Gas & Electric Company, Southern California Edison Company (SCE), and Southern California Gas Company (jointly, the Utilities) to begin to prepare overviews of past and current IDSM activities they have undertaken. While further direction for the presentations will be provided in early January 2015, the Utilities should be prepared to include pilots such as the SCE Preferred Resources Pilot in the presentation.
* * *
Furthermore, I invite parties of R.14-10-003 to suggest other panelists who have engaged in such activities in California. Suggestions should be emailed no later than January 5, 2015 to the assigned Judge with carbon copies of the email to the entire service list.
