When you’re ready to improve your energy efficiency, lower your utility bills and help protect the environment, trust Beaches Energy Services! We are proud to offer energy rebates that make it easy to invest in your home and save a little money.
Beaches Energy offers a total of up to $1250 in energy rebates, like solar rebates and HVAC rebates, for residential upgrades that improve your energy efficiency, and up to $5000 in commercial lighting upgrades.
How Do I Get Energy Rebates
- Carefully read and complete the rebate forms below.
- Gather all required documentation, including proof of purchase invoices. Incomplete paperwork is a common reason for rebate denials. Please ensure all forms are fully filled out with accurate information to avoid delays.
- Submit rebate forms and proof of purchase invoices to Beaches Energy in person, by mail or scan and email to customercare@beachesenergy.com.
Note: Forms and documentation must be submitted within 90 days of installation. Incomplete or missing paperwork will result in delays or denials of your rebate, so please double-check your submission.
