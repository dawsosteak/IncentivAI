Effective November 28, 2025, the California Energy Commission (CEC) is no longer accepting new applications for the Energy Conservation Assistance Account (ECAA) loan program under Program Opportunity Notices (PONs) 22-001 and 22-002. The CEC is currently updating the PONs and plans to reopen them in the future. The CEC will continue awarding loans that are already on the ECAA waitlist.
If you would like to receive announcements related to the program, subscribe to the ECAA Program on this page or via the CEC Subscriptions for the Energy Conservation Assistance Act topic. You can also send an inquiry by sending an email to Energy Conservation Assistance Act.
