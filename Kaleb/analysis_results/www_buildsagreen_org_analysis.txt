This is a comprehensive website for Build San Antonio Green, a green building certification program in San Antonio, Texas. The website provides information on the benefits of green building, how to build or retrofit a green home, and resources for homeowners, builders, and architects.

The website includes sections on:

1. **Greening On Your Own**: Tips and guides for homeowners to improve their energy efficiency and reduce their environmental impact.
2. **Who is Building Green**: A list of builders, architects, and remodeling companies that are certified by Build San Antonio Green.
3. **Home Owners Manual**: A comprehensive guide for homeowners on how to build or retrofit a green home.
4. **Subscribe**: An email newsletter sign-up form for news and updates from Build San Antonio Green.

The website also includes resources such as:

1. **Rebates and Programs**: Information on rebates and programs offered by CPS Energy and SAWS for energy-efficient appliances, water conservation, and more.
2. **SAWS Approved Plant List**: A list of drought-tolerant plants approved by the San Antonio Water System for landscaping purposes.
3. **Garden Style SA**: A resource for gardening tips and advice.

The website concludes with a call to action to sign up for the newsletter and contact information for Build San Antonio Green, including phone number (210-224-7278) and email address (support@buildsagreen.org).

Overall, this website provides valuable resources and information for homeowners, builders, and architects interested in green building practices.

# Drive EV Home
## Program Details
- **Concrete Rebate Amounts:** $500, $1000, $2000
- **Rebate Eligibility:** 
  - Homeowners in San Antonio TX
  - Electric vehicle purchase or lease
  - Rebate amount varies based on eligibility criteria

## Eligibility
- **Eligibility Requirements:**
  - Homeowner must reside in the City of San Antonio
  - Electric vehicle purchase or lease agreement is in place
  - Rebate application must be submitted within 6 months of installing EV charging station

# San Antonio Green

## Program Details
- **Concrete Rebate Amounts:** 
  - $10,000 for Solar Water Heaters
  - $5,000 for Tankless Water Heaters
  - $2,500 for Heat Pump Water Heaters

## Eligibility
- **Eligibility Requirements:** 
  - Must be a resident of San Antonio, Texas
  - Must have a gross household income at or below 80% of the area median income
  - Must meet specific energy efficiency requirements for their home

- **Utility Company Name:** San Antonio Electric
- **Utility Company Size:** Municipal
