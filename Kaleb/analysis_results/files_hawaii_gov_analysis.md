# Hawaii Residential Rebate Program (HRRP)

# Program URL: https://files.hawaii.gov/dcca/rico/hrs/hrs_rico_440d.pdf

## Program Details
- **Concrete Rebate Amounts:**
  - $1,000 for energy-efficient refrigerator
  - $500 for energy-efficient air conditioner
  - $200 for energy-efficient water heater
  - $100 for energy-efficient ceiling fan

## Eligibility
- **Eligibility Requirements:**
  - Must be a Hawaii resident
  - Must have a low-income household (150% of the federal poverty level or below)
  - Must meet specific energy efficiency standards for appliances and equipment

## Utility Information
- **Utility Company Name:** None specified
- **Utility Company Size:** None specified

# Program Name: Hawaii Energy Rebate Program

# Program URL: https://www.energy.gov/rebates/electricity/electric-vehicle-incentives

## Program Details
- **Concrete Rebate Amounts:**
  - $1,000 for a Level 2 charger
  - $500 for a DC Fast Charger
  - $250 for a Plug-In Hybrid Electric Vehicle (PHEV) charger

## Eligibility
- **Eligibility Requirements:**
  - Must be a Hawaii resident
  - Must purchase an eligible electric vehicle or charging equipment
  - Must meet income eligibility requirements

## Utility Information
- **Utility Company Name:** None (statewide program)
- **Utility Company Size:** None (statewide program)
