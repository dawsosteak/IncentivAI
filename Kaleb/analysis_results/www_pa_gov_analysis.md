To request a patient's prescription record, you can submit a request form to the PA PDMP Office. The form requires the following information:

*   The patient's name
*   Date of birth (DOB)
*   Type of identification provided (e.g., driver's license, state ID)
*   Current address and previous addresses for which the patient is requesting prescription records

**What Information Will Be Provided**

The PA PDMP Office will provide the following information:

*   A list of all prescriptions filled or dispensed to the patient within the last seven years
*   The type of controlled substance prescribed
*   The dosage and quantity of the medication
*   The date of dispensing

**Important Notes**

*   The PA PDMP Office cannot provide prescription records for deceased patients.
