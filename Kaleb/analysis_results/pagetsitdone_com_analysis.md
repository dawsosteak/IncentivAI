This is a webpage for the Pennsylvania Department of Community & Economic Development, specifically focused on the state's manufacturing industry. The page provides an overview of the industry, its economic impact, and various resources available to manufacturers.

Here are some key points from the webpage:

1. **Manufacturing Industry Overview**: The webpage highlights that Pennsylvania is home to the 6th largest manufacturing industry in the US by employment, with over 558,800 employees.
2. **Economic Impact**: The state's manufacturing industry generates $111 billion in gross domestic product (GDP) annually.
3. **Funding & Resources**: The page lists various programs and resources available to manufacturers, including:
	* Manufacturing PA Training-to-Career Grant Program
	* Foundations in Industry Training (FIT) Grants
	* Manufacturing PA Innovation Program
	* WEDnetPA
	* Ben Franklin Technology Partners (BFTP)
4. **Regional Clean Hydrogen Hubs Tax Credit Program**: This program provides tax credits to expanding businesses in critical manufacturing sectors that purchase clean hydrogen and natural gas from a Regional Clean Hydrogen Hub.
5. **Industrial Resource Centers (IRC)**: The webpage mentions the Industrial Resource Centers, a network of seven private nonprofits located throughout the state, which provide localized support to manufacturers.

Overall, this webpage aims to promote Pennsylvania's manufacturing industry, highlight its economic benefits, and provide resources for businesses looking to grow or expand in the state.

This is a webpage for the Pennsylvania Department of Community & Economic Development's (DCED) BusinessPA program, which provides export assistance to Pennsylvania businesses. The webpage includes information on:

1. **Global Opportunities**: Pennsylvania products and services travel to 213 markets worldwide, representing 5.5% of the state's GDP.
2. **BusinessPA Team**: The international trade team offers confidential and customized programs and services to Pennsylvania businesses of all sizes and at all stages of growth.
3. **Services**: The BusinessPA team provides guidance on export processes and market entry strategy development, coordinates business trips with in-country teams, qualifies buyers and partners, supports international trade missions and trade shows, and conducts research on foreign companies.
4. **Network**: With headquarters in Harrisburg, PA, local support through 10 Regional Export Network (REN) partners statewide, and 13 PA Authorized Trade Representatives (ATRs) around the world.

The webpage also includes a list of countries covered by BusinessPA's network:

* Asia/Australia: Australia, China, India, Indonesia, Korea, Malaysia, New Zealand, Philippines, Singapore, Taiwan, Thailand, Vietnam
* Americas: Canada, Mexico
* Europe: Austria, Belgium, Bulgaria, Croatia, Czech Republic, Estonia, Finland, Denmark, Germany, Greece, Hungary, Ireland, Latvia, Lithuania, Netherlands, Norway, Poland, Romania, Serbia, Slovakia, Slovenia, Sweden, Switzerland, United Kingdom
* Middle East: Jordan, Israel, Turkey
* Africa: Egypt

The webpage also includes contact information for the BusinessPA team and a link to download an export assistance fact sheet.

Overall, this webpage provides valuable resources and support for Pennsylvania businesses looking to expand their exports and reach new markets.
