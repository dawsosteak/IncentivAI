# Program Name: Payment Plan
# Program URL: https://www.municipalonlinepayments.com/dallastx

## Program Details
- **Offering Entity:** Dallas Municipal Court
- **Status / Dates:** Available 24/7, including weekends and holidays. Payments do not automatically draft.
- **Service Territory:** Dallas, TX
- **Eligible Measures / Equipment:** None specified
- **Concrete Rebate Amounts:**
  - $15 fee assessed on each criminal citation(s) if full payment amount is not received within the first 30 days of payment contract agreement or judgment.
- **Caps / Limits:**
  - No cap specified

## Eligibility
- **Who Can Apply:** Anyone with a citation
- **Eligibility Requirements:** Must be at least 18 years old and provide acceptable photo identification
- **Exclusions:** None specified

## Application
- **How to Apply:** Online, by phone, by mail, or in-person
- **Required Documentation:** Acceptable form of identification (Social Security cards and selfies are not valid)
- **Where to Apply (link if present):** https://www.municipalonlinepayments.com/dallastx
