

--- SOURCE: https://bpu.org ---

This is a comprehensive website for Brainerd Public Utilities (BPU) in Minnesota. It provides information on various services, including electricity and water, as well as educational resources and community news.

The website has several sections:

1. **I WANT TO**: This section allows users to access their account information, view payment options, get rebates, and start or stop service.
2. **Schedule of Fees**: This section lists the fees for various services, including electricity, water, meter installation, and pole attachments.
3. **Turn Off/On Water**: This section provides information on turning off or on water services, including a list of fees associated with it.
4. **Tampering With Meter**: This section outlines the consequences of tampering with a water meter, including a fee for repairs.
5. **Water Meters**: This section lists the cost of different types of water meters.
6. **Bulk Water Sales**: This section provides information on bulk water sales, including charges for residential and commercial customers.
7. **Pole Attachments**: This section outlines the costs associated with pole attachments.
8. **Security Lights**: This section provides information on security lights, including a monthly rate per light.
9. **Oversize Loads**: This section explains how oversized loads are handled, including estimates for material costs.
10. **BPU Equipment and Employees**: This section lists the rates for hourly employee wages and materials.

The website also has links to educational resources, such as:

1. **Energy Education**: Provides information on energy-saving tips and resources.
2. **Water Education**: Offers information on water conservation and usage.
3. **Utilities Education**: Provides information on utility services and operations.

Additionally, the website includes:

1. **News & Updates**: A section for news and updates from BPU.
2. **Contact Details**: Contact information for BPU, including business office hours and email address.
3. **Privacy Policy**: A link to the website's privacy policy.

Overall, this website provides a comprehensive resource for customers of Brainerd Public Utilities, offering information on various services and educational resources to help them better understand their utility usage.
