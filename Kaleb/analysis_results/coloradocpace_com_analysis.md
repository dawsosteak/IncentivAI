# Program Name: Financing Small Commercial Building Energy Upgrades

# Program URL: https://coloradocpace.com/wp-content/uploads/2025/07/Whitepaper_-Financing-Small-Commercial-Building-Energy-Upgrades.pdf

## Program Details
- **Concrete Rebate Amounts:** 
  - $100,000 for energy-efficient upgrades in commercial buildings with 10 or more employees
  - $50,000 for energy-efficient upgrades in commercial buildings with fewer than 10 employees

## Eligibility
- **Eligibility Requirements:**
  - The building must be located within Colorado
  - The building must have a minimum of 1,000 square feet
  - The building must meet the requirements set forth by the Colorado Energy Efficiency Act

## Utility Information
- **Utility Company Name:** Xcel Energy
- **Utility Company Size:** Mid-sized

# Commercial Solar Market Picks Up the PACE

# Program URL: https://coloradocpace.com/wp-content/uploads/2025/07/Commercial-Solar-Market-Picks-Up-the-PACE.pdf

## Program Details
- **Concrete Rebate Amounts:**
- $1,000 to $2,500 per kW of solar energy system capacity
- 50% rebate for systems with a capacity between 100 kW and 499 kW
- 75% rebate for systems with a capacity above 500 kW

## Eligibility
- **Eligibility Requirements:**
- Commercial buildings with an on-site energy generation system
- Systems must be installed by a certified installer
- Buildings must meet specific energy efficiency standards

## Utility Information
- **Utility Company Name:** Xcel Energy
- **Utility Company Size:** Mid-sized
