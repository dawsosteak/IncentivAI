# Program Name: PGE Energy Efficiency Rebate Program

# Program URL: https://www.pge.com/en/about-us/community-renewable-energy/rebates-and-incentives

## Program Details
- **Concrete Rebate Amounts:**
  - $200 for energy-efficient lighting
  - $100 for energy-efficient ceiling fans
  - $50 for energy-efficient window treatments
  - $500 for energy-efficient home upgrades (e.g., insulation, doors)

## Eligibility
- **Eligibility Requirements:**
  - Must be a PGE customer
  - Must have a metered account
  - Must meet specific energy usage requirements

## Utility Information
- **Utility Company Name:** Pacific Gas and Electric Company (PGE)
- **Utility Company Size:** Large
