# Utility Rebate Program Analysis

## Program Details
- **Program Name:** Eagle County Rebates
- **Concrete Rebate Amounts:**
  - $2,500 (Residential Energy Efficiency Projects)
  - $7,500 (Residential Fuel Switching Projects)
  - $5,000 (Commercial and Multifamily Energy Efficiency Projects)
  - $5,000 (Commercial and Multifamily Fuel Switching Projects)

## Eligibility
- **Eligibility Requirements:**
  - Pre-approval is required for major renovations or new construction projects.
  - Projects must meet the ENERGY STAR program requirements.

## Project Types
### Residential Energy Efficiency Projects
- Replacing electric baseboard heating with a heat pump

### Residential Fuel Switching Projects
- Replacing natural gas to electric resistance water heater
- Replacing fossil fuel system with:
  * A heat pump for space heating, water heating, or snowmelt
  * A heat-recovery or heat pump ventilation system

### Commercial and Multifamily Energy Efficiency Projects
- EV Charging (Aspen Electric customers only)
- Insulation and Air Sealing
- Appliances and Cooking
- Design Assistance and Commissioning
- Control Systems
- Data Management Equipment and Cooling

### Commercial and Multifamily Fuel Switching Projects
- Replacing a fossil fuel system with:
  * A heat pump for space heating, water heating, or snowmelt
  * A heat-recovery or heat pump ventilation system

# Energy Savings for Eagle County Residents 

## We’re here to help Eagle County residents lower energy use at every stage.  

### Eligible Energy Efficiency Projects  
#### Space Heating 
  * Replacing electric baseboard heating with a heat pump 
  Rebate limited to $500.

#### Control Systems 
  * Programmable thermostat
  * Outdoor temperature reset
  * Variable speed motor/pump
  * Building management system
  * Recommissioning

#### Insulation and Air Sealing 
  * Roofs
  * Attics
  * Walls
  * Floors
  * Crawlspaces
  * Pipes
  * Ducts

#### Design Assistance and Commissioning 
  * Major renovations or new construction
  * Energy modeling
  * Engineering design peer review 

Pre-approval is required.
#### Induction Range 
Must be switching from gas range.  
Rebate limited to $500.

#### Electrical Upgrade 
  * Smart Panels
  * Upgrades outside of an electrification project

#### Water Heating 
  * Switching from electric resistance to hybrid heat pump water heater
  * Switching from natural gas to electric resistance

#### EV Charging 
Aspen Electric customers only.  

#### Eligible Fuel Switching Projects  
### Space Heating, Water Heating, Snowmelt, and Ventilation  
Replacing a fossil fuel system with:
  * A heat pump for space heating, water heating, or snowmelt
  * A heat-recovery or heat pump ventilation system


Design costs and electrical capacity upgrade costs associated with a completed heat pump project will be included in the project cost used to determine the rebate.
#### New Construction 
  * All-electric individual homes without natural gas lines, which use heat pump technology for space and water heating and heat recovery technology for fresh air ventilation

If the building code prohibits natural gas, then no rebates will be provided.

#### Custom Rebates  
Custom rebates are considered for projects that do not fall within the other project types but produce significant energy savings.
