# Utility Rebate Program Analysis

## Program Details
- **Program Name:** Eagle County Rebates
- **Concrete Rebate Amounts:**
  - $2,500 (Residential Energy Efficiency Projects)
  - $7,500 (Residential Fuel Switching Projects)
  - $5,000 (Commercial and Multifamily Energy Efficiency Projects)
  - $5,000 (Commercial and Multifamily Fuel Switching Projects)

## Eligibility
- **Eligibility Requirements:**
  - Pre-approval is required for major renovations or new construction projects.
  - Projects must meet the ENERGY STAR program requirements.

## Project Types
### Residential Energy Efficiency Projects
- Replacing electric baseboard heating with a heat pump

### Residential Fuel Switching Projects
- Replacing natural gas to electric resistance water heater
- Replacing fossil fuel system with:
  * A heat pump for space heating, water heating, or snowmelt
  * A heat-recovery or heat pump ventilation system

### Commercial and Multifamily Energy Efficiency Projects
- EV Charging (Aspen Electric customers only)
- Insulation and Air Sealing
- Appliances and Cooking
- Design Assistance and Commissioning
- Control Systems
- Data Management Equipment and Cooling

### Commercial and Multifamily Fuel Switching Projects
- Replacing a fossil fuel system with:
  * A heat pump for space heating, water heating, or snowmelt
  * A heat-recovery or heat pump ventilation system

# Utility Rebate Program Analysis

## Program Details
- **Program Name:** Aspen Energy Assessments
- **Concrete Rebate Amounts:** 
  - Free for Community Priority Participants
  - $100 (a $600 value) for residential properties that don't qualify as a Community Priority Participant
  - Additional costs based on building size and utility bills

## Eligibility
- **Eligibility Requirements:** 
  - Residential buildings must complete the free or discounted energy assessment form
  - Commercial and multifamily buildings can schedule a walk-through assessment with a CORE Energy Concierge
  - Utility bills, basic information about the building, and access to space are required for completion of the assessment

## Utility Information
- **Utility Company Name:** AspenCore (formerly Aspen Energy)
- **Utility Company Size:** Nonprofit organization (501(c)(3))
