# Program Name: Hawaii Renewable Energy Rebate Program

# Program URL: https://cca.hawaii.gov/wp-content/uploads/2026/02/HAR-16-94-2022-02-07-1.pdf

## Program Details
- **Concrete Rebate Amounts:**
  - $500 for residential solar water heaters
  - $750 for commercial solar water heaters
  - $1,000 for industrial solar water heaters
- **Other Incentives:**
  - Net metering credits
  - Property tax exemptions

## Eligibility
- **Eligibility Requirements:**
  - Must be a Hawaii resident or business owner
  - Must have a minimum of 10% renewable energy system capacity
  - Must meet specific building codes and zoning requirements

## Utility Information
- **Utility Company Name:** Hawaii Electric Companies, Inc.
- **Utility Company Size:** Large
