# Manufacturing Equipment Grant Program
## Program Details
- **Concrete Rebate Amounts:** 
  - Up to $25,000

## Eligibility
- **Eligibility Requirements:** 
  - Established as for-profit entities primarily engaged in manufacturing;
  - Gross receipts of less than $5 million in 2023;
  - Having a physical location (or headquarters) in Rhode Island that began manufacturing operations on or before January 1, 2021;
  - Having 50 or fewer employees (including independent contractors that work an average of 37.5 hours per week);
  - In compliance with all local, state, and federal laws and regulations;
  - NOT in, or planning to enter, federal bankruptcy or state receivership;
  - NOT owned by a nationally or regionally owned franchise; and
  - Organized as one of the following: Limited Partnership (LP), Limited Liability Partnership (LLP), C-Corporation, S-Corporation, B-Corporation, Cooperative, or Limited Liability Company (LLC).

## Utility Information
- **Utility Company Name:** Commerce RI
- **Utility Company Size:** Not specified

# Manufacturing Equipment Grant Program

## Program Details
- **Concrete Rebate Amounts:** 
  - $25,000 (up to 50% of downpayment for financed purchase or up to 50% of cash purchase)
- **Grant Use:** Reimbursement for equipment/machinery purchases

## Eligibility
- **Eligibility Requirements:** 
  - Manufacturing business established as for-profit entity primarily engaged in manufacturing
  - Gross receipts less than $5 million in 2023
  - Physical location (or headquarters) in Rhode Island with manufacturing operations on or before January 1, 2021
  - Having 50 or fewer employees
  - In compliance with local, state, and federal laws and regulations
  - Not in or planning to enter federal bankruptcy or state receivership
  - Not owned by a nationally or regionally owned franchise
  - Organized as one of the following: Limited Partnership (LP), Limited Liability Partnership (LLP), C-Corporation, S-Corporation, B-Corporation, Cooperative, or Limited Liability Company (LLC)

## Utility Information
- **Utility Company Name:** Rhode Island Commerce Corporation
- **Utility Company Size:** Small to Medium
