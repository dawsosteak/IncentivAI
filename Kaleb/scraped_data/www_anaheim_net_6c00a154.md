#  Refrigerator Recycling Rebate Program 
## Recycle Your Refrigerator/Freezer & Receive $50
Anaheim Public Utilities customers can receive $50 for recycling their old spare refrigerator or freezer.
## How the Program Works
  1. Recycle your refrigerator or freezer (you will need to arrange an independent pickup service).
  2. Complete the [program application](https://anaheim.net/DocumentCenter/View/53447/Refrigerator-Recycling-Rebate-Program-Application-English-and-Spanish-0224) or [apply online](https://anaheim.net/FormCenter/Public-Utilities-6/Refrigerator-Recycling-Rebate-Program-334) to confirm program participation eligibility.
  3. You will receive your incentive check within six (6) weeks of application receipt and approval.


## Requirements
  * Must be Anaheim Public Utilities residential electric customers.
  * Only one operating unit may be recycled for incentive every five (5) years.
  * Recycled unit must be at minimum 10 cubic feet and a maximum 32 cubic feet. Mini refrigerators, such as wine coolers or dorm coolers, and commercial-sized refrigerators do not qualify.


Customers seeking information on bulky item collection services can be found [here](https://www.anaheim.net/6276/Bulky-Item-Collection). 
_*Program is subject to available funding._
