Pursuant to Commission Rules of Practice and Procedure, Rule 8.1(c), this ruling provides notice to the parties of Rulemaking 14-10-003 that the California Public Utilities Commission will host a Residential Electric Rate Summit on December 4, 2017 and a Rate Design Forum on December 11-12, 2017. One or more Commissioners (including a potential quorum of Commissioners), Advisors, and Administrative Law Judges may be in attendance at these public events, but no official Commission action will be taken at these events. Rule 8.1(c) of the Commission’s Rules of Practice and Procedure states, in part, that interested parties do not need to report a communication that occurs at “a public hearing, workshop, or other public forum noticed by ruling or order in the proceeding, or in the record of the proceeding.”
* * *
The 2017 Residential Electric Rate Summit will be held as follows:
