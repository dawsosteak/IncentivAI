# Why Work for California?
![Great Benefits](https://workforcalifornia.ca.gov/images/benefits.png)
Great Benefits
![Job Security](https://workforcalifornia.ca.gov/images/job-security.png)
Job Security
![Work with a purpose](https://workforcalifornia.ca.gov/images/doing-good.png)
Work with a Purpose
As the largest employer in California, the State of California offers more than 600 career paths in more than 200 departments, meaning no matter where you are with your education or experience level, we have the job for you. From benefits to advancement opportunities, becoming a State employee can provide you with the employment security and growth you desire.
