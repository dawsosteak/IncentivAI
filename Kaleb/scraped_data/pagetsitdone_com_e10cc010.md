## **New Opportunities Await** Growing Businesses
Are you looking to expand or relocate your business in PA? The **BusinessPA Team** is ready to assist you in implementing your business growth strategy.
Pennsylvania has a [strategic location](https://pagetsitdone.com/why-pa/location_and_infrastructure "Locate or Expand"), a [world-class workforce](https://pagetsitdone.com/why-pa/workforce "Workforce"), and an excellent mix of [affordability and quality of life](https://pagetsitdone.com/why-pa/quality_of_life "Link to Quality of Life page") that makes it the best place on the East Coast to do business. Combined with a business-friendly tax environment in which our corporate net income tax rate is on a path to 4.99 percent – among the lowest in the nation – we are confident that the next location for your business should be right here in the Keystone State.
Take a closer look at Pennsylvania and give the BusinessPA Team a call at **717.787.8199.** You can also explore the map below for contact information for professional staff in each of our regional offices and for staff in our headquarters in Harrisburg.
Are you a small business owner? We've got you covered. Dedicated resources and support are available at our [Business One-Stop Shop website.(opens in a new tab)](https://business.pa.gov/)
