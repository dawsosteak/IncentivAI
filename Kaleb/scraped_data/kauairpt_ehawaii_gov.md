# Kauaʻi Real Property Tax Payments
Instructions
To get started, search for a tax bill balance by entering your **15-digit parcel ID / TMK number (including owner sequence number)** with or without dashes into the Search box. Example: TMK **1-1-234-005-6789-000** or **112340056789000**. Your parcel ID / TMK number can be found on your real property tax bill received in the mail.
**Can't find your 15-digit parcel ID / TMK number?** Search for your parcel ID / TMK number via [kauaipropertytax.com](http://www.qpublic.net/hi/kauai/search.html). Once you obtain the 12-digit number from the site, add your 3-digit owner sequence number to the end of the 12-digit number. The full 15 digits represents your parcel ID / TMK number.
