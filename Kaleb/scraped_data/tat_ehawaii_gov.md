#  **County of Maui** TA Tax Payments
This **payment portal** is used to make a payment to the **County of Maui** for the County portion of the Transient Accommodations Tax that you report on your State filed Transient Accommodations Tax Returns (TA-1 or TA-2). 
**zero proceeds** on your returns, no payment would be due to the County; therefore, **no action is necessary on this payment portal**. 
## Log-in payment
[Log in to myHawaii](https://tat.ehawaii.gov/tat/maui/login)
Log in to save property information, access payment history, and enjoy a quicker process for future payments.
## Guest payment
[Pay without logging in](https://tat.ehawaii.gov/tat/maui/public/guestpay)
Pay as a guest (no login) but please note that history and property information will not be saved.
