#  Public Records Request - Calls for Service 
Requests may be submitted for a call for service at a specific location, a location over a time frame or types of calls/incidents.   
  

  * Request for incident history will be reviewed for the type of incident and release of information.
  * Calls for service will be prepared for a location / address and time frame.
  * The requester will be contacted with the amount due for copy fees which are .75 cents for the first page and .20 cents for each additional page.
  * All requests are processed in 7 to 10 days.


[Click here to submit a request](https://cityofanaheimcapd.nextrequest.com/)
