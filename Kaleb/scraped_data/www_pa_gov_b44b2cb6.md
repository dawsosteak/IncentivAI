Local, state, and federal government websites often end in .gov. Commonwealth of Pennsylvania government websites and email systems use "pennsylvania.gov" or "pa.gov" at the end of the address. Before sharing sensitive or personal information, make sure you're on an official state website.

## Your Experience Matters
At the Commonwealth of Pennsylvania, we value your skills and experience. Currently, 92% of state government jobs are open to skilled Pennsylvanians without a four-year degree. That’s 65,000 opportunities available to you.

## Why Work With Us?
Our benefits package is designed to support your overall well-being, helping you thrive both at work and in life.
  * **Financial Security:** Retirement plans and financial planning resources.
  * **Physical Health:** Comprehensive health insurance and fitness incentives.
  * **Mental Wellness:** Counseling services and wellness programs.


Wonder how these benefits work in real life? Our team members share their experiences. Discover how our benefits can help you succeed both personally and professionally.

## Our Commitment to Diversity & Inclusion
_At the Commonwealth of Pennsylvania, we believe that a diverse and inclusive workplace is essential for innovation, growth, and the success of our public service mission. We are committed to fostering an environment where every employee feels valued, respected, and empowered to contribute their unique perspectives and talents. Our dedication to diversity, equity, and inclusion ensures that we not only reflect the communities we serve but also create a culture where all voices are heard and everyone has the opportunity to thrive._
