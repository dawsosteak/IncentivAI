#  Brookhurst Community Center 
![Brookhurst Community Center](https://www.anaheim.net/ImageRepository/Document?documentId=2764)Looking for a facility to host your banquet, wedding, Quinceañera or meeting? Brookhurst Community Center meets your needs. Centrally located in the City of Anaheim near the I-5 and 91 freeways, Brookhurst Community Center serves all of Orange County and beyond.
Operated by the City of Anaheim, the facility’s peaceful park setting and lovely flower gardens create a tranquil atmosphere for your events. Add the personal touch of our professional staff and you’ll see why Brookhurst Community Center is your special place.
## Amenities
Let the Brookhurst Community Center assist you in planning your next event. The Center’s spacious multipurpose room with wood flooring offers splendid accommodations for wedding receptions, dances, and trade shows. The beautiful garden patio area is ideal for intimate weddings, brunches, and luncheons. Our 4 meeting rooms provide business and adult education groups with a professional setting for training sessions, seminars, and classes. To enhance your program audio visual equipment and lecterns are available at no additional charge. The center has the following features:
  * 16,000 square foot conference and banquet facility
  * Meeting accommodations for 25 to 500 people
  * Banquet seating for up to 400 people (300-350 including dance floor)
  * Multipurpose room with wood floor, public address system, and kitchen
  * Kitchen available for catering staging area
  * Alcohol service permitted upon approval (Additional fees apply, security guards required)
  * Round and rectangular banquet tables
  * Beautifully covered patio with landscaped garden
  * Four meeting rooms
  * Complete climate control systems
  * Free parking for 350 cars
  * Free WiFi
  * Portable stages
