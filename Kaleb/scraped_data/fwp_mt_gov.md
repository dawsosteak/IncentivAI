## Mountain Lion: Biological Samples in the Cabinet Mountains
Montana FWP returns south of Libby for mountain lion research in the Cabinet Mountains. With the help of houndsmen, the team will collect biological samples to compare with past research for the management of mountain lion populations in Montana.
[ Watch more FWP work  ](https://www.youtube.com/user/MontanaFWP)
