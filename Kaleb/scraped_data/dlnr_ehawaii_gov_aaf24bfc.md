###  Welcome to DAR's online services! 
Apply for a new or renew commercial marine licenses/permits/registrations and submit reports. 
#### Individual/Business Licenses/Permits
  * [New Application](https://dlnr.ehawaii.gov/cmls-public/app/new-application)   
Apply for commercial/business fisheries licenses/permits. 
  * [Renewal Application](https://dlnr.ehawaii.gov/cmls-public/app/renew-license)  
Apply to renew licenses/permits. 
  * [Add New License or Permit Type](https://dlnr.ehawaii.gov/cmls-public/app/add-license)  
Apply to add new licenses/permits to an existing license/permit. 


#### Vessel License/Registration
  * [New BFVR](https://dlnr.ehawaii.gov/cmls-public/app/new-vessel-registration)  
Apply for a Bottomfish Vessel ID Registration which applies to any vessel used to catch Deep 7 
  * [Renew BFVR](https://dlnr.ehawaii.gov/cmls-public/app/renew-vessel-registration)  
Apply to renew and/or update a Bottomfish Vessel ID Registration. 
  * [New CMVL](https://dlnr.ehawaii.gov/cmls-public/app/new-cmvl)  
Apply for a Commercial Marine Vessel License. _Must have a valid Commercial Marine License to apply._
  * [Renew CMVL](https://dlnr.ehawaii.gov/cmls-public/app/renew-cmvl)  
Apply to renew a Commercial Marine Vessel License. _Must have a valid Commercial Marine License to apply._


#### Commercial Reporting
  * [Submit a Fishing Report](http://dlnr.ehawaii.gov/cmls-fr)  
Fulfill Commercial Marine License report requirement by filing a Deep 7 Bottomfish and/or Monthly Fishing Report. 
  * [Submit a Dealer Report](https://dlnr.ehawaii.gov/odr)  
Fulfill Dealer report requirement by filing a weekly Dealer Report. 


Fees can be found on DAR's website at: <https://dlnr.hawaii.gov/dar/licenses-permits/>
Fees are payable by credit or debit card. All processed application payments are non-refundable.
All transactions submitted via Internet are processed as applications and are subject to verification. If the pending transaction is approved, the appropriate license, permit, or registration will be sent to you within five business days. 
* * *
DAR reserves the right to refuse to issue a license, permit, or registration to any person who does not provide complete and accurate information.
