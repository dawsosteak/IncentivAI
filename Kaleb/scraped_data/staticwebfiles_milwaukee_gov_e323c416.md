  * #### Develop vacant lot reuse handbook

  * #### Analyze zoning codes 
  * #### Implement grow zones and additional green overlay zones
  * #### Increase tree planting and preservation
  * #### Green access neighborhood study
  * #### Promote eco-tourism as a growth sector for employment
