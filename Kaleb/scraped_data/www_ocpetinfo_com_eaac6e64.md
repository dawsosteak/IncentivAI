#  Online Pet Licensing and License Renewal Form 
Content block block-countyoc-content 
Text block 
Body 
Thank you for choosing to use OC Animal Care’s online Licensing payment process!
By clicking our License and License Renewal Form, you will be directed to a secure site with a third-party vendor for payment processing.
Please feel free to call us at [**(714) 935-6848**](tel:7149356848) with any questions you may have. Our licensing specialists are here to assist you Monday-Friday (excluding holidays) from 8:00 a.m. - 5:00 p.m.
Licensing discounts are available to Active or Veteran US Military personnel or senior citizens ages 60+, if the dog is spayed/neutered.

Each contract city within the County has different zoning requirements regarding the number of animals it allows per property. Some contract cities have zoning requirements that do not allow for Animal Permits in their city. It is your responsibility to be familiar with your city’s zoning requirements.
