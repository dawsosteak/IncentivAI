##    
About the Delaware Criminal Justice Council
_"It is not enough that Justice be done, but that Justice be seen to be done."_
  

The Delaware Criminal Justice Council is an independent body committed to leading the criminal justice system through a collaborative approach that calls upon the experience and creativity of the [Council](https://cjc.delaware.gov/council-membership/), all components of the criminal justice system and the community. We continually strive for an effective system which is fair, efficient and accountable.  
  
[Read More  for Delaware Criminal Justice Council](https://cjc.delaware.gov/about/)
