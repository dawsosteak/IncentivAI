#  Energy Efficiency
California has led the nation in energy efficiency programs since the 1970s. Due to the state’s long standing efficiency programs, per capita energy use has remained flat, while the rest of the US has increased by about 33 percent. 
The CPUC regulates investor-owned utility (IOU) ratepayer-funded energy efficiency programs. The CPUC works with the IOUs, other portfolio administrators, and vendors to develop programs and measures to transform technology markets within California using ratepayer funds.
  * [CPUC's role in regulating Energy Efficiency](https://www.cpuc.ca.gov/industries-and-topics/electrical-energy/demand-side-management/energy-efficiency/cpucs-role-in-energy-efficiency-programs)

## Information for Consumers
If you are looking for EE Programs for your home, business or property, please see the webpage of the EE Program Administrators that serve your area:
  * [Pacific Gas & Electric EE Programs](https://www.pge.com/en/save-energy-and-money/energy-saving-programs.html) (serves Northern California)
  * [Marin Clean Energy Programs](https://www.mcecleanenergy.org/customer-programs/) (serves Contra Costa, Marin, Napa, and Solano Counties)
  * [Bay Area Regional Energy Network](https://www.bayren.org/programs-rebates) (serves Bay Area)
  * Tri County Regional Energy Network (serves San Luis Obispo, Santa Barbara, and Ventura Counties) 
  * Northern Rural Energy Network (serves Alpine, Amador, Butte, Calaveras, El Dorado, Humboldt, Lake, Lassen, Mendocino, Mariposa, Nevada, Placer, Plumas, Sierra, Sutter, Tuolumne, and Yuba Counties) 
  * Central Rural Energy Network (serves Fresno, Inyo, Kern, Kings, Madera, Merced, Mono, Monterey, San Benito, San Joaquin, San Luis Obispo, Stanislaus, and Tulare Counties)
  * Southern California Edison (serves Southern California)
  * [Southern California Gas Programs](https://www.socalgas.com/save-money-and-energy) (serves Southern California)
  * Southern California Regional Energy Network (serves Southern California)
  * [San Diego Gas and Electric Programs](https://www.sdge.com/node/28306) (serves San Diego County and Southern Orange County)
  * San Diego Regional Energy Network
