# 2024-34 Strategic Plan Anaheim: priorities that guide our city
Anaheim is a dynamic city that delivers exceptional services to provide a high quality of life for residents, a thriving environment for businesses and a welcoming atmosphere for visitors.
In looking to the future, Anaheim always strives to improve and promote growth for the betterment of our city.
Anaheim has a strategic plan of community priorities and guiding principles to lead our work on behalf of residents, businesses and visitors. A strategic plan is a chance to think big about larger issues that impact the daily life of our city today and in years to come.
The goal of a strategic plan is to outline high-level priorities, objectives and issues that can help guide policy decisions by our City Council and the work and planning of city departments.
We know many critical priorities for Anaheim include addressing homelessness, affordable housing, public safety and more. Our strategic plan includes those topics as well as others.
The goals and priorities outlined in the strategic plan are reflective of what we have heard from the community.
Anaheim’s top high-level priorities include addressing blight and improving major street corridors, programs and benefits for older adults and expanding affordable housing.
We spent months collecting input from everyone in our community. On Feb. 10, the Anaheim City Council held a workshop to hear the findings, additional community feedback and began collaborating on what the Strategic Plan would look like.
At the April 2 City Council meeting, the Anaheim City Council unanimously approved the Strategic Plan for 2024-2034.
