## A Global Team that **Speaks Your Language**
Pennsylvania’s global investment representatives serve as business advocates and liaisons between potential foreign investors and BusinessPA. These representatives are committed to supporting international companies and eliminating the barriers that foreign companies might encounter while investing, expanding, or operating a business in Pennsylvania.
