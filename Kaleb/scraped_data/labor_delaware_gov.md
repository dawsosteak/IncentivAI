# Let's get to work.
  
  

The Delaware Department of Labor promotes a strong economy by empowering workers, job seekers, and employers – ensuring safety and justice in the workplace; partnering with employers to cultivate a skilled workforce; and connecting people to benefits, employment, training and labor market information.
  

![Smiling man with a yellow hard hat](https://labor.delaware.gov/wp-content/themes/dosgic_LABOR_theme/img/facebook-labor-yoast.jpg)  
[Learn More](https://labor.delaware.gov/about)
  
  
  

# Looking to File an Unemployment Claim?
  

Looking for information about Unemployment Insurance Claims? This section contains useful information for claimants about how to file a claim and also many of the other services offered by the Delaware Department of Labor.
  
[File Now (Opens in a new window.)](https://uics.delawareworks.com/Forms/Form_WL1 "Opens in a new window")
  
  
  

![A stylistic illustration showing a disabled person enjoying the outdoors with his parents and two siblings.](https://labor.delaware.gov/wp-content/themes/dosgic_LABOR_theme/img/PaidLeave-Homepage_Image_690.png)
![Delaware Paid Leave. Family and Medical Leave Insurance Program](https://labor.delaware.gov/wp-content/themes/dosgic_LABOR_theme/img/Delaware-Paid-Leave-Logo.png)
Delaware Paid Leave insurance program goes into effect January 1, 2026. It's a win-win for employees who need support during a health or family event, and for employers who want to attract and retain top-quality personnel. If your business has 10 or more employees, you're required to participate. Get to know the guidelines now so you're ready to fulfill the requirements later.
  
[Get Your Business REady](https://labor.delaware.gov/delaware-paid-leave/)
