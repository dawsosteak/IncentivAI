# What We Do:
Green Building // Solar 101 // EV 101 // Community Engagement
Build San Antonio Green is a 501(C)(3) that works with builders and developers to certify green homes in San Antonio. Our Goal is to create awareness and interest among the builders and the homeowners in resource-efficient building methods, materials, and technologies. We do this primarily through our program and the certification of homes as green. We also educate homeowners and buyers so they can make informed choices to reduce energy and water consumption and work with our Partners to create programs for air quality improvement, water conservation, energy efficiency and renewable energy.
Build San Antonio Green was founded by Bill Sinkin in 2001. Build San Antonio Green manages a nationally award winning residential green certification program that has certified over 15,800 projects to date resulting in 25 MW of Peak Demand Reduction and 407 million lbs of CO2 to be saved since its inception 21 years ago.
Our Mission is to protect and enhance the quality of life of the citizens of the metropolitan San Antonio Area by developing standards by which to certify newly constructed and existing structures to green standards, and to provide leadership, expertise, and education for the wise, efficient, and sustainable use of energy and resources.
We strive to fulfill our mission through our Green Building Program and our free, unbiased, technical consumer decision tools for solar and EV options here in San Antonio: Bring Solar Home and Drive EV Home.

# **What is Green?**
### Our certified projects meet a rigorous list of requirements to ensure:
  * **Energy Efficiency** – BSAG homes use less energy and provide greater comfort than standard homes.
  * **Water Efficiency** – BSAG homes use less water indoors and outdoors
  * **Efficient Use of Materials** – Efficient techniques, such as advanced framing, provide greater efficiency with less material.
  * **More Healthful** – Reduced VOC’s and particulate matter, along with proper air flow and efficient filtration provide a more healthful environment
  * **Site Preservation** – For minimal disruption of the site, trees and vegetation are preserved as extensively as possible. Recyclable waste is diverted from the landfill.

## Energy Conservation
Ensure a well-insulated, well-sealed home. Seal any cracks, such as around windows, into the attic, baseboards, etc. 
Additionally, upgrade appliances to ENERGY STAR qualified models. 
Ensure that HVAC ducts are in good repair and sealed properly. 
If possible, hire an Energy Rater to identify the biggest potentials for improvement. 
Many energy conserving improvements qualify for [rebates from CPS Energy](https://www.cpsenergy.com/en/my-home/savenow.html)

## Water Conservation 
For indoor water use, install low-flow aerators on sinks and low-flow shower-heads.

Take advantage of the[ rebates and programs](https://www.saws.org/conservation/residential-outdoor-programs-rebates/) that SAWS offers for water conservation. And check out Garden Style SA for helpful gardening tips.

## Health
Use low or no-VOC paints, sealants and adhesives. Choose carpeting that is Carpet & Rug Institute Green Label Certified. Install a high-efficiency filter in your HVAC system. Ensure that gas appliances are venting properly and safely.

# What We Do:
Green Building // Solar 101 // EV 101 // Community Engagement
Build San Antonio Green is a 501(C)(3) that works with builders and developers to certify green homes in San Antonio. Our Goal is to create awareness and interest among the builders and the homeowners in resource-efficient building methods, materials, and technologies. We do this primarily through our program and the certification of homes as green. We also educate homeowners and buyers so they can make informed choices to reduce energy and water consumption and work with our Partners to create programs for air quality improvement, water conservation, energy efficiency and renewable energy.
Build San Antonio Green was founded by Bill Sinkin in 2001. Build San Antonio Green manages a nationally award winning residential green certification program that has certified over 15,800 projects to date resulting in 25 MW of Peak Demand Reduction and 407 million lbs of CO2 to be saved since its inception 21 years ago.
Our Mission is to protect and enhance the quality of life of the citizens of the metropolitan San Antonio Area by developing standards by which to certify newly constructed and existing structures to green standards, and to provide leadership, expertise, and education for the wise, efficient, and sustainable use of energy and resources.
We strive to fulfill our mission through our Green Building Program and our free, unbiased, technical consumer decision tools for solar and EV options here in San Antonio: Bring Solar Home and Drive EV Home.

# **What is Green?**
### Our certified projects meet a rigorous list of requirements to ensure:
  * **Energy Efficiency** – BSAG homes use less energy and provide greater comfort than standard homes.
  * **Water Efficiency** – BSAG homes use less water indoors and outdoors
  * **Efficient Use of Materials** – Efficient techniques, such as advanced framing, provide greater efficiency with less material.
  * **More Healthful** – Reduced VOC’s and particulate matter, along with proper air flow and efficient filtration provide a more healthful environment
  * **Site Preservation** – For minimal disruption of the site, trees and vegetation are preserved as extensively as possible. Recyclable waste is diverted from the landfill.

## Energy Conservation
Ensure a well-insulated, well-sealed home. Seal any cracks, such as around windows, into the attic, baseboards, etc. 
Additionally, upgrade appliances to ENERGY STAR qualified models. 
Ensure that HVAC ducts are in good repair and sealed properly. 
If possible, hire an Energy Rater to identify the biggest potentials for improvement. 
Many energy conserving improvements qualify for [rebates from CPS Energy](https://www.cpsenergy.com/en/my-home/savenow.html)

## Water Conservation 
For indoor water use, install low-flow aerators on sinks and low-flow shower-heads.

Take advantage of the[ rebates and programs](https://www.saws.org/conservation/residential-outdoor-programs-rebates/) that SAWS offers for water conservation. And check out [Garden Style SA ](https://www.gardenstylesanantonio.com)for helpful gardening tips.

## Health
Use low or no-VOC paints, sealants and adhesives. Choose carpeting that is Carpet & Rug Institute Green Label Certified. Install a high-efficiency filter in your HVAC system. Ensure that gas appliances are venting properly and safely.
