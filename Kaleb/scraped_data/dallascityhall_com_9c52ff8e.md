**Request a Payment Plan Online**
To choose this option you must be able to download the Contact Information form (Payment plan paperwork, is provided during the online setup) and upload the completed form with acceptable photo identification.
You must be at least 18 years of age to complete the application form with acceptable photo identification. [**Acceptable form of identification**](https://dallascityhall.com/departments/courtdetentionservices/Pages/Frequently-Asked-Questions.aspx#Acceptable) (Social Security cards and selfies (pictures) are NOT valid forms of identification).
Notice: By paying your citation online, you will be entering a plea of "Guilty" or "No Contest" and waiving your rights to a jury trial. Your citation will be recorded as a conviction and reported to the Texas Department of Public Safety
Payment plan Terms:
  * Pay a 15% down payment. The remaining balance will be split between 4 monthly payments to satisfy the entire balance within 120 days. (Payments do not automatically draft. You have to make the payment each month.)
  * Alternate payment plan options may be available by coming into the Municipal Court.
  * An additional $15 fee will be assessed on each criminal citation(s) only if the full payment amount of the citation is not received within the first 30 days of the payment contract agreement or judgment.


Once the payment plan is approved, payments may be made online, by phone, by mail, or in-person.
Online is available 24/7, including weekends and holidays. You may pay by debit card or credit card. Visa and MasterCard are accepted.
![masterCard Visa image](https://dallascityhall.com/departments/courtdetentionservices/PublishingImages/Pages/payment-plan/masterCard_Visa.png)
[Click Here to Request Online](https://www.municipalonlinepayments.com/dallastx)**(NOTE:** at this time, Internet Explorer will not work for online payments.)
******* When you sign up online to request a payment plan, please note that this is a **REQUEST ONLY**. You are NOT approved until you have been contacted by email from the Municipal Court. You can expect additional details and instructions by email from the Court within five (5) business days. If you do not receive an email within five (5) business days, you may be missing something required to process the request. Paperwork will be mailed to the last known address we have on file with the required steps to move forward.)*******
**A convenience fee will be charged for each online transaction.** This fee covers the cost incurred by the City of Dallas to permit the offer of an online option for your convenience. Individuals who wish to avoid this fee may handle their case by mail, in-person with the Municipal Court at 2014 Main Street, or the Marshal’s Office at 1600 Chestnut Street.
