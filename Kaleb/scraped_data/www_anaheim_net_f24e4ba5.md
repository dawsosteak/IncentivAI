# Resources & Services for Adults
**Online Resources**   
You can access many of our online resources for free with your Library card number. Take free online classes to master new skills, access thousands of current magazines, and more.
**Career Resources**  
The Library is a great place to start your job search, with resources for discovering a new career path, gaining new job skills, creating a cover letter and resume, and finding job opportunities.
**Citizenship Corner**  
New citizens can find helpful resources needed to succeed in the United States naturalization test. The Citizenship Corner at Central Library offers assistance with community partners, virtual citizenship classes, study materials, and more. You can also find books focusing on naturalization, finances, employment, and more.
