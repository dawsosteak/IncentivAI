# Regional **Export** Network
![A group of Regional Export Network officials, in business attire, pose for a group photo in a conference room](https://s7d9.scene7.com/is/image/statepa/1722954449354?fmt=webp)
## Lean on Our In-State Network of Trade Experts
Staffed by professional business development consultants, the **Regional Export Network (REN)** serves as the first point of contact for our clients. REN partners provide export development services to manufacturers and service firms located within their territory.
## Services include:
  * Export counseling
  * Market research and market entry strategy development
  * Education programs and technical support
