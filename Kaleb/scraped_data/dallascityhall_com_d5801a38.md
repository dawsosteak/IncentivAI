As North Texas braces for its coldest weather of the season, we want to inform our customers about how the winter storm may impact garbage, recycling, and brush and bulky item collections scheduled for Thursday and Friday this week.
  

## Our Commitment to Service
Sanitation is committed to keeping operations on schedule, even during inclement weather. Customers should assume that collections will occur on time unless we announce otherwise. However, this week’s Arctic air mass and expected snowfall (1–3 inches or more) may present challenges that require adjustments to our schedule.
  

## How Weather May Impact Operations
Our crews will report to work on Thursday and Friday as they are safely able. Supervisors will scout road conditions early each day, and routes will begin only if deemed safe. Here’s what you should know:
  * **Late Starts or Cancellations** : If conditions remain unsafe by 11 a.m., collections will be suspended for the day.
  * **Schedule Adjustments** :
    * If Thursday collections are canceled, they will occur Friday.
    * If Friday collections are canceled, they will occur Saturday.
    * In the event that both days are canceled, Thursday’s collections will occur Saturday, and Friday’s will be held until the following week.
    * If there is a late start on Thursday and/or Friday, it may be determined that all routes cannot be completed. In such cases, all available resources may be focused on prioritizing on-time garbage collection for that day, with recycling potentially delayed until the following week.  



We recognize that weather-related changes can be inconvenient, but these measures ensure the safety of our crews, who face risks like frostbite and hypothermia while working in freezing conditions, as well as the dangers posed by icy roads for heavy equipment and the potential for sagging or downed utility lines.
  

## Stay Informed
To keep our customers informed about any changes, we’ll provide real-time updates through multiple channels:
  * Download our mobile app: Search "Dallas Sanitation" in the iPhone [App Store](https://apps.apple.com/us/app/dallas-sanitation-services/id1079425049) or Google [Play Store](https://play.google.com/store/apps/details?id=net.recollect.dallas&hl=en_US&gl=US)
  * Sign up for email and text alerts on our website by entering your address in the [calendar tool on our website](https://dallascityhall.com/departments/sanitation/Pages/default.aspx).
  * Check our website regularly for service updates
  * Monitor your email if enrolled in our notification system  



We encourage you to check frequently for updates as conditions evolve.
  

## Thank You for Your Patience
We appreciate your understanding and flexibility as we navigate these weather challenges. Together, we can ensure the safety of our workers while maintaining reliable service for our community.
Stay warm and safe this week!
