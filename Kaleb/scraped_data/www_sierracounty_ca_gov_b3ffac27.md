#  Elections 
## Duties & Responsibilities
  

The Elections Office performs duties as mandated by state, federal, and local laws. The Elections Office handles a variety of responsibilities including:
  * Registering voters and maintaining the voter registration files for the County of Sierra
  * Administering and conducting all federal, state, county, school district, special district, and municipal elections
  * Providing information and issuing nomination documents to candidates and campaigns
  * Filing and maintaining public access campaign statements for the candidates for each election and certain elected officials


  

## Transparency
  

The Elections Office is open to the public for inspection of our practices and procedures.
