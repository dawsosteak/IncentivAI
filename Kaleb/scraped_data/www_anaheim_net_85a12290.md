#  Welcome to the First-Time Homebuyer Interest List Portal 
Please complete the pre-application screening to determine your eligibility. Submitting your information adds you to the interest list and does not guarantee program eligibility or funding. Applications will be reviewed in the order received, and eligible applicants will be contacted with next steps.
[**ENROLL ONLINE HERE**](https://housingportal.anaheim.net/Fthb)
## **Next Steps**
• Applicants will be contacted in the order of submission.  
• All information provided will be verified, and eligibility will be confirmed. Applications will be removed from the waitlist if any fraudulent statements are discovered.  
• If your application is selected, you will be asked to complete a more detailed application and provide the required supporting documents.
