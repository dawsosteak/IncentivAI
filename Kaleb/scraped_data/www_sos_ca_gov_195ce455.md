# Student Voter Registration
Choose Language 
English español
## Overview
The Student Voter Registration program was created to ensure every school in California provides eligible students with a meaningful opportunity to register, or pre-register, to vote. The Secretary of State's office contacts schools annually to gather contact information as required by Elections Code and to provide voter registration cards, when requested.
![Student Voter Registration Project, California Secretary of State](https://admin.cdn.sos.ca.gov/elections/images/svr.png)
## Annual Survey and Order Form
You may submit your school's survey response and order voter registration cards electronically using the following form:  [Annual Survey and Order Form](https://forms.office.com/g/mdSKjaCETA)
## Online Voter Registration
Online voter registration can be offered via a hyperlink or QR code posted on the school’s website.
Hyperlink: <https://registertovote.ca.gov/>
QR Code: [![Secretary of State - Online Voter Registration](https://elections.cdn.sos.ca.gov/bug/qr-online-voter-registration.png)](http://registertovote.ca.gov/)
If you would like to add a button to your webpage, instructions are available <https://www.sos.ca.gov/elections/register-vote-buttons>.
Example button:
[![Secretary of State - Register to Vote](https://elections.cdn.sos.ca.gov/images/register-to-vote.png)](http://registertovote.ca.gov/)
We also have a QR code flyer that can be printed and displayed on campus: [Register to Vote QR Code Flyer (PDF)](https://elections.cdn.sos.ca.gov/outreach/pdf/register-to-vote-qr-code-test.pdf)
## Annual Report
The Secretary of State is required to report to the Legislature each year on student voter registration efforts undertaken by the Agency. 
The annual reports are available at <https://www.sos.ca.gov/administration/agency-reports/student-voter-registration-agency-reports>
