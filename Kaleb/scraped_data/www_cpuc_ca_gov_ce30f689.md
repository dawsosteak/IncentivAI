#  Consultant and Contract Opportunities
Discover the consulting and contract opportunities available with us and how you can bid. 
**Contracting Opportunities can now be found on the State of California’s E-Procure website.** Opportunities that the commission currently has available will be listed below and will include the name of the opportunity, the “Event ID” number and the end date.
To download a copy of the opportunity, you must [visit the E-procurement website](https://caleprocure.ca.gov/pages/index.aspx) and search for bids. You can search for the opportunity, by using one (or all) of the following criteria and clicking the search button:
  * Event ID -- shown in the listing below
  * Event Name -- the contract number (just the RFP/RFQ portion) 
  * Department -- enter 8660


It is the policy of the California Public Utilities Commission (CPUC) to promote and encourage Small Business (SB) and Disabled Veteran Business Enterprise (DVBE) participation in all procurement and contracting activities. CPUC offers SB preferences and DVBE incentives to certified bidders. Go to **[OSDS Website](https://www.dgs.ca.gov/PD/Services/Page-Content/Procurement-Division-Services-List-Folder/Apply-for-or-Re-apply-as-Small-Business-Disabled-Veteran-Business-Enterprise)** to learn about California’s Small Business (SB) and Disabled Veteran Business Enterprise (DVBE) certification program
All CPUC solicitations are posted to the State of California’s **[Cal e-Procure Website](https://caleprocure.ca.gov/pages/Events-BS3/event-search.aspx)**. CPUC recommends all suppliers and bidders become registered on Cal e-Procure. You can learn how to register here: **[Sell to the State](https://caleprocure.ca.gov/pages/bidder-vendor.aspx)**
The Cal e-Procure system includes an advertisement feature. Suppliers and bidders can place ads on a solicitation to promote themselves and find subcontractors or prime contractors to partner with in submitting a solicitation response. To place adds you must be registered on Cal e-Procure. Once you have logged in, click Post Vendor Ad to get started. The following link has more information on accessing bid solicitations and placing ads. **[Learn how to access bid solicitations and place ads](https://youtu.be/m8NrKwl2z9g)**

## NOTICE OF INTENT TO AWARD
**Preferred Interpreting Service** and **LLC**
**California Public Utilities Commission**
IFB No. 20NS0009 for: American Sign Language (ASL) Interpretation Services November 10, 2020 On October 14, 2020, the California Public Utilities Commission (CPUC) released a competitive solicitation "IFB 20NS0009 American Sign Language (ASL) Interpretation Services". The purpose of this solicitation is to fund one contractor to provide certified American Sign Language (ASL) interpreters on an "as-needed" basis to perform in-person and remote ASL interpretation services for CPUC employees, or prospective CPUC employees, throughout the State of California. The bids were screened and evaluated using the criteria and process dictated in the solicitation. The proposed awardee is as follows: Proposed Awardee: Since a written request by a bidder was received to publicly post this notice, this notice is posted as an Addendum to the IFB in Cal eProcure and to the CPUC's public website at https://www.cpuc.ca.gov/contracting/. If the CPUC is unable to timely execute a funding agreement with the Proposed Awardee, the CPUC, at its sole discretion, reserves the right to cancel or otherwise modify the pending award.
