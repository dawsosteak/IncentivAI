Anaheim’s annual State of the City isn’t just any state of the city.
It’s an epic event worthy of a city that’s home to Walt Disney’s original Disneyland theme park, the playoff-contending Anaheim Ducks and Angels Baseball, now in its 60th year at the Big A.
This year is no exception. 
Join us for always fun and engaging storytelling by Mayor Ashleigh Aitken, emcee Ally Kay of the Ducks, special guest Charles Phoenix and featured guest Disneyland Resort President Jill Estronio!
Tables and seats are still available. Proceeds benefit the Anaheim Community Foundation and youth, families, seniors and others across our city.
