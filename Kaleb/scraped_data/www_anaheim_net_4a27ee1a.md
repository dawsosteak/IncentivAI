#  Go Digital and Save! 
Help reduce the need for paper bills and save! Earn up to $15 in one-time bill credits by enrolling in paperless billing and automatic payments using your checking account. Sign up for power outage notifications on Anaheim Alert, the city’s digital hub for emergency notifications for additional savings.*
**Paperless Billing + Automatic Bill Pay = $10  
Anaheim Alert = $5**
![Paperless Billing](https://www.anaheim.net/ImageRepository/Document?documentID=37902)
[Log in](https://myapu.anaheim.net/iwr/user/login.seam?cid=150) to your online account to enroll in paperless billing to receive your bills via email.
Within your online account, click “My Profile”, then click on “Communication Preferences”. On this page, select to have paperless billing.
![Auto Bill Pay](https://www.anaheim.net/ImageRepository/Document?documentID=37904)
While in your online account, enroll in automatic payments using your checking account and never miss a payment.
![Anaheim Alert ](https://www.anaheim.net/ImageRepository/Document?documentID=37903)
Sign up for [Anaheim Alert](https://www.anaheim.net/5387/Anaheim-Alert) to receive updates and notifications for outages and emergencies. 
Be sure to select "Yes" under "Sign Up for Electric Outage Notification" to receive bill credit.
_*Must be a new participant in each program. Credit is not recurring._ For additional questions on how to enroll in these programs, please give us a call at 714-765-3300.
