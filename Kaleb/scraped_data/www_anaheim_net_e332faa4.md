#  Canyon Companions 
Join OCNC staff in caring for the trails and grounds within Oak Canyon Nature Center. This is a great opportunity to learn about the environment and give back to your local community. High school students can earn community service hours. Planned projects are scheduled for each week. **Pre-registration is required! Consent form needed for participants 17 years and under.**
**Bring: Closed toed shoes, comfortable clothing, Water**
**Schedule & Fee Information**  

Canyon Companions is held on Saturdays from 12:00 pm - 2:00 pm.
**Registration**  

Pre-registration is required, although groups of ten or more must contact the nature center before hand to make reservations.
**[Online registration for Canyon Companions](https://secure.rec1.com/CA/city-of-anaheim/catalog/index/c707307d3cac644835dc11440c9ddbcd?filter=c2VhcmNoPStjYW55b24rY29tcGFuaW9ucw==) is now available.**
**  
**
_**Please note that the Nature Center cannot take volunteers who are referred by courts. Court referrals may volunteer with the Code Enforcement division._
