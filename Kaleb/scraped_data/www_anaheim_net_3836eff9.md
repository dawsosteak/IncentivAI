#  Beach Boulevard 
The undergrounding along Beach Boulevard extends through the city limits from south of Ball Road to north of Lincoln. This project is an integral part of the Beach Boulevard Specific Plan which outlines the improvements to the Beach Boulevard corridor that also include roadway and property improvements, signage, and upgraded street lighting. The undergrounding is currently being coordinated with Southern California Edison (SCE) and affected telecommunication companies. Street and median improvements are being performed by the Public Works Department. Construction started in October 2023 and is estimated to be completed by early 2026.
