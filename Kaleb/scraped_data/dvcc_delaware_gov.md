# If you feel you are in danger **call 911!**
  
  

[![Picture of a phone icon](https://dvcc.delaware.gov/wp-content/themes/dosgic_DVCC_theme/img/dvcc-icons-wired-hotline.jpg)](https://dvcc.delaware.gov/24-hour-hotline-numbers/)
## 24 Hour Hotline Numbers
The Domestic Violence Hotline numbers are perhaps the single most important resource. All hotline numbers are confidential and available 24/7.
[Learn More About 24 Hour Hotline Numbers ](https://dvcc.delaware.gov/24-hour-hotline-numbers/)  

[![Picture of an office phone icon with a number on it](https://dvcc.delaware.gov/wp-content/themes/dosgic_DVCC_theme/img/dvcc-icons-wired-numbers.jpg)](https://dvcc.delaware.gov/victim-service-resources/)
## Victim Resource Numbers
Each county has resource and hotline numbers. All hotline numbers are confidential and available 24/7.
[Learn More About Victim Resource Numbers ](https://dvcc.delaware.gov/victim-service-resources/)  

[![Picture of a handbook icon with a raised hand implying 'NO' on it](https://dvcc.delaware.gov/wp-content/themes/dosgic_DVCC_theme/img/dvcc-icons-wired-dynamics.jpg)](https://dvcc.delaware.gov/dynamics-domestic-abuse/)
## Dynamics of Domestic Abuse
Read about the scope and definition of domestic violence as it pertains to dating, in the workplace, and immigration.
[Learn More About Dynamics of Domestic Abuse ](https://dvcc.delaware.gov/dynamics-domestic-abuse/)  

[![Picture of a mother and child icon](https://dvcc.delaware.gov/wp-content/themes/dosgic_DVCC_theme/img/dvcc-icons-wired-family.jpg)](https://dvcc.delaware.gov/family-visitation-centers/)
## Family Visitation Centers
Family Visitation Centers provide a continuum of services designed to help address custody and visitation issues in families with a history of domestic violence and abuse.
[Learn More About Family Visitation Centers ](https://dvcc.delaware.gov/family-visitation-centers/)  

  
  

![Picture of a hand icon open with a heart floating above it symbolizing help and trust](https://dvcc.delaware.gov/wp-content/themes/dosgic_DVCC_theme/img/dvcc-icons-wired-help.png)
## Safety Alert
If you are in danger, please use a safer computer, or call 911, a [local hotline](https://dvcc.delaware.gov/24-hour-hotline-numbers/), or the U.S. National Domestic Violence Hotline at 1-800-799-7233 and TTY 1-800-787-3224. Learn more [technology safety tips (Opens in a new window.)](http://nnedv.org/internetsafety.html) There is always a computer trail, but you can leave this site quickly by using our quick escape button.

## Domestic Violence Intervention Program
* * *
This program is designed to assist those that need to use Delaware Courts for any domestic related issues. For example, a criminal trial as a witness, civil issues such as divorce, child custody or support, protection from abuse orders, or any other domestic issue. [Learn More About the Domestic Violence Intervention Program ](https://capitolpd.delaware.gov/dv-vip/index.shtml)
