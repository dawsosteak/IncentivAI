#  Growth happens**in Pennsylvania**
![A group of employees wearing shirts with the logo for the company Kettle and Fire cut a ceremonial ribbon outside a new manufacturing building](https://s7d9.scene7.com/is/image/statepa/kettle%20and%20fire%20ribbon%20cutting?fmt=webp)
## Do Business Here.
## **Unlock Opportunities.**
## The BusinessPA team is ready to help you implement your business growth strategy.
Pennsylvania has the [strategic location, world-class workforce, and excellent mix of affordability and quality of life](https://pagetsitdone.com/why-pa "Link to the Why PA page on the Pennsylvania Gets It Done site.") that makes it the best place on the East Coast to do business. Combined with a business-friendly tax environment in which our corporate net income tax rate is on a path to 4.99 percent – among the lowest in the nation – and we are confident that the next location for your business should be right here in the Keystone State.
Take a closer look at what Pennsylvania has to offer and call the BusinessPA Team at 717.787.8199, or visit our [Development and Expansion](https://pagetsitdone.com/locate_or_expand/development_and_expansion_support "Link to Development and Expansion page") page to find regional office staff contacts. Have a question? [Contact us via our webform](https://pagetsitdone.com/contact_us "Link to Contact Us page"), and we'll connect you with the right people, programs, and resources to help your business grow.

## Sites & Incentives
In 2024 alone, the Commonwealth invested a staggering $500 million on site development programs, including $400 million toward [PA SITES(opens in a new tab)](https://dced.pa.gov/programs/pennsylvania-strategic-investments-to-enhance-sites-program-pa-sites/), a program that supports companies with funds for planning and construction. We also offer incentives for site redevelopment, projects in targeted economic development zones, and so much more. Read on to see the ways we're making it easy to make your next move right here in Pennsylvania.
[ EXPLORE SITES & INCENTIVES](https://pagetsitdone.com/locate_or_expand/sites_and_incentives)

## Foreign Direct Investment (FDI)
More than than 5,600 foreign-owned companies operate in Pennsylvania, and the list is growing — for good reason. With a diverse economy that spans industries from agriculture to advanced technology, and a GDP rivaling the size of most countries, PA is an ideal place for international companies looking to relocate or expand in the U.S. 
[ EXPLORE FDI](https://pagetsitdone.com/locate_or_expand/foreign_direct_investment)

## International Trade
Received a trade lead from another country and need export assistance? Having trouble finding a qualified partner in a new market? Wrestling with foreign regulations to get your product into a new market? We offer a wide range of programs and services to help you address these and many other international business challenges. Learn more about trading from and with Pennsylvania, and connect with regional experts who can help you get it done.
