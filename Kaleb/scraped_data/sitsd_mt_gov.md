# Montana SITSD
## Welcome to SITSD
![Kevin Gilbertson, State CIO](https://sitsd.mt.gov/_images/KevinGilbertson.jpg)
**Kevin Gilbertson, State CIO**
As the State Chief Information Officer, I'm honored to lead our talented team of professionals as we collaborate with state agencies to deliver exceptional IT services to the Treasure State.
The State Information Technology Services Division drives the future of digital government in Montana with targeted innovation and dedicated public service. We focus on delivering unparalleled IT services to enable modern, innovative, secure, and accessible digital government experiences.
We work closely with technology leaders in Montana state agencies, partners in other states, and the private sector to improve digitization, retire technical debt, and use a consultative approach.
Above all, we are committed to building a future of excellence in service to all citizens.
## About Us
The State Information Technology Services Division (SITSD) serves as the backbone of Montana's IT infrastructure, delivering secure and innovative technology services essential for state operations. Our mission is to provide unparalleled IT services to enable modern, innovative, secure, and accessible digital government experiences for Montana. We are committed to transparency, trustworthiness, innovation, service, and teamwork, enhancing government efficiency through strategic use of technology.
## What We Do
SITSD ensures the stability, security, and modernization of critical systems across all state agencies. Our offices cover the following business functions:
![Image of a hand holding a tablet and stylus](https://sitsd.mt.gov/_images/light-man-hand-pen.jpg)
Technology Office
Manages application development, hosting, and software services to ensure efficient and secure operation of state websites and applications.
![Image of a computer chip design](https://sitsd.mt.gov/_images/Opportunity.jpg)
Innovation and Transformation Office
Enhances IT service delivery through project management, process optimization, and stakeholder training.
![Image of a man looking at a computer screen where a virtual meeting is taking place](https://sitsd.mt.gov/_images/stock-images/GettyImages-1286802154.jpg)
Operations Office
Supports operational IT needs with network security, customer service, enterprise platforms, and desktop support to ensure seamless functionality across agencies.
![Image of servers and ethernet cables](https://sitsd.mt.gov/_images/stock-images/networkingcables600.png)
Digital Infrastructure Office
Supports cloud adoption and maintains data centers and network architecture for reliable, scalable infrastructure.
![Image of a hand pointing to an image of a lock](https://sitsd.mt.gov/_images/featured2.jpg)
Information Security Office
Safeguards IT infrastructure by implementing security policies, monitoring threats, and ensuring compliance with regulations
