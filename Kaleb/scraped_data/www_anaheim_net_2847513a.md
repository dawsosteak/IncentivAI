#  Dusk-to-Dawn Lighting (Residential) 
## The Dusk-to-Dawn Lighting Program offers high-efficiency outdoor lighting to keep your home bright and safe.
### **Increase visibility with dusk-to-dawn and security lights.**
![20W Res Security Light w. Motion Sensor](https://www.anaheim.net/ImageRepository/Document?documentID=28841)
![D2D Greenlite Light Bulb](https://www.anaheim.net/ImageRepository/Document?documentID=25051)
![115w PaR38](https://www.anaheim.net/ImageRepository/Document?documentID=28852)
Outdoor security light with dusk-to-dawn function and motion detection. 
LED A19 bulb with dusk to dawn features allows any light fixture to turn on at night and off in the morning. 
LED Par 38 bulb with dusk-to-dawn features allows a celling or can light fixture to turn on at night and off in the morning. 
###  **Program Criteria**  
  

  * Participants must be Anaheim Public Utilities electric customers with current and non-delinquent accounts.
  * Lights must be installed within the 30-day period. For new hardwired fixtures, it is recommended that participants obtain multiple quotes from licensed electricians and also obtain a permit from the building department for installation.
  * Lights must be maintained and used for a minimum of five years from installation.
  * Participants must provide access for inspection of the installed fixture(s).
  * Participants are eligible to receive up to two (2) free light fixtures for their residential home.


Program participation is limited to once every 5 years. This program is subject to available funding.
To view multi-family and commercial property Dusk-to-Dawn lighting options, please visit our [Commercial Dusk-to-Dawn Program webpage](https://anaheim.net/2628/Dusk-to-Dawn-Lighting).
###  [**Qualifying Income Levels**](https://anaheim.net/6423/Qualifying-Income-Levels)  
  

Low-Income Limits as defined by the California Department of Housing and Community Development (adjusted annually).
