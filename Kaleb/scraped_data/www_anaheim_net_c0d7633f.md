#  Cerritos | Nutwood 
The undergrounding along Cerritos Avenue extends from Nutwood Street to Euclid Street, and on Nutwood Street from Katella Avenue to Ball Road. The project is coordinated with affected telecommunication companies, railroad, and the Public Works Department for final street paving. Construction started in May 2024 and is estimated to be completed by Spring 2026.
