# Delaware Division of Human & Civil Rights
![Delaware Division of Human & Civil Rights](https://humanandcivilrights.delaware.gov/wp-content/themes/dosgic_Human_And_Civil_Rights_theme/img/DHCR_logo_400px.png)
[ Listen ](https://app-na.readspeaker.com/cgi-bin/rsent?customerid=7262&lang=en_us&readid=readSpeak_test&url=https://humanandcivilrights.delaware.gov/ "Listen to this page using ReadSpeaker")
We promote and enforce laws against discrimination and foster amicable relationships among the diverse populations of Delaware.
## Welcome
The [Division of Human and Civil Rights](https://humanandcivilrights.delaware.gov/about/) and the [Delaware Human and Civil Rights Commission](https://humanandcivilrights.delaware.gov/commission-members/) administer Delaware’s [Fair Housing](http://delcode.delaware.gov/title6/c046/index.shtml) and [Equal Accommodations](http://delcode.delaware.gov/title6/c045/index.shtml) laws. These laws protect you against discrimination in housing and in places that serve the public. The Commission promotes amicable relationships among the State's various racial, ethnic and cultural groups and works to increase public awareness of civil and human rights in Delaware. 
View our calendar for the most up to date information on events and training.
[View Calendar](https://humanandcivilrights.delaware.gov/events-calendar/)
## Popular Resources
### [Fair Housing Information Center](https://humanandcivilrights.delaware.gov/fair-housing-information-center/)
Learn about your rights to Fair Housing, who the law protects, and the signs of possible housing discrimination.
### [About the Delaware Human and Civil Rights Commission](https://humanandcivilrights.delaware.gov/commission-members/)
Since its founding in 1961, the members of the Delaware Human and Civil Rights Commission have worked to ensure a more accommodating state for everyone.
### [File a Discrimination Complaint](https://humanandcivilrights.delaware.gov/fair-housing-information-center/how-to-file-a-complaint/)
If you feel you have been the victim of illegal discrimination, you can file a complaint on-line, by phone, or in person at one of our offices.
