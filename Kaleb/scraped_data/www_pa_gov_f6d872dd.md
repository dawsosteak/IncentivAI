# Submit a Right-to-Know Request to the Emergency Management Agency
Request a record from the Pennsylvania Emergency Management Agency via the Right-to-Know Law.
##  On this page On this page
  * [Overview](https://www.pa.gov/agencies/pema/contact/right-to-know#overview)
  * [Submit A Request](https://www.pa.gov/agencies/pema/contact/right-to-know#submit-a-request)
  * [Contact Us](https://www.pa.gov/agencies/pema/contact/right-to-know#contact-us)
  * [Hours](https://www.pa.gov/agencies/pema/contact/right-to-know#hours)


## Overview
A written request to the Emergency Management Agency under the Right-to-Know Law must be addressed to our Open Records Officer Kristen Bradley.
## Submit A Request
Fill out and submit the [Right-to-Know Law Request Form(opens in a new tab)](https://www.pa.gov/content/dam/copapwp-pagov/en/pema/documents/about-us/standard%20rtkl%20request%20form.pdf) (PDF):​
  * Submit By Email
  * [ Submit By Phone ](tel:717-651-2001)
  * Submit By Fax


### By Mail
**Kristen Bradley**  
PEMA Open Records Officer  
1310 Elmerton Ave.  
Harrisburg, PA 17110
## Contact Us
Contact Pennsylvania's Office of Open Records with questions.
  * Contact Us By Email
  * [ Contact Us By Phone ](tel:717-346-9903)
  * [ Contact Us By Fax ](Tel:717-425-5543)


## Hours
Regular hours of our Right-to-Know Law office: 8 a.m. to 4 p.m. Monday through Friday. Requests received after the close of regular business hours will be deemed to have been received on the following business day.
