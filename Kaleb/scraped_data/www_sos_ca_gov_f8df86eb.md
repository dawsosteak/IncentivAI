# Video Resources Library
## **Video Resources Library**
**Welcome to the Business Programs Division Video Resources webpage. Here you will find an archive of previously held virtual events as well as a growing library of instructional videos to assist you with your[bizfile Online](https://bizfileonline.sos.ca.gov/) and other business filings. For additional assistance, please email our team at bizfile@sos.ca.gov.**
### How to Create an Account
* * *
Learn how to create a user account with **biz** file Online.
### How to Establish User Access Control
* * *
Learn how to request access and establish filing permissions to an entity, including how to request a PIN via **biz** file Online.
### How to Terminate Your Business Entity  

* * *
Learn how to terminate your entity using **biz** file Online.
### How to File a Statement of Information Virtual Workshop
* * *
Learn tips on how to file the required statement online at **biz** file Online.
### Nonprofit Virtual Workshop
* * *
Learn the various types of Nonprofit Corporations and how to register one online.
### Nonprofit Virtual Workshop Part 2
* * *
Hear from state and federal subject matter experts in this second installment of the Nonprofit Virtual Workshop.
### National Notary Public Day Virtual Workshop  

* * *
Hear from our Notary Team as they share updates, improvements, and best practices for optimal success as a notary in California.
### Statement of Information: Suspension Prevention  
and Recovery Virtual Workshop
* * *
Learn about all the steps necessary to prevent your business from being suspended and how to recover your business if it is suspended.
