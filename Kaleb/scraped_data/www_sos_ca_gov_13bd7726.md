# Tools for Success
As California's regularly scheduled general elections approach, the California Student Mock Election web page is updated with all the materials it takes to stage a well-run student mock election and help young people become informed and engaged voters.
### UPCOMING CALIFORNIA STUDENT MOCK ELECTION
### **October 6, 2026**
[![Secretary of State - Student Mock Election: How to Participate](https://elections.cdn.sos.ca.gov/mock-election/2026/sme-how-to-participate1.png)](https://youtu.be/aQxzsgWSGPc)
Each school that registers by September 9, 2026, receives a welcome packet in the mail containing the following items, which will be available in Spring 2026:
  * [Student Mock Election Welcome Letter (PDF)](https://elections.cdn.sos.ca.gov/mock-election/welcome-letter.pdf)
  * [Mock Election Poster (PDF)](https://elections.cdn.sos.ca.gov/mock-election/poster.pdf)
  * ["I Voted" stickers (DOCX)](https://elections.cdn.sos.ca.gov/mock-election/ivoted.docx)
  * [Pre-Registration Brochure (PDF)](https://elections.cdn.sos.ca.gov/outreach/highschool/pre-reg-flyer.pdf)
  * [High School Student Poll Worker Flyer (PDF)](https://elections.cdn.sos.ca.gov/pdfs/student-poll-worker-flyer.pdf)


#### Supply Kits (available September 2026):
**Paper supply kits** will be distributed beginning September 2, 2026, to schools that registered by that date and requested paper ballots. Paper supply kits contain:
  * Mock Election Ballot (PDF)
  * Instructions for Tabulating and Reporting Election Results (PDF)
  * Election Results Reporting Form (PDF)
  * Mock Election Voter Information Guide (PDF)


**Electronic supply kits** will be distributed via email beginning September 2, 2026. Electronic supply kits contain:
  * Microsoft Forms Voting Instructions and a link to the Microsoft Forms Ballot
  * Microsoft Forms Vote Tally Spreadsheet (XLSX)
  * Fillable PDF Mock Election Ballot
  * Fillable PDF Voting Instructions
  * Instructions for Tabulating and Reporting Election Results (PDF)
  * Election Results Reporting Form (PDF)
  * Mock Election Voter Information Guide (PDF)


The Official Voter Information Guide for the November 3, 2026 General Election is available on the Secretary of State's website: [Official Voter Information Guide](https://voterguide.sos.ca.gov/)
If you have any questions regarding the California Student Mock Election, please email us at[ ](https://elections.cdn.sos.ca.gov/mock-election/school-report-form.pdf)MyVote@sos.ca.gov.

## What is pre-registration?
If you are 16 or 17 years old and meet all other voter eligibility requirements, you can pre-register to vote at RegisterToVote.ca.gov. Simply complete the online pre-registration application and on your 18th birthday, you will automatically be registered to vote. Who is eligible to pre-register? What if you need to update your address or political party preference before you turn 18? Update your information at RegisterToVote.ca.gov. Voter registration applications are also available at your local county elections office, Department of Motor Vehicles, and post office.
