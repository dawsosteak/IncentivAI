# PA Emergency Management Agency
We’re here to make sure Pennsylvania is prepared for and can recover from emergencies and disasters.
[ For Emergency Managers ](https://www.pa.gov/agencies/pema/emergency-managers)
[ For Pennsylvanians ](https://www.pa.gov/agencies/ready)
![County 911 dispatcher at his workstation.](https://s7d9.scene7.com/is/image/statepa/911hub?ts=1772743727688&dpr=off)
## Overview
Monitoring and responding to changing conditions in Pennsylvania. Connecting county emergency managers with everything they need to prepare for, respond to, and recover from emergencies. 
[**About Us →**](https://www.pa.gov/agencies/pema/about)
