## **Welcome to Promote the Vote California!**
### **Empowering Communities.  
Strengthening Democracy. Building Connections.**
Promote the Vote California is an initiative created by the Secretary of State to promote a thriving business community that values civic engagement. We aim to foster partnerships, empower communities, and amplify voices to create a strong and inclusive California. We believe in the power of collaboration to achieve sustainable and meaningful progress.
Join the movement - **Promote the Vote!**
### Get Involved, Make a Difference
From providing exclusive toolkits to hosting dynamic events and campaigns, we’re here to support you every step of the way. Whether you're a small business, an employee community, or a nonprofit, you’ll find practical tools and opportunities to:
  * **Strengthen your community.**
  * **Empower your workforce.**
  * **Amplify civic engagement.**


Discover our **Resource Hub** —your one–stop destination for election tools and voter-focused educational resources. Stay informed and join us in building a democracy that is inclusive, accessible, and impactful for everyone.
### Why Partner with Us?
At Promote the Vote California, we believe genuine partnerships are key to building a stronger civic culture. Whether it's locally owned businesses, community organizations, or leading industries, we’re proud to work alongside those helping shape California’s future. Through our program, you’ll gain:
  * **Trusted Tools:** Access pre-made messaging kits, social media campaigns, and outreach materials designed for easy implementation.
  * **Visibility:** Showcase your partnership with us on our webpage.
  * **Support:** Participate in our climate-friendly campaigns and virtual events that minimize our carbon footprint while maximizing impact.


### Empowering California, Together
Our mission is clear: to inspire year-round civic action and to bridge gaps in voter education. We’re leveraging the power of **social media** to counter misinformation and spark meaningful conversations statewide. We focus on creating trusted messengers within workplaces and communities to amplify voices, build trust, and energize collective action.
### Join Us
Join the movement today! Let’s create a California where civic engagement doesn’t stop at the ballot box but becomes a daily way to strengthen our communities.
Together, we can build a stronger, more inclusive civic culture.
Get started by completing our [Contact Us](https://www.sos.ca.gov/elections/promote-vote-ca/contact-us) form.
