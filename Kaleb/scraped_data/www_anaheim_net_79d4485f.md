#  Bicycle Registration 
Bicycle Registration is provided by the Anaheim Police Department for City of Anaheim residents only. Your bicycle information is stored in our local database, and then accessible in the event your bicycle is lost or stolen.   
  
You will need the following information to register your bicycle:  
  

  * Serial Number
  * Make (manufacture)
  * Model
  * Frame Size and Type
  * Wheel size
  * Proof of purchase or a picture of the bicycle.


![Official Bike Reg Logo](https://www.anaheim.net/ImageRepository/Document?documentID=16339)
If you can’t locate your serial number, bring your bicycle to the Anaheim Police Department and we will register your bicycle in person. Please call (714) 765-1860 if you have any questions or need assistance.  
  
[To start the Bicycle Registration online, click here](https://secure.coplogic.com/dors/startreport/101423100/198863300/en)  
  
As a reminder, by law, bicycle riders under 18 years of age must wear a bicycle helmet while riding on a public road (CVC 21212). Wearing a helmet at any age can significantly reduce the risk of severe or fatal head injury in case of a fall. [To learn more about Anaheim’s effort to promote helmet safety, click here](http://www.anaheim.net/1924/Wear-Your-Helmet-Like-A-Pro)
