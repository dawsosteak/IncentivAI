## [New Farmers, Start Here](https://agriculture.delaware.gov/agland-preservation-planning/)
Agriculture is full of exciting and rewarding opportunities. Farming is a tough job, but at the heart of it all, you'll see a vibrant community contributing to the future of our nation's health and food security.
[LEARN MORE about New Farmers, Start Here ](https://agriculture.delaware.gov/agland-preservation-planning/)

## [Explore Delaware's Buy Local Guide](https://agriculture.delaware.gov/communications-marketing/buy-local-delaware-guide/)
Buying local means buying fresh - buying the best. Buying a local Christmas tree, ice cream, fresh produce, meats and eggs - and much more - directly from Delaware farms means you're getting the best farm products you can.
[LEARN MORE about Explore Delaware's Buy Local Guide ](https://agriculture.delaware.gov/communications-marketing/buy-local-delaware-guide/)
[![Get Your Nursery Industry License](https://agriculture.delaware.gov/wp-content/uploads/sites/108/2018/01/dda-nursery-license.jpg)](https://nila.delaware.gov)  

## [Get Your Nursery Industry License](https://nila.delaware.gov)
The Plant Industries Section licenses businesses that grow, distribute or sell nursery stock, Christmas trees and cut flowers.
[LEARN MORE about Get Your Nursery Industry License ](https://nila.delaware.gov)
[![The 2019 Racing Season has Begun!](https://agriculture.delaware.gov/wp-content/uploads/sites/108/2018/01/dda-horse-racing.jpg)](https://agriculture.delaware.gov/horse-racing/%20)  

## [The 2019 Racing Season has Begun!](https://agriculture.delaware.gov/horse-racing/%20)
Find specific information on all Delaware horse races in the state, whether on the flat at [Delaware Park (Opens in a new window.)](http://www.delawarepark.com/racing/race-info/) or harness racing at [Dover Downs and  (Opens in a new window.)](https://www.doverdowns.com/horse-racing/)[Harrington Raceway (Opens in a new window.)](http://harringtonraceway.com/home/).
[LEARN MORE about The 2019 Racing Season has Begun! ](https://agriculture.delaware.gov/horse-racing/%20)
[![Get help identifying various pests at no cost](https://agriculture.delaware.gov/wp-content/uploads/sites/108/2018/02/dda-identify-pest.jpg)](https://agriculture.delaware.gov/pest-identification/)  

## [Get help identifying various pests at no cost](https://agriculture.delaware.gov/pest-identification/)
From identifying livestock and poultry diseases, animal rabies, residential pests (like bedbugs, termites, carpenter ants, and rodents), or identifying tree health problems, the Department of Agriculture is ready to assist. 
[LEARN MORE about Get help identifying various pests at no cost ](https://agriculture.delaware.gov/pest-identification/)
[![Help Preserve Delaware Farmland into the Future ](https://agriculture.delaware.gov/wp-content/uploads/sites/108/2018/02/dda-preserve-farmland.jpg)](https://agriculture.delaware.gov/agland-preservation-planning/the-preservation-program/)  

## [Help Preserve Delaware Farmland into the Future ](https://agriculture.delaware.gov/agland-preservation-planning/the-preservation-program/)
Delaware’s Agricultural Lands Preservation program invites landowners to voluntarily preserve their farms. The program allows Delaware family farmers to know that their family’s history in agriculture, their hard work, and dedication to the land, the environment, and the industry will last forever.
[LEARN MORE about Help Preserve Delaware Farmland into the Future  ](https://agriculture.delaware.gov/agland-preservation-planning/the-preservation-program/)
[![Reforest your own property, or help plant urban trees.](https://agriculture.delaware.gov/wp-content/uploads/sites/108/2018/02/dda-plant-trees.jpg)](https://agriculture.delaware.gov/forest-service/forest-conservation-programs/)  

## [Reforest your own property, or help plant urban trees.](https://agriculture.delaware.gov/forest-service/forest-conservation-programs/)
The Delaware Forest Service offers a variety of tree seedlings each spring for landowners to reforest their property, and we organize an urban and community forestry program. 
[LEARN MORE about Reforest your own property, or help plant urban trees. ](https://agriculture.delaware.gov/forest-service/forest-conservation-programs/)

## [New Farmers, Start Here](https://agriculture.delaware.gov/agland-preservation-planning/)
Agriculture is full of exciting and rewarding opportunities. Farming is a tough job, but at the heart of it all, you'll see a vibrant community contributing to the future of our nation's health and food security.

## [Explore Delaware's Buy Local Guide](https://agriculture.delaware.gov/communications-marketing/buy-local-delaware-guide/)
Buying local means buying fresh - buying the best. Buying a local Christmas tree, ice cream, fresh produce, meats and eggs - and much more - directly from Delaware farms means you're getting the best farm products you can.
[LEARN MORE about Explore Delaware's Buy Local Guide ](https://agriculture.delaware.gov/communications-marketing/buy-local-delaware-guide/)

The Plant Industries Section licenses businesses that grow, distribute or sell nursery stock, Christmas trees and cut flowers.
[LEARN MORE about Get Your Nursery Industry License ](https://nila.delaware.gov)

## [The 2019 Racing Season has Begun!](https://agriculture.delaware.gov/horse-racing/%20)

## [Get help identifying various pests at no cost](https://agriculture.delaware.gov/pest-identification/)
From identifying livestock and poultry diseases, animal rabies, residential pests (like bedbugs, termites, carpenter ants, and rodents), or identifying tree health problems, the Department of Agriculture is ready to assist. 
[LEARN MORE about Get help identifying various pests at no cost ](https://agriculture.delaware.gov/pest-identification/)
[![Help Preserve Delaware Farmland into the Future ](https://agriculture.delaware.gov/wp-content/uploads/sites/108/2018/02/dda-preserve-farmland.jpg)](https://agriculture.delaware.gov/agland-preservation-planning/the-preservation-program/)  

## [Help Preserve Delaware Farmland into the Future ](https://agriculture.delaware.gov/agland-preservation-planning/the-preservation-program/)
Delaware’s Agricultural Lands Preservation program invites landowners to voluntarily preserve their farms. The program allows Delaware family farmers to know that their family’s history in agriculture, their hard work, and dedication to the land, the environment, and the industry will last forever.
[LEARN MORE about Help Preserve Delaware Farmland into the Future  ](https://agriculture.delaware.gov/agland-preservation-planning/the-preservation-program/)

## [Reforest your own property, or help plant urban trees.](https://agriculture.delaware.gov/forest-service/forest-conservation-programs/)
The Delaware Forest Service offers a variety of tree seedlings each spring for landowners to reforest their property, and we organize an urban and community forestry program. 
[LEARN MORE about Reforest your own property, or help plant urban trees. ](https://agriculture.delaware.gov/forest-service/forest-conservation-programs/)
