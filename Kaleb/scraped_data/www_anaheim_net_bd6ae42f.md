#  Department Orders 
Department Orders establish and document the administrative guidelines and specific procedures of the Public Utilities Department consistent with City of Anaheim policies and applicable regulations. Department Orders collectively facilitate staff efficiency, effectiveness, and accountability by establishing and communicating standards and processes by individual and group responsibilities.
