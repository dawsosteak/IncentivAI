#  Food Assistance
The Supplemental Nutrition Assistance Program (SNAP) offers a monthly benefit to help those in need buy food and food products. Other programs, like the Food Distribution Program and Women, Infant and Children (WIC), may also be able to help. If you need help with food immediately, please reach out to your local food bank.

#  Utility Assistance
The Low Income Home Energy Assistance Program (LIHEAP) may be able to help eligible Missourians pay their home energy bill. LIHEAP also provides emergency services to those in crisis.

#  Healthcare
We may be able to help you with certain medical costs. If you are approved for help, you will have healthcare coverage through Missouri Medicaid (MO HealthNet). This healthcare coverage is different than Medicare and it can help with benefits not normally covered through that program, like nursing home care and personal care services.
**NOTE:** If you used to have healthcare coverage through MO HealthNet but you no longer do, you will need to apply for MO HealthNet again.
