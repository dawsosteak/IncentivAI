#  About 
## About the Anaheim Police Department
Established in 1875, the Anaheim Police Department is the largest police department in Orange County and is a full-service police department with approximately 750 total staff members, including 430 sworn police officers. The Anaheim Police Department is committed to providing the highest level of public safety services through proactive policing, professionalism, and community partnerships.
Services provided to the community include 9-1-1 emergency response, proactive law enforcement, traffic enforcement and collision investigation, crime investigations, community and school policing, operation and oversight of the city’s jail facility, as well as several speciality services, including air-support, K-9’s, gang suppression, narcotic enforcement, SWAT, mounted patrol, special event and emergency management, and forensics.
## Message from the Chief of Police
At the Anaheim Police Department, our top priority is public safety, and our mission is to ensure Anaheim remains a safe place to live, work, and play. We are committed to providing professional service to everyone in our community, guided by integrity, accountability, and respect. Our officers and staff work every day to build trust, respond to challenges, and protect the quality of life that makes Anaheim such a special place. We are proud to serve this community and remain focused on delivering the level of service you expect and deserve.
Chief Manny Cid
### [FAQs](https://pd.anaheim.net/Faq.aspx?TID=16) 

[ How do I file a compliment? ](https://pd.anaheim.net/27/About) 
    
If you want to share your experience and provide us with your feedback to help us recognize our department personnel for delivering exceptional service, please [complete the online form in English](https://pd.anaheim.net/FormCenter/Customer-Service-5/Customer-Service-Personnel-Compliment-En-46) or [complete the online form in Spanish](https://pd.anaheim.net/FormCenter/Customer-Service-5/Servicio-al-Cliente-Elogio-de-un-Emplead-47). 

[ How do I file a complaint? ](https://pd.anaheim.net/27/About) 
    
If you wish to file a formal complaint, please complete a Personnel Complaint Form. You may obtain this form at any of these locations:
  * Any police department station
  * The City Clerk's Office
  * Any Anaheim Public Library
  * The Community Services Office
  * Complete the [Personal Complaint Form (PDF)](https://pd.anaheim.net/DocumentCenter/View/139)


All forms should be submitted to:
Anaheim Police Department  
425 S Harbor Boulevard  
Anaheim, CA 92805
Or, you can [complete the form online in English](https://pd.anaheim.net/FormCenter/Customer-Service-5/Customer-Service-Personnel-Complaint-Eng-49), or [complete the form online in Spanish](https://pd.anaheim.net/FormCenter/Customer-Service-5/Customer-Service-Personnel-Complaint-Spa-48). 

[ What will happen to the officer / employee? ](https://pd.anaheim.net/27/About) 
    
It will depend on the situation and what the officer's or employee's actions were. If the employee's actions were criminal, he / she will be dealt with in the same manner as any other citizen. If the officer's actions were improper but not criminal, he / she may be disciplined. 

[ Will I be told of the results of the investigation? ](https://pd.anaheim.net/27/About) 
    
Yes. You will receive a letter from the Office of the Chief of Police advising you of the disposition of your complaint.
