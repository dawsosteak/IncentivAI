#  Beach Boulevard Specific Plan 
![Beach Boulevard specific plan banner](https://www.anaheim.net/ImageRepository/Document?documentID=2887)
![Ball](https://www.anaheim.net/ImageRepository/Document?documentID=24386)
  
  
On November 20, 2018, the City Council approved the BBSP and Final Environmental Impact Report (EIR). On December 18, 2018, the City Council adopted the related ordinances with the project becoming effective on January 17, 2019.
