#  Welcome to the Online Notary Public 
Apply for Notary Public Commission and manage renewals, payments, and exam scheduling  

1. Submit an application 
     Do this to apply for Notary Public Commission.  
Fee: $20 

2. Schedule an examination 
     Do this after successfully applying and you are instructed to schedule an exam.  
Fee: $10 

3. Order & pay for the commission 
     Do this after passing the exam.  
Fee: $100
* * *
Remote Online Notary (Optional) [What's this?](https://notary.ehawaii.gov/notary/public/welcome.html) 

1. Submit a RO Notary application 
     Do this to apply for Remote Online Notary.  
Fee: $20 

2. Schedule a RO Notary examination 
     Do this after successfully applying and you are instructed to schedule an exam.  
Fee: $10 

3. Order & pay for the RO Notary commission 
     Do this after passing the exam.  
Fee: $100

## Notary Office
Hours of Operation 
Monday-Friday (except holidays), between 8AM-12PM.   
Appointments are recommended. 
Parking Information 
In general, we **do not provide or validate parking**. 
For parking availability for exams, see our [Examination Schedule](https://notary.ehawaii.gov/notary/public/welcome.html) page. 
Please be informed that Act 54 Session Laws of Hawaii 2020 has become effective as of January 1, 2021 and HAR Chapter 5-11 has become effective on February 2022. Notaries public should read and educate themselves as to the most recent updates to the Notaries Public statute and administrative rules.   
Visit the Department of the Attorney General's [Notaries Public Documents](https://ag.hawaii.gov/notaries-public/notaries-public-documents/ "Notaries Public Documents") web page for info.

Remote Online Notary
A remote online notary public is a notary public located in the State of Hawaii that is able to perform a notarial act for a remotely located individual using communication technology.

About an eHawaii.gov Account
An eHawaii.gov account provides a single-sign-on service for you to access certain State of Hawaii _eHawaii.gov_ secure websites. 
With an eHawaii.gov account, many web services allow you to: 
  * Check the status of requests
  * Check your payment history
  * Manage your notifications
