#  Protective Fencing Rebate 
Anaheim Public Utilities (APU) is offering a rebate **up to $1000 towards the installation of protective fencing** around existing electrical equipment on private property to protect against vandalism and theft of wire.
In addition, to assist with building permit costs, APU offers **up to $200 towards obtaining a permit** for the fence installation. 
![fencing](https://www.anaheim.net/ImageRepository/Document?documentID=57371)
The approval process for new construction is a **coordinated effort between the Planning and Building Department, APU, and the customer's contractor**. 
### Program Steps:  
  

**1. Review** the [Protective Fencing Rebate Program Guidelines](https://www.anaheim.net/DocumentCenter/View/57373/Protective-Fencing-Rebate-Program-Guidelines) (page 1) and [Utility Equipment Screening Manual (UESM)](https://www.anaheim.net/DocumentCenter/View/57372/Utility-Equipment-Screening-Manual-UESM), including but not limited to Fencing – Section 3.02.
**2. Contact Electrical Engineering** at 714-765-5156 to inquire about Screening Equipment* requirements, such as necessary clearances to allow for operation and maintenance of equipment.
**3. Obtain a**[**permit for installation**](https://www.anaheim.net/5557/Fence-and-Wall-Zoning-Permit)
  * **A site plan** for the new construction will need to be submitted by the customer's contractor, with concurrence from the property owner to the Planning and Building Department including elevation details and landscape plans showing screening methods and how the utility equipment will fit within the area.


If you have **questions about the permitting process** , please call Planning Services at 714-765-5139.  
  

**4. Install your new fencing** in accordance with the building permit. 
  * After approval and during installation, a utility inspector will check for compliance with the UESM to ensure it meets the required clearances.
  * Request a building inspection according to your permit


**5.****Complete** the [Protective Fencing Rebate Program Application](https://www.anaheim.net/DocumentCenter/View/57373/Protective-Fencing-Rebate-Program) **and submit** supporting documents
Supporting documents include:
    * Completed rebate application
    * Copy of Planning Department permit card showing final sign-off
    * All fencing purchase and installation receipts
    * A current utility bill for the same address as fencing (optional)


**6. Send** or **Email** the completed rebate application, permit, paid invoice, and supporting documentation to: 
Protective Fencing Rebate Program  
201 S Anaheim Blvd., Suite 801  
Anaheim, CA 92805
