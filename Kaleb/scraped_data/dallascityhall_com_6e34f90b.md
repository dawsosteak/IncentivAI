# ​​Keep Dallas Beautiful Homepage
  

Keep Dallas Beautiful (KDB) is a 501 (c)(3) nonprofit focusing on the three main pillars of our affiliates, Keep Texas Beautiful and Keep America Beautiful: litter prevention, recycling, and beautification. Through our initiatives and renewed focus under Code Compliance Services, KDB aims to educate and engage residents in Dallas about the importance of a clean and healthy city.  

  

## Programs  

Community Hand Tool Program  

Dallas Code Compliance's Community Hand Tool Program is designed to provide volunteer groups with equipment to mow grass, cut vegetation, and clean litter in their neighborhoods. The tools are also available for clean-up projects and beautification events. This program serves all areas of the City of Dallas. The group must be a Non-Profit Organization, Neighborhood Association, or other Community Group to be eligible to participate. Volunteers must also sign an equipment/loaner rental agreement.  

[Learn More](https://dallascityhall.com/departments/codecompliance/Pages/hand-tool-program.aspx)
Love Your Block  

Love Your Block encourages Dallas residents to take pride in their neighborhoods and actively contribute to their improvement. Whether it is planting flowers, mowing a neighbor’s lawn, or picking up litter, there is no limit to the ways you can show love for your block. Although applications for this year’s Love Your Block mini-grant program have closed, there are still many ways we can support your efforts. Please take a moment to complete the survey below and let us know how you'd like to get involved—and how we can make next year’s mini-grant program even better.  

[Learn More](https://dallascityhall.com/departments/codecompliance/keep-Dallas-beautiful/Pages/love-your-block.aspx)
Adopt-A-Spot  

Adopt-A-Spot gives Dallas residents the opportunity to partner with Code Compliance and Keep Dallas Beautiful to care for a selected public space in the city for up to one year. By adopting a spot, participants commit to keeping the area clean and litter-free. Ready to make a difference in your community?   

[Learn More](https://dallascityhall.com/departments/codecompliance/keep-Dallas-beautiful/Pages/adopt-a-spot.aspx)  

Community Clean Trash-Off  

Twice a month, Code Compliance hosts a free city-wide cleanup event, giving Dallas residents the opportunity to responsibly dispose of bulk trash, tires, household hazardous waste, and documents for shredding. These events have helped remove thousands of pounds of debris from neighborhoods across Dallas, keeping our city cleaner, safer, and more beautiful. (Dates may change depending on the weather- Please review city calendar).  

[Learn More](https://dallascityhall.com/departments/codecompliance/keep-Dallas-beautiful/Pages/community-clean-trash-off.aspx)
