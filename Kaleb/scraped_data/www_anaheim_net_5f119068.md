#  Business Resource Guide 
As a community-owned, not-for-profit utility, Anaheim Public Utilities (APU) is dedicated to providing high-quality electric and water services at some of the most competitive rates in Orange County.
APU’s electric grid is approximately 65% underground with an ongoing conversion program to place overhead lines in major roadways underground. Reliable service is provided through 14 substations and high-speed communications. For very large, critical business operations, customers may invest in emergency circuits to minimize downtime.
Anaheim’s water system has high reliability and fire flows that support Anaheim Fire & Rescue’s Class 1 Insurance Services Office (ISO) rating. The Orange County groundwater basin provides the majority of APU’s water, and imported water provides the balance. Having both resources provides reliability and operational flexibility.
To learn about APU’s key performance metrics, visit [Anaheim.net/USI](https://www.anaheim.net/DocumentCenter/View/6234/Utilities-Success-Indicators).

## AFFORDABLE UTILITY SERVICE
  

### [COST-BASED RATES](https://www.anaheim.net/2003/Rates-Rules-Regulations)
APU offers cost-based rates that help businesses with affordable utility services. Rates recover the cost to operate, maintain, and invest in infrastructure. And for new electric customers or those seeking to expand their enterprise by 200 kilowatts (kW), APU provides an economic development rate that includes discounts to help you grow.
### [REBATES AND EFFICIENCY PROGRAMS](https://www.anaheim.net/2533/Commercial)
To help optimize your operations, our efficiency specialists offer expert guidance and personalized recommendations at no cost. Whether it’s upgrading to high-efficiency equipment or transitioning to drought-tolerant landscaping, we can help you identify improvements that reduce operating costs and extend equipment life.

## SAFETY & RELIABILITY
  

### [WILDFIRE MITIGATION](https://www.anaheim.net/5487/Wildfire-Mitigation-Plan)
Electric and water systems in high Fire Threat Zones are hardened, with 98% of electric lines already underground, as well as hydrants inspected and tested regularly. We work closely with public safety teams to continue making important investments, while addressing citywide safety, reliability and affordability.
### CAPITAL INVESTMENTS 
With more than 750 miles of water mains and 1,200 miles of electric lines, APU’s capital improvement program aims to enhance service reliability and replace aging infrastructure. This includes pipeline, hydrant, and valve replacements; as well as significant, ongoing investments in electric grid reliability, such as transformer replacements and undergrounding power lines.
### TECHNOLOGY SYSTEMS 
APU owns 136 miles of fiber optic lines to manage its critical electric and water systems, allowing fast detection and response to outages and the ability to re-route power through remote operations. Dark fiber is available for businesses to lease for high-speed internet access and other broadband services.

## SUSTAINABILITY OPTIONS
  

### [GREEN POWER](https://www.anaheim.net/6118/Green-Power)
This rate option is available to customers seeking to reduce their carbon footprint. APU offers higher levels of renewable energy that may be purchased at an additional cost to achieve 60% or 100% green power to cover customer loads.
### [ELECTRIFICATION](https://www.anaheim.net/6333/Commercial-EV-Programs)
APU provides no-cost assessments for electric fleet and building electrification. The assessment is an effective tool to plan out investments in electric infrastructure.
### [REGIONALLY RECYCLED WATER](https://www.anaheim.net/5817/Groundwater-Treatment-Program)
Anaheim pumps from the reliable OC Groundwater Basin—managed by Orange County Water District—that is recharged from the Santa Ana River and supplemented by recycled water to provide a sustainable, drought-proof water source.
### [REPLACING LAWNS](https://www.anaheim.net/6639/Turf-Replacement-Program-for-Commercial-)
APU assists businesses seeking to replace their turf with drought-tolerant landscaping with design assistance and rebates.
