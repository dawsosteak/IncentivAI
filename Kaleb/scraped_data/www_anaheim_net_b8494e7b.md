  1. [Attic Fan (Solar/Standard)](https://www.anaheim.net/5242/Building-Projects#tab2f228fbf-a365-4845-ac22-448fdd73fa72_0)


Solar or standard attic fans run on sun power and pull superheated air out of your attic to be replaced by cooler outside air. This reduces the amount of work your HVAC system has to do to cool your upstairs living area, which is especially useful if you have an upstairs bonus room or bedroom that gets uncomfortably hot in summer. 
  

## Installation/Efficiency Criteria 
  

  * Solar: permanently mounted on the roof or in the attic
  * Standard: permanently mounted on a roof or in the attic with a smart thermostat

  1. [Exterior Wall Insulation](https://www.anaheim.net/5242/Building-Projects#tab2f228fbf-a365-4845-ac22-448fdd73fa72_1)


Exterior wall insulation reduces the transfer of cold or warm air by filling the space between your walls with a layer of fiber material. Many older homes have little to no exterior wall insulation, resulting in unnecessarily high heating and cooling energy costs. Retrofitting exterior wall insulation can yield significant energy savings as well as enhanced comfort.   

##    
​Installation/Efficiency Criteria 
  

  * Adding a minimum of R-13+

  

## ​Incentive Amount   

$0.10 per sq. ft.

  1. [High Performance Windows](https://www.anaheim.net/5242/Building-Projects#tab2f228fbf-a365-4845-ac22-448fdd73fa72_2)


Heat gain and loss through windows account for up to 50% of a home’s heating and cooling needs. Many technological improvements have been made in recent years that have advanced the insulating quality of windows which can help lower your energy costs.
  

## Installation/Efficiency Criteria 
  

  * [ENERGY STAR](https://www.energystar.gov/products/residential_windows_doors_and_skylights/key_product_criteria#:~:text=With%20the%20equivalent%20energy%20performance,than%20or%20equal%20to%200.42)® certified
  * U-Factor of 0.28 or less 
  * Solar Heat Gain Coefficient of 0.23 or less 


  

## Incentive Amount 
$1 per sq. ft.
  

  

[Click here](https://www.anaheim.net/1481/Home-Incentives-Rebate-Guidelines-Applic) for guidelines and how to apply.

  1. [Window Film](https://www.anaheim.net/5242/Building-Projects#tab2f228fbf-a365-4845-ac22-448fdd73fa72_3)


There are many different grades, shades, colors, and thicknesses of window films built to offer solutions to a variety of challenges including heat and glare reduction, thermal insulation, UV filtration and even safety and security.   
  

## ​Installation/Efficiency Criteria 
  

  * Solar Heat Gain Coefficient of 0.39 or less
  * Only single-pane, exterior windows qualify. North-facing windows excluded

  

## ​Incentive Amount  

$1.00 per sq. ft.

  1. [Fire Resistant Attic Insulation](https://www.anaheim.net/5242/Building-Projects#tab2f228fbf-a365-4845-ac22-448fdd73fa72_4)


Fire-resistant attic insulation is a layer of fiber that reduces the risk of fires starting in the attic while at the same time decrease the energy demands of heating and cooling systems. The effectiveness of this insulation is evaluated by its Class Fire Rating and R-Value, which quantifies heat exchange resistance.   
  
​
## Installation/Efficiency Criteria
  

  * Class 1 (A) Fire Rating   
(Invoice must detail Fire Spread 0-25)
  * R-30 rating or greater  



## ​Incentive Amount   

$0.35 per sq. ft.  
Rebate cap of $750  
  

  
[Click here](https://www.anaheim.net/1481/Home-Incentives-Rebate-Guidelines-Applic) for guidelines and how to apply.

  1. [Fire Resistant Vents](https://www.anaheim.net/5242/Building-Projects#tab2f228fbf-a365-4845-ac22-448fdd73fa72_5)


These vents are designed to block embers and fire from entering the home through the attic during extreme situations such as wildfires or local vegetation fires
  

Installation/Efficiency Criteria 
  * Installation of Fire Resistant Attic Insulation is Recommended
  * Product must be approved under the [California Fire Marshal’s Building Materials Listing Program for use in Wildland Urban Interface Areas ](https://osfm.fire.ca.gov/what-we-do/fire-engineering-and-investigations/building-materials-listing)
