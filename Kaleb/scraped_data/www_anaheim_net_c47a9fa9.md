#  City Administration 
## Anaheim city manager
  
Anaheim's city manager is responsible for the daily operations of one of California’s largest cities with a $2.4 billion annual budget, 350,000 residents, 20,000 businesses and 25 million yearly visitors.
The city manager works with Anaheim’s mayor and City Council to implement their policy direction for the city.
Greg Garcia is currently interim city manager.
The city manager oversees 13 city departments, including Anaheim Public Utilities, the Anaheim Police Department, Anaheim Fire & Rescue and Convention, Sports & Entertainment, operator of the Anaheim Convention Center.
