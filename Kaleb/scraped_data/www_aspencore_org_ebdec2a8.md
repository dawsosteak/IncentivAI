## Grow your skill set while driving meaningful climate solutions in the Roaring Fork Valley.

Join us at CORE and be part of a team that’s driving real change in our community. CORE bridges the gap between what is aspirational and attainable. ###  Why Work at CORE?  

At CORE, you’ll be part of a mission-driven team working to make our community cleaner, healthier, and more sustainable—all while enjoying great benefits and work-life balance.
  * Live and work in the Roaring Fork Valley with access to outdoor adventures and a sustainability-focused community.
  * Flexible work scheduling, generous paid time off, and 10 paid holidays per year.
  * Zero premium, low-deductible health insurance, plus affordable dental and vision options.
  * $750 annual health benefit that can be applied to a discounted ski pass.
  * 3% Simple IRA retirement match to help you plan for the future.
