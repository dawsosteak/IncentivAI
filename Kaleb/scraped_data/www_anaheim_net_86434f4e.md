#  Event Ticket Distribution Disclosures (Form 802) 
In accordance with the provisions set forth in [Title 2, Division 6 of the California Code of Regulations, Section 18944.1 Gifts: Agency Provided Tickets or Passes](https://www.fppc.ca.gov/content/dam/fppc/NS-Documents/LegalDiv/Regulations/Index/Chapter9-5/18944.1.GiftsAgency-Provided-Tickets-or-Passes.pdf) and the City of Anaheim Ticket Policy, Agency Report of: Ceremonial Role Events and Ticket/Pass Distributions (Form 802) are posted within 30 days of distribution of tickets.
_*The filings listed below are from January 1, 2022 to present._
