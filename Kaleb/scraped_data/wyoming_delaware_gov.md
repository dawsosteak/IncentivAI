## No Election Notice
TOWN OF WYOMING, DELAWARE PUBLIC NOTICE MUNICIPAL ELECTION 2026 The Town of Wyoming will NOT be having a council election on February 28, 2026 Council Positions: One (1) Mayor seat with a term of three (3) years (March 2026 to March 2029) Only one (1) candidate filed for this position- filing the open seat: Doug Denison Two (2) Council seats with a term of office of two (2) years (March 2026 to March 2028) Only two (2) candidates filed for these positions – filling the open seats Tracy Johovic Kyle Dixon The incumbents will be sworn into their respective positions at the March 2026 Council Meeting. For more information, please contact the office staff between normal office hours at Town Hall, 120 W. Camden Wyoming Ave., Wyoming, DE or by telephone at 302-697-2966.
