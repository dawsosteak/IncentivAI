#####  Highest-Priority Strategies
  * #### Establish an inter-agency working group to identify opportunities to improve, introduce, and better coordinate multi-modal transportation options
  * #### Design city streets for multi-modal travel to accommodate the travel needs of all users of the public way
  * #### Develop a "Safe Routes to School" initiative for pedestrians and bicycle users
