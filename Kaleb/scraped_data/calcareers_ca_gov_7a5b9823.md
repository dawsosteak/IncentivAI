System Maintenance - CalCareers:  CalHR will perform two scheduled CalCareers system maintenance windows on Thursday April 23, 2026. From 7:00 AM - 9:00 AM, CalCareers will experience a full outage. From 9:00 PM to 12:00 AM, some online exams will be unavailable. We apologize for the inconvenience. 
Senior Legal Typist and Legal Secretary Exams:  The Senior Legal Typist and Legal Secretary exams will be going offline starting Friday, April 10, 2026, by 8:00 AM. The new exams are anticipated to be online Wednesday, April 29, 2026. We apologize for any inconvenience. 
CalHR Job Center:  The CalHR Job Center, located at 1810 16th Street, Sacramento, CA 95811, is open every Tuesday. Our hours are 9:00 AM - 12:00 PM and 1:00 PM - 3:00 PM. A staff member will be available to assist you in navigating the CalCareer website for job opportunities. This is not a job fair event. 
Senior and Supervising Telecommunications Engineer Examinations:  The Senior Telecommunications Engineer and Supervising Telecommunications Engineer exams will be going offline starting Monday, May 4, 2026, by 8:00 AM. The exams are anticipated to be online Thursday, May 7, 2026. We apologize for any inconvenience. 
Career Counseling:  The CalCareers Team now provides career counseling services to State job seekers. For more information and to make an appointment, please see the Career Center section of the Help/Tutorials page.
