# Embrace a World-Class **Quality of Life**
![An outdoor cafe patio in a historic Pennsylvania town on a cloudy day](https://s7d9.scene7.com/is/image/statepa/Warren-MainStreet-1?fmt=webp)
## In Pennsylvania, we don't just work hard — we live life to the fullest.
Whether you’re seeking an urban cultural center or a quieter rural life, a lifestyle that fits you awaits in PA — a state that wholeheartedly embraces the importance of work-life balance.
We do more than just work hard — we live fully. Ranked among the top ten states for [quality of life(opens in a new tab)](https://wallethub.com/edu/best-states-to-live-in/62617 "Link to Wallethub article")​, PA provides a perfect blend of affordability and access to culture and recreation. From vibrant cities like Philadelphia and Pittsburgh to charming suburbs and small towns regularly recognized on [best-of lists(opens in a new tab)](https://www.niche.com/places-to-live/search/best-places-to-live/s/pennsylvania/ "Link to Niche.com article"), this is a place where both your employees and your family can thrive.
We’re proud to be home to world-class education and healthcare systems, including [two of the top children’s hospitals in the country(opens in a new tab)](https://health.usnews.com/health-news/best-childrens-hospitals/articles/best-childrens-hospitals-honor-roll-and-overview "Link to US News health article")​. Pennsylvania’s commitment to education shines brightly throughout the state with its [high-performing school districts(opens in a new tab)](https://www.niche.com/k12/search/best-school-districts/s/pennsylvania/ "Link to Niche.com school districts article") and [seven of the top 100 universities(opens in a new tab)](https://www.usnews.com/best-colleges/pa?_sort=rank&_sortDirection=asc) in the U.S.
Close to both the Midwest and the East Coast, the Keystone State's prime location — paired with our [reputation for stunning outdoor scenery(opens in a new tab)](https://10best.usatoday.com/awards/best-destination-for-fall-colors/ "Link to USA Today article") — make it a great state to grow your business while enjoying a high quality of life. World-class hiking, camping, skiing and countless other outdoor activities are only a short drive away from our bustling cities and cozy small towns, so you can enjoy all the benefits that Pennsylvania has to offer no matter where you want to live,
Pennsylvania has also earned a reputation as a housing haven, where affordability reigns supreme. Whether you prefer to live in [bustling urban centers like Pittsburgh(opens in a new tab)](https://www.realtor.com/news/trends/pittsburgh-pennsylvania-most-affordable-large-housing-market/), or growing cities like Harrisburg and Lancaster, PA boasts a low cost of living compared to many northeastern states. In fact, we're one of the [most affordable northeastern states for first-time homebuyers(opens in a new tab)](https://www.realtor.com/advice/hyperlocal/salary-you-need-to-buy-home-pennsylvania/), putting homeownership in reach for more people and making it easier to enjoy a high quality of life here, no matter what you do.
> The collaborative nature of businesses, economic, educational, and philanthropic organizations in the area that continually work to improve the quality of life here make it an outstanding location for our company, our employees, and their families."
_— Seifi Ghasemi, CEO of Air Products (Lehigh Valley)_

WalletHub ranks Pennsylvania among the [top ten states for quality of life(opens in a new tab)](https://wallethub.com/edu/best-states-to-live-in/62617 "Link to Wallethub Quality of Life ranking"), and CNBC puts us in the [top 10 for affordability(opens in a new tab)](https://www.cnbc.com/2024/07/12/americas-10-cheapest-states-to-live-in.html) — the only state on the East Coast to make the list. Suburbs like Chesterbrook earn accolades as the [best places to raise a family(opens in a new tab)](https://www.niche.com/places-to-live/search/best-places-for-families/ "Link to Niche Best Places for Family article"), with four Pennsylvania communities being among the best in the country. And for first-time homebuyers, we're one of the [most affordable(opens in a new tab)](https://www.realtor.com/advice/hyperlocal/salary-you-need-to-buy-home-pennsylvania/) Northeastern states to buy a home.
![An aerial image of Bedford, PA. Buildings, roads and foliage extend into the distance, surrounded by forest-covered mountains and a blue sky.](https://s7d9.scene7.com/is/image/statepa/25986_gov_keystonecomm_erd_004)
![](https://s7d9.scene7.com/is/image/statepa/Penn_State_adobestock_533167539)
On top of our continued investments in our K-12 schools, Pennsylvania's ecosystem of nearly 300 postsecondary institutions has something for everyone. We're home to three of the nation's [top 50 universities(opens in a new tab)](https://www.usnews.com/best-colleges/pa?_sort=rank&_sortDirection=asc "US News rankings for top universities"), but innovation is happening everywhere - from the thriving robotics and engineering scene in Pittsburgh, to the cutting-edge life sciences research happening in Lehigh Valley.
Pennsylvania colleges are recognized for their track record of [producing future leaders(opens in a new tab)](https://time.com/7333710/best-colleges-future-leaders-2026/), giving companies a robust talent pipeline to help them grow. We're also continuously investing in workforce development programs, helping support careers in our in-demand industires - from manufacturing to agriculture, and beyond.
![](https://s7d9.scene7.com/is/image/statepa/Penn_State_adobestock_533167539)
![A crowd explore a colorful arts and culure festival on a closed-off street in downtown Lebanon, PA.](https://s7d9.scene7.com/is/image/statepa/lebo%20unveiled)
Whatever your interests are, you'll never run out things to experience in Pennsylvania. For art aficionados, PA is home to over 80 art museums, including the iconic [Philadelphia Museum of Art(opens in a new tab)](https://www.philamuseum.org/ "Link to Philadelphia Museum of Art website") and the [Andy Warhol Museum(opens in a new tab)](https://www.warhol.org/ "Andy Warhol Museum official website"). We're also home to world-class music venues hosting local talent like the [Philadelphia Orchestra(opens in a new tab)](https://philorch.ensembleartsphilly.org/ "The Philadelphia Orchestra official website"), and big-name bands regularly make tour stops at venues like [Hersheypark Stadium(opens in a new tab)](https://www.hersheyentertainment.com/hersheypark-stadium/ "Link to Hersheypark Stadium official website").
For foodies hungry for something new, check out Pennsylvania's [Michelin-star restaurants(opens in a new tab)](https://www.abc27.com/pennsylvania/pennsylvania-earns-first-michelin-star-restaurants/), serving up unforgettable dining experiences. We also boast [over 530 craft breweries(opens in a new tab)](https://www.brewersassociation.org/statistics-and-data/state-craft-beer-stats/?state=PA), ranking third in the nation, and [more than 400 wineries(opens in a new tab)](https://pennsylvaniawine.com/about "Link to Pennsylvania WIneries official website"). Whatever your drink of choice, cheers to that.
![A crowd explore a colorful arts and culure festival on a closed-off street in downtown Lebanon, PA.](https://s7d9.scene7.com/is/image/statepa/lebo%20unveiled)
![A riverside town nestled among the mountains during autumn.](https://s7d9.scene7.com/is/image/statepa/tidioute-warrencovisitorsbureau)
From water trails and hiking, to snowsports and biking, Pennsylvania is full of opportunities to get the blood pumping and to soak in Vitamin D. With 86,000 miles of streams and rivers, over 6,700 miles of hiking trails, 125 free state parks, and 20 ski resorts, there are outdoor opportunities for everyone. Thrill seekers can take on hiking paths on the [Appalachian Trail(opens in a new tab)](https://appalachiantrail.org/explore/explore-by-state/pennsylvania/ "Link to Applachian Trail official website") or take a lift up to [Blue Knob Ski Resort(opens in a new tab)](https://blueknob.com/ "Link to official website fro Blue Knob") for the most challenging slopes in the state, while relaxation enthusiasts can enjoy beaches at [Presque Isle State Park(opens in a new tab)](https://www.pa.gov/agencies/dcnr/recreation/where-to-go/state-parks/find-a-park/presque-isle-state-park.html "Link to Presque Isle State Park website") or birdwatch at the famous [Hawk Mountain(opens in a new tab)](https://www.hawkmountain.org/ "Link to Hawk Mountain official website").
Our four seasons bring the fun year-round — whether it’s leaf-peeping in the fall, skiing in the winter, or kayaking in the summer. Whether you want adventure or escape, PA has no shortage of ways to get some fresh air away from it all.
