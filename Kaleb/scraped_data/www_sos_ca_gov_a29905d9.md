If you have a Election Voter Complaint to file, please complete the form below and submit it to the Secretary of State, Elections Division staff.
**All fields marked with an asterisk (*) are required.**
## **Complainant Information**
Full Name *
Street Address (Include Apt # if applicable)
City
State
Daytime Phone Number (include area code) *
Evening Phone Number (include area code)
Email Address *
## **Person(s) or Organization(s) Against Whom Complaint is Brought**
Name(s)
Organization(s)
Position(s) of person(s) (if applicable)
## **Statement of Facts**
Date(s) of alleged event(s)
Time(s) of alleged event(s)
Location(s) of alleged event(s)
Name and phone numbers of witnesses or other victims (if applicable)
