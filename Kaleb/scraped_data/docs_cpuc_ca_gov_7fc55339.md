**FOR SOUTHERN CALIFORNIA GAS COMPANY**
ADMINISTRATIVE LAW JUDGE’S RULING CLARIFYING INSTRUCTIONS
* * *
This Ruling clarifies that Southern California Gas Company shall jointly file the Competitive Solicitation Working Group’s Status and Final Reports with Pacific Gas and Electric Company, San Diego Gas & Electric Company, and Southern California Edison Company. On March 24, 2016, an Administrative Law Judge Ruling was issued establishing a working group in this proceeding for the purpose of developing a competitive solicitation framework to target the reliability needs within the areas identified by analysis performed in Rulemaking 14-08-013 et al. The Ruling also directed Utility representatives on the Working Group to file a status report, on June 1, 2016 and a final report on August 1, 2016. The language within the Ruling clearly stated that while the Working Group is directed to jointly develop each report, the Utilities, would file the status report on June 1, 2016 and the final report on August 1, 2016. The
* * *
> “Utilities” were indicated as Pacific Gas and Electric Company (PG&E),
**FILED**
San Diego Gas & Electric Company (SDG&E;), Southern California Edison Company (SCE) and Southern California Gas Company (SoCalGas). However, in the Ruling Paragraph, SoCalGas was inadvertently omitted from the list of 5-13-16 01:07 PM
* * *
R.14-10-003 KHY/ek4
* * *
- 2 Utilities required to jointly file the Status and Final Reports on behalf of the Working Group. This Ruling clarifies that the omission was inadvertent and that SoCalGas is directed to jointly file both reports with the other three utilities on behalf of the Working Group. IT IS RULED that Southern California Gas Company shall jointly file the Competitive Solicitation Working Group Status and Final Reports with Pacific Gas and Electric Company, San Diego Gas & Electric Company, and Southern California Edison Company. Dated May 13, 2016, at San Francisco, California.
* * *
