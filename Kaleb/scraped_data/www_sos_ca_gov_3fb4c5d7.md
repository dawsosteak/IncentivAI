# Service Options
## Service Options - Business Entities
### **Online**
Fastest Service is provided online at [bizfileOnline.sos.ca.gov](http://bizfileOnline.sos.ca.gov) including 24-Hour Expedite ($350) and Same-Day ($750). Online services include all filings for Corporations, Limited Liability Companies, Limited Partnerships, Cooperatives, Name Reservations, Foreign Name Reservations, Unincorporated Common Interest Developments, and Out of State Associations including initial filings, statements of information, amendments, terminations, mergers and conversions. Certificates of Status and Certified Copies are available online at [bizfileOnline.sos.ca.gov](http://bizfileonline.sos.ca.gov/).
### **Drop-Off Service (Special Handling Service)**
A request dropped off in person in Sacramento, 1500 11th Street, Monday – Friday 8:00 a.m. to 5:00 p.m. except state holidays will receive priority processing over requests submitted by mail. Drop-off requests must include a separate, non–refundable special handling fee for each request. 
  * $15 special handling fee is applicable for each filing request.
  * $10 special handling fee is applicable for each copy (order) request.


**Note:** Special handling fees are in addition to applicable filing fees, reservation fees, or order fees. Special handling fees are retained whether requests are approved or rejected. The special handling fee does not apply to Statements of Information, Statements by Common Interest Development Association, or any request submitted online or by mail.
### **Preclearance Services**
A customer may submit a business entity document for preclearance prior to an intended filing date, requesting completion of preclearance within 24 hours.
  * **24–Hour** Preclearance Service - **$500.00** : _Drop-Off-Only in Sacramento_. A customer may submit a business entity document for preclearance prior to an intended filing date, requesting completion of preclearance within 24 hours. Preclearance response guaranteed within 24 hours (e.g., submitted Monday at 11:00 a.m., preclearance response available Tuesday by 11:00 a.m.), excluding weekends and state holidays.


### **Expedite Services**
A customer may submit a business entity document for expedited filing, requesting completion in one of three of the below-specified time frames.
  * **24–Hour** Filing Service (Class C) - **$350.00** : 
  * **Same Day** Filing Service (Class B) - **$750.00** : Online or Drop-Off in Sacramento. Filing response guaranteed by 4:00 p.m. the same day. Note: The document must be received by the Secretary of State by 9:30 a.m., and a filing confirmation or filing response will be available the same day by 4:00 p.m.
  * **4–Hour** Filing Service**(Class A) -****$500.00******: Drop-Off-Only in Sacramento. Filing response guaranteed within 4 hours (e.g., submitted Monday at 10:00 a.m., filing confirmation or filing response available Monday by 2:00 p.m.) Note: A document must be precleared and approved to be eligible for the 4-Hour Filing Service.****


******A document submitted to the Secretary of State for expedited filing will be reviewed and a filing response will be provided within the time frame requested, indicating whether the document has been filed or rejected. A document must be**precleared and approved to be eligible for 4-Hour expedite service.** If the document is rejected, the filing response will state the reason(s) for the rejection. A customer may request delivery of the filing confirmation or filing response by one of the following means: electronically or customer pick up.******
