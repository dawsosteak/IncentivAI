#### [**Walk-In Court (Proof or Plea):**](https://dallascityhall.com/departments/courtdetentionservices/Pages/Walk-In-and-Past-Due.aspx)
#### This court is available to any person who chooses to resolve their citation(s) through entering a plea of no contest, guilty or showing proof of compliance.
  

#### Walk-in court (proof or plea) is open and accessible to the public as a first come first served docket. Sign-in begins at 7:30 a.m. and the court opens at 8:30 a.m. There is a limited number of people that can be scheduled per docket. Once the morning docket is full, you can wait on the 1:00 p.m. docket or come back another day. Once the afternoon docket is full, you can return another business day to sign up for the walk-in court (proof or plea).

#### Options for Walk-In Court (Proof or Plea)
  * #### Plead [**guilty or no contest**](https://dallascityhall.com/departments/courtdetentionservices/Pages/How-Much,-Pleas-and-Links.aspx#Plea) to the citation(s); 
  * ####  [**Compliance / Dismissal proof**](https://dallascityhall.com/departments/courtdetentionservices/Pages/Compliance-and-Dismissals-Information.aspx) ;
  * #### [**Pay the fine**](https://dallascityhall.com/departments/courtdetentionservices/Pages/Pay-Your-Ticket.aspx), ([**payment plans**](https://dallascityhall.com/departments/courtdetentionservices/Pages/Payment-Plan.aspx) may be available upon request);
  * #### Request a pre-trial hearing (**see details below); 
  * #### Request [**community service**](https://dallascityhall.com/departments/courtdetentionservices/Pages/Community-Service-Information.aspx) or [**work release**](https://dallascityhall.com/departments/courtdetentionservices/Pages/Work-Release-Information.aspx); 
  * #### Request [**deferred disposition**](https://dallascityhall.com/departments/courtdetentionservices/Pages/Deferred-Disposition-Home.aspx) (probation); 
  * #### Request to take [**DSC (driver's safety course)**](https://dallascityhall.com/departments/courtdetentionservices/Pages/Driving-Safety-Course-Home.aspx) (Once you are past the 21 days, the amount may be higher or this option may no longer be available.); 
  * #### Submit request and proof for [**time served**](https://dallascityhall.com/departments/courtdetentionservices/Pages/Time-Served-Information.aspx).


#### [**Past Due Hearing Request:**](https://dallascityhall.com/departments/courtdetentionservices/Pages/Walk-In-and-Past-Due.aspx)
  

#### If it is past the first 21 days of issuance of the citation, you must appear in person at the Court window to enter a plea of not guilty. A cash bond in the amount owed will be required to request a pre-trial date once you are past due.
####    
You may also consult and or hire an attorney to represent you on your citation(s).
