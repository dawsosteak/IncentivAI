#  La Palma Water Complex 
![La Palma 2](https://www.anaheim.net/ImageRepository/Document?documentID=23304)
![La Palma 1](https://www.anaheim.net/ImageRepository/Document?documentID=23305)
The La Palma Complex serves the residences and businesses of Central and West Anaheim providing water supply and peak pumping capacity to fight fires and meet maximum day water demands. The facilities include the followings:
  * (1) 4 million gallon (4 MG) water storage reservoir that stores water
  * (1) well that pumps groundwater to fill reservoir
  * (1) pump Station that pumps water from reservoir to water distribution system


The dedication for the La Palma Water Complex was held on July 31, 2018.
