#  Outreach Events 
Anaheim Public Utilities has a presence at over 40 community events a year, ranging from resource fairs to community festivals. We value the opportunity to visit our customers, provide information on our many programs, and answer questions. For more details on events near you, call 714-765-4250.
Don’t forget to follow our Anaheim Public Utilities [Facebook](https://www.facebook.com/anaheimutilities) and [Instagram](https://www.instagram.com/anaheimutilities/) pages to stay up to date on upcoming community events and special promotions.
![](https://www.anaheim.net/ImageRepository/Document?documentID=53943)
> Anaheim Public Utilities promotional items, contests, and opportunity drawings are open to Anaheim residents only (proof of residency may be required).
> City of Anaheim employees and their immediate family are not eligible to participate.
