# Special Filings
The Secretary of State administers numerous miscellaneous filings made pursuant to specific statutory requirements. Miscellaneous filings include:
  * Registry of Public Agencies  
(California Government Code section [53051](http://leginfo.legislature.ca.gov/faces/codes_displaySection.xhtml?lawCode=GOV&sectionNum=53051.))  
  

  * City and County Charters and Amendments  
(California Government Code sections [34460](http://leginfo.legislature.ca.gov/faces/codes_displaySection.xhtml?lawCode=GOV&sectionNum=34460.) & [23713](http://leginfo.legislature.ca.gov/faces/codes_displaySection.xhtml?lawCode=GOV&sectionNum=23713.))  
  

  * Joint Powers Agreements and Amendments  
(California Government Code section [6503.5](http://leginfo.legislature.ca.gov/faces/codes_displaySection.xhtml?lawCode=GOV&sectionNum=6503.5.))  
  

  * Registration of Claim as Successor–In–Interest  
(California Civil Code section [3344.1](http://leginfo.legislature.ca.gov/faces/codes_displaySection.xhtml?lawCode=CIV&sectionNum=3344.1.))  
  

  * Immigration Consultants  
(California Business and Professions Code section [22443.1](http://leginfo.legislature.ca.gov/faces/codes_displaySection.xhtml?lawCode=BPC&sectionNum=22443.1.))  
  

  * Specific business bonds and other "not so common" documents received pursuant to statutory requirements.
