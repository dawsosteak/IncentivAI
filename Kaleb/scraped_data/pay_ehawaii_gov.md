## What is Hawaii.govPayments?
Designed from the ground up **for the people of Hawaiʻi** , Hawaii.gov Payments is built on a rock-solid foundation of experience in processing secure payments since 2000 by NIC and Tyler Technologies. Read about [NIC's payments services](https://nichawaii.egov.com/payments).
Hawaii.gov Payments provides a simple, secure system to pay government invoices online. Track payment history, pay multiple invoices at once, and sign up to receive alerts when invoice are ready to pay.
**Select or search for a service** to get started.
