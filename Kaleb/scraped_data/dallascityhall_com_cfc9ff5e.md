# Employment Opportunities
Join the Dallas City Attorney's Office: Seeking Talented and Committed Individuals to Serve the Public  

  

**Why Work for the Dallas City Attorney’s Office?**
The Dallas City Attorney’s Office is one of the largest municipal legal departments in the country, representing the City of Dallas across a diverse range of legal matters. Our office provides both transactional and advisory services as general counsel, and we manage the majority of the City’s litigation in-house.
  

Joining our team offers the opportunity to be part of a dynamic, high-impact environment, contributing to the legal needs of one of the nation's most vibrant and fast-growing cities.  

  

  

**The Dallas City Attorney’s Office Offers the Following Benefits:**
  * Hybrid Work Schedule  

  * 12 Paid Holidays
  * Paid Parental Leave
  * Employee Retirement Fund  

  * Sick and Vacation Leave
  * Wellness Incentive Program
  * Employee Assistance Program  



**How to Apply**
To apply, please submit the following materials to  
ATTResumes@dallas.gov  

  

  * Resume
  * Cover letter
  * Writing sample (no more than 10 pages)  



In your email, kindly specify the job position you are applying for. 
**Note:** A writing sample is required for all attorney positions.
