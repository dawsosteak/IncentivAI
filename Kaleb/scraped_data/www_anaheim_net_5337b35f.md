#  Nature Nights 
  1. ![Slideshow NN](https://www.anaheim.net/ImageRepository/Document?documentID=69654)


**Meet experts from a variety of natural fields. Each evening begins with a twilight walk at 7:00 pm led by our staff guides, followed by a formal presentation at 7:30 pm.**
**2026 Summer Dates: Every Wednesday from June 24 - August 5**
****June 24- Reptiles From Around the World****
****July 1-******O****range County Birds of Prey**
**July 8- Wild Wonders**
**July 15- Doodlebug Animal Adventures**
**July 22- Fire and Ice**
**July 29- Our Solar System**
**August 5- Flashlight Walk**
## Registration & Admission
Admission is **free****, but** **r****egistration is required** , and a $5 donation per family is suggested.
[Online registration](https://secure.rec1.com/CA/city-of-anaheim/catalog/index/5319d52dd9b5999941925a93e21df0f3?filter=c2VhcmNoPU5hdHVyZSUyME5pZ2h0cw==) for Nature Nights opens May 4.
