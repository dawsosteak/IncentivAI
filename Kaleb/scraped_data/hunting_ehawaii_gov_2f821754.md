## Aloha and E Komo Mai!
Hawai`i Hunting licenses are now available online! Your license will be instantly issued after you successfully complete the purchase process.
Click [here](javascript:newWindow\("/hunting/hunting_req.html"\);) to view the requirements to purchase a hunting license online. 
**For all non-resident hunters purchasing a Hawaii hunting license for the first time**, please go to the "Non-resident Exemption Request Form" link above and complete the online form. Once processed, an email notification will be sent with additional instructions.
This is a secure site. Steps have been taken to ensure that all personal and credit card information entered here will not be used fraudulently.
## License Type and Cost Structure 
Licenses purchased are valid from the date of purchase through Fiscal Year 2026 (Date of purchase - June 30, 2026) *ALL SALES FINAL - NO REFUNDS
| Resident | Non-Resident | Senior | Kalaupapa Patient   
---|---|---|---|---  
Hunting License  
including Wildlife Conservation Stamp* | $20.00 | $105.00 | $10.00 | $10.00  
Game Bird Stamp* | $10.00 | $10.00 | $10.00 | $10.00  
* Both Wildlife Conservation Stamp and Game Bird Stamp are offered as colored images and printed as part of the electronic license.
**A valid Hawaii hunting license is required for all hunting in the state, and the resident and non-resident licenses are for sale at this site. The cost is $10 for residents and $95 for non-residents.**
