# Celebrating Our Renovated Basalt Office
After several months of construction, CORE's newly renovated Basalt office is officially open. Yesterday's open house brought familiar faces, new connections, and great conversations about CORE's ambitious plans for 2026. With office renovations complete, CORE is excited to be working with the building owners, Harry Teague Architects, and Basalt River Lofts HOA, to upgrade the building's mechanical system. With assistance from CORE and Holy Cross, the natural gas boilers that heat the building will be replaced with cold-climate air-to-water heat pumps to improve energy efficiency and indoor/outdoor air quality.

#  Stop Losing Heat Every Winter
If your home or building is drafty, you could be losing heat and money all winter. To identify solutions, contact CORE to set up a home energy assessment. After an assessment, a CORE Energy Concierge will help you make a plan to improve the energy efficiency of your home through upgrades like air sealing, insulation, or heat pumps. CORE funding helps make improvements more affordable across Pitkin, Eagle, and Garfield Counties.
  

Rebates for homes up to $10,000  
Grants for businesses up to $200,000
  

Home energy assessments are FREE for community priority participants and $100 for all other residences.
  

Upgrade once. Save every winter.
