#  Utilities Permit Assistant 
## Permit Process Help
  

  * Have a construction project that involves new or upgraded connections to Anaheim’s electric and water system? 
  * Not sure where to start with the permitting and inspection process?


The utilities permit assistant service is designed to help you through the permitting process for your residential, commercial or industrial project. Our goal is to reduce the time and cost to obtain your project permits and inspection approvals.   
  
Contact the Utilities Permit Assistant today via email or phone at 714-765-5006.
