TRENDS
* * *
Since the last 15-Day Report of Registration in a presidential general election year (October 20, 2008):
* * *
 The total voter registration in the state increased from 17,304,091 to 18,245,970.  The percentage of the total number of registered voters compared to the number of people who are eligible to register to vote increased from 74.6% to 76.7%.  The percentage of voters who have no party preference increased from 19.9% to 20.9%.  The percentage of people registered with a qualified political party decreased from 79.4% to 77.2%.  The percentage of voters registered with the Democratic Party decreased from 44.4% to 43.7%.  The percentage of voters registered with the Republican Party decreased from 31.4% to 29.4%.
