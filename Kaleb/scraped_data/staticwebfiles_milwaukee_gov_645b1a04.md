##### WHAT CAN I DO?
Use the resources below to be more sustainable in your home or business, and to help your neighborhood. You can help us reach our goals!
  * [The Harbor District:](http://harbordistrict.org/) The Harbor District Initiative brings together local, state, and federal government efforts, private sector interests, and community enthusiasm to achieve a world-class revitalization 
  * [UWM:](http://www4.uwm.edu/power/designers-take-on-the-inner-harbor) University of Wisconsin-Milwaukee develops ideas for on Inner Harbor Redevelopment 
  * [Milwaukee Journal Sentinel:](http://www.jsonline.com/business/milwaukee-looks-to-revive-inner-harbor-area-in-walkers-point-b9975947z1-220050791.html) Coverage of the Inner Harbor Redevelopment efforts 


Share your commitment with others. Use #ReFreshMKE to show us how you're ReFreshing Milwaukee through social media, and see how others are part of #ReFreshMKE.
