# Resources and Historical Information
Choose Language 
English español
## [History of California Initiatives](https://www.sos.ca.gov/elections/ballot-measures/resources-and-historical-information/history-california-initiatives)
This study of initiatives by the Secretary of State's office offers a concise history of initiatives and the initiative process. The data contained here is arranged in several ways in order to accommodate various research perspectives and goals.
## [UC Law San Francisco Databases](https://repository.uclawsf.edu/ca_ballots/)
UC Law San Francisco has a searchable database of information on California ballot initiatives and propositions from 1911 to 2020. The UC Law SF site also offers PDF versions of ballot pamphlets from 1911 to 2020.
## [County, City, School District & Ballot Measure Election Results](https://www.sos.ca.gov/elections/county-city-school-district-ballot-measure-election-results)
Following each local election cycle, the California Elections Data Archive (CEDA) collects and compiles results from city, county, school district, and local ballot measure elections.
## [Reports on County and Municipal Initiative Measures](https://www.sos.ca.gov/elections/ballot-measures/resources-and-historical-information/reports-county-and-municipal-initiative-measures)
County elections officials and municipal legislative bodies are required to file a report with the Secretary of State in each odd-numbered year that contains information regarding measures placed on the ballot by the citizen initiative process.
