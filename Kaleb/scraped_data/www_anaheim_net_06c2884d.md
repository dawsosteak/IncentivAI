  1. [Uninterruptible Power Supply](https://www.anaheim.net/5243/Back-Up-Power#tab8f1813c3-6136-4462-af5a-49d940f367ab_0)


An uninterruptible power supply (UPS), also known as battery backup, provides emergency power to critical devices during an unforeseen power outage. An UPS provides power stored in a battery to allow for safe, orderly shutdown of connected equipment (e.g. computers). Medical device UPS systems are designed to provide an uninterrupted power source to keep your medical device running when it is supposed to such as CPAP Machines. 
## Installation/Efficiency Criteria
[ENERGY STAR® ](https://www.energystar.gov/productfinder/product/certified-uninterruptible-power-supplies/results)Certified (for standard UPS only)
## Incentive Amount
$50 for standard equipment (Maximum of 2)
$200 for medical equipment - Must presently be receiving [Medical Lifeline Rate](https://www.anaheim.net/DocumentCenter/View/369/Medical-Lifeline-Allowance-Application-PDF?bidId=) (Maximum of 2)
[Click here](https://www.anaheim.net/1481/Home-Incentives-Rebate-Guidelines-Applic) for guidelines and how to apply.

  1. [Electric Portable Power Station](https://www.anaheim.net/5243/Back-Up-Power#tab8f1813c3-6136-4462-af5a-49d940f367ab_1)


A portable power station is a rechargeable battery-powered generator that can supply power to multiple devices at the same time. In addition, medical portable power stations are also available to help keep your equipment on during any loss of power. Best of all, you may be eligible to receive up to two (2) rebates for purchasing eligible Portable Power Stations, per residential address.   
  

## ​Product Criteria   

Battery Powered (minimum 100watt AC output)  
  

## ​Incentive Amount   

$50 for standard equipment (Maximum of 2)  
$200 for medical equipment - Must presently be receiving [Medical Lifeline Rate](https://www.anaheim.net/DocumentCenter/View/369/Medical-Lifeline-Allowance-Application-PDF?bidId=) (Maximum of 2)
