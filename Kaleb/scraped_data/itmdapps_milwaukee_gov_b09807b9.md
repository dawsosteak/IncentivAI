1. Enter your **email address or phone number** in the username field.  
2. Click the corresponding **Forgot Your Username** link (retrieve by email or retrieve by phone) based on what you entered in the username field.  
3. Take note of your new username provided in the popup. After your click OK in the popup, this username will automatically populate the username field.  
4. Click **Forgot Your Password**. This will provide you with email and/or phone options to receive a link to reset your password. Follow the link sent to you to your email/phone and update your password.  
5. After resetting your password, you can optionally go to <https://itmdapps.milwaukee.gov/LoginMKE> to change your username.
