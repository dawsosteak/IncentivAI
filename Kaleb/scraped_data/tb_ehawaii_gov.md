# Welcome!
This website is for **clients** /**patients** needing TB clearance and **health care providers** referring patients and reporting results. 
**Client** /**Patient:**
  1. **Log in** to get started.
  2. Setup profile or link TB account (one-time step)
  3. Communicate with the TB Program team.
  4. Check status & download clearance certificate


**Health Care Provider:**
  * **Log in** to begin
  * Verify your professional license (one-time step)
  * Add new NDR forms & link patients
  * Update NDR information


# Log in
Log in to access protected services.
If you don't have an _eHawaii.gov_ or _myHawaii Single Sign On (SSO)_ account, you can sign up on the myHawaii login page. After your initial login, you may need to enter additional information.
[Go to SSO login](https://sso.ehawaii.gov/sso/login?service=https://tb.ehawaii.gov/j_spring_cas_security_check)
