#  Pay Permit Fees 
Anaheim Next offers easier access, convenient payment options, and real-time status updates on your permits. [![Anaheim Next - Fire Opens in new window](https://www.anaheim.net/ImageRepository/Document?documentId=68260)](https://aca-prod.accela.com/ANAHEIM/Default.aspx) Fire Construction Permit (FPI) and Building Fire Permit (BFP) payments may be completed by following the step-by-step instructions below. 
  1. Log in to Anaheim Next or register for an account, if you don’t already have one. ![Image of the Anaheim Next homescreen](https://www.anaheim.net/ImageRepository/Document?documentId=69896)


  1. Once logged in, click on the Fire tab and select Search Fire Records/Make Payments from the dropdown. ![Image of the Fire Dropdown Menu in AnaheimNEXT](https://www.anaheim.net/ImageRepository/Document?documentId=69897)


  1. Under the General Search section, enter the complete permit number in the Record Number box. Do not enter any additional information in any other box and click Search. ![Image of the Record Search for Fire in AnaheimNEXT](https://www.anaheim.net/ImageRepository/Document?documentId=69900)


  1. Permit record will appear. Click on Payments tab and select Fees from the drop down.![Image of the Payments Screen for Fire in AnaheimNEXT](https://www.anaheim.net/ImageRepository/Document?documentId=69899)


  

  1. Total outstanding fees will appear. Click on Pay Fees. ![Image of where one would click on the Pay Fees Button in AnaheimNEXT](https://www.anaheim.net/ImageRepository/Document?documentId=69898)


  1. Click on the Add to Cart button. Then select the Choose Payment Method button.
    1. Please note, payments can be paid with Visa, MasterCard, or Discover (American Express is not accepted).
  2. Fill out card information sections to complete payment.


If you require further assistance or have any questions, please call our Fire Development team at (714) 765-4000.
