# Ambulatory Surgery Center Reports
Data found in the reports listed below were obtained from the annual Ambulatory Surgery Center questionnaires. Click "Show +" to expand a section, and "Hide -" to collapse it. To open a specific report or file, select the desired year and click the "Submit" button.
An [Ambulatory Surgery Center Geographic Information Systems (GIS) layer](https://www.pasda.psu.edu/uci/DataSummary.aspx?dataset=903) is created each year and available for download as a shapefile, or which can be added to a map as a map service (ArcMap, Google Earth, WMS) from the [Pennsylvania Spatial Data Access (PASDA) website](https://www.pasda.psu.edu/).
Attention data users: Due to a shift in questionnaire administration and reporting, our Ambulatory Surgery Center data and reports changed from fiscal year to calendar year periods. The 2014-2015 Ambulatory Surgery Center Reports were the last based on fiscal year. The first calendar year reports are for 2016. 
Starting with 2016, reports are in Excel format.
**Individual Reports:**
Most recent data:
  * **1:[Utilization and Services by Facility and County](https://www.pa.gov/content/dam/copapwp-pagov/en/health/documents/topics/healthstatistics/healthfacilities/surgerycenterreports/documents/ambulatory_surgery_centers_2024_1.xlsx)** - Patient visits by age, ultrasound exams, diagnostic X-rays, operations, number of operating rooms and availability of 5 selected services.
  * **2:[Medical Staff with Clinical Privileges in Selected Departments/Services](https://www.pa.gov/content/dam/copapwp-pagov/en/health/documents/topics/healthstatistics/healthfacilities/surgerycenterreports/documents/ambulatory_surgery_centers_2024_2.xlsx)** - Active medical staff with clinical privileges in 19 selected specialties.
  * **3:[Full-time and Part-time Workers at Facility](https://www.pa.gov/content/dam/copapwp-pagov/en/health/documents/topics/healthstatistics/healthfacilities/surgerycenterreports/documents/ambulatory_surgery_centers_2024_3.xlsx)** - From 1995-1996 through 2017, this section inlcuded payroll and contracted breakouts within full-time and part-time categories, starting with 2018, categories only show full-time and part-time (inlcudes all workers).
