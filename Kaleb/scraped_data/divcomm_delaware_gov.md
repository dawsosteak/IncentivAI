## 800 MHz Radio
On October 15, 1993 the State of Delaware purchased a Statewide 800 MHz Digital Trunked Radio System to provide statewide communications for all state, county and municipal government agencies, fire and emergency medical services, and a select number of federal agencies. The system was designed to provide 95% in-street coverage using a portable radio.
[Learn more about the 800 MHz System](https://divcomm.delaware.gov/digital-800-mhz-statewide-trunked-radio-system/)
