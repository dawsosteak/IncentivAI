### News Highlight
The level 2 driving restriction for New Castle County has been downgraded to a level 1. This means that drivers should avoid traveling on roadways, unless there is a significant safety, health, or business reason to do so. All motorists are urged to drive with extreme caution. On Tuesday, 2/24/2026, the City of New Castle offices will open on a normal schedule. In order to allow Public Works to continue to cleanup from the storm, solid waste pickup will be delayed one additional day. Yard waste pickup is canceled for the week of 2/23-2/27. Solid waste pickup originally scheduled for Monday, February 23rd, 2026 will be collected on Wednesday, February 25th, 2026. Solid waste pickup originally scheduled for Tuesday, February 24th, 2026 will be collected on Thursday, February 26th, 2026. Recycling will be picked up on Friday, February 27th, 2026. Residents are again reminded to remove any snow or ice from sidewalk within 24 hours of the end of the storm. Thank you!

## Experience The City of New Castle
![Living in New Castle](https://newcastlecity.delaware.gov/files/2017/03/living-higlight-1.jpg)
### [Living in New Castle](https://newcastlecity.delaware.gov/living-in-new-castle/)
New Castle is great place to live, work and play. New Castle is known for its thriving historic downtown, outdoor open spaces and recreation including Battery Park, multiple playgrounds, trails, nature areas, tennis courts, sailing club, library and museums.
[View More ](https://newcastlecity.delaware.gov/living-in-new-castle/)
![Visiting New Castle](https://newcastlecity.delaware.gov/files/2023/12/new-castle-video-tour-homepage.png)
### [Visiting New Castle](https://newcastlecity.delaware.gov/visitor-information/)
Historic New Castle, Delaware is located just 10 minutes south of downtown Wilmington with easy access from I-95 and I-295. Overlooking the Delaware River, Historic New Castle is the oldest continuously occupied town in the Delaware Valley.
[View More ](https://newcastlecity.delaware.gov/visitor-information/)
![Business in New Castle](https://newcastlecity.delaware.gov/files/2017/03/business-highlight.jpg)
### [Business in New Castle](https://newcastlecity.delaware.gov/doing-business-in-new-castle/)
New Castle is a business-friendly City with expedited permitting, community policing and competitive, reliable electric and water service. There are a variety of locations including historic downtown, three business parks, shopping centers and individual sites.
[View More ](https://newcastlecity.delaware.gov/doing-business-in-new-castle/)
