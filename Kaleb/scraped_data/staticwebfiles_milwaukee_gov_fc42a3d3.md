#####  Highest-Priority Strategies
  * #### Document and institutionalize the City's existing Energy Management Plan
  * #### Promote and enhance Milwaukee's Smart Energy Hub and create an energy innovation center
  * #### Create innovative financing options for energy efficiency and clean energy
  * #### Advocate for a sustainable energy future with We Energies, the Wisconsin PSC, and State lawmakers
  * #### Advocate for a smart electric grid and Valley Power Plant improvements
