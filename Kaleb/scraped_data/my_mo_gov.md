## Welcome to the FSD Benefits Portal!
Here you can create an account and login to complete your MO HealthNet annual renewal, check the status of your benefits, and more!
We encourage you to explore other valuable resources on our website at [mydss.mo.gov](https://mydss.mo.gov/).
Do you have questions about creating your account? You can find answers in our Frequently Asked Questions (FAQs)!
[ View the FAQs ](https://mydss.mo.gov/family-support-division-benefit-portal-faqs)
