#  Utilities Owned Solar Projects 
**Solar in Anaheim! Anaheim Public Utilities owns ten solar photovoltaic systems located on the map below, nine on school property and one located at the Anaheim Convention Center. They have a combined solar capacity of 3.9 megawatts which produces over 6,000,000 million kilowatt hours annually, which is enough energy to power 1,000 homes.  
  
**Click on the school mascot logo or the convention center logo to see real time performance of the solar PV system at that location and learn about how solar energy works. Please allow up to a minute for the webpage to fully load.
