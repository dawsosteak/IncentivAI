#  Motor Incentive Program 
### Upgrading your motor system can save more money than you think.
  

Anaheim Business Energy Efficiency Solutions Program offers incentives to their customers for energy efficient upgrades for replacing existing and operating motors. One way to reduce your energy costs is to switch your standard inefficient motor with a new premium energy efficient motor.
Measure | Efficiency range | Hp Range | Incentives | Maximum Incentive  
---|---|---|---|---  
Motor/Pump | 86.5% -96.0% | 5-Hp - 200-Hp | $60 - $1,400 per unit | 25% of project cost or $50,000 whichever is less  
If you are ready to implement your motor/pump replacement project, please download the following information and contact us about your project. (All projects are subject to funding availability)
  

  

###  **Program Steps**  
  

  * Download the Program Guidelines and [Motor Incentive Table](https://www.anaheim.net/DocumentCenter/View/26605/ABEES-Motor-Incentives-Table-FY-20-21) documents.
  * Download the [Motor Incentive Application](https://www.anaheim.net/DocumentCenter/View/26606/Motors-Application-FY-20-21): (re-name file using account address and name)
  * Download and complete all required tax forms (including W-9 & 590)
  * If you have any questions or concerns about the NEMA Motor Incentive Program, call your energy representative at Anaheim Public Utilities, or fill out this[online form](https://www.anaheim.net/FormCenter/Public-Utilities-6/Energy-Efficiency-Solutions-61). A representative will contact you within 2-3 business days.
