# Export Delaware
Grow your business around the world.  
The Delaware Department of State can help you get ready to export, introduce you to partners overseas and help you close the deal.
  
  

[![Picture of roadside pointing to the left and to the right](https://export.delaware.gov/wp-content/themes/dosgic_EXPORT_theme/img/global-expand-icons-customers.jpg)](https://export.delaware.gov/international-business-trips/)  

### Find new customers overseas
Join a Export Delaware international business trip and meet new customers, partners and distributors to help grow your company sales internationally.
  

[![Picture of two business men talking and problem solving](https://export.delaware.gov/wp-content/themes/dosgic_EXPORT_theme/img/global-expand-icons-consultations.jpg)](https://export.delaware.gov/about-us/services/)  

### Get one-on-one consultations
Sit down with seasoned export professionals to explore export opportunities or ways to expand your current export activities to new markets.
  

[![Picture of an open hand with money falling into it](https://export.delaware.gov/wp-content/themes/dosgic_EXPORT_theme/img/global-expand-icons-grants.jpg)](https://export.delaware.gov/step-grant/)  

### Cover costs with grants
Find out if your company qualifies for a grant to cover the cost of international travel, marketing activities or other export related expenses.
  

  

# About Us
Export Delaware is an office within the Delaware Department of State.  
We help small and medium sized businesses to grow. We help Delaware-based businesses reach new markets overseas.
Export Delaware has been ranked one of the Top 8 State Export Programs nationally by the SBA in terms of return on investment and results.
  
![Image of the Department of State seal](https://export.delaware.gov/wp-content/themes/dosgic_EXPORT_theme/img/sos_seal_blue.jpg)
