# HMOAB Online Certification
## Apply for and renew your hoisting machine operator certification
Toggle navigation
  * [](https://hmoab.ehawaii.gov/cert/welcome.html)
  * [](http://hmoab.hawaii.gov/faqs/)



![](https://hmoab.ehawaii.gov/cert/app/img/banner-sm.jpg) ![](https://hmoab.ehawaii.gov/cert/app/img/banner-md.jpg) ![](https://hmoab.ehawaii.gov/cert/app/img/banner.jpg)
Who can use this service? 
This service is for crane operators to apply and renew their certification online. 
Getting Started. 
  1. Log in with your eHawaii.gov account
  2. Fill out application and attach required documents
  3. Payments can be made via credit card or eCheck


Once approved, your certification will be mailed promptly.
Additional Questions? 
Read the FAQs on our [Help page](http://hmoab.hawaii.gov/faqs/). 
Log in to access protected services.
If you don't have an _eHawaii.gov_ or _myHawaii Single Sign On (SSO)_ account, you can sign up on the myHawaii login page. After your initial login, you may need to enter additional information.
[](https://sso.ehawaii.gov/sso/login?service=https://hmoab.ehawaii.gov/cert/j_spring_cas_security_check)
