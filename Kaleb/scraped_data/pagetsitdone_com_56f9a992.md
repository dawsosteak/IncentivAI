**Video Summary:** The CEO of Lampire Biological Laboratories, as well as several employees, discuss what the company’s mission is, how the company grew over time, why they enjoy working there, and the impact their work has on the life sciences industry.

**GREGORY F. KRUG:** Lampire got started by me showing up to the wrong place for a job interview in between my sophomore and junior year. I was supposed to be at a cattle farm and I ended up at a horse farm.
Lampire is a company that produces many variety of products and services to the life science industry The life science industry being the biotechnology, pharmaceutical diagnostics.
The inspiration was that here I could be working in my field, working with animals that I love and knowing that I was helping mankind.
