PennWATCH contains data reported by commonwealth agencies as required by Act 18 of 2011. 
Questions regarding specific data should be directed to the PA Agencies that reported it. 
Please direct requests for records to the [PA Agencies Right to Know Law](https://pennwatch.pa.gov/PAAgenciesRTK) open records officer of the agency whose records are sought. 
Experiencing technical issues with the PennWATCH site? Please email a description of the issue to: RA-PennWatchData@pa.gov
