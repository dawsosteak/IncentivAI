# Voter Registration
May 4, 2026 is the deadline to register to vote in the 2026 primary election.
[ Register to vote or update your information (opens in a new tab)](https://www.pavoterservices.pa.gov/Pages/VoterRegistrationApplication.aspx)
![a "Vote" button sitting on an American flag](https://s7d9.scene7.com/is/image/statepa/DOS-Hero-1200x600-VoteButton?ts=1764947630517&dpr=off)
## Registering to Vote
To vote in Pennsylvania, you must first register to vote at least 15 days before the election.  

You can register to vote online, by mail, or at a number of government agencies, including Pennsylvania Department of Transportation (PennDOT) Photo License and Driver’s License Centers.
[ Voter registration requirements ](https://www.pa.gov/agencies/vote/voter-registration/voter-registration-requirements)
[ How to register to vote ](https://www.pa.gov/services/vote/register-to-vote)
[ Information for first-time voters, including voter ID ](https://www.pa.gov/agencies/vote/voter-support/new-voters)
## Updating Your Registration
If you are already registered to vote in Pennsylvania, you do not have to register again unless you need to change your name, address, or political party.
[ How to update your registration ](https://www.pa.gov/agencies/vote/voter-registration/update-my-registration)
[ Check to see if you are registered to vote ](https://www.pavoterservices.pa.gov/Pages/voterregistrationstatus.aspx)
[ Notify the county where you are registered if you move out of state ](https://www.pa.gov/content/dam/copapwp-pagov/en/vote/resources/documents-and-forms/Voter_Request_to_Cancel_Registration_English.pdf)
