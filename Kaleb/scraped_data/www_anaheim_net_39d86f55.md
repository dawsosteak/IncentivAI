#  Ambulance Program 
![Honda - Ambulances cropped](https://www.anaheim.net/ImageRepository/Document?documentID=55991)
The Anaheim Fire & Rescue Department (AF&R) is a proud and dedicated emergency medical services (EMS) provider. EMS incidents account for approximately 80% of AF&R's call volume. The goal of AF&R is to deliver the highest quality of medical care to members of the community with dignity, respect, and compassion.
Fees are charged for the emergency medical care provided patients. The City currently bills ambulance transport fees as well as a first responder fee. While the ambulance transport fee partially covers the cost of the transport, the first responder fee provides for the initial treatment and evaluation, regardless whether an ambulance transport occurs.
Even if a patient has insurance, their insurance may not cover the cost of the ambulance transport or may pay only a portion of the bill. This applies to Medicare, Medi-Cal, and private insurers. AF&R's billing contractor, Wittman Enterprises, will make every attempt to obtain payment from a patient's insurance carrier(s) but, if they are unable to verify insurance coverage or an insurance carrier either denies a claim or pays only a portion of the bill, then the patient is responsible for any unpaid charges resulting from the ambulance transport.
**Approved Ambulance Rates (effective July 1, 2025)**
[Paramedic Fees for Ambulance Program (Resolution 2025-057)](https://www.anaheim.net/DocumentCenter/View/55994/FY-2024-25---Paramedic-Fees-for-Ambulance-Program-Resolution-2025-057)
**Ambulance Billing Questions**
Information regarding an ambulance bill may be obtained from Wittman Enterprises at [www.webillems.com](https://www.webillems.com) or (800) 906-6552. From the website, a patient or authorized representative can obtain the balance on the account, update the patient's insurance information, or make a payment.
Representatives from Wittman Enterprises are available Monday-Friday, 8:00am - 4:30pm PST. To protect patient privacy, representatives must speak directly with the patient or the minor patient's parent or legal guardian. Unless authorized by a patient to receive medical information and records, third-party callers may only update a patient's insurance information.
**FireMedics Program**
Members of the FireMedics Program are not responsible for the first responder fee or any out-of-pocket costs related to an ambulance transport (after Wittman Enterprises bills their insurance). Payments made by a patient's insurance will be accepted as payment in full, ensuring no out-of-pocket expenses for the FireMedics member. The cost to join the FireMedics program is $5 per month or, $60 per year. To learn more about the FireMedics program and join, please [click here](https://www.anaheim.net/615/Anaheim-Fire-Rescue-FireMedics-Program).
