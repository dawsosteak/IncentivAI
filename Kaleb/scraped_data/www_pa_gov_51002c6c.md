# Renew Your Vehicle Registration
You need to renew your vehicle registration to drive your vehicle on public roads. If you don't renew it, you could get fined or even lose your right to drive.
[ Renew your registration online (opens in a new tab)](https://www.dmv.penndot.gov/vehicle_services/vrlogin.jsp?navigation=true)
[ Download the renewal form to send in the mail ](https://www.pa.gov/content/dam/copapwp-pagov/en/penndot/documents/public/dvspubsforms/bmv/bmv-forms/mv-140.pdf)

## Overview
Renewing your vehicle and/or trailer registration can, in most cases, be completed [online](https://www.dmv.penndot.gov/vehicle_services/vrlogin.jsp?navigation=true), at any of PennDOT's [Online Messenger services](https://www.dmv.penndot.gov/locator/locator.jsp?navigation=true) or through the mail. Mailing instructions can be found on the back of the applicable forms.   

To [renew online](https://www.dmv.penndot.gov/vehicle_services/vrlogin.jsp?navigation=true) you will need your registration plate number, title number, insurance information, odometer reading, a valid credit card, and a printer. At the end of the transaction, you will be given the opportunity to print a receipt and a permanent registration credential. PennDOT will no longer mail a registration card for registrations renewed online.

## Retired Status Registration
If you are retired, you may qualify for a discounted registration fee.

## Free Registration for a Vehicle Owned by a Veteran (Eligible Veterans Only)
Pennsylvania has eliminated registration and title fees for a passenger vehicle or truck weighing 9,000 pounds or less owned by a military veteran who meets certain eligibility requirements.
[ Learn more and download the application form ](https://www.pa.gov/services/dmv/apply-for-free-registration-for-eligible-veterans)

[Example: For a Passenger Vehicle – You will take the full registration rate for a one year registration fee and divide that fee by 12 months. That will tell you how much your registration costs each month. After you determine what the fee per month is, you will multiply it by the number of months not covered by the current registration. For example, if your Chosen expiration date for seasonal registration is September of this year, and the current registration expires in July of the same year. An increase in fee of two additional months would be required.] • Submit a check or money order made payable to the Commonwealth of Pennsylvania for the prorated registration fee.
