## _2016 Ruling in Rulemaking (R.) 14-10-003. The rescheduled deadline is_
Monday, May 9, 2016 for Opening Comments and Monday, May 23, 2016 for
* * *
Reply Comments.
* * *
The Utility Reform Network (TURN) filed a motion on April 8, 2016 requesting a four-day extension of time to file comments to the April 4, 2016 Ruling regarding regulatory incentives. In its motion, TURN explained that the extension is needed because TURN is simultaneously participating in hearings in another proceeding. TURN stated that it informally polled other parties and no one opposed the extension of time, which would be equally granted to all parties.
* * *
Pacific Gas and Electric Company sent the Assigned Administrative Law Judge an email on behalf of itself, San Diego Gas & Electric Company, and Southern California Edison Company (the Utilities) also requesting an extension of time. The Utilities requested an extension of two weeks, to May 16 for opening comments and May 31 for reply comments, explaining that the additional time is needed due to the complexity of the issues raised by the Ruling. Like TURN, the Utilities also informally polled all parties and received no opposition to the extension of time, which also would be equally granted to all parties.
* * *
