# **Government Information  
Center**
![Government Information Center](https://gic.delaware.gov/wp-content/themes/dosgic_GIC_theme/img/gic-new-logo.png)
### **THIS IS WHAT WE DO**
The Government Information Center (GIC) helps citizens connect to their government via the Internet. The GIC assists state agencies, divisions, and local governments with the creation and maintenance of their websites. We also help lead the statewide Common Look & Feel (CLF) branding initiative.
Web & Graphic Design
###### **WEB & GRAPHIC DESIGN**
We create websites and digital branding assets specifically tailored for State of Delaware entities. Our focus is on delivering clear, accessible solutions that make online services easy and seamless for the citizens of the First State.
Common Look & Feel
###### **COMMON LOOK & FEEL**
State of Delaware websites are unique in that they are governed by a Common Look & Feel design standard that is both developed and maintained by the GIC.
Delaware.gov Portal
###### **DELAWARE.GOV PORTAL**
The homepage for the First State, Delaware.gov is a web portal that serves as a simple single-source of information about and for Delaware state government.
Municipality Websites
###### **MUNICIPALITY WEBSITES**
The GIC provides design and training assistance to local governments, including designing and hosting responsive websites in a content management system.
### **GIC Blog**
#### **Elevating Government Digital Services**
The GIC is excited to share an inside look at what is going on from inside our world. We look forward to our agencies connecting over common core topics that all center around helping Delawareans find what they need when it comes to Delaware State Government. From web development, project methodology, CLF, videography, branding efforts, consultancy and more, the GIC is excited to share, inspire and connect with the common goal of leveling up Delaware State Government.

### **THIS IS WHAT WE DO**
The Government Information Center (GIC) helps citizens connect to their government via the Internet. The GIC assists state agencies, divisions, and local governments with the creation and maintenance of their websites. We also help lead the statewide Common Look & Feel (CLF) branding initiative.
[Web & Graphic Design](https://gic.delaware.gov/web-standards/)
###### [**WEB & GRAPHIC DESIGN**](https://gic.delaware.gov/web-standards/)
We create websites and digital branding assets specifically tailored for State of Delaware entities. Our focus is on delivering clear, accessible solutions that make online services easy and seamless for the citizens of the First State.
[Common Look & Feel](https://gic.delaware.gov/common-look-feel/)
###### [**COMMON LOOK & FEEL**](https://gic.delaware.gov/common-look-feel/)
State of Delaware websites are unique in that they are governed by a Common Look & Feel design standard that is both developed and maintained by the GIC.
[Delaware.gov Portal](https://gic.delaware.gov/state-portal/)
###### [**DELAWARE.GOV PORTAL**](https://gic.delaware.gov/state-portal/)
The homepage for the First State, [Delaware.gov](https://delaware.gov "This link opens in another tab/external link") is a web portal that serves as a simple single-source of information about and for Delaware state government.
[Municipality Websites](https://gic.delaware.gov/delaware-municipal-websites/)
###### [**MUNICIPALITY WEBSITES**](https://gic.delaware.gov/delaware-municipal-websites/)
The GIC provides design and training assistance to local governments, including designing and hosting responsive websites in a content management system.
### **GIC Blog**
#### **Elevating Government Digital Services**
The GIC is excited to share an inside look at what is going on from inside our world. We look forward to our agencies connecting over common core topics that all center around helping Delawareans find what they need when it comes to Delaware State Government. From web development, project methodology, CLF, videography, branding efforts, consultancy and more, the GIC is excited to share, inspire and connect with the common goal of leveling up Delaware State Government.
