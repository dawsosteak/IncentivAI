#####  Highest-Priority Strategies
  * #### Expand green jobs opportunities with a new green jobs program 
  * #### Create business open house program for mentoring Milwaukee youth
  * #### Develop strategic framework for expansion of existing youth programs
  * #### Expand transitional jobs opportunities
  * #### Model City Business Development Agency policies on Menomonee Valley Industrial Center Wage policies
  * #### Provide active support of entrepreneurship by City departments
