# Anita Clarke-Valencia
[Civil Division](https://www.anaheim.net/Directory.aspx?DID=102)  
Title: Deputy City Attorney  
Phone: [714-765-5169](tel:7147655169)
  
  

Ms. Clarke-Valencia handles civil litigation matters against the City of Anaheim. Her practice includes civil rights, constitutional law, and tort law. Prior to joining the Anaheim City Attorney’s Office, her practice primarily focused on police liability and employment matters. Her experience further includes extensive criminal prosecution experience in California, Oregon, and Washington. She is admitted to practice law in California state courts, Oregon, the Ninth Circuit Court of Appeals, and the Central, Southern, and Eastern United States District Courts of California.
Ms. Clarke-Valencia received her juris doctorate from University of Oregon School of Law and a Bachelor of Arts from California State University – San Bernardino.
