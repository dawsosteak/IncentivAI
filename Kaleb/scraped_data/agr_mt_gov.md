**Montana State Grain Lab  
Agriculture Inspector   
Supervisor Position **
The Montana Department of Agriculture is accepting applications for a Agriculture Inspector Supervisor located in Great Falls, MT. Open until filled with first review of candidates February 9, 2026.   
  
The State Grain Laboratory, located in Great Falls, is responsible for the operation of a statewide grain and commodity inspection program. Accurate and unbiased sampling inspections and testing are performed in compliance with federal and state grain standards. The State Grain Laboratory is the only federally designated, official grain inspection service in the state of Montana.

**2026 International  
Year of the Woman Farmer   
Nominations Open **
As we look forward to the new year, we are excited to celebrate 2026 as “The International Year of the Woman Farmer”.   
  
The International Year of the Woman Farmer gives the global community a chance to highlight that fact and celebrate the incredibly important role women play in ensuring global food security. In May, 124 Nations around the globe voted for the resolution to make this happen.   
  
Are you a woman in agriculture? Is there a woman in your life that you’d like to recognize? As part of the “International Year of the Woman Farmer”, the Montana Department of Agriculture (MDA) will be collecting stories statewide to amplify the voices of woman farmers and ranchers.
