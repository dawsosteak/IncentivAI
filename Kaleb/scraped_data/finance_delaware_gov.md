# We manage the funds that are critical to state government.
The Department of Finance promotes Delaware's fiscal health fairly and efficiently by forecasting, generating, collecting and accounting for funds that are essential for state government services.
  

[about Department of Finance](https://finance.delaware.gov/about-finance/)

## Finance Divisions
* * *
  

### [Division of Revenue](https://revenue.delaware.gov/)
Collects, administers, and audits personal and business taxes due to the State.
[about Division of Revenue](https://revenue.delaware.gov/)
  
  

### [Office of Unclaimed Property](https://unclaimedproperty.delaware.gov/)
Reunites owners with lost or abandoned property such as bank accounts, securities and uncashed checks. Works with businesses to ensure compliance with the State’s holder reporting laws.
[about Office of Unclaimed Property](https://unclaimedproperty.delaware.gov/)
  
  

  
  

### [Division of Accounting](https://accounting.delaware.gov/)
Promotes financial efficiency, transparency and accountability by preparing State financial reports, reviewing internal controls, managing statewide accounting systems, and providing a secure e-Supplier Portal for State vendors.
[about Division of Accounting](https://accounting.delaware.gov/)
  
  

### [State Lottery Office (Opens in a new window.)](https://www.delottery.com/)
The Delaware State Lottery operates and ensures the integrity of legal gaming activities in Delaware including traditional lottery games sold by retailers, sports wagering, and casino and internet-based video lottery and table games.
[about State Lottery Office (Opens in a new window.)](https://www.delottery.com/)
