#  Contract Administration 
Contract Administration is responsible for the management, monitoring and administration of all capital improvement construction contracts and projects as well as all design and construction of single and multi-level public buildings and miscellaneous facilities through out the City  
  
This section works closely with our inspection section and other city departments/divisions to ensure compliance with budgets, plans and specifications.
