# Condominium Association Biennial Registration
Welcome to the Condominium Association Biennial Registration web application. To register, you will need: 
  * **project number** printed on notice of your registration;
  * **email address** if you wish to receive acknowledgement of this transaction by email; and 
  * **credit card** (American Express, Discover, MasterCard or Visa) or **checking account** information. 


**TERMS & CONDITIONS**
By proceeding with the application, you agree...
  * to conduct a transaction with the Department of Commerce and Consumer Affairs, State of Hawaii, by electronic means; 
  * to submit a registration application by electronic means;
  * that the electronic registration application has the same legal effect and legal consequences as a signed paper document; 
  * that the laws of the State of Hawaii apply to this transaction;
  * to the deadlines and times applicable to this transaction; and
  * that times are in Hawaii Standard Time.


**Next registration period is in 2027**. 
### Important Dates: 

Bond exemption reapply deadline:
    May 1, 2025 

Biennial reregistration deadline:
    May 31, 2025
