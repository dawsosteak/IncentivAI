Delaware SUN Bucks (Summer EBT)   
SUN Bucks helps families with eligible school-age children buy food during the summer. [Click here](https://assist.dhss.delaware.gov/Common/SystemCompatibility?Origin=IsSUNBucksProgramOnly) to apply for 2026 summer benefits.  
  

SNAP Stolen Benefit Replacement   
If you had food benefits stolen from your SNAP EBT card by electronic fraud from October 1, 2022 to December 20, 2024,   
you may be eligible to get your benefits replaced.   
We cannot replace benefits stolen after December 20, 2024 because of a change to federal rules.   
You must complete and return the SNAP Stolen Benefit Replacement Request form within 60 days of the theft to see if you qualify.   
  
Click below to download the SNAP Stolen Benefit Replacement Request form.

WIC (Special Supplemental Nutrition Program for Women, Infants, and Children)   
The WIC Program aims to safeguard the health of low-income women, infants, and children up to age 5   
who are at nutrition risk by providing nutritious foods to supplement diets, information on healthy eating, and referrals to health care.   
  
[Click here](https://www.dhss.delaware.gov/dhss/dph/chca/dphwichominf01.html) to learn more about the WIC program.
