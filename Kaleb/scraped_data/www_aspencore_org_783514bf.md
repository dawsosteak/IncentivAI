#  Find Maximum Savings for Your Energy Efficiency Projects.  

##  Cutting your carbon footprint doesn't mean you have to drain your savings. CORE can help you save money while saving energy.  

[ Contact Our Energy Concierge ](https://energysmartcolorado.formstack.com/forms/core_lead)
[ Book An Energy Assessment ](https://energysmartcolorado.formstack.com/forms/residential_enrollment)
![CORE staff member conducting a home energy assessment in Aspen, demonstrating their hands-on approach to climate solutions.](https://lirp.cdn-website.com/06c12080/dms3rep/multi/opt/Stott-s+Mill9-1920w.png)
### CORE provides funding for energy efficiency improvement projects for residential, commercial, and multifamily buildings.   

In addition to receiving funding from CORE for energy-saving projects, you may be eligible for additional funding through [Holy Cross Energy](https://www.holycross.com/rebates/), the [State of Colorado](https://energyoffice.colorado.gov/federal-funding-incentives/inflation-reduction-act-tax-credits-incentives), or the [federal Inflation Reduction Act](https://www.rewiringamerica.org/app/ira-calculator).
