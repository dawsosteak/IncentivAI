###  Hours of Operation
Monday - Friday, 7 a.m. - 9 a.m.
Saturday - 7 a.m. - 4 p.m.
Sunday, CLOSED
  

_Open to Dallas residents only, NO commercial customers._  

There is no charge for Dallas residents in passenger cars, station wagons, pickups, and trailers less than 15 feet long hauling their own waste from their residences to the landfill or transfer station   

A valid driver’s license is required to enter the facility. You must also show proof of Dallas residency using either a government-issued ID with a valid city of Dallas address, or a current City of Dallas Utility Bill (water bill) in the name of the person entering the facility.
