##### WHAT CAN I DO?
Use the resources below to be more sustainable in your home or business, and to help your neighborhood. You can help us reach our goals!
  * [Me²:](http://www.smartenergypays.com/homeowners) Make your home more energy efficient 
  * [Me² Commerical:](http://www.smartenergypays.com/businesses) Improve your commercial building's efficiency with PACE financing 
  * [Milwaukee Shines:](http://www.MilwaukeeShines.com) Milwaukee's Solar Program helps make going solar easier for home and business owners 
  * [ME3:](http://city.milwaukee.gov/ME3) No-cost sustainable manufacturing program for small-medium manufacturing companies 
  * [Focus on Energy:](http://www.FocusOnEnergy.com) Wisconsin's statewide energy program provides incentives for energy efficiency upgrades and renewable energy installations 
  * [DSIREUSA:](http://dsireusa.org/incentives/index.cfm?re=0&ee=0&spv=0&st=0&srp=1&state=WI) Database of State Incentives for Renewables and Efficiency provides information on federal and state incentives, tax credits, and financing programs 
  * [Milwaukee Wind Turbine:](http://city.milwaukee.gov/sustainability/WindProject) Learn about Milwaukee's wind turbine and see real time data on the clean energy it's generating for the Port of Milwaukee 
  * [Wisconsin Energy Research Consortium:](http://www.energywercs.org/) Industry group focused on growth of Energy, Power and Control industry cluster in Wisconsin 
  * [MATC:](http://www.matc.edu/ecam/) Milwaukee Area Technical College hosts energy-related course streams through Center for Energy Conservation and Advanced Manufacturing. 
  * [Midwest Renewable Energy Association:](https://www.midwestrenew.org/milwaukee) The MREA offers workshops and trainings on renewable energy technologies 
  * [NABCEP:](http://www.nabcep.org/) North American Board of Certified Energy Practitioners provides national certification for renewable energy professionals 
  * [Milwaukee Community Service Corps:](http://www.milwaukeecommunityservicecorps.org/) Vocational training organization providing solar and other job training opportunities for Milwaukee's youth 
  * [KEEP:](http://www4.uwsp.edu/cnr/wcee/keep/%20) Wisconsin's K-12 Energy Education Program offers professional development opportunities for Wisconsin teachers
