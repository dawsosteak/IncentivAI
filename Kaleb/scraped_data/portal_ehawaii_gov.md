# Certificate of Good Standing (COGS)
!devices showing Certificate of Good Standing \(COGS\)
[Go to Website](https://hbe.ehawaii.gov/documents/search.html)
Purchase and print a certificate of good standing for your Hawaiʻi business.
Developed in partnership with:
  * Business Registration Division
  * Department of Commerce and Consumer Affairs


* * *
Fees: $7.50 969 transactions this year
## Related Services
### Annual Business Filings
File your annual business report with the Department of Commerce and Consumer Affairs.
### Business Registration
Register a business online in the state of Hawaiʻi.
### [Business Search](https://hbe.ehawaii.gov/documents/search.html)
Allows you to search all businesses registered in the state of Hawaiʻi. Most documents and filings are available to purchase.
### Business Data List Builder
Allows you to build a customized list of businesses registered in the state of Hawaiʻi.
