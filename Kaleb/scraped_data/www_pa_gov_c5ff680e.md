# Put Your Military Experience to Work in Pennsylvania
Transitioning back to civilian life or starting a new career? We value your service and welcome veterans, National Guard, and Reserve members to apply their unique skills and dedication in public service with us.
## Why Work for Pennsylvania?
[View all benefits(opens in a new tab)](https://pasnprod.service-now.com/extkb?id=kb_article&sysparm_article=KB0010170)
  * Support for Veterans
We offer special benefits that recognize your military service and ongoing commitment to the National Guard or Reserves
  * [ Veterans' Preference As a qualified veteran, you get preference when applying for positions with the Commonwealth. ](https://www.pa.gov/agencies/employment/veterans/veterans--preference)
  * [ Military Leave You’re entitled to paid and unpaid leave for service duties, including stipends during unpaid absences. ](https://pasnprod.service-now.com/extkb?id=kb_article&sysparm_article=KB0010170)


# Build Your Career with the Commonwealth
### Honoring Veterans with Benefits, Growth, and Opportunity
## Benefits Overview
![](https://s7d9.scene7.com/is/image/statepa/paid-timeoff-benefits-employment)
### Paid Leave
Receive up to 15 days of paid military leave annually for active or inactive duty. Pennsylvania National Guard members can receive unlimited paid leave when ordered to state duty by the Governor.
[ Learn more ](https://pasnprod.service-now.com/extkb?id=kb_article&sysparm_article=KB0010170)
![](https://s7d9.scene7.com/is/image/statepa/healthwellnessbenefits-employment)
### Unpaid Leave
Up to 5 years of unpaid leave for military duty.
[ learn more ](https://pasnprod.service-now.com/extkb?id=kb_article&sysparm_article=KB0010170)
![](https://s7d9.scene7.com/is/image/statepa/retirement-benefit-employemnt)
### Health Benefits
Keep your health benefits during periods of military duty, with options to decline coverage if you're on long-term leave.
[ learn more ](https://pasnprod.service-now.com/extkb?id=kb_article&sysparm_article=KB0010170)
![](https://s7d9.scene7.com/is/image/statepa/retirement-benefits-employment)
### Retirement Credit
You can purchase service time toward retirement for certain periods of military duty, enhancing your pension.
[ learn more ](https://pasnprod.service-now.com/esc?id=kb_article&sysparm_article=KB0010170)
![](https://s7d9.scene7.com/is/image/statepa/retirement-benefit-employemnt-3)
### Monthly Stipend
Permanent employees receive up to $520 per month while on unpaid military leave for active duty.
[ learn more ](https://pasnprod.service-now.com/extkb?id=kb_article&sysparm_article=KB0010170)
