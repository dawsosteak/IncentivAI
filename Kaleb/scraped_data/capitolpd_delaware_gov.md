> _Providing the highest quality of police services utilizing training and technology to create a safe environment for everyone._
![Picture of William Thompson, Delaware Capitol Police Chief](https://capitolpd.delaware.gov/wp-content/uploads/chief.jpg) **William Thompson**  
Capitol Police Chief
Welcome to our website. Thank you for taking the time to learn more about the Delaware Capitol Police. It is my distinct honor and privilege to lead this diverse and unique section of the Delaware [Department of Safety and Homeland Security](https://dshs.delaware.gov).
The men and women of this agency provide compassionate, transparent, and professional service to the citizens we serve. Every day, our officers ensure the safety and well-being of members of the Executive, Judicial, and Legislative branches of government, as well as all citizens conducting business in and around the state facilities under our protection.
Don’t be surprised if you see us assisting various law enforcement agencies beyond our normal duties. With statewide jurisdiction and the extensive experience of our officers, we can provide support anywhere in the state. We regularly take on new responsibilities as our agency continues to evolve and change in our ongoing effort to serve our citizens better.
I encourage you to continue exploring our agency. You can follow us on [Facebook](https://www.facebook.com/DECapitolPolice/) and [Instagram](https://www.instagram.com/delawarecapitolpolice/). If you have any questions or comments, please don’t hesitate to contact us.
