Information about the California Energy Commission’s funding awards, including notices of proposed awards and notices of selection.
## Proposed Awards and Selections
Proposed awards and selections are contingent upon Energy Commission approval. For questions, please contact the Energy Commission agreement officer for the solicitation.
Solicitations are ordered by the last awarded date. For solicitations awarded in 2021 and earlier, please submit a [Public Records Act Request](https://www.energy.ca.gov/contact/public-records-act-requests).
