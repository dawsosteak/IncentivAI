#  Scholarship Opportunity & Optional Paid Internship 
### As a community-owned, not-for-profit utility, Anaheim Public Utilities supports the educational efforts of Anaheim's youth through scholarships.  
  

### To honor outstanding high school seniors who are interested in pursuing a degree in science, technology, engineering, or mathematics, Anaheim Public Utilities is offering an opportunity for a $5,000 scholarship to be used towards college expenses as well as an optional paid internship.  
  

Utilities are influenced by the technological advancements and discoveries coming from the STEM community (science, technology, engineering, and mathematics). Although largely operating in the background of everyday life, utilities are part of the community, providing reliable, sustainable water and power. Similarly, how would you positively impact your community through a STEM-related career?
## The application period for 2026 has closed.
### Eligibility:
  * Student must submit an application and answer all 3 essay questions using correct spelling, punctuation, and grammar conventions. 
  * Student must be pursuing post-secondary education or a degree in a STEM related field.
  * Student must live in Anaheim and receive electric and/or water service from Anaheim Public Utilities. 
  * Student must be enrolled as a high school senior at the time student's application is submitted.
  * Student will be enrolled as a full time student in a two or four year accredited post-secondary institution or trade-school in the upcoming academic year.
  * Student’s parents or legal guardians cannot be employed by the City of Anaheim.
  * Optional paid six-week internship with Anaheim Public Utilities to take place during the months of June and July.
