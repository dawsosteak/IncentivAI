#  Tales and Trails 
Spend your Thursdays with us in our cozy Interpretive Center for our Parent-and-Me program! Each class features a special story time, a hike, a hands-on activity, and an up-close animal interaction. With new themes every week, there’s always something exciting to discover. Tales & Trails is for ages 2-5.
**Adult participation required.**
Thursdays from**9:00 am - 10:00 am & 10:30 am - 11:30 am.**
Cost:**$12 per child.**
