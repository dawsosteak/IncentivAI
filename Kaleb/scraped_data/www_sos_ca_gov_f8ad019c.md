# Sacramento Office – Driving Directions
The California Secretary of State’s main office is located at 1500 11th Street, Sacramento, CA 95814, in the heart of downtown Sacramento at the corner of 11th and O Streets. Metered street parking is available.
## Driving Directions
There are five different major roadways that flow into downtown Sacramento:
  * **I–5** (Interstate 5)
  * **I–80** (Interstate 80)
  * **CA–99** (Highway 99)
  * **I–80–BR** ("Business Route 80," also called the "Capitol City Freeway")
  * **US–50** (United States Highway 50, also called the "El Dorado Freeway")


## From the North/Northwest (Woodland, Redding):
  * Take **I–5 South** into downtown Sacramento.
  * Take the **Q Street** exit.
  * Turn left on **11th Street**.
  * Arrive at 1500 11th Street, at the corner of 11th and O Streets.


## From the Northeast (Auburn, Truckee):
  * Take **I–80-Business Route West** into downtown Sacramento.
  * Take the **P Street** exit.
  * Turn right on **11th Street**.
  * Arrive at 1500 11th Street, at the corner of 11th and O Streets.


## From the East (Placerville, El Dorado Hills):
  * Take **US–50 West** to the CA-99/US–50/I–80/I–80–BR interchange in downtown Sacramento.
  * At the interchange, do not take any off–ramps and continue on **I–80–Business Route West**.
  * Take the **16th Street** exit and veer right onto **16th Street**.
  * Turn left onto **P Street**.
  * Turn right on **11th Street**.
  * Arrive at 1500 11th Street, at the corner of 11th and O Streets.


## From the South, Interstate 5 (Los Angeles, Stockton):
  * Take **I–5 North** into downtown Sacramento.
  * Take the **Q Street** exit.
  * Turn left on **11th Street**.
  * Arrive at 1500 11th Street, at the corner of 11th and O Streets.


## From the South, Highway 99 (Fresno, Modesto, Elk Grove):
  * Take **CA–99 North** to the CA–99/US–50/I–80/I–80–BR interchange.
  * Take **San Francisco** exit onto **I–80–Business Route West**.
  * Take the **16th Street** exit and veer right onto **16th Street**.
  * Turn left onto **P Street**.
  * Turn right on **11th Street**.
  * Arrive at 1500 11th Street, at the corner of 11th and O Streets.


## From the West/Southwest (San Francisco, Vacaville, Davis):
  * Take **I–80 East** (continuing on **I–80–Business Route East**) to the I–5/I–80–BR interchange.
  * Take the **I–5 North** off-ramp, and after it veers left to connect with **I–5** , take the **Q Street** exit.
  * Turn left on **11th Street**.
  * Arrive at 1500 11th Street, at the corner of 11th and O Streets.


## Via Sacramento Regional Transit Light Rail (Meadowview [Blue Line] or Folsom [Gold Line]):
  * Take either line (**Blue** or **Gold**) into downtown Sacramento.
  * Exit at the **Archives Plaza** stop.
  * Arrive at 1500 11th Street, at the corner of 11th and O Streets.
