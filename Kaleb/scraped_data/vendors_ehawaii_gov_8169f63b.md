New and renewal applications will require a Tax Compliance Report (TCR) from the IRS, which can be downloaded for free at: <https://www.irs.gov/individuals/tax-compliance-report>.  
Tax Exempt Organizations can also submit a Letter of Determination. If you are unable to obtain a TCR or Letter of Determination, an IRS Compliance Waiver may be requested from the Department of Taxation.  
Please upload the TCR, Letter of Determination, or DoTax Waiver by clicking on your Status and editing your application. [Download the guide](https://vendors.ehawaii.gov/hce/static/tcr-vendor-guide.pdf?v=202604130606)
