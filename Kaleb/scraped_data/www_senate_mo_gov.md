Welcome to the Missouri Legislative Library! The Legislative Library works to serve the archival and research needs of the General Assembly legislators, staff and constituents. We offer a variety of digital and print tools to assist in this mission. If you have questions, contact us by chat on this website or using the information on our [Contact Us](https://www.senate.mo.gov/LegislativeLibrary/Contact.html) page.

The Legislative Library also maintains extensive print collections organized into the categories listed below.
  * Small book collection focused on Missouri History and legislative policy (Search library print materials using the [Legislative Library Catalog](https://mogaleg.booksys.net/opac/mll/index.html#menuHome)) 
  * Legislative History materials (Browse our growing collection of digitized materials using our new [Legislative History Database](https://www.senate.mo.gov/LegislativeLibraryprogram/)). 
  * Revised Statutes of Missouri (1825 – Present)
  * House and Senate Journals (1800 - Present) 
  * Session Laws (Territorial Assembly – Present)
  * Newspapers and Magazines
  * Policy Journals
  * Law Reviews
  * Missouri State Government Documents
  * Missouri Constitutional Convention reports
