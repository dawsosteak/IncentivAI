# Forms, Samples and Fees
* * *
[![BizFile](https://bpd.cdn.sos.ca.gov/bizfile-online/button3.png)](https://bizfileonline.sos.ca.gov/)
Over 140 Business Filings, Name Reservations, and Orders for Certificates of Status and Certified Copies of Corporations, Limited Liability Companies and Limited Partnerships available [online](https://bizfileonline.sos.ca.gov/).
* * *
The Secretary of State offers two options for submitting business entity filings depending on your entity and filing type. 
## **1. File Online - Fastest Service**
[![Online Filing - https://bizfileonline.sos.ca.gov/](https://bpd.cdn.sos.ca.gov/images/computer.jpg)](https://bizfileonline.sos.ca.gov/)
The business entity filings can be submitted directly [online](https://bizfileonline.sos.ca.gov/).
Over 140 Business Filings, Name Reservations, and Orders for Certificates of Status and Certified Copies of Corporations, Limited Liability Companies and Limited Partnerships available [online](https://bizfileonline.sos.ca.gov/).
##  **2. File by Mail or In Person**
All business forms can be submitted by mail or in person. [Locate the form](https://www.sos.ca.gov/business-programs/business-entities/forms#locate-your-form)[Contact Information - Business Entities](https://www.sos.ca.gov/business-programs/business-entities/contact/) webpage.
