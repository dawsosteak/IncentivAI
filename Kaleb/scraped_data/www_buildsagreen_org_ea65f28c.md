## SPARC Party 
SPARC stand for Smart, Progressive, and Resilient City and that is exactly what this event is all about. This annual honors event is a chance for our partners, members, and friends to come together to celebrate our progress and look to the future. Be a part of this event by becoming a sponsor today!
[ ![](https://images.squarespace-cdn.com/content/v1/628be4b2441f004cc1ba0ced/5d70931b-86e5-4235-b365-d0be849c35cc/SOLARFEST-SAN-ANTO-BLOCK.jpg) ](https://www.buildsagreen.org/solarfest)
##  Solar Fest 
_October 19, 2024_
_9 AM-1 PM_
_The Greenline Park at Brooks_
Solar Fest is a FREE family event annually. Come celebrate with the community at this free, fun, family-friendly festival that will include a variety of activities and engaging opportunities to learn how to save energy, go solar, and how to stay engaged with our great city’s local resiliency efforts.
