# Form 409 - Limited Liability Company (LLC) Statement of Members
The [Form 409](https://www.fppc.ca.gov/siteassets/documents/tad/forms/campaign/409.pdf) is filed by a Limited Liability Company (LLC) if the LLC qualifies as a committee or committee sponsor. The statement must disclose all members who have a membership interest in the LLC of at least 10% or who have made a cumulative capital contribution of at least $10,000 to the LLC within the 12 months prior to qualification as a committee or sponsor of a committee, as well as other detailed information.
