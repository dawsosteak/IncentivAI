## Overview
Annual report detailing all development permits issued within your municipality. This report must be filed each year, even if no permits were issued in the time period.
## Reporting Period
January 1 to December 31 of the previous year.
## Deadline
Submit by February 28 every year.
## Submit Your Report
Submit your report online. Include a copy of ordinances, updates, or amendments enacted by your municipality from Jan. 1 to Dec. 31 of last year.
