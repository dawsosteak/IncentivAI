# Authorized Trade **Representatives**
![A group of Authorized Trade Representatives, in business attire, pose for a group photo in a conference room](https://s7d9.scene7.com/is/image/statepa/BTW2PA%20ATR%20Group%20Photo%20Central%20PA?fmt=webp)
## Going Global? We Have Experts **Around the World.**
Pennsylvania’s network of Authorized Trade Representatives provide on-the-ground business intelligence and in-country support to help companies jumpstart their international business and sales strategies.
**Services include:**
  * Competitive analysis​


  * Customized business matchmaking & partner searches​


  * Background checks​


  * Industry contacts​


  * Market research


Most of these services are available to Pennsylvania companies at no cost. For more information or to connect with our global network of consultants, contact BusinessPA’s International Trade team—see our list below to get in touch with the right person or connect with your local [Regional Export Network](https://pagetsitdone.com/locate_or_expand/trade/regional_export_network) partner.
