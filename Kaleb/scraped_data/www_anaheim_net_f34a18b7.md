**Our Program Philosophy**
The Tiny Tots Academy is a Recreational Program for children ages 3-5 that recognizes the unique and individual needs of each child. We prioritize parent involvement as we carry out programming to ensure that we are supporting our Tiny Tots inside and outside of the program. We strive to be a resource for the community as we work _with_ parents to strengthen our Tiny Tots’ social-emotional development through socialization, self-esteem building, and self-confidence.
**Mission Statement**
At the Tiny Tots Academy, we help our kids get F.I.I.T. for kindergarten by building their **F** oundational skills, encouraging them to **I** nteract with kids their age, and having a safe space to use their **I** magination, all while implementing classroom **T** raditions.

Classes are held year-round and offered Monday through Friday from 9:00 a.m. - 12:00 p.m. There are 3 different enrollment options available:
  * Monday, Wednesday, and Friday
  * Tuesday and Thursday
  * Monday through Friday


NOTE: All participants must be fully potty trained. Pull-ups or diapers are not permitted.
The Tiny Tots Academy is now accepting toddlers ages 3 and up! For children under 3 years old **and** fully potty trained, we encourage families to schedule a tour to determine their child’s program readiness.
  
For more information or to schedule a tour, call (714) 765-4980. Registration can be completed [online](https://secure.rec1.com/CA/city-of-anaheim/catalog/index/27d2d8c29b3ae3bd16a3e94d53463cb2?filter=c2VhcmNoPXRpbnkrdG90cythY2FkZW15).
