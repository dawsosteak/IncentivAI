# Request a Certificate, Letter of Recognition, or Resolution from the Secretary of State
On behalf of the Office of California Secretary of State Shirley N. Weber, Ph.D., we are honored to recognize California residents who contribute to their communities. Please complete the form below and submit it at least 30 business days before the recognition is needed. All requests will be reviewed and processed by the Secretary of State’s Recognition Unit. Please note that submission does not guarantee fulfillment.
If you have any questions, please contact us at SOSRecognition@sos.ca.gov
