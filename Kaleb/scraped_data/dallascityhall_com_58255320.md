## News & UPDATES  

**6/24/2025**
[Information on Alley to Curb Point of Service Transition](https://dallascityhall.com/departments/sanitation/Pages/curbtransition.aspx)
Sanitation is working to improve the safety, efficiency, and long-term sustainability of garbage and recycle collection services. This transition focuses on discontinuing alley service only in the most problematic locations.
**4/04/2025**
[Helping Hands Program 2025 Renewals](https://dallascityhall.com/departments/sanitation/Pages/Helping-Hands-Program-2025-Renewal.aspx)
Effective February 1, 2025, participants who have been enrolled for more than two years must renew their enrollment.
**1/15/2025**
[2025 Tax Exemption Certification](https://dallascityhall.com/departments/sanitation/Pages/2025-Sales-and-Use-Tax-Exemption-Certificate.aspx)
The Sales and Use Tax Exemption Certificate for all applicable sales tax exempt accounts are due for calendar year 2025. All non-compliant accounts will be modified to remove the exemption after the deadline.
**1/07/2025**
[Winter Weather Advisory: Solid Waste Collections on Thursday and Friday (Jan 9 & 10, 2025)](https://dallascityhall.com/departments/sanitation/Pages/Winter-Weather-Advisory---Solid-Waste-Collections-on-Jan-9-and-10%2c-2025.aspx)
As North Texas braces for its coldest weather of the season, we want to inform our customers about how the winter storm may impact garbage, recycling, and brush and bulky item collections scheduled for Thursday and Friday this week.
**12/30/2024**
[No Brush & Bulky Item Collection Week of Dec. 30](https://dallascityhall.com/departments/sanitation/Pages/No-Brush-and-Bulky-Item-Collection-Week-of-December-30%2c-2024.aspx)
There will be no brush and bulky item collections this week as it falls on the 5th Monday of the month. Regular collections will resume next week.
**11/05/2024**
[City of Dallas Offers Post-Election Sign Recycling at Transfer Stations](https://dallascityhall.com/departments/sanitation/Pages/City-of-Dallas-Offers-Post-Election-Sign-Recycling-at-Transfer-Stations.aspx)
