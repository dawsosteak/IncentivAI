##  Access CPUC: Chapter 1 - Welcome to the CPUC 
Access CPUC: Learn about us and how we're here to help consumers, how to get involved in our proceedings, and how to file complaints. Contact news at cpuc.ca.gov to obtain a free DVD of Access CPUC, which contains English, Chinese, and Spanish subtitles, as well as forms and materials. Access CPUC features Bay Area journalists Tomas Roman and Barbara Rogers, as well as CPUC staff. 
##  About the Communications Team 
The Communication team in the External Affairs Division provides information and assistance to the news media, the public, and other stakeholders about the CPUC's many pioneering and innovative programs.
