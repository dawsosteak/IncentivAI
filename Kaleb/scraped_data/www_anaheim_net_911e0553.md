#  Turf Replacement Program 
![Ditch the Turf banner](https://www.anaheim.net/ImageRepository/Document?documentID=65290)
Anaheim and its regional partners are encouraging residents and businesses to help the environment by reducing potable water use for the irrigation of lawns.
One option for customers is to ditch their grass turf by planting native, drought-tolerant plants that**promote biodiversity** , significantly **reduce water use** by using drip irrigation (by as much as 70%), and**reduce heat islanding** when low water-use trees and plants are installed rather than pavers or synthetic turf.
### Turf Replacement Program Rebate
Through the Metropolitan Water District of Southern California’s (Metropolitan) SoCal Water$mart Program, Anaheim Public Utilities customers are eligible to receive $3 per square foot of turf removed, up to 5,000 sq. ft. for residents.
**Combine with our TreePower program** for [4 free trees](https://www.anaheim.net/FormCenter/Public-Utilities-6/TreePower-Program-58) to support your project! Choose from more than 40 available species! Select “Trees for Turf Removal” when you apply.
**Contact us for a free consultation** to discuss turf replacement options and rebate requirements*.  
Call 714-765-4218.
* * *
_*Rebates are available on a first-come, first-served basis. Synthetic turf is not eligible._
  

### Turf Replacement Program Rebate Requirements
  
To be eligible for a rebate, you **must apply and receive project start approval before removing any turf.**
**Synthetic turf is not eligible.  
**
Please visit [socalwatersmart.com](https://www.socalwatersmart.com/) to view program requirements and begin the application process.  
  
New front yard and/or parkway projects must follow [City of Anaheim Landscaping Requirements](https://anaheim.net/1064/City-of-Anaheim-Landscaping-Requirements). Prior to installation, the **Anaheim Planning Department must review and approve the proposed landscape design.** Please call Planning Services at 714-765-5139 for assistance.
