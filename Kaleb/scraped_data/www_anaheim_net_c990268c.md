Operated by the City of Anaheim, the gymnasium is a special place for our community to come together. This award winning facility opened in January, 2007 providing a much needed site for youth and adult sports and senior programs.
## Location
The gymnasium is conveniently [located](http://goo.gl/maps/VUd76) adjacent to the 91 freeway at the Weir Canyon Road exit.
## Amenities
The facility has a regulation size high school basketball or volleyball court with electronic scoreboards and can be configured to use 2 smaller practice courts with a divider.
## Programs
### Youth
Youth programs are offered on a seasonal basis and include:
  * Basketball leagues
  * Camps
  * Clinics
  * Volleyball leagues


To register for current programs please visit [anaheim.net/play](https://www.anaheim.net/play)  
  

### Adult
Adult programs include:
  * [Men’s basketball leagues](https://www.anaheim.net/943/Basketball-Leagues)
  * Coed Volleyball League


##  Seniors  
Senior programs include:  

  * Pickleball
  * Table Tennis

Gym Facilities
This facility offers open gym time to the public and is available for rental. View [](https://www.anaheim.net/2392/)[open gym calendar.](https://www.anaheim.net/DocumentCenter/View/42929/3-March-2022)
