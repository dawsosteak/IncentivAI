**Notary Public Name Change Application**
**Item Instruction**
**Pg 2**
Instructions for Completing A notary public name change application is only accepted in the event of a legal name change. Read all instructions and information carefully. Questions regarding this application should be directed to the Notary Public Section at (916) 653-3595. Once completed mail your form to Notary Public Section, 1500 11th Street, 2nd Floor, Sacramento, CA 95814. 1a. Enter your current seven-digit commission number. 1b. Enter your current commission expiration date in MM/DD/YYYY format. 1c. Enter the month, day, and year you were born in MM/DD/YYYY format. 1d. Enter your legal first name as it appears on your California Driver’s License or California Identification Card. Note: This name is public information and will be provided to the public upon request. 1e. Enter your legal middle name as it appears on your California Driver’s License or California Identification Card. If you do not have a middle name, check the “NMN” box. Note: This name is public information and will be provided to the public upon request. 1f. Enter your legal last name exactly as it appears on your California Driver’s License or California Identification Card. The name entered will be used for identification purposes and must match your picture identification used for filing your oath. Note: This name is public information and will be provided to the public upon request. 1g. Enter a suffix if it appears on your Californi a Driver’s License or California Identification Card. Acceptable suffixes include Jr., Sr., I, II, III, IV, or V. Note: This name is public information and will be provided to the public upon request. 1h. Enter a direct email address to reach you if there is an issue with your application or commission, if any. 1i. Enter a direct telephone number to reach you if there is an issue with your application or commission, if any. 2a. Type or print your first name exactly as you want it to appear on your amended commission. You may choose to use only the initial of your first or middle name for your commission first name; or you may choose to use your middle name as your commission first name. Titles and ditto marks are not acceptable. When you file your oath and bond with the county clerk, you will be required to present identification that must substantially match the requested amended commission name. 2b. Type or print your middle name exactly as you want it to appear on your amended commission. You may choose to use only the initial of your middle name as part of your commission name; or you may choose not to have a middle name or middle initial in your amended commission name. 2c. Type or print your commission last name exactly as it appears on your California’s Driver’s License or California Identification Card. Your amended commission last name must match the legal last name in Section 1f. A full last name is required. If your last name is “Smith-Jones,” you must enter “Smith-Jones.” The use of “Smith” or “Jones” by itself is not acceptable. 2d. Type or print your suffix as you want it to appear on your commission. If your California Driver’s License or California Identification Card includes a legal suffix, it is part of your legal last name and must be part of your commission last name. Your amended commission suffix must match the legal suffix in item 1g. 3a. Enter the name of the business where you will perform notary public services. If you do not work for a business, do not have a business name, are self-employed, or are unemployed, leave the field blank and check the “Self-Employed” box. 3b. Enter the name of the California county where your business is located. Do not abbreviate the name of the county. If you have indicated self-employed as your principal place of business, or are intending to be a mobile notary, you may enter the county of your residence in item 3b. 3c. Enter the address of your business where you will perform 50% or more of your notary public duties. Do not enter a P.O. Box number or commercial mailbox. Do not abbreviate the name of the city. If you have indicated self-employed as your principal place of business, or are intending to be a mobile notary, you may enter your residence address as the business location address required in item 3c. Note: This address is public information. This information will be posted on our website and provided to the public upon request. 3d. Enter the mailing address where you would like to receive notary public information, if different than your business address. Your amended notary public commission packet will be mailed to this address. The mailing address may be a P.O. Box. Note: This address is public information. This information will be posted on our website and provided to the public upon request. 3e. Enter your residence address. Enter the address where you live. Do not enter a P.O. Box number or commercial mailbox. Note: This address is public information and will be provided to the public upon request. NOTARY NAME CHANGE APPLICATION (REV 6/2025)
