# Become an Employer Partner with the Office of Vocational Rehabilitation (OVR)
##  On this page On this page
The Office of Vocational Rehabilitation offers employer-focused services in Pennsylvania, aiding in recruitment and hiring needs. Find out how you can give your business a competitive edge by hiring people with disabilities! Contact the Business Services Team via [email(opens in a new tab)](https://www.pa.gov/services/dli/LI-OVR-BSOD@pa.gov) at one of our [District Offices(opens in a new tab)](https://www.dli.pa.gov/Individuals/Disability-Services/ovr/Pages/OVR-Office-Directory.aspx) for more information.

Become an Employer Partner with the Office of Vocational Rehabilitation (OVR) 
To become an employer partner with the Office of Vocational Rehabilitation (OVR), contact the Business Services Team via [email(opens in a new tab)](https://www.pa.gov/services/dli/LI-OVR-BSOD@pa.gov) or at one of our [District Offices(opens in a new tab)](https://www.dli.pa.gov/Individuals/Disability-Services/ovr/Pages/OVR-Office-Directory.aspx) for more information.

Frequently Asked Questions 
How do I become an employer partner?
