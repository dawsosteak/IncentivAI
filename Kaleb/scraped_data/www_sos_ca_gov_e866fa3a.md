# Check Your Voter Registration Status
Choose Language 
English español
Don't remember if you're registered to vote? Need to check what name you're registered under or what political party you're registered with? Check your registration status online at [voterstatus.sos.ca.gov.](https://voterstatus.sos.ca.gov/)
[![Check My Voter Status](https://elections.cdn.sos.ca.gov/images/myvoterstatus.png)](https://www.sos.ca.gov/elections/\\\\voterstatus.sos.ca.gov)
Some county elections officials also allow you to check the status of your voter registration through their website, by telephone, or both.

Not sure what county you live in? Simply enter your zip code with the [U.S. Census Bureau](http://quickfacts.census.gov/cgi-bin/qfd/lookup) or call our voter hotline at (800) 345-VOTE.
If you are not registered to vote, you can fill out an online [voter registration application](https://www.sos.ca.gov/elections/voter-registration). You can also print a [blank application](http://www.eac.gov/voter_resources/register_to_vote.aspx) or pick up an application at your county elections office, any Department of Motor Vehicles office, and many post offices, public libraries, and government offices. To have a paper application mailed to you call your [county elections office](https://www.sos.ca.gov/elections/voting-resources/county-elections-offices) or the Secretary of State's toll-free voter hotline at (800) 345-VOTE.
