#  Volunteer Opportunities 
### Canyon Companions
Join our staff in caring for the trails and grounds of Oak Canyon Nature Center. This is a fantastic opportunity to learn about the environment while giving back to your community. High school students can also earn community service hours. 
Weekly projects are planned, and pre-registration is required. **Participants 17 and under need a signed consent form**. All materials are provided. Groups should reserve in advance with the Nature Center staff
_**Please note that the nature center cannot take volunteers who are referred by the courts. Court referrals may be volunteered by the Code Enforcement division._
**When:** Saturdays, 12:00 pm.-2:00 pm.
**Ages:** 12 & up 
**Bring:** A good attitude, any forms you need filled out, and a guardian to sign a waiver.
### O.A.K.S. Day Camp
Oak Canyon Nature Center offers quality programs for the community and needs volunteers ages 14 to 17 to help with summer day camps for children ages 5-12. Volunteers assist staff and supervise campers. To apply for the Summer VolunTEEN Program, applicants must complete an orientation, including fingerprinting and a background check
Visit the [VolunTEEN Program page](http://www.anaheim.net/4829/Summer-VolunTEEN-Program) for the current year's application and orientation dates.
### STEPS TO BECOMING A VOLUNTEEN
  1. Complete the application
  2. Attend an orientation
  3. Return completed VolunTEEN application during an orientation.
  4. New and Returning VolunTEENs must attend an orientation with a parent or guardian.
  5. First time, applicants must have their fingerprints taken for a background check; this will be completed by the Program Specialist during your appointment. A PHOTO ID IS REQUIRED FOR FINGERPRINTING. A school ID, driver’s license, or passport is acceptable.
  6. Submit proof of Tuberculosis (TB) Clearance, dated within the past two years.
  7. Teens will be notified of acceptance and placement via mail.
  8. Attend your program training as scheduled by your VolunTEEN supervisor.
  9. Have fun, gain experience, and meet new friends!


**For questions or additional information, please call the VolunTEEN Program Specialist at (714) 765-4994 OR emailvolunteeroffice@anaheim.net**
