##### WHAT CAN I DO?
Use the resources below to be more sustainable in your home or business, and to help your neighborhood. You can help us reach our goals!
  * [City of Milwaukee Comprehensive Plan:](http://city.milwaukee.gov/Plansandstudies/AreaPlans.htm) City of Milwaukee has 13 area plans that guide future neighborhood development 
  * [Department of City Development:](http://city.milwaukee.gov/DCD) Supports business and real estate development 
  * [Milwaukee Economic Development Corporation:](http://www.medconline.com/) MEDC provides low-interest loans to support investment in Milwaukee 
  * [Neighborhood Improvement Development Corporation:](http://city.milwaukee.gov/NIDC) NIDC works with City departments and the community to improve neighborhoods with rehab programs and financing 
  * [Targeted Investment Neighborhoods:](http://city.milwaukee.gov/TargetedInvestmentNeighborhoodsTINs.htm) TINS provides resources to improve housing and strengthen property values 
  * [Milwaukee County Parks:](http://county.milwaukee.gov/Parks) Maps and activities in Milwaukee County's award winning park system 
  * [Urban Ecology Center:](http://urbanecologycenter.org/) UEC fosters ecological understanding as inspiration for change, neighborhood by neighborhood 
  * [Milwaukee River Greenway Coalition:](http://www.milwaukeerivergreenway.org/) Comprehensive vision for restored urban wilderness in three communities
