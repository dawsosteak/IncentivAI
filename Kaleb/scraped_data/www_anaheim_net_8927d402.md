#  Congregate Lunch Program 
![Congregate Lunch Photo](https://www.anaheim.net/ImageRepository/Document?documentID=57375)
### What is a congregate lunch program?
The Congregate lunch program is a community event designed to provide healthy, nutritious meals to older adults at the Brookhurst Community Center. This program aims to improve the nutritional intake and overall health of participants and offer opportunities for social interaction and engagement. This helps reduce isolation and promotes a sense of community. The lunch program provides an opportunity for staff to get to know participants and assist them in connecting with other support services and resources, contributing to their overall well-being and independence.
### Do I have to pay for congregate lunch?
Voluntary contributions are welcome but not required, with a suggested donation of $3.00 for those who are age 60 and over. Guests under 60 can participate with a donation of $5.00. The congregate lunch program, as part of an older adult nutrition initiative, aims to reduce hunger, food insecurity, and malnutrition while promoting social engagement and supporting the overall health and well-being of our older Anaheim residents.
### Who can attend congregate lunch?
The congregate lunch program is open to all adults aged 60 and older. 
### What are the benefits of congregate lunches?
**Improved nutrition:** The lunches provide older adults with nutritious, well-balanced meals, helping them obtain essential nutrients such as protein, calcium, and fiber. This can help prevent chronic conditions like heart disease, hypertension, and osteoporosis. 
**Dietary variety:** Congregate lunches allow older adults to enjoy a variety of meals they might not want to or be able to prepare themselves. A varied, interesting diet is not only healthy but also enhances overall well-being.
**Social connection:** Social isolation can lead to loneliness, depression, high blood pressure, and early-onset dementia. Regularly attending the lunch program provides older adults the opportunity to meet new people, form lasting friendships, and build a strong support network. This foundation for a thriving social life can make independent living easier.
**Greater food security:** Regular access to nutritious food is crucial for overall health. The congregate lunch program helps address this issue, with 54% of participants reporting that a congregate meal provides at least half of their daily food intake.
### Where is the congregate lunch program located?
The Brookhurst Community Center is located at 2271 W. Crescent Ave., Anaheim, CA. Monday through Friday from 11:00a.m. - 1:00p.m. Entertainment is available to enjoy on most Thursdays. For information regarding transportation to the congregate lunch site or other Older Adult and Senior services please call (714) 765-4511.
