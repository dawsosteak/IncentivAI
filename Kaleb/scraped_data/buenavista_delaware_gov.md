## [History of Buena Vista](https://buenavista.delaware.gov/history-buena-vista/)
Delaware and U.S. Statesman John Middleton Clayton built Buena Vista between 1845 and 1847. Find out more about the estate.
[Learn More ](https://buenavista.delaware.gov/history-buena-vista/)

## [Meetings, Trainings, Retreats](https://buenavista.delaware.gov/meetings/)
Want to have meetings? We can accommodate you! Depending on how you choose to configure Buena Vista for your event, you can create a formal or casual atmosphere.

## [Events](https://buenavista.delaware.gov/buena-vista-events/)
Unsure how Buena Vista can be best used to host your event? Our staff supports [Special Events](https://buenavista.delaware.gov/special-events/) and our own [Buena Vista Events](https://buenavista.delaware.gov/buena-vista-events/). Contact Us for pricing, availability, or to schedule a tour.
[Learn More ](https://buenavista.delaware.gov/buena-vista-events/)
