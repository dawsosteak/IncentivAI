## Frequently asked questions
We’re here to guide you through the Commonwealth of  
Pennsylvania grants process.  

###  How do I submit an application? 
To apply for grants, you need to [create a Keystone Login account](https://keystonelogin.pa.gov/). 
If you need assistance with your account or resetting your password, please contact: 
  * Phone: 877-328-0995


###  Who can I contact for more information on grants?
Grants are offered by many agencies. This means that every grant has a specific contact information based on the agency providing the funding. To find help, [select a grant for contact information](https://www.pa.gov/grants/search).
###  How can I be a successful grant applicant?
If you’re applying for a Pennsylvania state grant, here are some tips to help you do well:
  * **Read the rules carefully.** Every grant has its own steps, deadlines, and reporting requirements. Be sure to understand the rules for the grant you’re applying for.
  * **Watch out for common mistakes.** Some people forget to include forms, make errors in their budget, or don’t explain their goals clearly.
  * **Take the training.** The [Grantee Integrity Training](https://www.pa.gov/agencies/osig/what-we-do/protecting-state-grants-and-contracts/grantee-integrity-training-) is free and helps you learn how to follow the rules and use your grant funding the right way. It’s especially helpful for first-time applicants and supports Pennsylvania’s commitment to fairness for all. 
  * **Ask for help.** If you’re not sure about something, contact the grant program contact directly. They can answer your questions or connect you to someone who can.


If this is your first time managing a grant, the training is a great place to start. 
[ Sign up for Grantee Integrity Training ](https://www.pa.gov/agencies/osig/what-we-do/protecting-state-grants-and-contracts/grantee-integrity-training-)
###  Where can I get technical support with my application? 
Technical support for applying for grants is available. 
#### **Department of Community and Economic Development (DCED) grants**
The DCED Customer Service Center can be contacted at: 
  * Hours: Monday through Friday, 8:30 a.m. until 5 p.m. 
  * Phone: 800-379-7448
  * Email: ra-dcedcs@pa.gov


#### **All other agency grants**
The Enterprise eGrants Customer Service Center can be contacted at: 
  * Hours: Monday through Friday, 7 a.m. to 6 p.m.
  * Phone: 833-448-0647
  * Email: egrantshelp@pa.gov


###  Who is eligible for state grants?
Grants are available for many purposes. Every grant listed on pa.gov/grants has specific eligibility requirements. The opportunities listed are for various types of applicants, including:
  * Businesses 


  * Government


  * Higher education


  * Individuals 


  * Libraries or library systems


  * Nonprofits


[ Filter by your specific needs ](https://www.pa.gov/grants/search)
###  What types of funding are offered?
While grants are the main funding type offered, other types of funding may be available. Commonwealth of Pennsylvania agencies provide the following: 
  * Grants: Money given for a specific purpose. Grants do not need to be repaid.


  * Loans: Money given that you must repay. 


  * Tax credits: A reduction of the money from the taxes owed.


  * Cooperative agreements: The transfer of something of value, not cash. 


  * Delegation agreements: A contract with specific responsibilities outlined.


[ Search by funding type ](https://www.pa.gov/grants/search)
###  What agencies offer grants?
Many Commonwealth of Pennsylvania agencies offer grants. pa.gov/grants features funding opportunities from more than 20 agencies.
[ Search by funding by agency ](https://www.pa.gov/grants/search)
###  How are applications evaluated?
Every grant has unique guidelines and processes. To learn more about eligibility requirements, [select a grant opportunity](https://www.pa.gov/grants/search).
