#  Planning Commission 
![Council Chambers](https://www.anaheim.net/ImageRepository/Document?documentID=7052)
The Planning Commission is comprised of seven City residents, appointed by the City Council to review both developmental proposals and policies related to land use within the City. The Commission meets alternating Mondays. The meetings are open to the public and public comment on city planning matters is encouraged.
