### Federal Workers, California Welcomes You!
Join our workforce and contribute to impacting millions of people, just like you.
We're recruiting professionals in key roles related to firefighting, weather forecasting and modeling, natural resource management, medical and mental health, and the sciences.
California values hard work, service, and looking out for each other. Your experience matters here — whatever your background or field, there’s a place for you to serve and make a difference in California.
