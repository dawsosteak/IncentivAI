#  Building Division - Electronic Plan Review 
Anaheim Building is pleased to announce the acceptance of online plan submittal using our new GoPost portal.
**If you already have a Public Works or Planning account, you will still need to create a new GoPost account for Building.**
[Go Post](https://anaheim-portal.eplansoftreview.com/#/login?portal=AnaheimBuilding) is your one stop place to:
  * Upload Initial and Resubmittals of Plans
  * Submit Applications and Forms
  * Receive Corrections


Please Note…for the best experience, we recommend using an updated version of **Chrome or Edge**
## Payment Required
Payment is required prior to your plan being accepted. After you complete your submittal, you will receive an email with the amount due, you may pay this amount by contacting the Building department at 714-765-5153. Once payment is received your plan will be routed for review.
