#  Electronic Plan Review Information 
## **Electronic Plan Review Information**
  1. **Create a GoPost login, if you don’t already have one:**
    1. [GoPost online portal](https://anaheim.eplansoftreview.com/#/login?portal=AnaheimFire)
    2. [Walkthrough Video Guide](https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=EeDPsGL2tUQ)
  2. **Project Submission & Payment**
    1. For new submittals, click the green plus sign in the lower right corner.
      1. Fill out project information and upload plans, [Plan Review Application](https://www.anaheim.net/DocumentCenter/View/54379/Fire-Construction-Permit-Application----Online-Fillable?bidId=), and any additional supporting documents.
         * Ensure the application is complete and that the contractor’s license, workers’ comp., and Anaheim business license are active to speed up processing and reduce the delay of project acceptance.
      2. Click on the green SUBMIT button in the top right corner to complete the submission.
    2. For resubmittals, locate existing project on your dashboard and click on three vertical dots, then click “Edit.”
      1. Upload revised plans and any additional documents as needed.
      2. Click on green SUBMIT button on top right corner to complete submission.
    3. Please allow 2-3 business days for our Fire Development staff to review the submission. If submission is deemed complete, you will receive instructions on how to make the payment.
      1. Payments can be made online with a Visa, Master Card or Discover card (No American Express) through our [Anaheim Next](https://aca-prod.accela.com/ANAHEIM/Default.aspx) portal or by check made payable to: City of Anaheim
    4. After receiving payment, the project will be processed for plan check review.
  3. **Plan Review**
    1. Please allow up to 30 business days for initial plan reviews or up to 15 business days for resubmittal plan reviews.
    2. To request an expedited plan review, please email Assistant Fire Marshal David Rodriguez at DRodriguez4@anaheim.net and include FPI#. If approved, he will notify the Fire Development team to expedite the submittal.
  4. **Corrections**
    1. You will receive an email notification if the plans require corrections.
    2. Corrections package may be downloaded by opening the project on GoPost and clicking on the Deliverables tab.
  5. **Approval & Permit Issuance**
    1. You will receive an automatic system notification once your project is approved. Please contact our Fire Development team at (714) 765-4000 to schedule an appointment to pick up your issued permit.
    2. Stamped approved plans will be emailed at the time of permit pickup.


If you require further assistance or have any questions, please call our Fire Development team at (714) 765-4000.
