## How We Help
![Two business people shaking hands.](https://gss.omb.delaware.gov/images/home/contracting.jpg)
#### Contracting and Procurement Services
The State of Delaware Procurement Portal, [MyMarketplace](https://mymarketplace.delaware.gov/), provides comprehensive information on how to do business with the State, and provides access to bid solicitations, awarded contracts, and other procurement details.
  

![Group of Fleet Services vehicles.](https://gss.omb.delaware.gov/images/home/fleet.jpg)
#### Fleet Services
The [Fleet Services](https://gss.omb.delaware.gov/fleet/index.shtml) provides access to vehicles and transportation services to state agencies. The team operates a fleet of approximately 2,400 vehicles for dispatch and direct agency assignment. Daily dispatch vehicles come with 24-hour roadside assistance and all operational costs covered by a nominal fee charged to the agency, and are available from more than 22 pickup sites. The section is also responsible for investigation and resolution of all citizen complaints relating to potential abuse or misuse of all agency/school district owned vehicles.
![Delaware Surplus Services items for sale, chairs, kitchen items, and vehicles.](https://gss.omb.delaware.gov/images/home/surplus-collage.jpg)
#### Delaware Surplus Services
[Surplus Services](https://gss.omb.delaware.gov/surplus/index.shtml) is responsible for all Federal and State surplus property programs for the state of Delaware. Our goal is to simplify all of our services for our customers: declaring excess property, transferring excess property to another agency or fire company, and includes the removal of excess property.
![Person using a copy machine.](https://gss.omb.delaware.gov/images/home/copier.jpg)
#### Copier Resource Management Program
The [Copier Resource Management Program](https://gss.omb.delaware.gov/copier/index.shtml) ensures that State agencies acquire copiers that are appropriate for their work load and are cost effective. With no cost to your agency, we will consult on-site to help you consider new technologies and determine the best solution for your operation(s).
![Desk with laptop with printing and color dots on the screen.](https://gss.omb.delaware.gov/images/home/ppo.jpg)
#### Printing and Publishing
The [Printing and Publishing Office](https://gss.omb.delaware.gov/ppo/index.shtml) manages full service printing needs for State Agencies along with outsource design services. We also offer limited on-site design, layout and print consulting service(s).
![Food Distribution tractor trailers.](https://gss.omb.delaware.gov/images/home/food.jpg)
#### Food Distribution Program
The [Food Distribution Program](https://gss.omb.delaware.gov/copier/index.shtml) administers the USDA food commodity distribution program in Delaware. This program distributes food commodities to eligible schools and other child nutrition programs, elderly feeding programs, and to emergency feeding organizations serving the needy.
![Person delivering boxes.](https://gss.omb.delaware.gov/images/home/messenger-serv.jpg)
#### Messenger Services
The [](https://gss.omb.delaware.gov/ppo/index.shtml)[Messenger Services](https://gss.omb.delaware.gov/messenger/index.shtml) maintains effective courier routes for daily mail processing for all agencies, some school districts and charter schools. We sort and deliver more than 1.0 million pieces of interdepartmental mail annually while typically providing a 24-hour turnaround time.

# State Property
#### Eligibility Requirements
There are no eligibility requirements for State property. However, State law requires that we advertise available property for purchase to State agencies and volunteer fire companies for a two-week period. After the two-week period, the property is available to be sold to the general public. See a copy of our [ Flowchart](https://gss.omb.delaware.gov/surplus/documents/flowchart.xls)
  

#### Bulletin of Available State Property

#### List Service
If you would like to receive a copy of the Surplus Services Bulletin and other Surplus Services information via email, you can subscribe to our list.
##### Registration Instructions:
Send an email to: listserv@listserv.delaware.gov
The email should have a **blank Subject** and the body of the email should be exactly as listed below with nothing but the following information. Please make sure you remove any signature information your email may have.
  * Subscribe surplus Firstname Lastname

The team operates a fleet of approximately 2,400 vehicles for dispatch and direct agency assignment. Daily dispatch vehicles come with 24-hour roadside assistance and all operational costs covered by a nominal fee charged to the agency, and are available from more than 22 pickup sites. The section is also responsible for investigation and resolution of all citizen complaints relating to potential abuse or misuse of all agency/school district owned vehicles.
![Delaware Surplus Services items for sale, chairs, kitchen items, and vehicles.](https://gss.omb.delaware.gov/images/home/surplus-collage.jpg)
#### Delaware Surplus Services
[Surplus Services](https://gss.omb.delaware.gov/surplus/index.shtml) is responsible for all Federal and State surplus property programs for the state of Delaware. Our goal is to simplify all of our services for our customers: declaring excess property, transferring excess property to another agency or fire company, and includes the removal of excess property.
![Person using a copy machine.](https://gss.omb.delaware.gov/images/home/copier.jpg)
#### Copier Resource Management Program
The [Copier Resource Management Program](https://gss.omb.delaware.gov/copier/index.shtml) ensures that State agencies acquire copiers that are appropriate for their work load and are cost effective. With no cost to your agency, we will consult on-site to help you consider new technologies and determine the best solution for your operation(s).
![Desk with laptop with printing and color dots on the screen.](https://gss.omb.delaware.gov/images/home/ppo.jpg)
#### Printing and Publishing
The [Printing and Publishing Office](https://gss.omb.delaware.gov/ppo/index.shtml) manages full service printing needs for State Agencies along with outsource design services. We also offer limited on-site design, layout and print consulting service(s).
![Food Distribution tractor trailers.](https://gss.omb.delaware.gov/images/home/food.jpg)
#### Food Distribution Program
The [Food Distribution Program](https://gss.omb.delaware.gov/copier/index.shtml) administers the USDA food commodity distribution program in Delaware. This program distributes food commodities to eligible schools and other child nutrition programs, elderly feeding programs, and to emergency feeding organizations serving the needy.
![Person delivering boxes.](https://gss.omb.delaware.gov/images/home/messenger-serv.jpg)
#### Messenger Services
The [](https://gss.omb.delaware.gov/ppo/index.shtml)[Messenger Services](https://gss.omb.delaware.gov/messenger/index.shtml) maintains effective courier routes for daily mail processing for all agencies, some school districts and charter schools. We sort and deliver more than 1.0 million pieces of interdepartmental mail annually while typically providing a 24-hour turnaround time.

# State Property
#### Eligibility Requirements
There are no eligibility requirements for State property. However, State law requires that we advertise available property for purchase to State agencies and volunteer fire companies for a two-week period. After the two-week period, the property is available to be sold to the general public. See a copy of our [ Flowchart](https://gss.omb.delaware.gov/surplus/documents/flowchart.xls)
