## State Property
  

### **Eligibility Requirements**
There are no eligibility requirements for State property. However, State law requires that we advertise available property for purchase to State agencies and volunteer fire companies for a two-week period. After the two-week period, the property is available to be sold to the general public. See a copy of our [Flowchart](https://gss.omb.delaware.gov/surplus/documents/flowchart.xls) so that you can better understand our process.
### **Bulletin of Available State Property**
No Current Surplus Bulletin
[Vehicle Bulletin](https://gss.omb.delaware.gov/surplus/documents/vehicle-bulletin.pdf?ver=0120) - List is updated monthly
### **List Service**
If you would like to receive a copy of the Surplus Services Bulletin and other Surplus Services information via email, you can subscribe to our list.
#### Registration is a 3-Step Process:
  1. Enter your current email address into the box below, and select the button labeled “Sign Up.”
  2. An email from **“Surplus Confirmation”** will be sent to the email address provided to verify your information. You will be prompted to click on the confirmation link in the email to finish the registration process. If you do not receive the confirmation email, try checking your email's spam and junk mail folders.
  3. Once the verification process is complete, a final confirmation email from **“Lyris ListManager”** will be sent to you to verify registration.


####  **Note:** At this time, the system does not support texting capabilities
  

To remove yourself to the list, please send an email to Normajane Davall .
