# Solar Fest 2025
## **October 4th , 2025**
#### **9AM to 1PM**
## **The Greenline at Brooks**
**_2_**** _532 Sidney Brooks St, San Antonio, TX 78235_**
### Join us at this year’s Solar Fest! This free, fun, family-friendly festival is a wonderful opportunity to celebrate with the community. Enjoy a variety of activities and engaging experiences, including learning how to save energy and water, exploring solar power, discovering Electric Vehicles, and staying informed about our city’s local resiliency efforts. Don't miss out on this exciting event!
