# Grants and Funding
Every funding opportunity available to emergency managers in Pennsylvania, all in one place.
![Governor Shapiro and emergency management officials look at flood damage on the side of a torn-up road.](https://s7d9.scene7.com/is/image/statepa/flood-damage-1?ts=1724179812383&dpr=off)
## Grant Opportunities
We administer federal and state grants that provide funding for emergency preparedness and recovery from disasters. Browse opportunities below.
  * [ Apply for a Border Protection Grant This grant provides funding to secure U.S. borders, and can be used in Erie County. ](https://www.pa.gov/services/pema/border-protection-grant)
  * [ Apply for a Cybersecurity Grant This grant helps state and local governments address online risks and strengthen cybersecurity infrastructure. ](https://www.pa.gov/services/pema/cybersecurity-grant)
  * [ Apply for an Emergency Management Performance Grant This grant provides funding for state and county governments to improve their emergency response capabilities. ](https://www.pa.gov/services/pema/emergency-management-performance-grant)
  * [ Apply for a Flood Mitigation Assistance Grant This grant provides funds to homes, businesses, and municipalities in good standing with the National Flood Insurance Program. ](https://www.pa.gov/services/pema/flood-mitigation-grant)
  * [ Apply for a Grant for Damages Due to a Federally Declared Disaster This grant provides funding to pay for damages incurred as a direct result of a federally declared disaster. ](https://www.pa.gov/services/pema/federally-declared-disaster-grant)
  * [ Apply for a Hazard Mitigation Grant This grant helps communities put hazard mitigation measures in place after a major Presidential disaster declaration. ](https://www.pa.gov/services/pema/hazard-mitigation-grant)
  * [ Apply for a Hazardous Materials Emergency Preparedness Planning and Training Grant This grant provides funding to prepare for chemical emergencies, purchase HAZMAT equipment, and more. ](https://www.pa.gov/services/pema/hazmat-prep-grant)
  * [ Apply for a Hazardous Materials Response Fund Grant This grant provides supplemental emergency preparedness funding at county and state levels. ](https://www.pa.gov/services/pema/hazmat-response-grant)
  * [ Qualify for a High Hazard Potential Dams Grant This grant aims to eliminate the risk of potentially high-hazard dams in Pennsylvania. ](https://www.pa.gov/services/pema/high-hazard-dams-grant)
  * [ Apply for a Homeland Security Grant This grant provides funding for planning, training, exercises, and other preparedness activities. ](https://www.pa.gov/services/pema/homeland-security-grant)
  * [ Apply for a Nonprofit Security Grant This grant provides funding for physical and cybersecurity enhancements to nonprofits at risk of a terrorist or other extremist attack. ](https://www.pa.gov/services/pema/nonprofit-security-grant)
  * [ Apply for a Pre-Disaster Mitigation Grant This grant provides funding to reduce risk to people and property when it comes to future natural hazards. ](https://www.pa.gov/services/pema/pre-disaster-grant)
