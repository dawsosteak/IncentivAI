# News Releases and Advisories
## California Secretary of State Communications Department
The Communications Department oversees all media relations for the California Secretary of State’s office and keeps the public informed of the latest news from the agency’s various divisions: Elections, Business Programs, Political Reform and State Archives. Media representatives interested in information about the Secretary of State’s office or obtaining interviews should call [(916) 653-6575](tel:\(916\)%20653-6575) or email sospress@sos.ca.gov.
