For more than 40 years, William Roth has called North Dallas home - raising his family, building a thriving small business, The William Roth Company, and volunteering in numerous civic and education organizations to improve the lives of fellow Dallasites. William Roth earned a bachelor's degree from George Washington University and a law degree from Southern Methodist University.  

His civic background includes the following organizations:
