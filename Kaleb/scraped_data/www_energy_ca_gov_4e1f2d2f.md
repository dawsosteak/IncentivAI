# 
Data Exploration Tools 
Data exploration tools provide an easy and accessible way to explore data and visualize trends, outliers, and patterns. CEC interactive maps and dashboards empower users to access, explore, analyze, and download various energy related datasets. For questions, contact data@energy.ca.gov
