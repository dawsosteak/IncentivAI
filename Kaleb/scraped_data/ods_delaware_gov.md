## Our Mission
The Office of Defense Services provides legal services to indigent and incarcerated clients statewide. The ODS maintains offices in New Castle County, Kent County and Sussex County.
[LEARN MORE Our Mission](https://ods.delaware.gov/our-mission/)
  

## About ODS
Learn more about the Office of Defense Services (ODS) various departments and divisions like Central Administration, the Public Defender and the Office of Conflicts Counsel.
[LEARN MORE About ODS](https://ods.delaware.gov/office-defense-services/)
  

## Our Services
The Office of Defense Services has been an innovator in developing services to better serve our clients and the criminal justice system.
[LEARN MORE Our Services](https://ods.delaware.gov/our-services/)
  

## Interpreters
The Office of Defense Services will provide necessary interpretive services for your legal representation free of charge.
