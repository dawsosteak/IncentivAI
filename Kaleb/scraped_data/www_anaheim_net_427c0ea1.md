#  Energy & Water Saving House 
The interactive Energy & Water Saving House shows you how you can lower your utility bills by installing efficiency measures, and provides links to many electric and water rebates available.

Using a drip system will minimize water loss through evaporation or runoff. Using a drip system in mulched areas is beneficial because it can directly wet the soil and prevent mulch from washing away. Drip systems use 20 to 50 percent less water than traditional pop-up sprinklers, and depending on the area, can save up to 30,000 gallons of water per year.") [![Image of rotating nozzles](https://local.anaheim.net/Utilities/EnergySavings/CloseUp_EXT_03sm.png)](https://local.anaheim.net/Utilities/EnergySavings/CloseUp_EXT_03.jpg "Rotating nozzles apply water more slowly to help reduce runoff and encourage healthy plant growth. Placing two to three inches of mulch around trees and shrubs can save up to 100 gallons per day.") They can reduce outdoor water use by up to 70% without sacrificing the quality or health of your landscape.  Soil moisture sensor systems are eligible for a rebate. <a href='https://anaheim.net/945/Water-Rebates-Incentives'>For more information</a>") [![Image of energy-efficient air conditioning](https://local.anaheim.net/Utilities/EnergySavings/CloseUp_EXT_08sm.png)](https://local.anaheim.net/Utilities/EnergySavings/CloseUp_EXT_08.jpg "Switching to an energy-efficient central air conditioning and heating system can save you between 20-50 percent, depending on your upgrade.  <a href='https://anaheim.net/1467/Home-Incentives-Program-Rebates'>For more information</a>") [![Image of energy-efficient pool pump](https://local.anaheim.net/Utilities/EnergySavings/CloseUp_EXT_09sm.png)](https://local.anaheim.net/Utilities/EnergySavings/CloseUp_EXT_09.jpg "There are a variety of ways to save money on your pool: By upgrading to an energy-efficient pool pump, turning your heat down a few degrees or when not in season or in use, and using pool covers to save energy. <a href='t/1467/Home-Incentives-Program-Rebates'>For more information</a>") [![Image of solar panels](https://local.anaheim.net/Utilities/EnergySavings/CloseUp_EXT_10sm.png)](https://local.anaheim.net/Utilities/EnergySavings/CloseUp_EXT_10.png "Adding solar panels is an excellent way to reduce energy use and costs inside of your home.
