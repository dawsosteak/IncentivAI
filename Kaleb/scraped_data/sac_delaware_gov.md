#  State of Delaware  
**Statistical Analysis Center**
  
  

[ Listen ](https://app-na.readspeaker.com/cgi-bin/rsent?customerid=7262&lang=en_us&readid=readSpeak_test&url= "Listen to this page using ReadSpeaker webReader")
  

##    
Mission
The Delaware Statistical Analysis Center provides the Governor, Legislature, and Criminal Justice Agencies with a capability for objective research analysis, and projections relating to criminal justice issues in order to improve the effectiveness of policy making, program development, planning, and reporting as delineated in Chapter 89 sections 8901-8905.  
  
[Read More about Delaware SAC](https://sac.delaware.gov/about/#mission)
![Winners of the Statistical Analysis Centers Winners of the 2019 Douglas Yearwood National Publication Award for outstanding reports, courtesy of The Justice Research and Statistics Association newsletter website.](https://sac.delaware.gov/wp-content/themes/dosgic_SAC_theme/img/2019-yearwood.jpg)  

## **Delaware SAC Wins National Award**
Delaware's Statistical Analysis Center has won the 2019 Douglas Yearwood National Publication Award for outstanding reports.
[Learn More  (Opens in a new window.)](http://www.jrsa.org/pubs/forum/articles/yearwood-2019.html)
