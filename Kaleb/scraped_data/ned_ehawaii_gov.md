Who is this app for?
This online application is for those seeking a Hawaii controlled substance registration. Registration is required for every person or organization that administers, prescribes, and/or dispenses any controlled substances within the State of Hawaii.
Practitioners, APRNs, physician assistants, in-state pharmacies, and out-of-state/mail pharmacies may submit an online application. All other applicants are required to complete the mail-in application which can be found on <https://law.hawaii.gov/divisions/law-enforcement-division/narcotics-enforcement-division/>.
What is required to use this site?
You will need an eHawaii.gov account. You may already have an account if you have used any other State of Hawaii online services such as:
  * MyPVL - DCCA Professional Vocational License renewals
  * Dept. of Health - Marriage License and Birth Certificate online ordering and tracking
  * Hawaii Business Express


Carefully read the instructions before logging in to ensure you have the necessary documents required. You may visit <https://law.hawaii.gov/divisions/law-enforcement-division/narcotics-enforcement-division/> for more information.
