# Behavioral Health Council
In October 2023, Governor Josh Shapiro established the Pennsylvania Behavioral Health Council through an executive order. The purpose of this action and development of the Council is to address any gaps in access, affordability, and delivery of mental health and substance use disorder services in the Commonwealth of Pennsylvania.
[ Management Directive 720.05 Amended ](https://www.pa.gov/content/dam/copapwp-pagov/en/governor/behavioral-health-council/Executive-Order-2023-20-Pennsylvania-Behavioral-Health-Council.pdf)
[ Press Release ](https://www.pa.gov/governor/newsroom/2023-press-releases/governor-shapiro-signs-executive-order-streamlining-mental-healt)

### Meet the Behavioral Health Council
The Council is comprised of 33 members, and it includes a diverse array of stakeholders with representation from state, county, and local governments, the provider community, advocacy groups, and individuals with lived experiences. All will seek to create a more holistic healthcare delivery system in Pennsylvania.

**REPORTS**
Secretary Hossain took roll call. Secretary Hossain gave some opening remarks and introduced the topic for the meeting, which was a continuation of the funding streams for behavioral health. He mentioned that there will first be some presentations to guide our understanding of the topic, then a discussion with Council members led by Dr. Finello.
* * *
The speaker from the state was from the Pennsylvania Department of Insurance, Lindsi Swartz, who is the Director – Life, Accident, and Health Rate and Policy Form Review in the Bureau of Life, Accident, and Health Insurance. The other speakers represented commercial insurance providers. They were Dr. Mike Shepherd, who is the Senior Medical Director for Behavioral Health at Geisinger Health Plan and Dr. Matthew Hurford, who is the Vice President of Behavioral Health at UPMC Insurance Division. After the presentations, Dr. Finello led a facilitated discussion with questions that were given to Council members prior to the meeting. The results of the discussion will be used as part of the Statewide Action Plan.
* * *
Dr. Finello ended the meeting with some closing remarks and thanked the Council members for their time.
