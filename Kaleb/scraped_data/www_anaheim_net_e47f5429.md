#  Youth Sports Camps, Clinics & Leagues 
## Activities
  
Programs are offered seasonally on a year round basis. View which camps, clinics and leagues are currently being offered in the [online programs and activities guide](https://www.anaheim.net/play).  
  

## Registration
  
[Online registration](https://www.anaheim.net/play) is available to sign up for community activities. You may also fill out the [Youth Sports Registration and Hold Harmless Form](https://www.anaheim.net/DocumentCenter/View/2328) and take it in on the first day of the camp, clinic or league your child is attending.
