#  EMT Scholarship Program 
Anaheim Fire & Rescue, in partnership with the Anaheim Community Foundation, is proud to offer an Emergency Medical Technician (EMT) Scholarship Program for those interested in a career in Fire. This program complements our existing Explorer Program and Ambulance Operator Program by providing financial assistance to applicants pursuing their EMT certification, a prerequisite for becoming an Ambulance Operator.
### **How It Works**
The EMT Scholarship Program is available to students ready to begin their EMT courses at a local community college or private school. 
### **Eligibility and Benefits**
Age Requirement: Applicants must be at least 18 years old.  
Scholarship Coverage: Up to $1,700 for tuition, books, uniforms, and the National Registry Exam.  
Unused Funds: Funds are not disbursed directly to recipients. All funding is paid directly to the educational institution. Any unused funds will remain with the Anaheim Community Foundation and be allocated to future scholarship recipients.  
Selected recipients: Need to provide a headshot and status updates on attaining EMT certification to assist in promoting the EMT Scholarship in the future.
### **To Apply**
We are accepting applications from April 9, 2026, through May 8, 2026. Please review the program [flyer](https://www.anaheim.net/DocumentCenter/View/70394/EMT-Scholarship-Flyer) for important details. To apply, download and submit this [application](https://www.anaheim.net/DocumentCenter/View/70395/2026-EMT-Scholarship-Application) form. Please read all instructions carefully to ensure you submit a complete application.
### **Donate Now**
Anaheim Fire & Rescue actively seeks donations and fundraising opportunities to sustain this scholarship program. Your contributions will directly impact aspiring public safety professionals in our community. If you would like to donate, please [click here](https://app.donorview.com/9Kv01).
### **Contact Us**
For more information, call us at 714-765-4036.
