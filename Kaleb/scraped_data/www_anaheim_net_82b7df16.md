#  Bill Assistance 
We understand that some of our customers may experience financial setbacks or hardships.
We offer several options to assist with bill payment and help get you back on track. We also provide the necessary documentation you may need to seek third-party assistance.  
  
Give us a call at 714-765-3300 and we'll walk you through the best options for your situation. 
  1. [Bill Deferrals](https://www.anaheim.net/6524/Bill-Assistance#taba48ad0e0-b279-4812-8596-7b070949272b_0)


We understand that some of our customers may experience financial setbacks or hardships. For those who have fallen behind, we offer payment extensions and will work with you to get you back on track.
## Extensions & Payment Plans
A customer may request a 10 calendar day extension if they are not able to pay by the bill due date.
For past due bills requiring a longer period of time to pay, a customer alternatively may request a payment plan. See [Deferred Payment Assistance](https://www.anaheim.net/DocumentCenter/View/2045/Deferred-Payment-Assistance-PDF?bidId=) for more information about each option.
## Monthly Billing
Monthly Billing is a monthly bill for bi-monthly billed customers. See [Monthly Billing](https://www.anaheim.net/formcenter/public-utilities-6/monthly-billing-enrollment-form-130) to read more about the billing option and submit a form for enrollment.
## Level Pay
Level Pay is a monthly bill plan where a customer may make an average payment based on their last 12 months of previous usage. See [Level Pay](https://anaheim.net/6537/Level-Pay-Plan) for more information.
  1. [EMERGENCY ASSISTANCE PROGRAM](https://www.anaheim.net/6524/Bill-Assistance#taba48ad0e0-b279-4812-8596-7b070949272b_1)


The Anaheim Public Utilities Emergency Assistance Program provides financial assistance to qualified residential customers facing temporary financial hardship. Eligible customers may receive a credit of **$250 toward electric service and $100 for water service,** once every three (3) years as emergency payment assistance.
  * [Emergency Assistance Application](https://anaheim.net/FormCenter/Public-Utilities-6/Emergency-Assistance-Application-373)


  1. [INCOME-QUALIFIED PROGRAMS](https://www.anaheim.net/6524/Bill-Assistance#taba48ad0e0-b279-4812-8596-7b070949272b_2)


Customers who meet income-qualified guidelines may be eligible to participate in a number of income-qualified programs. 
Programs include senior, military veteran, and long-term disabled discounts, community-produced solar bill credit, as well as home efficiency programs. See [Income-Qualified Programs](https://www.anaheim.net/2546/Income-Qualified-Programs) for more information.
  * [Income-Qualified Programs Application (English)](https://www.anaheim.net/DocumentCenter/View/25981/Income-Qualified-Energy--Water-Discount-Application--?bidId=)
  * [Income-Qualified Programs Application (Spanish)](https://anaheim.net/DocumentCenter/View/25982/Income-Qualified-Energy--Water-Discount-Application-Spanish?bidId=)


  1. [FEDERAL LOW INCOME ASSISTANCE PROGRAMS](https://www.anaheim.net/6524/Bill-Assistance#taba48ad0e0-b279-4812-8596-7b070949272b_3)


### Electric Bill Assistance Program
Anaheim customers can receive utility payment assistance on their **electric bill** through the federally-funded **Low Income Home Energy Assistance Program (LIHEAP)** administered through the Community Action Partnership of Orange County (CAPOC).   
  
Funding is based on monthly income. Customers may view income qualifications and apply online or call CAPOC at (714) 839-6199 for more information. Once approved, a bill credit will be **directly applied to your account**.
  * [Low Income Home Energy Assistance Program (LIHEAP)](https://www.caliheapapply.com/)


  1. [MEDICAL ALLOWANCES](https://www.anaheim.net/6524/Bill-Assistance#taba48ad0e0-b279-4812-8596-7b070949272b_4)


If you or someone who lives in your home uses **life-support equipment** , you may be eligible to pay a lower rate for some of your electricity. View the application below for details and a list of qualifying medical equipment. 
  * [Medical Lifeline Allowance Application](http://anaheim.net/DocumentCenter/View/369)
