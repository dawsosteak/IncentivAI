## **3 Ways to Vote in the Statewide Special Election**
[![Voting options](https://elections.cdn.sos.ca.gov/images/make-plan-vote-vote-early.jpg)](http://caearlyvoting.sos.ca.gov/)[![Voting options](https://elections.cdn.sos.ca.gov/images/make-plan-vote-vbm.jpg)](https://www.sos.ca.gov/elections/voter-registration/vote-mail)[![Voting options](https://elections.cdn.sos.ca.gov/images/make-plan-vote-vip.jpg)](https://www.sos.ca.gov/elections/polling-place)
**All California active registered voters will receive a vote-by-mail ballot for the November 4, 2025, Statewide Special Election. Your county elections office will begin mailing vote-by-mail ballots no later than October 6, 2025.**
### Early Voting
California's early voting period began October 6, 2025, and allows you to cast your vote in person before Election Day. 
Vote-by-mail ballot drop-off locations opened around the state on October 7, 2025.
Find an [early voting and ballot drop-off location](https://caearlyvoting.sos.ca.gov/).
### Mail-In Voting
Any registered voter may vote by mail. Your ballot must be postmarked or placed in a drop-box by 8:00 p.m. on Election Day.
Request a [replacement vote-by-mail ballot](https://www.sos.ca.gov/elections/voter-registration/vote-mail).
To make sure your ballot is counted, return it early by mail, drop box, or vote center. If you mail it, do it early — or walk it into the post office and request a postmark.
### Vote on Election Day 
You can vote in person on Election Day by going to your designated voting location.
[Find your polling place](https://www.sos.ca.gov/elections/polling-place)
## **Register to Vote!**
You can register to vote or update your voter registration in just a few minutes. The last day to make changes online or change your political party for the November 4, 2025, Statewide Special Election, was October 20, 2025.
If you missed the deadline, don't worry—you can still [register the same day](https://www.sos.ca.gov/elections/voter-registration/same-day-reg) you vote at your [local voting location](https://www.sos.ca.gov/elections/polling-place).

## **What's on The Ballot?**
[![Official Voter Information Guide](https://elections.cdn.sos.ca.gov/images/vig-square-sml.png)](https://voterguide.sos.ca.gov/)
If you are a registered voter, you will receive two voter information guides by mail:
  * Your **State Voter Information Guide**(VIG) contains information about when and where to cast your ballot, statewide ballot propositions, and your rights as a California voter. The State Voter Information Guide is only mailed prior to a statewide election.
  * Your **County Voter Information Guide** contains a sample of your ballot and information about local candidates and ballot measures, and, for some counties, the location of your polling place. A County Voter Information Guide is sent prior to statewide and local elections.


### State Voter Information Guide
The Official Voter Information Guide for the November 4, 2025, Statewide Special Election can be found online at [voterguide.sos.ca.gov](https://voterguide.sos.ca.gov/).
[Prop 50 Quick Reference Guide](https://voterguide.sos.ca.gov/quick-reference-guide/50.htm)
### Local Candidates and Measures
The candidates and questions on your ballot depend on the county you live in. Contact your local [county elections office](https://www.sos.ca.gov/elections/voting-resources/county-elections-offices) to see a sample ballot.
## **Important Dates & Deadlines**
**Monday | OCTOBER 20, 2025**
[Deadline to Register to Vote Online or by Mail](https://www.sos.ca.gov/elections/voter-registration)
The last day to register to vote or update your voter registration online or by mail. Registrations being mailed must be postmarked by October 20, 2025.
* * *
**OCTOBER 21 - NOVEMBER 4, 2025**
[Same Day Voter Registration](https://www.sos.ca.gov/elections/voter-registration/same-day-reg)
Eligible citizens who need to register or re-register to vote within 14 days of an election can complete this process to register and vote at their county elections office, polling place, or vote center. Their ballots will be processed and counted once the county elections office has completed the voter registration verification process.
* * *
**OCTOBER 6 - NOVEMBER 4, 2025**
[Early Voting](https://caearlyvoting.sos.ca.gov/)
  * Vote centers opened for early in-person voting in all [Voter’s Choice Act](https://www.sos.ca.gov/voters-choice-act) counties beginning on October 25, 2025.


[Find Your Local Voting Location](https://caearlyvoting.sos.ca.gov/)
* * *
**THURSDAY | OCTOBER 28, 2025**
[Recommended Deadline To Request a Replacement Vote-By-Mail Ballot to be Delivered by Mail](https://www.sos.ca.gov/elections/voter-registration/vote-mail)
To receive a replacement vote-by-mail ballot by mail, submit your request for a replacement vote-by-mail ballot application by October 28, 2025 to help facilitate its timely arrival. For requests after this date, contact your [County Elections Office](https://www.sos.ca.gov/elections/voting-resources/county-elections-offices). 
[Request a Replacement Vote-By-Mail Ballot by Mail](https://elections.cdn.sos.ca.gov/vote-by-mail/pdf/replacement-application.pdf)
* * *
**BY TUESDAY | NOVEMBER 4, 2025**
[Return Mail-In Ballot](https://www.sos.ca.gov/elections/where-and-how)
BY MAIL: Make sure your vote-by-mail ballot return envelope is postmarked by November 4. No stamp needed! To make sure your ballot is counted, return it early by mail, drop box, or vote center. If you mail it, do it early — or walk it into the post office and request a postmark.
DROP BOX: Drop off your completed vote-by-mail ballot at a secure official drop box in your county at any time between October 7 through the close of polls on November 4. [Find a Drop Box or Voting Location Near You](https://caearlyvoting.sos.ca.gov/)
IN PERSON: Drop your completed vote-by-mail ballot off at a secure drop box, polling place, vote center, or county elections office by 8:00 p.m. on November 4. Voting locations will be available in all counties before Election Day. [Voting locations](https://caearlyvoting.sos.ca.gov/) offer voter registration, replacement ballots, accessible voting machines, and language assistance.
TRACK YOUR BALLOT: Sign up at  _[wheresmyballot.sos.ca.gov](https://california.ballottrax.net/voter/)_ to receive updates on the status of your vote-by-mail ballot by text message (SMS), email, or voice call.
* * *
**TUESDAY | NOVEMBER 4, 2025**
[Election Day](https://www.sos.ca.gov/elections/voter-registration)
This is the final day you can cast a vote. Polls are open from 7:00 a.m. to 8:00 p.m.. As long as you’re in line by 8:00 p.m., you will be able to cast your vote.
[Find Your Vote Location](https://www.sos.ca.gov/elections/polling-place)
