Discover the Express Business Center (EBC), a division of the Office of Procurement Services. The EBC delivers a range of affordable services to City departments, government employees and the public.
![EBC Logo](https://dallascityhall.com/departments/procurement/PublishingImages/ebc-logo.png)
## Services we can assist you with:  

  * Color and B/W Printing
  * Posters, banners, photo prints
  * Signage
  * Flyers and Brochures
  * Newsletters
  * Mail Services
  * Notary Services
  * Faxing Services
  * Snack foods, beverages, candies, ice cream, medicines, sandwiches, balloons, gift items, etc.
  * and much more come see us!


UPS package drop-off service is available at the EBC (M-F pick up at 4 p.m.).
