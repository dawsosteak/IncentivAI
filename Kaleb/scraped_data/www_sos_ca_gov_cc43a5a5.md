# Committee Annual Fee and Penalty
Government Code section 84101.5 imposes an annual fee on all active (not terminated) state and local campaign committees. The annual fee of $50 is due no later than January 15, 2026, if the committee will continue to exist in 2026, regardless of the level of activity.
_To process your payment online, you will be required to enter your Committee ID Number. Please enter payment information for only one Committee at a time._ Payments that are not able to be matched to an outstanding liability, based on the information provided, will be refunded and the fee and/or penalty will remain due to California Secretary of State.
**If the committee only owes the $50 annual fee, please pay a total amount of $50.**
Acknowledge & Proceed With $50 Payment
**If the committee _paid_ the $50 annual fee after the due date, please pay only the $150 penalty. **
Acknowledge & Proceed With $150 Payment
**If the committee has _not paid_ the $50 annual fee, please pay a total amount of $200. **
Acknowledge & Proceed With $200 Payment
If you have any questions regarding your payment amount due, or to process a single payment for multiple committees, please contact the California Secretary of State Political Reform Division between 8:00am - 5:00pm, Monday-Friday, at 916-653-6224.
