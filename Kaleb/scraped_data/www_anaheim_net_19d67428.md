#  Waste and Recycling Collection 
## Waste and Recycling Collection Schedule
The city of Anaheim **contracts with Republic Services** to provide waste collection and recycling services. Access the [map](https://www.anaheim.net/DocumentCenter/View/40815/Residential-trash-collection-map-Dec-2021) to find your residential collection day.
Download the [Solid Waste Franchise Agreement](https://www.anaheim.net/DocumentCenter/View/57801/REPUBLIC-WASTE-SERVICES-OF-SOUTHERN-CALIFORNIA-LLC).
## Holiday collection schedule
If your regular collection day falls on or after one of these holidays, your trash, recycling and green waste will be picked up one day late.
### **Holiday**
| 
### **Day and date**
| 
### **Residential collection**  
---|---|---  
Memorial Day | Monday, May 25, 2026 | One day delay  
Fourth of July | Saturday, July 4, 2026 | holiday falls on a Saturday, there will be **no** delays  
  
Labor Day | Monday, September 7, 2026 | One day delay  
Thanksgiving  
| Thursday, November 26, 2026  
| One day delay  
  
Christmas Day  
| Friday, December 25, 2026  
| One day delay  
  
New Year's Day  
| Friday, January 1, 2027  
| One day delay  
  
  

When the holiday falls on a weekday or a Sunday, residential trash collection will be delayed one day for the remainder of the week. If the holiday falls on a Saturday, there will be **no** delays.
