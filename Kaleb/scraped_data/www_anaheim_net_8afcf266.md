#  Notice of Preparation and Scoping Meeting 
As the first step in the PEIR process, the City prepared a Notice of Preparation (NOP) and will hold a public scoping meeting. The purpose of the NOP and the public scoping meeting is to solicit comments and suggestions regarding the scope and content of the PEIR to be prepared.
## Notice of Preparation (NOP)
[](https://www.anaheim.net/DocumentCenter/View/42370/Anaheim-GPU-NOP_Final)[](https://www.anaheim.net/DocumentCenter/View/42712/Anaheim-GPU-NOP_Final)[](https://www.anaheim.net/DocumentCenter/View/42712/Anaheim-GPU-NOP_Final)
[Click here](https://www.anaheim.net/DocumentCenter/View/42712/Anaheim-GPU-NOP_Final) for the PDF copy of the NOP.
The NOP comment period ran from February 16, 2022 to March 18, 2022, and is now closed.
## Public Scoping Meeting
The City held a virtual public scoping meeting to provide members of the public with an opportunity to learn about the project, ask questions, and provide comments about the scope and content of the information to be addressed in the PEIR. You can find the details and materials for the scoping meeting below: 
**Date:** Wednesday, March 2, 2022
**Time:** 6:00 p.m.
**Where:** On-line (Zoom)
**Materials:**[PowerPoint Presentation](https://www.anaheim.net/DocumentCenter/View/42710/Scoping-Meeting-PPT---Website-Posting)
