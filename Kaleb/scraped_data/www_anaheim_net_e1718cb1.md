#  Level Pay Plan 
The Level Pay Plan offers a solution for those seeking **consistent utility bills** year-round.
Based on your usage from the last 12 months, **an average will be applied to your bills** for 11 months. Differences between actual usage and estimated usage will be sorted out in the last month.
  * An adjustment, or true-up, will be applied in the final bill to ensure that the total paid matches the actual usage by refunding an excess amount or charging for any additional usage beyond what was estimated.


Please note that the Level Pay Plan _does not_ reduce the amount of your bill. Customers are still responsible for paying the full amount of energy used.
## Plan Details & Requirements  
  

  * The Level Pay Plan option is available for customers on a **monthly billing cycle**. If you would like to sign up for the Level Pay Plan but are currently billed every other month (bimonthly), we can switch you to monthly payments. 
  * Eligible customers will need to have a**minimum of 12 months' billing history** at their current address to enroll in the Level Pay Plan. The history helps calculate an average amount that takes into consideration seasonal differences in usage.
  * Your utility account must be **in good standing** and **clear of any remaining balance**.


Note: At this time, **solar accounts are not eligible** for the Level Pay Plan. All customers are eligible to receive their bills monthly, without the Level Pay Plan. 
  * Call 714-765-3300 to enroll.

## Level Pay Plan – FAQ’S  
  

**_How is my Level Pay Plan calculated?_**  
A: The Level Pay Plan is calculated based on your prior 12-month usage history. It is an average amount billed each month for the first 11 months, and then a true-up bill will occur the last month. There will be quarterly reviews where you might see a change in your average Level Pay amount.
**_My Level Pay amount due changed from the last bill, why?_**   
A: Accounts are quarterly reviewed and adjusted accordingly. 
**_My Level Pay Plan amount due is too high, can it be adjusted?_**  
A: Accounts are quarterly reviewed and adjusted accordingly. If you feel the amount is still too high, please contact customer service.
**_Can I be removed from the Level Pay Plan program?_**  
A: Yes, customers can elect to be removed from the Level Pay Plan at any time. Customers can re-enroll in the program, but all outstanding charges must be current.
**_Can I get a payment extension or promise to pay?_**  
A: Yes. However, this would require you to be removed from the Level Pay Plan program. Customers can re-enroll in the program, but all outstanding charges must be current. 
**_Can credit on the Level Pay Plan be used towards the payment due?_**  
A: Credits resulting from the billing of the account cannot be used towards the Level Pay amount. 
**_Can I make an overpayment?_**  
A: Yes, overpayments on your account will be applied towards your future Level Pay amount due. 
**_I am moving to another address in Anaheim, will I still be enrolled in Level Pay Plan?_**  
A: Customers need to be at their place of business for a minimum of 12 months in order to have enough usage history to calculate a level pay amount. Please contact us to re-enroll on the anniversary date of your move. We’d be happy to sign you up again.
**_I missed my payment, what happens with my Level Pay Plan?_**   
A: Customers who fail to submit full payment by the due date will be candidates for removal. Customers can re-enroll after settling their outstanding balance.
