#  Get Involved 
13th Marine Expeditionary Unit (13th MEU)  
The 13th Marine Expeditionary Unit, stationed out of Camp Pendleton was formally adopted by the Anaheim City Council in 2007 to show support for the military personnel and their families.  
  
The 13th MEU adoption committee works effortlessly to support the 13th MEU Marines and Sailors and their families on an ongoing basis, both when the unit is at home and deployed. In return, the USMC will provide community support by participating in local school programs and various city functions. 
![A group of 4 Marines representing the 13th MEU](https://www.anaheim.net/ImageRepository/Document?documentID=3922)
To learn more about the [13th MEU adoption committee](https://www.anaheim.net/1673/Anaheim-Marine-Adoption-Committee) and how to support Anaheim's adopted military unit, please visit our [Donations and Sponsors page](https://www.anaheim.net/FormCenter/City-Administration-10/Military-Banner-Sponsors-78).
