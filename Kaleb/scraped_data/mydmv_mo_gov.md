## Alert Messages
  * ![alert message](https://mydmv.mo.gov/img/alert1.png)
Scam Alert! 
The Missouri Department of Revenue is warning Missourians of ongoing nationwide text scams. These texts are often threatening, claiming to be from the Missouri Department of Revenue or Department of Motor Vehicles and may include a warning regarding unpaid traffic violations, outstanding toll charges, or fines owed. 
  

  * ![alert message](https://mydmv.mo.gov/img/alert1.png)
Business License May Be Expired 
If your business license expired on 12/31/2025, you are not permitted to operate as a Missouri Dealer by Missouri state statute. It is very pertinent that you renew ASAP. Beginning 02/01/2026, any business license account with an expired business license status will no longer be authorized users for MyDMV, Online Notice of Sale, Temporary Permit Registration, and Notice of Lien Record Lookup. Once your license has been made active, your account accesses will be re-activated. If you need assistance, please email dealerlic@dor.mo.gov or call 573-526-3669 option 7.

#  Department of Revenue Electronic Notification Services 
The Department of Revenue offers electronic notification for Missouri citizens to sign up to receive renewal notices and other DOR related information by email or cellular/mobile text message! If you would like to start receiving notifications from the Department regarding your Motor Vehicle Renewal, Permanent Disabled Placard, Boat Renewal, or other DOR related information, please subscribe through the electronic notification option below. 
Once subscribed for cellular/mobile text message alerts, MoNotify will send a text alert to the subscribed cellular/mobile number confirming the subscription. You MUST confirm to receiving the alerts before the Department can begin sending notifications, including renewal notices, to the cellular/mobile number provided. 
NOTE: A record can only have one email and one cellular/mobile number subscription. 
If you would like to subscribe to receive your driver license or nondriver license renewal notices visit <https://mydmv2.mo.gov/TAP/?link=Notifications>
If you would like to subscribe to receive individual or business taxation notifications, please visit the online [MyTax Portal.](https://mytax.mo.gov/rptp/portal/home)
##  Subscribe or Update 
Subscribe or Update 
Unsubscribe 
I would like to subscribe to receiving electronic notifications or to update my current subscription. 
NOTE: When subscribing to receive electronic notifications, you will not receive a paper renewal by mail. If updating your current subscription, please note that any updates made will override any previous information provided. 
I would like to unsubscribe to receiving electronic notifications. 
NOTE: When unsubscribing within 3 months of expiration, you will not receive a paper renewal by mail. To unsubscribe, you will be required to re-enter all data for electronic notifications. 
##  Email / Cellular/Mobile Number 
Please provide the following information for the option you wish to receive an electronic notification: 
When unsubscribing an electronic notification will be transmitted to the email and/or SMS currently on file. To unsubscribe to all electronic notifications (email and SMS/Text Message), you will need to provide the following information: 
Email Address
Confirm Email Address
Cellular/Mobile Number
