# Together We Can Do More
## CORE works collaboratively with nonprofits throughout Colorado to increase our carbon reduction efforts
Recognizing that we’re stronger together, we align our work with these valued partners to amplify and strengthen climate action.  

“Alone we can do so little; together we can do so much.” — Helen Keller  
That’s why we’re proud to work alongside other mission-driven organizations across Colorado.

## Walking Mountains Science Center
Service Area: Eagle Valley
A black and white drawing of a star on a white background.
Walking Mountains Science Center provides locals and visitors from pre-k to gray with opportunities to explore nature, gain a scientific understanding, and learn about the many wonders of our mountain environment through natural science and sustainability programs. Walking Mountains offers home energy assessments, financial incentives, and energy efficiency programs to help property owners save money and improve their buildings. ﻿
