#####  Highest-Priority Strategies
  * #### Complete a resource recovery audit 
  * #### Increase resource recovery options
  * #### Establish resource recovery policies
  * #### Design a food waste diversion and composting program
  * #### Advocate for expanded producer responsibility (EPR) legislation and actively work with stakeholders to fund Implementation Program
  * #### Expand Neighborhood Ambassadors and Neighborhood Environmental Ambassadors Programs
