# Workforce Services Division
The Workforce Services Division (WSD) is charged with three primary goals to ensure that workforce development in Montana remains strong and growing.
  * Montana employers understand the labor market and have a skilled and ready workforce that meets their needs, today and tomorrow.
  * Montana workers understand the job market and have access to skills development opportunities and job search assistance services.
  * Business, job seekers, educators, students, workforce and economic development partners, and the general public all have a place to go to learn about the workforce and a way to connect with each other to keep it growing.


WSD provides services to all Montana businesses and citizens via a network of 18 [Job Service offices](https://montanaworks.gov/job-service-montana/index.html) and 4 dedicated [engagement teams](https://wsd.dli.mt.gov/business-engagement) staffed by committed and talented workforce professionals serving workers, businesses, and education entities across the state. If you have any questions about how we might help you, please feel free to explore our website, contact your nearest Job Service office or the engagement team right for you.
