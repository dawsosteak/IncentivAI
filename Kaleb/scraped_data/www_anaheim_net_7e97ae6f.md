#  Fiber Lease Agreement 
  
Anaheim Public Utilities operates a fiber optics network to control and monitor its water and electric systems and provide connectivity for municipal services. The fiber network has spare capacity that broadband service providers can lease to provide high-speed internet access to their customer in Anaheim. Here’s the link to view the fiber optics lease agreement: [Sample fiber optics lease agreement](http://anaheim.net/DocumentCenter/View/23291/Sample-Fiber-Lease-Agreement) and [fiber lease rates](http://www.anaheim.net/DocumentCenter/View/23293/Anaheims-Fiber-Optics-Leasing-Guidelines). 
For more information on wireless improvements by various cell companies providers, please refer to the below links:  
  

  * [Verizon](https://www.verizon.com/about/our-company/5g/what-5g)
  * [AT&T](https://www.att.com/5g/consumer/)
  * [T-Mobile](https://www.t-mobile.com/5g)
  * [Sprint](https://www.sprint.com/en/landings/5g.html)
  * [Crown Castle](https://www.crowncastle.com/communities/5g-information)
  * [Mobilitie](http://www.mobilitie.com/connectedcity/index.html)
