Montana offers a variety of opportunities to help you make a difference in your community and further your career. We offer competitive benefits, work-life balance, and family friendly policies. As a service-oriented employer, we seek employees with a passion for customer service. Explore the links below to apply for a state job or learn more about our career opportunities and the benefits of working for the state.
Visit your local [Job Service Workforce Center](https://montanaworks.gov/job-service-montana/#js-locations) or view our [Candidate User Guide](https://statecareers.mt.gov/_docs/userguideCandidateTutorial.pdf) to get help applying for a job.
### EMPLOYER OF NATIONAL SERVICE
![Americorps Logo](https://statecareers.mt.gov/_images/AC_State-Logo_MT-stacked.jpg) ![Peace Corps Logo](https://statecareers.mt.gov/_images/peacecorps.png)
The State of Montana is an [Employer of National Service ](https://americorps.gov/partner/partnerships/employers-national-service-search) and strongly encourages AmeriCorps and Peace Corps alumni to apply for jobs with state government. Montana State Government is the ideal place to continue your commitment to service.
**Please note** , we do not award hiring preference to national service alumni.
### CONTACT US
Do you have questions about our online application system? Please contact [State HR Help](https://statehr.custhelp.com/).
If you have questions about a specific job listing or agency hiring process please contact the agency directly. You can find contact info on the bottom of each posted job listing.
