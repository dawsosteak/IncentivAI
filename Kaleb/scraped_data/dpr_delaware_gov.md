## [Interstate Telehealth Registrations](https://dpr.delaware.gov/applyingforlicense/)
Pursuant to [_24 _Del. C._ Ch. 60_](https://delcode.delaware.gov/title24/c060/index.html), health-care providers licensed by the professional licensing boards listed in [_§ 6002_](https://delcode.delaware.gov/title24/c060/index.html#6002), may apply for an interstate telehealth registration providing the requirements in § 6002(c) are met. See the [**Telehealth Registration**](https://dpr.delaware.gov/#all) page on your profession’s webpage for instructions on how to apply.

## [Interstate Telehealth Registrations](https://dpr.delaware.gov/applyingforlicense/)
Pursuant to [_24 _Del. C._ Ch. 60_](https://delcode.delaware.gov/title24/c060/index.html), health-care providers licensed by the professional licensing boards listed in [_§ 6002_](https://delcode.delaware.gov/title24/c060/index.html#6002), may apply for an interstate telehealth registration providing the requirements in § 6002(c) are met. See the [**Telehealth Registration**](https://dpr.delaware.gov/#all) page on your profession’s webpage for instructions on how to apply.
