#### Registration
###### (Getting Started)
Registration is easy and only takes a few minutes.
You need to provide your Social Security Number, date of birth, and some information from an earnings statement with an issue date between January 2017 and today. An earnings statement could be a direct deposit or warrant notice. Upon successful validation, you will be asked to enter your email address and create a user name and password.
