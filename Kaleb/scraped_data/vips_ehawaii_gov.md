## Aloha and Welcome to Volunteers in Public Service (VIPS) to the Courts
The mission of the VIPS Program is to facilitate and promote opportunities for citizen involvement and participation within the Judiciary based on the needs of the Judiciary and the skills, talents and interests of the volunteers. The goals of the program are: 
  1. To increase public awareness of the Judiciary, its programs and the judicial process;
  2. To enhance the level of services to the community and Judiciary clients through effective use of volunteers working with court personnel.


Volunteering provides many benefits, such as work experience, professional and personal contacts, enhancing or retention of work skills, and the opportunity to help others and make a difference. 
### Volunteering on Oahu
To volunteer on Oahu, fill out our easy-to-use [on-line application](https://vips.ehawaii.gov/vips/app/newApplicantType.html;jsessionid=1B8EE8F740810CF272E15949909A535D.app6). [Click here](http://www.courts.state.hi.us/docs/docs6/volunteer_positions_final.pdf) to view a list of available volunteer opportunities for adults. Positions are also available for [high school students](http://www.courts.state.hi.us/docs/docs6/vips_hs_flyer_for_website.pdf). 
### Volunteering on the Neighbor Islands
If you are interested in volunteering on Hawaii, Kauai, Lanai, Maui or Molokai, fill out our online [Volunteer Information Form](https://forms.ehawaii.gov/view.php?id=61) and indicate your skills and areas of interest. 
**_Hint:_** To help us place you in a volunteer position based on your skills and our immediate needs, it is a good idea to include your resume. You can either upload it or paste it in text format in the Volunteer Information Form.
