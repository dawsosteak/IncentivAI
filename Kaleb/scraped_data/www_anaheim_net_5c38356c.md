#  Community Services Volunteer Opportunities 
Interested in becoming a City of Anaheim Community Service Department volunteer? Our volunteers currently serve in a variety of rewarding positions throughout the city.
## Oak Canyon Nature Center:
  

### [**CANYON COMPANIONS**](https://secure.rec1.com/CA/city-of-anaheim/catalog/index/166ee0763533bae05422885e53631db1?filter=c2VhcmNoPWNhbnlvbiUyMGNvbXBhbmlvbnM=)
Possible activities include: trail clearing, stream maintenance, non-native plant removal and habitat restoration. All work materials are provided. 
### [**VOLUNTEEN PROGRAM (Summer only)**](https://anaheim.net/4829/Summer-VolunTEEN-Program)
This volunteer program is for youth ages 13 to 17 and provides teens with an opportunity to assist in a wide variety of programs based on their interests, location, and availability. 
## East Anaheim Gymnasium:
  

### **YOUTH SPORTS COACH ASSISTANTS**
Possible activities include: coaching and assisting coaches in youth sports camps such as volleyball, soccer and basketball, as well as special projects at the East Anaheim Community Center and Gymnasium.
## Downtown Anaheim Community Center and Brookhurst Community Center:
  

### **ACTIVE OLDER ADULT PROGRAMS**
Possible activities include: interacting, socializing and participating in activities with the seniors. Volunteers may also be asked to help with senior center special events.
**[S.T.A.R.S. AFTER SCHOOL PROGRAM](http://www.anaheim.net/2385/Anaheim-STARS) & [FUN ON WHEELS MOBILE RECREATION PROGRAM](http://www.anaheim.net/2240/Fun-on-Wheels)**
Are held at different sites throughout the city and programming takes place after school until 6:00 p.m. Possible activities include homework help, games, fitness fun and arts and crafts.
## More Information:
  

To become a volunteer, applicants must be at least 13 years of age, fill out an application, and make an appointment for a fingerprint background check. Some volunteer assignments also require the submission of a TB clearance dated within the last 2 years. Fingerprinting is free, but applicants are responsible for the cost of the TB test. For more information or to make a fingerprinting appointment, contact the Volunteer Coordinator.
### **Court Appointed Community Service Hours**
Individuals who need to complete court-mandated community service hours must call (714) 765-5158 for the [Graffiti Removal Program](https://www.anaheim.net/689/Graffiti-Removal).
### **Other Volunteer Opportunities**
There are many other non-city-managed organizations providing valuable volunteer services in Anaheim and the surrounding area, including [OneOC](https://www.oneoc.org/), [Anaheim Volunteer Match](https://anaheim.volunteermatch.org/) and [Love Anaheim](http://www.loveanaheim.org/). Information on what they do and how to become involved can be found by contacting the organization directly.
