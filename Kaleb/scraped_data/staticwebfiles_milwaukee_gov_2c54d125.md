##### WHAT CAN I DO?
Use the resources below to be more sustainable in your home or business, and to help your neighborhood. You can help us reach our goals!
  * [Me²:](http://www.smartenergypays.com/homeowners) Make your home more energy efficient 
  * [Me² Commercial:](http://www.smartenergypays.com/businesses) Improve your commercial building's efficiency with PACE financing 
  * [ME3:](http://city.milwaukee.gov/ME3) No-cost sustainable manufacturing program for small-medium manufacturing companies 
  * [Milwaukee Shines:](http://city.milwaukee.gov/milwaukeeshines) Learn how to add solar to your home or business 
  * [Menomonee Valley Sustainable Design Guidelines:](http://www.renewthevalley.org/media/mediafile_attachments/06/46-guidelinesfinalprintableversion.pdf) Get ideas for how to make your next development sustainable 
  * [Wisconsin Green Building Alliance:](http://wgba.shuttlepod.org/) Member based non-profit providing education about and training on green building 
  * [HELP:](http://city.milwaukee.gov/EmergencyLoanProgram.htm) Homeowners Emergency Loan Program provides financing for emergency home improvements for income-eligible residents. 


Share your commitment with others. Use #ReFreshMKE to show us how you're ReFreshing Milwaukee through social media, and see how others are part of #ReFreshMKE.
