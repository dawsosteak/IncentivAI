#  Planning & Development Section 
## **Services Provided**
The Planning & Development Section of the Community Risk Reduction Division completes reviews and inspections for new construction and fire protection plans. Our role is to ensure projects meet fire and life safety codes in order to support safe building design and construction throughout our community.
## **Permits**
Fire construction permits are required for specific construction and fire protection activities to ensure compliance with fire and life safety codes. For more information, please visit our [Fire Construction Permits](https://www.anaheim.net/6759/Fire-Construction-Permits) page.
