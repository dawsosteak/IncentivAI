# **RESIDENTIAL PERMITS**
DCH Body
Page Content
A Residential Permit is an official approval from the City of Dallas that allows you to start construction, renovation, or major repairs on your home. It ensures that your project meets safety codes and building standards designed to protect you, your family, and your community.  
  
Think of it as a partnership between you and your local building department — working together to make sure your home improvements are safe, sturdy, and built to last. Whether you’re adding a new deck, remodeling a kitchen, or building your dream home, obtaining a permit helps keep your investment secure and your neighborhood strong.
_[DallasNow](https://dallascityhall.com/departments/sustainabledevelopment/Pages/DallasNow.aspx)_ is an online portal used by the City of Dallas for all of our permitting services. Below you will find a list of forms and checklist to help with the process. ​
