## **Montana Sage Grouse Habitat Conservation Program - NEWS**
**_The Sage Grouse Website may be undergoing brief outages for maintenance. Please save your work frequently._**
The Montana Sage Grouse Habitat Conservation Program works to sustain viable sage grouse populations and conserve habitat. Montana’s Sage Grouse Conservation Strategy is based on the collaborative work of diverse Stakeholders. The Montana Sage Grouse Oversight Team and the Program implement Executive Order 12-2015 and the Greater Sage Grouse Stewardship Act in partnership with, landowners, state and federal agencies, and many private diverse stakeholders so that Montanans maintain control of their lands, wildlife and economy.

## Montana Sage Grouse Oversight Team
The Montana Sage Grouse Oversight Team (MSGOT) oversees implementation of Montana's Sage Grouse Conservation Strategy by the Sage Grouse Habitat Conservation Program (Program). MSGOT and the Program implement the Greater Sage Grouse Stewardship Act, along with Executive Orders 12-2015 and 21-2015. Individual MSGOT members also implement Montana's Sage Grouse Conservation Strategy through their respective agencies and programs.
MSGOT is administratively attached to the Governor’s Office for administrative purposes. 
MSGOT is required to meet at least quarterly. A complete audio recording, a summary of the audio recording, and meeting materials are archived for each meeting. Meeting Archive is available on this page.

## Mitigation
The Montana Sage Grouse Oversight Team approved the mitigation documents below on October 4, 2018. The administrative rules took effect January 12, 2019. The Habitat Conservation Program is now implementing the approved administrative rules and the mitigation system.
Information for developers can be found after logging into the web application. Click on “New Project” in the upper right corner and look for new information on the Instructions Page. Information for credit providers can be found on [the new Credits webpage](https://sagegrouse.mt.gov/CreditProject). Please contact the Program if you need assistance.

## Administrative Rules
The Montana Sage Grouse Oversight Team adopted final administrative rules pertaining to Stewardship Fund grants, the habitat quantification tool, and mitigation. The final adoption notice was published in the Montana Administrative Register on January 11, 2019. The rules took effect on January 12, 2019. A copy of all administrative rules implementing the Montana Sage Grouse Stewardship Act is below. They are also available on the web at <http://mtrules.org> by searching for the term "sage grouse".
  * [Title-14_Apr-1-2019.pdf](https://sagegrouse.mt.gov/documents/Title-14_Apr-1-2019.pdf)
  * [Notice 14-8 amendment ARM 14.6.103](https://sagegrouse.mt.gov/documents/AdoptionNotice)
