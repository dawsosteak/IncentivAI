# All Californians Count - Census 2020
The U.S. Constitution requires that every person living in the United States is counted every 10 years. Ensuring all Californians are fairly and accurately counted in the 2020 Census is imperative if our state is going to receive its fair share of federal funding and Congressional representation for the next decade.
