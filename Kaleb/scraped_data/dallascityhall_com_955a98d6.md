## 
SubTitleHeader
Councilmember Kathy Stewart serves as the District 10 Dallas City Council representative. Elected in 2023, Kathy is serving in her first term as the representative of northeast Dallas, including Lake Highlands and Hamilton Park.
Having been residents of Lake Highlands for 40 years, Kathy and her husband Robb share a passion for serving the community. Kathy, an attorney by profession, has served in many roles in this neighborhood, including owning and operating Highlands Cafe with other LH families for 10 years; serving as PTA president for three Lake Highland area schools, president of White Rock Valley Neighborhood Association, and Executive Director of the Lake Highlands Public Improvement District (PID) among others. 
Kathy is currently serving as Chairman of the Parks, Trails and the Environment Committee and Vice Chairman of the Public Safety Committee. She is also a member of the Economic Development Committee, the Transportation Committee and the Ad Hoc Committee on Legislative Affairs and the Ad Hoc Committee on the Pensions.
Kathy is here to serve and represent you, and is committed to working hard to ensure that District 10 is a safe, prosperous, and vibrant place to live, work, and raise a family.
