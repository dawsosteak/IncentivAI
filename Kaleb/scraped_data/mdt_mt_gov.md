# Your Department of Transportation
MDT is a workforce of over 2,000 people across Montana determined to do all we can so that you arrive at your destination safely. 
We know a well-planned and functioning transportation system contributes to the quality of life of Montana residents and the economies of our communities.
Whether you are riding, walking, biking, driving, using public transit, or even flying, MDT has played a part in getting you where you are going.

# MDT Employee Memorial
MDT's employee memorial was created to ensure that the individuals who have passed in the line of duty are not forgotten.
It honors those employees who have lost their lives while serving Montana, and is a reminder to always drive safely, especially when passing through work zones.
[Learn about the Memorial](https://mdt.mt.gov/mdt/memorial.aspx "Learn about the Memorial")
