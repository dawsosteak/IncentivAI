# Facility Access Plan Submission and Review System
## Disability and Communication Access Board
Toggle navigation
  * [](https://fau.ehawaii.gov/fau/wf/welcome.html)
  * [](https://fau.ehawaii.gov/fau/wf/page/help.html)


Who can use this service? 
This service is for engineering firms to submit and manage their document transmittal forms online to DCAB. 
Getting Started. 
  * Log in with your eHawaii.gov account. [](https://fau.ehawaii.gov/fau/wf/page/welcome.html)
An eHawaii account is the free, single-sign-on account to access secure hawaii.gov services. Need an account? [Sign up](https://fau.ehawaii.gov/fau/account/new.do), it''s free!
  * Fill out online documents and submit to DCAB.
  * Drop off blueprints and submit payment via check or credit card.
  * Using the application, work with a Facility Access Specialist throughout the review process to get a Final Letter of Approval.


Additional Questions? 
Read the FAQs on our [Help page](https://fau.ehawaii.gov/fau/wf/help.html). Once approved, your certification will be mailed promptly.
