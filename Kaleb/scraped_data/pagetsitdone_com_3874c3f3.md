## Pennsylvania
### Partnering with Business to
### **Get it Done**
### In Pennsylvania you find innovative minds, vibrant communities, and a collaborative business ecosystem dedicated to getting it done.
With new leadership, a fierce competitive direction, and growing momentum, Pennsylvania is freshly energized to welcome businesses from all over the country and the world. From technology to life sciences and beyond, thriving Pennsylvania companies are the driving force behind big plans that shape our future. Our businesses are powered by our 6.5-million-strong workforce, world-class higher education institutions, and targeted workforce development programs that fill our resilient talent pipelines.
![Governor Josh Shapiro cuts a blue ribbon with help from United Parcel Service \(UPS\) employees, in a warehouse filled with shelves stacked high with shipping boxes.](https://s7d9.scene7.com/is/image/statepa/23952_gov_amazon_dz_016-copy-1)
Our strategic location on the U.S. East Coast, close to four of the nation’s 10 largest markets, higher education institutions that foster a dynamic and diverse workforce, and inclusive and supportive business and startup ecosystem all contribute to Pennsylvania’s status as a leader in business. See why doing business here is just good business.

From our innovations in energy to our mature manufacturing ecosystem, Pennsylvania’s diverse economy is supported by the strong backbone that is our key industries.
