#  Urban Water Management Plan 
The City of Anaheim (City) would like to invite all interested persons to the public hearing for the City’s 2025 Urban Water Management Plan (2025 UWMP), including the proposed Water Shortage Contingency Plan (WSCP). The public hearing will be held in the 2nd Floor Gordon Hoyt Conference Room of Anaheim West Tower, 201 S. Anaheim Boulevard, on Wednesday, April 22, 2026, at 5:00 p.m., or as soon thereafter as this matter can be heard. The purpose of the hearing is to consider public comments prior to recommending that the City Council adopt the proposed 2025 UWMP, including the proposed WSCP.
The proposed 2025 UWMP and WSCP are prepared in accordance with the California Urban Water Management Planning Act (Act). The Act requires the City, as an urban water supplier, to prepare and adopt an Urban Water Management Plan to analyze water management issues unique to its water service area, including local and imported water supply sources, forecasted water demands, water conservation issues, and water shortage contingency planning. The City is required to update its plan at least once every five years with last adoption by the City Council occurring in 2021.
ALL INTERESTED PERSONS are invited to attend the public hearing and express opinions on the above-described matter. Members of the public can also submit comments electronically for Board Member consideration by sending them to apupubliccomments@anaheim.net prior to the public hearing.
Copies of the proposed 2025 UWMP and WSCP are available for public review on this webpage and at the following locations:
  * Anaheim Central Public Library (500 W. Broadway)
  * Anaheim Canyon Hills Branch Library (400 Scout Trail)
  * East Anaheim Branch Library (8201 E. Santa Ana Canyon Road)
  * Anaheim Euclid Branch (1340 S. Euclid Street)
  * Anaheim Haskett Branch Library (2650 W. Broadway)
  * Anaheim Sunkist Branch (901 S. Sunkist Street)
  * Office of Anaheim City Clerk (Suite #217 Front Counter at 200 S. Anaheim Boulevard)
  * Anaheim West Tower (First Floor Front Counter at 201 S. Anaheim Boulevard)


For more information, please contact David Kim, Principal Civil Engineer, at 714-765-4206 or dkim@anaheim.net.
