Welcome Employers Missouri is pleased to provide employers with an easy, fast, cost effective way to meet reporting requirements. This website provides information and online reporting options for:  

  * Reporting new hires 
  * Reporting when employees under wage assignment are no longer employed 
  * Responding to employment verification requests

If you have already registered, please login to begin reporting online.   
If you have not yet registered and would like to take advantage of online reporting, [click here to become a registered Employer or registered Payroll Service Provider](https://www.missouriemployer.dss.mo.gov/register.aspx).   
Our goal is to make reporting easy and convenient. If you have any questions about online reporting or require technical assistance, please [contact us](https://www.missouriemployer.dss.mo.gov/ContactUs.aspx).
