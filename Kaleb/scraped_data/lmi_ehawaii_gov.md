# Home
Welcome to the Hawai'i Directory of Green Employers website - a growing online directory of green employers in Hawai'i.  
[Find Green Employers](https://lmi.ehawaii.gov/green/find-employers.html "Link to Find Green Employers")
The Hawaii Department of Labor and Industrial Relations (DLIR) defines green employers as businesses that employ workers in occupations in these core areas: 
>   * Generate clean, renewable, sustainable Energy 
>   * Reduce pollution and waste; conserve natural resources; recycle
>   * Energy efficiency 
>   * Education, training and support of green workforce 
>   * Natural, sustainable, environmentally-friendly production 


Read [Hawaii's Green Definitions](https://greenjobshawaii.hirenethawaii.com/vosnet/default.aspx?pg=DEFINITIONS "Link to Green Definitions") for more information. 
In this Directory, employers can post profiles that describe their operations, specify their core occupations, and describe the skills and education they want in employees. Jobseekers, students, their counselors and advisors, and others can access the employer profiles to learn about these companies and the workers they require. The Hawaii Green Jobs Initiative is funded by the American Recovery and Reinvestment Act of 2009 and made available through the U.S. Department of Labor—Employment and Training Administration.
