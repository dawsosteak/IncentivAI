Located in the heart of Anaheim, the Downtown Anaheim Community Center is a hub designed to empower residents and strengthen the community by offering a variety of services and programs that enhance the quality of life for all. Through welcoming spaces and inclusive programming, the Center supports recreational, educational, and social opportunities for residents and stakeholders alike.
### **Book Your Memorable Experience**
The facility features a spacious Assembly Hall with over 4,200 square feet of hardwood flooring and soaring 12-foot ceilings. With seating for 160 to 200 guests, it’s an ideal venue for wedding receptions, birthdays, baby showers, milestone celebrations, anniversaries, and more. Just outside, a beautifully landscaped patio provides a warm and inviting atmosphere—perfect for creating a cozy lounge area or adding cocktail tables for relaxed guest mingling or networking before or during events.
The Center also offers flexible meeting rooms that accommodate groups from 15 to 50, with carpeted or hardwood flooring options. These spaces are ideal for business meetings, training sessions, workshops, and educational programs for adults and families.
We understand that every event is unique, and we’re committed to making yours a seamless and memorable experience.
In addition to its event and meeting facilities, the Downtown Anaheim Community Center proudly houses the Older Adults Senior Center, the Center Gallery, and the Family Resource Center, offering a wide array of resources that connect, support, and enrich the Anaheim community.
