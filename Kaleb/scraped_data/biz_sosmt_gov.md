## What's New
**Standard processing time.** Montana is seeing a surge in business registrations, and we are experiencing a very high filing volume. Thank you for your patience and doing business in Montana. If you have not received an email update on your filing, please check the status of your application in your Work Queue. 
**File Your Annual Report** - The 2026 Annual Report filing period opens January 1 and runs through April 15 for free.
  * [Search](https://biz.sosmt.gov/search/business/) for your business.
  * Click the entity name.
  * Select "File Annual Report" on the right. 


**Received a Dissolution Notice?** Here's the good news, it is easy to reinstate your business. Check out [How to Reinstate](https://help.sosmt.gov/en/articles/13265665-how-to-reinstate-a-business) for step-by-step instructions. The page will guide you through filing any missing Annual Reports and submitting your reinstatement application. Thank you for doing business in Montana. 
#### Log In
  * Existing Users- Log in with current username and password. Username and Password not working? Please select Login and click Reset Password or Forgot Username. The information will be sent your email address. 
  * New Users- click Login and create an account.
