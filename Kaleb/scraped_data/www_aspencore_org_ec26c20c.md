#  Understanding Funding Basics  

##  Are you interested in receiving funding from CORE for your building improvement project?  

Read the fine print before submitting your application or reach out to our Energy Concierge Team for more information.
[ Contact An Energy Concierge ](https://energysmartcolorado.formstack.com/forms/core_lead)
![CORE staff member conducting a home energy assessment in Aspen.](https://lirp.cdn-website.com/06c12080/dms3rep/multi/opt/DSC00148--281-29-1920w.JPG)
CORE provides funding for residential, commercial, and multifamily building electrification and energy efficiency projects in Pitkin, Eagle, and Garfield Counties.
###  Discover If Your Project Qualifies For CORE’s Grants or Rebates  

#### How much funding is available?
Funding is limited. Residential rebates are available for up to $10,000, while commercial and multifamily projects can qualify for rebates up to $50,000. Grants provide funding up to $200,000. Eligibility and funding amounts vary based on project type, location, and participant qualifications. Applications are accepted on a first-come, first-served basis.
#### What is a multifamily building?
Multifamily buildings are defined as complexes with four or more units in the building. Multifamily projects must be in the common area of the building and/or every unit of the building.﻿
#### Does the program change?
While CORE staff attempt to make changes to funding programs before the beginning of each quarter, any aspect of the grant or rebate programs may be changed at any time and without notice.
