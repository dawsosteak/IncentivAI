This Email Ruling confirms the schedule for comments to the August 1, 2016 Competitive Solicitation Framework Working Group Report (Report) and allows for reply comments. As further described below, parties shall file comments to the Report no later than August 22, 2016 and reply comments no later than August 31, 2016.
* * *
On March 24, 2016, a ruling was issued establishing a working group to develop a competitive solicitation framework. The ruling described the scope, tasks, and schedule for the working group, including a deadline for a preliminary and final report. The working group timely served its final report on August 1, 2016. As directed in the March 24, 2016 ruling, parties may review the report and file comments on the contents of the report no later than August 22, 2016. The ruling
