#  Ethics 
## **Ethics Division**
The Ethics Division was created in August 2024 and is a new division within the City Attorney’s Office primarily providing legal advice and counsel in the areas of ethics, conflicts of interest and permissible political activities to the city council, department heads, executive staff, boards and commissions, and other city staff. The division also provides training and information, advises on compliance with required filings related to financial interests, conducts ethics policy analysis, evaluates existing laws related to ethics and makes recommendations when appropriate, and monitors State and other legislation as it relates to ethics to ensure that the city is compliant. Through these efforts, the Ethics Division helps ensure that the City is committed to its transparency, accountability, and ethical mandates. 
##  **Ethics Division Personnel**
The Ethics Division of the City Attorney’s Office consists of an assistant city attorney who serves as the [Ethics Officer](https://www.anaheim.net/Directory.aspx?EID=885) for the City, manages the division, and reports to the City Attorney.
