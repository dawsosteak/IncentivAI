No brush & bulky item collection week of december 30  

DCH Body
_**​1st Monday Week Collections Begin Jan. 6**_  

  

December 30, 2024
There will be no brush and bulky item collections this week as it falls on the 5th Monday of the month. Regular collections will resume next week according to the schedule below.
  

Collection Week | Setout Dates  
| Collection Period  
---|---|---  
1st Monday Week | Thurs, Jan 2 - Sun, Jan 5 | Mon, Jan 6 - Sat, Jan 11  
2nd Monday Week | Thurs, Jan 9 - Sun, Jan 12 | Mon, Jan 13 - Sat, Jan 18  
3rd Monday Week | Thurs, Jan 16 - Sun, Jan 19 | Mon, Jan 20 - Sat, Jan 25  
4th Monday Week | Thurs, Jan 23 - Sun, Jan 26 | Mon, Jan 27 - Sat, Feb 1  
  

To find your specific collection week, please [click here](https://dallascityhall.com/departments/sanitation/pages/brush_and_bulky.aspx).
**Additional Information**
Complete program rules and a 2025 collection schedule PDF calendar (in English and Spanish) are available at the site linked above. The schedule is also included in your December/January water bill.

For other Sanitation matters, visit our [Contact Us](https://dallascityhall.com/departments/sanitation/Pages/contactsanitation.aspx) page.
