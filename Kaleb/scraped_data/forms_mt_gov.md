# Department of Environmental Quality's Asbestos Training Course Approval Application form
Training Provider applications will be accepted October 1 to November 15 for the following two-year period beginning January 1. Each discipline and course type (initial or refresher) shall be submitted on a separate application for processing purposes. Applications will be available online and hard copy prior to the open enrollment period. Course material specified under ARM 17.74.364(5) should be submitted electronically with the exception of course examinations and answer keys which are to be send “hard copy” only. 
The open enrollment period also offers an opportunity to request additional instructors and provide documentation verifying qualifications under ARM 17.74.364(3). Existing instructors need not reapply, unless seeking approval for additional disciplines to instruct. All instructors should be listed for each discipline and course type.
