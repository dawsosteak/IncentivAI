It's easy to view your home electric usage anywhere you have Internet access. You can also get customized tips for saving energy and money.
[City of Austin Utilities Online Customer Care](https://coautilities.com/wps/wcm/connect/occ/coa/home) offers detailed information on energy usage. Log in and click _Your Energy Use_ to get started and view these details: 
  * See how much energy you’ve used by the day, month, or year 
  * Compare your usage to previous time periods, or to similar households in your neighborhood 
  * View usage in dollars or in kWh 
  * See how average daily temperatures affect your bill 
  * Take the Home Energy Analysis quiz to get customized tips to save energy 
  * Download data to Green Button for further analysis 


When you log in to [City of Austin Utilities Customer Care](https://coautilities.com), you will also see a projection of your next bill. You can also set email alerts to notify you when your bill approaches a threshold of your choosing. 
Don’t have a username or password yet? [Register with Online Customer Care](https://coautilities.com)
###  Do You Have Installed Solar on Your Home? 
Austin Energy’s **Solar Insights** feature in the [City of Austin Utilities customer portal](https://coautilities.com/wps/wcm/connect/occ/coa/home) makes it easier for residential solar customers to track energy usage and solar production—all in one place.
**What You Can Do:**
  * View solar generation and electricity use side by side
  * Explore interactive graphs and detailed billing info
  * Download and export your energy data


This streamlined experience helps you stay informed and engaged with your energy use.
