##### WHAT CAN I DO?
Use the resources below to be more sustainable in your home or business, and to help your neighborhood. You can help us reach our goals!
  * [Recycle for Good:](http://www.MilwaukeeRecycles.com) Milwaukee's single sort recycling program, now accepting more materials 
  * [Milwaukee Department of Public Works:](http://city.milwaukee.gov/mpw) information on garbage, recycling, bulk items, and self-help centers 
  * [Milwaukee Self-Help Centers:](http://city.milwaukee.gov/mpw/divisions/operations/environmental/sanitation/SelfHelpCenters.htm) Milwaukee residents can safely dispose or recycle of a variety of refuse 
  * [Milwaukee Composting & Yard Waste:](http://city.milwaukee.gov/mpw/divisions/operations/environmental/sanitation/Compost.htm) City of Milwaukee info on yard waste collection and composting opportunities 
  * [Household Hazardous Waste Collection:](http://www.mmsd.com/HHW.aspx) MMSD details on how to safely dispose of household hazardous waste 
  * [Keep Greater Milwaukee Beautiful:](http://www.kgmb.org/) Organization that provides environmental education and community improvement programs


Share your commitment with others. Use #ReFreshMKE to show us how you're ReFreshing Milwaukee through social media, and see how others are part of #ReFreshMKE.
