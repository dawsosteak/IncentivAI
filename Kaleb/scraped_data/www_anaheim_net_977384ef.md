#  Fire Explorers 
The Anaheim Fire & Rescue Explorer program is a hands-on training program designed for community youth ages 15-20 who are interested in a career in the fire service.
## Purpose
To assist the youth of our community in their selection of a career path within fire and emergency services and to challenge them to become responsible citizens through career experiences, leadership opportunities, and community service activities.

The Fire Explorer program is comprised of youth from within the community. Firefighters from Anaheim Fire & Rescue instruct and oversee program activities with an emphasis placed on youth safety. At no time do Explorers enter burning structures, and they are never used in place of professional first responders, however they may earn the privilege of riding along on Fire Apparatus.
## Eligibility Requirements
  * Must be between the ages of 15 (and have completed the 8th grade) and 21 years old.
  * Must maintain a GPA of 2.0 or above
  * Physically capable of performing basic fire service tasks (ex. running, lifting, climbing, crawling)


## Activities
### **Meetings are every other Tuesday, 5:30 - 9:00 pm.**
Explorers are taught basic fire and life-saving related skills. Meetings often combine physical fitness routines, classroom learning, and manipulative skills practice.
### **Community Events**
Explorers are often requested to participate in volunteer activities during Community Engagement events.
### **Ride Along Program**
After six months of successful program participation and completion of the Ride Along task book, Explorers are given the opportunity to ride along on fire engines, trucks, and ambulances. Explorers gain a unique experience, observing first-hand what is required of firefighters operating on real emergencies.
## For more information
If you have questions about the program, send an email to FireExplorer@anaheim.net.
## **Join the Interest List**
Interested in becoming an Anaheim Fire Explorer? If you meet the eligibility requirements, complete the Interest Form below to be notified when positions become available. [Fire Explorers Interest Form](https://docs.google.com/forms/d/e/1FAIpQLSeewLbQ7PWzJc8IK7x3GlHYCrOAgECNNMpiBDcXpQGdxJBz4Q/viewform?usp=dialog)
Priority for enrollment is based on the following:
  * Any Anaheim Public Safety Pathways Graduate
  * Any resident of the City of Anaheim
  * Any student currently enrolled at any school in the City of Anaheim.
