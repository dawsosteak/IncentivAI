# Talent Showcase
The Talent Showcase in Columbia is a great chance for job seekers to find new career opportunities with the State of Missouri. Job seekers are invited to provide a resume and create a display or presentation to highlight their skills, achievements, and experience to find a career fit for them.
Connect with several state agencies as they circulate among booths to find job seekers whose skills and abilities align with their needs. This opportunity reflects the commitment to ensure all job seekers can successfully and effectively engage with recruiters at the State of Missouri. Job seeker registration is required, as space is limited!
May 7, 2026
1:00 to 3:00 p.m.
Boone Electric Cooperative
1413 Rangeline St., Columbia, MO 65201

#### Testimonials
"At the State of Missouri an employee can **work their way up the ranks** with the correct work experience and/or education. Employees **collaborate** every day with a variety of individuals, and work on a variety of assignments. These tasks allow State of Missouri employees to **work hard** and show others what they are capable of, opening many **opportunities for promotion and career advancement**."
![Author](https://mocareersmogov.swipepages.media/2023/10/64dcf230a62a9d00111b8eca/oa-logo.png)
###### Crystal Wessing
Office of Administration Interim Director of Facilities, Management, Design and Construction
"We’re encouraged to **challenge ourselves** and we’re given the freedom to solve old problems in new ways. This is a **great place to work** if you are someone like me who enjoys challenging the “that’s how it’s always been done” mindset through **continuous process improvement**. This has allowed us to improve our level of service to the citizens of our state and hopefully sets the bar for what they can come to expect from state government."
![Author](https://mocareersmogov.swipepages.media/2023/10/64dcf230a62a9d00111b8eca/department-of-economic-development-150.webp)

I also appreciate that I am encouraged to challenge myself and take part in **professional development opportunities** that will help me both as a state employee and as a person."
