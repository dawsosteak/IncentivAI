## Occupational and Professional Licensing
The Employment Standards Division in Montana is in charge of supervising the Occupational and Professional Licensing Boards. These boards are mandated by state law. Each board's role is to make sure that the laws and regulations pertaining to different professions are upheld. They achieve this by issuing licenses and overseeing the activities of specific jobs and professionals within the state of Montana.
### Public
Verify whether your professional is licensed or has any complaints.
[License Search](https://ebizws.mt.gov/PUBLICPORTAL/searchform?mylist=licenses) [File a Complaint](https://bsd.dli.mt.gov/filing-complaint.html)
### Professionals
Apply or renew your license with the state of Montana.
[Apply for a License](https://ebiz.mt.gov/POL/Default.aspx) [Renew a License](https://ebiz.mt.gov/POL/Default.aspx)
