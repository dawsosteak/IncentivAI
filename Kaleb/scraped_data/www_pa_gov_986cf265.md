# Explore Paid Internships, Apprenticeships & Fellowships
Make an impact with hands-on experience in the Commonwealth of Pennsylvania. Work on meaningful projects, gain mentorship from leaders, and grow in a community that values your success.  

Best of all, you’ll earn while you learn. All opportunities are paid positions.

## Internships
Get hands-on with projects that resonate with your career aspirations and personal passions. Benefit from the mentorship of seasoned professionals who are not just experts in their fields but are also dedicated to nurturing the next generation of leaders. Our internships, available to both high school and college students, are designed to build a strong foundation for your future, preparing you for a career committed to public service and creating positive change.   

[ View Open Internship Opportunities  ](https://www.pa.gov/services/employment/apply-for-internships-with-the-commonwealth0)
![](https://s7d9.scene7.com/is/image/statepa/balancedlife?ts=1725552167977&dpr=off)
## Apprenticeships
Our rewarding apprenticeships provide high-quality career pathways for high school, graduates and job seekers to launch their careers with the Commonwealth. With a focus on structured on-the-job training, concentrated classroom learning, and mentoring from professionals in the industry, our programs open the door to success.
[ Explore Openings ](https://www.pa.gov/services/employment/apply-for-apprenticeships-with-the-commonwealth)
![](https://s7d9.scene7.com/is/image/statepa/fellowship-card?ts=1725552176266&dpr=off)
## Fellowships
Our fellowship programs offer contributions in areas like health, education, science, and technology. Work on significant projects, attend enriching workshops and collaborate with passionate colleagues to drive change. ​​​​
[ Learn more ](https://www.pa.gov/services/employment/apply-for-fellowships-with-the-commonwealth)
