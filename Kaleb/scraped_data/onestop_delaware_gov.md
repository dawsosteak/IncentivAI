#  Welcome to the Delaware One Stop
The registration and licensing portal for businesses operating in the State of Delaware
[Learn more](https://onestop.delaware.gov/Prepare_Learn)
Create an account to begin your business license registration or renew your existing business licenses.
[Create an account](https://onestop.delaware.gov/Start_Account) _or_ [Sign in](https://onestop.delaware.gov/Account_SignIn)
Trade names (often referred to as a "DBA" or "Doing Business As"), formerly managed by the Delaware Superior Court, are now managed by the Division of Revenue effective February 2, 2026. Learn more and get started with [registering your trade name](https://onestop.delaware.gov/Operate_TradeNames). 
All One Stop transactions are now processed through [Go DE's secure payment gateway](https://go.delaware.gov?utm_source=onestop&utm_medium=website&utm_campaign=notification). This change enhances security and reliability. When you make a payment, you will be redirected to Go DE to complete your transaction safely. 
LaborFirst, supporting Paid Leave, has launched.  Employers, TPAs, and PEOs should visit [LaborFirst](https://labor.delaware.gov/laborfirst?utm_source=onestop&utm_medium=website&utm_campaign=notification) for instructions on how to sign up for State credentials and system documentation. Paid Leave questions? Visit the [program website](https://labor.delaware.gov/delaware-paid-leave-is-coming?utm_source=onestop&utm_medium=website&utm_campaign=notification).

[Delaware.gov](https://Delaware.gov/) is a comprehensive resource for all your Delaware business needs.
##### Visit the award winning Delaware First Steps website if you:
  * Have a business idea and are thinking of starting a company in Delaware.
  * Need resources to help you write a business plan.
  * Want to learn about resources for growing your business.


[Visit Delaware First Steps](https://firststeps.delaware.gov/)
##### Visit the Delaware Division of Revenue website if you:
  * Are already registered as a withholding agent and need to pay withholding taxes.
  * Want to learn about Delaware's services for business taxpayers.
  * Need a comprehensive list of business tax forms.


[Visit Delaware Division of Revenue](https://revenue.delaware.gov)
##### Visit the Delaware Division of Corporations website in order to:
  * Check business name availability.
  * Reserve a business name for future use.
  * File your annual report and pay franchise taxes for your business.
  * Research the types of business entities you can form in Delaware.


[Visit Delaware Division of Corporations](https://corp.delaware.gov)
##### Visit the Division of Small Business if you:
  * Need assistance navigating government processes.
  * Want to connect with business support organizations.
  * Are interested in business capital.


[Visit Delaware Division of Small Business](https://business.delaware.gov/)
