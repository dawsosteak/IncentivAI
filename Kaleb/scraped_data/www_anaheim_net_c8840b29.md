# ADU Express Process
# _A New ADU Review Experience_
##  STEP 1: VERIFY ELIGIBILITY
  * ### Check your property’s eligibility for ADU Express by searching your property address on the [**ADU Express Eligibility Tool**](https://gis.anaheim.net/portal/apps/instant/lookup/index.html?appid=70092ca40660457db6f60cfae2884600).
  * ### If you are eligible, congratulations! Go to Step #2 to Continue the process!
  * ### Not eligible for ADU Express? Questions about alternatives? Visit the [**ADU Anaheim landing page**](https://www.anaheim.net/6349/) for information on the standard Building Division permit review and approval process. If you have any questions, email us at **ADUAnaheim@anaheim.net**.


##  STEP 2: SELECT A PRE-APPROVED ADU PLAN 
  * ### All potential plans for ADU Express can be found on the ADU [**Pre-Approved Plan Catalogue Page**](https://www.anaheim.net/6351/). Review the various sizes, options, and images and decide what you believe will work best for you. 
  * ### The "Notes" column of the catalogue will provide you with important information about each plan set. 
  * ### Once you have selected a pre-approved plan, move on to Step 3. 


##  STEP 3: CONTACT CITY STAFF 
  * ###  **THIS IS IMPORTANT** : Once you pick your plan, **contact Anaheim Public Utilities at (714) 765-6847** to schedule a free “meter spot”. This process helps you plan for the additional electrical needs of your new ADU. When you call for a meter spot, please request the folowing: "**A 400 AMP, Multi-Meter Location**."
    * ### Please note: Staff cannot meet to create a site plan until you obtain this information from Anaheim Public Utilities. Usually, the meter spot site visit occurs within three business days of contacting Anaheim Public Utilities. 
  * ### Once you have received your meter spot report from Anaheim Public Utilities, please contact Planning and Building staff at **ADUAnaheim@Anaheim.net** to provide the Meter Spot and pre-approved plan number you wish to move forward with. Staff will then review and provide any additional documents to be completed.


##  STEP 4: MEET WITH CITY STAFF
  * ### Staff will continue to update you as the final review and approvals are completed. Once all aspects of the application receive preliminary approval, staff will schedule a meeting with you in City Hall to create your Site Plan, review construction requirements, and answer any questions you have. 


##  STEP 5: AS PART OF THE APPLICATION REVIEW
  * ### All ADU Express materials will be uploaded into the online portal. Staff will assist with this during our in-person meeting. It is through the online portal that you will download and print your construction documents. 


##  STEP 6: BUILDING DIVISION REVIEW AND APPROVAL
  * ### Following your in-person meeting, the Building Division will provide information on applicable development impact and/or school districts fees. All fees must be paid prior to building permit issuance. You will be provided information on which school district to pay (there are six in Anaheim) and where they are located.
  * ### Once you return from paying necessary school fees, staff should be able to issue your approved building permit, all within one day!


##  STEP 7: PERMITS AND INSPECTIONS
  * ### Inspections will be required throughout the construction process and at varying stages. Please visit [**www.Anaheim.net/Next**](http://www.Anaheim.net/Next) to utilize the City's automated system to schedule inspections.
