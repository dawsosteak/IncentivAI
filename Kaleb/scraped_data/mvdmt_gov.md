# Montana Motor Vehicle Division
## Montana's Updated Driver License & ID
![Three creditial logos on IDs. REAL ID is black circle with star die cut in middle. Citizenship marker is black silhouette of an eagle. Organ donor is a black silhouette of a heart with the letter Y die cut in center.](https://mvdmt.gov/wp-content/uploads/2025/12/New-ID-Icons-2.png)
Montana driver licenses and identification cards have been redesigned to improve security and provide better protection for the citizens of Montana. 
[Learn more about the new credential](https://mvdmt.gov/new-driver-license/#new-credential)
## Your Driver License/ID Now on Your Phone
![Montana Mobile ID logo](https://mvdmt.gov/wp-content/uploads/2025/08/MTMobileIDLogo_x512.png)
Now you can add your Montana ID to your digital wallet! Businesses can scan mobile IDs to securely verify the age of their customers.
[Learn more about Montana Mobile ID](https://mvdmt.gov/mobile-id/)
