#  Annual Offer Price (AOP) 
### Effective through June 30, 2026
  * AOP = Avoided Energy Charges + Avoided Transmission Charges + Renewable Energy Adder
  * AOP = $35.70 + $11.50 + $15.30 = $62.50 per MWh


The time of delivery factors applied to the AOP as follows:
  * On-Peak: 1.5
  * Mid-Peak: 1.0
  * Off-Peak: 0.6


Season | On-Peak Price  
| Mid-Peak Price  
| Off-Peak Price  
  
---|---|---|---  
Summer | $93.80 per MWh  
| $62.50 per MWh  
| $37.50 per MWh  
  
Winter | N/A | $62.50 per MWh  
| $37.50 per MWh  
  
Time periods are defined as follows:
  * On-Peak: 
    * Summer weekdays, except holidays  
**4 p.m. - 9 p.m.**
  * Mid-Peak: 
    * Summer weekdays except holidays  
**12 p.m. - 4 p.m. and 9 p.m. - 11 p.m.**
    * Winter weekdays except holidays  
**6 a.m. - 8 a.m. and 4 p.m. - 10 p.m.**
  * Off-Peak: 
    * **All other hours**


The summer season shall commence at 12 a.m. on July 1 and continue until 12 a.m. on November 1 of each year. The winter season shall commence at 12 a.m. on November 1 of each year and continue until 12 a.m. on July 1 of the following year.
Holidays are:
  * New Year's Day
  * Presidents' Day
  * Memorial Day
  * Independence Day
  * Labor Day
  * Veterans Day
  * Thanksgiving Day
  * Christmas Day


When any holiday listed above falls on Sunday, the following Monday shall be recognized as an off-peak period. No change in off-peak hours shall be made for holidays falling on Saturday.
