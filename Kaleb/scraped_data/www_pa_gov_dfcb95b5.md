## The .gov means it's official. 
Local, state, and federal government websites often end in .gov. Commonwealth of Pennsylvania government websites and email systems use "pennsylvania.gov" or "pa.gov" at the end of the address. Before sharing sensitive or personal information, make sure you're on an official state website.

# Ready Communities
Together we can prepare Pennsylvania communities for the unexpected, and make them safer places to live.
[ Know the Threats ](https://www.pa.gov/agencies/ready/hazards)
![Aerial view of a Pennsylvania neighborhood.](https://s7d9.scene7.com/is/image/statepa/pa-aerial-1?ts=1723059538866&dpr=off)
## Ready for Anything
Emergency managers plan to protect lives and property for those who depend on us.
[Community Planning](https://www.pa.gov/agencies/pema/ready-communities/planning)
![Flood water overtakes a neighborhood of homes and trees.](https://s7d9.scene7.com/is/image/statepa/flooded-pa)
### Flooding and Floodplain Management
It's our job to help local governments in Pennsylvania prepare their communities for potential flooding.
[ Flooding and Floodplain Management ](https://www.pa.gov/agencies/pema/ready-communities/flooding)
![Aerial view of a quaint Pennsylvania town.](https://s7d9.scene7.com/is/image/statepa/aerial-shrewsbury)
### Hazard Mitigation
We're working to reduce, eliminate, redirect, and avoid the effects of disasters in Pennsylvania.
[ Hazard Mitigation ](https://www.pa.gov/agencies/pema/ready-communities/mitigation)
![Hazmat team in orange vests.](https://s7d9.scene7.com/is/image/statepa/hazmat2)
### Hazardous Materials
Preparing for, responding to, and protecting Pennsylvania communities from chemical accidents.
[ Hazardous Materials ](https://www.pa.gov/agencies/pema/ready-communities/hazardous-materials)
![Plumes rise up from a nuclear power plant.](https://s7d9.scene7.com/is/image/statepa/nuclear-power-plant)
### Nuclear Power Plants
There are four operating nuclear power plants in Pennsylvania. 
[ Nuclear Power Plants ](https://www.pa.gov/agencies/pema/ready-communities/nuclear-power-plant-safety)
