#  Disaster Preparedness is Easy 
Have you heard the **Ground Shaking News**? Southern California is susceptible to earthquakes, don't wait, prepare now. [Click here](https://www.anaheim.net/DocumentCenter/View/12121/) to see the Ground Shaking News and make sure that you are prepared in the event of an earthquake!  
  
To learn about the Seven Steps to Earthquake Safety, click [HERE](https://www.earthquakecountry.org/sevensteps/?utm_source=Great+California+ShakeOut&utm_campaign=fec0b3cf4e-07%2F11%2F18+ShakeOut+2018+CA+Update+%232_COPY_02&utm_medium=email&utm_term=0_a4b2250a03-fec0b3cf4e-14334677).  
  
If you can pack a diaper bag (or gym bag), you can pack a disaster bag!  
  
Getting ready for emergencies is as simple as packing a bag with items you use regularly. New moms and dads know that if you can pack a diaper bag, you can pack a disaster bag. Take a look at our attached list and gather items from around the house to make either a diaper bag or disaster bag for each member of the family!
