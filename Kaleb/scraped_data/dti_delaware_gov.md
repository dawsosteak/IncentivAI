## Delaware Runs on Technology!


[![Go DE](https://dti.delaware.gov/wp-content/uploads/sites/141/2023/10/Go_DE_logo_500.jpg)](https://dti.delaware.gov/go-de/)  

### [Go DE](https://dti.delaware.gov/go-de/)
Go DE is a partnership among the Governor’s office, OST, GIC, and DTI to develop the new digital government portal for residents and visitors. This portal will provide a single human-centered entry to reach state services managed across many different agencies.  
  
[Learn More about Go DE](https://dti.delaware.gov/go-de/)
[![Delaware's GIS Broadband Hub](https://dti.delaware.gov/wp-content/uploads/sites/141/2022/03/gis-broadband-c.jpg)](https://broadband.delaware.gov)  

### [Delaware's GIS Broadband Hub](https://broadband.delaware.gov)
Expanding access to high-speed internet is a top priority. The Delaware Broadband Office is utilizing several federal funding sources to deploy high-speed internet to all Delawareans, close the digital divide, and bring full Digital Equity to Delaware.   
  
[Learn More about ](https://broadband.delaware.gov)
[![Digital Accessibility](https://dti.delaware.gov/wp-content/uploads/sites/141/2024/01/accessibility-innovation-500x500-1.jpg)](https://accessibility.dti.delaware.gov/)  

### [Digital Accessibility](https://accessibility.dti.delaware.gov/)
Digital accessibility is the concept of designing digital content to ensure equal accessibility for everyone – regardless of ability. Organizations should account for digital accessibility in both the design and the digital experience provided by their websites and other digital documents.  
  
[Learn More about ](https://accessibility.dti.delaware.gov/)
[![Delaware Identity Service](https://dti.delaware.gov/wp-content/uploads/sites/141/2023/10/delaware-identity-service-500.jpg)](https://dti.delaware.gov/state-agencies-portal/id-delaware-gov/)  

### [Delaware Identity Service](https://dti.delaware.gov/state-agencies-portal/id-delaware-gov/)
Delaware offers a single validated identity, built on Okta’s single sign-on solution, to anyone doing business with Delaware government for secure transactions across many agencies. A Delaware ID offers the state workforce unparalleled ease of use to access a more secure state network.  
  
[Learn More about ](https://dti.delaware.gov/state-agencies-portal/id-delaware-gov/)
