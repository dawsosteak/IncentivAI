Learn about our careers  
The Commonwealth of PA has thousands of jobs in nearly every field you can imagine. Use this site to learn about the jobs we offer, career paths in specific fields and current job openings in state agencies.   
  
You can also use this site to [view jobs with no requirement for a college degree](https://careers.employment.pa.gov/Home/NoDegreeRequired?page=1&education=50620180).   
  

  * [Browse Occupational Groups](https://careers.employment.pa.gov/#ogtab)
  * [Additional Search Options](https://careers.employment.pa.gov/#advanced)


  
We have over 2,600 jobs organized into categories (Occupational Groups) and sub-categories (Job Families) based on their duties and other characteristics.  
To get started, select an Occupational Group to view its Job Families and information about specific positions.  
Additional search options are available in the next tab.
