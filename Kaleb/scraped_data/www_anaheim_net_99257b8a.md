  1. [Air Purifier](https://www.anaheim.net/5241/Appliance-Fixtures#tabcda3968d-a837-4d81-8944-5e6aa37e2737_0)


Room air purifiers are portable electric appliances that remove fine particles, such as dust and pollen, from indoor air. ENERGY STAR® certified room air purifiers are 40% more energy efficient than standard models, saving customers about $30 annually on energy costs. Using ENERGY STAR portable purifiers is a great way to improve indoor air quality in a single room or area while using energy efficiently.
### Incentive Amount
### $30 (Maximum of 2)
### $50 (Maximum of 2) **with UV light**
###    
Installation/Efficiency Criteria
  * ###  [ENERGY STAR](https://www.energystar.gov/products/air_cleaners) Certified
  * ###  [ENERGY STAR](https://www.energystar.gov/products/air_cleaners) Certified **with UV light**

  1. [Heat Pump Dryer](https://www.anaheim.net/5241/Appliance-Fixtures#tabcda3968d-a837-4d81-8944-5e6aa37e2737_4)


Heat pump dryers take in ambient air, heat it, and recirculate it in the dryer to maintain the temperature without using much energy. This dryer passes humid air in the dryer drum through a condenser to remove the moisture without losing too much heat. Heat Pump dryers can save 20-60 percent more than conventional clothes dryers. 
### Incentive Amount
### $200
###    
Installation/Efficiency Criteria
  * ###  [ENERGY STAR](https://www.energystar.gov/productfinder/product/certified-clothes-dryers/results) Certified

  1. [Heat Pump Water heater](https://www.anaheim.net/5241/Appliance-Fixtures#tabcda3968d-a837-4d81-8944-5e6aa37e2737_5)


ENERGY STAR® certified electric storage water heaters use a highly efficient heat pump to transfer heat from the surrounding air to the water. Heat pump water heaters save a family of four approximately $330 per year on their electric bill. While a certified heat pump water heater costs more upfront, the savings will pay back the difference in approximately two years.
### Incentive Amount
### $400  
  

### Installation/Efficiency Criteria
  * ###  [ENERGY STAR](https://www.energystar.gov/productfinder/product/certified-water-heaters/results) Certified
