An open window into YOUR state government 
It’s your money, find out how it is being spent. PennWATCH lets you keep tabs on the state [budget](https://pennwatch.pa.gov/Budget), [spending](https://pennwatch.pa.gov/Spending), [revenue](https://pennwatchlegacy.pa.gov/revenue/Pages/default.aspx), [employees](https://pennwatch.pa.gov/Employees) and [performance](https://www.pa.gov/agencies/budget/publications-and-reports#sortCriteria=%40copapwpyear%20descending) – all in one website! 
To navigate this website, use the links provided on each page. Do not use the forward or back buttons on your browser.
