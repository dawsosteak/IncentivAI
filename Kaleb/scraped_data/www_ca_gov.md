## California by the numbers
39
million
California is home to nearly 40 million residents. One in four Californians is born overseas. 
15% 
of the US GDP
California contributes almost 15% to the US GDP. It is the world's fifth-largest economy. 
9
national parks
With the most national parks in the US, California also boasts 87 state parks and 63 state beaches.

# Website Accessibility Certification
## 2025 Certification
### Agency:
California Department of Technology 
### Certification date:
June 10, 2025 
### Contact:
ServiceDesk@state.ca.gov 
The undersigned certify that, as of **June 10, 2025** the website of the **California State Portal** is designed, developed, and maintained to be accessible. This denotes compliance with the following: 
  * California Government Code Sections [7405(external link)](https://leginfo.legislature.ca.gov/faces/codes_displaySection.xhtml?sectionNum=7405&lawCode=GOV), [11135(external link)](https://leginfo.legislature.ca.gov/faces/codes_displaySection.xhtml?sectionNum=11135&lawCode=GOV), and [11546.7(external link)](https://leginfo.legislature.ca.gov/faces/codes_displaySection.xhtml?sectionNum=11546.7&lawCode=GOV)
  * [Web Content Accessibility Guidelines 2.1(external link)](https://www.w3.org/TR/WCAG21?portal-california) published by the Web Accessibility Initiative of the World Wide Web Consortium 
  * At a minimum Level AA success criteria
