##### WHAT CAN I DO?
Use the resources below to be more sustainable in your home or business, and to help your neighborhood. You can help us reach our goals!
  * [Milwaukee County Transit System:](http://www.ridemcts.com/) Milwaukee's bus system with various routes and schedules 
  * [Zipcar Milwaukee:](http://www.zipcar.com/milwaukee) Car sharing program
  * [Milwaukee Bike Maps:](http://city.milwaukee.gov/maps4460.htm) City of Milwaukee has recommended bike routes, lanes and trails
  * [Midwest BikeShare:](http://www.midwestbikeshare.org/) Milwaukee's bike share program with rental bikes around the city
  * [Wisconsin Bicycle Federation:](http://wisconsinbikefed.org/) The Bike Fed works to make Wisconsin a great place to bike through education, legislation and involvement. Coordinates Safe Routes to School program 
  * [Metro Mountain Bikers:](http://metromountainbikers.com/) Local mountain bike club that promotes and protects environmentally responsible mountain bike opportunities in the metro area


Share your commitment with others. Use #ReFreshMKE to show us how you're ReFreshing Milwaukee through social media, and see how others are part of #ReFreshMKE.
There are so many resources available already. We are continually adding to this list and working to make it as accessible and usable as possible. Are we missing something? Let us know! Email your question or resource to sustainability@milwaukee.gov.
