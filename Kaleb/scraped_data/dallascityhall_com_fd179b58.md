Zoning Changes
  

Reviews and processes zoning change requests, including general zoning changes, specific use permits, planned development districts, and public deed restrictions. Also reviews and processes miscellaneous zoning items such as development or landscape plans, minor amendments, and alcohol spacing variance applications.
[**The City of Dallas is updating the Dallas' Development Code.  
Learn more & get involved at Dallas Zoning Reform**](https://www.dallaszoningreform.com/)  

  

[Tax documents and plats are obtained from Dallas County Records Building at 500 Elm Street. Click here](https://www.dallascounty.org/services/records.php)
