#  WAYC Teen Center 
The Project S.A.Y. (Support Anaheim's Youth) Teen Program is open to Junior High and High School youth, ages 13-17. The Teen Center is open Monday, Wednesday, Friday's from 3-7 p.m. and Tuesday's from 3:00 p.m. - 6:00 p.m. Teen activities include: Leadership activities, drop-in sports, homework help, computer lab, fitness programs, development workshops, tutoring, and youth excursions,
