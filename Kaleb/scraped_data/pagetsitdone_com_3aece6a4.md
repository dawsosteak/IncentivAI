## Helping Companies Reach **New Markets**
Through our International Trade program, every year we provide hands-on support to hundreds of Pennsylvania companies looking to do business across the globe. By leveraging our network of global trade partners, providing access to resources and information, and helping companies make critical business connections, we're invested in your international plans. Take a look at what we accomplished recently, and check out a few stories from companies we've helped get stuff done.
