## Explore the connections between PA and **current partner countries**
In today’s global economy, international commerce is more than a trend. Pennsylvania companies export to international markets and international companies are choosing Pennsylvania as a location for their U.S. entity.
We provide informative fact sheets for 31 countries that do business in Pennsylvania. Why? Because the data doesn't lie - global companies are finding real success here.
