|  |  Welcome to the **_Department of Natural Resources and Environmental Control (DNREC)_** eGov site. This is a new interactive, user-friendly, fast, electronic web portal that will allow citizens do business with DNREC 24 hours a day, 7 days a week from the convenience of their homes and businesses. Here you can buy: [Fishing Licenses](http://www.dnrec.delaware.gov/fw/Services/Pages/Rec-Lic.aspx) [Hunting Licenses](http://www.dnrec.delaware.gov/fw/Services/Pages/Rec-Lic.aspx) [Conservation Access Pass](http://www.dnrec.delaware.gov/fw/Services/Pages/Rec-Lic.aspx) [Waterfowl (Duck) Stamps](http://www.dnrec.delaware.gov/fw/Services/Pages/Rec-Lic.aspx) [Trout Stamps](http://www.dnrec.delaware.gov/fw/Services/Pages/Rec-Lic.aspx)  
You may also: Obtain [boat registration, ramp certificate, duplicate card/decal, or address change](https://dednrec.secure.force.com/boatreg/) Renew your [waste water and disposal system licenses](https://egov.dnrec.delaware.gov/egovpublic/dnrec/disp?doc=WaterSubmit&op=watersubmit) Submit [Asbestos and Demolition Notices](https://egov.dnrec.delaware.gov/egovpublic/dnrec/disp?doc=publicsubmenu&op=asbestos&department=public) Track [your order](https://egov.dnrec.delaware.gov/egovpublic/dnrec/disp?doc=orderTracking&op=default&actionFrom=duplicateLicenseHelp&dept=5)   
To enable the Department provide the best customer service, please complete each transaction one **tab** or action at a time; i.e. buy fishing and hunting licenses, purchase park passes, submit asbestos reports or renew waste water licenses. If you are waiting for items to be mailed to you such as park passes, waterfowl (duck) and trout stamps or boat fishing license decals, you may check on the shipment status by clicking on the [Order Tracking](https://egov.dnrec.delaware.gov/egovpublic/dnrec/disp?doc=orderTracking&op=default&actionFrom=duplicateLicenseHelp&dept=5) tab above. If you have any questions, click the [Contacts](https://egov.dnrec.delaware.gov/egovpublic/dnrec/disp?doc=publicContact&op=showDefault&department=Public) tab above. Information collected at this site will be kept confidential as provided by State laws. Please refer to the State's [_Privacy Policy_](https://egov.dnrec.delaware.gov/egovpublic/includes/privacyPolicy.html) .
