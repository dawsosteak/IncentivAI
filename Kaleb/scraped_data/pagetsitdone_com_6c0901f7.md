**Video Title:** "Olympus — A Pennsylvania Success Story"
**Video Summary:** Julian Sauvagnares, CEO of Olympus Corporation of the Americas, as well as various employees from the company, speak about their experiences working at Olympus and describe why Pennsylvania has been a good place for the company to do business.
