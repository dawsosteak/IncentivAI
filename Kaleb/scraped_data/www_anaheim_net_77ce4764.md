#  Become a Contract Recreation Class Instructor 
The City of Anaheim has a goal of providing a variety of classes to its residents through the teachings of independent contractors. You can be one of those instructors! Share your knowledge, experience, and talent with the residents of Anaheim to enrich their minds, lead healthy lifestyles and learn a new activity or hobby.
##    
Instructor Forms
  

Complete the Contract Class Instructor Application and class proposal form to be considered for Fall 2022. 
  * [Become a Contract Recreation Class Instructor](https://www.anaheim.net/FormCenter/Community-Services-4/Recreation-Class-Instructor-Application-245)
  * [Submit a class proposal for Winter 2023](https://www.anaheim.net/FormCenter/Community-Services-4/Contract-Recreation-Class-Proposal-Form--272)


Once we receive your application or class proposal form, you will be contacted by Community Services Program Staff.
