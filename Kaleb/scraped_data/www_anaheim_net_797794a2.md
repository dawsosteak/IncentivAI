## Overview
  

The Water Savings Incentive Program is an effort to improve water use efficiency for commercial, industrial, institutional, agricultural and large landscape customers with qualifying projects. The program provides financial incentives for customized water efficiency projects including:
  

  * Installation of commercial or industrial high-efficiency equipment
  * Industrial process improvements
  * Agricultural and landscape water efficiency improvements
  * Water management services


Incentives will be reserved for projects on a first-come, first-served basis. Funding is limited and submitting an application does not guarantee the customer will receive payment.
  

For more information, contact the Water Efficiency Programs Manager at (714) 765-5029
