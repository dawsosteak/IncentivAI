#  Traffic Accident Reports 
The Anaheim Police Department Accident Report Database allows you to search for certain traffic related reports, such as non-injury accidents/collisions and hit-and-runs. For non-injury traffic accidents or hit-and-runs that were filed using the online reporting system, cases may take additional time to review before becoming available online. If there are additional criminal offenses associated with a traffic report, these cases may not be immediately available.
To locate a report you must select "California" for State, "Anaheim Police Department" for Jurisdiction, and enter a report number only.
