# Division of Forensic Science
Chemistry Unit ♦ DNA Unit ♦ Medical Examiner ♦ Toxicology
The mission of the Division of Forensic Science is to provide the highest quality scientific services to all members of the justice community and the State of Delaware. This is accomplished through a team of skilled and dedicated employees utilizing scientific equipment and validated methodologies appropriate to the forensic community. Throughout all sections, the mantra of this Division is “Science Serving Justice”.
It is my privilege to serve as the Director of the Delaware Division of Forensic Science and provide the highest quality forensic services to the people of Delaware.
**— John R. Evans, Director**
