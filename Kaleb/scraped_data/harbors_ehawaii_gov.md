# Welcome to DOT Harbors Payments
Make payments for Harbors parking, permits, and fishing vessels.
**A service fee of 5% plus a payment processing fee will be added to each payment.** You will have an opportunity to review the fees and total amount before you pay.
Please select _Start Harbors Invoice Payment_ below to make a payment.
[Start Harbors Invoice Payment](https://harbors.ehawaii.gov/wf/form/harbors.html)
