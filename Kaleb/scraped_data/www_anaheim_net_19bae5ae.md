#  Classes & Activities 
The Anaheim Community Services Activity Guide provides residents and non-residents of all ages a variety of classes that will enhance their life by learning a new hobby or skill, exercising, and making new friends. A copy of the guide can be found at all Anaheim community centers and libraries or [online](https://www.anaheim.net/4619/Online-Programs-Activities-Guide). We use only the best independent instructors for our courses to bring your family the most enjoyable experience possible. Let us help you enrich your mind, body, and spirit by signing up for any of our wonderful courses.
