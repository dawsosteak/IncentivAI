## Project Types
![](http://coloradocpace.com/wp-content/uploads/2023/07/20-2.png)
### [Contractors ](https://coloradocpace.com/start-a-project/ee-contractors/)
Whether you specialize in energy efficiency or renewable energy, Colorado C-PACE empowers contractors to propose no-money-down, cash-flow-positive projects to clients. With support from program experts, you can develop and present compelling, finance-ready projects at no cost to you. 
[Our Program for Contractors ](https://coloradocpace.com/start-a-project/ee-contractors/)
![](http://coloradocpace.com/wp-content/uploads/2025/07/651Corporate.jpg)
### [Building Owners ](https://coloradocpace.com/start-a-project/building-owners/)
Discover how Colorado C-PACE helps commercial and industrial property owners modernize buildings with no upfront costs. With 100% financing for energy upgrades and immediate positive cash flow, this program turns "too good to be true" into real results. 
[Our Program for Building Owners ](https://coloradocpace.com/start-a-project/building-owners/)
![](http://coloradocpace.com/wp-content/uploads/2023/12/appointment.jpg)
### [Developers ](https://coloradocpace.com/start-a-project/developers/)
Colorado C-PACE offers commercial real estate developers a powerful tool to reduce equity requirements, secure long-term non-recourse financing, and build high-performing, energy-efficient properties. Learn how to enhance project value while closing capital gaps in your stack. 
[Our Program for Developers ](https://coloradocpace.com/start-a-project/developers/)
![](http://coloradocpace.com/wp-content/uploads/2023/12/services-1.jpg)
### [Mortgage Holders ](https://coloradocpace.com/start-a-project/mortgage-holders/)
Mortgage holders play a key role in the C-PACE process. Learn how projects backed by utility cost savings, independent reviews, and reduced default risk can benefit your lending portfolio—while maintaining transparency and priority protections. 
[Our Program for Mortgage Holders ](https://coloradocpace.com/start-a-project/mortgage-holders/)
![](http://coloradocpace.com/wp-content/uploads/2025/07/government.jpg)
### [Counties and Government ](https://coloradocpace.com/start-a-project/counties-and-government/)
Counties must opt in for the Colorado C-PACE program to operate in their jurisdiction. See how local governments can support clean energy, job creation, and economic development by partnering with NEID and enabling commercial property owners to access C-PACE financing. 
[Our Program for Counties and Government ](https://coloradocpace.com/start-a-project/counties-and-government/)
![](http://coloradocpace.com/wp-content/uploads/2025/07/banking.jpg)
### [Capital Providers ](https://coloradocpace.com/start-a-project/capital-providers/)
Join a competitive, open marketplace where you can fund secure, clean energy improvement projects across Colorado. Colorado C-PACE welcomes all qualified capital providers and offers support in connecting you with finance-ready opportunities. 
[Our Program for Capital Providers ](https://coloradocpace.com/start-a-project/capital-providers/)

## About Colorado C-PACE
![title-image](http://coloradocpace.com/wp-content/uploads/2023/07/yellow-banner.png)
C-PACE enables owners of eligible commercial and industrial buildings to finance up to 100% of energy efficiency, renewable energy and water conservation eligible improvements. Financing is provided by private capital providers at competitive rates with repayment terms up to 25 years.
[Accessibility](http://coloradocpace.com/accessibility/)
