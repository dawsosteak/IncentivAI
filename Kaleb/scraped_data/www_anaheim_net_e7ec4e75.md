#  Permits & Requirements 
The City of Anaheim has over 60 spacious parks where you can get active and enjoy the outdoors. Our facility-staff is here to assist you in obtaining an athletic field, special event, or open space permit. 
## Applications
  * [Picnic Shelter Application](http://anaheim.net/play)
    * See below for the 2026 Picnic Shelter and Open Area fee schedule. Please note that not all shelter facilities offer sink and/or electrical access. Visit anaheim.net/play for more detailed facility information. Reservations must be made _at minimum_ 3 days in advance.
  * [Open Area Application](https://www.anaheim.net/DocumentCenter/View/68055/2026-Open-Area-Application)
    * Open Area applications are to be submitted for gatherings of 25 or more in parks that do not offer Picnic Shelter facilities. Please note that gatherings over 100 are considered Special Events.
  * [Special Event Application](https://www.anaheim.net/DocumentCenter/View/37724/Special-Event-Application-_PDF)
    * All Special Event Applications are required _at least_ 180 days prior to the event and will be accepted up to one year in advance of the event. Please be prepared to provide a proposed event layout.
  * [Athletic Field Application](https://www.anaheim.net/DocumentCenter/View/68028/2026-Application-Packet-FINAL)
    * A valid Certificate of Insurance compliant with the requirements outlined in the Athletic Field Application Packet will be required prior to permit allocation. Applications must be received _at least_ two weeks prior to the requested reservation date. All applications received within 10 days of the reservation date will be assessed a **$28 permit rush fee.**
  * [Film Permit Application](https://www.anaheim.net/DocumentCenter/View/68005/Film-Permit-Application)
    * All filming conducted in a City Park must first be approved by the Community Services Facility Scheduling Section. Please submit your application to facilityscheduling@anaheim.net or contact (714) 765-5155 for more information.
