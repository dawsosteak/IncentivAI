# Housing Element Certification
On October 14, 2025 the California Department of Housing and Community Development (HCD) issued a letter certifying that the City of Anaheim’s Housing Element is compliant with State Housing Element Law.
Visit the [Project Documents](https://www.anaheim.net/6704/) webpage and [Draft Housing Element](https://www.anaheim.net/6718/) webpage to see all other related project documents.
