## Natural Gas Regulation
Regulated natural gas utilities include Delmarva Power and Chesapeake Utilities
[![Electric Regulation](https://depsc.delaware.gov/wp-content/uploads/sites/54/2017/02/psc_spotlight_powerlines.jpg)](https://depsc.delaware.gov/electric-regulation/)
## Electric Regulation
The Delaware Public Service Commission only controls the distribution services under the regulatory control.
[![Water Regulation](https://depsc.delaware.gov/wp-content/uploads/sites/54/2017/02/psc_spotlight_water.jpg)](https://depsc.delaware.gov/water-regulation/)
## Water Regulation
Most aspects of water distribution, including the rates that can be charged, are regulated by the PSC.
