#  Get up to $200,000 to Advance Your Commercial or Multifamily Project  

## Save energy and money without breaking the bank. CORE provides funding for energy efficiency and electrification projects through grants and rebates.
[ Apply for a Commercial or Multifamily Rebate ](https://energysmartcolorado.formstack.com/forms/business_rebateapp)
![Aspen CORE connects locals with sustainable solutions and expert energy guidance.](https://lirp.cdn-website.com/06c12080/dms3rep/multi/opt/18-1-1920w.png)
Electrification projects in our commercial and multifamily building sector have the potential to make the biggest impact on reducing greenhouse gas emissions and reaching our climate action goals. Energy efficiency projects can also produce meaningful reductions in greenhouse gas emissions.  

###  Commercial and Multifamily Building Rebates  

Funding for commercial and multifamily building electrification projects is available to community members living in Pitkin County, Garfield County, and Eagle County. Rebates are available for existing buildings unless otherwise noted. Multifamily buildings are defined as complexes with four or more units in the building. Multifamily projects must be in the common area of the building and/or every unit of the building. 
#### Energy Efficiency &   
Fuel Switching Projects
**Rebate Amounts**
  * 50% of the project cost up to $25,000 for standard participants
  * 50% of the project cost up to $50,000 for [Community Priority Participants](https://www.aspencore.org/community-priority-participants)

#### Not sure where to start?
Not a problem; we're here to help! [Contact our Energy Concierge](https://www.aspencore.org/energy-concierge) at any stage of your building project. Find answers to other frequently asked questions about rebates on our [FAQ webpage](https://www.aspencore.org/faq).
#### Looking for more funding?
You may be eligible for even more funding! Local community members, from teachers and childcare workers to veterans and government staff, may qualify for savings of up to $10,000 on a single residential project, and up to $50,000 for a commercial or multifamily building project.
  

Check out our [Community Priority Participants](https://www.aspencore.org/community-priority-participants) criteria or our [Savings Calculator](https://www.aspencore.org/federal-state-and-local-funding-programs) to see if you qualify for more incentives.
  

Plus, CORE can help you find and unlock additional funding beyond the rebates we can provide.

##### Grant Eligible Projects
Grants are intended for larger, long-term, and more impactful projects where rebates are not enough to fulfill the project needs and where grants can make the difference in completing the project.
  

Energy efficiency and building electrification projects in existing buildings are the focus of CORE’s grant program, but applications for new construction are also considered. Grants are awarded for implementation projects only; design work is supported through the [**rebates**](https://www.aspencore.org/residential-rebates).
  

Grant applications are accepted on a rolling basis as funds allow. CORE staff works with interested parties to analyze the greenhouse gas impact of a project before an application is submitted. **Contact our Energy Concierge team to discuss your interest in applying for a grant.**
