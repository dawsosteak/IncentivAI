#  Donations 
## Why a $5 Entrance Donation?
  
Your donation goes directly to the Oak Canyon Nature Center and allows us to fund family and nature programming, trail improvements, museum exhibits, and more. Thank you for your support!   
  
Can I donate online?  
Yes! For nearly fifty years, Oak Canyon Nature Center's hands-on education programs, such as the Discover Nature Family Program and Nature Nights, have inspired guests of all ages to enjoy and protect our natural world. Generous donations from individuals, business, and corporations make programs like this possible. We hope you will consider joining others in our community in supporting our important work.  
  
Please make a tax deductible gift at [www.anaheimcf.org](http://www.anaheimcf.org "Anaheim Community Foundation") today to help us continue providing quality education through hands-on experiences with nature. We are grateful for your gift, and genuinely appreciate every dollar we receive.   
  
Thank you for partnering with us to expand the Oak Canyon Nature Center's educational outreach in the community.  
  

## Sponsors  

Program sponsorship opportunities are available for business and individuals. We would love to partner with you! Email us at ocnc@anaheim.net for more information.
