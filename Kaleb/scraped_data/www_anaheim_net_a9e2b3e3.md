#  Report STR Violations 
**Code Enforcement Hotline and Email**
The City has established **evening short term rental enforcement** from **8****p.m. to 2 a.m.** Below is the direct hotline and email address to send pictures or video for violations occurring related to short-term rentals. If reported in the evening, our night time officer will respond. If reported during the day, we will have the day time officer respond and enforce accordingly. 
**Short-Term Rental Hotline** (714) 745-0190
**Report Short-Term Rental Violations to Email Address** : STRs@anaheim.net (you may attach pictures or video)  

The STR Code Enforcement program is administered by the Community Preservation Division, which has the power to:
  * Investigate complaints and issue notices of violation
  * Issue Civil Citations according to the approved penalty schedule
  * Conduct compliance inspections when deemed necessary
