Our commercial rebate programs encourage energy efficiency and conservation to assist business owners in reducing their monthly electric costs.
Please call (855) 516-2105 or email rebate@esgroupllc.com for more information and to receive an application.
Commercial and Industrial Lighting
Business Custom Projects
Lighting (1-for-1 and other upgrades) and Custom projects are capped at 50% of cost, up to a maximum of $75,000. Pre-approval is required.
