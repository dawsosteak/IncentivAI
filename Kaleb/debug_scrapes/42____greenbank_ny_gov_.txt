About Us
NY Green Bank, a division of NYSERDA, is a specialized investment fund dedicated to bridging financial gaps and mobilizing private capital to advance the build-out of clean energy and renewable infrastructure in New York State.
Work with Us
We offer flexible capital solutions to bridge financing gaps for developers, property owners, service providers, and investors working on clean energy and renewable infrastructure in New York State.
Our Impact
We support projects that accelerate New York State’s clean energy market, mobilize private capital, and reduce greenhouse gas emissions.
NY Green Bank’s work includes
$2.6+ billion committed to clean and renewable energy
Bridging the capital gap to deploy more green energy in NYS
Supporting projects that reduce CO2e emissions
Transforming financing markets and mobilizing private capital
