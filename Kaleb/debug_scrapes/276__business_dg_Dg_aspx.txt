Program Participation
To reduce annual electric use and demand, IOUs must offer a variety of energy efficiency programs for residential and commercial customers, including market transformation programs (MTPs) and standard offer programs (SOPs). Specific program details for each of the IOUs can be found in the annual IOU reports.
