MDPACE
MDPACE is a statewide program that provides turn-key, low cost, standardized C-PACE (Commercial Property Assessed Clean Energy) program services to property owners, capital providers, contractors, and local governments in the state of Maryland.
MDPACE is a simple and effective way for county governments to create jobs, retain businesses, and keep dollars in the local economy without raising taxes – simply by helping to make commercial buildings more energy-efficient. Property owners accept a voluntary tax assessment to fund projects ranging in size from $50,000-$100,000.
Send our finance team and email to get your application started today!
MDPACE Program Principles
- Keep it Low Cost and Simple: Establish clear, concise guidelines and transparent processes without high fees.
- Keep it Open: Catalyze innovation and promote growth through open market innovation.
- Keep it Standard: Enable scale by implementing one set of rules for capital providers and contractors.
About C-PACE Programs
As an assessment like a real estate tax, C-PACE is a simple and effective way for county governments to realize a range of benefits by helping make commercial buildings more energy efficient. Learn more about the benefits of these programs, and the kinds of projects that qualify.
For Commercial Property Owners
With C-PACE, property owners benefit from no money down and extended repayment terms. They can realize positive cash flow from day one.
For Contractors
Contractors can use C-PACE to fund projects that would not get funded otherwise and to incorporate valuable energy efficiency measures into a project.
For Capital Providers
For capital providers, C-PACE is a financing product secured by the property and a method of improving the value of existing assets.
For Municipalities
C-PACE is a means for governments and municipalities to improve economic development and energy efficiency, all without using tax payer funds.
Jurisdictional Differences
Because PACE loans are enabled by jurisdictions adopting enabling legislation through an ordinance adhering to legislative principles, C-PACE loan administration procedures in Maryland vary by county.

Procurement & Technical Assistance
Simplifying Your Project
Clean energy and decarbonization projects requiring innovative solutions and careful planning to succeed. At MCEC, we simplify the process offering a comprehensive suite of technical services designed to accelerate project development and execution. From initial concepts to full-scale implementation, we provide the expertise, tools, and support needed to navigate complexities, overcome obstacles, and achieve impactful results faster.
RFPs & Announcements
RFP: Market Development & Governance Structure Analysis of Geothermal Projects
Project Design & Feasibility
Identify and work with partners to help project owners access development planning resources, determine eligibility for low and/or no cost financing, and complete a feasibility assessment.
Regulatory Navigation
Review federal and state regulatory requirements, offering guidance through permitting process and other program applications.
Financing & Grant Funding
Match organizational needs with low-cost financing and grant funding opportunities and implementation partners. Identify approved financial institutions and partners, offering grant writing assistance is also available.
Measurement & Verification
Identify and benchmark performance metrics and key performance indicators over time. Support tracking progress to confirm guaranteed and/or projected savings versus realized energy savings, and to quantify actual financial benefit versus projections.
Procurement Services
Obtain services directly on behalf of clients, and/or collaborate with them on procuring of projects and services consistent with MCEC’s statutory mission and objectives. MCEC serves as a strategic and neutral advisor, enabling us to provide tailored services to help clients at any stage in their procurement process. This includes the identification of approved project partners, evaluating proposals, and selecting vendors.
