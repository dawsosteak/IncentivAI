Residential EV Incentive
Up to $125 Available from BCREMC!
Be sure to explore additional state and federal incentives that may be available to you to maximize your savings. Take advantage of these special offers from BCREMC to enhance your electric vehicle experience:
EV Registration
Register your EV with BCREMC to stay informed about future programs, incentives, and updates just for EV owners.
How to Register:
Submit a photo of your BMV registration as proof of ownership.
Incentive:
BEV (Battery Electric Vehicle) owners will receive a $50 Mastercard Gift Card.
Time-of-Use (TOU) Enrollment
Both BEV and PHEV (Plug-in Hybrid Electric Vehicle) owners can participate in the TOU rate program and receive an incentive. The TOU rate encourages you to charge your electric vehicle during off-peak hours, at a reduced cost per kWh.
Eligibility:
EV owners that are current TOU participants or new TOU enrollees who commit to the program for at least 12 months.
Incentive:
Receive a $75 Mastercard Gift Card upon enrollment or confirmation of participation.
4-H Enrollment Fee Reimbursement
BCREMC is proud to support the local 4-H program by reimbursing the $20 participation fee to BCREMC members.
Year 2026 Incentive Programs Effective for installations January 1, 2026 through December 15, 2026. Be sure to explore additional state and federal incentives that may be available to you to maximize your savings. Links below.
HVAC Tune-Up
One incentive per member per calendar year.
Incentive – 50% of serviced cost up to $50:
- Must complete application and submit copy of a detailed receipt/invoice and be submitted within 90 days or within the same calendar year as serviced
- HVAC unit must be 3 or more years old to qualify
- Tune-up service must be performed by a licensed HVAC contractor
Electric Outdoor Equipment
Two incentives per member per calendar year.
- Equipment purchased must be new and have a minimum one-year warranty
- Equipment must be battery or plug-in electrical equipment
- Application and receipt must be submitted within 90 days of purchase date or in the same program year
Incentive – 50% of purchased equipment up to allotted amount:
- Handheld equipment (trimmer, leaf blower, chain saw) – up to $50
- Push-type equipment (snow blower, tiller, push mower) – up to $50
- Riding equipment (riding tractor, zero-turn mowers) – up to $150
Residential HVAC
Questions to Ask When Replacing Your HVAC Brochure
Two incentives per member.
- Equipment installed must be new and be AHRI certified
- Application and itemized receipt/invoice must be submitted within 90 days of installation or within same calendar year
- Rebates are for whole-home installation except for Single-room Mini-Split Heat Pumps
- Single-room Mini-Split Heat Pumps are only for single room addition to primary home and external outbuildings (garage, shed, barn, greenhouse, etc.)
- Minimum HSPF2 ratings: 7.6
Minimum SEER2 ratings:
- ASHP minimum SEER2 Tiered Levels 14.3 – 15.3; 15.4 – 17.2; 17.2 and Greater
- MSHP minimum SEER2 17.3
- SRMSHP minimum SEER2 17.3
- GEO – no minimum SEER2 rating – Incentive will be flat $2,000/unit
Air Source Heat Pump (Whole Home):
- Tier 1 – 14.3 – 15.3 SEER 2 = $300.00/unit incentive
- Tier 2 – 15.4 – 17.2 SEER 2 = $400.00/unit incentive
- Tier 3 – 17.3 & Greater SEER 2 = $500.00/unit incentive
Mini-Split Heat Pump (Whole Home):
- 17.3 SEER 2 & Greater = $500.00/unit incentive
Mini-Split Heat Pump (Single Room):
- 17.3 SEER 2 & Greater = $300.00/unit incentive
Geothermal Heat Pump:
- No SEER2 Requirement = $2,000/unit incentive
Water Heater
Two incentives per member.
- Minimum UEF ratings – .90 (must show proof of UEF ratings – ex. Technical spec’s or AHRIcertificate)
- Equipment installed must be new
- Application and receipt/invoice showing model # and cost (minus tax and labor) must be submitted within 90 days of installation or within same calendar year
Heat Pump Water Heater $500:
Wi-Fi enabled electric storage water heater $200
