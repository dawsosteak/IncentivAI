Residential Window / Patio Door Rebate
Consumers Power Inc. » Residential Rebates » Window / Patio Door Rebate
Offered to Consumers Power Inc. residential customers who meet eligibility requirements.
Pre-approval is strongly encouraged.
Requirements
Your home
- Primary heat source must be electric
- Must be a single-family, multi-family or manufactured home
- New construction homes are not eligible
Qualifying conditions
For projects completed on or after July 1, 2025:
- Pre-existing windows or patio doors must be either single- or double-paned. Pre-existing triple-paned windows or patio doors are not eligible.
- Replacement windows must have a U-factor of 0.30 or lower.
- Replacement patio doors must have a U-factor of 0.35 or lower.
- Rebates are not available for replacement windows or patio doors outside the electrically heated area (e.g., garages and workshops), or in any space with an overhead door.
Installations must meet the requirements highlighted on BPA’s Window Installation Checklist checklist. For complete specifications, instructions, and best practices for installations, see BPA’s contractors’ guide.
Verification
Pre-existing windows must be verified by one of the following options:
- Contractor notes
- Clear pictures (can include photos of old windows still on site)
- Audit by a utility representative (via email, quote or final invoice)
For projects completed before 7/1/25, the old windows must be single-pane or double-pane with metal frames.
For brief information about energy-efficient windows, see the U.S. Department of Energy’s 1-page guide.
Required Documents
- A copy of one NFRC sticker per window or the manufacturer’s window order confirmation with U-factor.
- The final invoice including the quantities and window measurements of each new window, the total cost (including labor) and purchase or installation date. Estimates, proposals or quotes are not accepted as the final invoice.
- Verification of proper installations and window specifications, either by photos or a final inspection, are verified before rebates are processed.
Rebates
Windows
Site Built Homes & Manufactured Homes
- U-factor 0.30 or lower: $8 per square foot
Multi-Family Homes: Contact us
Patio Doors
- U-factor 0.35 or lower: $8 per square foot
Multi-Family Homes: Contact us
Rebates are limited to 100% of the total project cost.
Last Updated: 10/1/25

Residential Exterior Door Rebate
Consumers Power Inc. » Residential Rebates » Exterior Door Rebate
Offered to Consumers Power Inc. residential customers who meet eligibility requirements.
Pre-approval is strongly encouraged.
Requirements
Your Home
- Primary heat source must be electric
- Must be a single-family, multi-family low-rise or manufactured home
- Multi-family mid/high-rise homes are not eligible
- New construction homes are not eligible
The Project
- The new exterior door must replace an uninsulated or otherwise thermally substandard exterior door
- The door must separate an electrically heated space from the outside or unheated area (e.g. an unheated garage)
- The new exterior doors must be pre-hung and ENERGY STAR® rated installed to BPA specifications
Verification
- Pre-existing doors must be verified by one of the following options:
- Contractor notes (via email, quote or final invoice)
- Clear pictures (can include photos of old doors still on site)
- Audit by a utility representative
Required Documents
- Verification of the installed exterior door either by photos or a final inspection
- A copy of one NFRC sticker per door or proof that the exterior door is ENERGY STAR rated
- The final invoice, including door order/purchase date, installed cost, manufacturer, model and quantity. Estimates, proposals or quotes are not accepted as the final invoice.
Rebate: $40 per door
Rebates are limited to 100% of the total project cost.
Allow 8 to 10 weeks after final inspection approval and receipt of all required documentation for rebate checks to be issued and mailed.
Last Updated: 10/1/25

Consumers Power Inc. » Residential Rebates » Insulation Rebate
Offered to Consumers Power Inc. residential customers who meet eligibility requirements.
Pre-approval is strongly encouraged.
Requirements
Your Home
- Primary heat source must be electric.
- Must be a single-family, multi-family or manufactured home.
- Manufactured homes are not eligible for the wall insulation rebate.
- New construction homes are not eligible.
Qualifications
- Rebates are not available for insulation upgrades outside the electrically heated area (e.g., garages and workshops), or any space with an overhead door.
- Installations must meet the requirements highlighted in BPA’s weatherization contractor’s guide. Please see the specifications given for each type of insulation project area (wall, attic, floor).
Verification
Pre-existing insulation must be verified by one of the following options:
- Contractor notes verifying beginning & ending R-value (via email, quote or final invoice).
- Clear pictures of the existing insulation with a tape measure showing the depth.
- Audit of the existing insulation by a utility representative.
- Wall insulation: In-progress pictures or notes from the contractor.
Required Documents
- Verification of the installed insulation either by photos using a tape measure to show its depth or a final site inspection of the upgraded insulation is required before the rebate will be processed
- Final invoice stating total cost, installation/purchase date, beginning and final R-value and the square footage of upgraded insulation
- Please note, all invoices must be final
- Estimates, proposals or quotes are not accepted as the final invoice
