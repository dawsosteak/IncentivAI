Lodi Electric Utility lends support to its commercial and industrial customers by providing on-bill zero-percent financing for energy efficient improvements including LED light projects both large and small, HVAC, manufacturing equipment, refrigeration, you name it. The program is called the Energy Efficiency Financing Program (EEFP) providing up to $150,000 to be paid back monthly over two years.
Energy Efficiency Financing Program Application
Eligibility
Customer must be assigned to the G1, G2, G3, G4, G5 and/or I1 electric utility rate. Requirements: customer must be current on all utility charges with no late charges for the past 18 months; must have an active COL account for the past 18 months; must receive a rebate through the COL Electric Utility Public Benefits/Energy Efficiency Program; and submit an energy efficiency audit/contractor's proposal.
The project cost - after rebate is applied - must be a minimum of $1,500. Loan maximum per "customer of record" is $50,000 for G1 and G2 customers and up to $150,000 for customers assigned to the G3, G4, G5 or I1 rate, with a maximum 2-year, 0% loan term.
See program application for more details.
