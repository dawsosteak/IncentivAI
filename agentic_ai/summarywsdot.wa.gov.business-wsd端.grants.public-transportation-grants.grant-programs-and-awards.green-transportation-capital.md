## Summary of Green Transportation Capital Grant Program

The Green Transportation Capital Grant Program provides funding for transit agencies in Washington state to implement capital projects that reduce carbon emissions. Key points include:

- **Funding Sources**: 76.7% from the Climate Commitment Act (CCA) and 23.3% from the Multimodal Account.
- **Eligible Applicants**: Various transit agencies including city, county, and regional systems.
- **Eligible Projects**: Electrification of transit fleets, infrastructure for charging stations, and fleet transition planning.
- **Match Requirements**: Tiered match percentages based on the agency's operating budget (ranging from 0% to 20%).
- **Awards**: $31.3 million allocated for 2025-2027, with eight projects receiving funding.
- **Evaluation Criteria**: Project description, scalability, benefits, budget, and readiness to proceed.
