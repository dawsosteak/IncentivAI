## Additional Information
**Background:** Illinois' energy conservation law began with the 1986 Public Utilities Act, requiring least-cost planning. The 2003 Energy Conservation Code established commercial standards, with residential codes mandated by the 2009 Energy Efficient Building Act. State-funded buildings must meet ASHRAE 90.1 and IECC standards with LEED certification requirements.

**Adoption Process:** Local governments can adopt stricter commercial codes but must align with state residential codes unless exemptions apply (municipalities with >1M population or pre-2009 code adopters).
