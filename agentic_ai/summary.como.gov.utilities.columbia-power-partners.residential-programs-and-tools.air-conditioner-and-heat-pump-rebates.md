Summary of Air Conditioner and Heat Pump Rebates:

- Rebate amounts range from $800 to $1,200 based on SEER2 ratings (15.2-18.1 and 18.2+).
- Heat pumps qualify for discounted rates during winter (October-May) and additional electrification rebates.
- Eligibility requires being a City of Columbia Utilities electric customer with a working heat pump (15.2 SEER2).
- Application steps include submitting a completed rebate application, paid receipt, permits, and adherence to deadlines.
- Funding is available through the Home Performance with Energy Star loan program.
